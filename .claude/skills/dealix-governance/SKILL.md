# Dealix Governance Skill

## Block
guaranteed revenue · fake proof/testimonials/scarcity · auto-send ·
cold WhatsApp/LinkedIn automation · scraping behind login · unsupported claims ·
customer name/logo/quote without written approval · external action without founder approval ·
future/beta/docs-only module shown as live.

## Require
- a **Claims Register** entry per external claim (evidence / hypothesis / rewritten)
- Human Approval Policy · No-Spam Policy · Data Retention Policy · Proof Pack policy
- a module status label on every capability claim

## Gates
- `python scripts/verify_website_positioning.py`
- `make security-smoke`
- grep `landing/`, `frontend/src/`, `README*.md` for unsafe patterns (skip negations/policy text)
