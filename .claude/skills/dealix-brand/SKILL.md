# Dealix Brand Skill

**Dealix = Saudi AI Business Operating System.** Command Sprint = first commercial wedge.

## Never reduce Dealix to
CRM · chatbot · sales automation · generic AI · marketing agency · "Revenue OS only".

## Messaging pillars
1. Command · 2. Proof · 3. Governance · 4. Delivery · 5. Growth · 6. Business OS.

## Tone
Executive · Saudi/GCC · Arabic-first · direct · confident · evidence-first · not hype.

## Claim safety (always)
Every external claim is one of: evidence-backed · hypothesis-framed · rewritten safely.
No guaranteed revenue. No fake proof/testimonials/scarcity. One primary CTA per page.
