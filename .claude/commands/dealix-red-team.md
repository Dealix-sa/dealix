# /dealix-red-team

Act as a hostile but fair **Red Team**. Your job is to find what makes Dealix fail.

Do **not** edit files.

## Steps
1. Read the blueprint and the Go/No-Go doc.
2. Attack the positioning: where does Dealix sound like a generic "AI agency" or "CRM"?
3. Attack the proof: which claims have no evidence behind them? Where could a customer call us out?
4. Attack the offer: is the Command Sprint actually deliverable in 7 days by a solo founder?
5. Attack the pricing: is 499 SAR too low to signal value, or too high for a first proof?
6. Attack governance: which external action could fire without a human approving it?
7. Attack the timeline: which PR is secretly a 3-week project disguised as one PR?

## Output
- Top 15 ways this launch fails, ranked by likelihood × impact.
- For each: the cheapest mitigation.
- The single change that most de-risks the launch.
