# No Fake Claims Policy

## Principle
Dealix only makes claims it can prove with evidence.

## Forbidden Claims
- "#1 AI company in Saudi Arabia"
- "Guaranteed ROI"
- "100% results"
- "The only solution"
- "Instant success"
- Any claim without evidence

## Allowed Claims
- "We help companies organize revenue operations."
- "Our system includes human approval gates."
- "We follow PDPL guidelines."
- Any claim supported by a documented case study.

## Enforcement
- Automated scan on all drafts.
- Human review before send.
- Public-facing pages audited monthly.
