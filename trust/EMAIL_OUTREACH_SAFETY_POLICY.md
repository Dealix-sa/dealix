# Email Outreach Safety Policy

## Requirements
1. **Verified targets only** — every recipient has a source URL.
2. **Unsubscribe link** — every email contains clear unsubscribe instructions.
3. **No fake claims** — no absolute or unverifiable statements.
4. **No guaranteed ROI** — no promises of specific returns.
5. **Rate limits** — max 25 emails/day, 10/batch, 90 seconds between sends.
6. **Opt-out respect** — stop sending immediately on unsubscribe.
7. **Deliverability** — SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured for dealix.me.

## Prohibited
- Purchased email lists.
- Cold blasting.
- Misleading subject lines.
- Hidden sender identity.

## Monitoring
- Daily review of bounce rate.
- Daily review of spam complaints.
- Suppression list updated automatically.
