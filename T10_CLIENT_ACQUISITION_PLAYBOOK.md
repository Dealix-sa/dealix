# T10 Client Acquisition Pack

## Purpose

T10 turns the revenue machine into client-facing acquisition assets.

## Outputs

- sector one-page offers
- proposal templates
- email drafts
- WhatsApp drafts
- manual LinkedIn notes
- discovery scripts
- founder daily sales command

## Sectors

- clinics
- agencies
- training
- logistics

## Daily commercial routine

1. Run the revenue machine.
2. Run T10.
3. Pick the highest scoring account.
4. Review the sector one-page offer.
5. Use the discovery script.
6. Prepare drafts only.
7. Approve before any external action.
8. Record results back into the proof report.

## Sales motion

Start with a small sprint, show operational proof, then expand into a managed monthly command room.

## Safety rule

All channel copy is draft only. The founder or owner must review before use.
