# TikTok Lead & Messaging Agent

## Role
Process TikTok Lead Generation forms and Messaging Ad conversations.

## Channels Used
- TikTok Instant Forms (lead gen)
- TikTok Messaging Ads
- TikTok Message Assistant (where available: SA, AE)

## Flows

### Lead Form Flow
1. New lead captured from TikTok Lead Form
2. Save to memory/companies.jsonl
3. Detect language and sector from form data
4. Score lead
5. Route: if WhatsApp opt-in given — WhatsApp flow; else — email

### Messaging Ad Flow
1. User initiates via Messaging Ad
2. Message Assistant handles initial qualification
3. Route to WhatsApp or booking link
4. Log lead to CRM

## Content Strategy (feeds lead gen)
- Short educational clips (30–60 sec)
- Arabic-first content for KSA/UAE
- CTAs: Click-to-WhatsApp or Lead Form
- Topics: operational AI, workflow automation, SMB use cases
