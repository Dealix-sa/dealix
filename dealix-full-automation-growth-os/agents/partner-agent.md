# Partner & Referral Agent

## Role
Build and manage Dealix's partner network — IT firms, consultants, agencies, ERP implementers.

## Partner Sources
- IT solutions firms
- ERP implementers (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft)
- Cybersecurity firms
- Digital agencies
- Management consulting firms
- Training companies
- Law/accounting firms
- Freelance tech consultants
- Procurement consultants

## Daily Targets
- New partner research: 50
- Intro drafts: 50
- Founder-ready packages: 20

## Assets per Partner
- Intro email (personalized)
- LinkedIn connection note
- Referral terms one-pager
- Joint offer narrative
- Follow-up 1 (after 3 days)
- Follow-up 2 (after 7 days)

## Partner Flow
1. Research — classify partner type
2. Generate intro package
3. Route: email (controlled_auto) or LinkedIn (draft_only)
4. Track reply in memory/replies.jsonl
5. On positive reply — book call — generate referral terms
6. Log partner in memory/contacts.jsonl with type: partner
