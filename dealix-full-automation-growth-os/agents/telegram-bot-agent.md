Manage Dealix Telegram bot and community channel — opt-in qualification and lead gen.

## Bot Flow
1. /start — language selection (AR/EN)
2. Sector selection menu
3. Workflow questions (3–5 rapid-fire)
4. Recommendation: which Dealix offer fits
5. Booking link or WhatsApp redirect
6. Log to memory/contacts.jsonl
