# Dealix Brand System v2

## Positioning
Dealix is an AI Business Transformation company.

We build operating systems for companies:
- WhatsApp Revenue OS
- Review Intelligence OS
- AI Business Command Center
- Brand Intelligence OS
- Growth Engine OS
- Customer Experience OS
- AI Agent Workforce OS
- Custom Enterprise Systems

## Core Arabic message
Dealix يحوّل فوضى التشغيل اليومية إلى أنظمة أعمال قابلة للقياس.

## Core English message
Dealix turns operational chaos into measurable business operating systems.

## Visual direction
- Dark executive background
- Electric blue accents
- Saudi green trust accents
- Clean dashboard-style visuals
- No childish robot imagery
- Strong Arabic CEO copy
- Before/after diagrams
- Screenshots and reports as proof

## Tone
- Executive
- Direct
- Strategic
- Saudi business aware
- Systems-first
- Trust-heavy

## What to avoid
- "نسوي بوت"
- "نسوي CRM"
- "نسوي داشبورد"
- "AI وخلاص"

- "نركب نظام تشغيل للشركة"
- "نقلل ضياع الفرص"
- "نوضح المتابعة"
- "نحول التقييمات إلى قرارات"
- "نطلع تقرير تنفيذي أسبوعي"
