# Dealix Visual Identity System

## Brand Positioning
Dealix is an AI-native business transformation company that builds operating systems for companies.

We do not sell chatbots.
We do not sell dashboards only.
We install measurable business systems.

## Core Message
Dealix builds AI Business Operating Systems that turn scattered WhatsApp conversations, weak follow-up, unmanaged reviews, slow proposals, and unclear reporting into measurable workflows and executive decisions.

## Brand Personality
- Executive
- Sharp
- Saudi-market aware
- Systems-first
- Trustworthy
- Strategic
- Technical but business-facing

## Visual Direction
### Colors
- Primary: Deep Navy / near-black
- Secondary: Electric Blue
- Accent: Saudi Green
- Neutral: Off-white / light gray
- Warning: Amber
- Success: Green

### Design Rules
- Use clean grids
- Use large executive headlines
- Use operational diagrams
- Use before/after workflow visuals
- Use dashboards/screenshots as proof
- Avoid childish AI robot visuals

## Typography Direction
- Arabic: modern geometric Arabic font
- English: clean SaaS/executive sans-serif
- Headings: strong and wide
- Body: clear, readable, business-like

## Visual Assets To Produce
- One-page offer
- Executive pitch deck
- Service cards
- LinkedIn founder posts
- WhatsApp proposal PDF
- Case study template
- Dashboard screenshots
- Weekly report PDF
