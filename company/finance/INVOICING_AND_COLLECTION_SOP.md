# Dealix Invoicing & Collection SOP

## Current context
Founder has freelance document and institutional bank account.

## Payment structure
- Setup fee paid before work starts.
- Monthly fee starts at go-live.
- Enterprise work starts with paid diagnostic.
- No unlimited scope.

## Invoice process
1. Confirm proposal acceptance.
2. Send payment details.
3. Issue invoice through compliant invoicing process.
4. Start work after payment confirmation.
5. Record payment in revenue ledger.
6. Send receipt/confirmation.

## Payment terms
- Diagnostic Sprint: 100% upfront
- Starter/Growth setup: 70% upfront, 30% before go-live
- Monthly retainer: upfront monthly
- Enterprise: milestone-based

## Collection rules
- No custom build without deposit.
- No production launch before payment milestone.
- Pause support after overdue period.
- Keep all payment proofs in finance folder outside public git if sensitive.

## Reminder messages
### Before due
السلام عليكم، تذكير بسيط بأن دفعة [PROJECT] مستحقة بتاريخ [DATE]. بمجرد تأكيد الدفع نكمل المرحلة التالية مباشرة.

### Overdue
السلام عليكم، يبدو أن دفعة [PROJECT] لم تصل حتى الآن. سنوقف التنفيذ مؤقتاً إلى حين تأكيد الدفع، ونكمل مباشرة بعد الاستلام.
