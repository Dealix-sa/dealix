# Dealix Commercial Rules v2

## Payment
- Diagnostic Sprint: 100% upfront
- Starter OS: 70% upfront, 30% before go-live
- Monthly support: paid at beginning of month
- Enterprise: milestone-based

## Do not start without
- clear scope
- payment confirmation
- client owner assigned
- data/source access confirmed

## No guarantee wording
Never guarantee exact revenue.
Use:
- expected improvement
- measurable workflow
- operational visibility
- reduced leakage
- faster follow-up

## Minimum offer ladder
1. Diagnostic Sprint: 7,500 - 25,000 SAR
2. Starter OS: 12,000 - 25,000 SAR
3. Growth OS: 25,000 - 75,000 SAR
4. Command Center: 75,000 - 180,000 SAR
5. Enterprise Custom: 180,000+ SAR
