# Commercial Rules v2

**Version**: 2.0 | **Last updated**: 2026-06-10

---

## Pricing Authority

Only the founder (Sami Assiri) can:
- Set or change prices
- Issue discounts
- Approve project scope changes
- Issue invoices or contracts

No automated pricing. No bots that issue quotes.

## Invoice Rules

- Platform: Zoho Invoice or Wave App (founder-operated manually)
- Currency: SAR only
- ZATCA: comply with e-invoicing requirements for B2B
- VAT: 15% included in all quoted prices (confirm with accountant)
- Payment methods: Bank transfer to Dealix account, STC Pay
- No PayPal, no foreign currency without explicit agreement

---

## Contract Rules

- All sprints require a written scope of work (even simple email confirmation)
- No verbal-only commitments for paid work
- Scope changes require written approval before work begins
- Refund policy: No refunds after work begins unless Dealix fails to deliver agreed outputs

---

## Revenue Recognition

- Sprint revenue: recognized on delivery of final output
- Retainer revenue: recognized monthly at start of period
- All receipts logged in `company/finance/REVENUE_LEDGER_TEMPLATE.csv`

---

## Cost Controls

- No SaaS tools above 200 SAR/month without founder approval
- No freelancers hired without written scope + rate agreement
- No external API spend above 500 SAR/month without approval
- Railway infra: review monthly, no autoscaling without limits set
