## Company stage gates
### Stage 1: Operating Company
- Production healthy
- Daily operating scripts work
- 3 offers ready
- CRM exists
- Legal/finance templates exist

### Stage 2: Revenue Company
- 1 paid diagnostic
- 1 weekly client report
- Revenue ledger updated
- 1 case study draft

### Stage 3: Repeatable Company
- 3 paying clients
- 2 repeatable verticals
- Delivery SOP reused
- Outreach assistant can operate SOP

### Stage 4: Enterprise-ready Company
- Trust evidence pack
- DPA/security docs
- Audit logs
- Vulnerability triage
- SLA and support process
