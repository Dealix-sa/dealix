# Dealix Autonomous Executive OS

## Purpose
This layer turns Dealix into a founder-led company that operates daily with minimal founder intervention.

It does not replace the founder. It prepares:
- Target companies
- Weakness analysis
- Outreach drafts
- Proposal stubs
- CRM updates
- CEO daily report
- Weekly board report
- Trust and production review
- Next actions

## Non-negotiable approval gates
The system may draft, score, prioritize, and prepare. It must not automatically:
- Send WhatsApp messages
- Send cold emails
- Sign contracts
- Issue invoices
- Charge customers
- Promise guaranteed revenue
- Delete data
- Export sensitive data
- Publish public content

## Human intervention target
Daily founder review should be reduced to:
- 10 minutes approving top outreach
- 10 minutes replying to warm leads
- 10 minutes reviewing CEO report
- 10 minutes updating won/lost/next actions

## Daily company loop
1. Production health check
2. Lead research
3. Target scoring
4. Draft generation
5. Proposal stub generation
6. CRM queue update
7. CEO report
8. Trust check
9. Founder approval queue

## Weekly company loop
1. Pipeline review
2. Revenue ledger review
3. Best segment analysis
4. Objection intelligence
5. Delivery capacity review
6. Trust posture review
7. Next week focus
