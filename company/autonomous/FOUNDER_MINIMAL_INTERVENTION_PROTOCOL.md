# Founder Minimal Intervention Protocol

## Daily founder review: 30-40 minutes

### 1. Open CEO report
company/reports/YYYY-MM-DD_AUTONOMOUS_CEO_REPORT.md

### 2. Open approval queue
company/outbox/YYYY-MM-DD_approval_queue.csv

### 3. Approve top 20 manually
Allowed statuses:
- approved_to_send
- research_more
- skip
- do_not_contact

### 4. Send manually
Use WhatsApp/email manually until approval-gated sending queue is implemented.

### 5. Update CRM
company/crm/pipeline.csv

## Weekly founder review: 45 minutes
- Review weekly board report
- Pick best segment
- Pick one offer to push
- Decide one product improvement
- Decide one trust improvement

## Never delegate blindly
- Payments
- Contracts
- Customer data export
- Production secrets
- External bulk sending
