# Dealix Company Operating System

This is the internal operating system that turns Dealix into a real founder-led transformation company.

It covers:
- Brand identity
- Offer system
- Daily lead generation
- Outreach drafts
- Proposal stubs
- CRM pipeline
- Finance and collection
- Legal/service templates
- Delivery SOPs
- Customer success
- Daily CEO report

Rule: Dealix drafts and prepares. The founder reviews before sending, signing, invoicing, or committing.
