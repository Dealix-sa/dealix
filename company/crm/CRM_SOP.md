# Dealix CRM SOP

## Statuses
- research_needed
- draft_ready
- contacted
- replied
- discovery_booked
- diagnostic_proposed
- proposal_sent
- negotiation
- won
- lost
- do_not_contact

## Daily CRM rules
- Every contacted company must have next_action.
- Every replied company must have next_followup_date.
- Every proposal must have deal_value_sar.
- Every lost deal must have lost reason in notes.
- Never keep sensitive personal data in git.

## Weekly review
- Reply rate
- Discovery booking rate
- Proposal rate
- Close rate
- Average deal value
- Best segment
- Best offer
- Main objection
