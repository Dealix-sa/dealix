# Founder Business Autopilot SOP

## Morning
1. Run production check.
2. Run founder business day.
3. Review top 20 outreach drafts.
4. Send manually after review.
5. Log responses.

## Midday
1. Reply to leads.
2. Book diagnostic calls.
3. Generate proposal stubs.
4. Push paid diagnostic sprint.

## Evening
1. Update statuses.
2. Count replies/calls/demos.
3. Extract objections.
4. Improve tomorrow's messaging.

## Weekly
- Pick winning segment.
- Kill low-response segment.
- Convert objections into content and offer improvements.
- Produce one case study or one public insight.
