# content/

Scaffolding for the Dealix Brand, Proof & Content Operating System v1.

These files are **templates and empty ledgers** intended to be copied or
mirrored into the private `dealix-ops-private` repository, where the
actual proof, calendar, published log, and approved claims are kept.

Public rules apply (see `docs/content/PROOF_LEVEL_POLICY.md`):
- No client identity in this public repo.
- No raw delivery feedback.
- No claim without evidence.

Files:
- `proof_library.md` — P0/P1/P2/P3 ledger template.
- `content_calendar.csv` — weekly publishing plan template.
- `published_log.csv` — record of what shipped and where.
- `content_ideas.md` — captured observations and drafts.
- `approved_claims.md` — claims cleared for public use, with evidence.
- `content_pipeline_influence.csv` — content-to-pipeline tracking.
- `templates/` — post and case-study templates.
