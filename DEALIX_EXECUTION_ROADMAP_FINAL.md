# Dealix Final Execution Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Operating Foundation
Goal:
All core systems documented, verified, and protected by GitHub checks.

Exit:
- master blueprint verifier passes.
- GitHub required checks active.
- private ops bootstrapped.
- company-check runs.

## Phase 2 — Revenue Proof
Goal:
Prove market interest and payment path.

Exit:
- 25 leads.
- 25 DMs.
- 3 samples.
- 1 proposal.
- payment / PO follow-up.
- weekly learning update.

## Phase 3 — Delivery Proof
Goal:
Deliver first paid or approved sprint.

Exit:
- client intake.
- delivery report.
- QA pass.
- handoff.
- feedback request.

## Phase 4 — Retention Proof
Goal:
Turn delivery into recurring support or next sprint.

Exit:
- client health score.
- retainer ask.
- feedback captured.
- productization signal logged.

## Phase 5 — Repeatability
Goal:
Repeat the same workflow until it is stable.

Exit:
- 3 paid sprints.
- 2 completed deliveries.
- 1 proof asset approved or anonymized.
- 1 retainer attempt.
- repeated workflow log.

## Phase 6 — Productization
Goal:
Automate only repeated, valuable, safe workflows.

Exit:
- 10 repeated executions.
- stable data model.
- stable QA.
- client demand for dashboard or ongoing system.
- SaaS readiness gate reviewed.

## Phase 7 — Scale
Goal:
Delegate or partner only after repeatability.

Exit:
- role scorecard.
- SOP.
- access control.
- QA metrics.
- partner/referral controls.
