# Dealix Priority Execution Sprint

## Purpose
Move Dealix from operating system setup to real market execution.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Duration
7 days.

## Primary Goal
Get the first paid Revenue Sprint or purchase-order/written approval.

## Secondary Goals
- Build first real pipeline.
- Send first founder-led outbound batch.
- Prepare first 3 samples.
- Send first proposal.
- Run first weekly learning review.
- Update one playbook based on evidence.

## Daily Targets

| Day | Focus | Required Output |
|---|---|---|
| Day 1 | Pipeline | 25 leads added |
| Day 2 | Outbound | 25 DMs sent |
| Day 3 | Samples | 3 samples prepared |
| Day 4 | Sales | 1 call or proposal |
| Day 5 | Payment | payment / PO / written approval pursued |
| Day 6 | Delivery | delivery template tested |
| Day 7 | Learning | weekly review + one system update |

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- No full delivery without payment, PO, or written approval.
- No guaranteed revenue claim.
- No client data in public repo.
- Every lead has a next action.
- Every proposal has follow-up date.
- Every week produces one learning decision.

## Success Criteria
- 25 leads exist in private pipeline.
- 25 leads contacted.
- 3 sample packs created.
- 1 proposal sent.
- 1 payment/PO/written approval attempt made.
- 1 weekly review written.
- 1 playbook updated.
