# Sprint Day Advancement Rule

## Purpose
Prevent moving to the next sprint day without evidence.

## Day Gates

### Day 1 → Day 2
Must have:
- 25 leads in pipeline.
- Each lead has next_action.

### Day 2 → Day 3
Must have:
- 25 contacted leads.
- Revenue action log updated.

### Day 3 → Day 4
Must have:
- 3 sample packs prepared.
- Each sample linked to prospect.

### Day 4 → Day 5
Must have:
- 1 proposal sent or 1 qualified call booked.

### Day 5 → Day 6
Must have:
- Payment / PO / written approval follow-up recorded.

### Day 6 → Day 7
Must have:
- Delivery report tested.
- QA checklist completed.

### Day 7 → Close Sprint
Must have:
- Weekly review.
- Learning decision.
- One system update or explicit reason.

## Rule
Evidence before advancement.
