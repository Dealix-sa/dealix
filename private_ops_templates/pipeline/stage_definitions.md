# Pipeline Stage Definitions

## New
Lead identified but not contacted.

## Qualified
Lead matches ICP and has business relevance.

## Contacted
Founder DM or first outreach sent.

## Replied
Prospect responded.

## Sample Sent
Sample pack sent.

## Call Booked
Call scheduled.

## Proposal Sent
Formal proposal sent.

## Paid
Payment, PO, or written approval received.

## Delivered
Revenue Sprint delivered.

## Retainer
Client converted to monthly support.

## Lost
Lead closed with reason.

## Rule
Do not move stages based on hope. Move stages based on evidence.
