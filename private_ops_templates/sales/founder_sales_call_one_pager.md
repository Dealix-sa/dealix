# Founder Sales Call One-Pager

## Goal
Qualify the prospect and close Revenue Sprint if fit is clear.

## Opening
Thanks for taking the time. I want to understand your current sales process and see if Revenue Sprint can create qualified opportunities for you.

## Qualification Questions
1. What do you sell?
2. Who is your best customer?
3. Average deal size?
4. How do you currently get leads?
5. Which sectors are you trying to reach?
6. What makes a lead bad fit?
7. If we delivered 30–75 qualified opportunities, what would make it successful?

## Fit Criteria
Good fit:
- B2B
- Saudi or Saudi-focused
- deal size above 5,000 SAR
- clear offer
- needs pipeline

Bad fit:
- wants guaranteed sales
- unclear offer
- low-ticket B2C
- refuses approval process

## Close
Based on what you shared, I recommend Revenue Sprint:
- qualified leads
- scoring
- outreach pack
- 14-day action plan

Work starts after payment, PO, or written approval.

## Trust Note
We do not guarantee sales or revenue. We deliver opportunity intelligence and execution assets.
