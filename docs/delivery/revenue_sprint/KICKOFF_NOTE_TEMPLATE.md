# Revenue Sprint Kickoff Note Template

The kickoff note is sent within 24 hours of payment being reconciled. It is a contract reminder, not a marketing email.

## Required fields

- Buyer name and named contact.
- Sprint ID.
- Sprint date range (start and final hand-off).
- Named bottleneck (one sentence).
- The artefact that will be produced (one sentence).
- The four buyer-facing deliverables, in order.
- The 4 sync timestamps proposed.

## Required references

- Link to [SCOPE.md](../../offers/revenue_sprint/SCOPE.md).
- Link to [REFUND_POLICY.md](../../offers/revenue_sprint/REFUND_POLICY.md).
- Link to the buyer's Proof Pack folder (private).

## Required language

- Bilingual: English first, Arabic second.
- One closing sentence stating that the buyer is free to ask any question by replying directly.

## Disallowed language

- Any of the banned phrases listed in [docs/trust/SAFE_LANGUAGE_LIBRARY.md](../../trust/SAFE_LANGUAGE_LIBRARY.md).
- Any promise of an outcome not in [DELIVERABLES.md](../../offers/revenue_sprint/DELIVERABLES.md).

## Sign-off

- Drafted by: Delivery Lead.
- Reviewed by: Revenue Lead.
- A1 approval before sending.
