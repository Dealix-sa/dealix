# Sprint Report Structure — هيكل تقرير السبرنت

## Purpose

Defines the structure of the deliverable PDF given to a buyer at the end of a Revenue Sprint. Same skeleton every sprint. Tailoring happens in content, not structure. (Originally requested filename: `REPORT_TEMPLATE.md` — renamed to avoid collision with internal artifact naming.)

## Deliverable PDF sections

### 1. Cover

- Buyer name and sector.
- Sprint ID.
- Date of handover.
- Prepared by (Delivery Lead).
- Disclaimer: "Estimated value is not Verified value / القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة."

### 2. Executive summary (one page)

- One paragraph: what we did.
- One paragraph: top finding.
- One paragraph: recommended next steps.
- Three to five bullet headline counts (counts only, no claims).

### 3. Scope confirmation

- Sector and sub-segments delivered.
- Geography delivered.
- Tier and add-ons.
- Anything requested but out of scope, with the recommended next rung.

### 4. The lead table

- Pointer to the file (`.xlsx` and `.csv`).
- Schema reference: [LEAD_TABLE_SCHEMA.md](./LEAD_TABLE_SCHEMA.md).
- Counts: total, top, secondary, monitor.
- Source-type distribution.

### 5. Top opportunities — narrative

For each of the top 5–10:

- Company name and one-paragraph public profile.
- Signal (why this company, this quarter).
- Source attribution.
- Recommended next step.

### 6. Methodology

- Public sources used.
- Scoring summary (link to [SCORING_RULES.md](./SCORING_RULES.md)).
- Limitations: stated plainly. What we did not see; what we excluded.

### 7. Next-step pack

- Pointer to the pack files.
- Summary of pack contents.

### 8. Notes on value and limits

- Estimated value framing.
- What the sprint cannot tell you.
- When to consider Data Pack (rung 3) or Managed Ops (rung 4).

### 9. Evidence

- Pointer to the evidence pack location in the buyer's secure share.
- Statement of retention per [docs/trust/AUDIT_POLICY.md](../../trust/AUDIT_POLICY.md).

### 10. Closing

- Confidentiality statement.
- Refund policy reference.
- Contact for follow-up questions (Delivery Lead).
- AR section summarizing sections 1–4 in parallel.

## Formatting rules

- One PDF = one report. No appendix files mixed in.
- Tables are tables, not screenshots.
- AR and EN are parallel sections, not translations.
- No marketing logos beyond the cover.

## AR — ملخّص

هيكل تقرير السبرنت عشرة أقسام: غلاف، ملخّص تنفيذي، تأكيد نطاق، جدول الفرص، أبرز الفرص بسرد، المنهجية، حزمة الخطوة التالية، ملاحظات القيمة والحدود، الأدلة، خاتمة. الهيكل ثابت لكل سبرنت، والتخصيص في المحتوى. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
