# Revenue Sprint Factory

## Purpose
Deliver Revenue Sprint repeatedly with quality, speed, and trust controls.

## Owner
Sami / Delivery owner.

## Review Cadence
Every delivery.

## Inputs
- client intake
- ICP
- target sectors
- exclusions
- lead sources
- evidence
- approval constraints

## Outputs
- qualified lead table
- scoring
- pain hypotheses
- outreach pack
- executive memo
- 14-day action plan
- handoff

## Factory Flow
1. Intake
2. Research
3. Deduplication
4. Scoring
5. Outreach draft
6. Executive memo
7. QA
8. Approval
9. Handoff
10. Feedback

## Rules
- No delivery without QA.
- No A-priority lead without evidence.
- No client report without approval.
- No overclaims.
- Client data stays private.
- Delivery scope must match proposal.

## Metrics
- delivery time
- QA pass rate
- rework count
- client feedback
- retainer conversion

## Evidence
- client intake
- report
- QA checklist
- handoff
- feedback
