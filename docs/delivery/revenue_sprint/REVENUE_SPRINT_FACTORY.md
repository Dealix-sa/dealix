# Revenue Sprint Factory

> The 14-day end-to-end production line.

## Sprint Definition

- **Length:** ≤ 14 calendar days (target: 10 working days).
- **Price:** 2,500–7,500 SAR.
- **Scope:** 25–50 qualified Saudi B2B accounts, bilingual outreach pack,
  executive memo, handoff call.
- **Customer experience:** Day 0 kickoff → Day 14 handoff + retainer ask.

## Stages

| Day | Stage | File |
|-----|-------|------|
| 0 | Intake | `CLIENT_INTAKE.md` |
| 1 | Onboarding call | `docs/client_success/ONBOARDING.md` |
| 2-3 | Research | `DELIVERY_PLAYBOOK.md` §research |
| 4 | Deduplication | `DELIVERY_PLAYBOOK.md` §dedup |
| 4-5 | Scoring | `SCORING_RULES.md` |
| 6-8 | Outreach pack | `OUTREACH_PACK_TEMPLATE.md` |
| 9 | Executive memo | `REPORT_TEMPLATE.md` |
| 10 | QA | `QA_CHECKLIST.md` |
| 11 | Approval | Founder T2 sign-off |
| 12 | Handoff | `HANDOFF_TEMPLATE.md` |
| 13-14 | Feedback + retainer ask | `docs/client_success/UPSELL_PLAYBOOK.md` |

## Outputs (delivered to customer)

- Spreadsheet / CSV of accounts with full enrichment (linked to evidence).
- Bilingual outreach pack (DM + email per account, ranked).
- Executive memo (1 page).
- Optional: anonymised case study capture for our own library.

## Outputs (kept internal)

- Updated sprint register (`dealix-ops-private/delivery/sprint_register.csv`).
- Friction-log entries for any process pain.
- Capital asset additions (named referrals, consented quotes, etc.).

## Capacity

- Founder-led: 2 sprints / month sustainable.
- With Delivery Analyst: 4 sprints / month sustainable.
- Above 4: bottleneck shifts to founder approval throughput.

## Time Budget per Stage

| Stage | Founder hours | Analyst hours |
|-------|----------------|----------------|
| Onboarding | 1.0 | 0.5 |
| Research | 2.0 | 6.0 |
| Dedup + Scoring | 1.0 | 2.0 |
| Outreach pack | 2.0 | 4.0 |
| Executive memo | 1.5 | 1.5 |
| QA | 1.0 | 1.0 |
| Handoff + retainer ask | 1.0 | 0.5 |
| **Total** | **~9.5** | **~15.5** |

Overruns are tracked. Two consecutive overruns of > 25% trigger a
template review.
