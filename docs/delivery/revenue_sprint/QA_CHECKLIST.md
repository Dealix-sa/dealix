# Revenue Sprint Delivery QA Checklist

Every Revenue Sprint deliverable must pass this checklist before handoff. The QA decision is recorded in the private delivery report.

## 1. Lead Quality
- [ ] Every lead matches the agreed ICP.
- [ ] Every A-priority lead has at least one evidence note.
- [ ] Duplicates have been removed.
- [ ] Bad-fit leads have been removed.
- [ ] Sector relevance is clear for every lead.
- [ ] Geography matches the agreed scope.

## 2. Outreach Quality
- [ ] Messages are specific to the prospect's context.
- [ ] Messages are short and readable.
- [ ] Call-to-action is clear and reasonable.
- [ ] No fake urgency.
- [ ] No guaranteed results, replies, meetings, or revenue.
- [ ] No claims of regulatory or compliance certification.
- [ ] Arabic and English versions (where applicable) carry the same meaning.

## 3. Trust Quality
- [ ] No sensitive client data is exposed in any public artefact.
- [ ] No unsupported public claim is present.
- [ ] No compliance or regulatory certification is claimed.
- [ ] No client name, logo, or quote is used externally without written approval.
- [ ] The no-overclaim policy has been read against this deliverable.

## 4. Delivery Quality
- [ ] Executive summary is present and accurate.
- [ ] Lead table is complete with required columns.
- [ ] Outreach pack is complete.
- [ ] 14-day action plan is present with concrete actions.
- [ ] Recommended next step is present.
- [ ] Risks and notes section is filled in honestly.

## 5. Handoff Quality
- [ ] Recommended first action is concrete.
- [ ] Operating cadence is documented.
- [ ] What-to-track list is present.
- [ ] Next Dealix support option is mentioned without pressure.

## Final Decision
One of:
- **PASS** — handoff approved.
- **FIX** — specific issues listed; handoff blocked until resolved.
- **BLOCKED** — escalation required (trust risk, scope gap, or missing approval).

The decision and notes are recorded in the private delivery report for that engagement.
