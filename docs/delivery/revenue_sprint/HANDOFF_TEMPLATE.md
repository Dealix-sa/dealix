# Handoff — Template

> Day 12. Live call. 30–45 minutes.

## Pre-call

- [ ] Send Proof Pack 24h before the call.
- [ ] Send the agenda 24h before the call.
- [ ] Note any items needing customer decision.

## Agenda

1. **Recap of scope** (2 minutes)
2. **Walk the Lead Table** (10–15 minutes)
   - Highlight A-priority accounts.
   - Show evidence per account live.
3. **Outreach pack walk-through** (5–10 minutes)
   - Read 1–2 examples aloud.
   - Confirm bilingual tone is acceptable.
4. **Executive memo** (5 minutes)
   - Walk through the 3 top opportunities.
   - Confirm next steps.
5. **Customer questions** (5–10 minutes)
6. **Retainer ask** (5 minutes)
   - Restate scope and price (per `OFFER_LADDER.md`).
   - "Should I send the scope?"
7. **Feedback request** (1 minute)
   - "What worked? What did not? What would you change?"

## Post-call

Within 24h:
- Send the recap email with action items and decisions.
- Send the retainer proposal (if asked).
- Send the structured feedback form.

Within 48h:
- Log the call notes in `dealix-ops-private/delivery/handoffs/<sprint_id>/`.
- Update sprint_register: delivered, retainer asked y/n.
- Log feedback as it arrives.

## What we do **not** do at handoff

- Push retainer aggressively if it is clearly not a fit.
- Discount on the spot.
- Promise future features.
- Talk past customer concerns.
