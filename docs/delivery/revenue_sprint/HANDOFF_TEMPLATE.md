# Handoff Template — Revenue Sprint

The handoff is a 30-minute call that closes the sprint and opens the next step.

## Pre-call
- Send the report and the outreach pack 24 hours before the call.
- Ask the client to skim the executive summary and top-tier leads.

## Agenda (30 minutes)
1. Walk-through of the top leads (10 min).
2. Walk-through of the suggested message angles (5 min).
3. Walk-through of the recommended cadence (5 min).
4. Client questions (5 min).
5. Next step proposal (5 min).

## Closing
- Confirm the feedback form will be sent within 24 hours.
- Confirm a check-in within 14 days.
- Name the recommended next engagement (often Managed Pilot or Revenue Desk).

## Materials left with the client
- Final report (PDF).
- Lead table (CSV).
- Outreach pack (PDF).
- Feedback form link.
- One-page summary of the recommended next engagement.

## Rule
The handoff call is a delivery, not a sales pitch. The next-step recommendation is one sentence, not five slides.
