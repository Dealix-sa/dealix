# Handoff Template — قالب التسليم

## Purpose

The 30-minute handover review at the end of a sprint, plus the written handoff note that follows. Same structure every time. Buyer leaves with clarity, not a sales pitch.

## Pre-read (24 hours before)

Send the buyer:

- Final report PDF.
- Lead table files (`.xlsx` + `.csv`).
- Next-step pack (PDF + editable).
- One-page summary of agenda for the call.

## The 30-minute agenda

| Minutes | Topic |
|---|---|
| 0–3 | Welcome; restate the sprint outcome. |
| 3–10 | Walk-through of top opportunities (3–5 highlights). |
| 10–18 | Walk-through of the next-step pack. |
| 18–24 | Methodology, limits, and what the sprint cannot tell you. |
| 24–28 | Buyer's questions. |
| 28–30 | Recommended next steps (no upsell pressure). |

## Written handoff note

Sent within 4 hours of the call. Template:

```
# Handoff — {{client_name}}, Sprint {{sprint_id}}
Date: <handover date>

## What you have
- Report PDF: <link>
- Lead table (.xlsx, .csv): <link>
- Next-step pack: <link>

## Top opportunities (recap)
1. {{company_1}} — {{signal_1}} — {{next_step_1}}
2. {{company_2}} — {{signal_2}} — {{next_step_2}}
…

## What we discussed on the call
- <one line per major point>

## Open questions
- <only if any; otherwise omit>

## Suggested next rung (if any)
- Data Pack (1,500 SAR): <only if a clear fit emerged>
- Managed Ops (2,999–4,999 SAR/mo): <only if pattern fits>

## Refund window
- The deliverable falls within scope. Refund policy applies for 7 calendar days from this note if scope-breach is later identified.

## Confidentiality and value framing
- This deliverable is yours; share internally as needed.
- Estimated value is not Verified value.

— Dealix
```

AR version sent in parallel.

## What never appears in handoff

- Pressure to retain or upsell.
- A claim about meetings, calls, or revenue.
- A logo or brand promise beyond what the offer documents.

## Post-handoff follow-up

- Day 7: short check-in note. "Anything blocking you from using the pack?"
- Day 14: feedback request per [docs/revenue/OFFER_EVOLUTION_SYSTEM.md](../../revenue/OFFER_EVOLUTION_SYSTEM.md).
- Day 30: retainer conversation, only if the buyer expressed continued need.

## AR — ملخّص

التسليم جلسة 30 دقيقة وبريد كتابي خلال 4 ساعات: ترحيب، جولة بأبرز الفرص، شرح حزمة الخطوة التالية، منهجية وحدود، أسئلة، خطوة تالية بلا ضغط بيع. متابعة بعد 7 و14 و30 يومًا. لا ضغط لتجديد، لا ادّعاء بمبيعات، لا شعارات وعد. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
