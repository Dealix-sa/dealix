# Client Intake — استلام بيانات العميل

## Purpose

The intake form a buyer completes before the sprint begins. Short, focused, and used as the contract for what's in scope. Anything not in the intake is not in the sprint.

## How to use

1. Sent immediately after paid invoice.
2. Buyer fills sections 1–6.
3. Delivery Lead reviews; flags any gap before Day 1 source plan.
4. If the decision-maker field is empty, a 20-min clarification call is required.

## What we do with this intake

- Used to build the source plan.
- Used to score and prioritize the lead table.
- Used to tailor the next-step pack.
- Stored in the private repo under the sprint folder.
- Confidential per [docs/offers/revenue_sprint/TERMS.md](../../offers/revenue_sprint/TERMS.md).

## Privacy

We do not collect any data that is not necessary for the sprint. We do not ask for customer lists, financials, or personal data. PDPL-aware practice per [docs/02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md](../../02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md).
