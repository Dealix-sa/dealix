# Client Intake — Revenue Sprint

The first 48 hours after payment.

## Required information
- Legal entity name and CR (if available).
- Primary contact name, role, phone, email.
- Sector and sub-sector.
- Existing tools (CRM, outreach, analytics).
- Target sector(s) for the sprint.
- Target geography.
- Definition of a "good lead" in the client's words.
- Exclusion list (competitors, suppressed accounts).
- Tone and language preference (Arabic / English / both).

## Required confirmations
- Scope (one paragraph), countersigned.
- Out-of-scope (one paragraph), countersigned.
- Timeline (start, milestones, end).
- Communication channel (single channel — WhatsApp or email).
- Single point of contact on the client side.

## Internal setup
- Create the client folder in `dealix-ops-private/clients/<client-slug>/`.
- Create the intake file from this template.
- Add the client to the Active Clients board with the sprint end date.
- Schedule the kickoff call within 48 hours.

## Rule
A sprint cannot move to Research without a completed intake. No exceptions.
