# Revenue Sprint Change Request Process

If the buyer asks for something not in [SCOPE.md](../../offers/revenue_sprint/SCOPE.md), the answer is never "sure, we'll throw it in". The answer is a Change Request.

## When to raise a CR

- The buyer asks for an additional artefact.
- The buyer asks to redirect the sprint to a different bottleneck.
- The buyer asks for additional sync time.
- The buyer asks to extend the timeline.

## How to raise a CR

- Delivery Lead writes a one-paragraph CR note in the sprint workspace.
- The CR names the requested change, the impact on scope, the impact on price, and the impact on timeline.
- The CR is shared with the buyer in writing within 24 hours.

## CR responses

- **Accept.** The buyer agrees in writing. The CR is logged in the AI Run Ledger. The kickoff note is updated.
- **Defer.** The work rolls to a follow-on engagement.
- **Decline.** Either side can decline. If the buyer declines a scope reduction, the original scope continues.

## What a CR cannot do

- A CR cannot move work into a Rung outside Revenue Sprint without a separate proposal.
- A CR cannot remove the refund posture.
- A CR cannot waive QA.
