- Name: [client name]
- Contact: [primary contact]

## 1. Situation
One paragraph. What the client asked for and the constraints we worked under.

## 2. Method
- Sources used (with passport reference).
- Scoring approach.
- Filters applied (sector, geography, deal-size band).

## 3. What We Found
Three to five findings. Each finding states:
- The finding.
- The evidence behind it.
- The implication for the client.

## 4. Recommended Next 14 Days
A dated action plan. Each action has:
- Owner.
- Due date.
- Expected signal of success.

## 5. Risks & Caveats
What this memo does NOT prove. Where the data is thin. What we would test next.

- Outreach pack (separate file).
- Source passport.
