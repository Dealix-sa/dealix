# Executive Memo / Report — Template

> One page. Customer-facing.

## Header

- Client: _name_
- Sprint period: yyyy-mm-dd to yyyy-mm-dd
- Sprint ID: _short ref_

## 1. Segment Summary (3–4 sentences)

What did we look at? What were the boundary conditions of the ICP we
researched, and what notable characteristics did the segment show?

## 2. Notable Patterns (3 bullets)

Concrete patterns we observed in the data:
- Pattern 1 with evidence reference
- Pattern 2 with evidence reference
- Pattern 3 with evidence reference

## 3. Top 3 Opportunities (specific)

For each:
- Account name
- Why it qualifies (signal + evidence)
- Suggested next step
- Confidence

## 4. Unknowns & Caveats

- What we could not verify and why.
- What would change our recommendation if known.

## 5. What We Suggest Next

A concrete recommendation:
- Continue with retainer? (with named scope and price)
- Run another Sprint in adjacent segment?
- Pause and gather more data?

## 6. Evidence Appendix

- Link to the lead table.
- Per-claim references for the bullets above.

## Quality Standards

- No overclaim, no guarantee.
- No filler ("we are excited", "we are delighted").
- Every number tied to a ledger entry.
- One page maximum.
