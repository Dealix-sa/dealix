# Revenue Sprint Retro Template

Every sprint ends with a 20-minute internal retro. The output is a learning note filed in `dealix-ops-private/learning/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.

## Retro structure (20 minutes)

| Minute | Item |
|---|---|
| 0-5 | Read the wrap-up brief out loud. |
| 5-10 | What worked. Be specific. |
| 10-15 | What did not work. Name root causes, not symptoms. |
| 15-20 | One change to make next sprint. Single owner. |

## Required fields in the learning note

- Sprint ID.
- Sector and buyer category (not the buyer's name unless internal-only).
- One sentence: what worked.
- One sentence: what did not.
- One sentence: the change we are making.
- The owner of the change.

## What goes into Company Memory

- Patterns that recur across three or more retros.
- See [docs/learning/COMPANY_MEMORY.md](../../learning/COMPANY_MEMORY.md).

## What does not go into the retro

- Buyer-personal blame.
- Speculation about future revenue.
- Anything that is not actionable.
