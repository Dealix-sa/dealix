# Revenue Sprint — Offer Definition

## Headline

**7-day productized Revenue Sprint** — SAR 499 — every external send founder-approved, evidence-backed.

## For Whom

Saudi mid-market companies (SAR 5M – 50M revenue) with existing sales motion that:
- Source leads manually
- Have an unmeasured / non-scored pipeline
- Want a productized starting point before committing to a retainer

## Scope (in)

- One Free Diagnostic call (30 min) — included
- 50 scored Tier-1-sector prospects, enriched
- 3 outreach message variants (Arabic + English)
- 3 objection responses (top objections per sector)
- 1 evidence pack — sources, methodology, sanitization

## Scope (out)

- Sending any messages on the client's behalf (we hand off; client sends)
- CRM integration
- Marketing creative / branding
- Long-term retainer commitment
- Any custom development

## Time

7 calendar days from kickoff (signed scope + payment received).

## Price

SAR 499 excl. VAT. Invoice on signed scope; delivery starts on payment receipt.

## Deliverables (each appears in the handoff doc)

- [ ] Scored prospect list (50 rows, CSV + readable PDF)
- [ ] Message variants (AR + EN, 3 each, with personalization slots)
- [ ] Objection responses (3 objections × 2 framings each)
- [ ] Evidence pack (sources, methodology, exclusions)
- [ ] Handoff doc (`HANDOFF_TEMPLATE.md` filled in)

## Trust Posture

- We never send on the client's behalf during a Sprint
- Every artifact passes `claim_guard.py`
- Source list is fully cited; nothing scraped
- Client data we collect lives in the private client workspace only

## Success Criteria

The Sprint is a success if:
- All 5 deliverables shipped within 7 days
- QA pass on every artifact
- Client feedback ≥ 8/10
- At least one outbound conversation started by client within 14 days post-delivery

## What The Sprint Does NOT Promise

- A specific number of replies
- A specific number of meetings booked
- ROI / revenue figures
- Lead-to-customer conversion guarantees

## Upsell Path

After Sprint:
- Data Pack (1,500 SAR) — deeper sector benchmark + 200 prospects
- Managed Ops (2,999/mo) — recurring delivery of leads + drafts + reports

Upsell offered at end of Sprint handoff call (no high-pressure).

## Cancellation

- Before kickoff: full refund minus any platform fees
- After kickoff: no refund (work has started)
- If we miss the 7-day deadline by > 48 hours without notice: pro-rated refund per `BILLING_POLICY.md`

## Why Sprint Exists

The Sprint is the **atomic unit** of Dealix delivery:
- Low enough cost to be a no-brainer for a qualified buyer
- Concrete enough that the buyer feels what we deliver
- Short enough that we ship and learn fast
- High enough margin (founder-led) to be sustainable

Every other rung exists because Sprint succeeded.
