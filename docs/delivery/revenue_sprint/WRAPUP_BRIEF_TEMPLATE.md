# Revenue Sprint Wrap-up Brief Template

A bilingual, two-page document. Sent by day 14. Calibrated. Honest about both wins and gaps.

## Page 1 — English

### What was done
One paragraph naming the bottleneck and the artefact.

### What worked
Bullet list. Each bullet maps to evidence in the Proof Pack snippet.

### What did not work
Bullet list. Honest. No spin. If something failed, name it.

### What to do next
Three bullets. Concrete. Buyer-owned.

### Numbers
Every figure is tagged: [Estimated], [Observed], or [Verified]. Untagged figures are not allowed.

## Page 2 — Arabic

A faithful Arabic mirror of page 1. Not a marketing translation.

## Required attachments

- Proof Pack snippet.
- AI Run Ledger ID(s) for the sprint.
- Refund posture reminder from [REFUND_POLICY.md](../../offers/revenue_sprint/REFUND_POLICY.md).

## QA before sending

The wrap-up brief is blocked from sending until [QA_CHECKLIST.md](./QA_CHECKLIST.md) is signed.

## Disallowed

- Any banned phrase from [docs/trust/SAFE_LANGUAGE_LIBRARY.md](../../trust/SAFE_LANGUAGE_LIBRARY.md).
- Any untagged number.
- Any reference to work the buyer did not commission.
