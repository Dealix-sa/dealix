# Outreach Pack Template — Revenue Sprint

What goes inside the outreach pack delivered to the client.

## Contents
1. **Cover page** — client name, sprint window, prepared by Dealix.
2. **Sector frame** — one page on the slice of the market chosen.
3. **Lead table** — A/B/C tiers with all required columns.
4. **Message angles** — one recommended angle per tier.
5. **Sample messages** — three sample messages, ready to send, each tied to a named lead.
6. **Suggested cadence** — based on `docs/acquisition/OUTREACH_CADENCE.md`.
7. **What to avoid** — sector-specific phrasing or claims to skip.
8. **Suppression note** — short note on excluded leads and why.

## Format
- One PDF or one shared doc.
- Arabic and English variants if the intake requested both.
- All messages pass `MESSAGE_QUALITY_STANDARD.md`.

## Rule
The outreach pack is a thing the client can use tomorrow without rework. If it needs interpretation, it is not ready.
