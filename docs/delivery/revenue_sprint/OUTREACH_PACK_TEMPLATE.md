# Outreach Pack Template — قالب حزمة الخطوة التالية

## Purpose

The next-step pack the buyer receives alongside the lead table. Tailored to their sector and ICP. Buyer's team sends — Dealix never sends on their behalf.

## What's in the pack

1. DM openers (LinkedIn or buyer's preferred B2B channel) — 3 variants.
2. Email openers — 3 variants.
3. Call openers — 2 variants.
4. Qualifying questions — 5 to 8.
5. Common objections + safe responses — 3 to 5.
6. Arabic and English versions side by side.

## Templates

### DM opener — Variant A (curiosity + signal)

```
Hi {{first_name_or_role}} — saw {{public_signal}}. We help {{sector}} operators in {{geography}} surface qualified opportunities, with evidence. Open to a 20-minute conversation about {{specific_topic}}?
```

### DM opener — Variant B (specific reference)

```
{{first_name_or_role}} — quick note: {{public_signal}} caught our attention. If you are mapping {{specific_topic}}, we have a focused way to surface evidenced opportunities for your review. Worth a brief call?
```

### Email opener — Variant A

```
Subject — {{specific_topic}}, {{geography}}

{{first_name_or_role}},

We work with {{sector}} operators in {{geography}} to surface qualified opportunities, backed by evidence. {{public_signal}} suggests {{specific_topic}} may be relevant for you this quarter.

If a 20-minute call to compare notes is useful, here is my calendar: {{link}}.

Either way, happy to share a one-page view of how this works.

{{sender_name}}
```

### Call opener

```
Thanks for the time. To use this well — I want to understand three things: how you are surfacing opportunities today, what signals you are watching, and what would make a focused 5–7 day input valuable for you. Sound okay?
```

### Qualifying questions (sample)

1. Who in your team owns surfacing new B2B opportunities today?
2. How do you decide which prospects to prioritize?
3. What signals do you trust (tenders, expansion news, public registries)?
4. What's the timeline by which a new opportunity becomes a closed deal?
5. What's tried and didn't work for you in the last year?

### Objection bank (sample)

| Objection | Safe response |
|---|---|
| "We already have a sales team." | "We don't replace sales — we surface a focused input your team can act on." |
| "Can you guarantee meetings?" | "No. Estimated value is not Verified value. We surface evidenced opportunities; conversion belongs to your outreach." |
| "Is this PDPL-compliant?" | "We run a PDPL-aware practice and don't include personal contact data without permission. We don't issue compliance attestations." |

## Tailoring rules

- Replace every placeholder before send. If a placeholder is empty, the pack is incomplete.
- One signal per opener — never combine multiple signals.
- Never include a guarantee, never name a third party without consent.
- Arabic version is parallel, not translated word-for-word.

## What the pack is not

- Not a sequence to be automated.
- Not pre-sent by Dealix.
- Not bulk-broadcasting material.
