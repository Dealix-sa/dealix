# Scoring Rules

> 25 points total. Each dimension explicit.

## Dimensions

### 1. ICP Fit (0–5)
- 5: Exact match (sector, size, geo).
- 4: Strong match; one dimension slightly off.
- 3: Two dimensions slightly off.
- 2: One dimension materially off.
- 0–1: Outside ICP.

### 2. Pain (0–5)
- 5: Visible recent pain trigger (e.g. funded round + hiring sales,
     visible product launch into a new segment, leadership change).
- 4: Documented sustained pain (e.g. flat hiring pattern in sales
     when company has revenue growth).
- 3: Inferred pain (e.g. small sales team relative to ARR).
- 1–2: Pain plausible but unverifiable.
- 0: No identifiable pain.

### 3. Authority (0–5)
- 5: Decision-maker is reachable directly.
- 4: Champion identifiable + can reach decision-maker.
- 3: Decision-maker identifiable; reach unknown.
- 1–2: Decision-maker not identifiable.
- 0: Org chart unclear.

### 4. Budget (0–5)
- 5: Visible recent funding or revenue making our Sprint price
     trivial.
- 4: Stable revenue indicating budget exists.
- 3: Reasonable budget assumed for the size.
- 1–2: Budget unclear.
- 0: Likely no budget.

### 5. Timing (0–5)
- 5: Trigger signal < 30 days old.
- 4: Trigger signal < 90 days old.
- 3: Plausibly currently relevant.
- 1–2: Stale signal.
- 0: No timing signal.

## Priority Bands

| Total | Priority |
|-------|----------|
| 20–25 | A |
| 14–19 | B |
| 8–13 | C |
| < 8 | Disqualified |

## Scoring Discipline

- Score is from evidence in the lead row. Not from vibes.
- "I think they would be a good fit" is not a score.
- If an evidence link cannot be found for a high score, the score is
  reduced.

## Sample size discipline

- Per Sprint, we expect approximately:
  - A: 5–10
  - B: 15–25
  - C: 10–15
  - Disqualified: 5–15
- If A-priority is < 3, the founder reviews the ICP definition before
  proceeding with outreach.
