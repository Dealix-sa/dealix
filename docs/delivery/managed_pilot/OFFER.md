# Managed Ops Pilot — Offer Definition

## Headline

**Managed Ops Pilot** — SAR 2,999 / month — 3-month pilot, monthly cancellable, productized revenue-ops on retainer with founder approvals.

## For Whom

Customers who completed a Sprint or Data Pack and want a recurring system rather than a one-off.

## Scope (per month, in)

- 100 new scored prospects
- Weekly outreach drafts (Arabic + English) — founder-approved before send
- Bi-weekly delivery report (KPIs, trends, recommendations)
- One sector-specific sample artifact (refresh)
- Monthly trust review + claim-guard audit
- One 30-min monthly working session with founder

## Scope (out)

- Sending on the client's behalf (we never have client send credentials)
- Marketing creative
- Hiring / managing the client's sales team
- CRM implementation (we work *with* the existing tool)
- Anything not productized at the next rung (refer to Custom AI for those)

## Time

Monthly cycle. 30-day notice to cancel.

## Price

- SAR 2,999/mo (entry, months 1–3)
- SAR 4,999/mo (expanded, months 3+) — bigger prospect volume, sector benchmark, advisor sync

## Deliverables Per Month

- [ ] 100 scored prospects (CSV + PDF)
- [ ] 4 weekly outreach-draft batches
- [ ] 2 bi-weekly reports
- [ ] 1 sample artifact refresh
- [ ] 1 trust review + audit log
- [ ] 1 founder working session (30 min)

## Trust Posture

- Approval matrix applied to every external action
- Suppression list honored
- Monthly trust audit logged in `trust/approval_log.csv` (private)
- Quarterly governance summary shared with client

## Cancellation

- Month-to-month, 30-day notice
- First-month refund window: 7 days from kickoff
- After month 1: no refunds for partial months

## Upsell Path

Custom AI (5K–25K/mo) — only after ≥ 3 months proven ROI on Managed Ops.

## What Managed Ops Does NOT Promise

- Specific revenue figures
- A team replacement
- Automation without approval gates
- Multi-country support
- White-labeling

## Pilot Done Criteria

The 3-month pilot is "successful" if:
- All 3 months delivered on time
- QA pass every month
- Client renews into month 4 OR explicitly closes with positive feedback
- Trust scorecard ≥ 90/100
- At least one verifiable outcome (replies, meetings, won deal) attributable to the system
