# Revenue Sprint — Sample Pack Template

## Purpose
A short, low-pressure artifact sent to a positive replier so they can decide
whether a paid Revenue Sprint is worth their time. The sample shows the
shape of the work — it is not the work itself.

## Sample Structure
1. **Prospect context** — what we read about them and why we picked them.
2. **Target customer hypothesis** — who their next 5 customers might look
   like, in one paragraph.
3. **5 sample opportunity targets** — table with company, why-fit, suggested
   angle, public evidence URL.
4. **7-day test plan** — what a paid Revenue Sprint would do in week 1.
5. **Trust note** — see below; mandatory verbatim.
6. **Next step** — one CTA, one calendar link.

## Trust Note (verbatim, mandatory)
> This sample is opportunity intelligence and outreach support. It does not
> promise replies, meetings, sales, or revenue. Outcomes depend on your
> team's follow-through and your product's fit with the targets shown.

## Rules
- Public data only. Source URL beside every claim.
- No CRM data, no scraped personal data.
- No revenue or reply claim.
- One CTA. One calendar link.
- Sample fits on one screen when scrolled.

## When to Send
Only after a positive reply on a Batch outreach and after the founder marks
the row `sample_approved` in `acquisition/approvals/<batch>.md`.
