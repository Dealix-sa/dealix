# Kickoff Protocol

## Purpose
A 30-minute first call that aligns the client and Dealix on what success looks like.

## Pre-call checklist
- Intake document drafted in `clients/<slug>/intake.md`.
- Proposal accepted and stored in `clients/<slug>/proposal.md`.
- Payment status confirmed (cash collected OR PO logged).

## Call agenda (30 min)
1. (5 min) Introduce the engagement and re-confirm outcomes.
2. (10 min) Walk through scope and what is out of scope.
3. (5 min) Confirm decision maker and approval cadence.
4. (5 min) Agree on weekly check-in slot.
5. (5 min) Define the first deliverable.

## Outputs
- Updated `clients/<slug>/intake.md` with any new constraints.
- A first deliverable date pencilled into the founder's calendar.
- A summary email within 24 hours.

## Anti-patterns
- Skipping the kickoff to "save time".
- Starting work before scope is confirmed in writing.
- Adding scope mid-call without explicit acknowledgement.
