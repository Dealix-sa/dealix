## Rules before you send | قواعد قبل الإرسال

- One page. Three priorities. One recommended next step. Nothing more.
- **No ROI promises. No guaranteed outcomes.** All language is opportunity- or
  pattern-framed. `governance_os.claim_safety` must pass on the final text.
- Bilingual: Arabic primary, English mirror.
- No platform access, no advanced analytics — those are tier 1+.
- Delivery mode: `approved_manual` — API draft + 30-minute founder review,
  then the founder sends. Dealix sends nothing.
- Inputs: the 6 intake answers via `/diagnostic.html` (~15 minutes of customer
  time). No scraping, no enrichment of the customer beyond what they provided.
