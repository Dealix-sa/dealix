# Client Value Reporting

> **Status:** Weekly report to the client. Monthly to the leadership.
> **Schema:** `schemas/launch/founder_daily_command.schema.json` (for the founder's daily view; the client version is a derivative).

## The weekly client report (1 page)

Sent every Monday at 8am. Structure:

### Header

- Client name.
- Week number (e.g. "Week 7 of the engagement").
- The proof metric (from the proposal).
- The actual metric (this week).

### 5 sections (1 short paragraph each)

1. **What we did this week.** (2–3 specific actions.)
2. **What worked.** (1 specific win with a number.)
3. **What didn't.** (1 specific gap with a number.)
4. **What we're tuning.** (1 specific change for next week.)
5. **The 5 things you should do this week.** (Pulled from the daily digest.)

### Footer

- Next review call date.
- Founder signature.
- "founder-approved" stamp.

## The monthly leadership report (3 pages)

Sent the first Monday of each month. Audience: the client's CEO/COO/VP.

### Page 1 — The numbers

| Metric | Last month | This month | Delta |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Lost follow-ups detected | 42 | 18 | -57% |
| Average reply time | 18h | 4h | -78% |
| Founder decision time | 2h/day | 15min/day | -87% |
| Deals in pipeline | 14 | 22 | +57% |
| Deals closed | 3 | 5 | +67% |

(All numbers must be real and verifiable. Made-up numbers are a banned claim.)

### Page 2 — The narrative

- What we shipped this month.
- What we tuned.
- What the team learned.
- What the client should do next month.

### Page 3 — The proof pack

- 1 anonymized before/after.
- 1 anonymized case study.
- 1 reference client (with permission).
- The proof_id of each.

## The "red" week (when numbers are bad)

If a week has bad numbers (the proof metric is missed), the report says so. Plainly. No spin.

Structure:

1. **What we expected.** The proof metric.
2. **What we got.** The actual.
3. **Why we got it.** The 1–2 specific reasons.
4. **What we're changing.** The 1–2 specific fixes.
5. **What you can do.** The 1–2 specific client actions.

The red week is not a failure. It is the system working. The point of the report is to surface the truth, not to hide it.

## The founder's daily view (not the client's)

The founder gets a daily view (see `04_DAILY_FOUNDER_COMMAND.md`). The client's view is a derivative, simplified, anonymized, and approved.

## The trust preflight

The report goes through the preflight. Banned:

- "Guaranteed improvement" without a real baseline.
- "100% satisfaction."
- "AI solved everything."
- Client names without permission.

## When to send

- Weekly report: every Monday 8am.
- Monthly report: first Monday of the month.
- Red week: same Monday + a founder call on Tuesday.

## When to update

- After every 10 weekly reports: which sections did the client actually read?
- After every 5 monthly reports: which metrics moved? Which didn't?
- After every renewal conversation: what changed in the format?
