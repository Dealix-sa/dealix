# Revenue Desk — Custom AI Tier

## Headline

**Revenue Desk (Custom AI)** — SAR 5,000 – 25,000 / month — bespoke integration, scoped per SOW, only for proven Managed Ops customers.

## For Whom

Customers who:
- Have been on Managed Ops for ≥ 3 months
- Demonstrated measurable ROI (replies, meetings, won deals attributable)
- Want sector-specific or company-specific customization beyond productized offers
- Are ready to commit to a 6-month minimum engagement

## Scope (per SOW; varies)

Possible scope items (priced per SOW):
- Custom integration with client's CRM / ERP / WhatsApp Business
- Sector-specific scoring model trained on client's history
- Dedicated approval matrix (client policies → code)
- Custom evidence pack format
- Quarterly strategic review with founder + advisor
- On-site sprint days (Riyadh / Jeddah / Dammam)

## Scope (out always)

- Anything that violates Dealix's Veto Rules (no client data leak, no A4 automation, no compliance claims)
- White-labeling Dealix as the client's brand
- Replacing the client's sales team
- Anything outside Saudi this quarter
- Anything that requires breaking the productized model for our other customers

## Time

- Setup: 30–60 days
- Ongoing: monthly cycle similar to Managed Ops, with quarterly review
- Minimum engagement: 6 months

## Price

- Setup fee: 50% of first 6 months' value, charged up front
- Monthly: SAR 5K – 25K depending on scope
- Annual prepay option: 10% discount if all paid up front

## Approval To Enter This Tier

A customer can move to Revenue Desk only if:
- Founder approves
- Advisor reviews (external check)
- Customer has 3+ months Managed Ops with positive Trust scorecard
- SOW signed by client + Dealix
- Custom approval matrix documented in code

## Trust Posture (elevated)

- Dedicated DPA (Data Processing Agreement)
- Documented data flows + retention
- Audit trail accessible to client on request
- Quarterly trust audit shared with client
- Incident response time: < 4 hours (vs. < 24 for Managed Ops)

## Cancellation

- Pre-setup: full refund minus advisor + legal costs
- During setup: pro-rated refund minus delivered hours
- During ongoing: 60-day notice (vs. 30 for Managed Ops)
- Data return + deletion: per DPA terms, within 30 days of termination

## Why This Tier Exists

Revenue Desk lets us serve customers who outgrow Managed Ops without breaking productization for the rest.

## Why It's Capped

Custom work has lower margin and higher trust risk. Cap: 2 active Revenue Desk customers at a time this year.

## Anti-Patterns (we refuse)

- Custom tier as the "default sale" — no, it's a graduation
- Custom work without SOW — no
- Custom work with no advisor review — no
- Custom work for clients with prior Trust incidents — no
- Custom work as a way to dodge the productized price ladder — no

## Done Definition (per month)

Same as Managed Ops + custom SOW deliverables shipped + quarterly review attended.
