# Sample Operations System
## Purpose
Create fast, trust-safe sample packs that convert interested prospects into Revenue Sprint proposals.
## Owner
Sami / Delivery owner.
## Sample Output
- 5 sample opportunities.
- why relevant.
- priority.
- evidence.
- suggested message.
- trust note.
## Sample Quality Bar
- lead matches ICP.
- relevance is clear.
- evidence exists.
- no overclaim.
- message is usable.
- next step is clear.
## Sample Flow
1. Choose best prospect.
2. Confirm sector and ICP assumption.
3. Find 5 relevant opportunities.
4. Add evidence.
5. Add suggested outreach angle.
6. Add trust note.
7. Send sample.
8. Log action.
## Rules
- No sample without evidence.
- No guaranteed result.
- No confidential data.
- No client name used publicly.
- No sample sent to bad-fit lead.
## Metrics
- samples prepared.
- sample-to-reply rate.
- sample-to-proposal rate.
- time per sample.
- sample quality score.
