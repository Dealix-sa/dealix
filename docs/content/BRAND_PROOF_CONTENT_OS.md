# Brand, Proof & Content Operating System
## Purpose
Turn Dealix operating evidence, delivery learning, client feedback, and founder insights into trust-building content and approved proof.
## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.
## Review Cadence
Weekly for content, monthly for proof and positioning.
## Core Principle
Content must serve trust, revenue, proof, learning, or founder authority.
## Content Flow
Delivery
→ Feedback
→ Proof Approval
→ Anonymized Insight
→ Founder Post
→ Sector Report
→ Case Study
→ Inbound Lead
→ Pipeline
## Rules
- No fake proof.
- No client name without approval.
- No guaranteed revenue claim.
- No guaranteed sales claim.
- No sensitive data.
- No inflated results.
- Every public claim needs evidence.
- AI can draft, Sami approves.
## Content Pillars
1. Saudi B2B revenue systems.
2. Founder operating discipline.
3. Trust-first AI operations.
4. Revenue Sprint lessons.
5. Sector opportunity insights.
6. Client-approved proof.
7. Delivery and pipeline learning.
## Metrics
- posts published
- approved proof assets
- anonymized insights
- inbound leads
- content-to-conversation rate
- case studies approved
- trust incidents
- pipeline influenced
