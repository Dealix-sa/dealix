## Rules

1. No fake proof.
2. No client name without written consent.
3. No outcome guarantees (see `NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md`).
4. Every public claim has an evidence link.
5. Founder personal social media is treated as Dealix surface.

## Metrics

- Posts per week (target: 3–5 LinkedIn, 7–14 X)
- Engagement (saved, shared, replied) — not followers
- Reply DMs from ICP
- Case studies approved per quarter
- Public claims with evidence links: 100%
