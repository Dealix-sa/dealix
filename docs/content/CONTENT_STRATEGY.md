# Content Strategy

Dealix publishes to become the trusted source for Saudi B2B growth systems.

## Pillars
1. Saudi B2B growth — sector dynamics, deal motions, buyer behavior.
2. Revenue systems — pipelines, retention, recurring revenue design.
3. AI-assisted sales — what works, what does not, where the risk is.
4. Founder lessons — first-person, specific, honest.
5. Trust-first automation — why guarantees and over-claims lose.
6. Case studies — proof, anonymized or approved.
7. Market signals — short, useful, dated.

## Channels
- LinkedIn (primary).
- X / Twitter (secondary).
- Written sector reports (quarterly).
- Public case studies (as approved).

## Cadence
- 3–5 LinkedIn posts per week.
- 1 sector report per quarter.
- 1 case study every 4–6 weeks.

## Voice
See `FOUNDER_VOICE.md`. Plain, specific, no hype, occasional Arabic phrasing.

## Measurement
- Inbound DMs and qualified leads attributed to content.
- Engagement on long-form posts (not vanity metrics).
- Replies from named accounts in target sectors.

## Rule
Publish what is useful, not what is impressive. The reader's time is the audit.
