# Content Strategy — استراتيجية المحتوى

## Purpose
Define the pillars, audience, tone, and frequency of Dealix's external content. Content exists to earn trust with Saudi business buyers and decision makers, not to chase reach.

## Owner
Founder. Strategy reviewed quarterly.

## Inputs
- Buyer interviews and ICP definition from `docs/01_go_to_market/`.
- Sector signals from `docs/sector-reports/`.
- Productization candidates worth surfacing.
- Trust artifacts from `docs/14_trust_os/`.

## Outputs
- This document.
- Pillar-tagged backlog of post ideas.
- Quarterly editorial calendar.

## Audience
- **Primary**: Saudi B2B founders and commercial leaders (revenue 5-50m SAR/year).
- **Secondary**: Investors, partners, ecosystem operators.
- **Not audience**: Mass consumer, hobbyist AI crowd, growth-hack tourists.

## Pillars (4)
1. **Evidence over hype** — case-safe patterns from delivery; how Dealix runs a sprint; what didn't work.
2. **Operating discipline** — A3, kill criteria, productization ladder, founder operating model.
3. **Sector intelligence** — quarterly sector reports + weekly observations, methodology disclosed.
4. **Trust and governance** — PDPL, AI provenance, refusal cases, escalations — what we won't do.

Each pillar gets ~25% of output. No fifth pillar without quarterly review.

## Tone
- Executive Saudi business: clear, decisive, evidence-forward.
- Arabic-first on founder voice; English mirrors in parallel.
- No marketing fluff. No "supercharge", "transform", "AI-powered".
- No emojis. No model name.

## Frequency
- LinkedIn: 3 posts per week (Sun, Tue, Thu).
- X: 5 posts per week (one per pillar + 1 thread weekly).
- Sector report: quarterly (≥ 1000 words, methodology disclosed).
- Case study: when written client approval is in hand; no schedule.

## Rules
1. No client name without written approval.
2. No outbound automation (cold WhatsApp, LinkedIn bots, scraping) ever, in copy or in practice.
3. No promised conversion rates, ROI, or sales numbers; use "estimated" with the disclosure.
4. Bilingual parallel structure on owned-page content.
5. The disclosure "Estimated value is not Verified value / القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة" appears on every customer-facing piece.

## Metrics
- Posts per pillar per quarter (balanced).
- Qualified inbound replies (count and source).
- Proof-attached rate.
- Saves vs likes ratio (saves matter; likes do not).

## Cadence
- Weekly publish plan.
- Quarterly strategy review.

## Evidence
- `evidence/content/strategy/<YYYY-Qn>.md`.

## Verifier
Founder.

## Runtime Command
`make content-strategy-review` — prints last quarter's pillar balance and qualified-reply count.
