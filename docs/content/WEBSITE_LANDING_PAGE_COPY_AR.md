## The 5 things to never put on a landing page

1. **A final price.** Ever.
2. **"Best tool."** No superlatives.
3. **"AI will replace X."** No fear-mongering.
4. **Testimonials without permission.** Empty for now.
5. **A "Sign up free" CTA** for a paid audit. Free is not the offer.

## The visual rules

- One color: dealix green (or whatever the brand is).
- One typeface.
- One hero image (founder photo, not stock).
- No animations except subtle hover.
- Mobile-first.

## The SEO rules

- Title: "Dealix — نظام تشغيل الإيرادات للشركات السعودية".
- Meta: "اكتشف الفرص الضايعة في الواتساب والمتابعة. audit 5 أيام، founder-approved pricing."
- H1: headline.
- H2: section headers above.
- Structured data: Organization + Product (the audit).
- Arabic + English hreflang.

## The conversion rules

- 1 CTA above the fold.
- 1 CTA at the end.
- 1 CTA in the trust section ("اعرف كيف").
- No "click here." Use "ابدأ بـ audit" or "تكلم مع founder."

## The trust preflight check

The landing page copy goes through the preflight. Banned phrases (same as outreach).
