# Founder Voice System
## Purpose
Define Sami's public voice as founder of Dealix.
## Voice Attributes
- direct
- practical
- strategic
- Saudi-aware
- operator-minded
- no fake hype
- evidence-first
## Content Style
Use:
- clear lessons
- operating systems
- real market observations
- frameworks
- examples
- honest constraints
Avoid:
- fake certainty
- exaggerated claims
- pretending traction that does not exist
- vague motivational posts
- client names without approval
- "guaranteed" language
## Founder Content Formula
Observation → Lesson → System → Action
Example:
Observation:
Many B2B companies do not have a lead problem. They have a prioritization problem.
Lesson:
A bad lead list creates noise. A scored opportunity system creates action.
System:
Dealix Revenue Sprint ranks opportunities by fit, evidence, and next action.
Action:
Start with 25 qualified leads, not 500 random contacts.
## Rule
Founder content should build trust before demand.
