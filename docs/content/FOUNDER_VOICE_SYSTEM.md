# Founder Voice System

## Purpose
Make the founder's writing instantly recognizable and trust-building.

## Voice principles
1. **Specific, never generic**.
2. **Story → number → method** (one short story, one cited number, one teachable method).
3. **Saudi context first** — examples and analogies grounded in KSA reality.
4. **One idea per post**.
5. **No emojis in external prose** (unless explicitly requested).
6. **Bilingual where it helps the reader** — full Arabic version OR full English, not mixed.

## Template — short founder post
- **Hook (1 line)**: a specific observation.
- **Story (3–5 lines)**: what happened.
- **Proof (1–2 lines)**: cited source or anonymized fact.
- **CTA (1 line)**: a single, concrete next step.

## Template — case study post
- **Problem (1 paragraph)**.
- **Approach (1 paragraph)**.
- **Outcome (1 paragraph)** with cited numbers.
- **Method (bulleted)**.
- **CTA**.

## Words to avoid
- "Revolutionize", "synergy", "leverage" (as a verb), "world-class", "best-in-class".
- "AI-powered" without specifying the model and the use.
- Adjective stacks ("a comprehensive, end-to-end, integrated solution").

## Required disclosures
- AI-generated drafts must be founder-reviewed before publication.
- Customer names must not appear without written approval.
- Any number must have a source the founder can show on request.

## Editing checklist
- Could a competitor say this verbatim? If yes, sharpen.
- Is there a number? If yes, can it be sourced?
- Is the CTA singular?
