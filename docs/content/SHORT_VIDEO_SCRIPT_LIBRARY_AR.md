## The filming rules

1. **Phone camera is fine.** Quality matters less than consistency.
2. **Good lighting.** Window light, not ceiling light.
3. **No background music.** Voice is the product.
4. **Subtitles in Arabic.** Most LinkedIn views are muted.
5. **One CTA per video.** Not three.
6. **No "subscribe" or "like" begging.** Trust over engagement-bait.

## When to update

- After 10 videos: if avg views < 200, the hook is weak. Rewrite.
- After 20 videos: if DM rate < 1%, the CTA is weak. Rewrite.
- After 30 videos: drop the formats that didn't work. Double down on the ones that did.
