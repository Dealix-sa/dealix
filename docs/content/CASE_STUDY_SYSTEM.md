# Case Study System

The system that turns every delivered engagement into public proof.

## Sources
- Sprint handoffs via `docs/delivery/revenue_sprint/CASE_STUDY_CAPTURE.md`.
- Pilot debriefs.
- Retainer milestones.

## Format
- One page, public-friendly.
- Sector and size, not the client name unless approved.
- Before / during / after structure.
- One quantitative or qualitative result.
- One client quote (with permission).

## Approval
- Client approval in writing before publishing.
- Internal Claim Guard pass.

## Distribution
- Added to `docs/content/PROOF_LIBRARY.md`.
- Republished as LinkedIn post and threaded on X.
- Used in proposals as a relevant proof point.

## Rule
A delivered engagement without a captured case study is a missed compounding round.
