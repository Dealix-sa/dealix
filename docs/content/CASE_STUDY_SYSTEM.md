# Case Study System
## Purpose
Turn successful Dealix delivery into approved, trust-safe case studies.
## Required Before Case Study
- delivery completed
- feedback received
- client approval
- claims reviewed
- sensitive data redacted
- exact wording approved
## Case Study Structure
1. Client context.
2. Problem.
3. Dealix approach.
4. Delivery.
5. What changed.
6. Client feedback.
7. Next step.
8. Trust note.
## Case Study Levels
- anonymized case study
- named case study
- full public story
## Rules
- No case study without proof approval.
- No inflated results.
- No confidential details.
- No revenue guarantee language.
- No claim without evidence.
## Evidence
- delivery report
- feedback
- proof approval
- claim review log
