# Sector Report System

Quarterly written reports that establish Dealix as the trusted source in a sector.

## Cadence
- One sector per quarter, set in the Monthly Strategy Review.

## Structure
1. Executive summary (one page).
2. Market frame — size, structure, key players.
3. Buying signals — what we are seeing in outreach.
4. Common pain patterns — three to five.
5. What is working — examples without naming clients.
6. What is not working.
7. Recommended actions for buyers.
8. Method and sources.

## Process
- Research over weeks 1–4.
- Writing in weeks 5–6.
- Internal Claim Guard pass in week 7.
- Publish in week 8.

## Distribution
- Public PDF on a dedicated landing page.
- LinkedIn post and X thread referencing the report.
- Direct sends to top-tier prospects in the sector.

## Rule
A sector report establishes authority only if it is honest about what is not working. Avoid the temptation to sound like a brochure.
