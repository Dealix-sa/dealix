# Sector Report System

## Purpose
Publish quarterly sector reports that demonstrate domain depth and feed inbound pipeline.

## Per-report cadence
- Pick one Primary ICP sector per quarter.
- 6–10 pages.
- Mix of: market scan, our observations, anonymized findings, suggested operating moves.

## Inputs
- Pipeline data for the sector (anonymized aggregates only).
- Public market data, properly cited.
- Customer interviews (with consent), anonymized.

## Structure
1. Executive summary (1 page).
2. Market snapshot (2 pages).
3. Our observations from the field (1–2 pages).
4. Three operating moves a Saudi mid-market player should consider (1 page).
5. Method appendix (1 page).

## Distribution
- Public PDF on the Dealix surface.
- LinkedIn long-form excerpt.
- Direct distribution to ICP prospects in that sector.

## Forbidden
- Reusing third-party reports without permission and citation.
- Including any customer name without written approval.
- Predictions presented as certainties.

## Quality gate
- Every claim sourced.
- Founder review before publication.
- Trust workflow approval for any named comparison.

## Pipeline effect
- Add a `content_pipeline_influence.csv` row per relevant inbound after publication.
- Review which sector report drove the most engagement after each quarter.
