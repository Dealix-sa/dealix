# Sector Report System
## Purpose
Turn Dealix sector research into useful public or private intelligence assets.
## Sector Report Inputs
- lead sourcing notes
- sector performance
- buyer patterns
- objection patterns
- delivery observations
- public market signals
## Report Types
- internal sector memo
- anonymized sector insight
- public mini report
- client-specific sector opportunity report
## Rules
- Use public or approved data only.
- Do not reveal client confidential insights.
- Do not claim market-wide certainty from small samples.
- Label observations as observations.
- Keep public reports trust-safe.
## Example Reports
- Saudi ERP vendor opportunity signals.
- B2B agencies and pipeline bottlenecks.
- Cybersecurity buyer outreach angles.
- Logistics B2B lead prioritization patterns.
