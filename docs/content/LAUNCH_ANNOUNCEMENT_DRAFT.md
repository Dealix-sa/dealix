## English

### Title

**Dealix — Governed AI Operations for Saudi B2B**

### Body

Today we announce that Dealix is available to Saudi B2B founders.

Dealix is not an AI tool, and not a bulk-outreach platform. It is an **operating capability with auditable proof**: we turn your company's scattered data into a revenue decision you can act on this week — which accounts first, what to write them in Arabic and English, how to follow up — and each output ships with a governance decision and an audit trail you can defend.

**What we publicly refuse:** no scraping, no cold WhatsApp, no LinkedIn automation, no bulk outreach, and no sending on your behalf without your explicit approval. These are not preferences — they are enforced in code.

**How to start — with no commitment:**

- A **Free Diagnostic** within 24 hours, bilingual, with founder approval logged.
- A **Revenue Intelligence Sprint** at a fixed 499 SAR over 7 days: 10 ranked accounts, a bilingual draft pack, a governance decisions log, a 14-section Proof Pack, and at least one reusable capital asset.

We accept a limited number of customers per month — not artificial scarcity, but real capacity. Start with the diagnostic. No commitment after it.
