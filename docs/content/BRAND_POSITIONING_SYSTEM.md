# Brand Positioning System

## Purpose
Make Dealix legible to a buyer in 8 seconds without overclaim.

## Statement
Dealix is a Saudi-led revenue intelligence operator. We help mid-market KSA companies turn their data into deals — through hand-built sprints, sector data packs, and managed ops retainers.

## Pillars
1. **Saudi-led, KSA-first**: built for Saudi market norms.
2. **Data → deals**: the lens is revenue, not generic analytics.
3. **Founder-built**: each engagement is delivered by, or under, the founder.

## What we are not
- We are not a generic AI agency.
- We are not a SaaS platform (yet).
- We are not a lead-list reseller.

## Tone
- Clear. Specific. Concrete.
- Bilingual where the audience expects it (Arabic + English).
- Confident, not boastful.

## Visual identity
- Minimal. Black/white/dark green palette.
- Tabular and structured, not stock-photo.

## Use of "we"
- "We" refers to Dealix as an operating system, not to a team larger than reality.
- Disclose team size when asked.

## Anti-patterns
- "Revolutionary AI platform" language.
- "We work with Fortune 500 companies" if we haven't.
- Borrowing other companies' positioning ("we're the X for Y").
