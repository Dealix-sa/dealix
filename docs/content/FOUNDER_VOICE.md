# Founder Voice

> Specific, direct, accountable. Bilingual when relevant.

## Voice attributes

1. **Specific.** Names, numbers, examples. No "many companies".
2. **Direct.** Short sentences. No filler.
3. **Accountable.** First person. The founder's name on the work.
4. **Bilingual.** Arabic for Saudi context; English for global craft.
5. **Evidence-led.** Every assertion has a source.
6. **Calm.** No outrage. No cliffhanger headlines.

## Voice in Arabic

- Direct Modern Standard Arabic, not corporate stiff.
- Use plain phrasing; avoid English transliterations when an Arabic word
  exists.
- Sector-specific terminology used carefully and explained on first use.

## Voice in English

- Plain, founder-led.
- Sentences ≤ 25 words on average.
- Numbers where possible.

## What to avoid

- "Game-changer"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Disruptive"
- "10x"
- Hype emoji-bait
- "We are excited to announce" (just announce)
- "I'm humbled" (almost always false)
- Any phrase from `NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md`

## What to keep

- "Here is what we learned."
- "Here is what worked. Here is what did not."
- "Here is the evidence."
- "I was wrong about [thing]; the correction is [thing]."
- "I do not know yet."

## Examples

### Good

> "Five sprints in. Reply rate: 18%. Top hook: a named incident at the
> prospect's competitor. Worst hook: 'we noticed you are growing'. The
> difference is specificity."

### Bad

> "Excited to share that we are seeing amazing results with our cutting-edge
> AI-powered outreach platform that is changing the game for B2B founders
> in Saudi Arabia 🚀"

## Self-Audit

Weekly: pick one post you wrote, score it on the six voice attributes.
A post scoring < 4/6 on any attribute is a lesson, not a model.
