# LinkedIn System

The operating model for the primary content channel.

## Post types
- **Insight** — one observation, one example, one implication.
- **Proof** — short case study, anonymized.
- **Lesson** — first-person founder lesson with the specific cost.
- **Signal** — short market observation with a date and source.

## Cadence
- 3–5 posts per week.
- One long-form (~250–400 words) per week.
- One sector signal per week.

## Format
- Hook in the first line.
- One concrete detail in the second line.
- Body in short paragraphs.
- One question or CTA at the end.

## Engagement
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours.
- DM open to anyone who comments seriously.
- Track named accounts that engage repeatedly.

## Measurement
- Inbound DMs from ICP-fit accounts (primary metric).
- Saves and reposts (signal of depth).
- Vanity likes ignored.

## Rule
Every post passes Claim Guard. Every post adds one specific thing the reader can use.
