# LinkedIn System

## Purpose
Use LinkedIn as a proof-distribution channel for founder-led commercial momentum.

## Posting cadence
- 2 posts per week.
- 1 thoughtful comment on 5 ICP-relevant posts per week.

## Post types
1. **Method post** — describe a specific play we use.
2. **Anonymized case post** — what happened with a client (no names).
3. **Sector observation** — a fact or pattern from the Saudi market with a source.
4. **Behind-the-scenes** — a build / learning / tradeoff we made.

## Format
- Max 1,300 characters for body posts.
- One hook line, one space, one paragraph, one CTA.
- No emojis unless requested by audience norms.

## Engagement etiquette
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours.
- Don't argue in comments; move long debates to DM.
- Don't tag people without permission.

## Forbidden
- Buying engagement.
- Engagement-pod participation.
- Posting screenshots of private conversations.

## Metrics
- Each post is logged in `content/published_log.csv`.
- Pipeline influence in `content/content_pipeline_influence.csv`.

## Founder bandwidth
- 30 min/day max on LinkedIn.
- 1 hour/week for batch drafting.
