# LinkedIn System

> 3–5 posts a week. Each one earns its own publication.

## Weekly Cadence

| Day | Pillar | Format |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Mon | Saudi B2B revenue systems | Specific example + lesson |
| Tue | Trust-first AI operations | Short principle + evidence |
| Wed | Sprint lessons | Pattern across recent sprints (with consent) |
| Thu | (Optional) Founder building in public | What changed this week |
| Fri | (Optional) Case study or proof asset | Named, consented |

## Post Length

- 4–8 short paragraphs.
- 1 specific number.
- 1 actionable takeaway.
- 1 question, optional, that invites a *useful* reply (not bait).

## Hashtag Discipline

- ≤ 3 hashtags per post.
- Same 3 every time we want longitudinal data: `#DealixSaudiB2B #FounderLed #RevenueIntelligence`.
- We do not chase trending hashtags.

## Replies

- Founder personally replies to substantive comments within 24h.
- ICP-fit reply → log in `dealix-ops-private/content/post_log.csv` with
  an inbound flag; potentially route into pipeline.
- Hostile / low-quality replies: ignore or graceful one-liner. Never
  argue.

## Direct Outreach From LinkedIn

When a post produces an inbound DM:

1. Capture in pipeline at stage **Replied** (skip Contacted).
2. Score on ICP rubric.
3. Respond personally; do **not** auto-respond.

## Forbidden Patterns

- "Comment 'yes' to receive..."
- "Tag a founder who needs to see this."
- Reposting your own content rapid-fire.
- Posting screenshots of others' content without permission.

## Measurement

Weekly:
- Posts published.
- Top-engaged post.
- Inbound DMs from ICP.
- Replies that converted to a call within 7 days.

Followers count is **not** the metric. Inbound from ICP is.
