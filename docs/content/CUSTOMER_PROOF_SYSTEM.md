# Customer Proof System

## Purpose
Turn delivery outcomes into approved proof assets.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Inputs
- delivery report
- client feedback
- client approval
- measurable result
- testimonial permission

## Outputs
- private proof note
- approved public case study
- anonymized insight
- founder post
- sector report

## Proof Levels

### P0 — Internal Only
Used for learning, not public.

### P1 — Anonymous Public
No client name, no sensitive details.

### P2 — Approved Named Proof
Client approves name and details.

### P3 — Full Case Study
Client approves story, claims, and quotes.

## Rules
- No public client name without approval.
- No fake result.
- No guaranteed revenue claim.
- No sensitive data.
- Claims require evidence.

## Evidence
- client feedback
- approval log
- delivery report
- case study approval
