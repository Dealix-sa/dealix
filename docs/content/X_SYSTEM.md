# X (Twitter) System

> Secondary channel. English-language amplification of LinkedIn primary.
> Low priority this quarter. Cap: 1 hour/week.

## Purpose
- Reach the international Saudi-watching community
- Build optionality for future GCC/MENA expansion content
- Stay visible to the AI / RevOps operator audience

## Cadence
- Repost weekly LinkedIn post in English thread form
- 1 standalone insight per week (optional)
- 3 considered replies per week to operator accounts

## Format
- Threads ≤ 7 tweets
- Single insight tweets ≤ 250 chars
- No emojis unless they map to specific Saudi sector signal
- No hashtag spam (≤ 2 hashtags per post)

## What We Don't Post On X
- Saudi sector-specific content (LinkedIn is primary)
- Anything that would fail claim_guard
- Replies to political / culture-war threads
- Quote-tweet criticism of competitors
- Anything to chase virality

## Measurement
- Same as LinkedIn (impressions, engagement, inbound)
- Tracked alongside LinkedIn in `learning/message_performance.csv`

## Kill Switch
- If no inbound from X in 12 weeks → pause for a quarter
- Founder time on X capped at 1 hr/week regardless of metrics

## Discipline
- Same approval tier as LinkedIn (A3)
- Same claim_guard pass requirement
- Same banned-language rules

## What This Refuses
- X as primary channel (it isn't)
- Buying followers / engagement
- Auto-posting (every post is founder-considered)
- Becoming a personality account (this is Dealix-adjacent founder)
