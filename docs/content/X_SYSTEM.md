# X System — نظام X

## Purpose
X is a secondary channel. Used for tactical patterns, sector observations, and short responses to founder peers. Lower volume than LinkedIn, higher signal density.

## Cadence
- 2-3 posts per week.
- Threads (3-7 tweets) reserved for one substantial pattern per week.
- No automation. No bot-driven replies. No scheduled-then-forgotten threads.

## Thread structure (when used)
- Tweet 1: the claim, one sentence.
- Tweet 2: the context — sector, scale, situation.
- Tweets 3-5: the observation, with one number and one named artefact if available.
- Tweet 6: the principle or rule extracted.
- Tweet 7 (optional): the cross-link to a Dealix doc or case-safe summary.

No hooks like "I'm about to share something nobody talks about". State the claim.

## Single-post structure
- One observation. One number or one artefact. Done.
- 280 characters used as a discipline. Threads only when a single post genuinely cannot carry the idea.

## Rules
- AR + EN parallel when the audience is Saudi business. Otherwise primary language follows context.
- No automation: no auto-DMs, no auto-replies, no engagement pods.
- No retweet of client material without written A2 consent.
- No screenshots of private channels.
- No "ratio bait" or controversial-take-for-views.

## Approval flow
- Single posts: founder writes and publishes.
- Threads: drafted in a document first, voice-checked against [`FOUNDER_VOICE.md`](FOUNDER_VOICE.md), then published.
- Claims tied to numbers reference [`PROOF_LIBRARY.md`](PROOF_LIBRARY.md).

## Operations
- Backlog maintained in `docs/content/calendar/YYYY-MM.md` with channel tag.

## Evidence
- Published thread links archived per quarter.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: 2-3/week posts; one thread per week when material exists.

## القسم العربي

**الغرض:** X قناة ثانوية للأنماط التكتيكية والملاحظات القطاعية والتفاعل القصير مع نظراء.

**الإيقاع:** 2-3 منشورات أسبوعيًا. thread واحد أسبوعيًا عند توفر مادة. لا أتمتة.

**هيكل الـthread:** تغريدة 1 الادعاء بجملة. تغريدة 2 السياق (قطاع، حجم، موقف). 3-5 الملاحظة مع رقم واحد وقطعة مُسمّاة إن وُجدت. 6 المبدأ المستخرج. 7 (اختيارية) رابط داخلي.

**القواعد:** AR + EN متوازيتان للجمهور السعودي. لا أتمتة، لا DMs آلية، لا engagement pods. لا إعادة تغريد لمواد عميل بدون A2. لا لقطات قنوات خاصة. لا "ratio bait".

**تدفق الموافقة:** المنشور المنفرد المؤسس يكتب وينشر. الـthread يُصاغ أولًا في وثيقة ثم يُفحص صوتيًا.
