# X (Twitter) System

> Short, frequent, observation-led. X is for thinking out loud.

## Cadence

- 1–2 posts per day.
- Threads: 1–2 per week, only when a topic needs ≥ 3 connected points.

## Pillars

Same as `CONTENT_STRATEGY.md`. X is the shorter, faster surface.

## Format

- 1–3 sentences for most posts.
- Threads: keep each tweet self-contained.
- Numbers and names where allowed.
- No emojis as decoration. (Emojis in posts only if the user explicitly
  expects them in that medium; usually no.)

## Bilingual

- Saudi-context posts in Arabic when the audience is Saudi B2B operators.
- Craft / global posts in English.
- We do not mix the two in one post.

## Founder Discipline

- No quote-tweet dunking.
- No participating in pile-ons.
- No relitigating disagreements after 24 hours.
- Mute, do not block, unless harassment.

## Measurement

- Inbound DMs from ICP per month.
- Threads that produced a downstream conversation.
- Posts that get cited externally (a higher trust signal than likes).

## Audit

Monthly: was any post a near-miss on the overclaim policy? Was any
post a personal opinion at odds with the company's safe language?

If yes: log, learn, do not repeat.
