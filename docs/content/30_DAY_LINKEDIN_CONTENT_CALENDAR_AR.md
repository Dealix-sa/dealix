## The 5 rules for posting

1. **One post per day, max.** More is noise.
2. **One off-day per week.** Saturday is the default off-day.
3. **One long-form per week.** Carousel or newsletter.
4. **Two videos per week.** 30s or 60s.
5. **Pin the best post every Sunday.**

## The hooks (the 30 hooks)

The hooks above are draft. The founder writes the final hook. The rule: 6–10 words, no clickbait, specific.

## The video scripts

See `SHORT_VIDEO_SCRIPT_LIBRARY_AR.md`. The 5 videos above use the templates there.

## The re-share rule

On day 31, re-share the best 3 posts from the month. New framing. New image. Same insight.

## The metrics

- Day 1: post count = 1.
- Day 7: post count = 5, profile views tracked.
- Day 14: post count = 10, DM count tracked.
- Day 30: post count = 22 (4 weeks × 5 + 2 long + 5 video), DM-to-discovery rate measured.

If post count < 22 by day 30, the founder missed days. The content plan is fine; the execution needs work.

## When to update

- Every 30 days, re-pick the 5 best posts and 5 worst posts.
- Drop the worst pillar. Keep the best.
- Rewrite the next 30 days with the lessons.
