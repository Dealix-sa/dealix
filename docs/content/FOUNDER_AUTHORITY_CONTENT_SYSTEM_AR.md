> **Status:** 10 pillars. Pick 1 to start, not all 10.
> **Companion:** `30_DAY_LINKEDIN_CONTENT_CALENDAR_AR.md` + `SHORT_VIDEO_SCRIPT_LIBRARY_AR.md`.

## The 5 content formats

| Format | Length | When |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Short post (text) | 80–150 words | Daily on LinkedIn |
| Long post (carousel) | 8–10 slides | Weekly |
| Short video | 30–60 seconds | 2–3x per week |
| Long video | 3–5 minutes | Weekly |
| Newsletter | 500–800 words | Weekly (optional) |

## The 5 tone rules

1. **Saudi, not generic.** Mention real cities, real sectors, real behaviors.
2. **Peer-to-peer, not teacher-to-student.** "I learned this" not "You should".
3. **Specific, not generic.** "I lost 12 leads in one week" not "leads are important".
4. **Honest, not aspirational.** "This is what didn't work" not "Here are 10 tips".
5. **Actionable, not philosophical.** "Try this for 5 days" not "Think about this".

## The 5 forbidden patterns

1. **"10 tips for..."** — listicles are dead.
2. **"AI will replace..."** — overclaim + fear-mongering.
3. **"Unlock your potential."** — corporate fluff.
4. **"As an industry leader..."** — no one talks like this.
5. **"Thoughts?"** — invites nothing. End with a specific question.

## The pillar-pick sequence

Week 1: Pillar 1 (Lost opportunities) + Pillar 2 (WhatsApp chaos). 3 posts each.
Week 2: Pillar 1 + Pillar 3 (Follow-up). 2 posts each.
Week 3: Pillar 4 (CRM alone) + Pillar 5 (Dashboard alone). 2 posts each.
Week 4: Pillar 6 (Founder's daily decision) + Pillar 7 (AI with approval). 2 posts each.

After 30 days: re-pick. The pillar that drove the most engagement wins.

## The post structure (5 elements)

1. **Hook** (1 line, 6–10 words).
2. **Pain** (2–3 lines, specific).
3. **Insight** (2–3 lines, the "I learned this").
4. **CTA** (1 line, specific).
5. **Repurpose** (1 line, the next format).

## The trust preflight check

Every post goes through the trust preflight. Banned patterns:

- "Guaranteed ROI."
- "100% effective."
- "Best tool ever."
- "AI will replace X."
- "Transform your business."

Allowed patterns:

- "I learned this."
- "I tried X for Y days."
- "This is what didn't work."
- "Try this for 5 days."
- "Reply with 'yes' if you want the template."

## The engagement rules

- Reply to every comment within 24 hours.
- Never auto-DM the engagers.
- Pin the best-performing post each week.
- Re-share the best posts after 90 days (with new framing).

## The metrics

Weekly:

- Post count by pillar.
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / impressions).
- Profile views.
- DMs received (not sent).
- DMs that became discovery calls.

If engagement rate < 2%, the hook is weak. Rewrite.
If DM-to-discovery rate < 10%, the CTA is weak. Rewrite.

## When to update

- Every 30 days, re-pick the pillars.
- Every quarter, retire the formats that don't work.
- Every 6 months, rewrite the tone rules.
