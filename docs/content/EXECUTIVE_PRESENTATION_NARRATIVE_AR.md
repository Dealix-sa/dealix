## The 5 things to never do in a presentation

1. **Don't go over 15 slides.** This is 10. Don't add.
2. **Don't read the slides.** Use them as anchors.
3. **Don't promise specific numbers.** Use "proof metric" language.
4. **Don't put a price on any slide.** Founder quotes later.
5. **Don't end with "any questions?"** End with "الخطوة الجاية: revenue leak audit. تحب؟"
