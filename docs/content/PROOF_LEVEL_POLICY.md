# Proof Level Policy
## Purpose
Classify what Dealix can say publicly based on approval and evidence.
## P0 — Internal Only
Used for company learning.
Cannot publish.
Examples:
- internal delivery notes
- private client feedback
- raw pipeline data
## P1 — Anonymous Public Insight
Can publish if anonymized and redacted.
Examples:
- "A Saudi B2B company had unclear ICP."
- "In a recent delivery, we found that lead prioritization was the bottleneck."
## P2 — Named Approved Proof
Can publish client name only with approval.
Examples:
- approved quote
- approved outcome
- approved logo use
## P3 — Full Case Study
Can publish after explicit approval of:
- client name
- story
- claims
- metrics
- quote
- visuals
## Rules
- No public proof without level classification.
- No client name without approval.
- No metric claim without evidence.
- No screenshot without redaction.
- No quote without permission.
