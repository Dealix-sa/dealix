## The 5 proof-oriented safe claims

For number-loving audiences (only when the proof pack exists):

1. "In past cases, the recovery ranged from SAR X to SAR Y per month."
2. "Past cases reduced lost follow-ups by ≥ X%."
3. "Past cases reduced founder decision time by ≥ X%."
4. "The audit identifies an average of X specific leaks per engagement."
5. "Past case n = [N], anonymized, [industry], [time period]."

(Every one of these requires a `proof_id` reference. If the proof pack does not exist, the claim is not allowed.)

## The 5 tone rules for safe claims

1. **"We can show you"** beats **"we guarantee"**.
2. **"We will measure"** beats **"you will see"**.
3. **"In past cases"** beats **"for you"**.
4. **"It depends on"** beats **"always"**.
5. **"If we miss it, we stop"** beats **"trust us"**.

## The 5 things to never say (even in a safe claim)

1. "We are the best."
2. "We are the only."
3. "We replace X."
4. "We guarantee X."
5. "AI will transform your business."

## The 5 language rules

1. **Use "نحدد"** (we identify) more than "نضمن" (we guarantee).
2. **Use "نقيس"** (we measure) more than "نحقق" (we achieve).
3. **Use "في حالات سابقة"** (in past cases) more than "لك" (for you).
4. **Use "مبني على"** (built on) more than "مضمون" (guaranteed).
5. **Use "بدون التزام"** (without commitment) more than "مجاناً" (free).

## When to update

- Every new proof pack = new safe claim.
- Every violation = retire the unsafe phrasing.
- Every new offer = 5 new safe claims.
