# No Overclaim Policy

## Purpose
Protect Dealix and its clients from unsupported, misleading, or risky claims in sales, marketing, delivery, and product surfaces.

## Scope
Applies to every artifact produced by Dealix, including but not limited to:
- founder DMs.
- sample packs.
- proposals.
- delivery reports.
- outreach messages.
- landing pages.
- case studies.
- LinkedIn posts.
- internal agent outputs that may be surfaced externally.

## Banned Claims
The following phrases (and any close paraphrase) are banned in every public or client-facing artifact unless backed by signed evidence:

- guaranteed revenue
- guaranteed sales
- guaranteed meetings
- guaranteed replies
- 100% automated sales
- fully compliant
- regulatory certified
- no-risk
- risk-free
- always-on
- never fails

## Required Framing
Every outward claim must follow this framing:
- Dealix delivers opportunity intelligence and execution assets.
- Outcomes depend on the client's offer, market, and follow-through.
- No guaranteed commercial outcomes.

## Evidence Requirement
A claim is permitted only if all of these are true:
1. The evidence exists in the private operations area.
2. The client (where applicable) has given written approval.
3. The claim is bounded (e.g., "in one engagement with X sector").

## Review
- Every outgoing artifact is reviewed against this policy before sending.
- Banned phrases trigger an automatic FIX in QA.
- Repeated violations escalate to founder review.

## Related Risks
This policy directly mitigates LLM application risks classified by OWASP, including sensitive information disclosure and prompt-injection-driven misrepresentation when agents produce client-facing text.
