# No-Overclaim Policy

Dealix wins by being trusted. Over-claiming is the fastest way to lose that.

## Policy
- Every claim names its source or is marked as an estimate.
- Every estimate has a method.
- Every outcome is described as a range, not a number, unless backed by a public case study.
- The word "guarantee" is reserved for items Dealix can legally and operationally guarantee. Almost nothing qualifies.

## What we never claim
- Specific revenue uplift for a specific client without a signed result.
- Regulatory approval we have not earned in writing.
- Capabilities of partner systems we do not own.
- Performance numbers we have not measured.

## Enforcement
- All public content runs Claim Guard.
- Periodic random audit of sent messages and proposals.
- An over-claim incident triggers `INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md`.

## Rule
A small loss from caution is much cheaper than a single loss from over-claiming.
