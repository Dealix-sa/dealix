# No Overclaim Policy

## Purpose
Make sure Dealix never promises outcomes it cannot evidence, never claims certifications it does not hold, and never sells revenue it cannot generate.

## Owner
Sami / Trust owner.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Banned Claims
- "Guaranteed revenue"
- "Guaranteed leads / sales"
- "ROI in N days, guaranteed"
- "Fully PDPL compliant" (without legal review on record)
- "Fully SOC2 / ISO 27001 compliant" (without active certification)
- "Endorsed by [authority]" (without written endorsement)
- Any "100%" / "always" / "never fails" framing about a future result

## Allowed Reframes
- "In recent deliveries we observed ..."
- "Our standard sprint produces ..."
- "We design our process to align with PDPL principles."
- "Based on the data we received, we estimate ..."
- "We will share the methodology and evidence behind any claim we make."

## Process
1. Before any public claim is published, it is checked against this list.
2. If a claim is uncertain, it is reframed to a process or evidence statement.
3. All approved public claims are logged in `claim_approval_log.csv` (private).
