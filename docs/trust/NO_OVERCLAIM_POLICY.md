# No-Overclaim Policy

## Purpose
Dealix operates on evidence. This policy defines the claims that Dealix will not make, in any public artefact, sales asset, delivery report, outreach message, or marketing surface.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Scope
Applies to:
- All public repositories.
- All marketing surfaces (landing pages, social posts, press, decks).
- All client-facing assets (proposals, reports, outreach messages on behalf of clients).
- All internal-but-shareable material that could leave Dealix custody.

## Forbidden Claims
The following claims are never to be made, in any language:

- **Guaranteed sales** — Dealix does not guarantee sales of any kind.
- **Guaranteed revenue** — Dealix does not guarantee revenue figures, growth percentages, or financial returns.
- **Guaranteed replies or meetings** — Dealix does not guarantee that prospects will respond or book meetings.
- **Fully autonomous sales** — Dealix is not a fully autonomous sales system; client execution is required.
- **No-risk** — No engagement is risk-free; risk is shared and documented.
- **Fully compliant** — Dealix does not certify legal, regulatory, or sector compliance.
- **Certified / authorised** — Dealix is not a certifying or authorising body.
- **Endorsement by a client** without that client's written approval.

## Required Evidence Before Any Claim
Before any of the following are claimed publicly, written evidence must exist in the private operations record:
- Customer counts.
- Engagement counts.
- Revenue figures.
- Case-study quotes.
- Sector coverage.
- Outcome metrics.

If the evidence is not present, the claim is not published.

## Honest Framing Patterns
Use these patterns instead of forbidden claims:

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| "guaranteed sales" | "qualified opportunities matched to your ICP" |
| "guaranteed revenue" | "opportunity intelligence and execution assets" |
| "fully autonomous outreach" | "outreach pack prepared for your team to execute" |
| "no-risk" | "scoped engagement with a defined start condition and refund policy for undelivered scope" |
| "fully compliant" | "operating boundaries documented; client owns compliance decisions" |

## Enforcement
- The QA checklist for every delivery includes a no-overclaim review.
- Pre-commit and CI checks flag forbidden phrases in public files.
- Any violation discovered post-publication is corrected and logged in the weekly learning review.

## Reporting a Concern
Anyone — internal or external — who sees a possible overclaim should raise it directly to the founder. Concerns are reviewed within one business day.
