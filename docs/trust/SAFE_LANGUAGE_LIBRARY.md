# Safe Language Library

## Purpose
Give every operator and agent ready-made wording for sensitive situations so they never need to improvise under pressure.

## Owner
Sami / Trust owner.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Categories

### Declining a bad-fit engagement (EN)
> "Thank you for the conversation. Based on what we discussed, this engagement is not the right fit for what we deliver today. I would rather be upfront now than create a delivery that doesn't serve you."

### Declining a bad-fit engagement (AR)
> "شاكر لك على وقتك. بناءً على ما تشاركنا فيه، هذا الارتباط ليس الأنسب لما نقدّمه حالياً. أفضّل أن أكون واضحاً معك الآن بدل أن نسلّم شيء لا يخدمك."

### Late payment follow-up (EN)
> "Following up on invoice [N] dated [D]. Could you confirm the expected payment date so we can plan the next delivery step? Happy to share any documents you may need."

### Late payment follow-up (AR)
> "متابعة بخصوص الفاتورة رقم [N] بتاريخ [D]. هل يمكن تأكيد التاريخ المتوقع للسداد حتى نخطط للخطوة التالية في التسليم؟ مستعد لمشاركة أي مستندات تحتاجونها."

### Refusing an A3 ask from a client
> "This step requires a written approval on our side before we can proceed. Once we have that recorded, we can move forward in the next 24 hours."

### Reframing an outcome claim
- Replace "guaranteed N leads" with "the sprint produces a qualified lead table sized to your sector."
- Replace "100% PDPL compliant" with "designed to align with PDPL principles; legal review is a separate engagement."
- Replace "ROI in 30 days" with "we measure ROI signals weekly and share them transparently."

### Communicating a delay
> "We are behind on [X] by [Y]. The cause is [Z]. The new committed date is [D]. Here is what we will do to prevent this in the next sprint."

## Rules
- Never invent legal claims.
- Never threaten.
- Never speak for a client before they have committed.
- Prefer process language over outcome language.
