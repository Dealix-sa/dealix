# Safe Language Library — مكتبة اللغة الآمنة

## Purpose

A pre-approved phrase bank for sales, delivery, and content. Drafters copy from here instead of inventing. New phrases are added by review, not by accident.

## Sales

| Context | Approved EN | Approved AR |
|---|---|---|
| Opening line | "We help Saudi operators surface qualified opportunities, with evidence." | "نساعد المشغّلين السعوديين على كشف فرص مؤهّلة مدعومة بأدلة." |
| Outcome framing | "You receive a reviewed lead table and a next-step pack." | "تستلم جدول فرص مراجَع وحزمة خطوة تالية." |
| Pricing | "499 SAR for the Revenue Sprint diagnostic." | "499 ريالًا لتشخيص سبرنت الإيرادات." |
| Timeline | "Five to seven business days." | "من خمسة إلى سبعة أيام عمل." |
| Refund | "If the deliverable falls outside the agreed scope, we refund the diagnostic fee." | "إذا خرج المنتج عن النطاق المتفق عليه، نُعيد رسم التشخيص." |

## Delivery

| Context | Approved EN | Approved AR |

| Handover | "Findings shared for your review and decision." | "النتائج مُشاركة لمراجعتك وقرارك." |

## Replacement table (quick reference)

| Avoid | Use |
|---|---|
| "transform your business" | "tighten your revenue motion" |
| "supercharge" | "shorten the time to a reviewed opportunity" |
| "leverage AI" | "use governed AI operations" |
| "next-gen" | "current, documented" |
| "world-class" | "audit-ready" |
| "revolutionary" | "operational" |

## Operational steps

1. Drafters search this file first; if no entry, propose one in a pull request.
2. New entries require Content Lead approval; revenue or compliance phrases require A3.
3. Phrases are reviewed against the [NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md](./NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md) banned list.

## AR — ملخّص

مكتبة اللغة الآمنة تجمع العبارات المعتمدة للمبيعات والتسليم والمحتوى. الكاتب يَنقل منها لا يَخترع. الإضافة تتم عبر مراجعة، والإيرادات والامتثال يطلبان موافقة A3. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
