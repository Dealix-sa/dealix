# Safe Language Library

> The phrase bank. Use these. They are pre-approved.

## Capability claims

- "Built for Saudi B2B revenue intelligence."
- "Bilingual outreach packs with linked evidence per lead."
- "Founder-led delivery, agent-assisted preparation, human approval before send."
- "Designed to surface qualified opportunities, not to replace your sales team."
- "Evidence-bound: every claim links to a verifiable source."

## Outcome language

- "Our last 5 Sprints averaged [X] qualified replies." (only when true)
- "[N] approved case studies available on request."
- "Designed to deliver [scope] in [time window] under typical conditions."

## Approval / autonomy language

- "Agents prepare; humans approve."
- "No outbound message is sent without founder review."
- "Customer data is processed under PDPL principles: lawful basis,
  minimisation, retention limits."

## Trust language

- "We do not guarantee revenue. We guarantee delivery of the scope you
  paid for."
- "If we discover we cannot deliver, we will tell you, refund the unused
  portion, and document the cause."
- "Every figure in this proposal is supported by a ledger entry we
  can share."

## Pricing language

- "Sprint pricing starts at 2,500 SAR. The scope determines the price."
- "Retainers begin once a Sprint is delivered and accepted."
- "We do not discount for new logos. We may scope down if the budget
  requires it."

## Refusal language

- "Based on what you have described, we are not the right fit."
- "We do not provide [thing]. We can refer you to [option] if helpful."
- "We do not guarantee outcomes. We can show evidence of previous
  delivery."

## Outreach openers (Arabic + English)

- "Saw [specific signal]. Built a 3-account sample for [their company].
  Want it?"
- "[شركتهم] لاحظنا [إشارة محددة]. حضّرنا نموذج لـ 3 حسابات. تحب نشاركه؟"

## Closing language

- "If this is a fit, the next step is a 20-minute call."
- "If not now, when would be a better time to revisit?"

## Anti-language (do not use)

See `NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md`.

## Adding to the library

Any new phrase added requires:

- Founder approval (T1)
- A test usage and a recorded conversion outcome
- An entry here with the use case
