Improve customer support speed and consistency with AI-suggested replies (draft-only by default).

## Price

Custom — scoped per engagement (Rung 5 / separate SOW). Not a fixed public price.

10–15 business days (as scoped)

Teams drowning in WhatsApp/email with repeating questions and no unified FAQ.

- message categories
- FAQ base
- suggested replies (draft-only in MVP)
- escalation rules
- SLA dashboard (as designed per engagement)

- medical/legal guarantees
- accessing PII without documented basis

Category coverage + draft review turnaround + reduction in unresolved threads (agreed definition).
