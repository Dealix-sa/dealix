# Proposal Asset System

> Proposal templates per offer × sector. Templates only; the actual
> sent proposal is generated by Sales OS and stored elsewhere.

## What goes in `assets/sales/proposals/`

- One template per `(offer, sector)` pair we actively sell.
- Templates use the existing approved offer ladder.
- No price changes happen in this layer; pricing lives in
  `docs/company/PRICING.md`.

## Filename convention

`proposal_<offer>_<sector>_v<NN>.md`

## Template structure

```
# Proposal — <Offer>, <Sector>

## 1. Outcome we're being hired for
(specific, measurable, no guarantee words)

## 2. What we do in the first 14 / 30 / 60 days
(concrete activities only)

## 3. What you'll have at the end of the engagement
(artifacts — diagnostic, passport, dashboards, etc.)

## 4. Investment
(from the offer ladder; no off-ladder discounts)

## 5. PDPL / ZATCA posture
(short — link to the formal doc)

## 6. Approval points
(named approval steps; no auto-renewal traps)
```

## Banned words (verifier flags)

- "guaranteed", "100%", "always", "never fails", "we will get you
  customers", "guaranteed leads", "risk-free".
