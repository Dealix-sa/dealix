# Refund Policy

When and how Dealix issues refunds.

## When refunds are considered
- Dealix failed to deliver the agreed scope within the agreed timeline.
- The client identifies a material defect in the deliverable that Dealix accepts.
- A regulatory or trust issue requires unwinding the engagement.

## When refunds are not appropriate
- The client achieved outcomes different from what they expected, but in scope.
- The client did not use the deliverables.
- Scope creep was declined.

## Authority
- Refund decisions are A3. Founder only.
- Logged in `dealix-ops-private/revenue/refunds.csv` and `DECISION_LOG.md`.

## Process
1. Client writes the request.
2. Founder reviews against the engagement record within 5 working days.
3. Decision communicated in writing with reasoning.
4. Refund issued through the original payment channel.
5. Post-mortem run; lesson captured in `EXPERIMENT_LOG.md` if structural.

## Rule
A refund handled well preserves the relationship and the reputation. A refund handled badly costs both.
