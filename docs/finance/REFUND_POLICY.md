## Late Delivery Credit

If Dealix delivers late:
- L1 (< 24 hr late, full scope) — no credit
- L2 (> 24 hr late OR partial scope) — pro-rated credit against next invoice
- L3 (failed scope OR trust incident) — full refund, written apology, optional offer pause

## How To Request

1. Email founder with: order reference + reason
2. Founder responds within 48 hr
3. If approved: refund processed via original payment method within 14 days
4. Documented refund row in `revenue/refunds.csv` (private) — internal only

## Trust Posture

- We don't make refunds painful
- We don't penalize honest dissatisfaction
- We do enforce the "work in progress" rule so we can keep prices low
- We do refund SEV-1/SEV-2 incidents proactively, without being asked

## What This Policy Refuses

- Open-ended satisfaction guarantees (we deliver process, not specific outcomes)
- Hidden non-refundable clauses
- "Store credit" instead of refund (we refund cash to original method)
- Punitive cancellation fees
