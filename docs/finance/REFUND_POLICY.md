# Refund Policy — سياسة الاسترداد

## Purpose
Define when refunds happen, when they do not, and the approval required. The policy is restrictive by design — refunds are a learning event, not a customer-service convenience.

## When refunds happen (eligible)
- Dealix cancels the engagement before any deliverable is started.
- Documented service-level breach: agreed deliverable not produced within the agreed window, and Dealix failed to remediate within a written remediation window.
- Documented misrepresentation in the proposal that the client catches and Dealix confirms.
- Mutual cancellation in writing, pro-rated.

## When refunds do not happen
- Client changes strategy or internal priority.
- Client cannot use the deliverable downstream (estimated value is not Verified value).
- Output produced but did not lead to a client's sale.
- Client requests scope different from the signed proposal.
- Late payment dispute (handle via reconciliation, not refund).
- Outcomes-based dissatisfaction without service-level breach.

## Approval
- Every refund requires written A2 approval per `docs/governance/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.
- A2 written record includes: invoice id, amount, reason, eligibility category above, lesson learned.
- Refunds above SAR 25k additionally require a 24-hour cooling period before execution.

## Rules
- A refund triggers an incident review per `docs/governance/INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md` if cause is service-level breach.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment source within 10 business days of approval.
- A refund does not waive Dealix's right to the work product already produced; usage rights revert.
- A refund event is logged in the finance register and feeds the engineering health review if applicable.

## Operations
- Client submits request in writing.
- Founder evaluates against eligibility list, writes A2 decision, logs.
- If approved: original payment source refunded; invoice marked refunded; usage rights notice sent.
- If denied: written rationale to client referencing the policy.

## Evidence
- Refund register stores: invoice id, request date, decision date, decision, A2 record, executed date, lesson.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: per request; monthly review of all refunds for systemic causes.

## القسم العربي

**يُسترد عندما:** ديلكس تلغي قبل أي مُخرج، خرق مستوى خدمة موثق بدون معالجة، تحريف موثق في الاقتراح، إلغاء متبادل كتابيًا بنسبة.

**لا يُسترد عندما:** العميل يغيّر الاستراتيجية، لا يستطيع استثمار المُخرج لاحقًا (القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة)، لم يقد المُخرج لبيع، يطلب نطاقًا مختلفًا، نزاع تأخر دفع، عدم رضى نتائج بدون خرق خدمة.

**الموافقة:** كل استرداد يحتاج A2 مكتوبة. فوق 25 ألف ريال: 24 ساعة تهدئة قبل التنفيذ.

**القواعد:** الاسترداد بسبب خرق خدمة يستدعي مراجعة حادثة. التنفيذ للمصدر الأصلي خلال 10 أيام عمل. حقوق الاستخدام تعود لديلكس.

**المالك:** المؤسس. **الإيقاع:** فوري عند الطلب، مراجعة شهرية شاملة.
