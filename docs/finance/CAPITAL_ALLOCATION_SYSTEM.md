# Capital Allocation System

## Purpose
Decide where Dealix spends founder time, cash, engineering, and attention.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Capital Types
- founder time
- cash
- engineering time
- brand attention
- customer trust
- data assets
- opportunity cost

## Allocation Priority
1. Revenue generation
2. Delivery quality
3. Trust and safety
4. Repeatability
5. Automation
6. Brand proof
7. Strategic options

## Rules
- Do not spend engineering time where a template solves the issue.
- Do not spend founder time where a checklist solves the issue.
- Do not spend cash before knowing the bottleneck.
- Do not automate a workflow that has not repeated.
- Do not accept revenue that damages trust or repeatability.
