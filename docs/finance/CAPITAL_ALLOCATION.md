# Capital Allocation

Even without external funding, the company has capital: time, money,
engineering effort, brand attention, customer trust, data assets. All
of these are scarce. All of these compound.

## Capital Types
- founder time
- cash
- engineering time
- brand attention
- customer trust
- data assets

## Allocation Priority
1. Revenue generation
2. Delivery quality
3. Trust and safety
4. Repeatability
5. Automation
6. Brand authority
7. Strategic optionality

## Rule
- Do not spend engineering time where a template would solve the issue.
- Do not spend founder time where a checklist would solve the issue.

## Weekly Capital Review

Every Friday, the founder reviews one question per capital type:

| Capital          | Question                                             |
|------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Founder time     | What did I do this week that a checklist could do?   |
| Cash             | What spend produced revenue or proof?                |
| Engineering time | Which PR moved a metric in `OPERATING_METRICS_CONTRACT.md`? |
| Brand attention  | Which post produced a qualified reply?               |
| Customer trust   | Did we keep every promise we made this week?         |
| Data assets      | What did we learn this week that the company now owns? |
