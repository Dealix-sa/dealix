# Capital Allocation — تخصيص رأس المال

## Purpose
How Dealix's available cash is divided between reserves, operations, growth, and owner draw. Allocation is rule-based, not vibes-based.

## Allocation buckets (post-tax, post-fixed-cost)

| Bucket | Target % | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Operating reserves | maintain 3 months fixed cost first | Safety; cannot fall below floor |
| Reinvest (delivery quality + product) | 35% | Tools, contractors, eval suite, content production |
| Growth experiments | 25% | Outreach, content amplification, partner enablement |
| Owner draw | 30% | Founder compensation per schedule |
| Buffer | 10% | Unallocated, deployed on opportunity within quarter |

Percentages apply after reserves floor is satisfied. If reserves are below floor, 100% of surplus replenishes reserves until restored.

## Owner draw
- Monthly fixed draw set by the founder; on schedule, treated as fixed cost.
- Distributions above the fixed draw require written A2 approval and only from the owner-draw bucket.
- Distributions reduce capital allocation flexibility; founder accepts the trade-off in writing.

## Growth experiments
- Each experiment requires a one-page brief: hypothesis, cost cap, success metric, kill criterion.
- Failed experiments are documented; no follow-on spend on a failed pattern within the same quarter.

## Rules
- Quarterly re-set: founder re-allocates based on actual cash position.
- No bucket borrows from another without written A2 and a payback plan.
- The buffer cannot be used for anything banned by [`docs/product/NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md`](../product/NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md).
- Allocation decisions are logged; no verbal-only allocation changes.

## Operations
- Monthly: founder records actual spend per bucket vs allocation.
- Quarterly: re-allocate based on actuals and forward plan.

## Evidence
- Allocation table stored in `docs/finance/registers/YYYY-Qn_allocation.md` (created each quarter).
- Variance to plan recorded with explanation.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: monthly variance review; quarterly re-allocation.

## القسم العربي

**الدلاء (بعد الضريبة والتكلفة الثابتة):** الاحتياطي التشغيلي يُملأ أولًا (3 أشهر)، إعادة استثمار 35%، تجارب نمو 25%، سحب المالك 30%، احتياطي مرن 10%.

**سحب المالك:** سحب شهري ثابت كتكلفة ثابتة، أي توزيع إضافي يحتاج موافقة A2 ومن دلو سحب المالك فقط.

**تجارب النمو:** ملخص صفحة واحدة لكل تجربة (فرضية، سقف تكلفة، مقياس نجاح، معيار إيقاف). التجارب الفاشلة لا تموَّل في نفس الربع.

**القواعد:** إعادة ضبط ربعية حسب السيولة الفعلية. لا اقتراض بين الدلاء بدون A2 وخطة سداد. الاحتياطي المرن لا يموّل أي شيء ممنوع في `NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md`.

**المالك:** المؤسس. **الإيقاع:** مراجعة فروقات شهرية، إعادة تخصيص ربعية.
