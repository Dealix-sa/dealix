# Invoice Workflow

The end-to-end invoice flow.

## Steps
1. Proposal accepted by the client (written).
2. Invoice drafted from the proposal scope and price.
3. A2 approval recorded on the draft.
4. Invoice issued and sent.
5. Reminder at day 7 if unpaid.
6. Reminder at day 14 if unpaid; founder check-in.
7. Reminder at day 21 if unpaid; escalation plan.
8. Payment received and reconciled.
9. Receipt issued.
10. Engagement kickoff triggered.

## Numbering and storage
- Sequential numbering per fiscal year.
- Stored in `dealix-ops-private/revenue/invoices/`.
- Linked to the client folder.

## Rule
A perfect proposal with a sloppy invoice is a sloppy company. Treat invoicing as part of the product.
