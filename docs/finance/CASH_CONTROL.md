# Cash Control

How Dealix protects its cash.

## Accounts
- One operating account.
- One reserve account (3 months of operating costs).
- Tax-set-aside account (per Saudi tax requirements).

## Movement rules
- Founder authorises any movement above the small-expense threshold.
- Reserve is refilled before any discretionary spend.
- Tax set-aside is moved monthly, automatic if possible.

## Expenses
- Categorised in line with the chart of accounts.
- Receipts stored within 7 days of the expense.
- Monthly review against budget.

## Cash forecast
- Rolling 90-day cash forecast.
- Updated weekly with cash collected and forecast cash needs.
- Stored in `dealix-ops-private/finance/cash_forecast.csv`.

## Rule
Cash is the most reliable signal of company health. Lose visibility on cash, lose visibility on the company.
