# Payment Capture OS

How Dealix moves from "signed proposal" to "captured payment" without
ever automating a charge the founder didn't approve.

## 1. Allowed payment surfaces

- Bank transfer (SADAD reference issued by the founder).
- Moyasar invoice link generated by the founder.
- Bank invoice with ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoice fields.

## 2. Trust gate

- Every invoice is generated against an approved proposal row.
- Payment instruments are **never** stored long-term inside Dealix
  runtime for auto-capture.
- Receipt and reconciliation are recorded in the payment register.

## 3. Outputs

`revenue/payment_register.csv`:
```
payment_id,proposal_id,account_id,instrument,amount_sar,
zatca_invoice_no,zatca_status,captured_at,status
```

## 4. ZATCA Phase 2

- Buyer + seller fields complete.
- VAT calculation transparent.
- e-invoice issued via integrations/zatca module (existing repo
  integration; do not duplicate).

## 5. KPIs

- Days from sign → captured.
- ZATCA acceptance rate (must be 100 %).
- Disputed invoice rate (low + investigated when it happens).

## 6. Banned patterns

- ❌ Auto-debit from saved instruments.
- ❌ Pricing surprises after sign.
- ❌ Refund commitments outside the proposal terms.
