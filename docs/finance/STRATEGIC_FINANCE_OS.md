# Strategic Finance OS

## Purpose
Tie finance decisions to growth, delivery, hiring, and productization.

## Decisions
- raise price
- reduce scope
- hire contractor
- buy tool
- invest in channel
- kill sector
- scale worker
- delay SaaS

## Inputs
- cash collected
- MRR
- gross margin
- proposal conversion
- delivery hours
- founder bottleneck
- AI/tool cost
- runway

## Rule
Capital follows proven bottlenecks, not excitement.
