# Finance

## Purpose
Track cash in, cash out, and recurring revenue so the founder can make pricing, hiring, and investment decisions from reality.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO (until a finance owner is named).

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Inputs
- Payments received.
- Invoices issued.
- POs and contracts signed.
- Expenses (tools, infra, contractors).
- Bank reconciliation.

## Outputs
- Cash collected this week and month.
- Cash expected (committed but unpaid).
- MRR snapshot.
- Runway estimate.
- Inputs to the daily Money section of the CEO brief.

## Rules
- Numbers must come from receipts, invoices, or bank records - never estimates.
- No claim of revenue without a paid invoice or signed PO.
- Sensitive financial data stays in the private ops repo, not in this public folder.
- Refunds and pricing exceptions require A2 founder approval and are logged.

## Metrics
- Cash collected per week.
- Cash expected vs. cash collected (forecast accuracy).
- MRR growth.
- Days of runway.
- Average days from invoice to payment.

## Evidence
- private revenue tracker (`revenue/cash_collected.csv`).
- MRR tracker (`revenue/mrr_tracker.csv`).
- invoice and PO files.
- bank statements.
