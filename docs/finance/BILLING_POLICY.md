# Billing Policy

## Cash-First Principle
- All Sprint and Data Pack work begins only after payment received
- All retainer months begin only after that month's invoice paid
- No barter, no equity-for-services, no "pay later" arrangements

## Accepted Payment Methods
- Bank transfer (preferred for retainers ≥ SAR 2,999/mo)
- Stripe (cards, including Apple Pay / Google Pay)
- Tabby / Tamara (BNPL for Sprint and Data Pack only)
- Cash on request (with tax invoice, < SAR 1,500 only)

All payments in SAR. VAT (15%) shown separately on every invoice.

## Invoice Terms
- Issued within 24 hours of: signed scope (Sprint/Pack), signed contract (retainer), monthly recurrence (retainer month N+1)
- Due on receipt
- Late fee: 1.5%/month after 30 days (rarely enforced, but documented)
- Tax invoice (Saudi-compliant) for every payment

## Refund Policy
- **Free Diagnostic** — N/A (free)
- **Revenue Sprint (SAR 499)** — full refund if cancelled before kickoff; no refund after kickoff (work in progress)
- **Data Pack (SAR 1,500)** — full refund if cancelled before kickoff; pro-rated refund if cancelled within 48 hr of kickoff
- **Managed Ops (SAR 2,999–4,999/mo)** — full refund of month 1 only if cancelled within 7 days of month 1 kickoff
- **Custom AI** — per SOW; default no refund for delivered work

## Late Delivery Credit
- L1 (< 24 hr late, scope intact) — no credit
- L2 (> 24 hr late OR partial scope) — pro-rated credit equal to days-late ratio applied to next invoice
- L3 (failed scope OR trust incident) — full refund, written apology, optional offer pause

## Refund Approval
- Sprint / Data Pack refunds < SAR 1,500 — founder approval, logged
- Managed Ops refunds — founder approval, logged
- Custom AI refunds — founder + advisor approval
- Any refund > SAR 10,000 — A4 (founder + advisor)

## What This Policy Refuses
- Hidden fees
- Auto-renewal without notice (we send a renewal notice 7 days before each retainer cycle)
- Charge-then-deliver mismatch
- Cash payments > SAR 1,500 (compliance + audit clarity)
- Crypto / unregulated payment rails
- Personal payment to founder's personal account (always business account)
