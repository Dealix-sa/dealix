# Billing Policy

> When we ask for money, and how.

## When we invoice

- **Sprint:** invoice issued within 24h of signed scope. Payment due
  before delivery start, unless a written exception by the founder
  documents the reason and the customer.
- **Pilot:** 50% on scope sign-off, 50% on midpoint review.
- **Retainer:** invoice on the 1st of each month for that month's
  service. Pre-paid.
- **Dealix OS:** custom; defined per contract.

## What every invoice contains

- Dealix legal entity name and address.
- Saudi VAT registration number (if applicable).
- Invoice number (sequential).
- Issue date and due date.
- Customer legal name and address.
- Customer reference / PO number (if any).
- Scope summary (one paragraph).
- Line items with unit price and quantity.
- VAT calculation (if applicable).
- Total in SAR.
- Bank details.
- Payment terms.
- Notes (e.g. "Payment due before delivery start").

## Payment Terms

- Default: Net 7 for Sprints, Pre-paid for Retainers.
- Maximum: Net 30 (requires founder T3 approval).
- Forbidden: Net 60+, "on signature", "best efforts to pay".

## Currencies

- Default: SAR.
- USD invoices: only with founder approval.

## VAT

- Saudi VAT registration once revenue threshold met (15% currently).
- Until registered: invoices state "VAT not applicable".

## Disputes

- A disputed invoice must be reported in writing within 7 days of receipt.
- A disputed invoice with no written reason is treated as overdue.

## Anti-Patterns

- "Send me the invoice after delivery."
- "We pay quarterly."
- "Add a discount line item to keep the headline price low."
- "Bill our holding company instead." (allowed only if PO names
  the holding company as the contracting party).

## Review

Quarterly.
