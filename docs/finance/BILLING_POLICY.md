# Billing Policy — سياسة الفوترة

## Purpose
Define when Dealix bills, who authorizes the bill, and what proof of delivery must exist before the invoice is sent.

## When to bill

| Engagement type | Trigger | Schedule |
|---|---|---|
| Sprint (fixed scope) | Signed proposal | 50% advance on signature, 50% on proof-pack delivery |
| Retainer (monthly) | Signed retainer | Monthly in advance, first day of period |
| Pilot | Signed pilot agreement | 100% in advance |
| Strategic engagement | Signed SOW | Milestone-based per SOW |

## Approval
- Every invoice requires founder sign-off before send.
- Delivery analyst may draft; analyst cannot send.
- Above SAR 100k single invoice: founder writes A2 approval entry per `docs/governance/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.

## Proof-of-delivery requirements

| Invoice trigger | Required proof |
|---|---|
| Signature advance | Signed proposal stored |
| Proof-pack delivery | Proof pack per `docs/delivery/PROOF_PACK_TEMPLATE.md` + client acceptance email |
| Monthly retainer | Prior month's weekly reports per `docs/client_success/WEEKLY_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md` (waived for first month) |
| Milestone | Milestone acceptance email from client |

## Rules
- ZATCA-compliant e-invoice issued through approved provider.
- Currency: SAR by default. Foreign currency only by exception and with written approval.
- Tax (VAT) applied per current KSA rates. No exceptions.
- No discount applied without founder approval; discounts logged.
- No services billed for work outside the signed scope. Out-of-scope work requires a change request per `docs/delivery/CHANGE_REQUEST_PROCESS.md`.

## Operations
- Draft built in invoice tool; reviewer is founder.
- After approval: send to client via email + PDF, log in invoice register.
- Reconciliation per [`INVOICE_WORKFLOW.md`](INVOICE_WORKFLOW.md).

## Evidence
- Every invoice row in the register links: proposal/contract, proof of delivery, sent date, paid date.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: bills issued on trigger; weekly outstanding review.

## القسم العربي

**متى نفوتر:** سبرنت — 50% عند التوقيع و50% عند تسليم حزمة الإثبات. Retainer — شهري مسبق أول الفترة. تجريبي — 100% مسبقًا. عقد استراتيجي — حسب المراحل.

**الموافقة:** كل فاتورة تحتاج توقيع المؤسس. المحلل يصوغ ولا يرسل. فوق 100 ألف ريال للفاتورة الواحدة: موافقة A2 مكتوبة.

**إثبات التسليم مطلوب:** اقتراح موقّع، حزمة إثبات + قبول العميل، تقارير أسبوعية للشهر السابق، قبول مرحلة.

**القواعد:** فوترة إلكترونية متوافقة مع ZATCA، الريال السعودي افتراضًا، ضريبة القيمة المضافة حسب الأنظمة، لا خصم بدون موافقة موثقة، لا فوترة لخدمات خارج النطاق إلا بطلب تغيير.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
