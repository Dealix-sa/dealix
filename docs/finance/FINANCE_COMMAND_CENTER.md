# Finance Command Center

## Purpose
Track cash, payment attempts, MRR, pricing experiments, and runway.

## Owner
Sami / Finance owner.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Inputs
- proposals
- payment records
- invoices
- MRR tracker
- pricing experiments
- expenses

## Outputs
- cash position
- expected cash
- payment follow-ups
- MRR status
- pricing learnings

## Rules
- No full delivery without payment, PO, or written approval.
- Discounts above 20% require founder approval.
- Pricing changes must be logged.
- Refunds are A3 / never automatic.

## Metrics
- cash collected
- cash expected
- MRR
- average deal size
- proposal-to-payment rate
- overdue payments
