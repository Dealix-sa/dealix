## Key Numbers (target steady state, month 6)

- Cash on hand: ≥ 3 months runway
- MRR: ≥ 25,000 SAR
- Gross margin estimate (Sprint): ≥ 60%
- Gross margin estimate (Retainer): ≥ 70%
- DSO (Days Sales Outstanding): ≤ 14
