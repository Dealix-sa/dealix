# Master Services Agreement (MSA) — Template / قالب اتفاقية الخدمات الرئيسية

> **Template only — lawyer review required before signature.**
> قالب فقط — تُراجع قانونيًا قبل التوقيع. لا يُعدّ مشورة قانونية.

This MSA governs all Transformation OS engagements (see
[DEALIX_TRANSFORMATION_OS_MASTER_AR.md](../DEALIX_TRANSFORMATION_OS_MASTER_AR.md)).
Per-engagement scope/price live in the SOW + the catalog, not here.

## 1. Parties / الأطراف
- **Provider / المزوّد:** Dealix
- **Client / العميل:** {{CLIENT_LEGAL_NAME}}, CR {{CLIENT_CR}}

## 2. Scope / النطاق
Services are defined per **Statement of Work (SOW)** referencing one or more
catalog `service_id`s. Prices are **estimates** finalized in each SOW after a paid
diagnostic. No revenue is recognized before `invoice_paid`.

## 3. Governance & Approvals / الحوكمة والموافقات
- Provider operates **approval-first**: no external send/commitment without Client
  approval (NO_LIVE_SEND). لا إرسال خارجي بدون موافقة العميل.
- No cold outreach, no LinkedIn automation, no scraping, no bulk blast.
- AI agents operate within scoped roles, allowed-tool registries, and human
  approval gates for sensitive actions; all logged.

## 4. Data Protection / حماية البيانات
Personal data handling is governed by the **DPA**
([DPA_TEMPLATE_AR_EN.md](DPA_TEMPLATE_AR_EN.md)), PDPL-aligned. See
`dealix/registers/compliance_saudi.yaml`.

## 5. Fees & Payment / الأتعاب والدفع
- Setup fee + monthly fee per SOW (ranges are estimates).
- Milestone-based invoicing (e.g., 40/30/30). ZATCA-compliant invoices.
- المبالغ بالريال السعودي. الدفع على مراحل مرتبطة بالتسليم.

## 6. IP / الملكية الفكرية
Client owns its data and delivered configurations. Provider retains its
pre-existing methods, frameworks, and reusable tooling.

## 7. Confidentiality, Warranties, Liability / السرية والضمانات والمسؤولية
- Mutual confidentiality. Provider warrants professional delivery — **no
  guaranteed business outcomes** (commitments are effort/KPI-based, not guarantees).
- Liability cap per SOW. لا ضمان لنتائج أعمال محددة.

## 8. Term, Termination, SLA / المدة والإنهاء و SLA
- Term per SOW; either party may terminate for material uncured breach.
- Pro-rata refund for undelivered milestones. SLA appended per SOW.

## 9. Governing Law / القانون الحاكم
Laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. أنظمة المملكة العربية السعودية.
