# Revenue KPI Tree

> One tree. Cash at the root.

```
Cash collected (week / month / quarter)
├── New cash
│   ├── New logos × Average first invoice
│   │   ├── Qualified opps × Win rate × First invoice band
│   │   │   ├── Sample → Sprint conversion
│   │   │   ├── Sprint → Pilot conversion
│   │   │   └── Pilot → Retainer conversion
│   │   └── Founder approval latency
│   └── New-logo gross margin
├── Expansion cash
│   ├── Healthy customers × Expansion rate × Upsell size
│   └── Pricing guardrail adherence
└── Retention cash
    ├── Logo retention × Average ARR
    ├── Save rate on amber / red customers
    └── Referrals × Referral conversion
```

## Distribution KPIs

- Drafts produced / week
- Brand-pass rate
- Suppression honour (must be 100 %)
- Reply quality per sector
- Cash-per-send (SAR per draft sent)

## Trust KPIs

- Approval latency
- Trust gate violations (target 0)
- Eval pass rate
- Audit completeness (target 100 %)

## Engineering KPIs (DORA)

- Deployment frequency
- Lead time for changes
- Change-fail rate
- Time to restore service

## Reporting

- Weekly KPI snapshot in the daily brief.
- Monthly report to the founder.
- Quarterly board pack.
