# Brand Voice

- **Arabic:** clear, practical, modern Gulf business Arabic. Confident, not hyped.
- **English:** executive, concise, operations-first.
- **Tone:** founder-led, credible, calm. We teach, we don't shout.
- **Stance:** AI with human approval; no blind automation; trust over hype.

## Do
- Lead with a real workflow pain.
- Show before/after thinking.
- Be specific to Saudi/GCC B2B reality.

## Don't
- No "game-changer", "revolutionary", "unlock the power".
- No guaranteed ROI or invented social proof.
- No fear-mongering or fake urgency.
