# 15 — Ads Readiness Gate

بوابة جاهزية الإعلانات المدفوعة. **GO للتخطيط والنسخ فقط. NO-GO للإطلاق الحي** قبل استيفاء كل الشروط.
Paid ads readiness gate. **GO for planning + copy only. NO-GO for live launch** until every
condition below is satisfied.

## ✅ GO now
- Audience research and segmentation (on paper).
- Ad copy drafting (claim-safe, bilingual).
- Channel and budget planning (no spend).
- Landing-page concept and message-match planning.

## ⛔ NO-GO — do not launch paid ads until ALL are true
- [ ] Landing-page tracking installed and verified
- [ ] UTM taxonomy defined and documented
- [ ] Conversion event defined and firing
- [ ] Privacy review complete (PDPL-aligned)
- [ ] Budget approved (owner + cap)
- [ ] Audience reviewed and approved
- [ ] Claim review passed (no guaranteed ROI / 100% / replace your team / automate everything / no human needed)
- [ ] Negative keywords list prepared
- [ ] Approval owner named and signed off

## Doctrine
> This repo contains **no API to launch ads**. Ads are a planning and copy artifact until the
> gate is cleared by a human owner. No spend is triggered by any script or workflow.
