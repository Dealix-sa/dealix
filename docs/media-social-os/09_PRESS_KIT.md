# Press Kit

## One-paragraph description
Dealix is a Saudi/GCC B2B AI Revenue & Operations OS. It installs workflow
systems where AI drafts and ranks the work while the operator approves every
step — no blind automation. Dealix focuses on facilities management,
contracting & project controls, real estate operations, legal & professional
services, and consulting/training/B2B services.
