## English — Company Boilerplate

Dealix is an AI Revenue & Operations OS for Saudi and GCC B2B companies. It helps a company find revenue opportunities, organize operations, and prepare customer-facing work — proposals, follow-ups, account summaries, and pipeline reviews — under a single governing rule: AI drafts and ranks; the founder reviews, approves, and sends manually; the system never sends externally. Dealix focuses on five verticals: facilities management; contracting and project controls; real estate and property operations; legal and professional services; and consulting, training, and B2B services.
