# سياسة عدم الإزعاج — No-Spam Policy

> **القاعدة / The Rule:** نتواصل فقط على أساس علاقة دافئة أو موافقة صريحة — يدويًا، عبر قوالب معتمدة، بعد موافقة بشرية.
> **We contact only on a warm-relationship or explicit opt-in basis — manually, via approved templates, after human approval.**

Canonical: `CLAUDE.md` Hard rules, `docs/00_constitution/NON_NEGOTIABLES.md`,
`docs/00_constitution/WHAT_DEALIX_REFUSES.md`. Sending is gated by `HUMAN_APPROVAL_POLICY.md`.

## ممنوع منعًا باتًا / Hard prohibitions

- **No cold WhatsApp automation.** No bulk/automated messaging to people who did not opt in.
- **No cold LinkedIn automation.** No connection/message bots, no auto-DM sequences.
- **No scraping behind login.** We do not scrape any source that requires authentication.
- **No scraping of personal data.** No harvesting of personal phone numbers, emails, or profiles.
- **No fake scarcity.** No "limited seats / closing soon" unless there is a real, documented cap.
- **No auto-send.** All outreach is drafted, queued, human-approved, then sent (see `HUMAN_APPROVAL_POLICY.md`).

## أساس التواصل المسموح / Allowed contact basis

We may reach a person only when at least one is true and documented:
- **Warm relationship** — prior business interaction, referral, or existing customer relationship.
- **Explicit opt-in** — the person submitted a form / requested contact (e.g. Business OS Score, Diagnostic, Sprint).
- **Inbound** — the person contacted Dealix first.

The basis is recorded as `opt_in_basis` in the Decision Passport (`HUMAN_APPROVAL_POLICY.md`).
No basis = no message.

## القوالب والإرسال / Templates and sending

- Outreach uses **approved templates** only. New templates require founder sign-off and a `CLAIMS_REGISTER.md`
  row for any claim they contain.
- Sending is **manual / human-approved** — one approved artifact at a time, never an automated blast.
- Every message includes a clear identity and an easy way to **opt out / stop**. Opt-outs are honored immediately
  and permanently.

## إطار PDPL / PDPL-aware framing

Aligned with Saudi **Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)** principles:
- **Lawful basis** — consent or legitimate, documented relationship before any direct marketing.
- **Purpose limitation** — contact only for the purpose the person engaged us for.
- **Data minimization** — collect and store only what is needed.
- **Right to object / withdraw** — honor opt-out and deletion requests (see `DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md`).

## بوابة قبل الإطلاق / Pre-launch gate

No outreach channel ships until: approved templates exist, opt-in basis is captured per contact,
opt-out works end-to-end, and `HUMAN_APPROVAL_POLICY.md` zero-auto-send tests pass.
