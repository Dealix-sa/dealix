# No-Spam Policy

> 400 بحث، وليس 400 إرسال.

Dealix is strong on intelligence and **restrained** on channels. Volume lives in
research, never in sending.

---

## Hard limits

- **No bulk sending.** Manual sends only, ~3–5/day.
- **No cold WhatsApp automation.** No automated messaging on any channel.
- **No personal phone numbers.** Official channels only.
- **No purchased lists.** No leaked / scraped contact data.
- **One tailored message per company.** No copy-paste blasts.

## Why

- Respect for recipients and for Saudi market norms.
- Protects deliverability and brand.
- Forces quality: a message worth the founder's manual send is a message worth
  receiving.

## Relationship to the funnel

| Stage | Volume |
|-------|--------|
| Researched | 300–400/day |
| Scored | ~80/day |
| Shortlisted | ~20/day |
| Drafted | ~10/day |
| **Sent (manual)** | **3–5/day** |
