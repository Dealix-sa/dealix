# 03 — 30 / 60 / 90 Day Market Entry Plan

> **Status:** Default plan. Adjust based on actual outcomes.
> **Companion:** `02_FIRST_7_DAYS_EXECUTION.md` + `04_DAILY_FOUNDER_COMMAND.md`.

## The plan in one screen

| Phase | Days | Objective | Top 3 weekly targets | Stop condition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Validate wedge | 1–30 | Find the sector + offer that converts | 1 audit delivered, 1 case study, 1 partner intro | if 0 audits after 30 outreach, pivot |
| Double down | 31–60 | Standardize delivery + content + partner | 3 paid pilots, 1 case study, 1 partner co-sell | if 1 client cancels, fix the delivery |
| Repeatable motion | 61–90 | Hire / contract; founder command room; enterprise | 1 setter hired, 1 enterprise conversation, 1 QBR | if 0 new clients, rewrite the offer |

## Days 1–30 (Validate the wedge)

### Weekly targets

- **Week 1:** 10 outreach, 1 discovery, 1 audit (or audit-equivalent).
- **Week 2:** 15 outreach, 2 discoveries, 1 audit delivered, 1 follow-up proposal.
- **Week 3:** 20 outreach, 3 discoveries, 1 paid pilot, 1 case study draft.
- **Week 4:** 20 outreach, 3 discoveries, 1 paid pilot in motion, 1 weekly review + decision.

### Daily rituals

- Morning: `scripts/founder_daily_command_dry_run.py`.
- Mid-day: approval queue review.
- End of day: log.

### Assets to produce

- `target_account.example.json` × 30.
- `outreach_draft.example.json` × 30.
- `approval_queue.example.json` growing to 30+ items.
- 1 case study draft (anonymized, with permission).
- 1 weekly review × 4.
- 1 monthly re-score of vertical + offer + ICP.

### Stop conditions (pause and pivot)

- After 30 outreach, < 3 replies → rewrite the message.
- After 3 discoveries, 0 audits → rewrite the offer.
- After 1 audit, 0 follow-up → the close is broken.
- After 1 audit, trust preflight violation → fix the preflight, not the audit.

### Decisions to make at Day 30

- Which vertical to double down on? (Re-score `SAUDI_VERTICAL_SELECTION_MATRIX_AR.md`.)
- Which offer to double down on? (Re-score the offer ladder.)
- Which persona to prioritize? (Re-rank the 5 personas.)
- Which content pillar to focus on? (Re-pick from the 10.)

## Days 31–60 (Double down)

### Weekly targets

- **Week 5:** 20 outreach, 3 discoveries, 1 new paid pilot, content pillar chosen.
- **Week 6:** 25 outreach, 4 discoveries, 1 new paid pilot, first case study published.
- **Week 7:** 25 outreach, 4 discoveries, 1 new paid pilot, first partner co-sell.
- **Week 8:** 30 outreach, 5 discoveries, 2 new paid pilots, 1 industry event attended.

### Daily rituals (same as 1–30)

### Assets to produce

- 1 published case study.
- 1 partner signed.
- 1 sales VA hired (or contracted).
- 1 content pillar active, 8 posts published.
- 1 monthly re-score.

### Stop conditions

- 2 clients cancel → fix the delivery.
- 1 trust preflight violation → fix the preflight.
- Reply rate drops below 10% → rewrite the message.
- Discovery-to-pilot rate drops below 30% → rewrite the close.

### Decisions to make at Day 60

- Hire a sales setter? (If 3+ paid pilots, yes.)
- Add the next offer? (If 2+ WhatsApp OS clients, propose Command Center.)
- Open a second vertical? (If the first vertical is full.)
- Move to enterprise? (If 1+ SMB is happy and willing to introduce.)

## Days 61–90 (Repeatable motion)

### Weekly targets

- **Week 9:** 30 outreach, 5 discoveries, 2 new paid pilots, founder command room live.
- **Week 10:** 35 outreach, 5 discoveries, 2 new paid pilots, 1 setter in seat.
- **Week 11:** 35 outreach, 5 discoveries, 2 new paid pilots, 1 enterprise conversation.
- **Week 12:** 40 outreach, 6 discoveries, 2 new paid pilots, 1 QBR, monthly review.

### Daily rituals (same)

### Assets to produce

- Founder command room in production.
- 1 setter trained.
- 1 enterprise conversation in motion.
- 1 partner co-sell motion defined.
- 1 QBR conducted.
- 1 monthly re-score.

### Stop conditions

- Setter quits in week 1 → coach or replace.
- Enterprise conversation stalls 4+ weeks → pause enterprise focus.
- Reply rate < 10% for 2 weeks → the message is broken, not the team.

### Decisions to make at Day 90

- Continue doubling down, or shift to a new vertical?
- Promote the setter to a closer?
- Open a new content pillar?
- Invest in a paid channel (sparingly)?
- Apply to an accelerator or an investor?

## The 5 cross-phase metrics

| Metric | Day 30 | Day 60 | Day 90 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Active paid pilots | 1 | 3 | 5+ |
| Case studies published | 0 (1 draft) | 1 | 2+ |
| Partners signed | 0 (1 intro) | 1 | 2+ |
| Outreach sent (cumulative) | ~80 | ~180 | ~330 |
| Reply rate | ≥ 20% | ≥ 20% | ≥ 20% |
| Discovery-to-pilot rate | ≥ 30% | ≥ 30% | ≥ 30% |

## The 5 cross-phase decisions

| Day | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| 30 | Double down vertical + offer + persona + pillar |
| 45 | First paid pilot's mid-point review (continue / pause / pivot) |
| 60 | Hire setter or stay solo |
| 75 | First renewal conversation |
| 90 | Rewrite the 90-day plan for the next quarter |

## The 5 things to NOT do in the 90 days

1. **Do not pursue 3+ verticals.** One is the wedge.
2. **Do not offer all 6 offers in the ladder.** Walk the ladder one offer at a time.
3. **Do not hire a salesperson before Day 60.** Founder closes first.
4. **Do not invest in paid ads before Day 75.** Content + outreach first.
5. **Do not skip the weekly review.** The plan is alive only if the review is alive.

## When to update the plan

- Every 30 days, re-write the 30/60/90 plan based on actual outcomes.
- Every 7 days, re-write the week based on actual conversion.
- Every day, run the daily command.
