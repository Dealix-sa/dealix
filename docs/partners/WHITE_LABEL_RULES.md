# White-Label Rules — قواعد العلامة الخاصة

## Purpose
Define when Dealix work may be delivered under a partner's brand, what the partner may and may not represent, and the guardrails that prevent reputational and trust risks.

## Precondition (gate to enable white-label at all)
White-label is not offered until Dealix has ≥5 active retainers running concurrently for at least 3 months. Reference: [`docs/product/NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md`](../product/NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md). Until the gate clears, white-label is auto-rejected.

## What is allowed (after the gate)
- Partner's brand on deliverables (cover pages, exports), subject to a co-mark mentioning Dealix's role in fine print where required by KSA disclosure norms.
- Partner is named to their client as the service provider. Dealix is a sub-provider with a written sub-processor agreement on data.

## What is not allowed
- Partner cannot claim ownership of Dealix's methods, prompts, templates, or proof packs.
- Partner cannot use Dealix's case studies or client list as their own.
- Partner cannot make capability claims beyond Dealix's published proofs.
- Partner cannot bypass Dealix's content rules. Banned claims remain banned regardless of brand.
- Partner cannot grant a third party access to Dealix work product.

## Guardrails (all required)
1. **Signed white-label agreement** with full IP, sub-processor, and confidentiality terms.
2. **Customer disclosure path** — if asked by the end client, partner must disclose Dealix's role honestly.
3. **Quality bar** — partner's delivery to their client must meet Dealix's delivery standards per `docs/delivery/DELIVERY_STANDARD.md`. Failures revoke white-label.
4. **Pricing floor** — partner cannot price below Dealix's documented floor without written A2 approval.
5. **Quarterly audit** — Dealix audits a sample of white-label deliveries each quarter; failures pause the arrangement until remediated.

## Rules
- White-label can be paused or revoked at any time for cause.
- A revoked arrangement archives the partner's right to continue using Dealix work product. Existing deliverables remain with the end client; new deliveries stop.
- White-label is never offered in the same sector where Dealix is publishing under its own brand without explicit founder strategic decision logged in writing.

## Operations
- Each white-label arrangement has its own folder under `docs/partners/active/<partner-label>/whitelabel/`.
- Quarterly audits documented in a one-page memo per arrangement.

## Evidence
- Signed white-label agreement, quarterly audit memos, customer disclosure record on request.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: gate-check ongoing; quarterly audit per active arrangement.

## القسم العربي

**الشرط المسبق:** White-label لا يُعرض حتى يكون لدى ديلكس ≥5 retainers نشطة متزامنة لـ3 أشهر. حتى يُفتح الشرط، الرفض تلقائي.

**المسموح بعد فتح الشرط:** علامة الشريك على المُخرجات مع إشارة إلى دور ديلكس في الهامش وفق أعراف الإفصاح السعودية. الشريك يُسمّى للعميل كمزود، وديلكس مزود فرعي باتفاقية معالج فرعي.

**غير المسموح:** ادعاء ملكية للأساليب/القوالب/الأدلة، استخدام case studies كأنها للشريك، ادعاءات قدرات تتجاوز أدلتنا، تجاوز قواعد المحتوى، منح طرف ثالث وصولًا.

**الحارسات الإلزامية:** اتفاقية موقّعة، مسار إفصاح للعميل النهائي، سقف جودة، سعر أدنى موثق (لا تخفيض بدون A2)، تدقيق ربعي.

**القواعد:** يُعلَّق أو يُلغى لأي سبب. الإلغاء يُنهي حق الاستخدام للأعمال الجديدة. لا يُعرض في قطاع تنشر فيه ديلكس باسمها بدون قرار استراتيجي موثق.

**المالك:** المؤسس. **الإيقاع:** فحص الشرط مستمر، تدقيق ربعي.
