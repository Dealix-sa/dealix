# White-Label Rules

When Dealix capability ships under a partner's brand.

## Allowed
- White-labelling delivery artifacts (reports, lead tables) with the partner's brand.
- White-labelling outreach packs.
- White-labelling sector reports for the partner's clients.

## Not allowed
- White-labelling that hides Dealix as the data source for the partner's clients when asked directly.
- White-labelling that misrepresents the partner's own capability.
- White-labelling of any artifact that has not passed Dealix QA.

## Requirements
- Written white-label agreement, signed.
- Quality gate: every white-labelled artifact passes the same QA as Dealix-branded.
- Trust gate: partner agrees to Claim Guard for any external claim about the capability.

## Pricing
- White-label engagements priced 20–40% above standard.
- Reflects the trust risk and the cost of maintaining quality through a partner.

## Rule
Our trust posture follows the artifact, not the brand on the cover.
