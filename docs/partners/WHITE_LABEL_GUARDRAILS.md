# White-Label Guardrails

## Purpose
Prevent premature white-label deals that create scope, trust, and delivery risk.

## Not Allowed Before
- 3 paid sprints
- 2 completed deliveries
- 1 retainer
- delivery QA stable
- partner trust proven
- pricing stable
- support capacity known

## Risks
- partner overpromises
- hidden client expectations
- scope creep
- weak quality control
- brand dilution
- data boundary confusion

## If Considered Later
Require:
- written scope
- approval rules
- delivery ownership
- pricing rules
- proof/publicity rules
- data boundary
- termination terms

## Current Decision
Defer.
