# Partner Referral Terms — Guardrails

> The non-negotiable boundaries around partner referral economics.
> The exact rates live in the partner program doc; this file lists
> what we **never** promise.

## We never promise

1. **Exclusivity in a sector** without a written contract, governance
   review, and a defined termination clause.
2. **Guaranteed referral revenue** to a partner. Referral fees pay
   on closed business only.
3. **Lifetime overrides** beyond what is in the approved program.
4. **Payment in advance** for referred meetings; payment is on
   collected revenue.
5. **Custom contract terms outside the offer ladder** without
   founder + governance sign-off.
6. **Free use of customer logos** by the partner without explicit
   permission rows.

## We always require

1. Counter-signed referral agreement before any commission accrues.
2. Attribution mechanism (referral ID or named introducer).
3. PDPL-aligned data handling between Dealix and partner.
4. Quarterly partner review tied to the partner pipeline CSV.

## How to handle "can you bend this?"

- Default answer: no.
- Escalation path: founder + governance review row in
  `<PRIVATE_OPS>/partners/governance_reviews.csv`.
- If approved, the exception is recorded with scope, expiry, and
  rationale.
