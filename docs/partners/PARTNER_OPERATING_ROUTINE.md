# Partner Operating Routine

## Weekly
- log partner referrals
- qualify partner leads
- update partner pipeline
- check trust risks

## Monthly
- review partner performance
- review referral terms
- review partner quality
- decide continue / fix / kill / defer

## Partner Lead Rules
- every referral enters `pipeline_tracker.csv`
- source recorded
- no special pricing unless approved
- scope remains Dealix-owned
- partner does not approve claims

## Rule
A bad partner is worse than no partner.
