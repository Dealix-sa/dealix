# Partner Ecosystem OS v3

## Purpose
Build a referral and co-sell network around Dealix.

## Partner Categories
- ERP implementers
- CRM consultants
- cybersecurity advisors
- agencies
- accountants
- legal/compliance consultants
- startup ecosystem operators
- chamber/event organizers
- SaaS founders

## Partner Motions
- referral ask
- co-sell opportunity
- sector report collaboration
- white-label later
- enterprise intro

## Weekly Target
- 10 referral asks
- 3 partner conversations
- 1 qualified intro

## Rules
- partner cannot promise outcomes
- partner cannot change price/scope
- commission only on collected cash if agreed
- all partner leads logged
