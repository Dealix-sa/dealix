# Partner Strategy

> Targeted, evidence-backed, founder-led.

## Categories of Partner

1. **Adjacent service providers** — B2B agencies, ERP/CRM consultants,
   marketing studios. They have customers; we have the wedge product.
2. **Tools and platforms** — CRM vendors, sales-tech tools whose users
   are our ICP.
3. **Investors / venture community** — portfolio support partnerships.
   (Deferred until we have proof to support a portfolio without
   founder time leak.)
4. **Community / association** — Saudi B2B founder networks; do not
   join more than 2.

## Pre-Conditions

We pursue any partnership only after:

- ≥ 2 sprints completed.
- ≥ 1 approved case study.
- A clear value proposition we can articulate in one sentence to a
  potential partner.

## What we offer partners

- Referral commission (per `REFERRAL_TERMS.md`).
- Co-branded proof artifacts (where the partner contributes context).
- First look at sector-specific outputs.

## What we do **not** offer

- Exclusivity in a sector.
- White-label of our delivery (we sign the work).
- Equity in exchange for distribution.
- Discounts on our offers that violate `OFFER_LADDER.md` floor.

## Selection Criteria for a Partner

- Their customers overlap with our ICP (≥ 60%).
- They are willing to introduce us to ≥ 3 customers in 90 days (named).
- They have a trust posture compatible with ours.
- They will sign the standard agreement.

## Anti-Patterns

- "Strategic partnership" with no defined commercial mechanism.
- Partner-driven roadmap changes.
- Joint sales calls without a written scope of who does what.
- Letting a partner become a single point of dependency for pipeline.

## Review

Quarterly. Partners not producing within agreed timeframes are
gracefully de-listed.
