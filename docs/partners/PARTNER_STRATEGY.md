# Partner Strategy

Partners that accelerate revenue without diluting trust.

## Why partners
- Reach buyers Dealix would not reach directly.
- Shorten sales cycles via warm introductions.
- Bundle complementary capability.
- Extend delivery without hiring.

## Partner types
- Marketing agencies.
- Consultancies.
- ERP / CRM vendors.
- Accounting firms.
- Business-setup firms.
- Software houses.

## Engagement levels
- **Referral** — a partner that sends qualified intros for a referral fee.
- **Co-sell** — joint pursuit, joint scope, defined revenue split.
- **White-label** — partner brands Dealix capability under their banner (limited and explicit).
- **Strategic** — long-term joint go-to-market.

## What partners may not say
- Guaranteed revenue.
- Legal or regulatory compliance certified by Dealix.
- Official certification from any party.

## Rule
A partner is an extension of Dealix's trust posture. The wrong partner costs more than zero partners.
