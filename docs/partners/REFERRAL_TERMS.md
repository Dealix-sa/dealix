# Referral Terms

The standard Dealix referral program.

## Eligibility
- Profile approved (`PARTNER_PROFILE.md`).
- Written agreement signed.

## Referral fee
- 10% of cash collected on the first paid engagement.
- 5% of cash collected on the second and third engagement.
- 0% beyond, unless an extension is agreed in writing.

## Attribution rules
- Referral must be made in writing, with the prospect's permission to introduce.
- Attribution window: 90 days from the first introduction.
- One partner per prospect; ties resolved by first written introduction.

## Payment
- Paid within 30 days of the cash being collected.
- Net of any taxes the partner owes locally.
- Logged in `dealix-ops-private/partners/referrals.csv`.

## What we will not do
- Pay referral fees on guaranteed-outcome promises.
- Pay referral fees for unsolicited cold leads with no introduction.

## Rule
The referral program rewards introductions, not noise.
