# Referral Terms — شروط الإحالة

## Purpose
Standard terms for partners who refer prospects to Dealix. One template. Departures from it require written A2 approval. Clarity here prevents disputes later.

## Fee structure
- **Referral fee: 10% of the first paid invoice on a closed engagement that originated from the partner's introduction.**
- Applies to sprints, pilots, and the first month of a retainer.
- Does not apply to subsequent retainer months, renewals, or expansion scope.

Optional alternative on case-by-case (requires A2 approval):
- 5% of the first three months of a retainer (instead of 10% of first invoice).

## Conditions to qualify
- Partner provided a warm written introduction at least 30 days before signature.
- Prospect was not already in Dealix's pipeline at the time of introduction (verified against pipeline log).
- Partner did not negotiate scope or price on Dealix's behalf without written authorization.
- Engagement closed within 6 months of the introduction. After 6 months the referral expires.

## Payment timing
- Paid within 14 business days of cleared advance payment from the customer.
- Paid by bank transfer in SAR. International wire by exception with fees on partner.
- Invoice required from partner; ZATCA-compliant if partner is a KSA registered entity.

## IP and confidentiality
- All work product, methods, prompts, templates, and proof packs remain Dealix IP.
- Partner does not acquire rights to Dealix work product by referring.
- Partner agrees to NDA for any confidential information shared during the introduction.

## Non-compete and non-circumvention
- Partner agrees not to directly deliver Dealix-equivalent services to the introduced client during the engagement and for 12 months after close, without written A2 consent.
- Dealix agrees not to bypass partner on subsequent engagements with the same introduced client during the same 12 months.

## Rules
- No retroactive referral fees. Introduction must precede pipeline entry.
- No "tip" or supplemental payment outside this structure. If a partner asks for more, the relationship is reviewed.
- No discount offered to a customer to "absorb" the referral fee. The fee is Dealix's cost.
- All terms in writing; verbal-only referral arrangements do not qualify.

## Operations
- Each referral logged in partner folder; introduction date, prospect label, pipeline check, qualification, outcome.

## Evidence
- Referral register; each row with introduction email, pipeline check, contract id, payment record.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: per referral; monthly partner review.

## القسم العربي

**هيكل الرسوم:** 10% من أول فاتورة مدفوعة على عقد مُغلق نشأ من تعريف الشريك. يسري على سبرنت وتجريبي وأول شهر Retainer. لا يسري على الأشهر التالية أو التجديد أو التوسعة.

**بديل (بموافقة A2):** 5% من أول ثلاثة أشهر Retainer بدلًا من 10% من أول فاتورة.

**شروط الاستحقاق:** تعريف دافئ مكتوب قبل التوقيع بـ30 يومًا على الأقل، العميل لم يكن في pipeline من قبل، الشريك لم يفاوض النطاق/السعر بدون تفويض، الإغلاق خلال 6 أشهر.

**توقيت الدفع:** خلال 14 يوم عمل من تحصيل مقدم العميل، بنكي بالريال، فاتورة من الشريك (متوافقة مع ZATCA إن كان مسجلًا).

**IP والسرية:** كل مُنتج وعملية ودليل ملكية ديلكس. لا تنتقل الحقوق بالإحالة. الشريك يوقّع NDA لأي معلومة سرية.

**عدم المنافسة وعدم الالتفاف:** الشريك لا يقدم خدمات مماثلة للعميل المُحال طوال العقد و12 شهرًا بعده بدون موافقة A2. ديلكس لا تتجاوز الشريك على عقود لاحقة لنفس العميل خلال نفس المدة.

**القواعد:** لا رسوم رجعية، لا "إكرامية" خارج الهيكل، لا خصم للعميل لتغطية الرسوم، كل شيء مكتوب.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
