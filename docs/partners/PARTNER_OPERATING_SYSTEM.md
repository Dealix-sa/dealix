# Partner Operating System

## Purpose
Use partners to create qualified referrals without losing trust, pricing
discipline, or delivery control.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Monthly.

## Partner Types
- referral partner
- agency partner
- implementation partner
- ecosystem partner
- strategic advisor
- channel partner later

## Partner Rules
Partners cannot:
- promise guaranteed revenue
- change Dealix pricing
- change scope
- approve discounts
- publish client proof
- represent Dealix without approval
- access private client data

## Partner Can
- refer qualified leads
- introduce decision makers
- co-create sector insights with approval
- help identify ICP opportunities

## Partner Qualification

**Good partner:**
- has access to Saudi B2B buyers
- understands trust boundaries
- does not overpromise
- brings qualified referrals
- respects Dealix scope and pricing

**Bad partner:**
- wants commission without qualification
- pushes bad-fit leads
- promises outcomes
- asks to white-label too early
- creates trust risk

## Metrics
- partner leads
- qualified referrals
- proposals from partner leads
- revenue from partners
- partner trust issues
