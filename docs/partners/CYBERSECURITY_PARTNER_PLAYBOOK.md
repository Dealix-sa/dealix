# Cybersecurity / GRC Partner Playbook

> Cybersecurity and GRC firms own the trust conversation. They get
> called when a CIO wants to be safe about AI. Dealix is the
> proof-safe answer.

## Who fits

- Saudi cybersecurity consultancies and managed security service
  providers.
- GRC / compliance consultancies (PDPL, NCA, SAMA, CMA).
- Pen-testing firms that already audit AI deployments.

## Why they partner with Dealix

- They are asked "is this AI safe to deploy?" — Dealix gives a real
  answer (PDPL Art. 5/13/14/18/21, ZATCA Phase 2, decision passport,
  approval-first audit trail).
- They get a recurring engagement reviewing each customer's Dealix
  decision passport rather than a one-off consulting hour.

## Joint offer shape

- Cybersecurity firm conducts pre-deployment review of Dealix in
  customer environment.
- Dealix provides the audit trail and the proof-safe asset library.
- Joint sign-off on customer-facing trust statements.

## What we never do

- Bypass their review process to ship faster.
- Co-sign claims they have not validated.
- Use their logo for marketing without an executed authorization.

## How to add a cybersecurity partner

1. Row in `partner_pipeline.csv` with `type=cybersecurity_grc`.
2. Set `trust_risk=high` by default; downgrade only after governance.
3. Pilot review on one anonymized customer scenario before joint
   selling.
