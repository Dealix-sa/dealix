# Referral Terms System

## Purpose
Standard referral terms that protect quality and don't erode margin.

## Standard terms
- **Referral fee**: 10% of first-engagement gross revenue (paid after the engagement is fully collected).
- **Eligible offers**: rungs 2, 3, and 4 (Sprint, Data Pack, Retainer).
- **Window**: 60 days from introduction to first paid engagement.
- **Exclusivity**: none.
- **Reporting**: monthly summary to the partner.

## Custom terms
- **Higher fee** (up to 20%): for partners who deliver a full pre-qualified deal.
- **Recurring share**: not offered by default; case-by-case for strategic partners.

## Required artifacts
- Signed agreement (PDF).
- Recorded in `partners/partner_tracker.csv` with `referral_terms`.
- Row in `trust/approval_log.csv` confirming founder approval.

## Payment
- After engagement is fully paid and the work is delivered.
- Within 30 days of full collection.
- Paid via bank transfer; invoice from the partner required.

## Anti-fraud
- Lead must be introduced in writing (email is fine).
- The lead must not already be in Dealix's pipeline.
- Self-referral or related-party referral is excluded.

## Termination
- Either party can terminate with 30 days' notice for new referrals.
- Open referrals continue under original terms.
