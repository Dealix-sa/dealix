# Referral Terms System

## Purpose
Define simple, safe referral terms before formal partnerships.

## Referral Model
Start simple:
- referral tracked
- commission only on collected cash
- no commission on unpaid pipeline
- no authority to change price/scope
- no public claims without approval

## Possible Commission
- 5–10% of first collected project
- or fixed referral fee
- retainer commissions require separate approval

## Rules
- No referral payout before cash collected.
- No partner discount without CEO approval.
- No partner promise outside approved scope.
- Every partner lead enters `pipeline_tracker.csv`.
- Every partner source tracked in `partners/partner_pipeline.csv`.
