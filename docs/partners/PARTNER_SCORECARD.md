# Partner Scorecard

How each partner is measured, monthly.

## Volume
- Qualified intros made.
- Joint pursuits opened.
- Joint pursuits closed.
- White-label engagements opened and delivered.

## Quality
- Win rate on intros vs Dealix-direct baseline.
- Average deal size on partner-sourced revenue.
- Retention rate on partner-sourced clients.
- Claim Guard incidents from partner activity.

## Behaviour
- Responsiveness to joint working sessions.
- Compliance with Referral Terms and White-Label Rules.
- Quality of the partner's own client base.

## Banding
- **Tier 1** — strategic, joint go-to-market.
- **Tier 2** — active referral and co-sell.
- **Tier 3** — opportunistic.
- **Inactive** — no activity in 90 days; relationship paused.

## Cadence
- Monthly partner scorecard updated.
- Quarterly partner review meeting with each Tier 1 and Tier 2 partner.

## Rule
A partner with no scorecard activity is not a partner. Pause kindly and reassess.
