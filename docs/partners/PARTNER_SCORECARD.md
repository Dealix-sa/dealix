# Partner Scorecard

> 90-day evaluation of every partner relationship.

## Metrics

| Metric | Target (90d) |
|--------|---------------|
| Named introductions sent | ≥ 6 |
| Qualified leads (ICP-fit) | ≥ 3 |
| Closed referrals (paid) | ≥ 1 |
| Trust incidents involving partner-sourced customers | 0 |
| Partner responsiveness (days to reply) | ≤ 3 |

## Quality Checks

- ICP fit of partner intros.
- Quality of context partner provides ahead of intro.
- Whether partner has cited Dealix accurately (no overclaim).

## Outcomes Map

| 90-day result | Decision |
|---------------|----------|
| All targets hit | Continue; consider deeper joint work |
| 1–2 targets missed but trajectory healthy | Continue with adjustments |
| 3+ targets missed or any trust incident | Graceful de-list |

## Documentation

- `dealix-ops-private/partners/scorecards/<partner>/<period>.md`

## Review

- Each partner scored quarterly.
- Top 3 partners reported in `BOARD_PACK_TEMPLATE.md`.

## Anti-Patterns

- Keeping a non-producing partner because the founder likes them.
- Renegotiating commission upward to "incentivise more intros" (no — fix
  the relationship or end it).
- Adding more partners while existing partners are inactive.
