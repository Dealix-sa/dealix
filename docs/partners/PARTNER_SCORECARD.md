# Partner Scorecard — بطاقة أداء الشريك

## Purpose
Quarterly snapshot of partner performance. Drives keep/pause/remove decisions. Same metrics every partner, same formula every quarter.

## Metrics (per partner per quarter)

| Metric | What it measures | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Warm intros submitted | Count of valid introductions per `REFERRAL_TERMS.md` qualification rules | Partner folder log |
| Pipeline conversion | % of intros that became discovery calls | Sales pipeline |
| Closed deals | Count of deals closed from this partner's intros this quarter | Closed-deal log |
| Closed-deal revenue (SAR) | Sum of first-invoice values for closed deals | Invoice register |
| Lead quality score | Average qualification score of submitted intros per `docs/sales/QUALIFICATION_SCORE.md` | Pipeline log |
| Process compliance | Number of process incidents (off-brand claims, unauthorized scope/price talk, late docs) | Founder log |
| White-label audit (if applicable) | Pass / fail on latest audit per `WHITE_LABEL_RULES.md` | Audit memo |
| Tone / brand alignment | Founder qualitative rating 1-5 | Founder note |

## Bands

| Tier | Trigger | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Top partner | ≥2 closed deals in the quarter AND zero process incidents AND tone ≥4 | Continue; consider strategic-partner conversation |
| Active partner | ≥1 closed deal OR ≥3 valid intros AND no critical incidents | Continue; quarterly check-in |
| Dormant | No intros and no deals for 2 quarters | Pause with notice; archive after 1 more quarter |
| At risk | Process incidents present OR white-label audit fail | Remediation plan within 14 days or remove |

## Rules
- A scorecard exists for every active partner each quarter, even with no activity. "Inactive" is a status, not a missing record.
- Process incidents trump quantity. A partner who closed three deals with a banned-claim post is at risk, not top.
- Removal is not punitive; it documents that the relationship did not produce value.

## Operations
- Quarterly: founder writes a one-page scorecard per partner, ≤500 words.
- Stored under `docs/partners/active/<partner-label>/scorecards/YYYY-Qn.md`.
- Outcomes summarized in the quarterly partner portfolio review.

## Evidence
- Each metric value links to its source.
- Decisions (keep/pause/remove) logged with date and rationale.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: quarterly.

## القسم العربي

**المقاييس:** تعريفات دافئة مُقدّمة، تحويل pipeline (% للمكالمات الاكتشاف)، صفقات مُغلقة، إيراد الصفقات (ريال)، نقاط جودة الـlead، الالتزام بالعملية، تدقيق White-label إن وُجد، اتساق النبرة 1-5.

**الفئات:** شريك ممتاز (≥صفقتان، صفر حوادث، نبرة ≥4). نشط (صفقة أو ≥3 تعريفات، لا حوادث حرجة). خامل (لا نشاط ربعين). في خطر (حوادث عملية أو فشل تدقيق).

**القواعد:** بطاقة لكل شريك نشط ربعيًا حتى لو صفر نشاط. الحوادث تتجاوز الكم. الإزالة توثيقية لا عقابية.

**التشغيل:** ربعيًا المؤسس يكتب بطاقة صفحة واحدة ≤500 كلمة لكل شريك. تُلخّص في مراجعة المحفظة.

**المالك:** المؤسس. **الإيقاع:** ربعي.
