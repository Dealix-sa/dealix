# Partner Onboarding Process — عملية تأهيل الشريك

> Note: A pre-existing checklist lives at `PARTNER_ONBOARDING.md`. This document defines the surrounding process, approval levels, and asset-sharing rules. The two are meant to be read together.

## Purpose
First 30 days of a new partner relationship: what gets signed, what gets shared, what approval levels apply. Avoids ad-hoc enablement and undocumented expectations.

## Pre-onboarding (signature)
- ICP fit confirmed per [`PARTNER_PROFILE.md`](PARTNER_PROFILE.md).
- Referral terms signed per [`REFERRAL_TERMS.md`](REFERRAL_TERMS.md).
- NDA signed.
- KYC light: company registration, beneficial owner, contact details on file.
- Partner folder created `docs/partners/active/<partner-label>/`.

## Week 1 — orientation
- 60-minute orientation call with founder. Agenda:
  1. Dealix services and what we deliver vs do not.
  2. Banned claims and content rules.
  3. Referral process: how to introduce, what to expect.
  4. Confidentiality and data handling.
  5. Quality standards and timelines.
- Provide partner with public-facing assets only.

## Week 2 — process drill
- Walk through a sample referral and a sample close (fees, proof).
- Confirm escalation channel and named contact on each side.

## Week 3 — first referral attempted
- Partner is encouraged (not required) to submit a first warm introduction.
- Founder runs the discovery call; partner observes if permitted by the prospect.
- Joint debrief after the call.

## Week 4 — review
- 30-day review meeting; outcomes logged.
- If alignment is poor, relationship paused or closed.

## Assets shared (controlled list)
- Public one-pager.
- Currently-published sector reports.
- Standard introduction email template.
- A short "what to say / what not to say" guide derived from [`FOUNDER_VOICE.md`](../content/FOUNDER_VOICE.md) and [`CONTENT_STRATEGY.md`](../content/CONTENT_STRATEGY.md).

Not shared: internal prompts, templates, code, eval suites, client list, case studies beyond public versions, proof library, pricing floors or internal margins.

## Approval levels referenced
- New partner approval: founder (after ICP check).
- Pricing exception requested by partner: A2 written.
- White-label request: gated per [`WHITE_LABEL_RULES.md`](WHITE_LABEL_RULES.md), separate process.
- Co-published content: A2 written and follows `docs/content/CONTENT_COMMAND_CENTER.md`.

## Rules
- A partner not actively delivering or referring after 90 days is paused with notice.
- Onboarding does not entitle a partner to white-label or co-branded marketing.
- Partner contact details and PII handled per `docs/governance/PDPL_DATA_RULES.md`.

## Operations
- Onboarding checklist tracked in `PARTNER_ONBOARDING.md` and supplemented by this process doc.

## Evidence
- Signed terms, signed NDA, orientation notes, 30-day review note.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: per partner; 30-day review.

## القسم العربي

**ملاحظة:** قائمة تحقق سابقة موجودة في `PARTNER_ONBOARDING.md`. هذا المستند يحدد العملية والموافقات وقواعد مشاركة الأصول. يُقرآن معًا.

**قبل التأهيل:** توافق ICP مؤكد، شروط إحالة موقّعة، NDA موقّعة، KYC خفيف، مجلد شريك.

**الأسبوع 1 — التوجيه:** مكالمة 60 دقيقة بأجندة: خدماتنا، الادعاءات الممنوعة، الإحالة، السرية، الجودة. أصول علنية فقط.

**الأسبوع 2 — تدريب:** سيناريو إحالة وإغلاق، تأكيد التصعيد والمسميات.

**الأسبوع 3 — أول محاولة:** تعريف دافئ، المؤسس يدير الاكتشاف، debrief مشترك.

**الأسبوع 4 — مراجعة:** اجتماع 30 يومًا، توثيق، إيقاف أو إغلاق إن لزم.

**الأصول المسلّمة:** one-pager علني، تقارير منشورة، قالب بريد تعريف، دليل قصير للنبرة. غير مسلّم: prompts، كود، قائمة عملاء، case studies غير علنية، حدود تسعير داخلية.

**القواعد:** شريك خامل بعد 90 يومًا يُعلّق. التأهيل لا يمنح white-label. PII وفق `PDPL_DATA_RULES.md`.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
