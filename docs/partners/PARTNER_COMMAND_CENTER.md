# Partner Command Center

## Purpose
Use partners to create qualified referrals without losing trust or focus.

## Owner
Sami / Partnership owner.

## Review Cadence
Monthly until active partner pipeline exists.

## Inputs
- partner candidates
- referral terms
- partner fit
- proof assets
- client success stories

## Outputs
- partner pipeline
- referral agreement
- partner onboarding
- referral source tracking

## Rules
- Partners cannot claim guaranteed revenue.
- Partners cannot change scope.
- Partners cannot offer discounts without approval.
- Every referral must enter pipeline_tracker.csv.

## Metrics
- qualified partner leads
- referrals
- partner-sourced proposals
- partner-sourced revenue

## Evidence
- partner_pipeline.csv
- referral notes
- proposal source
- payment records
