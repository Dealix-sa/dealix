## Rules

1. **No partnership before proof.** Until we have ≥ 2 completed sprints
   and at least one approved case study, partnerships are deferred.
2. **No exclusivity.** We do not sign exclusive partnership clauses.
3. **All commercial terms in writing.** No verbal commissions.
4. **Trust applies equally.** Partner-introduced customers receive the
   same governance.

## Metrics

- Partner-sourced revenue (this month, MTD)
- Conversion rate of partner referrals vs cold pipeline
- Partner-sourced retainer count
- Partner satisfaction (do they continue to refer?)
