# Partner Profile

The information we collect about every partner before any joint motion.

## Required
- Legal entity and CR.
- Primary contact and decision-maker.
- Sectors and client list (anonymised acceptable).
- Existing tools and processes.
- Referenceable clients (with permission).
- Any conflicts of interest.

## Trust check
- Public reputation review.
- Sanctions and suppression screening.
- Past public claims reviewed for over-claim risk.

## Fit assessment
- Does the partner sell to Dealix's ICP?
- Does the partner's delivery quality match Dealix's quality bar?
- Does the partner respect the trust posture?

## Storage
- `dealix-ops-private/partners/<partner-slug>/profile.md`.

## Rule
The profile is the bouncer. No profile, no partnership.
