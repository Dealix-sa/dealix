# Outreach Cadence

The default sequence for Saudi B2B outbound.

## Day 1
Personal founder message. Specific to the prospect's business. One sentence of context, one sentence of value, one CTA.

## Day 3
Soft follow-up with one new piece of specific value (a relevant data point, a market signal, a question).

## Day 7
Send a mini insight or a sample angle. This may be the Signal Sample itself if the prospect is high-fit.

## Day 14
Final polite close. Acknowledge no response, leave the door open, do not pressure.

## After day 14
Move to nurture via content. No further direct outbound for 90 days unless the prospect re-engages.

## Channels
- LinkedIn DMs to a public profile.
- Business email where the prospect's business publishes one.
- WhatsApp only if the prospect provides the number or it is publicly listed as the business number.

## Hard rules
- No aggressive spam. No more than four touches in 14 days.
- No false urgency ("last chance", "limited spots").
- No guaranteed-outcome claims.
- Every send is logged for audit and suppression handling.
