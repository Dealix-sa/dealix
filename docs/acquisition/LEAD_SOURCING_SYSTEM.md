# Lead Sourcing System
## Purpose
Create a repeatable way to find qualified Saudi B2B leads.
## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.
## Review Cadence
Weekly.
## Sources
- LinkedIn search.
- Google search.
- Saudi business directories.
- industry lists.
- chamber / event pages.
- vendor ecosystems.
- partner lists.
- competitor customer pages where public.
- founder network.
## Lead Quality Criteria
A lead is qualified only if:
- sector matches current ICP.
- company has clear B2B offer.
- likely buyer exists.
- Saudi relevance exists.
- next_action can be defined.
## Lead Fields
- company
- sector
- contact
- stage
- priority
- next_action
- last_touch
- notes
## Priority Rules
A = strong ICP + clear buyer + strong relevance.
B = likely fit but needs research.
C = weak but possible.
Lost = disqualified with reason.
## Weekly Target
25 qualified leads.
