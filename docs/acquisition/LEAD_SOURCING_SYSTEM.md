# Lead Sourcing System

How Dealix finds Saudi B2B prospects without spam or scraping abuse.

## Allowed sources
- Public business registries (CR lookups, public ministry data).
- Public LinkedIn profiles and company pages.
- Industry directories and chamber-of-commerce lists.
- Public news, funding announcements, and sector reports.
- Inbound from founder content and referrals.

## Prohibited sources
- Purchased lists of personal contact data.
- Scraped private platforms or private WhatsApp groups.
- Any source that violates the platform's terms of service.

## Process
1. **Define** the sector and ICP for the week (set in the Weekly CEO Review).
2. **Source** 50–100 candidate companies from allowed sources.
3. **Enrich** with public data: size estimate, sector, public contact, public signals.
4. **Score** with `ICP_SCORING_MODEL.md`.
5. **Park** anything under 60. **Promote** anything 60+ to outreach.
6. **Log** the source for each lead — required for audit.

## Rule
Every lead has a recorded source. A lead without a source cannot be contacted.
