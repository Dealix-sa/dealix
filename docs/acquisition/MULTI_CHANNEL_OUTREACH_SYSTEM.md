# Multi-Channel Outreach System

## Purpose

Use multiple channels without spam or over-automation.

## Channels

### Email / Contact Form
Best for:
- company-level contact
- partnership/sales inquiry
- low-risk first touch

### LinkedIn Manual DM
Best for:
- founder-led outreach
- decision makers
- warm context

### Partner Introduction
Best for:
- higher trust
- faster conversion

### Content / Inbound
Best for:
- trust building
- authority
- proof-based demand

### WhatsApp
Use only when:
- business contact is public
- message is relevant
- no mass sending
- opt-out respected

## Rules

- No bulk spam.
- No irrelevant messages.
- No guaranteed claims.
- Stop if declined.
- Log channel and source.
- Prefer drafts/approval before sending.

## Channel Cadence

- Day 0: first touch.
- Day 2: follow-up.
- Day 7: sample/value follow-up.
- Day 14: close/recycle.
