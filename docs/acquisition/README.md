# Acquisition

## Purpose
Define how Dealix sources, qualifies, and prioritises the right accounts to enter the Revenue Loop.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO (until a dedicated acquisition owner is named).

## Review Cadence
Weekly during the first 90 days, then monthly.

## Inputs
- ICP definition.
- Sector watchlists.
- Public signals (websites, hiring, news, regulator filings).
- Warm-list referrals.
- Past replies and call notes.

## Outputs
- Qualified lead list.
- A/B/C priority per lead.
- Reason for inclusion per lead.
- Entry to the Revenue Loop at stage "New".

## Rules
- No lead is added without a clear reason for inclusion.
- No sensitive personal data is captured beyond what is needed to make contact.
- Suppression/opt-out is respected before any contact is attempted.
- A-priority leads require evidence of fit, not opinion.

## Metrics
- Leads sourced per week.
- A/B/C distribution.
- Reply rate by sector and by message.
- Cost per qualified lead (founder hours).

## Evidence
- private pipeline tracker.
- sector experiment notes.
- weekly intelligence review.
