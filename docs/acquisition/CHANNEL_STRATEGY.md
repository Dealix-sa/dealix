# Channel Strategy

> Which channels, what mix, kill switches per channel.
> Channel choice = where the founder spends acquisition hours.

## Active Channels (this quarter)

| Channel | % of acquisition hours | Lead share target |
|---|---|---|
| Warm founder outreach (LinkedIn / email) | 50% | 60% of leads |
| Public learning content (LinkedIn posts, sector reports) | 25% | 20% of leads |
| Referrals + advisor network | 15% | 15% of leads |
| Partner channel | 10% | 5% of leads |
| Paid acquisition | 0% | 0% (DEFERRED) |

## Channel Detail

### 1. Warm Founder Outreach
- **Definition:** Personal LinkedIn DMs + emails from the founder, drafted or agent-drafted then approved
- **Volume:** 25 outreaches per week to fresh prospects
- **Cost:** Founder time only (~12 hr/week)
- **Kill switch:** Reply rate < 5% sustained for 2 weeks → diagnose ICP / message before changing volume

### 2. Public Learning Content
- **Definition:** Weekly LinkedIn post (one per week) + one quarterly sector report
- **Volume:** 1 weekly post (Thu), 1 sector report per quarter
- **Cost:** Founder + AI draft (~2 hr/week post, ~12 hr per quarter for report)
- **Kill switch:** No inbound from content after 6 weeks → reformat (video, carousel, podcast appearances)

### 3. Referrals + Advisor Network
- **Definition:** Explicit referral asks at end of every closed sprint + quarterly advisor sync
- **Volume:** 1 ask per closed deal + 1 advisor sync/quarter
- **Cost:** ~1 hr per ask + 2 hr per advisor sync
- **Kill switch:** Zero referrals after 5 closed sprints → revisit handoff script

### 4. Partner Channel
- **Definition:** Accountants, consultants, cloud partners serving same ICP
- **Volume:** 1 new partner conversation per month
- **Cost:** ~3 hr per partner conversation
- **Kill switch:** No partner-sourced lead by end of quarter → DEFER

### 5. Paid Acquisition
- **Definition:** Paid LinkedIn / Google / sector-specific ads
- **Status:** DEFERRED until:
  - 3 paid sprints delivered
  - CAC modelable from current data
  - LTV from at least one retainer
- **Why not now:** Without baseline conversion data, paid spend is gambling

## Channel Mix Discipline

- No channel exceeds 50% of acquisition hours (concentration risk)
- No channel goes below its kill threshold for > 2 weeks without action
- New channels require Strategy Filter pass + a 30-day experiment in `learning/EXPERIMENT_LOG.md`

## Why This Mix

- **Warm outreach** is the highest leverage channel for founder-led mid-market
- **Content** is the moat compounder (proof + reputation)
- **Referrals** validate the model and lower CAC
- **Partners** unlock segments founder can't reach alone
- **Paid** is too early — it would burn money without insight

## Channel Quality Bar

Every channel must produce leads that:
- Pass the ICP scoring threshold (60+)
- Are unique (deduped against pipeline)
- Have a real buyer name (no "info@ company.com")
- Came from an allow-listed source

## Channel ROI Tracking

Per channel, monthly:
- Leads sourced
- Leads qualified
- Calls booked
- Proposals sent
- Paid customers
- Cost (founder hours × hour cost OR ad spend)
- ROI = revenue closed / cost

Logged in `docs/learning/MONTHLY_STRATEGY_UPDATE.md`.

## Channel Experiment Process

To test a new channel:
1. Hypothesis written in `learning/EXPERIMENT_LOG.md`
2. Budget cap: ≤ 8 founder hours OR ≤ SAR 500 (for paid)
3. Duration: 30 days
4. Success metric defined up front
5. Weekly check-ins in Weekly CEO Review
6. End-of-experiment decision: adopt, iterate, kill (logged)

## Forbidden Channels (this year)

- Cold WhatsApp (suppression risk + platform ban)
- Cold SMS (compliance + platform ban)
- TV / radio / billboards (wrong format)
- Conference sponsorships > SAR 5K (wrong scale)
- Influencer partnerships (signal mismatch — we're operator, not lifestyle)
- Webinars without a clear ICP attached

## Review Cadence

- Weekly: channel mix vs target
- Monthly: kill-switch evaluation per channel
- Quarterly: rewrite channel mix
