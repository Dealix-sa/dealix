# Follow-up Rules

The follow-up is where most opportunities are won — and where most companies look desperate.

## Frequency caps
- Cold prospect: max 4 touches in 14 days. Then nurture for 90 days.
- Replied-once prospect: max 3 touches in 21 days. Then quarterly check-in.
- Active proposal: max 2 reminders before the proposal expires.
- Existing client: cadence defined in the retainer plan, not in this file.

## Tone
- Helpful, never anxious.
- Add value on each touch (a number, an insight, a question).
- Never reference the previous unanswered messages as a complaint.

## When to stop
- Explicit "no" from the prospect.
- "Not now" with no opening — move to nurture, no further direct contact for 90 days.
- The prospect lands on the suppression list.
- Four touches with no reply.

## Logging
Every follow-up logged with date, channel, and content reference. Used to audit suppression and frequency caps.

## Rule
A follow-up that does not add value is a complaint. Do not send complaints.
