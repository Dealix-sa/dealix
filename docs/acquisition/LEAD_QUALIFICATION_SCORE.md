# Lead Qualification Score
## Purpose
Score leads before outreach so Dealix focuses on highest-probability opportunities.
## Score Categories
| Category | Points |
|---|---:|
| B2B clear offer | 20 |
| Saudi relevance | 20 |
| medium/high-ticket potential | 20 |
| clear decision maker | 15 |
| visible growth/sales need | 15 |
| strong sector fit | 10 |
## Score Meaning
- 80–100 = A priority.
- 60–79 = B priority.
- 40–59 = C priority.
- Below 40 = reject or defer.
## Rules
- A-priority needs evidence.
- Do not send custom sample to C-priority unless strategic reason exists.
- Low score leads can remain in backlog but not active sprint.
## Evidence
- pipeline_tracker.csv
- lead notes
- source links
