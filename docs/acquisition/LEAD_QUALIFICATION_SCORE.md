# Lead Qualification Score

## Purpose
Score a new lead 0–100 to decide whether to invest founder time.

## Scoring rubric
| Dimension | 0 | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sector fit | Anti-ICP | Tangential | Secondary ICP | Primary ICP | Best-fit sector |
| Pain clarity | No pain signal | Generic | Specific KPI mentioned | Quantified loss | Quantified loss + deadline |
| Decision power | Junior | IC | Manager | Director | Owner / GM |
| Buying ability | Pre-revenue | Small | Mid-market | Mid-market with budget | Confirmed budget owner |
| Speed signal | Slow / lurker | Following | Engaged | Asking questions | Asking to buy |

Max score: 100.

## Decision thresholds
- ≥ 75: Priority A, contact within 24 hours.
- 50–74: Priority B, contact within the week.
- 25–49: Priority C, contact when capacity allows.
- < 25: Park; revisit next quarter.

## Where the score lives
For each lead: include the score in the `notes` column of `pipeline/pipeline_tracker.csv` as `score=NN`.

## When to re-score
- After every conversation that changes a dimension.
- Always before drafting a proposal.

## Anti-bias note
Score before any pricing conversation. Once price enters the discussion, the buyer's verbal urgency can inflate "speed signal" — discount accordingly.
