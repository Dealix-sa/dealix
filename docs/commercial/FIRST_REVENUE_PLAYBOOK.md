# First Revenue Playbook

A short, honest path to the first paid SAR.

1. **Pick the offer:** Revenue Proof Sprint (499 SAR).
2. **Pick 10 warm names** you can reach without cold outreach.
3. **Generate drafts:** `python scripts/commercial/run_money_now_sprint.py`.
4. **Approve & send manually** — you send, not the system.
5. **Book a call**, diagnose one revenue leak.
6. **Send a manual invoice draft**, take manual payment.
7. **Record `payment_received`** in the evidence file.
8. **Deliver work**, assemble an honest **proof pack**.
9. **Ask for a referral** only after a delivered proof pack.

Language rule: use "we expect / the goal is / we will measure". Never
"guaranteed". Never fabricate results, clients, or logos.
