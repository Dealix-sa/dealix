# Partner & Referral Loop

## Partner / Distributor Desk
Draft partner intros for agencies/distributors with overlapping customer bases.
Setup + success fee. Intros are drafts; the founder sends after mutual interest.

## Referral loop
Ask for referrals **only** after a `proof_pack_delivered` event exists for the
client. Prompts are drafts, opt-in, and manual — never mass or automated.
