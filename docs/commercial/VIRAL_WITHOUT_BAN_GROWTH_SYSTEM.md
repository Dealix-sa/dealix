# Viral Without Ban — Growth System

Grow by **value**, not by spam. Every output is a draft; distribution is manual.

## Allowed loops
1. Saudi Opportunity Snapshots
2. Revenue Leak Scanner checklists
3. B2G Readiness checklists
4. Proof Pack weekly insights
5. Referral prompts (post-delivery only)
6. Partner intro drafts
7. SEO market reports
8. LinkedIn / X / newsletter / video drafts
9. One-page PDF lead magnets
10. Manual high-signal outbound

## Blocked
Cold WhatsApp blasts · mass LinkedIn automation · fake personalization · fake
proof · fake urgency · government-influence claims · guaranteed-revenue claims ·
scraping-prohibited sources · no opt-out.

The content queue always ends with a soft CTA to request a custom Snapshot. It is
never auto-posted.
