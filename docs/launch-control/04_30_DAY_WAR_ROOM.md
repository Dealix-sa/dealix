# 04 — 30-Day War Room

خطة 30 يوم فعلية للتحول من تدشين إلى إيراد. كل أسبوع له هدف ومخرجات قابلة للقياس.
A real 30-day plan to move from launch to revenue. Every week has a goal and measurable outputs.

## Week 1 — Foundation & first contact
- Website polish (run `100_SITE_MANUAL_QA_CHECKLIST.md`).
- Build first **100 real leads** into the CRM (manual, consent-based).
- Founder reviews **top 50** drafts and approves a subset.
- Publish **5 founder posts** manually from the calendar.
- Sell the **first diagnostics** (paid).

## Week 2 — Iterate on signal
- Improve copy based on real replies.
- Run **400 drafts daily** (review-only).
- Hold discovery calls.
- Deliver the first diagnostics.
- Send first **pilot proposals**.

## Week 3 — Proof & pilots
- Build case-style proof assets (anonymized).
- Execute pilots.
- Open retainer conversations.
- Turn learnings into content.

## Week 4 — Scale winners
- Scale the winning verticals.
- Update the offer ladder.
- Build a referral loop.
- Prepare the monthly board report.

## Weekly cadence
| Day | Focus |
|---|---|
| Mon | Plan week, refresh leads, publish post |
| Tue–Thu | Outreach (manual, approved) + discovery calls |
| Fri | Delivery + proposals |
| Sat | Content + proof assets |
| Sun | Metrics review + next-week plan |

> Doctrine reminder: every outreach is **manual and approved**. No bulk send, no automation.
