# 30-Day War Room — Dealix Launch — غرفة عمليات الثلاثين يومًا

The first 30 days after launch, week by week. The goal is evidenced opportunities, not promised numbers. Every external action stays manual and founder-approved.

## Operating principle — مبدأ التشغيل

The system drafts, ranks, and recommends. The founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. No week in this plan enables external sending by the system.

## Week 1 — Stabilize the launch — تثبيت الإطلاق

**Focus:** confirm the public site holds and the review queue works.

- [ ] Verify all public routes daily; fix any Red item immediately.
- [ ] Confirm the 400 review-only drafts are accessible and correctly flagged.
- [ ] Run the daily command center loop (`05_DAILY_COMMAND_CENTER.md`).
- [ ] Manually post planned media/social items; no automation.
- [ ] Log every inbound intake as a founder review item.

**Exit:** site stable, queue working, zero send incidents.

## Week 2 — First conversations — المحادثات الأولى

**Focus:** discovery calls and paid diagnostics.

- [ ] Book and run discovery calls from inbound intake.
- [ ] Deliver paid diagnostics per the SOP.
- [ ] Send proposals manually after founder review.
- [ ] Track each opportunity as evidenced, not guaranteed.

**Exit:** first diagnostics delivered, first proposals out.

## Week 3 — Convert to pilots — التحويل إلى تجارب قيادية

**Focus:** move qualified diagnostics into pilots.

- [ ] Scope pilots against documented pilot scope.
- [ ] Confirm no sensitive data is processed before agreements are signed.
- [ ] Prepare delivery per `docs/03_commercial_mvp/` SOPs.
- [ ] Keep all external messages manual and approved.

**Exit:** at least one pilot scoped and agreed (target, not guarantee).

## Week 4 — Review and harden — المراجعة والتقوية

**Focus:** review the launch and lock what worked.

- [ ] Review the evidence pack; refresh artifacts if the build changed.
- [ ] Capture case-safe patterns; no real names, no PII.
- [ ] Tune the daily loop and the failure playbook from real incidents.
- [ ] Decide what graduates from review-only toward standard delivery.

**Exit:** 30-day review complete, decisions recorded.

## War room cadence — إيقاع غرفة العمليات

| Cadence | What | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Daily | Command center loop | Founder |
| Weekly | Scorecard refresh + week exit | Founder |
| On incident | Failure response playbook | Founder |

## Guardrails for all 30 days — ضوابط الثلاثين يومًا

- No automated sending. No bulk sending. No cold outreach.
- No paid ads live without tracking and compliance.
- No processing of sensitive data before an agreement.
- Every customer-facing figure carries the estimated-value disclosure.

## Arabic summary — ملخص عربي
