# Distribution Portfolio Operating System

## Purpose
Build Dealix distribution across multiple channels so growth does not depend on one channel.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Distribution Channels

### 1. Founder-Led Outbound
Targeted messages to approved B2B leads.

### 2. Contact Form Outreach
Low-risk company-level outreach through public contact forms.

### 3. LinkedIn Manual Outreach
Founder-led, high-context messages to decision makers.

### 4. Content-Led Inbound
Founder posts, sector insights, case studies, and operating lessons.

### 5. Partner Referrals
Agencies, consultants, vendors, and operators who can introduce B2B buyers.

### 6. Ecosystem Outreach
Events, directories, chambers, accelerators, and communities.

### 7. Client Expansion
Delivery → feedback → retainer → referral → proof.

### 8. Strategic Account Plays
High-value accounts with custom research and sample packs.

## Channel Rules
- Scale intelligence faster than sending.
- Scale sending only when follow-up capacity exists.
- No spam.
- No guaranteed revenue claims.
- No personal data misuse.
- Every channel has a scorecard.

## Weekly Portfolio Decision
Double Down / Maintain / Fix / Kill / Defer.

- `private-ops/distribution/channel_scorecard.csv`

## Compliance Anchors
- KSA CST anti-spam rules for messaging and calling activity.
- KSA PDPL for personal data handling and consent.
- Dealix governance: no guarantees, no automation of cold mass-send, human-approved workflows.
