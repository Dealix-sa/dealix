# Referral Engine OS

## Purpose
Create a controlled referral system that brings qualified B2B opportunities without overpromising.

## Referral Sources
- Consultants.
- Agency owners.
- ERP / CRM implementers.
- SaaS founders.
- Accountants.
- Legal / compliance advisors.
- Startup community operators.
- Chamber / event contacts.

## Referral Ask
> "I'm looking for Saudi B2B companies that sell high-ticket services or software and need better qualified opportunity flow."

## Referral Rules
- Commission only on collected cash if agreed.
- Partner cannot change price or scope.
- Partner cannot promise outcomes.
- Every referral is qualified.
- No public proof without approval.

## Weekly Target
- 10 referral asks.
- 2 partner conversations.
- 1 qualified referral.
