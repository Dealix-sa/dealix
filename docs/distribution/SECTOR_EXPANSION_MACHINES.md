# Sector Expansion Machines

## Purpose
Run one dedicated acquisition machine per priority sector.

## Sector 1 — ERP / CRM
Target:
- Odoo partners.
- Microsoft Dynamics partners.
- ERP implementers.
- CRM implementers.
- Business software providers.

## Sector 2 — Cybersecurity
Target:
- MSSPs.
- SOC providers.
- Cybersecurity consultants.
- Compliance / security vendors.

## Sector 3 — B2B Agencies
Target:
- B2B marketing agencies.
- Performance agencies.
- Web / dev agencies.
- Branding agencies.

## Sector 4 — Logistics / Industrial Services
Target:
- Logistics firms.
- Warehousing solutions.
- Supply chain services.
- Fleet technology providers.

## Sector 5 — Consulting / Implementation
Target:
- IT consultants.
- Digital transformation firms.
- Management consultants.
- Implementation partners.

## Per-Sector Output
- 100 researched leads.
- Top 25 approved lead candidates.
- 2 message angles.
- 1 sample idea.
- Sector scorecard.

## Weekly Decision
Double Down / Fix / Kill / Defer.

## 30-Day Build Plan
- Week 1: ERP/CRM 100 leads, Cybersecurity 50 leads.
- Week 2: Cybersecurity to 100, B2B Agencies 100.
- Week 3: Logistics 100, Consulting 100.
- Week 4: Clean, score, approve best 150. Send 25–50/day. Prepare samples and proposals.

## Daily Output Targets
- Research machine: 50–100 new lead records/day.
- Approval: 25–50 approved outreach/day.
- Conversion: 3–5 positive replies/week, 3 samples/week, 1 proposal/week minimum.
