# ABM Strategic Account Machine v2

## Relationship to existing docs
Works alongside, does not replace:
- `docs/ops/CEO_TOP50_TRACKER.csv` — the existing top-account tracker.
- `auto_client_acquisition/approval_center/` — every custom outreach to a strategic account passes the same approval surface as standard outreach.
- `docs/distribution/DISTRIBUTION_PORTFOLIO_V2.md` — Strategic Accounts is one channel in the portfolio.

## Purpose
Target high-value accounts with custom research and sample-first outreach.

## Monthly Output
- 10 strategic accounts
- 10 account briefs
- 10 custom outreach drafts
- 3 custom samples
- 1 proposal target

## Account Brief
- company profile
- offer
- buyer hypothesis
- pain hypothesis
- trigger events
- sample idea
- partner intro path
- outreach angle
- risk notes

## Rule
Strategic accounts get custom value before proposal. No proposal goes out before a sample or substantive value artifact has been shared and acknowledged.
