# Strategic Account Playbook

## Purpose
Create custom plays for high-value accounts.

## Use For
- Large ERP / CRM vendors.
- Strong cybersecurity firms.
- Large agencies.
- High-ticket B2B consultancies.

## Account Brief Template
- Company.
- Website.
- Offer.
- Target customers.
- Likely pain.
- Why Dealix fits.
- Sample idea.
- Outreach angle.
- Decision maker hypothesis.
- Risk notes.
- Next action.

## Rule
Strategic accounts get custom sample-first outreach, not generic batch messages.

## Cadence
- 1 strategic brief drafted per week (minimum).
- 1 sample-first touch executed per approved brief.
- Account brief is reviewed weekly in distribution review.
