# Sector Domination Playbook

## Purpose
Build repeatable market expertise in one sector at a time.

## Per Sector Target
- 100 accounts discovered
- 50 accounts enriched
- 25 A/B leads approved
- 25 outreach messages
- 5 replies
- 3 samples
- 1 proposal
- 1 payment path

## Sector Assets
- sector ICP
- buyer titles
- common pain
- message angles
- objection patterns
- sample template
- proposal variant
- proof angle
- partner list

## Sector Decision
Double Down:
if positive replies and sample interest.

Fix:
if replies exist but no samples.

Kill:
if no replies after quality outreach.

Defer:
if market is relevant but timing weak.

## Rule
Do not scale a sector until it produces conversion signal.
