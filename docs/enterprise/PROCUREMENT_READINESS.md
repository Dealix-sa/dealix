# Procurement Readiness

A checklist Dealix passes before procurement touches a deal. Every line here
exists because skipping it has cost time in past deals.

## Pre-procurement readiness

- [ ] Legal entity in good standing — see [`docs/legal/`](../legal/)
- [ ] Tax registration current — see [`docs/operating_finance/`](../operating_finance/)
- [ ] DPA template ready — [`docs/legal/DPA_TEMPLATE_AR.md`](../legal/DPA_TEMPLATE_AR.md)
- [ ] Sub-processor list current — [`docs/legal/SUB_PROCESSOR.md`](../legal/SUB_PROCESSOR.md)
- [ ] Insurance certificate available (on request)
- [ ] Banking details document (SAR account)
- [ ] DPO appointment letter — [`docs/legal/DPO_APPOINTMENT.md`](../legal/DPO_APPOINTMENT.md)
- [ ] Compliance certifications listed — [`docs/legal/COMPLIANCE_CERTIFICATIONS.md`](../legal/COMPLIANCE_CERTIFICATIONS.md)

## Deal-specific readiness

- [ ] Master Service Agreement (MSA) — [`docs/legal/ENTERPRISE_MSA_TEMPLATE.md`](../legal/ENTERPRISE_MSA_TEMPLATE.md)
- [ ] Statement of Work (SoW) drafted
- [ ] Payment milestones aligned to [`docs/revenue/INVOICE_FLOW.md`](../revenue/INVOICE_FLOW.md)
- [ ] DSAR response capability documented — [`docs/legal/DSAR_RESPONSE.md`](../legal/DSAR_RESPONSE.md)

## Owner

CEO (until the role is delegated per [`docs/people/HIRING_TRIGGER_SYSTEM.md`](../people/HIRING_TRIGGER_SYSTEM.md)).

## Non-negotiables

Procurement documents are signed only after deal-desk approval. No
commitment to payment terms or refunds outside the documented offers. See
[`docs/founder/DO_NOT_SAY.md`](../founder/DO_NOT_SAY.md).
