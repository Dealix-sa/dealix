that show up in every deal. Cuts the security-review stage from weeks to days.

## Cadence

- Versioned quarterly; reviewed during [`docs/founder/CEO_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md`](../founder/CEO_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md) quarterly cadence
- Updated immediately when any underlying doc changes

## Delivery

The packet is shared with the prospect's security/IT contact once
[STAKEHOLDER_MAP](STAKEHOLDER_MAP.md) confirms an active reviewer. Delivery
goes through the existing approval gates.
