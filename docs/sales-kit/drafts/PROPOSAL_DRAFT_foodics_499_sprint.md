## Problem statement — تشخيص المشكلة

Most Saudi B2B operators in SaaS Restaurant hold accounts and contacts but cannot reliably answer three questions: (1) which accounts to prioritize this week, (2) what to say to them in Arabic and English without compromising brand or compliance, (3) how to defend the recommendation publicly with evidence and an audit trail. This sprint produces governed answers to all three.

أغلب المُشغِّلين في قطاع SaaS Restaurant يملكون بيانات حسابات لكنهم لا يستطيعون الإجابة بثقة عن: من نتواصل معهم أولاً؟ ماذا نكتب لهم؟ وكيف نُدافع عن التوصية بدليل؟

## Inputs needed — المدخلات المطلوبة

- A CSV, CRM export, or manual list of accounts/contacts under your ownership.
- A named workflow owner on the Foodics side (sales lead / growth lead / founder).
- **A signed Source Passport** declaring data ownership, allowed use, and retention. This is non-negotiable; the sprint cannot begin without it.- A primary workflow declaration (one workflow per sprint).

---

## Deliverables — المخرجات (10 items)

1. Signed Source Passport on file.
2. Data Quality (DQ) baseline score across six dimensions.
3. Deduplication report with merge rules.
4. **10 ranked accounts** with explainable rubric (fit, signal strength, governance risk).
5. **Bilingual draft pack (AR + EN)** marked `draft_only` until your explicit approval.
6. Governance decisions log (7-decision matrix).
7. Redaction and block summary.
8. **14-section Proof Pack** with computed proof score (target ≥ **80**).
9. **At least one Capital Ledger asset** (reusable rule, template, or insight).
10. Bilingual handoff session (60 minutes) walking through the Proof Pack.
