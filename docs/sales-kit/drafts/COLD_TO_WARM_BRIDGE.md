# Cold-to-Warm Bridge — تحويل القائمة الباردة إلى دافئة

> The 20 named CEOs in `docs/ops/pipeline_tracker.csv` (Lucidya, Foodics, Salla, Lean, BRKZ, and others) are **COLD**. The 11 non-negotiables forbid cold-contacting them directly — no cold DM, no cold WhatsApp, no LinkedIn automation, no scraped intro. This document is the only sanctioned way to move them into the pipeline.

---

## 1. The principle — المبدأ

A cold name becomes a warm lead only when **the lead initiates contact** or **a real person introduces them**. Dealix never initiates. The three legal bridges:

1. **Inbound content engagement** — the founder publishes governed, bilingual content; the cold prospect engages (comment, react, reply); the founder responds *to their engagement* — that is now a warm, consent-based thread.
2. **Mutual-connection referral** — the founder asks a warm contact who knows the prospect for a named introduction. The warm contact sends the intro; the prospect opts in by replying.
3. **Free Diagnostic funnel** — the prospect submits the public diagnostic intake form themselves. Submission is the consent event; the engagement is warm from the first message.

If none of these three fire, the prospect stays cold and is not contacted. There is no fourth path.

---

## 2. The mechanics — آلية العمل

| Bridge | Founder action (allowed) | What is forbidden |
|---|---|---|
| Inbound content | Publish 600–800 word bilingual LinkedIn posts ending in one CTA to the free diagnostic. Reply only to people who engage first. | Tagging cold prospects to force visibility; DMing everyone who likes a post. |
| Referral ask | Ask warm contacts: "Do you know [name] at [company]? Would you introduce us?" The warm contact sends the intro. | Asking a contact for the prospect's number to message them directly. |
| Diagnostic funnel | Point all content and warm conversations at `dealix.sa` diagnostic intake. Prospect submits. | Pre-filling the form for them; treating a scraped email as an intake. |

Every warmed lead is logged with the bridge used and the consent event (engagement timestamp, referrer name, or intake submission ID) in the engagement ledger.

## 4. The 30-day bridge cadence — الإيقاع

- **Week 1:** Run the 20-contact warm motion (Warm Outreach Kit). Publish LinkedIn post #1. Identify mutual connections to the 5 priority CEOs.
- **Week 2:** Send referral-intro asks to warm contacts who know the priority CEOs. Publish LinkedIn post #2. Begin delivering free diagnostics for any warm leads who submitted intake.
- **Week 3:** Publish LinkedIn post #3 (a sanitised diagnostic case study, once the first diagnostic is delivered and consented). Follow up only on inbound engagement.
- **Week 4:** Review which cold names warmed via which bridge. Re-run qualification on each newly warm lead before any proposal.

No cold prospect is contacted directly at any point. Every move is a draft for founder approval.
