# 01 — Warm Outreach Framework — إطار التواصل مع القائمة الدافئة

> A personalizable bilingual (AR + EN) outreach-draft framework. The founder supplies
> 20–50 **real warm contacts** later and personalizes one draft per contact.
> **Every draft is for the founder to review and send manually. Nothing is sent by Dealix.**

---

## 1. What "warm" means here — تعريف "الدافئ"

A warm contact is a person the founder has **met at least once** in person or by a named
introduction. Personal relationship is the consent basis.

This framework **refuses**, by design:

- Cold outreach to strangers, purchased lists, or scraped lists.
- WhatsApp/LinkedIn automation, bulk sends, or "spray and pray."
- A new channel for the first message — use the channel the relationship already uses.
- Any "I found you on LinkedIn" introduction.

If you want to reach someone you have *not* met, that is a referral request to a mutual
contact — not an outreach draft. The framework does not generate stranger messages.

---

## 2. The message anatomy — تشريح الرسالة

Every warm draft has exactly four parts. Keep the whole message under 90 words.

| Part | Purpose | Length |
|------|---------|--------|
| **A. Personal anchor** | Names the real, shared context. Proves this is not a blast. | 1 line |
| **B. The one-line ask** | What you are building + the specific, small request. | 1 line |
| **C. The offer frame** | Free diagnostic first; the 499 SAR sprint is optional and named. | 1 line |
| **D. The open question** | One question only. The contact picks the answer space. | 1 line |

Rules:
- One question only. No deck, no PDF, no calendar link in the first message.
- Name the 499 SAR figure if pricing comes up; never negotiate price in chat.
- No guaranteed-outcome language. No "we will get you X deals."
- Match the contact's language (AR or EN) — do not send both.

---

## 3. The personalization slots — خانات التخصيص

Fill these per contact before drafting. Keep a row per contact in your tracker.

| Slot | Example | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| `{{contact_name}}` | "Abu Faisal" / "أبو فيصل" | Use the name the relationship uses. |
| `{{shared_context}}` | "from the Riyadh SME meetup in March" | The real anchor. If you cannot fill this, the contact is not warm. |
| `{{their_business}}` | "real-estate brokerage" / "وساطة عقارية" | Their sector, in their words. |
| `{{observed_signal}}` | "you mentioned chasing leads manually" | Optional. A real pain you heard them say. Leave blank if none — do not invent. |
| `{{channel}}` | WhatsApp or email | Whatever the relationship already uses. |

## 4. The master template — القالب الأساسي

### English

> Hi {{contact_name}} — good to reconnect since {{shared_context}}.
> I am building Dealix, a governed revenue-operations service for Saudi B2B companies:
> it finds and scores your best revenue opportunities and drafts approval-ready
> Arabic + English outreach — and nothing sends without your sign-off.
> I am looking for 2–3 companies to run a **free diagnostic**, with an optional paid
> **7-day sprint at 499 SAR** after it.
> Do you know a {{their_business}}-type company this might be useful for — or would it
> be useful for you?

### العربية

> أهلاً {{contact_name}} — سعيد بالتواصل من جديد بعد {{shared_context}}.
> أبني Dealix، خدمة عمليات إيرادات مُحوكَمة لشركات B2B السعودية: تجد وتُرتّب أفضل فرص
> الإيرادات لديك وتُجهّز مسودات تواصل عربية وإنجليزية جاهزة للاعتماد — ولا تُرسَل أي رسالة
> دون موافقتك.
> أبحث عن 2–3 شركات تجرّب **تشخيصاً مجانياً**، ويليه اختيارياً **سبرنت مدفوع 7 أيام بـ 499
> ريال**.
> هل تعرف شركة في مجال {{their_business}} قد يفيدها هذا — أو هل يفيدك أنت؟

If you have a real `{{observed_signal}}`, swap the first line of the body for:
"Last time we spoke you mentioned {{observed_signal}} — that is exactly the problem
Dealix is built to give you a governed handle on."

## 6. Reply handling — معالجة الردود

| Reply pattern | Founder action |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Interested** ("tell me more", names a company, asks for a call) | Within 24h (not sooner than 1h), reply offering the diagnostic intake link **or** a 20-minute discovery call. Then run the script in file 02. |
| **Asks for detail** (deliverables, pricing, sample) | One short reply, up to two links, never an attachment. A third back-and-forth without a call or intake = log as low-intent, leave the door open. |
| **Not interested** | Thank them, log it, never push. Offer to keep them on the no-automation general radar. Mention the referral program if natural. |
| **No reply in 7 days** | Leave the contact alone. Re-engage only on a natural occasion. No "circling back" follow-ups. |

## 7. Logging — التوثيق

Log each contact **at send time** with: channel, language, timestamp, and
`relationship_basis` (the real shared context — this is your consent record). One
outreach per contact. Contact 21 onward requires a fresh approval pass.
