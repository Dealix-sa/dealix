# Expansion Playbook — دليل التوسّع

How Dealix moves an account up the offer ladder honestly. Expansion is earned with evidenced value, proposed at the right moment, and always optional.

كيف ننقل العميل لأعلى السُّلَّم بصدق. التوسّع يُكتسب بقيمة مُثبتة، ويُقترح في الوقت المناسب، ويبقى اختياريًا دائمًا.

## The ladder — السُّلَّم

| From | To | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Diagnostic (499–2,500) | Pilot (5,000–25,000) | A finding worth proving on real data |
| Pilot (5,000–25,000) | Department OS (25,000–150,000) | Workflow is recurring and worth building |
| Pilot (5,000–25,000) | Retainer (3,000–25,000/mo) | Workflow is recurring but client wants it operated |
| Department OS | Retainer | Built system needs ongoing operation |
| Department OS / Retainer | Additional department | First workflow proven, next one mapped |
| Multiple Department OS | Enterprise (150,000+) | Several departments under one governance frame |

## When to propose — متى نقترح

Propose expansion only at a natural milestone: diagnostic delivery, Pilot close, phase end, or quarterly review. Never mid-cycle, never with fabricated urgency.

## How to propose — كيف نقترح

1. Lead with evidenced results from the current offer, labeled Estimated/Observed/Verified.
2. Name the specific next workflow or scale step — concrete, not "more."
3. State the price band from the ladder and what is included and excluded.
4. Make it optional: continuing as-is is a valid client choice.

## What expansion is not — ما ليس توسّعًا

- Not auto-renewal, not auto-escalation, not bundling the client into a tier they did not approve.
- Not a promise of ROI, sales, or conversion rates.
- Not pressure. If the client declines, the relationship continues at the current tier.

## Evidence gate — بوّابة الأدلة

No expansion proposal ships without a value-ledger reference showing Observed (and ideally Verified) value on the current offer. Estimated-only proposals must say so plainly. See [08_value_os/VALUE_LEDGER.md](../08_value_os/VALUE_LEDGER.md) and [07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md](../07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md).

## Approval boundary — حدود الموافقة

Every expansion proposal is a draft until founder approval and is delivered through the same manual, no-external-send process as all client artifacts.

## Metrics — المقاييس

- Expansion proposals made vs. milestones reached.
- Expansion acceptance (tracked, never promised).
- Time-in-tier before expansion.
- Net value growth per account (labeled).

## Forbidden — ممنوع

No "guaranteed ROI", "100%", "replace your team", "automate everything", "no human needed", or fabricated urgency in any expansion material.
