# Delivery & Client Success OS — نظام التسليم ونجاح العميل

The operating system for turning a signed offer into delivered, governed, bilingual work — and turning delivered work into retained, expanding accounts. This set covers all four delivery offers and the success motion around them.

## Governing rule — القاعدة الحاكمة

**AI drafts, ranks, and recommends. Founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.**

الذكاء الاصطناعي يكتب المسودات ويرتّب الأولويات ويوصي. المؤسس يراجع ويوافق ويرسل يدويًا. النظام لا يرسل إلى أي طرف خارجي إطلاقًا.

This rule binds every document below. It is restated in [05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md](../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md).

## Shared boundaries — حدود مشتركة

Every delivery doc declares the same five boundaries explicitly:

- **Human approval boundary** — no external artifact leaves without a logged founder approval.
- **Security boundary** — client data stays inside the agreed scope; PII is classified per [04_data_os/PII_CLASSIFICATION.md](../04_data_os/PII_CLASSIFICATION.md); retention follows [04_data_os/DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md](../04_data_os/DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md).
- **Value labeling** — outputs are labeled Estimated until evidenced. See [08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md](../08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md).
- **No external send** — Dealix never messages a client's prospects or customers on their behalf without explicit, per-batch approval.
- **Acceptance criteria** — each offer has a written definition of "delivered." In-flight is not delivered.

## Forbidden everywhere — ممنوع في كل المواد

Never use: "guaranteed ROI", "100%", "replace your team", "automate everything", "no human needed", or fabricated urgency.

ممنوع نهائيًا: "عائد مضمون"، "100%"، "نستبدل فريقك"، "أتمتة كل شيء"، "بدون تدخل بشري"، أو افتعال الاستعجال.
