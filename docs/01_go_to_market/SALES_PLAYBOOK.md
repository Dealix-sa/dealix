# Dealix — Sales Playbook

> **Status:** CANONICAL · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## 1. Principle

Sell **outcomes**, not features. The outcome is: "We produce your first clear
operating loop in 7 days." Every conversation is founder-led, approval-first, and
free of overpromise.

## 2. Funnel

```
Warm prospect → 20-min Discovery → Free Diagnostic (3 pains/3 blockers/1 step)
   → Command Sprint (499–1,500 SAR, 7 days) → Command Pack handoff
   → Customer Success questions → Managed Business OS (2,999 SAR/mo)
```

## 3. Discovery call — the 10 questions

1. How do opportunities enter your company?
2. Where does follow-up get lost?
3. How do you know an offer is strong?
4. How is the service delivered?
5. How does management know what to decide?
6. Where do problems recur?
7. Where does customer information get lost?
8. What needs approval?
9. What proof do you use to convince a customer?
10. If we fixed one thing in 7 days, what would it be?

Don't pitch features — pitch the outcome: "We produce your first clear operating
loop."

## 4. Objection handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We already have a CRM." | A CRM stores records. Dealix produces a weekly decision rhythm and proof. The Sprint shows the difference in 7 days. |
| "We have a sales team." | Good — the Sprint makes their follow-up, offers, and objections measurable and gives them a Next Action Board. |
| "AI is risky." | That's why Dealix is approval-first: nothing is sent automatically, every external action needs human approval, and every claim needs evidence. |
| "Too early / no time." | The Sprint is 7 days and needs ~2 hours of your time. The output is yours to keep regardless. |
| "Price?" | 499–1,500 SAR for a paid proof. If the outputs aren't useful, you don't continue. |

## 5. From Sprint to subscription

Only offer the monthly when at least one is true (Product Family Map sale rule):
- Customer paid for the Sprint.
- Customer received the Command Pack.
- Customer said the outputs are useful.
- There are clear next actions.
- There is a weekly-recurring pain.

## 6. What sales never does

- Never auto-sends WhatsApp/email or runs sequences without human approval.
- Never scrapes or buys contact lists.
- Never says "certified."
- Never discounts outside `PRICE_AUTHORITY.md`.
- Never promises revenue numbers or guaranteed outcomes.
