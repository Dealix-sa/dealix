## 3. Discount rules

**No discount without all five:**
1. A reason.
2. A duration (the discount expires).
3. A counterpart (what the customer gives — a case study, a testimonial, a referral, a faster close).
4. Approval (founder only at this stage).
5. A logged record (date, customer, amount, counterpart) in this file's change log.

## 4. What cannot be discounted

- Governance / compliance work.
- Anything that would price the engagement below its delivery cost.
- The principle of human approval (it is not a paid add-on; it is always on).

## 5. Who can approve a discount

At this stage: **the founder only.** When a sales role is added, define their
discount ceiling here before they sell.
