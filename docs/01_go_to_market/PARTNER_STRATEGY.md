# Dealix — Partner Strategy

> **Status:** FUTURE (do not start before 3 approved Command Packs) · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## 1. Gate

Do not open partnerships before **3 approved Command Packs** exist. Partners need
proof to sell; we need proof before we ask them to.

## 2. Target partners

- Marketing agencies
- Consultancies
- Training firms
- CRM implementation firms
- Tech firms serving SMBs

## 3. The partner offer

> You own the customer relationship. Dealix gives you an AI operating system to
> deliver Revenue + Command + Proof. We help with diagnosis, the proof pack, and
> operation.

## 4. Commission model

| Partner type | Commission |
|---|---|
| Referral | 10–15% |
| Co-sell | 20% |
| Co-delivery | 30% |
| Strategic | Custom |

## 5. Co-delivery rules

- Partners deliver under the same doctrine: approval-first, no auto-send, no
  scraping, no overclaim.
- All customer-facing drafts still require human approval.
- Proof and claims still flow through Proof OS / the claims register.

## 6. What we need before scaling partners

- Partner offer kit (one-pager + Command Pack sample, anonymized).
- Referral tracking + partner scoring.
- Revenue-share agreement template (legal review required).
- A repeatable delivery template partners can follow.
