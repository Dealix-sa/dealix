## 1. Sales motion (this stage only)

Start with:
- Founder-led outbound
- Warm introductions
- Manual LinkedIn (no automation)
- Partner referrals
- Client proof loops

Do **not** start with paid ads.

## 3. ICP for the first 90 days

Saudi B2B services companies: marketing agencies, consultancies, training firms,
tech firms, business-solution firms, operations firms, professional-services
offices.

**Why:** they have follow-up chaos, proposals, customers, delivery, and missing
proof — and they decide faster than enterprise.

**Rules:** sent manually by the founder, one at a time, to warm or personally
relevant contacts. Never bulk-sent. Never auto-sent.
