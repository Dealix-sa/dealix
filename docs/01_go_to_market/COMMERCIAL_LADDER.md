# COMMERCIAL LADDER — سلّم العروض

> The five-rung ladder is the priced, sequenced path from first contact to retainer.
> Every website CTA and growth asset routes into the **top three rungs only**:
> **Business OS Score → Diagnostic → Command Sprint**.

## The ladder

| Rung | Offer | Price (SAR) | What the customer gets | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | **Free AI Ops Diagnostic** | 0 | A governed read of where revenue/ops leak + a prioritized next-action list | `BETA` |
| 1 | **Dealix Command Sprint** (Revenue Intelligence Sprint) | 499 | The first sellable wedge — see deliverables below | `BETA` |
| 2 | **Data-to-Revenue Pack** | 1,500 | Structured data → scored accounts → governed draft pack | `BETA` |
| 3 | **Managed Revenue Ops** | 2,999–4,999 / mo | Ongoing governed operating rhythm + monthly proof | `BETA` |
| 4 | **Custom AI Service Setup** | 5,000–25,000 + 1,000/mo | Bespoke governed AI workflow for the customer's stack | `INTERNAL` |
| Slow track | **AI Governance Review** | 25,000–50,000 | Enterprise governance/readiness engagement | `FUTURE` |

> Prices are list anchors, not guarantees of outcome. No rung guarantees revenue.

## Lead-in (the only three public CTAs)

1. **Get Business OS Score** — free, instant, low-friction lead capture → routes to Diagnostic.
2. **Book Diagnostic** — Rung 0 → qualifies for the Sprint.
3. **Start Command Sprint** — Rung 1, the first paid wedge.

Every page picks **one** of these as its single primary CTA.

## The wedge: Dealix Command Sprint (Rung 1)

The Command Sprint is the first commercial wedge. It includes:

- Market Intelligence Lite
- Revenue Map
- Proof Register
- Executive Command Brief
- Approval Register
- Next Action Board
- Delivery Lite
- Upsell Recommendation

Delivery detail: `docs/04_delivery/COMMAND_SPRINT_DELIVERY.md`.

## Ladder logic (why this order)

- **Score → Diagnostic**: turn anonymous interest into a qualified, governed read.
- **Diagnostic → Sprint**: convert the read into a paid, time-boxed proof of value.
- **Sprint → Pack/Managed Ops**: convert proof into recurring revenue.
- **Managed Ops → Custom AI**: expand into bespoke governed workflows.

No rung is sold on a guaranteed-revenue promise. Each rung sells **governed operating capability +
auditable proof**. See `docs/00_constitution/GOOD_REVENUE_BAD_REVENUE.md`.
