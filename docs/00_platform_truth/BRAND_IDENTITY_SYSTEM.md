# BRAND IDENTITY SYSTEM — نظام هوية Dealix

> Who Dealix is, how it sounds, and the lines it never crosses. Pairs with
> `VISUAL_IDENTITY_SYSTEM.md` (look) and `MESSAGING_HOUSE.md` (words).

## Essence

Dealix is a **Saudi-first AI Business Operating System** — serious, executive, evidence-first.
We sell **governed operating capability + auditable proof**, not AI hype.

## Brand pillars (the 6)

1. **Command** — one screen for what's happening and what's next.
2. **Proof** — every claim is backed by evidence (Proof Pack / Claims Register).
3. **Governance** — approval-first; nothing external happens without a human gate.
4. **Delivery** — we operate, not just advise; outputs ship.
5. **Growth** — loops that compound (proof → content → inbound → referral).
6. **Business OS** — 14 systems, one governed rhythm — not a single tool.

## Personality

Executive · Saudi/GCC · Arabic-first (English-ready) · direct · confident · calm ·
evidence-first · **not** hype, **not** playful, **not** generic-startup.

## Voice principles

- Lead with the customer's operating reality, not our features.
- Claim only what we can prove; otherwise frame as a hypothesis or rewrite.
- Short, declarative sentences. Numbers over adjectives. Arabic that reads native, not translated.
- One idea per section. One primary CTA per page.

## The "not a…" guardrail (say this whenever positioning blurs)

Not a CRM · not a chatbot · not a marketing agency · not a WhatsApp bot ·
not a generic AI tool · not "only Revenue OS".

## Hard brand rules

- No guaranteed-revenue claims.
- No fake proof, fake testimonials, or fake scarcity.
- No customer name/logo/quote without written approval.
- No future/beta module presented as live.
- Every external claim: evidence-backed, hypothesis-framed, or rewritten safely.

## Naming

- Company: **Dealix**.
- The wedge: **Dealix Command Sprint**.
- The free entry: **Business OS Score** / **AI Ops Diagnostic**.
- Systems are named "<X> OS" (Command OS, Revenue OS, …) — always with a status label.

## CTAs (the only three)

**Get Business OS Score · Book Diagnostic · Start Command Sprint.**
