## 2. Rung 0 — Free Business Diagnostic

- **Price:** Free · **Duration:** 20–30 minutes.
- **Goal:** qualification only. Do **not** give away everything.
- **Deliverable:** 3 pains + 3 blockers + 1 recommended next step.

## 3. Rung 1 — Dealix Command Sprint (THE WEDGE)

- **Price:** 499–1,500 SAR · **Duration:** 7 days.
- **Goal:** prove the first operating loop inside the customer's company.
- **Covers:** Revenue OS, Proof OS, Command OS, Governance OS, Client OS Lite,
  Delivery OS Lite.
- **Deliverable — Dealix Command Pack:**
  - Revenue Map
  - Next Action Board
  - Follow-up Drafts
  - Offer Upgrade
  - Objection Intelligence
  - Proof Register
  - Approval Register
  - Executive Command Brief

Full spec: [`../01_go_to_market/COMMAND_SPRINT_OFFER.md`](../01_go_to_market/COMMAND_SPRINT_OFFER.md).

## 4. Rung 2 — Business OS Setup

- **Price:** 3,000–5,000 SAR · **Duration:** 10–14 days.
- **Goal:** build an initial operating rhythm.
- **Includes:** Command dashboard, Revenue Map, Client Brain, Delivery Board,
  Proof Register, Approval Matrix.

## 5. Rung 3 — Managed Business OS (the core subscription)

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| Starter Command | 2,999 SAR/mo | Revenue + Proof + Command |
| Business Ops | 4,999–7,500 SAR/mo | + Client + Delivery |
| Executive OS | 12,000–20,000 SAR/mo | + Finance + Governance + Support |
| Partner / Enterprise | Custom | Custom platform + integrations |

**Subscription sale rule** — sell the monthly only after at least one of:
- Customer paid for a Sprint.
- Customer received the Command Pack.
- Customer said the outputs are useful.
- There are clear next actions.
- There is a pain that recurs weekly.

## 6. Rung 4 — Executive Command OS

- **Price:** 12,000–20,000 SAR/mo.
- **Goal:** executive management, measurement, and decisions across modules.

## 7. Rung 5 — Enterprise / Partner OS

- **Price:** Custom.
- **Goal:** integrations, governance, multiple units, training.
- **Gate:** requires Enterprise Readiness (see `docs/03_governance/` + security gates).

---

## 8. What each rung is NOT

- Rung 1 is not a SaaS subscription — it is a founder-led paid engagement.
- Finance OS is not a billing/invoicing provider; it is ZATCA-**aware** advisory.
- No rung includes auto-send, scraping, or cold outreach.
