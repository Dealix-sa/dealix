# PRODUCT FAMILY MAP — خريطة عائلة المنتج

> One company, one operating system, many surfaces. This map prevents Dealix from being
> reduced to any single tool (CRM, chatbot, "Revenue OS only").

## The one-line truth

**Dealix is a Saudi-first AI Business Operating System** — it turns scattered WhatsApp, Excel,
meetings, offers, follow-ups, delivery, client memory, finance, data, governance, and executive
decisions into **one governed operating rhythm**.

## The operating question loop (what the OS answers)

What is happening? → What should happen next? → Who approves? → What is the evidence? →
What is the next action? → (repeat)

## Three layers

| Layer | What it is | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Platform** | The 14 governed Operating Systems | Command · Market Intelligence · Revenue · Proof · Delivery · Client · Support · Finance · Data · Governance · Knowledge · Agent · Partner · Academy OS |
| **Packaged offers** | The 5-rung commercial ladder | Diagnostic → Command Sprint → Data Pack → Managed Ops → Custom AI |
| **Surfaces** | Where customers meet Dealix | `landing/` site · `frontend/` funnel + dashboard · Proof Packs · Founder ops cockpit |

## The wedge

The **Dealix Command Sprint** is the tip of the spear — the first sellable, provable unit of the
whole OS. It samples Market Intelligence, Revenue, Proof, Delivery, and Governance in one 499 SAR
engagement, then upsells into recurring Managed Ops.

## What Dealix is NOT (guard rails)

- Not a CRM — it governs the operating rhythm across tools, it doesn't just store contacts.
- Not a chatbot — it is approval-first decision infrastructure, not a reply bot.
- Not a marketing agency — it is software + governed delivery, not creative services.
- Not a WhatsApp bot — every external action is human-gated; zero auto-send.
- Not a generic AI tool — it is evidence-first and Saudi/GCC-specific.
- Not "only Revenue OS" — Revenue OS is one of 14 systems.
