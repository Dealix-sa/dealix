## English Section

| Product | Price | Duration / form | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free Business Diagnostic | Free | Short session | First read of where opportunities leak and what evidence is missing. |
| Dealix Command Sprint | 499–1,500 SAR | 7 days | Revenue + Proof + Command. |
| Business OS Setup | 3,000–5,000 SAR | Setup project | Configure the company's core systems. |
| Managed Business OS | 2,999–7,500 SAR/mo | Managed subscription | Ongoing operation of the core systems. |
| Executive Command OS | 12,000–20,000 SAR/mo | Executive subscription | Full executive command via Command OS. |
| Enterprise / Partner | Custom | Custom agreement | Enterprise scope or partner channel. |

**Note:** "Managed Business OS" widens the identity from "Managed Revenue Ops" to managed business operations, while Revenue OS remains the entry wedge the customer starts from.

### Ladder logic

Each step is a smaller commitment that earns the next. The Free Diagnostic feeds the Command Sprint; the Sprint's findings justify the Setup; the Setup makes the Managed subscription useful; Managed maturity unlocks Executive Command OS. No step promises a number — each delivers a decision and the evidence behind it.
