# Dealix — Public Positioning

> **Status:** CANONICAL · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05
>
> What we say publicly, and what we never say. Aligns with the No-Overclaim
> register (`dealix/registers/no_overclaim.yaml`).

---

## 1. Category

**Dealix is a Saudi AI Business Operating System.** Revenue OS is the first
commercial wedge.

## 2. Do NOT say

| Don't say | Why |
|---|---|
| "We are a CRM" | Commoditized — "I already have one." |
| "We are a chatbot" | Cheap, repeated, low trust. |
| "We are a marketing agency" | Sounds temporary. |
| "We are WhatsApp automation" | Triggers risk perception. |
| "We get you customers" | Overpromise. |
| "PDPL certified / ZATCA certified / NCA certified" | We are not certified. |
| "Fully automated" | Violates the approval-first doctrine. |

## 3. DO say

> Dealix is an AI-native business operating system that starts from revenue,
> decision, and proof, then expands into clients, delivery, support, finance,
> and governance.

Trust phrasing (allowed): **PDPL-aware**, **ZATCA-aware**, **NCA-aligned
controls**, **approval-first**.

## 4. Positioning pyramid

```
Category:     AI Business Operating System
First wedge:  Command Sprint
Core promise: clarity → decision → action → proof
Trust:        approval-first · PDPL-aware · ZATCA-aware · NCA-aligned
Market:       Saudi / GCC B2B
Expansion:    platform + partner ecosystem
```

## 5. Brand tone

Executive. Saudi. Trustworthy. Never hype. No "AI magic." Sell **operating
clarity**, not automation fantasy.

نبرة العلامة: تنفيذية، سعودية، موثوقة، لا تبالغ، لا تبيع "AI magic"، بِع وضوح
التشغيل.

## 6. The contrast that sells

| Customer hears | Impression |
|---|---|
| CRM | "I have one." |
| Chatbot | Cheap, repeated. |
| Agency | Temporary service. |
| AI automation | Risk. |
| **Business OS** | Strategic platform. |
| **Command Sprint** | Clear, fast experience. |

## 8. Claim discipline

Before any public claim:
1. It must exist in `dealix/registers/no_overclaim.yaml` with status `Production`.
2. It must not exceed the status in [`MODULE_STATUS_MAP.md`](MODULE_STATUS_MAP.md).
3. It must have evidence in Proof OS.

If those three are not all true, the claim does not go public.
