# Academy OS

> **Status:** `FUTURE` (adoption content exists; productized program is later) · **Plane:** Operating · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## Purpose

Training and adoption — make sure customer teams actually use Dealix. Adoption is
a necessary layer, not "extra content."

## Functions

- AI literacy
- Team onboarding
- Operating rhythm training
- Role playbooks
- Adoption score

## Why it matters

A recent study of GenAI usage in Saudi Arabia found high text usage alongside
strong interest in structured training, privacy, ethics, and responsible use.
This supports Academy OS as a **required adoption layer** inside Dealix, not an
optional add-on.

## Inputs / Outputs

**Inputs:** customer team, role definitions, operating rhythm. **Outputs:**
onboarding paths, role playbooks, adoption score.

## Approval & doctrine

Training content follows the same no-overclaim and responsible-use principles as
the rest of Dealix.
