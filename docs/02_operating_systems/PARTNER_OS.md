# Partner OS

> **Status:** `FUTURE` (do not open before 3 approved Command Packs) · **Plane:** Operating · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## Purpose

Partnerships and channels — let agencies, consultancies, and implementers deliver
Dealix to their own customers.

## Functions

- Referral tracking
- Partner scoring
- Partner offer kit
- Revenue share
- Co-delivery rules

## Commission model

| Partner type | Commission |
|---|---|
| Referral | 10–15% |
| Co-sell | 20% |
| Co-delivery | 30% |
| Strategic | Custom |

## Inputs / Outputs

**Inputs:** partner referrals, co-delivery engagements. **Outputs:** referral
tracking, partner scorecards, revenue-share records.

## Approval & doctrine

Partners operate under the full doctrine: approval-first, no auto-send, no
scraping, no overclaim. Revenue-share agreements require legal review.
