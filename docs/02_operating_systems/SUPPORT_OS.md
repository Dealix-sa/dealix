# Support OS

> **Status:** `DOCS_ONLY` (roadmap) · **Plane:** Execution + Decision · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## Purpose

Customer support system — classify issues, suggest replies, and surface recurring
problems.

## Functions

- Issue classification
- Suggested replies (drafts only)
- Escalation
- SLA breach detection
- Recurring issues
- Support intelligence

## Inputs

Support tickets, customer messages, SLA definitions.

## Outputs

Classified tickets, draft replies, escalation flags, recurring-issue report.

## Approval & doctrine

Suggested replies are **drafts** (approval class A2) — never auto-sent. Escalation
to a human is the default for anything sensitive.

## Status note

Support OS is specified but **not delivered yet**. Do not sell it as ready. It
enters the roadmap after the first Managed Business OS customers stabilize.
