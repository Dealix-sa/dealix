# Revenue OS

> **Status:** `LIVE / BETA` · **Plane:** Decision + Execution · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05
>
> The fastest commercial door — but **not** the identity. Revenue OS is the wedge.

---

## Purpose

Give a company a revenue **operating rhythm** — not another CRM. Present it as a
rhythm, never as "a CRM."

## Functions

- Lead intake
- ICP scoring
- Pipeline prioritization
- Follow-up drafts (drafts only — never auto-sent)
- Proposal improvement
- Objection intelligence
- Conversion review

## Positioning

Do **not** sell it as a CRM. Sell it as a **revenue operating rhythm**: every
week the company knows which opportunities matter, which follow-ups are overdue,
which offers to upgrade, and which objections recur.

## Inside the Command Sprint

Revenue OS produces the **Revenue Map**, **Follow-up Drafts**, **Offer Upgrade**,
and **Objection Intelligence** artifacts.

## Inputs

Opportunity list, current offer, follow-up examples, sales flow description.

## Outputs

Revenue Map, prioritized pipeline, follow-up drafts, offer upgrade
recommendations, objection intelligence.

## Approval & doctrine

- Follow-up messages are **drafts**. Nothing is sent automatically.
- No scraping, no list buying, no cold mass outreach.
- ICP scoring and prioritization are recommendations with confidence scores.
