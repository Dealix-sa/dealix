# Client OS

> **Status:** `INTERNAL` (delivered as "Client Brain Lite" in the Sprint) · **Plane:** Data + Decision · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## Purpose

The customer's memory. Especially valuable for services companies that lose track
of promises, follow-ups, and delivery.

## Functions — the account brain

- What did the customer request?
- What did we promise?
- What did we deliver?
- What are they waiting for?
- Where is renewal risk?
- Where is expansion opportunity?

## Inside the Command Sprint

Delivered as **Client Brain Lite** (`02_client_brain.md` in the customer folder):
a structured summary of what we know about the account and its opportunities.

## Inputs

Customer intake, opportunity list, prior communications the customer shares.

## Outputs

Account brain summary, promise/delivery ledger, renewal and expansion flags.

## Approval & doctrine

Only stores data the customer consents to share (Data OS minimum-data rule).
No scraping of customer or third-party data.
