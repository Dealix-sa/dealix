# Knowledge OS

> **Status:** `INTERNAL` · **Plane:** Data · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05

---

## Purpose

The company's memory — so knowledge, decisions, and learnings are not trapped in
people's heads or lost in chat threads.

## Functions

- SOPs
- Playbooks
- Objections
- Product knowledge
- Policies
- Customer learnings
- Decision history

## Inputs

Delivered engagements, decision logs, objection intelligence, SOPs the team writes.

## Outputs

Searchable playbooks, objection library, decision history, onboarding material
(feeds Academy OS).

## Approval & doctrine

Customer-specific learnings are stored under the same consent and retention rules
as Data OS. Nothing customer-identifying is reused publicly without approval.

- `docs/`, `prompts/`, internal playbooks.
