- Collections
- Margin
- Unit economics
- Offer profitability

## ZATCA posture (important)

ZATCA defines e-invoicing as converting paper invoice issuance into a structured
electronic process for exchanging and processing invoices and notes; the Saudi
"Fatoorah" program has two phases (Phase 1 began December 2021, Phase 2 began
January 2023).

**Therefore Dealix Finance OS is ZATCA-aware from the start — but it does NOT
claim to be a qualified e-invoicing provider unless that is actually built and
certified.** Today Finance OS is advisory: it helps a company understand pricing,
margin, and MRR; it does not issue compliant tax invoices.

## Inputs

Pricing, subscriptions, costs, collections data the customer shares.

## Outputs

MRR view, margin estimates, offer profitability, collections awareness.

## Approval & doctrine

Any financial recommendation is approval class A4 (needs approval + log). Finance
OS never executes payments, invoices, or financial commitments.
