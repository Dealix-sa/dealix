# Proof OS

> **Status:** `BETA` · **Plane:** Trust · **Owner:** Founder · **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-05
>
> The most important trust weapon. No claim without evidence. No case study
> without approval.

---

## Purpose

Make every claim Dealix or its customers make traceable to evidence, and turn
delivered work into approved, reusable proof.

## Functions

- Evidence ledger
- Proof pack
- Claims validation
- Before / after
- Approval-to-publish
- Case-study safe mode

## The two rules

1. **No case study without approval.**
2. **No claim without evidence.**

## Proof levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| L1 | Dealix produced an output |
| L2 | Customer reviewed the output |
| L3 | Customer approved |
| L4 | Customer used it |
| L5 | A measurable effect appeared |

Early on, **L2/L3 is enough**. Do not chase L5 prematurely.

## Inside the Command Sprint

Proof OS produces the **Proof Register** — what evidence exists, what is missing,
and what is safe to publish (with approval).

## Approval & doctrine

- Publishing any proof requires explicit customer approval (approval-to-publish).
- Claims that go public must also exist in `dealix/registers/no_overclaim.yaml`.
- "Safe mode" = anonymized case study until the customer approves attribution.
