# Partner Program

## Tiers

| Tier | Description | Revenue share | Cert. |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Referral | Refers customers; Dealix delivers | 10% | No |
| White-label | Sells Dealix offers under partner brand | 30% | Yes |
| Implementation | Delivers Dealix engagements with quality + governance | 20% | Yes |
| Strategic | Co-builds products + brings distribution | 40% | Yes |

## Approved claims

Partners may only use claims registered in
`hermes.partners.program.partner_claims.APPROVED_CLAIMS`. Forbidden
variants like "Dealix guarantees sales" or "Dealix replaces your sales
team" automatically fail the trust gate.

## Performance review

Quarterly. Verdicts:
- **Scale** — beat tier minimum × 1.5 with no issues.
- **Keep** — at or above tier minimum.
- **Coach** — single failing metric.
- **Drop** — multiple failing metrics.
