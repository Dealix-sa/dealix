Pricing in SAR. All packages assume PDPL compliance + opt-out + human approval for first 30 days.

## Canonical offer ladder

| Rung | Package | Price | Customer signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Free AI Ops Diagnostic | Free | Any first contact |
| 1 | 7-Day Revenue Proof Sprint | 499 SAR (one-time) | Wants a fast proof |
| 2 | Data-to-Revenue Pack | 1,500 SAR (one-time) | Has data to mine |
| 3 | Managed Revenue Ops | 2,999–4,999 SAR/month | Completed a pilot |
| 4 | Executive Command Center | 7,500–15,000 SAR/month | ≥3 pilots + 1 case study |
| 5 | Agency Partner OS | custom + 15–30% rev-share | ≥3 proof packs + partner agreement |

Rungs 0–1 ship a verified product deliverable. Rungs 3–5 are
founder-assisted / semi-automated today — Sami runs delivery manually.

## Sales motion

Free Diagnostic → 499 SAR Sprint (Proof Pack on day 7) → upgrade only after a
documented proof from the prior rung. No rung opens without real evidence.

## Agency / Partner revenue share (Rung 5)

| Partner type | Setup | Recurring share |
|---|---|---|
| Referral partner | 0 | 10% MRR for 12 months |
| Agency reseller | included in client price | 25% MRR while client active |
| Implementation partner | per-engagement SOW | 15% MRR for 6 months |

any real billing:

## Discount + bundle rules

- Annual prepay → 2 months free.
- 3 customer referral → 1 free month.
- Saudi-flag founders → 25% off the 499 SAR Sprint.
- Sponsor a case study → 50% off month 1 of a retainer.

## Refund policy

- Sprint (499 SAR): see `docs/REFUND_SOP.md` for the proof-based refund window.
- Retainer: 7-day money-back on first month.
- After 30 days: pro-rata.

## When to NOT discount

- Retainer contracts where the customer hasn't seen Sprint proof. Make them
  run the 499 SAR Sprint first.
- Customers asking "can you also send cold WhatsApp?" — politely decline.
  Dealix is inbound-only and draft-only; cold outreach is never sold.
- Anyone asking to skip the manual approval gate in the first 30 days.
