# Marketing Strategy

## North star

**Verified revenue first.** Every marketing channel reports against
verified revenue, not vanity metrics.

## Channels

- **GEO / AI search** — dedicated pages for high-intent topics. See
  `growth.geo.DEFAULT_PAGES`. Goal: be cited in AI answers for
  "AI governance Saudi Arabia", "agentic control plane",
  "AI revenue hunter", "white-label AI for agencies", "MCP risk
  review", "revenue attribution for AI", "AI agents with approval
  workflows".
- **Founder content** — Sami-led LinkedIn essays referencing assets
  registered in the asset store.
- **Partner program** — distribution via certified white-label
  partners.
- **Outbound** — Revenue Hunter Pilot offers as the entry point.
- **Trust assets** — case studies, evidence packs, customer quotes.

## Attribution

All channels feed `growth.revenue_attribution.attribute(...)`. Reports
roll up under the growth dashboard.
