# Revenue Streams

1. **Productized packages** (one-off engagements, see PRODUCT_PACKAGES.md).
2. **Monthly retainers** (Revenue Hunter Managed, AI Governance OS,
   Market Radar, MCP Governance, Strategic Partner).
3. **Partner revenue share** (10% referral, 20% implementation, 30%
   white-label, 40% strategic).
4. **Marketplace listings** (gated by `marketplace.readiness`).
5. **Public API** (gated by `api_platform.readiness`).

Every revenue stream is measured against the verification policy in
`growth.verified_revenue_loop`. Pipeline does not count.
