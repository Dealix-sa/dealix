# Dealix Proposal System

Do not send random proposals. Use one structure every time (adapt length to deal size).

## Unified proposal template

```markdown
# Proposal: [Service Name]

## Client Goal
What the client wants.

## Problem
What is broken or unclear today.

## Proposed Sprint
What Dealix will deliver in this engagement.

## Scope

**Included:**
- 

**Excluded:**
- 

## Timeline
Day-by-day or phase-by-phase.

## Deliverables
- 

## Client Responsibilities
- Data / access / stakeholders / approval turnaround

## Price
SAR X (or range + assumptions)

## Success Metric
How we know it worked (one primary metric).

## Governance
- Draft-only where applicable; approval before external channels
- No scraping; no guaranteed revenue claims
- PDPL / contactability respected

## Next Step
Payment / contract / kickoff date
```

Repo copy for reuse: `docs/templates/PROPOSAL_BODY.md` (keep in sync with this section).

## Document Standard Compliance

## Purpose
Defines this operating document's role inside Dealix Company OS.

## Owner
Sami (Founder). Reassign to the responsible operator when one is named.

## Review Cadence
Weekly until stable, then monthly.

## Inputs
- Relevant company data and signals.
- Founder decisions and customer evidence.

## Outputs
- Operating guidance, decisions, or templates produced by this document.
- Evidence captured for verification.

## Rules
- Must support revenue, delivery, trust, learning, or founder leverage.
- Must not introduce unsupported claims.
- Must preserve public/private boundaries.

## Metrics
- Completion status of the actions this document drives.
- Impact on revenue, delivery, trust, or founder leverage.

## Evidence
- Linked workflow, file, test output, customer interaction, or decision log.
