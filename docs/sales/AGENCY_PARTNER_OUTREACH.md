> 3 partner outreach drafts, bilingual, doctrine-aligned. Founder
> approves each before sending. Never auto-send. Never bulk.

**Merge fields:** `{{agency_name}}`, `{{founder_name_of_agency}}`,
`{{sector_focus}}`, `{{founder_name}}` (Dealix), `{{calendly_url}}`,
`{{partner_program_url}}`.

**Doctrine:** referral, implementation, and co-selling partnerships
formalize ONLY after 3 reference proof packs exist (per
