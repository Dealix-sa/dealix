# Founder Sales Call — One Pager

> Bring this to every discovery call. One page.

## Pre-call (10 min prep)

- Re-read the prospect's last 3 LinkedIn posts.
- Re-read our DM and their reply.
- Confirm the specific signal that triggered the score.
- Note one competitor we know in their sector.
- Re-read our `OFFER_LADDER.md` Sprint scope.

## Call Structure (30 min)

### 0–5 min: Set the frame
- "We have 30 minutes. The goal is to figure out if we are a fit."
- "I will ask you about your sales motion. Then I will tell you what
  we do. Then we decide if there is a next step."

### 5–20 min: Discovery
Use the questions below. Do not read; converse.

1. What does a healthy quarter for your pipeline look like?
2. How do you currently generate qualified opportunities?
3. What is the bottleneck (volume, quality, cycle time)?
4. Who are your typical customers (sector, size, geo)?
5. What does your outreach look like today?
6. Where does the founder spend their sales time?
7. What have you tried that did not work?
8. What does success in 90 days look like?

Listen for: ICP fit, pain, authority, budget signal, timing.

### 20–25 min: Our offer
- One paragraph: what Dealix does.
- One paragraph: the Sprint scope and price range.
- One sentence: what makes our delivery different (Arabic-native,
  evidence-bound, founder-led).

### 25–30 min: Next step
- "Based on what you described, here is what I would recommend." (be
  specific)
- Send a Sample first (free or 199 SAR) **or** scope the Sprint.
- Agree on next concrete step + date.

## Anti-Patterns

- Pitching for 25 minutes.
- "Let me send you a deck."
- Skipping discovery because the lead said they want to buy.
- Promising delivery in writing during the call.

## Post-call

Within 24h:

- Send a written recap with the agreed next step and date.
- Log the call in `dealix-ops-private/sales/call_notes/`.
- Update the pipeline stage.

## Disqualification Discipline

If, during the call, the prospect:

- Wants a guarantee → politely decline.
- Refuses Sample / Sprint → suggest a fit conversation instead.
- Out of ICP → refer where possible; thank them.
