## 4. Tomorrow morning — 30-minute founder action sequence

Do this in order, once, before the first coffee meeting.

**Minute 0–5 — Read the floor.**
- Open [`docs/CANONICAL_PRODUCT_NARRATIVE.md`](../CANONICAL_PRODUCT_NARRATIVE.md)
  section 4 (the one-liner). This is the only positioning you use today.
- Confirm the freeze: you sell **Free Diagnostic → 499 SAR Sprint** only.

**Minute 5–10 — Pick today's leads.**
- Open [`WARM_LIST_SCORED.md`](WARM_LIST_SCORED.md). Take the **Tier 1** rows only.
- Pick the **first 5** by score. These are today's outreach.

**Minute 10–25 — Personalize and queue 5 drafts.**
- Open [`OUTREACH_DRAFTS_BATCH1.md`](OUTREACH_DRAFTS_BATCH1.md).
- For each of the 5 leads: read the draft, replace every `[bracket]`, edit
  into your own voice. Each is headed "DRAFT — FOUNDER APPROVES BEFORE SEND".
- Send each one **manually**, one at a time, through the channel where a
  relationship or warm context already exists. No bulk send. No automation.
  Max 5 per hour (`launch_content_queue.md`, Execution rule 1).

**Minute 25–30 — Log and set the reply SLA.**
- Log every message you sent in `docs/ops/pipeline_tracker.csv`
  (`sent_at`, `message_version`, `channel`).
- Rule: when anyone replies, respond within 30 minutes. When anyone books a
  call, run [`DISCOVERY_SCRIPT.md`](DISCOVERY_SCRIPT.md) and fill the
  qualification scorecard inline.

**Then:** keep [`QUALIFICATION_ENGINE.md`](QUALIFICATION_ENGINE.md) and
[`OBJECTION_HANDLER.md`](OBJECTION_HANDLER.md) open during every call. If a
lead asks for cold WhatsApp, scraping, LinkedIn automation, or guaranteed
sales — reframe with `YES_BUT_PLAYBOOK.md`; do not improvise around the guard.

## 5. The non-negotiables — never break these | الخطوط الحمراء

Enforced by `tests/test_no_*.py`. Every draft and every call respects all of:

1. No guaranteed sales / conversion / ROI claims — frame as evidenced opportunities.
2. No cold WhatsApp.
3. No LinkedIn scraping or automation.
4. No bulk / mass outreach — every message personalized and sent manually.
5. No auto-send — the founder approves every external message.
6. No auto-book — booking is a human-approved action.
7. No purchased lists.
8. No "1 SAR pilot", "45-second reply", "AI sales rep", or "zero-code" copy.
9. PDPL-aware: no PII handling beyond what the customer consents to.
10. Respect opt-outs immediately and permanently.
11. No rung 2–5 pitching during the Commercial Freeze.

If a request needs one of these broken, decline cleanly and offer the safe
alternative (draft-only outputs / consent-based outreach / evidenced
opportunities).
