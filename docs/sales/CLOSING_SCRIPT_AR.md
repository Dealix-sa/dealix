# Closing Script

> **Status:** Draft. The close is a process, not a moment.
> **When:** only after the proposal has been sent and the prospect has read it.

## The principle

> The close starts in the discovery call, not the proposal debrief. By the time you say "do you want to start?", the answer is usually obvious.

## The 5-phase close

### Phase 1 — Re-cap (5 min, on the call)

> "خليني ألخص اللي اتفقنا عليه:"
>
> 1. [Pain 1]
> 2. [Pain 2]
> 3. [Proposed system]
> 4. [Scope]
> 5. [Out of scope]
> 6. [Success metric]
> 7. [Timeline]

Confirm. Adjust.

### Phase 2 — Confirm value (5 min)

> "لو نجحنا في [success metric 1] و [success metric 2] خلال [timeline]، هذا يستاهل [pricing placeholder]؟"

The pricing placeholder is from the proposal. The "?" is the question. Let them say yes or no.

### Phase 3 — Pilot, not commitment (5 min)

> "أقترح نبدأ بـ [pilot scope]. 4 أسابيع. لو ما اشتغل، نوقف. لو اشتغل، نكمل."

A pilot lowers the perceived risk. It also gives both sides an exit.

### Phase 4 — Proof metric (5 min)

> "مقاييس النجاح للـ pilot: [metric 1]، [metric 2]، [metric 3]. نقيس في الأسبوع 2 والـ 4."

The proof metric is the success criterion. Without it, the pilot is a hope.

### Phase 5 — Next action (2 min)

> "الخطوة الجاية: توقيع proposal + consent record + جدولة kickoff. متى تقدر توقع؟"

If they name a date: book it.
If they hesitate: "ما في مشكلة. خذ يومين تفكر، وأرسل لي رسالة."

## The 5 things to never do in a close

1. **Don't discount on the call.** "founder-approved discount" is the answer.
2. **Don't promise specific outcomes.** "Proof metric" is the answer.
3. **Don't close on a "maybe."** "Maybe" is a no. Walk.
4. **Don't pressure with a deadline.** "This offer expires tomorrow" kills trust.
5. **Don't close without a kickoff date.** A close without a date is a wish.

## The 5 signals the close is going well

1. They re-state the proof metric in their own words.
2. They ask "what does the kickoff look like?"
3. They ask about payment terms.
4. They name a specific date to sign.
5. They mention a colleague who needs to approve.

## The 5 signals the close is failing

1. They keep saying "I need to think."
2. They ask for a discount you did not offer.
3. They introduce a new objection that was not in the discovery.
4. They want to "loop in someone" but cannot name who.
5. They ask "what if it doesn't work?"

If 3+ signals, **walk**. Do not chase.

## The "what if it doesn't work" answer

> "لو ما اشتغل في الأسبوع 2، نوقف. أنت ما تخسر غير الوقت. نشتغل مع مقياس نجاح واضح. لو ما تحققت، نخرج بدون رسوم إضافية."

## The discount conversation

If they ask for a discount, your answer is:

> "التسعير founder-approved. ما عندي صلاحية أقلله. أقدر أحجز لك الـ pilot في [تاريخ] ونراجع بعد [N] أسابيع."

Do not negotiate on the call. The founder sets the price; the founder can adjust.

## The kickoff booking

Once they say yes:

1. Send the proposal for signature (DocuSign or similar).
2. Send the consent record template.
3. Book the kickoff call within 7 days.
4. Send the access checklist.
5. Add the deal to `templates/launch/sales_pipeline.example.json` with `stage: closed_won`.

## When to update

- After 5 closes: if signature rate < 60%, the close is weak — rewrite.
- After 10 closes: if pilot-to-renewal < 30%, the pilot scope is wrong.
- After 20 closes: if discount rate > 30%, the pricing is too high.
