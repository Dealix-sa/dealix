# Demo Script

> **Status:** Draft. 20 min, max.
> **When:** only after a discovery call, never as a cold demo.
> **Goal:** show one thing they do not have, not everything Dealix does.

## Hard rules

1. **Never demo cold.** Discovery first.
2. **Never demo everything.** One problem, one before/after, one decision.
3. **Never demo "the dashboard."** Dashboards are inputs. Demo decisions.
4. **Never demo on fake data if real data is available** (with permission).
5. **Never go over 20 min.**

## The structure (20 min, 3 phases)

### Phase 1 — Re-confirm (2 min)

> "مرحبا [الاسم]. شكراً على وقتك. في 20 دقيقة أعرض لك [الشئ الواحد]، ثم نقول 'نعم' أو 'لا'. مناسب؟"

State the 20-min cap. Honor it.

### Phase 2 — The before/after (12 min)

Pick the dominant pain from the discovery call. Demo one before/after.

**Lost follow-ups example:**

- Before: open a real WhatsApp thread (anonymized). Show 14 unread. Show the 5 that have been "no reply" for 2+ weeks. Show the cost.
- After: open the Dealix read-only view. Show the routing tag. Show the "no movement in 3 / 7 / 14 days" detector. Show the daily digest.

**Reporting black hole example:**

- Before: open a CRM. Show the static pipeline. Show the founder pinging each rep.
- After: open the Command Center. Show the daily digest. Show the stuck-deal flag. Show the approval queue summary.

**Pricing chaos example:**

- Before: open a Google Doc proposal. Show the scope + price in the same paragraph.
- After: open the Proposal OS view. Show the scope doc. Show the proof pack attached. Show the payment handoff policy (no price, no link, founder-approved).

### Phase 3 — The pilot close (6 min)

> "بناءً على اللي شفته، الـ [audit / pilot] يناسبك. الخطوات: [3 steps]. أرسل لك proposal خلال يومين. founder-approved pricing. مناسب؟"

If yes: book the next call.
If no: "ما في مشكلة. شكراً على وقتك."

## The 5 things to never do in a demo

1. **Don't show every feature.** Pick one pain, one fix.
2. **Don't demo "the system."** Demo the decision.
3. **Don't demo on fake data** if real data is available.
4. **Don't take questions during the demo.** "سؤال جميل، نجاوب عليه بعد ما نخلص الـ 20 دقيقة."
5. **Don't promise pricing on the demo.** "founder-approved" is the answer.

## The 5 things that make a demo great

1. **Open with the prospect's words.** Use the pain language from the discovery call.
2. **Show real impact in real time.** Click a button, show a result.
3. **Make it specific.** "في الأسبوع هذا، 5 leads ضايعة. لو ردينا على 2، كم هذا يساوي عندك؟"
4. **End with a decision.** "تحب نكمل؟" not "أي أسئلة؟"
5. **Send the follow-up within 1 hour.**

## The follow-up email (within 1 hour)

Subject: "الخطوة الجاية بعد الديمو"

> مرحبا [الاسم]،
>
> شكراً على الديمو. ملخص:
> 1. [Pain]
> 2. [What we showed]
> 3. [Next step]
>
> أرسل لك proposal خلال يومين. [التاريخ المحدد].
> لو عندك أي تعديل، قلّي.
>
> — [الاسم]

## When to update

- After 5 demos: if Phase 2 takes > 15 min, the demo is too broad — narrow.
- After 10 demos: if proposal-to-close < 20%, the close is weak — rewrite Phase 3.
- After 20 demos: if "send me info" deflections > 50%, the demo is too forward — pull back.
