# Demo Script (30 min)

Full bilingual script: [`../sales-kit/dealix_demo_script_30min.md`](../sales-kit/dealix_demo_script_30min.md).

## Structure
- 0-3 min: Rapport
- 3-10 min: Discovery (customer talks 60%)
- 10-20 min: Live demo on their scenario
- 20-25 min: ROI + the canonical ladder
- 25-28 min: Soft close — Free Diagnostic or 499 SAR Sprint
- 28-30 min: Next steps

## Golden rule
Customer speaks 60%, you speak 40%. Close to the canonical ladder
([`../OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md`](../OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md)) — never invent prices.
