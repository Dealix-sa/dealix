## 1. Vital stats (Saudi-specific) — is_estimate=true

- ~350-500 private hospitals + clinics (CR + MOH registry)
- ~80-150 active health-tech startups (Monsha'at + Wamda tracking)
- Vision 2030 target: 100% e-health coverage + telemedicine expansion
- Saudi e-health market: ~12-18 billion SAR by 2030 (estimate)
- Health-tech VC funding 2024: ~250M SAR raised across ~20 deals
- Compliance burden: PDPL + SFDA + MOH + Saudi Council for Health Specialties
- Insurance: CCHI (Council of Cooperative Health Insurance) framework drives B2B sales motion

## 2. Common pain (top 5)

1. **Patient data sovereignty pressure** — PDPL + MOH cross-border data restrictions
2. **Bilingual reporting** required for international medical tourism partners
3. **Insurance pre-authorization workflow** consuming staff hours
4. **Procurement cycles 60-180 days** — long sales motion
5. **Trust > features** — clinical evidence + governance > marketing flash
