# Saudi Consulting Sector Brief

> Source: `saudi_taxonomy.yaml#CONSULTING` · Effective: 2026-06-01
>
> **Strategic note:** Consulting firms are PRIMARILY channel partners,
> not direct customers. See AGENCY_PARTNER_OUTREACH.md for partnership
> motion. This brief covers the small subset that are direct customers.

## 1. Vital stats — is_estimate=true
- ~2,000+ active consulting firms in KSA
- Heavy government + enterprise client base
- Rapid growth post Vision 2030 (PIF + giga-projects)

## 2. Common pain (as direct customer)
1. Internal sales motion underdeveloped (focus on delivery)
2. Need warm intros + reputation outreach
3. Limited content marketing function
4. Bilingual thought leadership demands
5. Compliance for client data handling

## 3. Why Dealix wins
