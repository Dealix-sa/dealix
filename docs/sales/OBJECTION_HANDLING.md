## 1. "السعر مرتفع جدًا" · "Too expensive"

**AR — Reframe:**
"السؤال الصحيح ليس 'هل ٤٩٩ ر.س مرتفع' بل 'كم تخسر حاليًا من leads
لا يُتابع عليها؟'. متوسط شركة B2B سعودية يفقد ~٤٠٪ من leads بسبب
بطء التواصل. Sprint بـ ٤٩٩ ر.س يجيب على هذا السؤال بدقة لشركتكم
خلال ٧ أيام. لو الجواب 'لا نخسر شيئًا' — نعيد لك المبلغ."

**EN — Reframe:**
"The right question isn't 'is 499 SAR too much' — it's 'how many
leads are you losing today because nobody followed up in time?'
Saudi B2B averages ~40% leakage there. A 499 SAR Sprint quantifies
your specific number in 7 days. If the answer is 'we lose nothing,'
we refund."

**No-fake-proof rule:** the 40% figure is a category benchmark from
public reports — never quote without citation if used externally.

## Escalation playbook

If 3+ objections in one call:
1. Stop the pitch. Acknowledge it openly: "هذه أسئلة جدية — أحب
   فاوندر يسأل قبل التوقيع."
2. Propose a 30-min "no pitch, just questions" call later in the
   week.
3. Send 1-pager + Diagnostic option.
4. Founder personally follows up after 48h.

If objection is about the doctrine itself ("why not LinkedIn
automation?"): never compromise. The doctrine is the product.
