# Founder DM Pack

> The bilingual, scripted, founder-signed opening salvo.

## Principles

- One specific signal per DM.
- One question per DM.
- ≤ 6 sentences.
- Founder signature.
- No automation. No mail merge.

## Bilingual Variants

### Arabic

```
السلام عليكم [الاسم الأول]،

[إشارة محددة] في [الشركة] لفتتنا. عادة، هذا يعني [استنتاج محدد].

عندنا نموذج 3 حسابات يشبه نموذج عملاء [القطاع]. مدته 2 دقيقة قراءة.

تحب نشاركه؟

— سامي
دياليكس | Dealix
```

### English

```
Hi [first name],

Saw [specific signal] at [company]. Usually means [specific inference].

We put together a 3-account sample tailored to [their sector]. 2-minute
read.

Want it?

— Sami
Dealix
```

## Personalisation Layer

Each DM has, at minimum, one of:

- A specific named signal (hire, launch, funding, partnership, customer
  win).
- A specific named competitor mention.
- A specific recent piece of content from the prospect.

## Forbidden Openers

- "Hope you are doing well."
- "Quick question for you."
- "Can I get 5 minutes of your time?"
- "We help companies like yours..."
- Generic compliments.

## Tracking

Every DM logged in `dealix-ops-private/sales/dms_sent.csv` with:

- date_sent
- recipient_name
- recipient_company
- channel (LinkedIn / email)
- signal_cited
- reply (y / n)
- reply_quality (positive / neutral / negative / spam)
- next_action
