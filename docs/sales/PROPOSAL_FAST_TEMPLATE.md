# Proposal — Fast Template

> One page. Written same-day as the discovery call.

## Header

- To: [Prospect name + company]
- From: Sami (Dealix)
- Date: yyyy-mm-dd
- Valid until: 14 days from issue

## 1. What we heard

(3–5 bullets, paraphrasing the prospect's stated goals and constraints.)

## 2. What we recommend

One specific offer from `OFFER_LADDER.md`. Named.

- Rung: Sprint / Pilot / Retainer
- Scope: concrete deliverables
- Timeline: dates
- Price: SAR amount

## 3. Deliverables

- (concrete list, drawn from `OFFER_LADDER.md` for the chosen rung)
- (no vague items)

## 4. What you give us

- Confirmed ICP definition (we will write it down with you).
- Signed scope.
- Payment per `BILLING_POLICY.md`.

## 5. What we do not do

(2–3 specific items, e.g. "We do not guarantee closed revenue.", "We do
not send messages on your behalf without your per-send approval.")

## 6. Next step

- Reply with "go" by date.
- We will send invoice immediately.
- Delivery starts on payment / PO.

## Follow-Up Plan (private; not in customer-facing document)

- Day 0: send
- Day 2: follow-up note ("any questions on the scope?")
- Day 5: founder personal note
- Day 7: close (yes / no / specific blocker)
- Day 14: proposal expires; reissue requires fresh discovery

## Quality Standards

- One page. No more.
- Price is named.
- Scope is specific.
- Forbidden language scrubbed.
- No "we are excited" language.

## Discount Discipline

- ≤ 20% on standard pricing: founder approves T2.
- > 20%: founder T3 decision with named reason.
- Discounts in writing; never verbal.
