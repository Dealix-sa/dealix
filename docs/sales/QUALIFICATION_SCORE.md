## Decision bands

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **80+** | Pursue aggressively |
| **60–79** | Nurture / diagnostic |
| **40–59** | Low priority |
| **أقل من 40** | Disqualify |

Record the score and notes in your CRM or deal notes. If disqualified, document **why** for future playbook updates.
