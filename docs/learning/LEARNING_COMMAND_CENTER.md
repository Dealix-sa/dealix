# Learning Command Center — مركز قيادة التعلّم

## Purpose

Single index for the weekly intelligence rhythm. Every signal Dealix produces — pipeline, message, sector, pricing, delivery, win/loss — funnels here and exits as one of four outputs: a kept practice, a checklist, a playbook update, or a productized asset.

## Learning Rule (verbatim)

- Issue once = log it
- Twice = checklist item
- Three times = playbook update
- Five times = template
- Ten times = automate/productize

This rule is the only mechanism that converts noise into capital. Any signal observed without a logged owner is not learning.

## Operations

1. Every signal source writes to the router with a tag (sector, message, price, delivery, win/loss).
2. The weekly review aggregates by tag and applies the Learning Rule.
3. Outputs are committed: checklist edit, playbook PR, template file, productization candidate row.
4. Decisions, kills, doubles are logged in [COMPANY_MEMORY.md](./COMPANY_MEMORY.md).

## Evidence

- Weekly review notes in `dealix-ops-private/learning/weekly/YYYY-WW.md`.
- Monthly strategy notes in `dealix-ops-private/learning/monthly/YYYY-MM.md`.
- Each rule promotion (log to checklist to playbook to template to product) carries a commit SHA.

## Owner & cadence

- Learning Lead owns the index. Founder owns the monthly strategy update.
- Reviewed every Sunday alongside the trust ritual.

## AR — ملخّص

مركز قيادة التعلّم هو الفهرس الموحّد لإيقاع الأسبوعي. كل إشارة من خط الأنابيب أو الرسائل أو القطاعات أو التسعير أو التسليم تدخل هنا وتخرج بقرار: إبقاء، قائمة تحقق، تحديث playbook، أو منتج. القاعدة: مرة سجّل، مرتين قائمة تحقق، ثلاث تحديث playbook، خمس قالب، عشر منتج. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
