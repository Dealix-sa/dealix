# Monthly Strategy Update

A rolling, append-only file. One short paragraph per month.

## Template per entry

```
## YYYY-MM

### What changed in the market
One paragraph.

### What changed in Dealix
One paragraph.

### What we are betting on next month
One paragraph.

### What we are stopping
One paragraph.
```

## Rule
This file is the company's memory. It is short on purpose. The discipline is in the trimming, not in the volume.
