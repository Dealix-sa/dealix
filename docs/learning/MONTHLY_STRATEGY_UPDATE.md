# Monthly Strategy Update — التحديث الشهري للاستراتيجية

## Purpose

One meeting per month, 90 minutes, where the founder reconciles the last four weekly reviews into strategic outputs: confirmed decisions, kills, doubles, and the next 30-day operating focus. Annual plans drift in days. Monthly resets keep them honest.

## Agenda (90 minutes)

| Block | Minutes | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Last month read | 15 | what happened vs what we said would happen |
| Pipeline + revenue read | 15 | trajectory vs target; cohort effects |
| Sector + pricing read | 15 | any flags from [SECTOR_PERFORMANCE.md](./SECTOR_PERFORMANCE.md) and [PRICING_LEARNING.md](./PRICING_LEARNING.md) |
| Capital + productization | 15 | what was added to [docs/09_capital_os/CAPITAL_LEDGER.md](../09_capital_os/CAPITAL_LEDGER.md) and [docs/09_capital_os/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md](../09_capital_os/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md) |
| Trust + AI governance | 10 | incidents, audits, evals — see [docs/ai_management/AI_COMMAND_CENTER.md](../ai_management/AI_COMMAND_CENTER.md) |
| Decisions + kills + doubles | 20 | written, dated, owned |

## Outputs

```markdown
# Monthly Strategy — YYYY-MM

## Trajectory
- Revenue (estimated): actual / target / variance
- Active sprints: count
- Active retainers: count

## Decisions
- One line each, with owner and reversibility

## Kills
- What we stop doing, with the signal that triggered the kill

## Doubles
- What we amplify, with the evidence base

## Next-30 operating focus
- Three priorities, with measurable next steps

## Carry-overs
- Anything from last month still open, with new due date
```

## Operations

1. The note is committed to `dealix-ops-private/learning/monthly/YYYY-MM.md` by the second business day of the new month.
2. Every decision links to a dated evidence file. Naked claims are rejected at review.
3. Kills go into the permanent kills file. Doubles go into the permanent doubles file. Both feed [COMPANY_MEMORY.md](./COMPANY_MEMORY.md).
4. The next-30 focus replaces, not adds to, the previous focus list.

## Non-negotiables

- No carryover beyond two months without an explicit re-decision.
- No revenue claim stated as fact; all framed as estimated or observed until verified per [docs/08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md](../08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md).

## Evidence

- Monthly file in private ops repo.
- Linked PRs and ledger rows from the month.

## Owner & cadence

- Founder owns the meeting and the note.
- Held by the second business day of each month.

## AR — ملخّص

التحديث الشهري يجمع مراجعات الأسابيع الأربع في قرارات، إيقافات، مضاعفات، وتركيز الـ 30 يوماً القادمة. الناتج ملف مؤرّخ يربط كل ادّعاء بدليل. لا ترحيل بعد شهرين بلا قرار جديد. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
