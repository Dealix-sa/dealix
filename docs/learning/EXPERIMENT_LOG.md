# Experiment Log

> Every deliberate test of an assumption — pricing, message, channel, offer,
> delivery flow — gets a row here.

## Format

| ID | Date | Hypothesis | Variable | Sample | Success Metric | Result | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---:|---|---|---|

## Categories

- **Message** — outreach copy or hook.
- **Channel** — where we contact prospects.
- **Offer** — what we sell or how we package it.
- **Pricing** — price point, payment schedule, discount mechanic.
- **Delivery** — internal process or template change.
- **Agent** — change to an AI agent's prompt, model, or guardrail.

## Rules

1. Hypothesis is written before the test starts.
2. Sample size is pre-defined.
3. Result is recorded within 7 days of the test closing.
4. Decision is one of: Adopt, Adapt, Drop, Re-run.

## Linked Files

- `MESSAGE_PERFORMANCE.md` — message-level data.
- `PRICING_LEARNING.md` — pricing-specific outcomes.
- `SECTOR_PERFORMANCE.md` — by-segment outcomes.
- `WIN_LOSS_REVIEW.md` — deal-level outcomes.

## Anti-Patterns

- "Let's just try it" with no hypothesis.
- Changing two variables at once.
- Declaring success on N=1.
