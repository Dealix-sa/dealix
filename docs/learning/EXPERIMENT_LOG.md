# Experiment Log

Every deliberate test, recorded once.

| Date | Experiment | Hypothesis | Result | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | 25 DMs to accounting firms in Riyadh | Accounting firms reply faster than agencies | Pending | Continue |

## Template for a new entry

```
## Experiment ID
DLX-EXP-0001

## Date started
YYYY-MM-DD

## Experiment
What was tried.

## Hypothesis
What we expected.

## Method
How it was run.

## Result
What happened.

## Decision
Keep / Change / Kill.
```

## Rules
- Every experiment ends with a decision, even if the decision is "no signal yet, re-run".
- Decisions worth keeping become entries in `docs/founder/DECISION_LOG.md`.
- A failed experiment is a successful learning entry. Log it.
