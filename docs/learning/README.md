# Learning

## Purpose
Convert what the market teaches Dealix each week into changes the company actually makes.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Inputs
- Outreach results (DMs, replies, calls).
- Win/loss notes.
- Delivery QA failures.
- Trust escalations.
- Founder observations from calls.
- Experiment outcomes.

## Outputs
- Weekly Intelligence Review (`WEEKLY_INTELLIGENCE_REVIEW.md`).
- Updates to ICP, messaging, pricing, playbooks, or productization candidates.
- Experiment log.
- Decisions fed back into Revenue, Delivery, Trust, and Product.

## Rules
- Every week produces at least one learning decision tied to evidence.
- Repeated issues must update a checklist, template, or playbook - never just a memo.
- No learning is recorded without a source signal (call, delivery, QA, payment).
- Productization candidates only graduate after at least three repeating evidence points.

## Metrics
- Learning decisions per week.
- Playbooks / templates updated per month.
- Experiments completed vs. running.
- Conversion improvement traced to a learning decision.

## Evidence
- `docs/learning/WEEKLY_INTELLIGENCE_REVIEW.md`.
- private `learning/experiment_log.md`.
- weekly review files in private ops.
- updated playbooks linked to the source decision.
