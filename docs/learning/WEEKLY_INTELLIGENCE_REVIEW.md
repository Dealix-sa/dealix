# Weekly Intelligence Review — المراجعة الأسبوعية للذكاء التشغيلي

## Purpose

Convert one week of signals into one page of decisions. The review runs every Sunday, lasts 60 minutes, and produces a dated note that another founder could read in three minutes and act on.

## Agenda (60 minutes)

| Block | Minutes | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pipeline read | 10 | Founder | leads, DMs, samples, proposals vs target |
| Delivery read | 10 | Delivery Lead | sprints in flight, evidence completeness |
| Message performance | 10 | Content Lead | variants tested, winners, drops |
| Win/loss closures | 10 | Sales Lead | each closed deal categorized |
| Router promotions | 10 | Learning Lead | what advanced under the Learning Rule |
| Decisions + kills + doubles | 10 | Founder | written, dated, owned |

## Template

```markdown
# Weekly Review — YYYY-WW

## Pipeline
- Leads: actual / target
- DMs queued: actual / target
- Samples sent: actual / target
- Proposals out: actual / target

## Delivery
- Sprints in flight: list
- Evidence gaps: list (with owner + due date)

## Message performance
- Top variant: id, signal
- Killed variants: ids
- New variant queued: id

## Win/loss
- Closed-won: id, sector, root cause
- Closed-lost: id, sector, root cause

## Router promotions
- Signal → checklist | playbook | template | productized

## Decisions
- One sentence each. Owner. Reversibility.

## Kills

## Doubles

## Open questions for next week
```

## Operations

1. Inputs are pulled from the dashboard (see [docs/dashboard/CEO_DASHBOARD_V2.md](../dashboard/CEO_DASHBOARD_V2.md)) and the router.
2. The note is committed to `dealix-ops-private/learning/weekly/YYYY-WW.md` by Sunday 21:00 KSA.
3. Anything promoted produces a PR or a row in the relevant ledger before the meeting ends.
4. The note links every claim back to evidence — no naked assertions.

## Evidence

- Weekly file in the private ops repo.
- Promotion rows in [docs/09_capital_os/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md](../09_capital_os/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md) when applicable.

## Owner & cadence

- Founder runs the meeting. Learning Lead curates the agenda.
- Sundays, weekly, no exceptions. Missed week is itself a logged signal.

## AR — ملخّص

المراجعة الأسبوعية تحوّل إشارات الأسبوع إلى صفحة قرارات. مدّتها 60 دقيقة، مخرجها ملف مؤرّخ يشمل خط الأنابيب، التسليم، أداء الرسائل، فوز/خسارة، ترقيات الموجّه، وقرارات/إيقافات/مضاعفات. لا ادّعاء بلا دليل. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
