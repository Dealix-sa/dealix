# Win/Loss System
## Purpose
Turn every won, lost, or stalled opportunity into learning.
## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.
## Review Cadence
Weekly.
## Win Questions
- Why did they respond?
- Why did they trust us?
- What pain was clear?
- What made the offer valuable?
- What should be repeated?
## Loss Questions
- Was ICP wrong?
- Was message weak?
- Was timing wrong?
- Was price wrong?
- Was trust unclear?
- Was payment path unclear?
## Decision
Build / Fix / Kill / Defer / Continue
## Evidence
- win_loss_log.md
- proposal_tracker.csv
- pipeline_tracker.csv
- weekly review
