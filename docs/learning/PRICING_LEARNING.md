# Pricing Learning

Where everything we have learned about pricing lives.

## Inputs
- `docs/revenue/PRICING_EXPERIMENTS.md`.
- `WIN_LOSS_REVIEW.md`.
- Client feedback referencing price or value.

## What to track
- Floor prices that stuck.
- Floor prices that broke (lost too many deals).
- Negotiation patterns by sector.
- Most common objection per price band.
- Discount requests and what triggered them.

## Outputs
- Annual pricing point of view, summarized here.
- Inputs to the Monthly Strategy Review.

## Rule
A price change without a reason from this file is a guess. Reasons over instincts.
