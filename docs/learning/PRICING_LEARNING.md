# Pricing Learning — تعلّم التسعير

## Purpose

Detect when Dealix pricing is off — too low, too high, or wrong structure — and adjust deliberately, not reactively. Pricing changes follow evidence, not anecdote.

## Signals that pricing is off

| Signal | Direction | What it suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Proposal-to-signed > 70% with no negotiation | likely under-priced | raise anchor in next cohort |
| Proposal-to-signed < 15% across three quarters in fit sectors | likely over-priced or wrong structure | test lower-anchor or staged offer |
| Repeat scope creep without change-orders | structure off | move to milestone or retainer per [docs/03_commercial_mvp/RETAINER_PATH.md](../03_commercial_mvp/RETAINER_PATH.md) |
| Buyers anchor against a named alternative | positioning gap | revisit [docs/01_category/](../01_category/) |
| Buyers ask "what does Verified value look like?" | proof gap, not price gap | add case-safe summary, hold price |
| Long payment terms become normal | cash structure off | add deposit, shorten net |

## Definitions

- **Anchor** — the headline price first stated in the proposal.
- **Structure** — sprint fixed-fee vs milestone vs retainer; see [docs/03_commercial_mvp/SPRINT_PRICING_LOGIC.md](../03_commercial_mvp/SPRINT_PRICING_LOGIC.md).
- **Adjustment** — a deliberate change, logged with date and reason, applied to a forward cohort.

## When to adjust

1. Pricing is reviewed monthly against the signals table.
2. An adjustment proposal is written with: signal evidence, hypothesis, cohort, expected effect, rollback rule.
3. Adjustments apply only to new proposals; in-flight scopes are honored.
4. Effect is measured after a minimum of five proposals in the new cohort or 60 days, whichever comes later.

## Non-negotiables

- No price promise tied to a guaranteed outcome. Pricing reflects scope and evidence, not promised sales.
- Discounts are logged with reason. Repeated discounting at the same threshold is a signal the anchor is wrong, not the deal.

## Evidence

- Pricing change log: `dealix-ops-private/learning/pricing_changes.md`.
- Linked proposals show the anchor used and any discount applied.

## Owner & cadence

- Founder owns the adjustment decision.
- Sales Lead surfaces the signals at the monthly strategy update.

## AR — ملخّص

تعلّم التسعير يكتشف متى يكون السعر أو الهيكل خطأ، عبر إشارات محدّدة: تحوّل أعلى من 70% بلا تفاوض، أو أقل من 15% في قطاعات ملائمة، أو زحف نطاق دون أوامر تغيير. التعديل شهري، بشواهد، على cohort مقبل، مع قاعدة تراجع. لا وعد بنتائج. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
