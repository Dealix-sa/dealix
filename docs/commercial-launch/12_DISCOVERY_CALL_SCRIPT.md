# Discovery Call Script / نص مكالمة الاكتشاف

1. Frame (2 min): "Goal is to see if a reviewed AI workflow saves you time. No pressure."
2. Current state: Where do work items live? What breaks at month-end?
3. Pain quantification: How many hours/week on manual reporting?
4. Approval & data: Who approves outgoing reports? Any data constraints?
5. Trigger & timeline: What changed recently? Decision window?
6. Recommend: Map pain → AI Workflow Audit. Confirm scope & price (SAR).
7. Close: Agree diagnostic start date + manual next steps. No auto-send.
