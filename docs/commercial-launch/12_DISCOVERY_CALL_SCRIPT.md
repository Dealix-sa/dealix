# Discovery Call Script (15–30 min)

## 1. Frame (1 min)
"Goal today: understand where work leaks, and whether a short Audit is worth it.
No pressure, no automation — you approve everything."

## 2. Current state (8 min)
- Walk me through how {core workflow} happens today, start to finish.
- Where does it live — WhatsApp, email, spreadsheets, a system?
- Who touches it, and where does it get stuck?
- How do you report on it today, and how long does that take?

## 3. Pain & cost (5 min)
- What does that bottleneck cost you — time, deals, SLA penalties, rework?
- What's the trigger that made this matter now?
- If this worked perfectly, what would change?

## 4. Fit & next step (5 min)
- Recommend Audit or Pilot based on what you heard.
- State scope, timeline, price range (SAR), acceptance criteria.
- Reaffirm: data minimization, your approval on every step, NDA/DPA if needed.

## 5. Close
"Shall I send a one-page Audit proposal today? You decide after reading it."

## Disqualify if
No recurring workflow, no willingness to define acceptance criteria, expectation
of automated cold outreach, or demand for guaranteed ROI.
