# Objection Handling / معالجة الاعتراضات

Each objection: short response, detailed response, next action, continue/stop.

## Price is high / السعر عالٍ
- Short: Start with the low-risk paid diagnostic.
- Detailed: The diagnostic pays for itself by quantifying the bottleneck before any larger commitment.
- Next action: Offer AI Workflow Audit. Continue.

## We have a CRM / عندنا CRM
- Short: We sit on top, not replace.
- Detailed: Reviewed workflows complement your CRM; no rip-and-replace. Continue.

## We have IT / عندنا IT
- Short: We accelerate your IT, not bypass it. Continue.

## We don't need AI / لا نحتاج ذكاء اصطناعي
- Short: It's about reviewed workflow time saved, not AI for its own sake. Continue.

## Data concerns / نخاف على البيانات
- Short: Sample data only, approval gates, privacy-first, your control. Continue.

## Send info / أرسلوا معلومات
- Short: Sending a one-page offer; suggest a 15-min call. Continue.

## Not now / ليس الآن
- Short: Understood — may I follow up after <trigger>? Continue (scheduled).

## Who are you / من أنتم
- Short: Saudi/GCC B2B AI Revenue & Operations OS; share positioning one-pager. Continue.

## Case studies? / هل لديكم حالات
- Short: Share redacted sample proof asset. Continue.

## Does it send automatically? / هل يرسل تلقائيًا
- Short: No — drafts only; you approve and send manually. Continue.

## Is data safe? / هل البيانات آمنة
- Short: PDPL-aware, sample data only until agreement. Continue.

## Big change? / تغيير كبير
- Short: No — additive on top of current tools. Continue.

## Integrations? / تكامل مع أنظمتنا
- Short: Yes in Department OS/Enterprise; not required for diagnostic. Continue.
