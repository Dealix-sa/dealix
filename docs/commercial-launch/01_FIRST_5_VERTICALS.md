## 1. Facilities Management & Maintenance — إدارة المرافق والصيانة

**ID:** `facilities_management`

### ICP
- **EN:** FM and maintenance operators (200+ assets / multi-site) running reactive work orders across WhatsApp, Excel and ageing CAFM, with SLA penalties and revenue leaking through unbilled or mis-scoped jobs.
- **AR:** شركات إدارة المرافق والصيانة (200+ أصل أو مواقع متعددة) تدير أوامر العمل بشكل تفاعلي عبر واتساب وإكسل وأنظمة CAFM قديمة، مع غرامات SLA وتسرب إيراد بسبب أعمال غير مفوترة أو سوء تحديد النطاق.

### Buyer personas
- Operations Director
- FM Contract Manager
- Maintenance Manager
- Finance Controller

### Buyer titles
- Operations Director
- Head of Facilities
- FM Account Manager
- Maintenance Manager
- CAFM/Systems Lead
- Finance Manager

### Pain map
- SLA breaches and penalties from missed or untracked work orders
- Unbilled or under-scoped jobs leaking margin every month
- Reactive scheduling with no single source of truth across sites
- Manual handoffs between WhatsApp, Excel and CAFM
- No evidence trail when a client disputes completion

### Offer angle
- **EN:** Govern the work-order-to-invoice workflow so SLA risk and unbilled jobs surface before they cost you.
- **AR:** حوكمة مسار أمر العمل حتى الفاتورة بحيث تظهر مخاطر SLA والأعمال غير المفوترة قبل أن تكلفك.

### Offer ladder for this vertical
- **Entry:** AI Workflow Audit (Operations) (1,500–2,500 SAR)
- **Pilot:** Paid Pilot (5,000–25,000 SAR)
- **Retainer:** Monthly Retainer (Governed Ops) (3,000–25,000 SAR/mo)

### Proof asset required
- Work-order leakage map: a sample audit of 30 anonymized work orders showing SLA risk and unbilled value, with the top 3 governed fixes.

### Messaging angles (EN)
- Where SLA penalties are quietly accruing across sites
- How many work orders close without a billable record
- The 3 handoffs where completion evidence disappears

### Messaging angles (AR)
- أين تتراكم غرامات SLA بهدوء عبر المواقع
- كم أمر عمل يُغلق دون سجل قابل للفوترة
- أين تختفي أدلة الإنجاز في 3 نقاط تسليم

### Objections
- We already have a CAFM system
- Our team is too busy to change anything now
- Data is sensitive and lives with the client

### Qualification rules
- 200+ managed assets or 3+ sites
- Uses work orders / SLAs
- Has a named operations owner
- Some digital trail (CAFM/Excel) exists

### Disqualification rules
- Single technician / hobby operator
- No SLAs and no recurring contracts
- Pure hardware reseller with no service ops

### Compliance notes
- Never claim access to their CAFM or client data. Frame the audit as founder-reviewed and evidence-first on data they choose to share.

### Delivery promise
- A founder-reviewed leakage map within 7 business days of receiving a redacted work-order sample. No system access required.

### Success metrics
- SLA breach rate
- Unbilled work-order value recovered
- Work-order cycle time
- First-time-fix rate

## 2. Contracting & Project Controls — المقاولات وضبط المشاريع

**ID:** `contracting_project_controls`

### ICP
- **EN:** Mid-size contractors and project-controls teams managing multiple concurrent projects where variation orders, progress claims and subcontractor payments slip through manual approvals.
- **AR:** شركات مقاولات متوسطة وفِرق ضبط مشاريع تدير عدة مشاريع متزامنة حيث تتسرب أوامر التغيير والمستخلصات ومدفوعات مقاولي الباطن عبر اعتمادات يدوية.

### Buyer personas
- Projects Director
- Project Controls Manager
- Commercial/QS Manager
- CFO

### Buyer titles
- Projects Director
- Head of Project Controls
- Commercial Manager
- Quantity Surveyor
- Planning Manager
- CFO

### Pain map
- Variation orders approved late or lost, eroding claimable value
- Progress claims delayed by manual evidence gathering
- No governed approval trail when disputes go to arbitration
- Subcontractor payment certificates reconciled by hand
- Cash flow surprises from slow claim cycles

### Offer angle
- **EN:** Make the variation-to-claim approval chain evidenced and governed so claimable value is not lost to slow paperwork.
- **AR:** اجعل سلسلة اعتماد أمر التغيير حتى المستخلص موثّقة ومحوكمة حتى لا تُفقد القيمة القابلة للمطالبة بسبب بطء الأوراق.

- **Entry:** AI Workflow Audit (Operations) (1,500–2,500 SAR)
- **Pilot:** Paid Pilot (5,000–25,000 SAR)
- **Retainer:** Monthly Retainer (Governed Ops) (3,000–25,000 SAR/mo)

### Proof asset required
- Variation-order governance map: a redacted review of one project's variations showing approval gaps and at-risk claimable value.

### Messaging angles (EN)
- Variation value at risk because approvals lag the work
- How long a progress claim waits on manual evidence
- The approval steps with no audit trail for arbitration

### Messaging angles (AR)
- قيمة أوامر التغيير المعرّضة للخطر لأن الاعتماد متأخر عن التنفيذ
- كم ينتظر المستخلص أدلة يدوية
- خطوات الاعتماد بلا سجل تدقيق للتحكيم

### Objections
- We use Primavera/MS Project already
- Our QS team handles this manually fine
- Every project is different

### Qualification rules
- 3+ concurrent projects
- Uses variation orders and progress claims
- Named project-controls or commercial owner
- Recurring subcontractor payments

### Disqualification rules
- Single small fit-out job
- No formal claims process
- Pure labor-supply with no project controls

### Compliance notes
- Do not claim to access project ERP or contracts. Position as a governance map on a redacted sample the contractor chooses to share.

### Delivery promise
- A founder-reviewed variation-governance map within 7 business days of a redacted project sample. Read-only, no ERP access.

### Success metrics
- Claimable value recovered
- Claim cycle time
- Approval lead time
- Dispute exposure reduced

- **AR:** مكاتب المحاماة وشركات الخدمات المهنية حيث يعتمد استقبال القضايا وفحوص تعارض المصالح والفوترة وإنجاز المستندات على وقت الكبار والمتابعة اليدوية.

- Knowledge Manager
- Billing Manager
