# Retention & Expansion

## Retainer triggers
- Pilot/Department OS shipped and accepted.
- Client wants ongoing monitoring, improvements and reporting.

## Expansion path
1. Audit → Pilot (prove one workflow)
2. Pilot → Department OS (productionize)
3. Department OS → Retainer (sustain)
4. Retainer → second department / Enterprise (expand)

## Expansion signals
- New workflow pain surfaced during delivery
- New department asks for the same system
- Leadership wants cross-workflow governance

## Rules
- Expansion is proposed, never auto-charged.
- Each expansion gets its own scope and acceptance criteria.
- Renewal conversations use real delivery evidence, not inflated claims.
