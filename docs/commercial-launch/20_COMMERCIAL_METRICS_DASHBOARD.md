> Doctrine: AI generates and ranks drafts only. Founder reviews and approves.
> No external sending. No SMTP. No WhatsApp cold outreach. No LinkedIn automation. No scraping.

# Commercial Metrics Dashboard / لوحة المقاييس التجارية

Run: `python scripts/commercial_metrics_summary.py`

## Auto metrics (from the OS) / مقاييس آلية
- drafts_generated, founder_review_count, rejected_quality, rejected_compliance, needs_research, approved_manual, safety_violations, compliance_rejections, top_vertical, top_channel.

## Manual metrics (founder input only) / مقاييس يدوية
- manual_sent, replies_positive, replies_negative, reply_rate, qualified_calls, diagnostics_sold, pilots_sold, retainers_started, revenue_pipeline_sar, realized_revenue_sar, top_objection.

> Revenue is NEVER assumed. revenue_pipeline_sar and realized_revenue_sar are manual inputs (zero/sample by default).
