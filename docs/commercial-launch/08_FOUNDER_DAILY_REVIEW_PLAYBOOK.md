> Doctrine: AI generates and ranks drafts only. Founder reviews and approves.
> No external sending. No SMTP. No WhatsApp cold outreach. No LinkedIn automation. No scraping.

# Founder Daily Review Playbook / كتيب المراجعة اليومية للمؤسس

1. Run the generator: `python scripts/commercial_generate_400_drafts.py --target 400`
2. Open `outputs/commercial_launch/<DATE>/founder_review.md` and `top_50_priority.md`.
3. Select the top 20–50; check safety flags and risk levels.
4. Approve manually only. AI never sends.
5. Copy approved drafts by hand into the chosen channel (after external readiness for email).
6. Record replies and outcomes in `approved_manual_sends.example.csv` (your working copy).
7. Update objections and next-day sector focus in `next_actions.md`.
