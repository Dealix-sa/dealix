# Vertical Playbook — Legal & Professional Services
## المكاتب القانونية والخدمات المهنية

> **Privacy-first vertical.** Every message and workflow here leads with privacy,
> consent, data minimization, and "you approve every step". The compliance gate
> rejects any draft to this vertical that lacks consent-aware language.

### Sector thesis
Law firms and professional-services practices leak time and trust where matter
intake, engagement letters, billing follow-up and document workflows are manual.
Dealix installs a practice-ops OS — privacy-first, founder-approved.

### Why now
Growth in partners and practices, billing leakage, and compliance reviews make a
disciplined intake-to-billing OS valuable.

### ICP
Law firms and professional-services practices (10–300 staff) in KSA/GCC.

### Excluded ICP
Solo practitioners with no recurring intake; anyone wanting bulk cold outreach.

### Buyer personas & titles
- **Decision maker:** Managing Partner / Practice Manager / Operations Lead / Partner
- **Budget owner:** Managing Partner

### Top workflows
Matter intake → conflict check → engagement letter → time/billing → document
workflow → client status update.

### Top 10 pains
1. Manual matter intake & conflict checks
2. Inconsistent engagement letters/proposals
3. Leaky time & billing follow-up
4. Document workflows scattered across drives
5. Ad-hoc client status updates
6. No standard matter lifecycle
7. Missed billing milestones
8. Knowledge trapped with individuals
9. Compliance evidence hard to assemble
10. Renewal/retainer conversations under-managed

### Trigger events / buying signals
New partner, practice expansion, compliance review, billing-leakage finding.

### Offer ladder mapping
Audit (intake→billing map) → Pilot on one practice area → Department OS
(practice ops) → Retainer → Enterprise (multi-office governance).

### Messaging angles (privacy-first)
- **AR:** "خصوصيتك تحت سيطرتك. نبني نظام تشغيل لاستقبال القضايا والفوترة بتقليل البيانات — وأنت تعتمد كل خطوة."
- **EN:** "Your privacy, your control. We install an intake-to-billing OS with data minimization — and you approve every step."

### Examples / discovery / proposal
All examples carry explicit consent and privacy language. Discovery: how are
matters intaken, where does billing leak, how are documents controlled, how is
client status communicated? Proposal anchors on billing leakage or intake risk
with a privacy-by-design statement.

### Proof asset, delivery, metrics
Intake checklist, engagement-letter template, billing-milestone tracker.
Success: intake time ↓, billing leakage ↓, status updates standardized (targets).

### Risks & compliance
Highest-sensitivity vertical: PDPL-aware, NDA + DPA before any data, data
minimization, human-in-the-loop. No processing of sensitive data before a signed
agreement.

### What not to say / objections / founder notes
Never imply familiarity or "from our database". "We have confidentiality
concerns" → privacy-by-design, you approve every step, minimal data. Treat as a
trust-led, slower but high-value vertical.
