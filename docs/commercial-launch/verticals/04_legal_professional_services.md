# Vertical Playbook 04 — Legal & Professional Services (SENSITIVE)
# دليل القطاع 04 — المكاتب القانونية والخدمات المهنية (قطاع حسّاس)

> AI recommends and drafts. Founder approves. Nothing is sent automatically.
> **Sensitive sector:** privacy-first, human-in-the-loop language is MANDATORY.
> Draft-only. Every email draft includes an opt-out. Never imply replacing a lawyer.

## ICP — العميل المثالي
Law firms and professional-services practices (10–500 professionals) in KSA/GCC.
مكاتب قانونية وخدمات مهنية.

## Buyer personas — شخصيات المشتري
Managing partner (firm performance), operations partner (throughput), head of
knowledge (reuse), practice manager (intake/billing).

## Buyer titles — المسميات
EN: Managing Partner, Operations Partner, Head of Knowledge, Practice Manager.
AR: الشريك الإداري، شريك العمليات، رئيس إدارة المعرفة، مدير الممارسة.

## Company size fit — حجم الشركة
10–500 professionals.

## Geography fit — النطاق الجغرافي
KSA primary; GCC secondary.

## Pain map — خريطة الألم
- Manual intake / matter triage workload — عبء فرز القضايا يدويًا.
- Slow document-drafting turnaround — بطء صياغة المستندات.
- Knowledge trapped in individual inboxes — معرفة محتجزة لدى الأفراد.
- Billing / time-capture leakage — تسرّب الفوترة وتسجيل الوقت.

## Trigger events — أحداث محفّزة
Practice expansion; new partner hire; client-volume spike; a knowledge initiative.

## Discovery questions — أسئلة الاكتشاف
1. How are new matters intaken and triaged today?
2. Where does drafting turnaround slow down?
3. How is firm knowledge reused across matters?
4. Where does time-capture leak?
5. Which task would you most want supported — under your control?

## Qualification criteria — معايير التأهيل
10+ professionals; an operations/knowledge owner; named throughput pain; openness
to human-in-the-loop support (not replacement).

## Disqualification criteria — معايير الاستبعاد
Solo practitioner with no ops owner; expects to "replace lawyers"; unwilling to keep
human review; wants client data shared before any agreement.

## Offers — العروض
Entry audit (499–2,500); supported drafting/intake pilot on limited, consented data
(5,000–25,000); knowledge/ops retainer (3,000–25,000/mo); enterprise practice OS (150,000+).

## Arabic messaging angles
"ندعم فرق المكاتب القانونية في فرز القضايا وتسريع الصياغة مع بقاء المراجعة البشرية
في كل خطوة — الذكاء يقترح، والمحامي يقرر، وبياناتكم تبقى لديكم. لا نستبدل المحامي."

## English messaging angles
"We support legal teams on intake and drafting speed while human review stays on
every step — the AI drafts, the lawyer decides, your data stays with you. We do
not replace the lawyer."

## Cold email draft example (review-only)
Subject: Supporting your intake and drafting — under your control
Body: Hi {Managing Partner} at {Firm}. We support legal teams on matter intake and
drafting turnaround with a system where human review stays on every step — it
drafts, your lawyers decide, and your data stays with you. We start with the Entry
Diagnostic; no client data is needed for the audit.
CTA: Would a short 15-minute call this week work for you?
Opt-out: Reply STOP to opt out.

## Follow-up draft example
Following up on my earlier note — the goal is to support intake/drafting with your
lawyers fully in control, starting with a short diagnostic that needs no client data.
Should I send a one-page brief? Reply STOP to opt out.

## LinkedIn manual draft example
{Title} at {Firm} — we support legal teams on intake/drafting speed with human
review on every step (we don't replace lawyers). Happy to share a short overview?

## Website form draft example
This is Dealix. We support legal teams on intake and drafting turnaround with human
review on every step, your data stays with you, starting with a short diagnostic
that needs no client data. We'd welcome a brief call.

## Objection handling
- "Will this replace our lawyers?" → No. It supports them; the lawyer decides on
  every step. Next: audit with no client data.
- "Client confidentiality?" → Privacy-first, limited and consented data only, your
  data stays with you, human review throughout. Next: scope + DPA before any data.

## Proof assets required
Intake/drafting workflow map, privacy-first audit template, DPA template (out of repo).

## Delivery scope
Audit maps intake/drafting/knowledge/billing flow with **no client data**.
Diagnoses top pains, recommends a privacy-first pilot, lists risks.

## Implementation timeline
Audit 3–7 days; pilot 14–30 days (consented, limited data); department OS 30–90 days.

## Success metrics
Faster intake triage, faster drafting turnaround, better knowledge reuse, less
time-capture leakage. Measured in pilot, never guaranteed up front.

## Compliance notes
**Sensitive.** Privacy-first language mandatory; opt-out on every email; no client
data before a signed agreement/DPA; PDPL alignment; human review always on.

## Risk notes
Highest-risk vertical. Never imply replacing lawyers, never imply data access,
never request client data in first touch, never claim guaranteed outcomes.

## Founder review notes
Treat every legal draft as medium+ risk by default. Double-check privacy-first
language present. Verify firm before any manual send.

## No-go claims
No "replace your lawyer", no "full automation", no guaranteed outcomes, no
"we accessed your data", no client-data requests up front.

## What not to say
Don't imply autonomy over legal judgment; don't request confidential data; one
offer per message; never fake familiarity.
