# Consulting and B2B Training — الاستشارات والتدريب للأعمال

Vertical playbook. AI drafts and ranks; the client's team reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

## ICP — العميل المثالي

Consulting firms and B2B training providers in Saudi Arabia producing high volumes of proposals, reports, and client communications, where quality and tone consistency matter.

شركات الاستشارات ومزوّدو التدريب للأعمال في السعودية الذين ينتجون كميات كبيرة من العروض والتقارير ومراسلات العملاء، حيث تهم جودة المحتوى واتساق النبرة.

## Excluded ICP — العميل المُستبعد

Solo consultants with low document volume; buyers expecting unreviewed client deliverables.

## Buyer personas — شخصيات المشتري

- Partner/principal focused on proposal throughput.
- Delivery lead focused on report consistency.

## Buyer titles — مسميات المشتري

Managing Partner, Principal, Engagement Manager, Head of Delivery, Business Development Lead.

## Top pains — أبرز الآلام

- Slow proposal drafting.
- Inconsistent report quality across consultants.
- Repetitive client communications consuming billable time.

بطء صياغة العروض؛ جودة تقارير غير متسقة؛ مراسلات عملاء متكررة تستهلك وقتًا قابلًا للفوترة.

## Triggers — المحفزات

Proposal volume spikes, hiring constraints, push for delivery standardization.

## Discovery questions — أسئلة الاستكشاف

1. How many proposals do you draft monthly?
2. Where does report quality vary?
3. Who reviews client deliverables before they go out?
4. What would faster, consistent first drafts free up?

## Arabic drafts — مسودات عربية

**DRAFT for founder review — outreach.**
> مرحبًا [الاسم]،
> أعمل مع شركات الاستشارات والتدريب على تسريع المسودات الأولى للعروض والتقارير مع بقاء المراجعة لديكم. الذكاء الاصطناعي يجهّز المسودة وفريقكم يراجع ويرسل. إن كانت نظرة قصيرة على سير عملكم مفيدة، يسعدني مشاركة تدقيق مختصر.
> إن لم يكن مناسبًا، ردّوا بـ "غير مهتم" ولن أتابع.

## English drafts — مسودات إنجليزية

**DRAFT for founder review — outreach.**
> Hello [Name],
> I work with consulting and training firms on speeding up first drafts of proposals and reports, with your team's review kept in place. AI prepares the draft; your team reviews and sends. If a short look at your workflows is useful, I can share a brief audit.
> If this isn't relevant, reply "no thanks" and I won't follow up.

## Objections — الاعتراضات

See [09_OBJECTION_HANDLING.md](../09_OBJECTION_HANDLING.md). Sector-specific: "Our work is bespoke" → drafts handle the repetitive scaffold; your experts shape the substance.

## Delivery scope — نطاق التسليم

Draft templates for proposals, report sections, and client communications; ranked queue; manual send by the client.

## Proof assets — أصول الإثبات

Anonymized proposal-drafting time baseline vs observed. Labeled "Hypothetical / case-safe template" unless client consents.

## Compliance notes — ملاحظات الامتثال

No external sending by the system. No client engagement PII in case assets. See [07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md](../07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md).

## What not to say — ما لا يُقال

No "automate everything", no "replace your team", no "guaranteed ROI", no "100%". Never imply unreviewed client deliverables.

## Success metrics — مقاييس النجاح

Estimated time saved per proposal/report; first-draft consistency (reviewer-rated); proposal throughput (observed). Labeled estimated until verified.
