# Vertical Playbook — Consulting, Training & B2B Services
## الاستشارات والتدريب وخدمات B2B

### Sector thesis
Consulting/training/B2B-services firms rebuild proposals from scratch, keep the
pipeline in one person's head, and lose context at delivery handover. Dealix
installs a revenue-ops OS for proposals, pipeline, follow-up and handover —
founder-approved.

### Why now
New service lines, team growth, and competitive proposals make a repeatable
revenue OS a direct margin lever.

### ICP
Consulting, training and B2B-services firms (5–200 staff) in KSA/GCC.

### Excluded ICP
One-off freelancers; firms with no repeatable offer.

### Buyer personas & titles
- **Decision maker:** Managing Director / Founder / Business Development Lead
- **Influencer:** Delivery leads, BD managers
- **Budget owner:** MD / Founder

### Top workflows
Lead → qualification → proposal → follow-up → delivery handover → renewal/upsell.

### Top 10 pains
1. Proposals built from scratch every time
2. Pipeline lives in one person's head
3. Inconsistent post-meeting follow-up
4. Delivery handover loses context
5. Missed renewals/upsells
6. No standard qualification
7. Win/loss never analyzed
8. Quoting inconsistent
9. Reporting to partners is manual
10. Referrals not systematically pursued

### Trigger events / buying signals
New service line, team growth, lost-deal review, renewal season, new BD hire.

### Offer ladder mapping
Audit (pipeline & proposal map) → Pilot on one offer/pipeline → Department OS
(revenue ops) → Retainer → Enterprise (multi-team).

### Messaging angles
- **AR:** "خط الأنابيب في ذهن شخص واحد؟ نبني نظام تشغيل للعروض والمتابعة والتسليم — أنت تعتمد كل خطوة."
- **EN:** "If your pipeline lives in one person's head, growth is fragile. We install a revenue-ops OS for proposals, follow-up and handover — you approve every step."

### Examples / discovery / proposal
Governed examples (single offer, opt-out on email, manual LinkedIn). Discovery:
how are proposals built, where does the pipeline live, what happens after a
meeting, how is delivery handed over? Proposal anchors on proposal-cycle time or
pipeline fragility.

### Proof asset, delivery, metrics
Proposal template library, pipeline board, handover checklist. Success: proposal
time ↓, follow-up consistency ↑, renewals captured (targets).

### Risks & compliance / what not to say / objections / founder notes
Client data minimization; public contact only for outreach. No guaranteed ROI.
"We already do this in spreadsheets" → that's the fragility; we make it a system.
Best dogfood vertical — Dealix runs its own GTM on this OS.
