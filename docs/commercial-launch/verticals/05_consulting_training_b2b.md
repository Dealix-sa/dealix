# Vertical Playbook — Consulting, Training & B2B Services

> **AR:** الاستشارات والتدريب وخدمات الأعمال · **Non-negotiable:** AI drafts/scores/recommends; founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

## ICP
Boutique consultancies, training providers, and B2B service firms selling repeatable engagements in KSA/GCC.

## Excluded ICP
B2C-only trainers; one-off freelancers with no pipeline.

## Buyer personas
- Founder/MD (DM)
- Engagement Lead (influencer)
- Coordinator (user)
- Owner (budget owner)

- **Decision maker:** Founder/MD (DM)
- **Influencer:** Engagement Lead (influencer)
- **User:** Coordinator (user)
- **Budget owner:** Owner (budget owner)

## Main workflows
- Lead qualification
- Proposal draft prep (review-only)
- Engagement reporting
- Follow-up sequencing (manual)

## Top pains
- Inconsistent pipeline
- Proposal turnaround
- Engagement reporting
- Follow-up gaps

## Trigger events
- Pipeline dry spell
- Scaling delivery team
- Client reporting demands
- Lost-deal pattern

## Buying signals
- Hiring BD/coordinators
- Complaints about proposal speed
- Manual CRM

## Disqualification signals
- No repeatable offer
- B2C only

## Offer ladder (SAR)
- **AI Workflow Audit** — 499–2,500 SAR
- **Paid Pilot** — 5,000–25,000 SAR
- **Department OS** — 25,000–150,000 SAR
- **Monthly Retainer** — 3,000–25,000 SAR/month
- **Enterprise Custom OS** — 150,000+ SAR
- **Entry offer:** AI Workflow Audit (499–2,500 SAR)
- **Pilot offer:** Paid Pilot (5,000–25,000 SAR)
- **Department OS:** 25,000–150,000 SAR
- **Retainer:** 3,000–25,000 SAR/month
- **Enterprise:** 150,000+ SAR

## Objections
- "Is this spam?" → Review-only, opt-in, opt-out honored, founder sends personally.
- "ROI?" → We show evidence and estimates, never guarantees.
- "We do it manually." → That's the point — we prepare, you stay in control.

## Delivery scope
- Inputs, outputs, timeline, acceptance, security boundary, handover, upsell path (see `docs/delivery-os/`).

## Proof assets
- Sample proposal
- Engagement report template

## Compliance notes
- Opt-in / opt-out respected; PDPL-aware; no scraping; no auto-send.
- No sensitive client data processed before agreement.

## What NOT to say
- No guaranteed ROI / revenue numbers.
- No "fully automated outreach" claims.
- No unverified client names or results.

## Success metrics
- Audits requested, diagnostics sold, pilots sold, pipeline SAR — manual inputs until real data exists.
