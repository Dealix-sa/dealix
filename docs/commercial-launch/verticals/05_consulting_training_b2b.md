# Vertical Playbook 05 — Consulting, Training & B2B Services
# دليل القطاع 05 — الاستشارات والتدريب وخدمات B2B

> AI recommends and drafts. Founder approves. Nothing is sent automatically.
> Draft-only. Every email draft includes an opt-out.

## ICP — العميل المثالي
Consulting, training, and B2B service firms (5–1,000 employees) with an active
proposal pipeline and multiple concurrent engagements. شركات استشارات وتدريب وخدمات B2B.

## Buyer personas — شخصيات المشتري
Managing director (growth), delivery director (engagement throughput), head of
operations (resource tracking), BD lead (pipeline).

## Buyer titles — المسميات
EN: Managing Director, Delivery Director, Head of Operations, Business Development Lead.
AR: العضو المنتدب، مدير التسليم، رئيس العمليات، قائد تطوير الأعمال.

## Company size fit — حجم الشركة
5–1,000 employees.

## Geography fit — النطاق الجغرافي
KSA primary; GCC and mixed international secondary.

## Pain map — خريطة الألم
- Proposal/SOW drafting bottleneck — اختناق صياغة العروض ونطاقات العمل.
- Manual engagement/resource tracking — تتبع يدوي للمشاريع والموارد.
- Slow reporting to clients/leadership — بطء التقارير.
- Weak knowledge reuse across engagements — ضعف إعادة استخدام المعرفة.

## Trigger events — أحداث محفّزة
Pipeline surge; a new service line; team scaling; a leadership reporting push.

## Discovery questions — أسئلة الاكتشاف
1. How long does a proposal/SOW take to draft today?
2. How are engagements and resources tracked?
3. Where does client/leadership reporting slow down?
4. How is knowledge reused across engagements?
5. Which manual task most limits growth?

## Qualification criteria — معايير التأهيل
Active pipeline; a delivery/ops owner; named drafting or reporting pain; data exists.

## Disqualification criteria — معايير الاستبعاد
No pipeline; no ops owner; expects guaranteed win-rates; wants automation without review.

## Offers — العروض
Entry audit (499–2,500); proposal/reporting pilot on limited dataset (5,000–25,000);
delivery-ops retainer (3,000–25,000/mo); enterprise services OS (150,000+).

## Cold email draft example (review-only)
Subject: Consulting: faster proposals
Body: Hi {Managing Director} at {Company}. We help consulting teams speed up
proposal/SOW drafting and unify reporting with an AI operations system that runs
fully under your control — it drafts, you decide, your data stays with you. We start
with the Entry Diagnostic within days.
CTA: Would a short 15-minute call this week work for you?
Opt-out: Reply STOP to opt out.

## Objection handling
- "We already have templates." → We speed the drafting and reuse around them. Next: audit.
- "Our engagement data is sensitive." → Limited dataset, your data stays with you,
  human approval on every step. Next: scope first.

## Proof assets required
Proposal/reporting workflow map, anonymized turnaround example (only if real), audit template.

## Delivery scope
Audit maps proposal/engagement/reporting/knowledge flow, diagnoses top pains,
recommends a pilot, lists risks. No production-system connection during audit.

## Implementation timeline
Audit 3–7 days; pilot 14–30 days; department OS 30–90 days.

## Success metrics
Faster proposal turnaround, cleaner engagement tracking, faster reporting, better
knowledge reuse. Measured in pilot, never guaranteed up front.

## Compliance notes
Consented/public contacts; opt-out on every email; no client data in first touch;
no guaranteed win-rate claims.

## Risk notes
Never promise win-rates or revenue. Keep human-in-the-loop.

## Founder review notes
Prioritize firms in a pipeline surge or launching a new service line. Verify before send.

## No-go claims
No guaranteed win-rates, no "replace your team", no "full automation", no data-access implication.

## What not to say
Don't imply access to their engagement data; no fake urgency; one offer per message.
