# Vertical Playbook — Facilities Management & Maintenance

> **AR:** إدارة المرافق والصيانة · **Non-negotiable:** AI drafts/scores/recommends; founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

## ICP
FM operators, maintenance contractors, and integrated facility services firms managing multiple sites (malls, offices, compounds, hospitals) in KSA/GCC.

## Excluded ICP
One-person handyman shops; pure equipment resellers with no service ops.

## Buyer personas
- Operations Director (DM)
- FM/Account Manager (influencer)
- Dispatcher/Coordinator (user)
- GM/Owner (budget owner)

- **Decision maker:** Operations Director (DM)
- **Influencer:** FM/Account Manager (influencer)
- **User:** Dispatcher/Coordinator (user)
- **Budget owner:** GM/Owner (budget owner)

## Main workflows
- Work-order intake & triage
- Preventive maintenance scheduling
- Client SLA reporting
- Vendor/technician dispatch

## Top pains
- Reactive work orders and SLA breaches
- Manual scheduling and reporting
- Poor visibility across sites
- Slow client reporting

## Trigger events
- New multi-site contract won
- SLA penalties incurred
- Client demands monthly reporting
- Scaling headcount

## Buying signals
- Mentions of CAFM/CMMS gaps
- Hiring coordinators
- Complaints about reporting

## Disqualification signals
- No service contracts
- No budget owner reachable
- Pure product business
