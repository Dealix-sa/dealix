# Vertical Playbook 01 — Facilities Management & Maintenance
# دليل القطاع 01 — إدارة المرافق والصيانة

> AI recommends and drafts. Founder approves. Nothing is sent automatically.
> Draft-only. Every email draft includes an opt-out.

## ICP — العميل المثالي
Multi-site facilities operators (50–5000 employees, 5+ managed sites): FM
contractors, in-house FM departments, integrated facility services companies in
KSA/GCC. مشغّلو مرافق متعددو المواقع في السعودية والخليج.

## Buyer personas — شخصيات المشتري
Operations leader who owns SLA performance and cost; maintenance leader who owns
uptime; contract manager who owns vendor reconciliation.

## Buyer titles — المسميات
EN: Facilities Director, Operations Manager, Maintenance Manager, FM Contract Manager.
AR: مدير المرافق، مدير العمليات، مدير الصيانة، مدير عقود التشغيل.

## Company size fit — حجم الشركة
50–5000 employees, 5+ managed sites, active SLA contracts.

## Geography fit — النطاق الجغرافي
KSA primary; UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman secondary.

## Pain map — خريطة الألم
- Reactive maintenance and missed SLAs — صيانة رد فعلية وتجاوز اتفاقيات الخدمة.
- Manual work-order tracking across sites — تتبع أوامر العمل يدويًا عبر المواقع.
- No single view of preventive maintenance — غياب رؤية موحدة للصيانة الوقائية.
- Slow vendor/contract reconciliation — بطء تسوية عقود الموردين.

## Trigger events — أحداث محفّزة
New multi-site contract; an SLA-penalty quarter; CAFM/ERP migration; portfolio
expansion. عقد جديد متعدد المواقع؛ ربع فيه غرامات؛ ترحيل نظام؛ توسّع المحفظة.

## Discovery questions — أسئلة الاكتشاف
1. How do work orders flow across your sites today?
2. Where do SLA breaches usually originate?
3. How much of maintenance is preventive vs reactive?
4. How is vendor reconciliation handled at month-end?
5. Which single report does leadership keep asking for?

## Qualification criteria — معايير التأهيل
5+ sites; named SLA pain; an operations owner who can approve a pilot; data
exists in some system (even spreadsheets).

## Disqualification criteria — معايير الاستبعاد
Single small site; no budget owner; expects guaranteed savings; wants full
automation with no human approval.

## Offers — العروض
- Entry: AI Workflow Audit (499–2,500 SAR, 3–7 days).
- Pilot: SLA/work-order pilot on a limited dataset (5,000–25,000 SAR).
- Retainer: monitoring + monthly leadership report (3,000–25,000 SAR/mo).
- Enterprise: multi-department FM operations OS (150,000+ SAR).

## Arabic messaging angles — زوايا الرسائل بالعربية
"نساعد فرق المرافق على تقليل الصيانة الرد فعلية وتجاوزات SLA عبر نظام يعمل تحت
إشرافكم الكامل — الذكاء يقترح وأنتم تقررون، وبياناتكم تبقى لديكم."

## English messaging angles
"We help FM teams cut reactive maintenance and SLA breaches with a system that
runs under your control — the AI drafts, you decide, your data stays with you."

## Cold email draft example (review-only)
Subject: Facilities: cutting reactive maintenance
Body: Hi {Operations Manager} at {Company}. We help facilities teams tackle
reactive maintenance and missed SLAs with an AI operations system that runs fully
under your control — it drafts, you decide, your data stays with you. We start
with the Entry Diagnostic: a workflow map and pain diagnosis within days.
CTA: Would a short 15-minute call this week work for you?
Opt-out: Reply STOP to opt out.

## Follow-up draft example
Following up on my earlier note — the path to fewer SLA breaches still stands,
starting with a short diagnostic. Should I send a one-page brief? Reply STOP to opt out.

## Website form draft example

## Objection handling — معالجة الاعتراضات
- "We have a CAFM already." → We sit on top of it; the diagnostic shows gaps you
  already feel. Next: book the audit.
- "We worry about data." → Limited dataset, your data stays with you, human
  approval on every step. Next: share scope, not data, first.

## Proof assets required — أصول الإثبات
Workflow map sample, anonymized SLA-improvement example (only if real), audit
deliverable template.

## Delivery scope — نطاق التسليم
Audit: map current work-order + SLA flow, diagnose top 3 pains, recommend a pilot,
list risks. We do **not** connect to production systems during the audit.

## Implementation timeline — الجدول
Audit 3–7 days; pilot 14–30 days; department OS 30–90 days.

## Success metrics — مؤشرات النجاح
Reduction in reactive ratio, faster work-order closure, fewer SLA breaches,
on-time vendor reconciliation. (Measured in pilot, never guaranteed up front.)

## Compliance notes — ملاحظات الامتثال
Public/consented contacts only; opt-out on every email; no personal data in first
touch; no guaranteed-savings claims.

## Risk notes — ملاحظات المخاطر
Avoid "we'll cut costs X%" without a cited case study. Keep human-in-the-loop.

## Founder review notes — ملاحظات مراجعة المؤسس
Prioritize multi-site operators in an SLA-penalty quarter. Verify each prospect
before any manual send.

## No-go claims — ادعاءات ممنوعة
No "full automation", no "replace your team", no guaranteed ROI, no "we accessed
your data".

## What not to say — ما لا يُقال
Don't imply we already have their data; don't use fake urgency; don't stack
multiple offers in one message.
