# Vertical Playbook — Contracting & Project Controls

> **AR:** المقاولات وضبط المشاريع · **Non-negotiable:** AI drafts/scores/recommends; founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

## ICP
Mid-size contractors and project-controls teams running multiple active projects (civil, MEP, fit-out) in KSA/GCC.

## Excluded ICP
Single-trade subcontractors with no PMO; suppliers only.

## Buyer personas
- Projects Director (DM)
- Project Controls Manager (influencer)
- Planner/QS (user)
- Owner/Partner (budget owner)

- **Decision maker:** Projects Director (DM)
- **Influencer:** Project Controls Manager (influencer)
- **User:** Planner/QS (user)
- **Budget owner:** Owner/Partner (budget owner)

## Main workflows
- Progress reporting
- Cost & variation tracking
- Document control
- Submittals/RFIs

## Top pains
- Manual progress & cost reporting
- Variation/claim tracking
- Document control chaos
- Cash-flow visibility

## Trigger events
- New large project award
- Client/consultant reporting demands
- Cost overruns
- Audit/claim disputes

## Buying signals
- Hiring planners/QS
- Complaints about reporting cadence
- ERP/Primavera gaps

## Disqualification signals
- No active projects
- No PMO function
- Owner unreachable

## Objections
- "Is this spam?" → Review-only, opt-in, opt-out honored, founder sends personally.
- "ROI?" → We show evidence and estimates, never guarantees.
- "We do it manually." → That's the point — we prepare, you stay in control.
