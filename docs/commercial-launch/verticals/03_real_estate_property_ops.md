# Vertical Playbook — Real Estate & Property Operations
## العقار وإدارة الأملاك

### Sector thesis
Leads from listings and tenant operations leak without a structured follow-up
and reporting system. Dealix installs a property-ops OS for lead follow-up,
renewals, collections visibility and owner reporting — founder-approved.

### Why now
High new-supply handovers, occupancy pressure, and owner-reporting demands make
operational discipline a competitive edge.

### ICP
Property management / leasing / asset management teams (20–500 staff) in KSA/GCC.

### Excluded ICP
Individual brokers; firms with no recurring management portfolio.

### Buyer personas & titles
- **Decision maker:** Property Operations Manager / Leasing Director / Asset Manager / GM
- **Influencer:** Leasing agents, collections lead
- **Budget owner:** GM / Owner

### Top workflows
Lead capture → follow-up → viewing → offer → renewal → collections → owner report.

### Top 10 pains
1. Listing leads lost without structured follow-up
2. Tenant requests & renewals tracked manually
3. Weak collections / arrears visibility
4. No standardized proposal/quote process
5. Slow, inconsistent owner reporting
6. Renewal cycles missed
7. Viewing-to-offer conversion invisible
8. Maintenance hand-offs lost
9. Portfolio KPIs scattered
10. No single tenant communication history

### Trigger events / buying signals
New tower handover, leasing campaign, portfolio acquisition, occupancy drop,
new Ops Manager hire.

### Offer ladder mapping
Audit (lead & renewal map) → Pilot on one building/portfolio → Department OS
(leasing + reporting) → Retainer → Enterprise (multi-portfolio).

### Messaging angles
- **AR:** "عملاء الإعلانات يضيعون بلا متابعة؟ نبني نظام تشغيل للمتابعة والتجديدات وتقارير الملاك — أنت تعتمد كل خطوة."
- **EN:** "Listing leads vanish without structured follow-up. We install a property-ops OS for follow-up, renewals and owner reporting — you approve every step."

### Examples / discovery / proposal
Governed cold email + manual LinkedIn + intake copy. Discovery: where do leads
land, how are renewals tracked, how long does owner reporting take, which
building underperforms? Proposal anchors on lead leakage or renewal risk.

### Proof asset, delivery, metrics
Lead follow-up board, renewal calendar, owner-report template. Success: lead
follow-up rate ↑, renewals captured earlier, reporting time ↓ (targets).

### Risks & compliance / what not to say / objections / founder notes
Tenant data is personal data — strict minimization & consent. No guaranteed ROI.
"We use a portal" → portals store data; we operate the workflow + comms.
Good volume wedge; many small portfolios = repeatable Department OS.
