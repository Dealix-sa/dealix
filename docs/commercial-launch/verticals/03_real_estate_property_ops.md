# Real Estate and Property Ops — العقار وتشغيل الأملاك

Vertical playbook. AI drafts and ranks; the client's team reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

## ICP — العميل المثالي

Property management and real estate operations companies in Saudi Arabia handling leasing, renewals, tenant communications, and owner reporting across portfolios.

شركات إدارة الأملاك وتشغيل العقار في السعودية التي تدير التأجير والتجديدات ومراسلات المستأجرين وتقارير الملاك عبر محافظ متعددة.

## Excluded ICP — العميل المُستبعد

Individual landlords with a handful of units; firms expecting auto-sent tenant outreach without opt-in.

## Buyer personas — شخصيات المشتري

- Property operations head responsible for occupancy and renewals.
- Owner-relations lead responsible for reporting quality.

## Buyer titles — مسميات المشتري

Head of Property Management, Leasing Manager, Operations Manager, Owner Relations Manager, General Manager.

## Top pains — أبرز الآلام

- Slow drafting of lease and renewal documents.
- Inconsistent tenant and owner communications.
- Manual, repetitive owner reporting.

بطء صياغة عقود الإيجار والتجديد؛ مراسلات غير متسقة للمستأجرين والملاك؛ تقارير ملاك يدوية متكررة.

## Triggers — المحفزات

Renewal season, portfolio growth, owner complaints about reporting, occupancy targets.

## Discovery questions — أسئلة الاستكشاف

1. How many renewals and lease documents move monthly?
2. Where do tenant follow-ups slip?
3. Who drafts owner reports, and how long does it take?
4. What does a delayed renewal cost in occupancy?

## Arabic drafts — مسودات عربية

**DRAFT for founder review — outreach.**
> مرحبًا [الاسم]،
> أعمل مع فرق تشغيل العقار على تسريع صياغة وثائق التجديد ومراسلات المستأجرين وتقارير الملاك. الذكاء الاصطناعي يجهّز المسودة وفريقكم يراجع ويرسل. إن كانت نظرة قصيرة على سير عملكم مفيدة، يسعدني مشاركة تدقيق مختصر.
> إن لم يكن مناسبًا، ردّوا بـ "غير مهتم" ولن أتابع.

## English drafts — مسودات إنجليزية

**DRAFT for founder review — outreach.**
> Hello [Name],
> I work with property operations teams on speeding up renewal documents, tenant communications, and owner reporting. AI prepares the draft; your team reviews and sends. If a short look at your workflows is useful, I can share a brief audit.
> If this isn't relevant, reply "no thanks" and I won't follow up.

## Objections — الاعتراضات

See [09_OBJECTION_HANDLING.md](../09_OBJECTION_HANDLING.md). Sector-specific: "Tenant messaging is sensitive" → human review stays; WhatsApp only after opt-in; no bulk sending.

## Delivery scope — نطاق التسليم

Draft templates for renewals, tenant notices, and owner reports; ranked queue; manual send by the client.

## Proof assets — أصول الإثبات

Anonymized renewal-drafting time baseline vs observed. Labeled "Hypothetical / case-safe template" unless client consents.

## Compliance notes — ملاحظات الامتثال

No external sending by the system. No tenant or owner PII in case assets. WhatsApp only after opt-in. See [07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md](../07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md).

## What not to say — ما لا يُقال

No "automate everything", no "replace your team", no "guaranteed ROI", no "100%". Never imply auto-sent tenant outreach.

## Success metrics — مقاييس النجاح

Estimated time saved per renewal/report; tenant follow-up completion (observed); owner-report turnaround. Labeled estimated until verified.
