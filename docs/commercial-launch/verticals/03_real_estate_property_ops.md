# Vertical Playbook — Real Estate & Property Operations

> **AR:** العقارات وعمليات الأملاك · **Non-negotiable:** AI drafts/scores/recommends; founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

## ICP
Property managers, developers, and owners' associations managing residential/commercial portfolios in KSA/GCC.

## Excluded ICP
Individual brokers; pure listing portals.

## Buyer personas
- Property Operations Manager (DM)
- Leasing/CS Lead (influencer)
- Coordinator (user)
- Asset Owner (budget owner)

- **Decision maker:** Property Operations Manager (DM)
- **Influencer:** Leasing/CS Lead (influencer)
- **User:** Coordinator (user)
- **Budget owner:** Asset Owner (budget owner)

## Main workflows
- Tenant request triage
- Lease/renewal tracking
- Collections reminders (review-only drafts)
- Owner reporting

## Top pains
- Tenant request handling
- Lease & renewal tracking
- Collections follow-up
- Owner reporting

## Trigger events
- Portfolio growth
- High vacancy/churn
- Owner reporting demands
- Collections backlog

## Buying signals
- Hiring property coordinators
- Complaints about tenant response
- Manual lease tracking

## Disqualification signals
- Single unit
- No management mandate
