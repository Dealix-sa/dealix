# Client Onboarding

## Day 0 — Agreement
- Signed proposal/SOW; NDA and DPA where data is involved.
- Named client owner + Dealix owner.
- Agreed acceptance criteria and success metrics (as targets).

## Day 1 — Access & scope
- Least-privilege access to only what the workflow needs (data minimization).
- Confirm the single workflow in scope and its current state.

## Day 2–3 — Kickoff
- Walk the current workflow end-to-end.
- Confirm bottleneck and the metric we'll move.
- Set the review cadence and the approval owner.

## Guardrails
- No sensitive data before the agreement is signed.
- The client approves every external step; Dealix never sends on their behalf.
- All access is logged (audit trail).
