# Commercial Launch OS — نظام الإطلاق التجاري

The operating system for taking Dealix from zero paying clients to a repeatable revenue motion in Saudi Arabia. This set defines who we sell to, what we sell, how we price it, how we reach buyers, and how we deliver and retain.

## Governing rule — القاعدة الحاكمة

**AI drafts, ranks, and recommends. Founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.**

الذكاء الاصطناعي يكتب المسودات ويرتّب الأولويات ويوصي. المؤسس يراجع ويوافق ويرسل يدويًا. النظام لا يرسل إلى أي طرف خارجي إطلاقًا.

This rule is non-negotiable and is restated in [05_CHANNEL_POLICY.md](05_CHANNEL_POLICY.md) and [07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md](07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md).

## Operating principles — مبادئ التشغيل

- **Evidence over claims.** We present estimated value and label it as estimated. Verified value is reserved for closed cases. See [ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md](../08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md).
- **Manual send, always.** No external message leaves without founder approval and a manual action.
- **Small first.** We lead with an Audit, prove value, then climb the [offer ladder](02_OFFER_LADDER_SAR.md).
- **Five verticals, not fifty.** Focus compounds. See [01_FIRST_5_VERTICALS_STRATEGY.md](01_FIRST_5_VERTICALS_STRATEGY.md).
- **Bilingual parity.** Every buyer-facing artifact ships AR + EN.

## Forbidden everywhere — ممنوع في كل المواد

Never use: "guaranteed ROI", "100%", "replace your team", "replace lawyers", "automate everything", "no human needed", "from our database", "as discussed" (when no prior discussion happened), or fabricated urgency. See [07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md](07_COMPLIANCE_AND_SAFETY_GATES.md).

ممنوع نهائيًا: "عائد مضمون"، "100%"، "نستبدل فريقك"، "نستبدل المحامين"، "أتمتة كل شيء"، "بدون تدخل بشري"، "من قاعدة بياناتنا"، "كما اتفقنا" (دون اتفاق سابق)، أو افتعال الاستعجال.
