# Channel Policy / سياسة القنوات

## Email / البريد
- Draft generation only. No SMTP. No sending API. No bulk send. No automatic sequence. Opt-out required.
- External readiness BEFORE any manual send: SPF, DKIM, DMARC, Google Postmaster, bounce tracking, suppression list, unsubscribe handling, warm-up/ramp plan, founder approval.

## LinkedIn / لينكدإن
- Manual draft only. No scraping, no bots, no auto-connect, no auto-message, no browser automation. Founder copies manually.

## WhatsApp / واتساب
- No cold outreach. No outbound automation. Inbound / opt-in manual replies only. Any WhatsApp draft = manual_review_only. A number without opt-in is no-go.

## Website forms / نماذج الموقع
- Draft only. No auto-submit. Founder/person copies manually. No browser automation.

## Phone / الهاتف
- Manual only. No dialer, no auto-call.

## Per-channel word limits / حدود الكلمات
- cold_email: 180 words (manual_only)
- follow_up: 180 words (manual_only)
- linkedin_manual: 100 words (manual_only)
- website_form_manual: 120 words (manual_only)
- whatsapp_optin_reply: 80 words (manual_review_only)
