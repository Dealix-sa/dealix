# Founder Daily Review — المراجعة اليومية للمؤسس

The daily cadence that keeps the pipeline moving while every external action stays manual.

## Daily loop — الحلقة اليومية

**English.** Each morning the founder reviews the ranked queue of AI-prepared drafts and recommendations, approves or edits, and sends manually. Each evening the founder updates CRM stages and logs outcomes.

**عربي.** كل صباح يراجع المؤسس قائمة المسودات والتوصيات المرتّبة من الذكاء الاصطناعي، يوافق أو يعدّل، ثم يرسل يدويًا. كل مساء يحدّث مراحل CRM ويسجّل النتائج.

## Morning checklist — قائمة الصباح

- Review AI-ranked leads in `researched` and `draft_generated`.
- Read each draft; check for opt-out line, no faked familiarity, no forbidden phrases.
- Approve and send manually, or send back for redraft.
- Move leads to `manually_contacted`.

## Evening checklist — قائمة المساء

- Log replies: move to `replied_positive` or `replied_negative`.
- Book diagnostics; move to `diagnostic_booked`.
- Update the value ledger with observed outcomes.
- Suppress or disqualify where appropriate.

## Time-box — تحديد الوقت

| Block | Duration |
|---|---|
| Morning review and send | 45–60 min |
| Midday call follow-up | as booked |
| Evening CRM and ledger | 30 min |

## Guardrails — ضوابط

**English.** The founder is the only sender. The review is where the governing rule is enforced — see [05_CHANNEL_POLICY.md](05_CHANNEL_POLICY.md) and [23_LEAD_OPS_FINAL_QA.md](23_LEAD_OPS_FINAL_QA.md).

**عربي.** المؤسس هو المُرسِل الوحيد. المراجعة هي حيث تُطبَّق القاعدة الحاكمة.
