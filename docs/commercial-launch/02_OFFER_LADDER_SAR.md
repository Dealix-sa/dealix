## Claims discipline

- **no_guaranteed_roi**: yes
- **no_exaggerated_claims**: yes
- **conservative_and_provable_only**: yes
- Every commercial claim must be conservative, evidence-first, and provable. No ROI guarantees. No data-access claims.

## How to move a buyer up the ladder

1. **Entry Diagnostic / AI Workflow Audit (499–2,500 SAR):** low-risk first value. Founder-reviewed map + top 3 fixes.
2. **Paid Pilot (5,000–25,000 SAR):** only after the diagnostic proves value. One governed workflow, measured before/after.
3. **Department OS (25,000–150,000 SAR):** scale one department after a successful pilot.
4. **Monthly Retainer (3,000–25,000 SAR/month):** for workflows that repeat monthly.
5. **Enterprise Custom OS (150,000+ SAR):** multi-department, scoped per engagement.

> Never promise outcomes you cannot prove. Every claim must be conservative and evidence-first.
