# Offer Ladder (SAR)

> Prices are **indicative ranges**. No ROI is guaranteed. Any results figures in
> messaging are estimated targets or non-guaranteed examples only.

| Stage | Offer | Price (SAR) | Duration | What the client gets |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|----------------------|
| Audit | **AI Workflow Audit** | 499 – 2,500 | 3–7 days | Workflow map, bottleneck diagnosis, risk map, pilot recommendation |
| Pilot | **Paid Pilot** | 5,000 – 25,000 | 14–30 days | Working pilot, limited dataset, approval gates, measurable workflow output |
| Department | **Department OS** | 25,000 – 150,000 | 30–90 days | Production-ready internal workflow OS |
| Retainer | **Monthly Retainer** | 3,000 – 25,000 / mo | monthly | Monitoring, improvements, reporting, support |
| Enterprise | **Enterprise Custom OS** | 150,000+ | custom | Multiple workflows, governance, custom integrations |

## How a client moves up
1. **Diagnostic / Audit** proves the bottleneck and the highest-value workflow.
2. **Paid Pilot** ships a working, approval-gated workflow on a limited dataset.
3. **Department OS** productionizes the workflow for a whole department.
4. **Monthly Retainer** keeps it monitored and improving.
5. **Enterprise Custom OS** spans multiple workflows with governance.

## What we never do
- Guarantee ROI or specific revenue numbers.
- Send anything externally on the client's behalf without their explicit approval.
- Process sensitive data before a signed agreement (and DPA where relevant).

The machine-readable source of truth is `config/commercial_offers.json`.
