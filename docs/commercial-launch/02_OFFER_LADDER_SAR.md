# Offer Ladder (SAR) — سلّم العروض بالريال السعودي

> AI recommends and drafts. Founder approves. Nothing is sent automatically.

All prices in **Saudi Riyal (SAR)**. Pricing and contracting happen **outside
this repository**, manually, after a founder decision.

| Stage | المرحلة | Price (SAR) | Duration | Deliverable |
|-------|---------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| Entry Diagnostic — AI Workflow Audit | تشخيص أولي — تدقيق سير العمل | 499–2,500 | 3–7 days | workflow map + pain diagnosis + pilot recommendation + risk notes |
| Paid Pilot | تجربة مدفوعة | 5,000–25,000 | 14–30 days | working pilot + approval gates + limited dataset + measurable workflow result |
| Department OS | نظام تشغيل القسم | 25,000–150,000 | 30–90 days | internal operating system for one department |
| Monthly Retainer | اشتراك شهري | 3,000–25,000 / mo | monthly | monitoring, improvements, reporting, workflow tuning, monthly leadership report |
| Enterprise Custom OS | نظام تشغيل مؤسسي مخصص | 150,000+ | custom | custom AI operating system across multiple workflows/departments |

## Ladder logic / منطق السلّم

1. **Entry Diagnostic** is the wedge — low risk, fast, proves we understand the
   workflow before anyone commits budget.
2. **Paid Pilot** turns the diagnosis into a working, measurable result inside
   approval gates and on a limited dataset.
3. **Department OS** operationalizes one department end-to-end.
4. **Monthly Retainer** keeps it tuned and reported.
5. **Enterprise Custom OS** scales across departments.

التسلسل: تشخيص → تجربة → نظام قسم → اشتراك → مؤسسي. كل رتبة تثبت القيمة قبل
الرتبة التالية.

## What we never promise / ما لا نعد به أبدًا

- ❌ Guaranteed ROI — لا نضمن عائدًا.
- ❌ "Reduce costs 70%" without a real, cited case study.
- ❌ "Full automation" in sensitive sectors — لا أتمتة كاملة في القطاعات الحساسة.
- ❌ "Replace your employees / replace your lawyer."
- ❌ "We accessed/extracted your data" / "from our database."
- ❌ Hype, fake urgency, or unprovable claims.
