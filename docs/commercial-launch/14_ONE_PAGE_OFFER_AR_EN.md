**EN —** An AI Revenue & Operations OS for Saudi/GCC B2B teams. We run one repetitive, document-heavy workflow at a time. AI drafts and verifies; your team approves; nothing is sent automatically. Not a CRM, not a chatbot, not an agency, not a mass-outreach tool.
