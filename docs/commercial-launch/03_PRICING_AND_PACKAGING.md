# Pricing & Packaging

All prices in **SAR**. Ranges reflect scope, data volume and number of workflows.
No ROI is guaranteed.

## Packaging principles
- **Land with the Audit.** Low-risk, fast, proves the bottleneck.
- **Prove with the Pilot.** One workflow, limited data, explicit acceptance criteria.
- **Scale with Department OS.** Production-ready, single department.
- **Retain with monitoring.** Improvements + reporting + support.
- **Expand to Enterprise.** Multi-workflow, governance, integrations.

## What is in / out of each tier

| Tier | In scope | Out of scope |
|------|----------|--------------|
| Audit | Workflow map, bottleneck + risk diagnosis, pilot recommendation | Building software, integrations |
| Pilot | One working workflow on limited data, approval gates | Multi-department rollout, prod SLAs |
| Department OS | Production workflow OS for one department | Other departments, custom enterprise integrations |
| Retainer | Monitoring, iterative improvements, reporting, support | New department builds (scoped separately) |
| Enterprise | Multiple workflows, governance, custom integrations | Anything not in the signed SOW |

## Discounts & payment
Discounts and refunds follow the existing approval policy
(`dealix/config/approval_policy.yaml`). Payment/checkout is a manual,
founder-owned step — never auto-charged by this system.

## Pricing guardrails
- Never quote a guaranteed return.
- Anchor on the cost of the bottleneck, not on hours.
- Estimated outcome numbers must be labelled as targets, not promises.
