# External Go-Live Requirements

These are the **external** prerequisites the founder must satisfy *outside this
repo* before any manual outreach. This system never sends; it only prepares.

## Email deliverability (before any manual cold email)
- Domain DNS configured
- **SPF** record published
- **DKIM** signing enabled
- **DMARC** policy published
- Google Postmaster Tools monitored
- Bounce tracking + suppression process in place
- Sender reputation warm-up / ramp plan
- Complaint handling process

## Sales infrastructure
- Manual CRM (stages per `config/crm_pipeline_schema.json`)
- Calendly (or equivalent) for booking
- Payment/checkout — manual, founder-owned (never auto-charge)

## Legal / privacy
- Privacy policy published
- Terms of service published
- DPA template ready for data engagements
- PDPL-aware handling; legal/privacy review completed
- Named manual-approval owner (founder)

## Channel-specific
- **WhatsApp:** only with explicit opt-in; no cold WhatsApp.
- **LinkedIn:** manual only; no automation/auto-connect.
- **Website forms:** no auto-submit.
- **Paid ads:** do not launch without tracking (UTM) + legal/privacy review.

## Go-live gate
Do not begin manual outreach on a channel until its row above is fully green.
