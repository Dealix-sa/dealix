# Objection Handling

Conservative, evidence-first responses. Never over-promise. When in doubt,
offer the low-risk diagnostic and a proof asset.

## Universal objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already have a system / CRM / CAFM." | "Great — we don't replace it. We map where the *workflow around it* leaks and govern the gaps. The diagnostic works on a redacted sample, no system access." |
| "We're too busy right now." | "That's exactly the symptom. The diagnostic takes 20 minutes of your time and we do the work on a sample you share. You get the top 3 fixes either way." |
| "Our data is sensitive." | "We never need access to your live data or your clients' data. We work on redacted samples you choose, founder-reviewed, with no retention beyond the engagement." |
| "Does this guarantee results?" | "No, and we won't claim it does. We share a proof asset first so you can judge the value before paying for anything beyond the diagnostic." |
| "We've tried AI / automation before and it didn't fit." | "Most tools push automation first. We start with governance and one workflow, measured before/after. If it doesn't prove value in the pilot, we stop." |
| "Why should we trust a cold message?" | "You shouldn't yet. That's why the first step is a small, founder-led diagnostic with a proof asset — not a contract." |

## Vertical-specific

### Facilities Management
- *"Our CAFM handles this."* → "The leakage is usually in the handoffs around the
  CAFM — WhatsApp, Excel, completion evidence. We map those, not the CAFM itself."

### Contracting & Project Controls
- *"We use Primavera and our QS team."* → "We don't touch the schedule. We govern
  the variation-to-claim approval chain so claimable value isn't lost to slow paperwork."

### Real Estate & Property Ops
- *"Collections is the owner's job."* → "Understood. We govern the renewal and
  follow-up cadence so renewals and arrears stop depending on memory — that protects
  the owner relationship too."

### Legal & Professional Services
- *"Client confidentiality makes this impossible."* → "Privacy-first is the whole
  point. No access to privileged matters — we work on a redacted, non-privileged
  sample with a full audit trail."

### Consulting, Training & B2B
- *"Our work is too bespoke to systematize."* → "We systematize the *operations*
  around delivery — proposals, reporting, handover — so your people spend more time
  on the bespoke work, not the admin."

## Golden rules

1. Never guarantee ROI or outcomes.
2. Never claim you can access their data.
3. Always offer the proof asset before the ask.
4. If a buyer says no, send the opt-out and move on — no pressure, no deceptive urgency.
