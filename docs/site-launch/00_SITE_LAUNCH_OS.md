# Site Launch OS — Dealix Public Website — نظام إطلاق الموقع

The operating system for launching the Dealix public website (Next.js at `apps/web`). This document is the index; each numbered file is a working artifact the founder reviews before publishing.

## Governing rule — القاعدة الحاكمة

**EN:** AI drafts, ranks, and recommends. Founder reviews, approves, and sends manually. The system never sends externally.

**AR:** الذكاء الاصطناعي يصيغ ويرتّب ويوصي. المؤسس يراجع ويعتمد ويرسل يدويًا. النظام لا يرسل خارجيًا إطلاقًا.

Every page, headline, and CTA on the public site must be consistent with this rule. No page may imply that Dealix sends external messages, runs cold outreach, scrapes data, or automates sending on a customer's behalf.

## What this OS covers — ما الذي يغطّيه

- **01 — Page Map.** Every public route, its purpose, its primary CTA, and AR/EN status.
- **02 — SEO Checklist.** Per-page metadata, OpenGraph, Twitter cards, sitemap, robots, canonical, JSON-LD, multilingual handling, and link integrity.
- **03 — Copy Deck (AR + EN).** The approved bilingual copy for hero, problem, solution, the first five verticals, offers in SAR, and the three CTAs.
- **04 — Manual QA Checklist.** The human review steps the founder runs before publish.

## Audience — الجمهور

Saudi and GCC B2B decision-makers: founders, operations leaders, commercial directors, and finance owners in facilities management, contracting and project controls, real estate and property operations, legal and professional services, and consulting and training.

## Non-negotiables on the public site — غير قابل للتفاوض

1. No guaranteed ROI, no "100%", no "replace your team", no "automate everything", no "no human needed", no fake urgency.
2. No description of scraping, cold WhatsApp automation, LinkedIn automation, or bulk outreach as offered services.
3. Every customer-facing page that states a value figure carries the disclosure: "Estimated value is not Verified value / القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة".
4. Human-in-the-loop and approval-first language appears on the homepage, the trust page, and the pricing page.
5. Bilingual parity: each AR section has a matching EN section of equal structure and length.
