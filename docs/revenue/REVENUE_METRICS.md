# Revenue Metrics

## Daily

- DMs sent
- Replies received
- Calls booked
- Proposals sent
- Cash collected

## Weekly

- Lead count (new)
- Reply rate (replies ÷ DMs)
- Call rate (calls ÷ replies)
- Proposal rate (proposals ÷ calls)
- Close rate (paid ÷ proposals)
- Average deal size (SAR)
- Cash collected

## Monthly

- MRR (recurring)
- Total cash collected
- Net new retainers
- Retention (logo and revenue)
- Average deal size
- Gross margin estimate (cash collected − direct delivery cost)
- Founder hours per paid client

## Quarterly

- Retainer conversion rate (Sprint → Managed Ops)
- Case studies published
- Referral count and source breakdown
- Pricing experiment outcomes

## Targets (Stage 1)

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Reply rate | ≥ 15% |
| Call rate | ≥ 30% of replies |
| Close rate (Sprint) | ≥ 25% of proposals |
| Cash collected / month | ≥ SAR 10,000 |
| MRR (by month 6) | ≥ SAR 15,000 |

## Definitions

- **Cash collected** — money in the bank, not invoiced.
- **MRR** — monthly recurring contracts active on the first of the month.
- **Proposal** — A2-approved document sent to a prospect.
