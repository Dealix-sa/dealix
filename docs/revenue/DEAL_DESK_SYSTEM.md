# Deal Desk System

The forum (currently solo CEO) that approves any deal departing from the
standard offer in [PRICING_AND_PACKAGING](PRICING_AND_PACKAGING.md).

## When deal desk fires

- Custom scope (anything beyond a packaged offer)
- Discount > 10% off list
- Payment terms beyond net-30
- Multi-period commitments
- Bundled services with a partner
- Any contract under negotiation

## Approval checklist

1. Scope is named, time-bounded, and measurable
2. Pricing is recorded against the rubric in [PRICING_AND_PACKAGING](PRICING_AND_PACKAGING.md)
3. Discount (if any) maps to a documented reason (strategic logo / multi-year / volume)
4. Payment terms align with [PAYMENT_RECONCILIATION](PAYMENT_RECONCILIATION.md) capacity
5. Trust gates pass — see [`docs/00_constitution/NON_NEGOTIABLES.md`](../00_constitution/NON_NEGOTIABLES.md)
6. Close plan exists ([CLOSE_PLAN_TEMPLATE](CLOSE_PLAN_TEMPLATE.md))

## Log

Every deal-desk decision goes through [`docs/founder/DECISION_LOG_SYSTEM.md`](../founder/DECISION_LOG_SYSTEM.md)
with `type: pricing` (if pricing) or `type: bet` (if scope expansion).

## Non-negotiables

Deal desk records intent. Cash collection still flows through
[INVOICE_FLOW](INVOICE_FLOW.md) + Moyasar. No commitment to refunds,
payment terms beyond documented offers, or service guarantees. See
[`docs/founder/DO_NOT_SAY.md`](../founder/DO_NOT_SAY.md).
