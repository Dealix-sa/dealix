# Sample Factory

A repeatable way to produce a high-quality "first artifact" for a
prospective customer — a diagnostic snapshot, a sector pulse, a
mini-account map. Always founder-approved before release.

## 1. Sample types

| Type | What it is | Time-box |
|---|---|---|
| Diagnostic snapshot | A bilingual one-pager scoring the prospect on Dealix's lens. | < 60 minutes generated, founder approved < 30 minutes. |
| Sector pulse | A 5-page Saudi B2B sector signal report. | < 4 hours assembly. |
| Account map | A named-account intelligence pack on a target list. | < 1 day. |

## 2. Inputs

- Public registries + customer-provided data with consent.
- Approved providers.
- Brand templates (`docs/brand/*`).

## 3. Outputs

- PDF + bilingual landing block.
- Row in `revenue/sample_releases.csv` with `evidence_url`.

## 4. Trust gate

- Every sample passes the brand_guardian (voice + claims).
- Every sample is **founder-approved** before it leaves Dealix.
- Customer logos in the sample require explicit consent.

## 5. KPIs

- Samples produced per week.
- Approval rate.
- Sample → meeting conversion.
- Sample → proposal conversion.

## 6. Banned patterns

- ❌ Auto-mailing samples to scraped lists.
- ❌ Inflated metrics or fabricated benchmarks.
- ❌ Reusing a customer's confidential data in a sample for another
  account.
