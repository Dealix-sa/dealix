# Revenue Leadership System

The CEO's operating cadence for revenue. Daily / weekly / monthly rituals
that stay close to the cash and away from vanity.

## Daily — 10 minutes

- Read the pipeline section of the daily brief (see [`docs/founder/CEO_DAILY_BRIEF_SYSTEM.md`](../founder/CEO_DAILY_BRIEF_SYSTEM.md))
- Acknowledge any positive replies waiting > 24h
- Surface any approval-queue items at `latency > 24h` (see [`docs/strategy/KPI_DEFINITION_DICTIONARY.md`](../strategy/KPI_DEFINITION_DICTIONARY.md))

## Weekly — 30 minutes (Wed 14:00 KSA)

[PIPELINE_REVIEW_RHYTHM](PIPELINE_REVIEW_RHYTHM.md) drives this. Output: the
list of next-step nudges and the bucket of deals that need a [CLOSE_PLAN_TEMPLATE](CLOSE_PLAN_TEMPLATE.md).

## Monthly — 60 minutes (1st Monday)

- [WIN_LOSS_REVIEW](WIN_LOSS_REVIEW.md) — last month's closed-won and closed-lost interviews
- [PRICING_COUNCIL](PRICING_COUNCIL.md) — pricing changes considered
- Sector view from [`docs/strategy/BEACHHEAD_SECTOR_SCORECARD.md`](../strategy/BEACHHEAD_SECTOR_SCORECARD.md)
