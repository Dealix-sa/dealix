# Offer Ladder

## The Five Rungs

| Rung | Offer | Price (SAR) | Goal | Conversion Target to Next Rung |
|---|---|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | Free Diagnostic | 0 | Disqualify fast, identify Sprint candidates | 30% |
| 2 | Revenue Sprint | 499 | Prove value in one focused deliverable | 40% |
| 3 | Data / Pipeline Pack | 1,500 | Demonstrate repeatable, deeper delivery | 50% |
| 4 | Managed Ops (Revenue Desk) | 2,999–4,999/mo | Convert to recurring revenue | n/a |
| 5 | Custom AI / Enterprise | 5,000–25,000 | Bespoke build for qualified retainers | n/a |

## Rules

1. A new prospect cannot skip from Rung 1 to Rung 5 in the same month.
   Exceptions require A2 approval in `DECISION_LOG.md`.
2. Every rung has a written delivery checklist in `dealix/workflows/`.
3. Every rung has a proposal template in `templates/`.
4. A new rung is added only after the existing rungs have evidence
   (see Revenue Rule in `REVENUE_MODEL.md`).

## Sequencing Rule

- Rungs 1–2 are designed for first-touch.
- Rung 3 is the qualifier for retainer.
- Rung 4 is the goal.
- Rung 5 is reserved for retainers that have asked for it.

## Anti-Patterns

- Bundling rungs to inflate ticket size on first sale.
- Quoting Rung 4/5 to a prospect who has not completed Rung 2.
- "Discovery + Sprint" combos that exceed 8 founder hours without payment.
