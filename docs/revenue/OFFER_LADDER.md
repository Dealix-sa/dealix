# Dealix Offer Ladder

The five-rung ladder. Each rung has one purpose, one price band, one deliverable.

## 1. Signal Sample
- **Price:** Free / 199 SAR
- **Purpose:** Open the conversation.
- **Deliverable:** A short, specific signal tailored to the prospect (lead sample, market snapshot, or pain hypothesis).
- **Time to deliver:** Same day to 48 hours.

## 2. Revenue Sprint
- **Price:** 2,500–7,500 SAR
- **Purpose:** First paid result.
- **Deliverable:** Scoped, time-boxed engagement. One outcome. One report.
- **Time to deliver:** 5–10 working days.

## 3. Managed Pilot
- **Price:** 9,500–25,000 SAR
- **Purpose:** Prove execution at scale of one client.
- **Deliverable:** Multi-week engagement with weekly cadence and proof checkpoints.
- **Time to deliver:** 4–8 weeks.

## 4. Revenue Desk
- **Price:** 5,000–20,000 SAR / month
- **Purpose:** Recurring revenue.
- **Deliverable:** Monthly retainer covering a defined slice of revenue operations.
- **Time to deliver:** Continuous, with monthly reporting.

## 5. Dealix OS
- **Price:** Custom
- **Purpose:** Enterprise SaaS / managed OS.
- **Deliverable:** Productized platform or licensed operating system for a buyer who wants to internalize the system.
- **Time to deliver:** Quarterly engagement.

## Ladder rules
- Never start a prospect at rung 5.
- Never skip rung 1 with a cold prospect.
- Move one rung at a time unless the prospect explicitly buys higher.
- Each rung must produce evidence usable for the next rung.
