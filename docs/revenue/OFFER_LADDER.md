# Offer Ladder — سُلَّم العروض

## Purpose

Five rungs from a small first commitment to a substantial engagement. Each rung is a sellable, deliverable unit. Each rung produces the evidence the next rung needs.

## The ladder

| Rung | Name | Price (SAR) | Deliverable | Time | Buyer signal needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Free Diagnostic Call | 0 | 30-min call + one-page summary | 1 day | Curiosity, named role |
| 2 | Revenue Sprint | 499 | Reviewed lead table + next-step pack | 5–7 days | Sector + ICP defined |
| 3 | Data Pack | 1,500 | Sector-specific data pack + methodology note | 10–14 days | Wants depth before retainer |
| 4 | Managed Ops | 2,999–4,999 / month | Monthly lead operations + reviews | rolling | Repeatable need |
| 5 | Custom AI Engagement | 5,000–25,000 | Scoped engagement: data build, workflow design, agent setup with governance | 4–12 weeks | Specific operational problem |

## Rung rules

1. **Buyer chooses their next rung.** Dealix recommends; never auto-upgrades.
2. **Skips are allowed** if the buyer brings their own evidence of fit.
3. **Each rung has its own scope doc.** Out-of-scope requests roll to the next rung.
4. **Each rung carries its own refund posture.** See [CASH_RULES.md](./CASH_RULES.md).

## What each rung produces (for the next)

- Rung 1 → qualified intake form.
- Rung 2 → 1–3 prioritized opportunities with source attribution.
- Rung 3 → sector pattern doc + decision frame.
- Rung 4 → monthly operating cadence + tracked outcomes.
- Rung 5 → owned asset (data pack, workflow, governed agent) plus runbook.

## Anti-patterns

- Promising rung 5 outcomes at rung 2 pricing.
- Bundling rungs in one proposal to a buyer who has not finished the current rung.
- Discounting below the band without A2.

## AR — ملخّص

سُلَّم خماسي: تشخيص مجاني، ثم سبرنت 499 ريالًا، ثم حزمة بيانات 1,500، ثم عمليات مُدارة شهريًا 2,999–4,999، ثم ارتباط مُخصَّص 5,000–25,000. المشتري يختار درجته التالية، وكل درجة تنتج دليل ما بعدها. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
