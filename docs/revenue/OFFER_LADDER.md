# Dealix Offer Ladder

Five productized offers, ordered by price and depth. Every customer enters at a known rung. Every retainer exits to a higher rung.

## Purpose
Define the canonical Dealix offer ladder so sales, delivery, and content always speak in the same vocabulary, and so the founder has a known price/scope to quote in any first reply.

## Owner
Sami (Founder, Revenue OS).

## Review Cadence
Monthly. Prices reviewed quarterly.

## Inputs
- Closed-won prices from the pipeline tracker.
- Delivery hours per offer (private).
- Customer feedback per offer.
- Competitor pricing scans.

## Outputs
- Updated offer table.
- Updated proposal templates referencing exact rungs.
- Sales OS scripts and outbound templates aligned to the ladder.

## Rules
- Every offer must have a fixed scope, fixed price, fixed deliverable.
- Every offer must produce evidence (sample, report, dashboard, proof pack).
- Discounts > 15% require founder approval and a logged reason.
- New offers are not invented mid-conversation; they are added here first.

---

## The Five Rungs

### Rung 1 — Signal Sample
- **Price:** Free (gated by qualified meeting).
- **Scope:** One narrowly scoped data sample (e.g., 50 enriched accounts, one buyer-signal pull, one CRM hygiene snapshot).
- **Outcome:** Buyer sees Dealix output quality in their own hands within 48 hours.
- **Time:** 1–2 days.

### Rung 2 — Revenue Sprint
- **Price:** 499 SAR.
- **Scope:** 7-day Revenue Intelligence Sprint. Source passport, DQ score, account scoring, draft outreach pack, governance review, proof pack.
- **Outcome:** First paid proof of value. Bridge to a paid pilot.
- **Time:** 7 days.

### Rung 3 — Managed Pilot (Data Pack)
- **Price:** 1,500 SAR (one-off).
- **Scope:** Sprint plus an extended account pack: 250 scored accounts, three outreach sequences, one playbook.
- **Outcome:** Customer has a working revenue motion.
- **Time:** 2 weeks.

### Rung 4 — Revenue Desk (Managed Ops Retainer)
- **Price:** 2,999–4,999 SAR / month.
- **Scope:** Monthly account scoring, outreach drafts, weekly review, proof pack refresh.
- **Outcome:** Recurring revenue stream for Dealix; predictable pipeline for the customer.
- **Time:** Monthly, indefinite.

### Rung 5 — Dealix OS (Custom AI)
- **Price:** 5,000–25,000 SAR / month.
- **Scope:** Custom AI workflows, dedicated agents, deeper integrations, executive reporting.
- **Outcome:** Dealix becomes embedded in the customer's revenue function.
- **Time:** Multi-month engagement.

---

## Movement Rules

- A lead that paid Rung 2 must be offered Rung 3 within 7 days of delivery.
- A customer at Rung 3 must be offered Rung 4 within 30 days of delivery.
- A customer at Rung 4 must be offered Rung 5 only after three consecutive successful months.

## Metrics
- Conversion rate from Rung 2 → Rung 3.
- Conversion rate from Rung 3 → Rung 4.
- MRR contribution per rung.
- Mean time from first reply to first paid rung.

## Evidence
- Pipeline tracker stages aligned with rung names.
- Proposal templates per rung in `docs/sales/`.
- Paid invoices in private ops referencing exact rung.
