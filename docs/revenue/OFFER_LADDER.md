# Offer Ladder

> The five rungs. Fixed scope. Fixed price. Fixed delivery time.
> If a buyer asks for "something in between", you say no or move them to the right rung.

## Rung 1 — Free Diagnostic

- **Price:** 0 SAR
- **Time:** 30-minute call + 1-page diagnostic doc
- **Deliverable:** A one-page document identifying the top 3 revenue gaps and the next move
- **Audience:** Tier 1 ICP fit-score ≥ 60
- **What it's not:** A free pilot, a free strategy session, free consulting
- **Trust gate:** No client data captured; no automation enabled
- **Goal:** Qualify + book the Sprint (target 30% conversion)

## Rung 2 — Revenue Sprint (PRIMARY ENTRY)

- **Price:** SAR 499 (excl. VAT)
- **Time:** 7 calendar days
- **Deliverable:** 
  - Sanitized lead list (50 scored prospects)
  - Outreach message set (3 variants, Arabic + English)
  - Sample reply playbook for first 3 objections
  - Evidence pack documenting method + sources
- **Audience:** Tier 1 ICP, post-Diagnostic
- **Trust gate:** Public claims about results: prohibited until evidence pack signed off
- **Goal:** Prove process + earn 1,500 SAR Data Pack or retainer conversation (target 40% conversion)

## Rung 3 — Data Pack

- **Price:** SAR 1,500 (excl. VAT)
- **Time:** 5 calendar days
- **Deliverable:**
  - Sector benchmark report (sanitized peer data)
  - 200-prospect scored list, enriched
  - Top 10 priority targets with personalized message variants
  - Suppression-list cleaning service
- **Audience:** Post-Sprint customer or warm referral
- **Trust gate:** Sources cited; evidence pack required
- **Goal:** Bridge to Managed Ops (target 30% conversion)

## Rung 4 — Managed Ops (RECURRING TARGET)

- **Price:** SAR 2,999 / month (entry) → SAR 4,999 / month (expanded)
- **Time:** Monthly, ongoing
- **Deliverable per month:**
  - 100 new scored prospects per month
  - Weekly outreach drafts (founder-approved before send)
  - Bi-weekly delivery report (KPIs, trends, recommendations)
  - One sector-specific sample artifact per month
  - Trust review + claim-guard audit
- **Audience:** Post-Data Pack or post-Sprint with momentum
- **Trust gate:** Monthly evidence review; quarterly governance summary
- **Goal:** 12+ month retention; expansion to 4,999 by month 3

## Rung 5 — Custom AI

- **Price:** SAR 5,000 – 25,000 / month
- **Time:** Scoped per engagement; setup 30–60 days, then ongoing
- **Deliverable:**
  - Custom integration with client's existing CRM/data
  - Sector-specific agent (scoring, enrichment, sequencing)
  - Quarterly strategic review with founder + advisor
  - Dedicated approval matrix tuned to client policies
- **Audience:** Managed Ops customers month 3+ with proven ROI
- **Trust gate:** SOW + DPA + custom approval matrix; founder + advisor approval to start
- **Goal:** Anchor relationships, 24+ month LTV

## Cross-Rung Rules

- A buyer can skip Rung 1 if warm intro
- A buyer cannot skip Rung 2 to land at Rung 4 (must prove first)
- A buyer cannot buy two rungs simultaneously (one at a time, sequential)
- A buyer can move down a rung (Managed Ops → Sprint) for one-off projects after retainer ends
- A buyer cannot get a hybrid / custom rung this quarter — refuse

## Refusal Patterns

- "Can you do a custom thing?" → "Not in our productized lineup. Let me show you where you fit on the ladder."
- "Can I get a discount on the Sprint?" → "The Sprint is 499 SAR. The cheaper option is the Free Diagnostic."
- "Can you also do my marketing?" → "We don't. We'll happily refer you."
- "Can we pay you in shares / outcomes?" → "No. We accept SAR via bank transfer, Stripe, or Tabby."

## Adding A Rung

A new rung gets added only if:
- 5+ existing customers asked for the same thing
- It can be productized within 14 days
- It fits Strategy Filter
- It doesn't cannibalize an existing rung

Removing a rung requires customer migration plan + ledger entry.

## Review Cadence

- Monthly: conversion rates per rung
- Quarterly: rung pricing review (per `PRICING_STRATEGY.md`)
- Annually: rung structure (add/remove)
