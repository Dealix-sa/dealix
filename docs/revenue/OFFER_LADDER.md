# Offer Ladder

> Five rungs. Each rung has a price, scope, deliverable, and graduation path.

## Rung 0 — Signal Sample

- **Price:** Free or 199 SAR
- **Time to deliver:** 30–45 minutes
- **Scope:** 3 named accounts in the prospect's ICP, with one outreach
  hook per account, evidence linked, bilingual.
- **Deliverable:** A 1-page PDF + 1-paragraph strategic note.
- **Purpose:** Trust earned in minutes, not weeks.
- **Graduation:** Direct ask to Sprint.

## Rung 1 — Revenue Sprint

- **Price:** 2,500–7,500 SAR
- **Time to deliver:** ≤ 14 days
- **Scope:**
  - 25–50 qualified Saudi B2B accounts, fully researched.
  - Bilingual outreach pack (DM + email).
  - Executive memo summarising the segment.
  - QA-passed Proof Pack.
  - Handoff call.
- **Deliverable:** `OUTREACH_PACK_TEMPLATE.md` + report.
- **Purpose:** First paid proof of capability.
- **Graduation:** Retainer ask at the handoff.

## Rung 2 — Managed Pilot

- **Price:** 9,500–25,000 SAR
- **Time to deliver:** 4–6 weeks
- **Scope:** Multiple sprint-equivalent waves, founder-led outreach for the
  client, weekly reporting, opportunity surfacing, refinement of ICP.
- **Deliverable:** Pilot end-of-engagement report + retention proposal.
- **Purpose:** Deeper engagement for clients with serious budget.
- **Graduation:** Retainer or Dealix OS.

## Rung 3 — Revenue Desk (Retainer)

- **Price:** 5,000–20,000 SAR / month
- **Cadence:** Monthly
- **Scope:** Continuous lead supply, opportunity surfacing, weekly report,
  bi-weekly review call.
- **Deliverable:** Weekly Proof Pack + monthly executive review.
- **Purpose:** Predictable MRR and compounding moat.
- **Graduation:** Custom Dealix OS for the client.

## Rung 4 — Dealix OS

- **Price:** Custom (not before repeatability)
- **Cadence:** Multi-month, custom
- **Scope:** Embedded revenue operations system for the client — integrated
  with their CRM, custom data pipelines, agents tuned to their voice.
- **Deliverable:** Operational system + dashboards + governed AI agents.
- **Purpose:** Strategic anchor accounts.
- **Graduation:** Reference customer for the eventual SaaS productization.

## Ladder Rules

1. We do **not** offer custom work below Rung 1 pricing.
2. We do **not** skip rungs upward (Sample → Pilot is allowed; Sample → OS
   is not).
3. We **may** skip rungs downward (a Pilot customer can later take a
   single Sprint, e.g. for a new segment).
4. Every rung has a documented **graduation script** the founder can run.
5. A rung that has not been sold in 90 days is reviewed in
   `OFFER_EVOLUTION_SYSTEM.md`.

## Pricing Justification

| Rung | What the customer pays for |
|------|---------------------------|
| Sample | Proof of personalised, bilingual research |
| Sprint | A 2-week engagement that would cost their internal team 6–8 weeks |
| Pilot | A 4–6 week test of a continuous motion |
| Retainer | Predictable supply of qualified opportunities |
| OS | A bespoke revenue intelligence system |

## Review

Quarterly. Update with `OFFER_EVOLUTION_SYSTEM.md`.
