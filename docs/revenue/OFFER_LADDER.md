# Offer Ladder

## Purpose
Define the 5 productized offers used to move every lead from awareness to retainer.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Ladder

### Rung 1 — Free Diagnostic (0 SAR)
- Goal: produce a 1-page revenue/operational signal map.
- Time: <= 60 minutes.
- Output: diagnostic memo with 3 prioritized actions.
- Conversion target: 30% to Sprint.

### Rung 2 — Revenue Sprint (499 SAR)
- Goal: deliver one tangible revenue artefact (lead list, sector scan, qualified pipeline).
- Time: <= 7 days.
- Output: executive memo + qualified lead table + 14-day action plan.
- Conversion target: 40% to Data Pack.

### Rung 3 — Data Pack (1,500 SAR)
- Goal: deliver a sector data pack with evidence-ranked accounts.
- Time: <= 14 days.
- Output: data pack + outreach pack + scoring methodology.
- Conversion target: 30% to Managed Ops.

### Rung 4 — Managed Ops (2,999 - 4,999 SAR / month)
- Goal: ongoing revenue operations: pipeline, outreach, review cadence.
- Time: monthly retainer.
- Output: weekly delivery + monthly executive review.
- Conversion target: 20% to Custom AI.

### Rung 5 — Custom AI (5,000 - 25,000 SAR / engagement)
- Goal: bespoke AI workflow that materially compresses cost or grows revenue.
- Time: engagement-scoped.
- Output: working AI workflow + measurable KPI movement + handoff pack.

## Rules
- Never skip rungs without explicit founder approval.
- Never quote above ladder without proof pack.
- Discounts require founder approval.
- Each rung must have a proof example before it is sold.
