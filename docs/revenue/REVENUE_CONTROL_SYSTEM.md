# Revenue Control System

## Purpose
Make sure Dealix is always moving toward cash collected, proof, and retainer conversion.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Daily.

## Inputs
- pipeline_tracker.csv
- outreach activity
- replies
- calls
- proposals
- payments
- delivery status

## Outputs
- daily revenue focus
- next action per lead
- proposal follow-up
- payment path
- retainer candidate list

## Rules
- No lead without next_action.
- No proposal without follow-up date.
- No full delivery without payment, PO, or written approval.
- No bad-fit client accepted only for short-term cash.
- Revenue is good only if it improves cash, proof, repeatability, trust, or learning.

## Metrics
- leads added
- DMs sent
- replies
- calls booked
- proposals sent
- payments received
- retainer conversion

## Evidence
- pipeline tracker
- proposal notes
- payment record
- weekly review
