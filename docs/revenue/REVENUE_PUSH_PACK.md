# Revenue Push Pack

## Purpose
Define what to do when Dealix needs more sales activity now.

## Trigger
Use when:
- pipeline below 25 leads
- DMs below 25/week
- no replies
- no proposal
- no payment movement

## Actions
1. Add 25 qualified leads.
2. Prioritize A/B/C.
3. Send founder DM.
4. Log every action.
5. Prepare 3 samples.
6. Follow up in 48 hours.
7. Convert best reply into proposal.

## Metrics
- leads added
- DMs sent
- replies
- samples
- proposals
- payment follow-ups

## Evidence
- pipeline_tracker.csv
- revenue_action_log.csv
- sample packs
- proposal notes
