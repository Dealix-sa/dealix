## Revenue Quality Tiers

Not all revenue is equal. We rank each won deal:

- **A — Compounding.** Customer is ICP, delivered well, retainer-bound,
  generates referrals or case study.
- **B — Clean.** Customer is ICP, delivered well, no retainer yet, no
  drag.
- **C — Costly.** Customer is borderline ICP, delivery cost high or
  trust risk, marginal repeatability.
- **D — Bad.** Customer pulled scope, churned early, or required
  policy exceptions.

Target mix:

- Month 1–3: ≥ 50% A+B
- Month 4–6: ≥ 70% A+B
- Month 7+: ≥ 85% A+B; 0 D

## Pricing Floor & Ceiling

- **Sprint floor:** 2,500 SAR. Below this, delivery is below margin.
- **Sprint ceiling (pre-PMF):** 7,500 SAR. Above this triggers Pilot
  scoping.
- **Retainer floor:** 5,000 SAR / month. Below this is mis-priced.
- **Retainer ceiling (pre-PMF):** 20,000 SAR / month. Above this triggers
  Dealix OS scoping.

Floors are not negotiated. Ceilings trigger a scope-up, not a discount-down.

## Revenue Mix Decisions

- If Sprint volume > 2x retainer count → invest in retainer ask quality.
- If retainer count grows but Sprint count drops → ICP starvation; refresh
  outbound.
- If Sample volume is high but Sprint conversion is low → sample is
  generic; tighten ICP and hooks.
