# Dealix Revenue Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Revenue Sprint
One-time service. Entry offer. Targeted, async-friendly, fixed scope.
Currently SAR 499 (entry) and SAR 1,500 (data pack).

### 2. Managed Pilot
Higher-touch implementation over 4–8 weeks. SAR 5,000–15,000 range.
Used to qualify a client for Revenue Desk.

### 3. Revenue Desk
Monthly recurring revenue. SAR 2,999–4,999 per month. Includes weekly
drafting, scoring, reporting, and a quarterly review.

### 4. Partner / White-label
Channel partnerships with existing Saudi consultancies. Revenue share
documented per partner. Deferred until 3 retainers exist.

### 5. Dealix OS (Enterprise / SaaS)
Productized version of the OS after delivery patterns repeat.
Deferred until 10 retainers exist and 6 have used the same workflow.

## Current Priority

**Revenue Sprint → Managed Pilot → Revenue Desk.**
Streams 4 and 5 are blocked from active selling until the previous streams
have evidence.

## Revenue Rule

No new revenue stream becomes active until the previous stream has evidence
(at least 3 paying customers with proof packs on file).

## Mix Targets (12-month)

| Stream | Share of Revenue | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Sprints + Packs | 30% | Entry, demand discovery |
| Managed Pilot | 20% | Bridge |
| Revenue Desk | 50% | Core MRR |
