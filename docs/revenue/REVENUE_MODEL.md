# Revenue Model

How Dealix earns money, in plain language.

## Revenue streams
1. **Signal Sample** — free or 199 SAR. Opens the conversation, qualifies the lead.
2. **Revenue Sprint** — 2,500–7,500 SAR. First paid result. One-time engagement.
3. **Managed Pilot** — 9,500–25,000 SAR. Multi-week proof of execution.
4. **Revenue Desk** — 5,000–20,000 SAR / month. Recurring monthly retainer.
5. **Dealix OS** — custom. Enterprise managed OS or licensed platform.

## Targets (rolling)
- 70% of monthly cash from recurring (Revenue Desk + OS).
- 30% from new Sprints and Pilots feeding the funnel.
- No single client > 30% of monthly cash.

## Margin model
- Sprint / Pilot — labor-driven. Margin protected by scope discipline.
- Revenue Desk — productized. Margin protected by template reuse.
- OS — high-margin once built; carries product investment cost.

## Definition of revenue
Revenue is recognised when cash is received and the invoice is closed, not when a proposal is signed.

## Rule
Every new offer must answer: who pays, how much, how often, and what proof closes the next sale.
