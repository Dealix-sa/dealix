# Qualification Rules

A prospect must pass this filter before any time, content, or message is invested.

## ICP filter (must pass all)
- Saudi-based or Saudi-active business.
- Has identifiable revenue motion (B2B sales, leads, accounts).
- Has a budget signal (existing tools, headcount, prior vendors).
- Has a decision contact reachable.

## Disqualifiers (any one disqualifies)
- Asks for guaranteed revenue.
- Asks for guaranteed legal or compliance certification.
- Wants to pay only after delivery without a contract.
- Sector or use case excluded by `docs/trust/CLAIM_GUARD.md` or sanctions screening.
- Listed in the suppression list.

## Scoring
Use `docs/acquisition/ICP_SCORING_MODEL.md` to score 0–100. A prospect under 60 is parked, not chased.

## Rule
A prospect that does not pass the filter is not a prospect. It is a distraction.
