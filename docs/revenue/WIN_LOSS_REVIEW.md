# Win / Loss Review

## Format (Per Deal)

| Date | Client | Offer | Price | Outcome | Reason Category | Reason Detail | Lesson |
|---|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|

## Outcomes

- **Won** — invoice issued, payment received.
- **Lost** — explicit no, or 21 days of silence after follow-up.
- **No Decision** — prospect deferred to a defined later trigger.

## Reason Categories

### Win Reasons
- Trust posture
- Saudi context fit
- Founder relationship
- Speed of turnaround
- Specific deliverable promise
- Referral / warm intro

### Loss Reasons
- Price too high for the offer
- Wrong stakeholder
- Wrong timing (budget, leadership change)
- Built in-house instead
- Chose another vendor
- Lost trust signal during sales (e.g., overclaim risk)
- Scope mismatch

## Cadence

- Per-deal entry within 7 days of outcome.
- Weekly summary in `WEEKLY_CEO_REVIEW.md` (rolled up).
- Monthly trend in `docs/learning/WIN_LOSS_REVIEW.md` (decisions captured).

## Rule

A loss without a recorded reason and lesson is treated as an open audit item
until completed. We do not move on until we know why.
