# Pricing Power System

## Purpose
Increase Dealix pricing through proof, clarity, and repeatable delivery.

## Pricing Levers
- stronger ICP
- better evidence
- faster delivery
- clearer output
- retainer path
- approved proof
- reduced risk
- better client fit

## Price Increase Triggers
- 3 paid sprints completed
- delivery QA stable
- client feedback positive
- demand exceeds capacity
- retainer conversion appears

## Discount Rules
- No discount without scope reduction.
- No discount above 20% without CEO approval.
- Discounts must not damage trust or positioning.
- Record every discount experiment.

## Metrics
- average deal size
- close rate
- delivery hours
- margin
- retainer conversion
