# Cash Rules

## Purpose
Define the conditions under which Dealix accepts money, starts work, and recognizes revenue.

## Start Conditions
Work starts only after one of the following is confirmed:
1. Bank transfer received and reconciled.
2. Payment gateway confirmation (Moyasar or equivalent).
3. Purchase order from authorized signer.
4. Written approval from the decision maker via email or signed document.

## No-Work-Without-Approval Rule
- No discovery work, no sample customization, and no delivery prep is performed before a start condition is met.
- Pre-sales samples are pre-built templates, not bespoke work.

## Pricing
- Revenue Sprint price points: 2,500 / 4,500 / custom SAR.
- Custom pricing requires a written scope and founder approval.
- No verbal pricing commitments.

## Discounts
- Discounts above 10% require founder approval and a written reason.
- Discounts are recorded in `dealix-ops-private/revenue/revenue_action_log.csv`.

## Refunds
- No refund is processed without a written request.
- Refund decisions require founder approval and an evidence trail.

## Revenue Recognition
- Revenue is recognized upon delivery acceptance per the delivery report.
- Retainers are recognized monthly based on service period.

## No-Go Conditions
Do not accept work where:
- Customer requests guaranteed sales or revenue.
- Scope is unclear or undefined.
- Decision maker is not identified.
- Customer refuses the start condition.
