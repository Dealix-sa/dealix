# Cash Rules

## Rule 1 — Invoice Before Work

No delivery work begins before an invoice is issued. The Free Diagnostic is
the only exception and is capped at 4 founder hours.

## Rule 2 — Payment Before Final Delivery

Sprints and Packs require payment in full before the final deliverable is
shared. Drafts may be shown after partial payment if explicitly agreed
in the proposal.

## Rule 3 — Retainer Day-One

Retainers are billed on day one of each month. Late payment triggers a
pause on new work after seven days.

## Rule 4 — No Personal Discounts

Discounts must be documented in `docs/strategy/PRICING_STRATEGY.md`. Ad-hoc
discounts are not allowed.

## Rule 5 — Refunds

Refunds require A3 approval per `docs/trust/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md` and a
written reason in `DECISION_LOG.md`. No exceptions.

## Rule 6 — SAR-Native

All client invoices are issued in SAR. International prospects receive
SAR pricing with a converted USD reference for clarity only.

## Rule 7 — Cash Visibility

Cash collected is updated in the Daily Command Brief every working day.
Cash expected is updated every Monday.

## Rule 8 — Reserve

Maintain a six-month operating reserve before any new hire or large
spend commitment. Cash above the reserve is the only cash available
for growth bets.

## Rule 9 — No Founder Loans In

The company does not run on founder cash injections after the first
operating year. If cash runs low, the answer is more sprints or fewer
expenses, not a personal top-up.

## Rule 10 — Receipt of Funds

Every payment is reconciled within 48 hours into the cash tracker.
