# Closing Playbook

How Dealix moves a prospect from Proposal Sent to Paid.

## Default sequence
1. **Send.** Proposal sent with a calendar link to a 20-minute decision call.
2. **Confirm.** Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours. Offer to walk through.
3. **Walk-through.** 20-minute call. Surface objections directly.
4. **Address.** Send a written response to objections within 24 hours.
5. **Decision call.** Yes / no / scope adjustment.
6. **Yes.** Invoice issued same day. Kickoff scheduled on payment.
7. **No.** Reason logged. Suppression check. Optional drip into nurture.

## Common objections and responses
- "Too expensive" → Anchor on the cost of doing nothing for one quarter.
- "Not the right time" → Offer the Signal Sample to keep the relationship warm.
- "We do this internally" → Offer a one-time benchmark Sprint, not a retainer.
- "Need to speak to my partner" → Offer a joint call with both decision-makers.

## Non-negotiables
- No work begins before payment.
- No discounts on the first engagement. Adjust scope, not price.
- No verbal commitments. Everything in writing.

## Rule
A close is the moment the founder hands the prospect to the delivery system. If the system is not ready to receive, do not close.
