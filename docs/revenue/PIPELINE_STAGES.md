# Pipeline Stages

## Purpose
Provide one canonical definition for pipeline stages so reporting and movement are based on evidence, not anticipation.

## Stage Definitions

### New
Lead identified but not contacted.
- Evidence: source captured, ICP fit hypothesis written.

### Qualified
Lead matches ICP and has business relevance.
- Evidence: sector match, deal size assumption, decision-maker identified.

### Contacted
First founder-led outreach sent.
- Evidence: DM or email body recorded; channel timestamp.

### Replied
Prospect responded.
- Evidence: response captured in pipeline note.

### Sample Sent
Sample pack delivered.
- Evidence: sample pack reference + send timestamp.

### Call Booked
Discovery or qualification call scheduled.
- Evidence: calendar invite or written confirmation.

### Proposal Sent
Formal proposal delivered.
- Evidence: proposal file + amount + follow-up date.

### Paid
Payment, PO, or written approval received.
- Evidence: payment receipt / PO / written approval reference.

### Delivered
Revenue Sprint delivered with QA evidence.
- Evidence: delivery report + QA checklist.

### Retainer
Client converted to monthly support.
- Evidence: retainer agreement.

### Lost
Lead closed with reason.
- Evidence: loss reason recorded.

## Movement Rule
Move stages based on evidence. Hope is not a stage transition.

## Reporting
- Weekly review counts stage transitions, not stage hopes.
- Conversion rates calculated from stage exit timestamps.
