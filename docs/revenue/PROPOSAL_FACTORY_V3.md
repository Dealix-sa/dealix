# Proposal Factory v3

## Purpose
Convert qualified interest into approved proposal drafts quickly.

## Proposal Inputs
- company
- sector
- trigger
- sample result
- pain hypothesis
- recommended offer
- price band
- start condition
- trust notes

## Proposal Types
- Starter Revenue Sprint
- Standard Revenue Sprint
- Premium Revenue Sprint
- Managed Pilot
- Revenue Desk Retainer
- Enterprise Command Center

## SLA
- Draft within 24h after qualified trigger.
- Follow-up within 48h.
- Decision push within 7 days.
- Close/recycle within 14 days.

## Rules
- No proposal without clear next action.
- No guaranteed revenue.
- No hidden scope.
- No delivery without start condition.
