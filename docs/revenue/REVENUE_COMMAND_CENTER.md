# Revenue Command Center

## Purpose
Make Dealix move daily toward cash collected, proof, and retainer conversion.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Daily.

## Inputs
- pipeline_tracker.csv
- revenue_action_log.csv
- proposal notes
- payment follow-ups
- call notes
- weekly review

## Outputs
- one revenue focus per day
- next action per lead
- proposal follow-up
- payment/PO path
- retainer candidate list

## Daily Revenue Rule
Every day must include at least one revenue-moving action:
- send DMs
- follow up
- prepare sample
- send proposal
- ask for payment / PO / written approval
- move client toward delivery or retainer

## Pipeline Rules
- Every lead has stage.
- Every lead has next_action.
- Every proposal has follow-up date.
- Every lost lead has reason.
- No full delivery without payment, PO, or written approval.

## Metrics
- leads added
- DMs sent
- replies
- calls booked
- samples sent
- proposals sent
- payment attempts
- payments received
- retainer candidates

## Evidence
- private pipeline
- revenue_action_log.csv
- proposal notes
- payment record
- weekly review
