# Proposal Rules

## Required Fields

Every proposal must contain:

1. Client legal name and contact.
2. Offer name and rung (matching `OFFER_LADDER.md`).
3. Scope — bulleted, finite list of deliverables.
4. Out-of-scope — what is explicitly excluded.
5. Timeline with start and end dates.
6. Price in SAR, payment terms, VAT note.
7. Trust statement (No-Overclaim) and approval matrix reference.
8. Signature block.

## Approval Gate

Every external proposal is an A2 action. The proposal must be approved
by the founder and logged in `DECISION_LOG.md` before being sent.

## Templates

Proposal templates live in `templates/` and `docs/templates/`. Use the
template that matches the offer rung; do not free-form a proposal unless
the offer is Custom AI (Rung 5).

## Versioning

Each sent proposal is filed in the proof library with a version number.
A revised proposal is a new version, never an overwrite.

## What Proposals Do NOT Include

- Guaranteed revenue uplift in absolute SAR.
- Compliance certifications we do not hold.
- Names of clients without their written consent to be referenced.
- Bonus deliverables added verbally and not in the document.

## Time Targets

- Proposal turnaround after Discovery: ≤ 48 hours.
- Acceptance window: 14 days from issue date.

## Lost Proposals

A lost proposal is recorded in `WIN_LOSS_REVIEW.md` within 7 days of
the loss with reason and category.
