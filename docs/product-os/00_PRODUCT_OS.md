# Product OS

> **Purpose (EN):** Master index for product.
>
> **الغرض (AR):** الفهرس الرئيسي للمنتج.


## Modules

- Lead Engine, Draft Factory, Founder Review Queue, Service Engine, Trust Engine, Commercial Dashboard, Delivery OS, Client Success OS, Analytics, Audit/Evidence Pack.


## Boundaries

- AI prepares; humans approve; system never sends.


---

### Operating footer
- **Owner:** Founder
- **Next action:** Review and approve
- **GO/NO-GO:** GO (internal asset) — external actions remain founder-gated

> **Non-negotiable:** AI drafts, analyzes, scores, ranks, recommends, and prepares. The founder reviews, approves, sells, sends manually, and signs off. **The system never sends externally.**
>
> **Safety boundary:** No automated email/WhatsApp/LinkedIn sending, no scraping, no form auto-submit, no live paid ads, no secrets. All outputs are local, review-only artifacts requiring founder approval before any external action.
