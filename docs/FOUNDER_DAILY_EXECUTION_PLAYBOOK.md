## THE RITUAL (Non-Negotiable)

### Morning Block: 45 minutes (8:00 AM - 8:45 AM)

**Step 1: Check Overnight Lead Funnel (10 min)**
- Open `/founder-leads.html` (your daily lead dashboard — system will populate this)
- Scan: New inbound diagnostics? New demo requests? Overnight responses from yesterday's outreach?
- Action: Reply to any demo requests with 30-min SLA (tomorrow at latest)

**Step 2: Send 1 Warm WhatsApp (20 min)**
- Open `/warm-intro-targets.html` (your prospect list; maintained by Sami only)
- Pick 1 prospect from the "Not Yet Contacted" list
- Draft 1 personal message (Khaliji Arabic, no template, under 50 chars):
  ```
  السلام عليكم 🙏
  أنا سامي من ديليكس، شركة سعودية في AI للمبيعات
  فيه 30 دقيقة مجانية لنشوف إن كان عندك فرصة في الـCRM
  وقت مناسب للاتصال الأسبوع الجاي؟
  ```
- Send personally (not a link, not a draft)
- Mark in spreadsheet: Date, time, status = "Sent"

**Step 3: Review & Approve AI Drafts (10 min)**
- Open `/decisions.html` — queue of AI-drafted items awaiting founder approval
- Items to review/approve:
  - Outreach to objection-flagged prospects (sales agent drafted responses)
  - Auto-generated diagnostic summaries (ready to send to prospects)
  - Customer follow-ups (day 3, 7, 14 reminders)
- Approve 3-5 items; flag any that need rewording

**Step 4: Check Calendar (5 min)**
- 1-2 demo slots booked for today? Tomorrow?
- If yes: prepare brief 1-pager on that company's sector

### Demo Block: As Scheduled (Usually 30-60 min)

**Format: 30-min Diagnostic Call**

**Opening (2 min):**
```
السلام عليكم 👋
أنا سامي، مؤسس ديليكس
شكراً إنك وجدت وقت لنا اليوم
```

**Discovery (8 min):**
- "شنو أكبر تحدي في المبيعات هسع؟"
- "كم عميل شهرياً؟"
- "كم منهم تقفل؟"
- "كيف تتابعهم؟"
- Listen more than talk. Take notes.

**Pitch (5 min):**
- "نحنا نقدم real-time visibility للمبيعات. كل عميل، كل اتصال مسجل، كل follow-up مقررة ما تنسى"
- Share 1 screenshot of dashboard (no live demo)
- "نبدأ بـ499 ريال، 14 يوم، zero risk. تشوف النتائج بنفسك"

**Close (7 min):**
- "ودك تجرب؟" (Do you want to try?)
- If yes: "احجز الجمعة، نبدأ الساعة 9 الصبح"
- If no: "ليش ما يشتغل معنا؟" (What's the blocker?) — listen, note it
- If maybe: "نرسل لك فيديو 3 دقايق; شوفه وقول لي رأيك"

**Post-Call (5 min):**
- Immediately update `/warm-intro-targets.html`:
  - Status: "Completed Diagnostic"
  - Outcome: "Yes", "No", "Maybe"
  - Next step: "Waiting pilot signature", "Objection: X", "Follow-up: Friday"

## GUARDRAILS & RULES

### ✅ DO

- **Send personal WhatsApps** (not templates, not links)
- **Use Khaliji Arabic** (not MSA; trust signals matter)
- **Sign your name** (Sami, not "Dealix Team")
- **Ask directly** ("ودك تجرب؟" = "Want to try?")
- **Take detailed notes** on objections (these teach you)
- **Be honest about price and timeline** (499 SAR, 14 days, no BS)
- **Listen 60% of the time** (founders talk 40%, ask questions 60%)

### ❌ DON'T

- **Send cold blasts** (NO_COLD_WHATSAPP gate enforced in code)
- **Use templates** (sounds foreign; kills trust)
- **Oversell features** (sell the outcome: "see results in 14 days")
- **Promise things you can't deliver** (delivery is 40h; be real)
- **Follow up 10 times** (max 3 follow-ups; then move on)
- **Work past 6 PM** (founder burnout = company death)
- **Demo on first call** (discovery first; demo on second call)
