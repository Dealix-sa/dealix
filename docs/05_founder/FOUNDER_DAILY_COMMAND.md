## 4. The discipline (what NOT to do)

- Don't build full SaaS before the first 3 customers.
- Don't open 10 sectors.
- Don't do a big public launch.
- Don't enable auto-send.
- Don't say "certified."
- Don't add agents without contracts.
- Don't change pricing without `PRICE_AUTHORITY.md`.
- Don't sell enterprise before readiness.
- Don't spend two weeks on docs with no sales.

## 5. The 10 standing decisions

1. Dealix = Business OS.
2. Command Sprint = first offer.
3. Revenue = wedge, not identity.
4. Proof = trust layer.
5. Governance = safety layer.
6. No auto-send.
7. No fake proof.
8. No public launch before 3 paid Sprints.
9. No new features before verification.
10. No customer claim without evidence.
