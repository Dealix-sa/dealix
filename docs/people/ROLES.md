# Roles

Each role at Dealix has one outcome it owns. No matrix. No shared accountability.

## Roles

| Role | Outcome owned | Backup |
|---|---|---|
| Founder | Existence of the company | Trust Lead |
| Trust Lead | The trust boundary holds | Founder |
| Revenue Lead | Booked revenue this month | Founder |
| Delivery Lead | Sprints close on time, in scope | Trust Lead |
| Customer Success Lead | Retainers renew | Revenue Lead |
| Data / AI Engineer | Agent reliability | Trust Lead |

## Boundary rule

- A role may not approve its own A1 actions.
- Trust Lead approvals are never delegated.
- Founder may delegate A1 to the named owner of the relevant function.

Founder.
