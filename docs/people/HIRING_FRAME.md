# Hiring Frame

Who Dealix hires and why. Hiring is downstream of revenue. The frame: hire only when the next paying customer requires a human we do not currently have.

## Roles in the order they are filled

1. Founder (in place).
2. Trust Lead (named A2 signer; can be the founder for the first three sprints).
3. Revenue Lead (owns the offer ladder and the pipeline).
4. Delivery Lead (owns sprint execution).
5. Data / AI engineer (only when agent reliability becomes the bottleneck).
6. Customer Success Lead (only when retainers reach the threshold defined in [docs/client_success/](../client_success/)).

## Filters

- Saudi market literacy (bilingual EN/AR is non-negotiable).
- Comfortable working inside the trust boundary (no shortcuts).
- Track record of finishing things, not just starting them.

## Disqualifiers

- A pattern of over-promising in past roles.
- An attachment to specific tools rather than to outcomes.
- Unwillingness to operate under approval gates.
