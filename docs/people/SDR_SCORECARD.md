# SDR / VA Scorecard

> Used at 30 / 60 / 90 day check-ins. Hard numbers, not vibes.

## Role Summary

Owns lead sourcing volume, initial enrichment, scheduled outreach (drafts founder-approved, sends by SDR), pipeline tracker hygiene.

## Daily Outputs

| Metric | Day 30 target | Day 60 target | Day 90 target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Leads sourced (qualified) | 8/day | 12/day | 15/day |
| Enrichment quality (spot-check pass) | ≥ 90% | ≥ 95% | ≥ 95% |
| Outreach sends (founder-approved drafts) | 15/day | 20/day | 25/day |
| Pipeline tracker hygiene (rows current) | 95% | 100% | 100% |

## Weekly Outputs

| Metric | Day 30 | Day 60 | Day 90 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly hours logged | 20 | 25 | 25 (capped) |
| Approval queue overflows (founder waiting > 4 hr) | < 2 | < 1 | 0 |
| Suppression-list breaches | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| Trust audit clean | yes | yes | yes |

## Behaviors

- Follows allow-listed sources only — no scraping, no purchased lists
- Logs every action in `pipeline/pipeline_tracker.csv` per `PIPELINE_STAGES.md`
- Asks before deviating from playbook
- Surfaces patterns (sector working, message landing, etc.) to founder weekly
- Does not negotiate scope or pricing with prospects

## Red Flags

- Source from non-allow-listed channels
- Skips claim_guard check on a draft
- Edits a founder-approved draft after approval
- Doesn't log a sent message
- Engages a suppression-list contact
- "Forgets" the pipeline tracker
- Adds prospects without fit score

## 30/60/90 Decision

- **30 days:** continue, with specific coaching points
- **60 days:** continue, expand scope if scorecard green
- **90 days:** continue OR move to FT OR end relationship

Each decision logged in `people/decisions.md` (private).

## Onboarding Plan (first 30 days)

- Week 1: shadow + intake docs + tooling setup
- Week 2: produce under founder review (every action)
- Week 3: produce under batch review (50% spot-check)
- Week 4: produce under standard supervision (10% spot-check)

## Compensation

- Hourly or part-time monthly retainer
- No commission this quarter (avoids gaming volume)
- Quarterly bonus if scorecard ≥ 95% sustained (capped)

## Termination

- 14-day notice either way
- Trust violation = same-day termination
- Non-trust performance issue = 30-day improvement plan + decision

## Tools The Role Uses

- LinkedIn (manual, no automation)
- Pipeline tracker (private repo CSV)
- Allow-listed enrichment sources
- Dealix internal agents (scoring, drafting) — under approval matrix

## What This Role Refuses

- Cold-channel automation
- Adding contacts from purchased lists
- Sending unreviewed drafts
- Engaging prospects beyond outreach cadence
- Negotiating with prospects on scope/price
