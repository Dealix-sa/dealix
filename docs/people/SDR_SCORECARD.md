# SDR Scorecard

## Purpose
Define the expected output and quality standard for an SDR or outbound assistant.

## Responsibilities
- find qualified leads
- prepare outbound queue
- update pipeline
- draft follow-ups
- log activity

## Not Allowed
- promise revenue
- send unapproved messages
- change pricing
- approve proposals
- access unnecessary client data

## Weekly Metrics
- leads sourced
- qualified leads
- DMs prepared
- replies
- bad-fit rate
- data quality
- pipeline completeness

## Quality Bar
- every lead has sector
- every lead has next action
- every A lead has reason
- no fake personalization
- no banned claims
