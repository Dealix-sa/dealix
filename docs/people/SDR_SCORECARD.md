# SDR Scorecard

How an SDR or VA is measured at Dealix.

## Weekly targets (starter band)
- Sourced leads: 100+
- Outreach sent (after QA): 80+
- Reply rate: 10%+
- Calls booked: 5+

## Quality gates
- 100% of sent messages pass `MESSAGE_QUALITY_STANDARD.md`.
- 100% of leads have a `source_url`.
- 0 sends to the suppression list.

## Behavior gates
- All A1 actions logged within 24 hours.
- Weekly self-review submitted each Friday.

## Review cadence
- Weekly 1-on-1 (15 minutes).
- Monthly scorecard review.
- Quarterly band review (move to higher targets if sustained).

## Rule
The SDR is a trust role, not a volume role. Quality gates outrank quantity targets.
