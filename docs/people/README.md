# People

## Purpose
Define how Dealix hires, onboards, and runs the small team that supports the founder without diluting trust or quality.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Monthly, plus after every hire, exit, or role change.

## Inputs
- Workload signals (founder hours, bottleneck loops).
- Hiring need cases (revenue, delivery, trust, product, ops).
- Contractor performance.
- Compliance and labour requirements.

## Outputs
- Role definitions and scopes.
- Hiring criteria per role.
- Onboarding checklist per role.
- Performance and exit playbooks.

## Rules
- No hire is made until the workload is proven and the role is defined here.
- Every contractor and employee signs the required NDAs before access (A3 - founder only).
- Access to sensitive systems follows least-privilege and is logged.
- People decisions never override Trust OS rules.

## Metrics
- Roles filled vs. open.
- Time to ramp (days to first independent delivery).
- Quality of work post-onboarding (QA pass rate, client feedback).
- Founder hours freed per hire.

## Evidence
- role definition files in this folder.
- onboarding checklists.
- private HR/contract files (not stored here).
- performance review notes.
