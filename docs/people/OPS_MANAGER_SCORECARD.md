# Ops Manager Scorecard

How an ops manager is measured at Dealix.

## Weekly targets
- 100% of active engagements have a current weekly report.
- 100% of A1 / A2 actions logged within 24 hours.
- 0 missed QA checkpoints.
- 100% of retainer renewals tracked at 45 / 30 / 14 / 7 day intervals.

## Quality gates
- 0 client incidents from missed handoffs.
- 100% of feedback forms sent within 24 hours of handoff.
- 0 unreviewed risks in `RISK_REGISTER.md` for more than two weeks.

## Behavior gates
- Weekly ops summary submitted each Friday.
- Monthly operations review presented to the founder.

## Review cadence
- Weekly 1-on-1 (30 minutes).
- Monthly scorecard review.
- Quarterly capability review (move toward owning vendor and partner ops).

## Rule
Ops is the silent system that makes everything else possible. Measure it like infrastructure.
