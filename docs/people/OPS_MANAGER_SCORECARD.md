# Ops Manager Scorecard — بطاقة أداء مدير العمليات

## Purpose
Performance metrics for the Ops Manager role, triggered at ≥5 active retainers. Owner of calendars, vendor management, contractor coordination, and operational dashboards.

## Metrics (monthly)

| Metric | Definition | Target (monthly) |
|---|---|---|
| Schedule integrity | % of standing client meetings held on schedule | ≥95% |
| Vendor / SaaS audit | Active vendors reviewed against necessity, cost, terms | quarterly cycle |
| Contractor coordination | Tasks delivered on time across contractors | ≥95% |
| Operational incident count | Calendar slips, vendor lapses, access issues | ≤2/month |
| Reconciliation support timeliness | Days from request to fulfilled finance reconciliation support | ≤2 days |
| Dashboard freshness | Operational dashboard updated by 5th of each month | yes/no |
| Approval-log discipline | A2 entries logged within 24h of approval given | 100% |

## Behaviors expected
- Maintains the operational dashboard sourced from registers (sales, delivery, finance, content).
- Coordinates contractor onboarding per [`CONTRACTOR_ONBOARDING.md`](CONTRACTOR_ONBOARDING.md).
- Triages internal requests; pulls founder in only on A-level approvals.
- Surfaces emerging triggers per [`HIRING_TRIGGERS.md`](HIRING_TRIGGERS.md) with evidence.
- Does not commit Dealix to external positions or claims; routes to founder.

## Bands

| Band | Score | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Top performer | All metrics at target; zero approval-log misses | Continue; expand scope |
| Meeting bar | Within 10% of target; clean approval discipline | Continue |
| Below bar | One metric >10% off; OR ≥3 incidents/month | 30-day plan |
| Off-process | Approval-log misses; or below-bar two months | Review per `REVIEW_CADENCE.md` |

## Rules
- Ops Manager has no unilateral authority on spend or pricing. All approvals route per `docs/governance/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.
- Ops Manager has no direct customer-facing commitments. Statements to customers route through founder or named delivery lead.
- Any process exception is logged; recurring exceptions become process change proposals.

## Operations
- Monthly review meeting with founder; written sign-off.
- Quarterly: contributes to compensation per `COMPENSATION.md`.

## Evidence
- Sources: operational dashboard, schedule log, vendor register, approval log.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder (manager); Ops Manager submits.
- Cadence: monthly review; quarterly summary.

## القسم العربي

**المقاييس الشهرية:** سلامة الجدولة ≥95%، دورة تدقيق موردين/SaaS ربعية، تنسيق مقاولين بالتسليم في الوقت ≥95%، حوادث تشغيلية ≤2/شهر، دعم مطابقة المالية ≤يومان، تحديث لوحة العمليات قبل اليوم الخامس، تسجيل A2 خلال 24 ساعة 100%.

**السلوكيات:** صيانة لوحة العمليات من السجلات، تنسيق تأهيل المقاولين، فرز الطلبات الداخلية، رصد محفّزات التوظيف، عدم التزام علني بأي موقف.

**الفئات:** ممتاز، يفي، تحت المعيار، خارج العملية.

**القواعد:** لا سلطة منفردة على الإنفاق أو التسعير. لا التزامات تجاه العملاء. الاستثناءات تُسجّل، المتكررة تصبح مقترحات تغيير عملية.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
