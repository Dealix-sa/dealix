# Ops Manager Scorecard

> Used at 30 / 60 / 90 day check-ins.

## Role Summary

Owns cross-sprint coordination, contractor management, process compliance auditing, Daily Brief operations, monthly close-the-books.

## Monthly Outputs

| Metric | Day 30 | Day 60 | Day 90 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Active retainers supported (without delivery slip) | 3 | 5 | 5–7 |
| Daily Brief on time (5/5 weekdays) | 80% | 95% | 100% |
| Monthly close-the-books accuracy (reconciled to bank) | 100% | 100% | 100% |
| Process audit findings closed within 14 days | ≥ 80% | ≥ 90% | 100% |
| Contractor scorecards updated on time | 100% | 100% | 100% |

## Weekly Outputs

| Metric | Day 30 | Day 60 | Day 90 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hours logged | 30 | 35 | 40 |
| Cross-team friction issues surfaced | timely | timely | timely |
| Weekly CEO Review inputs prepared | 100% | 100% | 100% |
| Trust audit summary prepared | 100% | 100% | 100% |

## Behaviors

- Process-first mindset: every miss → playbook update
- Cross-functional communication: pulls all systems together
- Calm in incident: knows incident response, executes the 4 R's
- Honest reporting: red numbers reported as red
- Founder protector: clears low-leverage tasks off founder's plate without losing oversight

## Red Flags

- Hides red numbers
- Skips trust audit "because nothing happened"
- Becomes the bottleneck (substitutes for the role they should manage out)
- Allows scope drift across multiple clients
- Allows incident response to slip

## Authority

- Process changes within existing playbooks: yes
- New role hires: founder + advisor only
- Pricing: founder only
- Trust approvals: founder only
- Strategic decisions: founder only

## Trust Posture

- Manages contractors under NDA + scoped access
- Approves contractor day-to-day, but escalates trust questions
- Generates monthly trust audit summary for founder review

## Compensation

- FT monthly salary (per `HIRING_TRIGGERS.md` cap)
- No revenue commission
- Quarterly bonus tied to retention rate + delivery on-time + trust scorecard

## 30/60/90 Decision

- **30 days:** continue with coaching
- **60 days:** continue, add scope (close-the-books, contractor management) if green
- **90 days:** confirm permanent OR end relationship

## Onboarding Plan (first 30 days)

- Week 1: read every doc in `docs/`; meet every contractor (if any)
- Week 2: shadow founder's day completely
- Week 3: own Daily Brief generation; founder verifies
- Week 4: own one cross-sprint coordination cycle

## What This Role Refuses

- Strategic decisions
- Founder-only approvals
- Substituting for any specialist role
- Becoming the company's email-replier (the role exists to make founder higher-leverage, not to become founder)
- Hiring without founder + advisor
