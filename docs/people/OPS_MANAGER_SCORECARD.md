# Ops Manager Scorecard

## Purpose
Define future operations role once Dealix has recurring client and delivery load.

## Responsibilities
- maintain trackers
- prepare weekly reviews
- chase internal follow-ups
- coordinate delivery timelines
- maintain evidence ledger
- prepare board pack inputs

## Not Allowed
- make pricing exceptions
- approve A2/A3 trust decisions
- change strategy
- publish proof
- send client commitments without approval

## Metrics
- tracker completeness
- weekly review readiness
- missed follow-ups
- delivery timeline adherence
- operating cadence completion
