# Hiring Trigger System

## Purpose
Prevent premature hiring and make every role tied to evidence.

## Hiring Triggers

| Role | Trigger | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| VA / Research Assistant | 5+ hrs/week repeated research/admin | `founder/founder_time_log.md` |
| SDR Assistant | 100 DMs/week with proven message | `message_performance.csv` |
| Delivery Analyst | 3 reports/week | delivery reports + QA |
| Ops Manager | 5 retainers | `mrr_tracker.csv` |
| Client Success | 10 retainers | `retention_tracker.csv` |

## No-Hire Conditions
Do not hire if:
- workflow is unclear
- demand is not repeated
- role has no scorecard
- founder cannot define output
- trust/data access is uncontrolled
- revenue cannot support cost

## Hiring Decision
Hire / Contractor / Automate / Defer / Kill

## Rule
Most early roles should start as contractor or part-time.
