# Hiring Triggers — محفّزات التوظيف

## Purpose
Quantitative thresholds that authorize a hire. Without a trigger, no hire is made — preventing premature org expansion. With a trigger, the founder is required to act within the stated window.

## Triggers

| Role | Trigger (sustained value) | Window to act |
|---|---|---|
| SDR / Virtual Assistant | ≥100 outbound DMs/week sustained for 2 weeks | Within 30 days |
| Delivery Analyst (FT) | ≥3 client reports/week sustained for 4 weeks | Within 30 days |
| Ops Manager | ≥5 active retainers running for ≥1 month | Within 45 days |
| Client Success | ≥10 active retainers running for ≥1 month | Within 45 days |
| Engineering Lead | Sustained build need + budget per `docs/finance/CAPITAL_ALLOCATION.md` | Within 60 days |
| Finance Lead | ≥SAR 250k MRR sustained for 3 months | Within 60 days |
| Additional analyst (each) | Existing analyst at >85% utilization for 4 weeks AND backlog growing | Within 45 days |

## Rules
- A trigger is "sustained" — a single spike does not authorize a hire.
- A trigger that fires and then drops below threshold in the next two weeks does not authorize a hire. Founder waits for re-confirmation.
- Founder cannot hire ahead of a trigger except with written A2 self-approval and a documented investment thesis.
- Founder cannot delay a hire past the window without written rationale (e.g., capital constraint). If delayed, the trigger remains armed and re-armed monthly.
- One hire at a time. Concurrent hires only by written exception.

## Reverse triggers (when to pause or remove)
- Role utilization <30% for 8 weeks → pause role; reassess scope.
- Role scorecard fails for 2 consecutive quarters → manage out or remove per [`REVIEW_CADENCE.md`](REVIEW_CADENCE.md).
- The trigger that authorized the role no longer holds for 8 weeks → reassess.

## Operations
- Founder watches trigger metrics weekly.
- When a trigger fires, founder records the event, the window, and the action plan.

## Evidence
- Trigger fires logged in `docs/people/role_changes_log.md` with date, metric value, and decision.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: weekly metric check; monthly summary.

## القسم العربي

**المحفّزات:**

| الدور | المحفّز | نافذة التحرك |
|---|---|---|
| SDR/مساعد افتراضي | ≥100 رسالة خارجية/أسبوع مستدامة لأسبوعين | 30 يومًا |
| محلل تسليم بدوام كامل | ≥3 تقارير عميل/أسبوع لـ4 أسابيع | 30 يومًا |
| مدير عمليات | ≥5 retainers لشهر | 45 يومًا |
| Client Success | ≥10 retainers لشهر | 45 يومًا |
| قائد هندسي | حاجة بناء مستدامة + ميزانية | 60 يومًا |
| قائد مالية | ≥250 ألف ريال MRR لـ3 أشهر | 60 يومًا |
| محلل إضافي | محلل قائم باستخدام >85% لـ4 أسابيع وتراكم متنامٍ | 45 يومًا |

**القواعد:** المحفّز "مستدام"، قفزة واحدة لا تكفي. هبوط بعد التحفيز لأسبوعين يُلغي. التوظيف قبل المحفّز يحتاج A2 ذاتية مع فرضية استثمار. التأخير عن النافذة بسبب موثق ممكن مع إعادة تسليح شهرية. توظيف واحد في كل مرة.

**المحفّزات العكسية:** استخدام <30% لـ8 أسابيع — إيقاف. فشل بطاقة لربعين — إنهاء. زوال المحفّز لـ8 أسابيع — إعادة تقييم.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
