# Hiring Triggers

> The conditions under which we hire. No "feel" hiring. Every hire is triggered.

## Principle

We hire only when the work is already happening and a human can verifiably take it off the founder. We don't hire for "growth potential". We don't hire because peers are hiring.

## Trigger Schema

A hire fires only when **all four** of:
1. Specific role from `ROLE_MAP.md`
2. Quantitative threshold met for 3+ consecutive weeks
3. Cash to cover 12 months of total burdened cost
4. Founder + advisor signoff

## SDR / VA (part-time)

**Threshold:** > 100 founder-drafted outreach per week sustained for 3 weeks, AND ≥ 1 delivery on-time slip attributable to outreach time.

**Cost cap:** ≤ SAR 4,000/mo burdened (PT contractor)

**Decision gate:** Weekly CEO Review

## Delivery Analyst (PT → FT)

**Threshold:** ≥ 3 active sprints sustained for 3 weeks, AND QA failure rate > 5% OR delivery time > 8 days average.

**Cost cap:** ≤ SAR 8,000/mo burdened (PT) or ≤ SAR 14,000 (FT)

**Decision gate:** Weekly CEO Review + advisor signoff

## Ops Manager (FT)

**Threshold:** ≥ 5 active Managed Ops retainers sustained for 6 weeks, AND founder time on internal ops > 30% of week, AND cash collected ≥ SAR 30K/mo for 2 months.

**Cost cap:** ≤ SAR 20,000/mo burdened

**Decision gate:** Weekly CEO Review + advisor signoff + 12-month cash plan

## Engineering Contractor (project-based)

**Threshold:** First paid Custom AI client signed + SOW requires technical work > 40 hr/month founder bandwidth.

**Cost cap:** ≤ 30% of that customer's monthly fee, project-based

**Decision gate:** Founder + advisor signoff per contract

## Disallowed Hires (this year)

- Marketing manager
- BDR / SDR full-time before PT sustained for 3+ months
- Engineering lead before 2+ Custom AI customers
- COO / President / "leadership" titles
- Anyone hired to "scale" before we've proven the unit economics scale
- Anyone whose role doesn't appear in `ROLE_MAP.md`

## Pre-Hire Checklist

- [ ] Threshold met for 3+ consecutive weeks (logged)
- [ ] Cash on hand for 12 months of burdened cost
- [ ] Role description matches `ROLE_MAP.md`
- [ ] Scorecard for the role drafted (see `SDR_SCORECARD.md`, etc.)
- [ ] Onboarding plan drafted (`CONTRACTOR_ONBOARDING.md` if contractor)
- [ ] Trust posture: NDA + scoped access drafted
- [ ] Founder + advisor signoff
- [ ] Decision logged in `decision_log.md`

## Post-Hire Review

- 30 days: scorecard check; on track?
- 60 days: scorecard check + role calibration
- 90 days: hire/no-hire decision (for contractors-to-permanent)

## When To Defer A Hire (even if triggers fire)

- Cash runway < 12 months at expected burdened cost
- Founder hasn't yet documented the role's playbook (you can't onboard without one)
- Active trust incident in progress
- Pending advisor / counsel signoff

## When To Reverse A Hire

- Scorecard fails at 30 / 60 / 90 day check
- Trust violation
- Role becomes unnecessary (e.g., automation makes the role obsolete)

Reversals are not failures — they're discipline. Log + learn.

## What This Refuses

- Hiring on optimism
- Hiring because "the company should look bigger"
- Hiring before the role is documented
- Hiring before cash supports it
- Skipping the advisor signoff
