# Hiring Triggers

Numbers that say "it is time".

| Role | Trigger | First hire form |
|---|---|---|
| SDR / VA | 100+ DMs / week sustained for 4 weeks | Part-time contractor |
| Delivery Analyst | 3+ active client engagements / week for 4 weeks | Part-time contractor |
| Ops Manager | 5+ retainers active | Full-time |
| Customer Success | 10+ retainers active | Full-time |
| Engineer | Product roadmap committed and revenue funding it | Full-time |

## Process when a trigger fires
1. Log a candidate decision in `DECISION_LOG.md`.
2. Draft the scorecard (`SDR_SCORECARD.md`, etc.).
3. Define the onboarding plan (`CONTRACTOR_ONBOARDING.md`).
4. Hire on a trial period first wherever possible.

## Rule
Hiring before the trigger is premature. Hiring two cycles after is overdue and costs more than a hire would have.
