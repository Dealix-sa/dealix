# People Operating Routine

## Weekly
- review founder bottlenecks
- update delegation log
- identify repeated tasks
- choose document / template / automate / delegate
- do not hire unless trigger exists

## Monthly
- review contractor needs
- review access log
- review role scorecards
- review hiring triggers
- review partner pipeline

## Before Delegating
Ask:
1. Is the task repeated?
2. Is the SOP clear?
3. Is QA defined?
4. Is data access controlled?
5. Is the risk low enough?
6. Can output be measured?

## Rule
Delegation without QA creates rework, not leverage.
