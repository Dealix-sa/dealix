# People, Delegation & Partner Operating System

## Purpose
Help Dealix scale founder capacity through documented work, safe delegation,
contractor support, and partner growth.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Monthly, and whenever founder becomes a bottleneck.

## Core Principle
Do not delegate unclear work. Document before delegation. QA before scale.

## Flow

```
Repeated Work
    → SOP
    → Template
    → QA Checklist
    → Delegation Candidate
    → Role Scorecard
    → Contractor / VA / SDR / Analyst
    → Performance Review
    → Scale or Kill
```

## Delegation Rules
- Founder keeps strategy, closing, trust approvals, pricing exceptions, and key client relationships.
- Repeated low-risk work can be delegated after SOP exists.
- Client-facing work requires QA.
- Anything involving claims, public proof, pricing, or sensitive data requires founder approval.
- No contractor gets unnecessary access to private data.
- No partner can change scope, price, claims, or delivery commitments.

## Metrics
- founder hours saved
- repeated tasks delegated
- QA pass rate after delegation
- contractor output quality
- partner-sourced leads
- partner-sourced revenue
- trust incidents
- rework count
