# Role Architecture

## Purpose
Define future roles Dealix may need, but only when triggers are met.

## Current Stage
Founder-led. No full-time hiring required yet.

## Future Roles

### VA / Research Assistant
**Purpose:** Support lead sourcing, data cleanup, formatting, and admin.

**Trigger:**
- founder spends 5+ hours/week on repeatable admin or research.

### SDR / Outbound Assistant
**Purpose:** Prepare lead lists and draft outbound queues.

**Trigger:**
- 100+ DMs/week target.
- message and ICP are proven.
- founder cannot keep up.

### Delivery Analyst
**Purpose:** Prepare lead tables, evidence notes, and draft reports.

**Trigger:**
- 3+ Revenue Sprint deliveries per week.
- QA process stable.

### Ops Manager
**Purpose:** Run operating cadence, trackers, reviews, and cross-system follow-up.

**Trigger:**
- 5 active retainers.
- founder bottleneck blocks delivery/revenue.

### Client Success Lead
**Purpose:** Own feedback, health scores, renewals, and retainer expansion.

**Trigger:**
- 10 retainers or recurring client load.

## Rule
Hire only when repeated demand exists and SOP is clear.
