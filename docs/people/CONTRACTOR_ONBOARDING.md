# Contractor Onboarding

The first two weeks for any contractor or part-time hire.

## Day 0
- NDA signed.
- Access provisioned (only what is needed).
- Read list shared: `FOUNDER_FOCUS_RULES.md`, role scorecard, `CLAIM_GUARD.md`, `APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.

## Day 1–3
- Walk through the system the contractor will use.
- Shadow one example end-to-end.
- Reproduce one example with founder review.

## Day 4–10
- Take a real task with founder review on every output.
- Daily 10-minute check-in.

## Day 11–14
- Take a real task with sampled review.
- First scorecard review.
- Trial-to-engagement decision.

## Access principles
- Least privilege by default.
- Time-limited tokens where possible.
- All access logged.

## Rule
A contractor who reaches week 2 without producing one real, reviewed output is misaligned. Cut early, kindly.
