# Contractor Onboarding — تأهيل المقاولين

## Purpose
First-day pack and onboarding sequence for any contractor (SDR, analyst, bookkeeper, designer, translator). Keeps access scoped, terms clear, and safety briefing consistent.

## Day-0 pack (signed before any access)
- Contractor agreement, scoped and time-bound.
- NDA.
- Personal data form (KSA tax/labor compliance).
- Background-check confirmation (sector-appropriate).
- Read-and-acknowledge: `docs/governance/PDPL_DATA_RULES.md`, `docs/governance/PII_REDACTION_POLICY.md`, `docs/governance/FORBIDDEN_ACTIONS.md`, [`docs/content/FOUNDER_VOICE.md`](../content/FOUNDER_VOICE.md).

## Day 1 — orientation (45 minutes)
1. What Dealix does and what it does not.
2. Voice and content rules; banned claims.
3. Data handling: PDPL, PII redaction, where data lives, what cannot leave the perimeter.
4. Channels: chat group, doc repo, where to ask questions.
5. The first task and its acceptance criteria.

## Day 1 — access provisioning (scope-minimum)
- Email or chat account with name and role.
- Repository access scoped to the folders the contractor needs.
- Tool access (CRM, drive, etc.) scoped by role per [`docs/governance/PERMISSION_MIRRORING.md`](../governance/PERMISSION_MIRRORING.md).
- 2FA on every account.
- No shared logins.

## Day 1 — safety briefing
- What to do if PII is encountered unexpectedly.
- What to do if a customer asks for something outside scope (route to founder).
- What to do if a vendor or partner requests data (route to founder).
- What to do on an incident (log, escalate, do not "fix and forget").
- Banned actions list: scraping, bulk outreach, off-brand claims, sending external messages on behalf of clients without explicit approval.

## Week 1 — supervised work
- First two tasks reviewed line-by-line by manager.
- Manager logs feedback in contractor folder.
- Daily 10-minute end-of-day check until pattern stabilizes.

## First-day pack checklist
- [ ] Contractor agreement signed
- [ ] NDA signed
- [ ] Tax/labor docs collected
- [ ] Read-and-acknowledge of governance docs
- [ ] Orientation call held
- [ ] Access provisioned (scoped, 2FA)
- [ ] First task assigned with acceptance criteria
- [ ] Safety briefing delivered
- [ ] Backup/escalation contact named

## Rules
- No work begins before the entire pack is signed.
- No access shared between contractors.
- All access reviewed at contract end and revoked within 1 business day.
- Any contractor breach of voice, redaction, or forbidden-actions rules triggers an incident review.

## Operations
- Pack stored in `docs/people/contractors/<contractor-label>/`.
- Quarterly: access audit per `docs/governance/AUDIT_LOG_POLICY.md`.

## Evidence
- Signed pack documents, access provisioning log, orientation notes.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder until Ops Manager is in role; then Ops Manager with founder for safety briefing.
- Cadence: per new contractor.

## القسم العربي

**حزمة اليوم 0:** عقد مقاول محدد النطاق والمدة، NDA، نموذج بيانات شخصية (PDPL/أنظمة العمل)، خلفية، إقرار بقراءة وثائق الحوكمة و`FOUNDER_VOICE.md`.

**يوم 1 — التوجيه (45 دقيقة):** ما تفعله ديلكس وما لا تفعله، قواعد الصوت والادعاءات الممنوعة، التعامل مع البيانات، القنوات، أول مهمة ومعايير قبولها.

**يوم 1 — تزويد الوصول:** بريد/شات باسم ودور، وصول المستودع بنطاق ضيق، أدوات بنطاق الدور، 2FA، لا حسابات مشتركة.

**يوم 1 — الإحاطة الأمنية:** ماذا تفعل عند مواجهة PII، طلب خارج النطاق، طلب بيانات من مورد، حادثة، قائمة الإجراءات الممنوعة (scraping، bulk outreach، ادعاءات خارج العلامة، رسائل بالنيابة عن العميل بدون إذن).

**أسبوع 1 — عمل مُشرَف:** أول مهمتين مراجعة سطرًا بسطر، فحص يومي 10 دقائق نهاية اليوم.

**القواعد:** لا عمل قبل توقيع الحزمة. لا مشاركة وصول. مراجعة وصول عند نهاية العقد وإلغاء خلال يوم عمل. أي خرق يستدعي مراجعة حادثة.

**المالك:** المؤسس ثم مدير العمليات بعد التوظيف.
