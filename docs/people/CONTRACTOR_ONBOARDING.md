# Contractor Onboarding

> Same trust posture as employees, scoped to one client (or one function) at a time.

## Pre-Day-1

- NDA signed and on file (`legal/nda_template.md` in private)
- Scoped access agreement (which client, which data, which tools)
- DPA addendum (if contractor handles client data)
- Calendar invite for Day 1 onboarding session
- Tools provisioned (least-privilege)
- Founder + advisor (if Custom AI scope) signoff in `decision_log.md`

## Day 1 (≤ 4 hr)

- Walk through:
  - Dealix mission + 12 Super Systems (high-level)
  - Trust posture (`APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`, `SUPPRESSION_LIST_POLICY.md`)
  - Public Repo Safety + Client Data Handling rules
  - The role they're playing + scorecard
  - Tools + access + escalation paths
- Confirm understanding in writing (their words back to us)
- Assign first task (small, reviewed)

## Days 2–7

- Buddy-style daily check-in (15 min)
- Every task reviewed before action
- Surface every uncertainty (no silent decisions)

## Day 7 — Calibration

- Review first week's work against playbook
- Adjust scope if needed (reduce, not expand)
- Re-confirm trust posture
- Plan next 2 weeks

## Days 8–30 — Probation

- Reviews shift from per-task to per-batch
- Scorecard tracking begins
- Weekly 30-min check-in
- Trust audit lite (any policy violations? approvals correct?)

## Day 30 — Decision

- Continue / coach / end relationship
- Logged in `people/decisions.md` (private)

## Trust Mechanics

- Contractor logged actions appear in `trust/approval_log.csv` under their name
- Contractor cannot approve A3/A4 (founder only)
- Contractor cannot access client data outside their scoped engagement
- Contractor cannot copy data outside the private repo
- Contractor cannot use Dealix data for any other purpose

## Termination Mechanics

- 14-day notice either way (unless trust violation)
- Trust violation = same-day termination + incident log
- Access revoked at termination notice
- NDA + DPA terms continue per legal docs
- Exit interview: lessons learned, both sides

## What Contractor Onboarding Refuses

- "Hit the ground running" (no — onboarding is structured)
- Skipping the NDA
- Skipping the trust walkthrough
- Allowing access creep (contractor scope expands without documented decision)
- Hiring contractors without `HIRING_TRIGGERS.md` thresholds met

## Specific Saudi Notes

- Saudi labor law compliance for any contract or freelance arrangement
- Tax / GOSI implications (if applicable) reviewed with counsel
- Visa / employment status verified for non-Saudis
- Bank account verification for payment

## Documentation Storage

- All contractor docs in `people/contractor_notes/{contractor_name}/` (private)
- NDA + DPA originals in `legal/signed/`
- Decisions in `people/decisions.md`
- Scorecards in `people/role_scorecards/`
