# Compensation Frame

How Dealix pays the humans in the loop. The frame is conservative until revenue is steady.

## Components

- Base salary aligned with Saudi market band for the role.
- Quarterly cash bonus tied to a single, named outcome (per [ROLES.md](./ROLES.md)).
- Equity for full-time hires after 12 months, scaled to seniority.

## Rules

- No commission-only roles in revenue. Commissions encourage over-promising.
- No retention bonuses negotiated in heat-of-the-moment.
- Every comp change is logged.
