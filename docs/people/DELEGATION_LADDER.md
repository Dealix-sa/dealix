# Delegation Ladder

## Purpose
Define how work moves from founder-owned to delegated safely.

## Level 0 — Founder Only
Strategic, trust-sensitive, pricing, closing, and judgment-heavy work.

Examples:
- strategic pivots
- closing important clients
- pricing exceptions
- public proof approval
- trust A2/A3 decisions

## Level 1 — Founder with Template
Founder still does the work, but template exists.

Examples:
- proposal draft
- delivery report
- weekly review
- sample pack

## Level 2 — Assisted
Contractor or assistant prepares, founder reviews.

Examples:
- lead research
- formatting reports
- collecting public evidence
- cleaning CSVs
- preparing draft sample pack

## Level 3 — Delegated with QA
Other person executes, founder reviews output quality.

Examples:
- lead sourcing
- lead table prep
- research notes
- dashboard update
- content draft collection

## Level 4 — Delegated with Metrics
Work is tracked by output quality and cadence.

Examples:
- weekly lead sourcing
- recurring report prep
- content repurposing
- client success admin

## Level 5 — Managed System
Someone owns the workflow and reports results.

Examples later:
- SDR
- delivery analyst
- ops manager
- client success lead

## Rule
No work moves up a level without quality evidence.
