# Role Map

> What roles exist in Dealix. What they own. What they don't.

## Current Org

- **Founder** — all roles below until otherwise stated
- **Agents** — specific functions, governed by `APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`
- **Advisor** — external, called in on high-risk decisions
- **Contractor(s)** — none active

## Roles (planned)

### Founder / CEO (current)

- Final approval on every external action
- Strategic direction + 90-day plans
- All decisions on hiring, pricing, scope, kills
- Sales: discovery, proposal, close
- Delivery: until first hire, all of it
- Trust: every approval gate
- Public face

**Not the founder's job (delegate first):**
- Routine lead sourcing (agent)
- Routine enrichment (agent)
- Draft generation (agent)
- Invoice generation mechanics (template + agent)

### SDR / VA (first likely hire)

**Triggers (per `HIRING_TRIGGERS.md`):** > 100 outreach/week sustained for 3 weeks + delivery on-time falling because of bandwidth

**Owns:**
- Lead sourcing volume (under allow-listed sources)
- Initial enrichment (under quality rules)
- Outreach scheduling (drafts approved by founder, sends by SDR)
- Pipeline tracker hygiene

**Doesn't own:**
- Trust approvals
- Pricing
- Scope decisions
- Customer escalations

### Delivery Analyst (second likely hire)

**Triggers:** > 3 active sprints sustained + QA scoring dropping

**Owns:**
- Sourcing for active sprints
- First-draft deliverables (founder reviews)
- QA checklist execution
- Handoff doc preparation

**Doesn't own:**
- Final QA signoff (founder)
- Client handoff call (founder)
- Trust approvals
- Sprint pricing or scope changes

### Ops Manager (third likely hire, requires 5+ active retainers)

**Triggers:** 5+ active Managed Ops + founder time on internal ops > 30% of week

**Owns:**
- Cross-sprint coordination
- Contractor management
- Process compliance auditing
- Daily Brief operations
- Monthly close-the-books

**Doesn't own:**
- Sales decisions
- Trust approvals
- Strategic decisions
- Hiring decisions

### Engineering Contractor (deferred — not this quarter)

**Triggers:** When custom AI tier needs technical work beyond founder bandwidth, AND first paid Custom AI client signed

## Role Boundaries (hard)

- Trust approvals: founder only, ever (until specific delegation rule logged)
- Pricing: founder only
- Hiring: founder only
- Public claims: founder only
- Client offboarding: founder only

## Role Boundaries (soft, can grow with trust)

- Outreach drafts: founder → SDR with batch approval
- Lead scoring: agent (already)
- Sample artifact updates: founder → Delivery Analyst with founder review

## Conflict Resolution

- Role conflicts: founder decides
- Process conflicts: per documented playbook; if no playbook, founder + log it as a process gap
- Trust conflicts: always escalate to founder + advisor

## Career Path (when applicable)

- SDR → Sales Lead (after 6+ months and 10+ closed sprints)
- Delivery Analyst → Delivery Lead (after 6+ months and 20+ delivered sprints)
- Ops Manager → Head of Ops (after 12+ months and 10+ retainers)

## Anti-Patterns

- Hiring before triggers fire
- Delegating trust approvals "just for vacation"
- Letting agents act in roles humans should hold (A3/A4)
- "Acting" titles without role clarity
- Founder doing role work that's been delegated (signals broken delegation)
