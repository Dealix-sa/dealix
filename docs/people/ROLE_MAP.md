# Role Map

> What each potential role does, and the workflow it leverages.

## Founder (current sole owner of all roles)

Owns everything. The Role Map below describes what to *delegate* as
triggers fire.

## SDR / Outbound Executor

- Workflows: outbound DM cadence (Level 2+), follow-up sequence (Level 2+).
- Inputs: ICP definitions, account research, outreach packs.
- Outputs: DMs sent, replies routed to founder, calls scheduled.
- Boundary: does **not** send proposals; does not negotiate.
- Autonomy: A3 sends with founder's per-send approval mechanism still
  enforced; or A2 once we have a measured pattern.

## Delivery Analyst

- Workflows: account research, scoring, outreach pack drafting,
  executive memo drafting (all at Level 2+).
- Inputs: client scope + ICP.
- Outputs: drafts ready for founder review.
- Boundary: does **not** approve handoff; founder still signs.
- Autonomy: A1 (drafts only).

## Operations Manager

- Workflows: pipeline hygiene, approval queue triage, capital review
  prep, invoice workflow.
- Inputs: revenue + delivery ledgers.
- Outputs: clean ledgers, approval-queue depth managed, weekly close.
- Boundary: founder signs all T2/T3.
- Autonomy: A2 internal sends; no external sends without founder
  approval.

## Client Success Lead

- Workflows: weekly retainer reports, monthly reviews, health-score
  scoring, intervention plans drafting.
- Inputs: retainer activity ledger.
- Outputs: drafted reports + intervention plans for founder approval.
- Boundary: founder still owns Tier A relationships.

## Engineer / Contractor

- Workflows: any active build approved through `FEATURE_INTAKE.md`.
- Inputs: written scope with success metric.
- Outputs: shipped feature meeting DORA targets.
- Boundary: cannot widen scope without intake; no build without intake.

## Content Producer

- Workflows: content cadence beyond founder's personal capacity.
- Inputs: founder voice samples, evidence library.
- Outputs: drafts for founder approval.
- Boundary: founder signs every public post that bears the founder's name.

## Cross-Role Rules

- All roles operate on **workflows**, not on "areas". A role has a
  workflow they own. Workflow + role + scorecard + kill-by date.
- All roles report to founder.
- No role hires another role without founder T3 decision.
