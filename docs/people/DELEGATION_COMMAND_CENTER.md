# Delegation Command Center

## Purpose
Move repeatable work from founder to templates, contractors, or team members.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Monthly.

## Inputs
- repeated manual work
- founder time log
- delivery bottlenecks
- sales volume
- client volume

## Outputs
- delegation candidate
- role scorecard
- onboarding checklist
- hiring trigger

## Rules
- Do not delegate unclear work.
- Document before delegating.
- Template before hiring.
- Hire only when repeated demand exists.

## Hiring Triggers
- 100 DMs/week = SDR or VA.
- 3 reports/week = Delivery Analyst.
- 5 retainers = Ops Manager.
- 10 retainers = Client Success.

## Metrics
- founder hours saved
- delegated tasks
- delivery speed
- QA pass rate after delegation

## Evidence
- founder_time_log.md
- delegation_log.md
- role scorecards
- weekly review
