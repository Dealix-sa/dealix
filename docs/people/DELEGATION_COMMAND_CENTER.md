## Principles

1. **No hire before workflow repeatability (Level ≥ 2).**
2. **No hire that increases the founder's bottleneck count.**
3. **No hire whose value is the founder feeling less busy.**
4. **Every hire has a 90-day kill-by-date** (review, continue, end).
5. **Contractors before employees** for new functions, until cadence
   stabilises.

## Metrics

- Workflows at Level ≥ 2 that are *not yet delegated*: we have leverage
  to capture.
- Workflows at Level ≥ 2 that *are* delegated: leverage realised.
- Time-to-onboarded for new hires.
- 90-day pass rate for new hires.
- Workflows that bounced back to the founder after delegation.
