# Founder Bottleneck System

## Purpose
Identify where Sami is blocking Dealix growth and decide whether to document,
automate, delegate, or kill work.

## Bottleneck Types
- revenue bottleneck
- delivery bottleneck
- trust approval bottleneck
- admin bottleneck
- research bottleneck
- content bottleneck
- reporting bottleneck
- product bottleneck

## Bottleneck Signals
- same task repeated 3+ times
- delayed proposals
- delayed delivery
- weekly review not completed
- pipeline not updated
- founder avoids task
- low-value admin consumes revenue time

## Decision
- Keep
- Document
- Template
- Automate
- Delegate
- Kill
- Defer

## Rules
- Do not delegate strategic decisions.
- Do not delegate unclear work.
- Do not automate broken workflows.
- Do not hire for work that should be killed.
- Do not hire before demand repeats.
