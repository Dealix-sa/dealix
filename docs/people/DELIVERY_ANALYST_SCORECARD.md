# Delivery Analyst Scorecard — بطاقة أداء محلل التسليم

## Purpose
Performance metrics for the delivery analyst role: production of client reports, data preparation, redaction, and supporting weekly reporting.

## Metrics (monthly)

| Metric | Definition | Target (monthly) |
|---|---|---|
| Reports delivered on-time | Count of deliverables shipped by agreed date | ≥95% of scheduled |
| Quality score (avg) | Founder rating of delivered artefacts 1-5 | ≥4.0 |
| Rework rate | % of delivered items requiring substantive revision | ≤10% |
| PII redaction incidents | Confirmed PII leaks or near-miss in delivered artefacts | 0 |
| Banned-claim incidents | Banned-language items caught in QA | 0 |
| Weekly report drafts on-time | % weekly client reports drafted by deadline | ≥95% |
| Source-citation completeness | % of metric claims in artefacts that link to a source row | 100% |

## Behaviors expected
- Drafts weekly reports per [`docs/client_success/WEEKLY_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md`](../client_success/WEEKLY_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md).
- Builds proof packs per `docs/delivery/PROOF_PACK_TEMPLATE.md`.
- Applies redaction per `docs/governance/PII_REDACTION_POLICY.md`.
- Flags any scope creep or change request to founder same-day.
- Respects voice rules per [`docs/content/FOUNDER_VOICE.md`](../content/FOUNDER_VOICE.md) on outbound documents.

## Bands

| Band | Score | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Top performer | Targets met or exceeded; zero incidents; clean rework | Continue; scope can grow |
| Meeting bar | Targets met or within 10% with zero incidents | Continue; coach |
| Below bar | One target >10% off OR rework >15% | 30-day plan |
| Off-process | Any PII or banned-claim incident; or below-bar two months running | Review per `REVIEW_CADENCE.md` |

## Rules
- A single PII or banned-claim incident triggers an incident review per `docs/governance/INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md` and a written remediation note.
- Rework is logged with the reason; patterns inform training.
- Quality score is based on a written rubric (timeliness, accuracy, voice fit, completeness).

## Operations
- Monthly: founder writes quality scores; analyst submits the count metrics; review meeting within 5 days.
- Quarterly: contributes to compensation per `COMPENSATION.md`.

## Evidence
- Each metric value sources from the delivery log, report archive, or incident log.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder (manager); analyst submits.
- Cadence: monthly review; quarterly summary.

## القسم العربي

**المقاييس الشهرية:** تقارير في الموعد ≥95%، متوسط جودة ≥4.0/5، نسبة إعادة العمل ≤10%، حوادث PII = 0، حوادث ادعاءات ممنوعة = 0، مسوّدات تقرير أسبوعي في الموعد ≥95%، اكتمال استشهاد المصادر 100%.

**السلوكيات:** صياغة تقارير أسبوعية، بناء حزم إثبات، تنقية PII، تصعيد تسرّب النطاق فورًا، احترام قواعد الصوت.

**الفئات:** ممتاز، يفي بالمعيار، تحت المعيار، خارج العملية.

**القواعد:** حادثة PII أو ادعاء ممنوع تستدعي مراجعة حادثة وملاحظة معالجة. إعادة العمل تُسجّل بأسبابها. الجودة تُقيَّم بـrubric مكتوب.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
