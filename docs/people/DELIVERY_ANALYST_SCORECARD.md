# Delivery Analyst Scorecard

How a delivery analyst is measured at Dealix.

## Weekly targets
- Sprint reports drafted: 1–2
- QA pass rate (first pass): 90%+
- Source completeness in lead tables: 100%
- Time per sprint: within the budget in `DELIVERY_PLAYBOOK.md`.

## Quality gates
- 0 over-claims caught at QA.
- 100% of leads have source URLs.
- 100% of reports have the executive summary in five bullets.

## Behavior gates
- Weekly status note submitted each Friday.
- Issues escalated within 24 hours.

## Review cadence
- Weekly 1-on-1 (20 minutes).
- Monthly scorecard review.
- Quarterly capability review (move toward owning a sprint end-to-end).

## Rule
The analyst is the last line before the client sees the work. Quality compounds; speed without quality does not.
