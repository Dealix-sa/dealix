# Delivery Analyst Scorecard

## Purpose
Define the expected output and quality standard for a delivery analyst.

## Responsibilities
- research qualified opportunities
- prepare lead tables
- collect evidence
- draft suggested angles
- support report preparation

## Not Allowed
- send final client report
- make unsupported claims
- publish client data
- change scope
- approve proof

## Weekly Metrics
- lead table quality
- evidence quality
- QA pass rate
- rework count
- delivery time saved

## Quality Bar
- A leads have evidence
- no duplicates
- ICP fit clear
- suggested angle is specific
- report is trust-safe
