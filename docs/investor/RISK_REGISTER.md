# Investor Risk Register

The investor-facing view of `docs/founder/RISK_REGISTER.md` — same risks, plainer language.

## Top risks
- **Founder concentration.** Today the founder is the single point of failure for closing and key approvals. Mitigation: documented systems, named hiring triggers.
- **Client concentration.** Risk if any single client exceeds 30% of monthly cash. Mitigation: target mix in the revenue model, pipeline depth.
- **Over-claim incident.** A single high-visibility over-claim damages trust disproportionately. Mitigation: Claim Guard, audits, founder approval on public claims.
- **Data exposure.** Client data leak through repo or export. Mitigation: data handling policy, retention policy, public repo safety policy.
- **Sector misallocation.** Pursuing the wrong sector burns months. Mitigation: sector performance review, monthly strategy review.
- **Macro shift.** Saudi B2B spend pattern changes. Mitigation: diverse sector mix, recurring base.

## Cadence
- Reviewed monthly.
- Each risk has an owner and a review date.

## Rule
Risk transparency builds investor trust. Hidden risks become deal-breakers.
