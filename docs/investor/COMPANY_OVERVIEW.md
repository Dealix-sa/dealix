# Company Overview

A short, plain-language description of Dealix for any third party.

## What Dealix is
A Saudi-native Revenue Company Operating System. Dealix helps Saudi B2B businesses turn attention into proven revenue using a combination of AI-assisted preparation, human-led execution, and trust-first governance.

## Who Dealix is for
Saudi-active B2B businesses with an identifiable revenue motion: agencies, consultancies, SaaS, professional services, vendors selling into mid-market and enterprise.

## What Dealix sells
A five-rung offer ladder: Signal Sample → Revenue Sprint → Managed Pilot → Revenue Desk → Dealix OS. See `docs/revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md`.

## How Dealix delivers
A productized delivery system: research, scoring, outreach pack, report, QA, handoff, feedback, case study. See `docs/delivery/revenue_sprint/DELIVERY_PLAYBOOK.md`.

## How Dealix governs
Approval matrix, Claim Guard, suppression list, data retention policy, audit policy. See `docs/trust/`.

## Stage
Founder-led. Operating with discipline, building toward sustainable recurring revenue.
