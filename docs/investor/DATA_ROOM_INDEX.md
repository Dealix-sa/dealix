# Data Room Index

The single index to every artifact a strategic partner, investor, enterprise buyer, accelerator, or government counterpart might request.

## Sections
1. Company overview — `COMPANY_OVERVIEW.md`
2. Metrics — `METRICS.md`
3. Roadmap — `ROADMAP.md`
4. Financial model — `FINANCIAL_MODEL.md`
5. Market thesis — `MARKET_THESIS.md`
6. Risk register — `RISK_REGISTER.md`
7. Pitch deck outline — `PITCH_DECK_OUTLINE.md`
8. Governance — links to `docs/trust/` policies.
9. People — links to `docs/people/ROLE_MAP.md` and `HIRING_TRIGGERS.md`.
10. Proof — links to `docs/content/PROOF_LIBRARY.md`.

## Access controls
- The data room is shared on demand, never by default.
- An NDA is signed before sensitive sections are shared.
- Sensitive items (financials, named clients) live in `dealix-ops-private/investor/` and are added to the share on a case-by-case basis.

## Maintenance
- Reviewed at the Monthly Strategy Review.
- Updated within 7 days of any material change.

## Rule
Be ready for the opportunity before it asks. A prepared data room is a strategic asset.
