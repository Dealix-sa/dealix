# Investor Metrics

The numbers Dealix is willing to share with strategic partners and investors under NDA.

## Headline metrics
- Cash collected (rolling 12 months).
- MRR (current, 12-month trend).
- Number of active retainers.
- Average deal size.
- Win rate on proposals sent.
- Retention rate on retainers (annual).
- Gross margin estimate.

## Operating metrics
- Outbound volume.
- Reply rate.
- Call-to-book rate.
- Conversion rates per pipeline stage.
- Time per Revenue Sprint.

## Trust and quality
- QA pass rate on delivered artifacts.
- Number of incidents (last 12 months).
- Refund rate.

## Source files (private)
- `dealix-ops-private/revenue/`
- `dealix-ops-private/product/metrics/`
- `dealix-ops-private/trust/`

## Definitions
- Use the canonical definitions in `docs/revenue/REVENUE_METRICS.md` and `docs/finance/MRR_DEFINITION.md`.

## Rule
One definition per metric, shared honestly. Numbers are not negotiated; methods are.
