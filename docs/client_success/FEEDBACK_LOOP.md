# Feedback Loop

The structured way Dealix captures what clients actually think.

## Triggers
- 24 hours after every sprint handoff.
- End of every retainer month.
- End of every quarter for long-running clients.

## Form (short, 7 questions max)
1. On a scale of 1–10, how useful was the work?
2. Which deliverable was most useful?
3. Which deliverable was least useful?
4. What did we miss?
5. What would you like more of?
6. Would you recommend Dealix to a peer? Yes / No / Maybe.
7. Can we quote you, anonymously or by name?

## Where it goes
- Raw responses: `dealix-ops-private/client_success/feedback/`.
- Summarized monthly into `docs/learning/WIN_LOSS_REVIEW.md`.
- Quotes (with permission) into `docs/content/PROOF_LIBRARY.md`.

## Rule
A client who is never asked for feedback churns silently. Always ask.
