# Renewal Playbook

The structured approach to every retainer renewal.

## 45 days before renewal
- Client Health Score review.
- Identify wins, gaps, and the one change the client mentioned in feedback.
- Founder briefed.

## 30 days before renewal
- Schedule the renewal review meeting.
- Send a one-page "year (or quarter) in review" summary.

## 14 days before renewal
- Present the renewal offer.
- If terms are changing, walk through the why and the value.
- Capture objections in writing.

## 7 days before renewal
- Address objections in writing.
- Send the renewed contract for signature.

## At renewal
- Signature, invoice, kickoff of the renewed period.
- Internal retrospective: what worked, what we will change.

## After renewal
- Update the engagement plan for the new period.
- Update the Client Health Score baseline.

## Rule
A renewal that is "surprise" to the client is a renewal we will lose. There are no surprises in a 45-day cadence.
