# Renewal Playbook — كتيب التجديد

## Purpose
How Dealix runs the renewal conversation: when to start it, how pricing changes on renewal, and how to ask for case-study consent at the same time without coupling the decisions.

## Timing
- Retainer: renewal conversation starts 30 days before contract end.
- Annual contract: 60 days before end.
- Pilot conversion: in the last week of the pilot.
- Risk-band clients: pull renewal conversation forward to the moment the risk window stabilizes per [`RETENTION_PLAYBOOK.md`](RETENTION_PLAYBOOK.md).

## Pre-conversation prep (founder)
- Review health score history (last 12 weeks).
- Review delivered proof packs and outcomes against the original proposal's stated objectives.
- Review feedback log highlights.
- Decide renewal pricing per the pricing rules below.

## Pricing on renewal

| Situation | Pricing move |
|---|---|
| Standard renewal, health ≥80 12-week average | Same price; or +0-5% inflation adjustment, written |
| Strategic tier, demonstrable value delivered | Same price; expansion if conditions per [`UPSELL_PLAYBOOK.md`](UPSELL_PLAYBOOK.md) are met |
| Risk history but recovered | Same price; do not raise. Do not concede either. |
| Scope creep absorbed during the term | Renewal restates scope at correct price; documented |
| Multi-quarter commitment offered by client | Up to 10% discount in exchange for the commitment, written |

- No verbal pricing commitments.
- No renewal at below the floor in `docs/sales/PROPOSAL_SYSTEM.md` without written A2 founder approval.

## Conversation structure (call)
1. What worked. Cite specific delivered outcomes (no inflated language).
2. What did not. Cite the feedback log honestly.
3. What we propose next term: scope, price, timeline.
4. Decision deadline (7-10 days).
5. (Separately) Consent for case study, if applicable. The two decisions are independent.

## Case-study consent (separate ask)
- Asked only of clients in Strategic or Retainer-ready tier.
- Consent must be written A2 per [`docs/content/CASE_STUDY_SYSTEM.md`](../content/CASE_STUDY_SYSTEM.md).
- Anonymized version offered as default; named only with explicit consent.
- Renewal is never conditioned on consent. Consent is never conditioned on renewal.

## Rules
- A renewal that fails closes cleanly: knowledge transfer, archive of artefacts, exit interview per [`FEEDBACK_LOOP.md`](FEEDBACK_LOOP.md).
- No "auto-renewal" without written prior agreement.
- All renewal terms documented in a fresh signed agreement.

## Operations
- Renewal pipeline maintained in the client portfolio review.
- Each renewal logged with outcome (renewed, expanded, churned, deferred).

## Evidence
- Renewal register row links: prior contract, new contract or churn reason, case-study consent record (separate).

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: per renewal window; monthly pipeline review.

## القسم العربي

**التوقيت:** Retainer قبل نهاية العقد بـ30 يومًا، عقد سنوي بـ60 يومًا، تحويل Pilot في آخر أسبوع، عميل بفئة خطر — بعد استقرار نافذة الخطر مباشرة.

**التحضير:** المؤسس يراجع تاريخ النقاط، حزم الإثبات مقابل أهداف الاقتراح، أبرز التغذية الراجعة، ويحدد التسعير.

**التسعير على التجديد:** صحة ≥80 لـ12 أسبوعًا: نفس السعر أو زيادة 0-5% تضخم مكتوبة. استراتيجي بقيمة مُثبتة: نفس السعر مع توسعة إن استوفيت شروط `UPSELL_PLAYBOOK.md`. تاريخ خطر متعافٍ: نفس السعر بدون رفع أو تنازل. تسرّب نطاق: إعادة بيان النطاق بسعر صحيح. التزام متعدد الأرباع: حتى 10% خصم مكتوب.

**هيكل المحادثة:** ما نجح بأدلة، ما لم ينجح بصدق، ما نقترحه، مهلة قرار. (منفصلًا) طلب موافقة case study.

**موافقة case study:** فقط للعملاء الاستراتيجيين/Retainer-ready، A2 مكتوبة، الاسم بإذن صريح، التجديد لا يُربط بالموافقة.

**القواعد:** التجديد الفاشل يُغلق بنظافة، لا تجديد تلقائي بدون اتفاق مسبق، عقد جديد لكل تجديد.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
