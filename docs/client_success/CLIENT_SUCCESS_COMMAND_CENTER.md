## Rules

1. Every Sprint ends with a retainer ask, written, with a price.
2. Every retainer has a named monthly review meeting.
3. Every retainer has a health score updated monthly.
4. Lost retainers are debriefed within 14 days.

## Metrics

- Retainer count (active)
- Net new retainers (month)
- Sprint → Retainer conversion rate
- Retainer renewal rate
- Average retainer tenure
- Expansion MRR / Contraction MRR
- NPS or feedback score (when sample ≥ 10)
