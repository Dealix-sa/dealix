# Retainer Packaging System

## Purpose
Convert Revenue Sprint value into recurring Revenue Desk support.

## Revenue Desk Offer
Monthly support for:
- weekly qualified opportunities
- message improvement
- pipeline review
- sector learning
- monthly opportunity report

## Retainer Tiers

### Starter
5,000 SAR/month
- weekly mini opportunity list
- message refinement
- monthly review

### Growth
10,000 SAR/month
- larger opportunity flow
- pipeline review
- follow-up support
- monthly learning report

### Strategic
20,000 SAR/month
- sector intelligence
- opportunity engine
- executive review
- ongoing optimization

## Retainer Rules
- Offer only after clear value.
- No guaranteed revenue.
- No unlimited scope.
- Monthly deliverables must be clear.
- Cancel/renewal terms must be clear.

## Metrics
- retainer asks
- retainer accepted
- MRR
- retention
- expansion

## Anti-Patterns
- Stacking tiers as a "trial" without a defined scope.
- Promising channel-specific results (calls booked, deals closed).
- Bundling features Dealix has not delivered consistently before.

## Trust Statement
Retainers sell consistency of intelligence and operating cadence,
not guaranteed business outcomes.
