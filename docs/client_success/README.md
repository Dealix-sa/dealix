# Client Success

## Purpose
Keep paid clients alive, healthy, and converting from one-off deliveries to retainers.

## Owner
Sami / Delivery owner (until a client success owner is named).

## Review Cadence
Weekly per active client.

## Inputs
- Delivery reports.
- QA results.
- Client feedback (qualitative and quantitative).
- Usage signals (replies, follow-ups, action taken on deliveries).
- Renewal and retainer dates.

## Outputs
- Client health score (Green / Yellow / Red).
- Next action per client.
- Retainer candidacy decision.
- Risk flag to the founder when a client is Yellow or Red.

## Rules
- Every active client has an owner, health score, and next action.
- A Red client is escalated to the founder within 24 hours.
- No retainer is sold without delivery proof and explicit client agreement.
- Sensitive client data (deliveries, internal notes) stays in the private clients repo.

## Metrics
- Clients in Green / Yellow / Red.
- Time from delivery to next purchase.
- Sprint-to-retainer conversion.
- NPS or qualitative satisfaction by client.
- Churn count and reason.

## Evidence
- private `clients/<client_name>/` folder.
- delivery reports.
- QA checklists.
- retainer renewal log.
