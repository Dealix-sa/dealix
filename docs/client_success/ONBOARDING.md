# Client Onboarding

> The first 7 days set the tone for the whole relationship.

## Day 0 — Scope signed, payment received

- Send confirmation email with:
  - Scope summary
  - Delivery window
  - Founder direct contact
  - Onboarding call slot

## Day 1 — Onboarding Call (30 min)

Agenda:

- Confirm ICP definition in writing.
- Confirm trigger signals to prioritise.
- Confirm tone (formal / direct / consultative) for Arabic + English.
- Confirm CRM / channel of delivery.
- Confirm reporting cadence.
- Confirm decision-maker for sign-offs.

Outputs:

- `dealix-ops-private/clients/<client_id>/scope_confirmed.md`
- `dealix-ops-private/clients/<client_id>/icp_definition.md`

## Day 2-3 — Initial research

- Founder runs ICP discovery on customer's segment.
- Customer-specific hook library built.
- Sample of 3 accounts shared informally for feedback.

## Day 4-5 — Customer feedback integrated

- Customer reviews the sample.
- Adjustments noted.
- Final research run starts.

## Day 6-10 — Sprint delivery

- Per `docs/delivery/revenue_sprint/REVENUE_SPRINT_FACTORY.md` (or
  existing delivery flow).

## Day 11-12 — QA + executive memo + handoff prep

- QA against checklist.
- Executive memo drafted, founder edits.
- Handoff materials prepped.

## Day 13 — Handoff call (30-45 min)

- Walk the customer through the Proof Pack.
- Demonstrate one specific opportunity end-to-end.
- Capture customer reactions live (recorded with consent or written
  immediately after).

## Day 14 — Retainer ask + feedback request

- Send retainer proposal (see `UPSELL_PLAYBOOK.md`).
- Send structured feedback request.
- Add customer to `dealix-ops-private/client_success/health_scores.csv`.

## Onboarding QA

After day 14, founder checks:

- [ ] Were scope, ICP, and tone confirmed in writing?
- [ ] Was the day-3 sample shared informally?
- [ ] Did the handoff demonstrate one specific opportunity?
- [ ] Was the retainer ask delivered with a price?
- [ ] Was feedback captured?
- [ ] Was the customer added to the health-score tracker?

Any "no" goes into the Friction Log with a process change.
