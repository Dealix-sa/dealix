# Client Onboarding

The first 14 days set the tone for the relationship.

## Day 0 (Payment received)
- Welcome message within 4 hours.
- Send the intake form and the kickoff calendar link.
- Set the client's record to "Active" in the operations folder.

## Day 1–2
- Kickoff call.
- Confirm scope, out-of-scope, and timeline.
- Confirm single point of contact, both sides.
- Confirm the communication channel.

## Day 3–7
- First weekly check-in (15 minutes).
- Share a short status note: what was done, what is next, any blockers.

## Day 8–14
- First substantive deliverable, even if interim.
- Capture early feedback.

## Standing rules
- Always one Dealix point of contact per client.
- Always one client point of contact, named in writing.
- Response times: within 4 hours on weekdays, 24 hours on weekends.

## Rule
A client should feel, after two weeks, that Dealix is more organized than their internal team.
