# Upsell Playbook — كتيب التوسعة

## Purpose
When to ask for expansion, what to offer, and how to ask. Upsell at the wrong time or with the wrong shape destroys trust faster than no ask at all.

## When to ask (necessary conditions)
- Client health score ≥90 for two consecutive weeks.
- At least one proof-pack delivered and accepted in writing.
- Client has used the latest deliverable (evidence in feedback log).
- No open Risk-band or Sev1/Sev2 issue in the last 30 days.
- Current engagement is past the midpoint (sprint) or past the third month (retainer).

If any condition is missing, do not ask. Build the missing condition first.

## What to offer (in order of preference)
1. **Adjacent scope** — same sector, same problem, deeper. Example: add a second sector report; extend a sprint with a new module.
2. **Retainer conversion** — sprint client moves to a monthly retainer with explicit scope per [`docs/sales/PROPOSAL_SYSTEM.md`](../sales/PROPOSAL_SYSTEM.md).
3. **Strategic engagement** — multi-quarter scope with milestone billing.
4. **Sector / report subscription** — recurring sector intelligence.

Do not offer: discounted bundles to justify a price floor; speculative work; anything banned by [`docs/product/NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md`](../product/NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md).

## How to ask
- The ask happens in person or on a call, not in chat.
- Founder frames the conversation: "Here is what worked. Here is the next problem I see. Here is what we'd do. Here is the price."
- Written follow-up within 24 hours: scope, price, timeline, what we need from the client.
- Decision deadline given: 7-14 days typical.
- No second-ask within the same week.

## Pricing on upsell
- Same per-unit pricing as new clients unless a documented loyalty term applies.
- No "internal price" or "off-the-record" discounts.
- Any concession is written, time-bounded, and tied to a specific reason (multi-quarter commitment, multi-scope bundle).

## Rules
- Upsell does not interrupt active delivery. If delivery is mid-cycle, the ask waits for a natural transition point.
- Refused upsell is logged with the reason; not re-asked until conditions change.
- Estimated value language follows the trust rules: no guaranteed sales, no fixed ROI claim, "estimated value is not Verified value".

## Operations
- Upsell-ready clients flagged in the monthly tier review.
- Each upsell conversation logged in the client folder with date, ask, response, follow-up.

## Evidence
- Upsell register row: client label, condition checks, offer made, decision, signed deal id (if any).

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: monthly trigger scan, ad-hoc execution.

## القسم العربي

**متى نطلب التوسعة (شروط لازمة):** نقاط صحة ≥90 لأسبوعين متتاليين، حزمة إثبات واحدة على الأقل مُسلّمة ومقبولة كتابيًا، العميل استخدم آخر مُخرج، لا قضية خطر أو Sev1/Sev2 خلال 30 يومًا، التزام تجاوز منتصف السبرنت أو الشهر الثالث من Retainer.

**ما نقدمه (بترتيب الأفضلية):** نطاق مجاور، تحويل لـ Retainer، عقد استراتيجي متعدد الأرباع، اشتراك تقارير قطاعية. ممنوع: حزم مخفضة لتبرير سقف سعري، عمل تخميني، أي شيء في `NO_OVERBUILD_POLICY.md`.

**كيف نطلب:** مكالمة أو لقاء، إطار: "ما نجح، المشكلة التالية، ما سنفعله، السعر". متابعة كتابية خلال 24 ساعة. مهلة قرار 7-14 يومًا. لا طلب ثانٍ نفس الأسبوع.

**التسعير:** نفس سعر العملاء الجدد ما لم يوجد شرط ولاء موثق. لا "سعر داخلي". أي تنازل مكتوب ومحدد زمنيًا وسببيًا.

**القواعد:** لا توسعة تقاطع التسليم النشط. الرفض يُسجّل ولا يُعاد طلبه قبل تغير الشروط. لغة القيمة تحترم: "القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة".
