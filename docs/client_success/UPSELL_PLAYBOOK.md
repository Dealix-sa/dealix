# Upsell Playbook

How Dealix grows account value without becoming pushy.

## When to upsell
- Client Health Score 90+ for two consecutive weeks.
- Client has consumed the current deliverable and asked for more.
- A new use case emerged in a working session.

## What to upsell to
- Sprint → Managed Pilot.
- Managed Pilot → Revenue Desk.
- Revenue Desk → Dealix OS (only for enterprise-fit clients).
- Add-on: sector-specific report, partner introduction, training session.

## How to upsell
- Present the upsell as a recommendation tied to a specific observation about the client.
- Keep the deck to two pages.
- Offer one option, not a menu.
- Anchor the value, then the price.

## What not to do
- No bundles the client did not ask about.
- No quarterly "upgrade conversations" that feel scripted.
- No discounts on the first upsell offer.

## Rule
The best upsell is the one the client suggests. Set the conditions for that, do not force it.
