# Retention Playbook

Retention is earned every month, not at renewal time.

## Monthly retention motions
1. Weekly status report on the same day each week.
2. Monthly business review with the client's primary contact.
3. One proactive recommendation per month (not asked for, valuable).
4. One thank-you note per quarter for a specific contribution.

## Watch signals
- Two consecutive weekly reports without a reply.
- Missed monthly business reviews.
- Decision latency growing.
- New vendor mentioned in passing.

## Recovery plays
- Founder check-in call within 7 days of a watch signal.
- Free additional deliverable in the next month, scoped tight.
- Reset of the engagement plan with explicit success criteria.

## Sunset (when it is right)
- Recommend the client take the work in-house with a clean handoff.
- Provide a final summary and the lead table.
- Leave the door open for re-engagement.

## Rule
A retained client is worth more than three new ones. Time spent retaining is rarely wasted.
