# Retention Playbook — كتيب الاحتفاظ

## Purpose
What to do when a client is in the Risk band (0-59) or the Nurture band (60-89). Action recipes, not aspirations.

## Risk-tier playbook (health 0-59)

### Trigger
- Weekly recompute lands score ≤59, or
- ≥20-point drop week-over-week (regardless of band), or
- Negative-tag feedback unresolved >7 days.

### Day 0-2
- Founder owns this client until the situation clears.
- Founder reads the last four weekly reports, the feedback log, and the contract.
- Internal note: hypothesis on root cause, named owner of each fix.

### Day 3-5
- Founder holds a direct conversation with the client. Agenda:
  1. Acknowledge what is not working.
  2. State what Dealix will change, with dates.
  3. Ask: what would make this engagement worth continuing.
- No discount offered as a first move. Discount is a last resort after scope/process fixes have been tried.

### Day 6-14
- Daily working delivery. Weekly report doubled to twice-weekly during this window.
- All commitments tracked with dates; missed dates escalate to founder same day.

### Exit criteria
- Score returns to ≥60 for two consecutive weeks → exit risk playbook.
- Score does not return → renewal conversation per [`RENEWAL_PLAYBOOK.md`](RENEWAL_PLAYBOOK.md) brought forward; mutual exit discussed; refund considered only per [`docs/finance/REFUND_POLICY.md`](../finance/REFUND_POLICY.md).

## Nurture-tier playbook (health 60-89)

### Goal
Move toward Retainer-ready by deepening one usage habit per quarter.

### Cadence
- Maintain weekly report and standing call.
- Each quarter: founder picks ONE habit to deepen — a metric the client will look at every week. Examples:
  - "Client meets every Sunday to review the scored-leads list."
  - "Client uses the sector report in next pitch."
- Document the habit in the client folder; reference it in weekly reports.

### Signals to watch
- Client referenced the habit unprompted → +20 evidence signal.
- Habit ignored for 4 consecutive weeks → escalate to founder review.

## Rules
- No retention by discount alone. Discounts without behaviour change repeat the problem.
- No silent escalation. Risk-tier clients know they are getting founder attention, framed as care, not alarm.
- All commitments made during retention work are logged and met or escalated.

## Evidence
- Each risk-tier engagement gets a one-page case file in the client folder, even after exit.

## Owner & cadence
- Owner: Founder.
- Cadence: trigger-based; daily during a risk window.

## القسم العربي

**كتيب الخطر (صحة 0-59):**

**المحفّز:** نقاط ≤59، أو هبوط ≥20 أسبوعيًا، أو تغذية سلبية معلقة >7 أيام.

**يوم 0-2:** المؤسس يتولى العميل، يقرأ آخر 4 تقارير وسجل التغذية والعقد، يكتب فرضية السبب الجذري ومالك كل إصلاح.

**يوم 3-5:** محادثة مباشرة مع العميل بأجندة: إقرار، التزام بتغيير وتواريخ، سؤال عن شرط الاستمرار. لا خصم في الخطوة الأولى.

**يوم 6-14:** تسليم يومي، التقرير الأسبوعي يصبح مرتين أسبوعيًا.

**معايير الخروج:** عودة النقاط ≥60 لأسبوعين متتاليين، أو تقديم محادثة التجديد.

**كتيب التغذية (60-89):** هدف الانتقال لـ Retainer. كل ربع المؤسس يختار عادة استخدام واحدة يعمّقها العميل. إذا تجاهل العميل العادة 4 أسابيع تتم مراجعة.

**القواعد:** لا احتفاظ بالخصم وحده. لا تصعيد صامت. كل التزام يُسجّل ويُلتزم به.

**المالك:** المؤسس.
