# Strategic Opportunity Map

## Objective
Identify the highest-leverage opportunities for Dealix across product, revenue, AI, data, trust, operations, and market position.

## Opportunity categories

| Category | Opportunity | Why it matters | First artifact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product platform | Turn workflows into a durable operating layer | Increases retention and switching cost | Platform roadmap |
| AI quality | Add evaluation and human review to every AI workflow | Builds enterprise trust | AI eval checklist |
| Data advantage | Improve account, opportunity, proof, and customer data | Creates defensibility | Data quality map |
| GTM expansion | Narrow wedge first, then expand by proof | Reduces wasted sales effort | ICP expansion ladder |
| Enterprise readiness | Prepare for procurement, security, and legal review | Unlocks serious B2B buyers | Enterprise packet |
| Observability | Trace API, AI, jobs, and customer-impacting flows | Faster debugging and reliability | Observability plan |
| Customer proof | Convert results into reusable proof assets | Improves sales conversion | Proof ledger |
| Partnerships | Use trusted channels to increase distribution | Lowers CAC and raises credibility | Partner map |
| Security posture | Strengthen trust before scale | Prevents avoidable blockers | Security scorecard |
| Founder cadence | Keep decisions focused and evidence-based | Prevents strategic drift | Weekly CEO memo |

## Opportunity thesis

Dealix should prioritize opportunities that do at least two of the following:

- Increase revenue quality.
- Improve customer trust.
- Create reusable data.
- Increase product usage frequency.
- Reduce founder bottlenecks.
- Improve enterprise readiness.
- Lower operational risk.
- Create measurable proof.

## 90-day strategic focus

| Focus | Outcome | Metric | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wedge ICP | One segment converts repeatedly | Qualified meetings and win rate | Founder/GTM |
| Proof engine | Every win creates proof | Proof assets created | CS/Marketing |
| AI governance | AI outputs are reviewed and evaluated | Eval pass rate | AI/Product |
| Product activation | Customers reach first value quickly | Time to value | Product/CS |
| Enterprise readiness | Dealix can pass basic buyer review | Packet completion | Ops/Legal |

## Avoid

- Adding tools with no owner.
- Installing libraries directly into runtime without test and review.
- Building features not connected to activation, revenue, trust, or retention.
- Expanding ICP before proof exists.
- Making AI claims that cannot be explained.

## Decision prompt

Before starting an opportunity, write:

1. What strategic pillar does it improve?
2. What metric should move?
3. What proof will show success?
4. What risk does it create?
5. Who owns it?
6. What is the smallest test?
