# Product Bet Portfolio

## Purpose
Manage product bets as a portfolio instead of a random roadmap.

## Bet types

| Bet type | Goal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Activation bet | Get customers to first value faster | Guided onboarding |
| Workflow bet | Increase weekly usage | Account research workbench |
| Trust bet | Increase buyer confidence | Proof pack and audit trail |
| Data bet | Improve proprietary signals | Opportunity and objection ledger |
| AI quality bet | Improve AI usefulness and safety | Evaluation test sets |
| Enterprise bet | Unlock larger buyers | Procurement packet and security overview |
| Integration bet | Reduce switching friction | CRM integration depth |

## Portfolio rules

- Keep 70% of work on core wedge and activation.
- Keep 20% on trust, data, and enterprise readiness.
- Keep 10% on exploratory bets.
- Do not start a new bet without stopping, finishing, or rejecting another.
