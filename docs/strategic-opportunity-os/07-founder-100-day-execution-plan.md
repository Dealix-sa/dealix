# Founder 100-Day Execution Plan

## Purpose
Turn the strategic opportunity system into execution over 100 days.

## Days 1-10: Lock the operating base

- Confirm ICP wedge.
- Confirm top 5 company KPIs.
- Review Company OS and Founder OS files.
- Assign owners for GTM, product, CS, AI, data, security, and ops.
- Fill the opportunity scorecard for top 10 ideas.

## Days 11-30: Build proof and pipeline

- Run founder-led outreach to high-fit accounts.
- Capture objections and lost reasons.
- Build the first proof ledger.
- Use deal review issues for every serious opportunity.
- Review customer or pilot onboarding flow.

## Days 31-50: Strengthen product and trust

- Prioritize product bets by activation, trust, and reuse.
- Register all AI workflows.
- Create first AI evaluation test set.
- Review data quality for account, opportunity, and customer data.
- Add security review for any high-impact workflows.

## Days 51-75: Prepare for scale

- Review channel tests.
- Build partner map.
- Complete enterprise readiness packet.
- Add observability plan for critical workflows.
- Review optional tooling adoption candidates.

## Days 76-100: Decide and compound

- Decide which channel to scale.
- Decide which product bet to double down on.
- Decide which tools to adopt or reject.
- Refresh financial and runway assumptions.
- Write a 100-day founder memo.

## 100-day outputs

- Updated one-page strategy.
- Opportunity portfolio.
- Product bet portfolio.
- GTM channel scorecard.
- AI and data governance status.
- Enterprise readiness packet.
- Founder memo.
- Next 100-day plan.

## Weekly founder commitments

- Talk to customers or market every week.
- Review pipeline every week.
- Review product activation every week.
- Review AI quality every release.
- Review risks every week.
- Stop one low-value activity every week.
