# CEO Targets — أهداف الرئيس التنفيذي

## Purpose

Define how Dealix sets targets at three cadences — weekly, monthly, quarterly — and the operating defaults that anchor the early commercial phase. Targets are inputs to behavior, not promises to outsiders.

## Weekly operating defaults

| Target | Value | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| New leads added | 25 | enough volume to stress the funnel without bulk outreach |
| DMs queued (warm / consented only) | 25 | every DM follows [docs/02_saudi_positioning/WHATSAPP_BOUNDARY.md](../02_saudi_positioning/WHATSAPP_BOUNDARY.md) |
| Sample summaries sent | 3 | each is case-safe per [docs/07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md](../07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md) |
| Proposals out | 1 | one quality proposal beats five forced ones |

These are operating defaults, not minimums for revenue claims. They are the rhythm that produces evidence over time.

## Monthly targets

- Cohort-based, set at the [docs/learning/MONTHLY_STRATEGY_UPDATE.md](../learning/MONTHLY_STRATEGY_UPDATE.md).
- Cover: sprints booked, sprints delivered, proof packs shipped, retainers converted.
- Each monthly target traces to a quarterly objective.

## Quarterly targets

- Three objectives maximum, each with one leading metric and one lagging metric.
- Each objective names a sector posture from [docs/learning/SECTOR_PERFORMANCE.md](../learning/SECTOR_PERFORMANCE.md).
- Each objective lists what it consciously will not pursue (anti-goals).

## How targets are set

1. Quarterly: founder drafts three objectives in the last week of the prior quarter.
2. Monthly: at the monthly update, the quarter objectives are translated into the next 30 days.
3. Weekly: the operating defaults stand; cohort-specific adjustments are noted in the weekly review.
4. Any change to a target is logged with reason in [docs/learning/COMPANY_MEMORY.md](../learning/COMPANY_MEMORY.md).

## Non-negotiables

- No target is set as a public promise. Targets are internal.
- No target is set on a vanity metric (followers, impressions, gross outreach).
- No target compensates for a missing proof base. If the proof base is thin, the answer is a sector report or a case-safe summary, not a higher DM target.

## Evidence

- Weekly actual vs target captured in `company_metrics.json`.
- Quarterly objective file: `dealix-ops-private/dashboard/quarterly_objectives/YYYY-Q.md`.

## Owner & cadence

- Founder owns the target stack at all three cadences.
- Reviewed weekly (actuals), monthly (cohort), quarterly (objectives).

## AR — ملخّص

أهداف ديليكس تُضبط على ثلاثة إيقاعات: أسبوعية (25 lead / 25 DM دافئ / 3 عيّنات / عرض واحد)، شهرية (cohort)، ربعية (ثلاثة أهداف بأضدادها). الأهداف داخلية، لا وعود خارجية، ولا تُضبط على مقاييس مظهرية. القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة.
