# CEO Targets

## Purpose
Define weekly and monthly operating targets for Dealix.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Inputs
- Current growth stage.
- Revenue goals.
- Founder capacity.
- Delivery capacity.

## Outputs
- Dashboard targets.
- Weekly review targets.
- Sales and delivery goals.

## Rules
- Targets must be realistic for current stage.
- Targets must focus on revenue, proof, and learning.
- No vanity targets without operating value.

## Weekly Targets
- 25 leads
- 25 DMs
- 5 replies
- 2 calls
- 1 proposal
- 3 samples
- 1 learning decision

## Monthly Targets
- 1 paid sprint minimum
- 1 case study
- 1 retainer attempt
- 1 pricing learning
- 1 playbook update

## Metrics
- Target completion rate.
- Missed targets.
- Conversion by stage.
- Learning decisions.

## Evidence
- pipeline tracker
- weekly intelligence review
- payment record
- delivery report
