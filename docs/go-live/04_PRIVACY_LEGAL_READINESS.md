# Privacy & Legal Readiness — جاهزية الخصوصية والقانون

The published legal foundation that must exist before go-live: privacy policy, terms of service, and a data processing agreement, all reviewed and PDPL-aware. This document is operational readiness, not legal advice.

الأساس القانوني المنشور الذي يجب أن يوجد قبل الإطلاق: سياسة الخصوصية، وشروط الخدمة، واتفاقية معالجة البيانات، جميعها مُراجَعة ومتوافقة مع نظام حماية البيانات. هذه جاهزية تشغيلية لا استشارة قانونية.

## Required documents — الوثائق المطلوبة

| Document | Purpose | Pass condition |
|---|---|---|
| Privacy policy | How data is collected, used, retained | Published AR + EN, reviewed |
| Terms of service | Scope, limits, no guarantees | Published AR + EN, reviewed |
| DPA | Processor obligations for client data | Available to sign per engagement |

## Privacy policy must state — يجب أن تنص سياسة الخصوصية

- What data is collected and the basis for processing it.
- That no PII is included in case studies or reports (counts, not names). See [../04_data_os/PII_CLASSIFICATION.md](../04_data_os/PII_CLASSIFICATION.md).
- Retention periods and deletion process. See [../04_data_os/DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md](../04_data_os/DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md).
- How to opt out, and that opt-outs are honored via the suppression list.
- That Dealix never sends external messages on a client's behalf without explicit approval.

## Terms of service must state — يجب أن تنص شروط الخدمة

- The offer ladder and that outputs are Estimated until evidenced.
- No guarantee of sales, ROI, conversion rates, or outcomes.
- The human-approval and no-external-send boundaries.
- PDPL-aware language. See [../02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md](../02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md).

## DPA must cover — يجب أن تغطي اتفاقية معالجة البيانات

- Roles: client as controller, Dealix as processor for client data.
- Scope of processing, sub-processors, security measures.
- Retention, deletion, and return of data at offboarding.
- Breach notification process. See [06_INCIDENT_AND_COMPLAINT_PROCESS.md](06_INCIDENT_AND_COMPLAINT_PROCESS.md).

## Legal review gate — بوّابة المراجعة القانونية

- [ ] Privacy policy reviewed and published (AR + EN).
- [ ] Terms reviewed and published (AR + EN).
- [ ] DPA template reviewed and ready to sign.
- [ ] PDPL-aware language confirmed across all three.
- [ ] No forbidden claim appears in any published document.

A legal review by a qualified reviewer signs off this gate before go-live. This document does not substitute for that review.

## Forbidden — ممنوع

No "guaranteed ROI", "100%", "replace your team", "automate everything", "no human needed", or fabricated urgency in any legal or marketing copy.
