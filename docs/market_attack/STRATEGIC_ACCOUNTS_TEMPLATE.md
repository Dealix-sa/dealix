case study + customer consent exist.

## Rules

- An account may only progress through stages with evidence; the
  approval_center gates external-facing transitions (scope_send,
  invoice_send, case_study_publish).
- No row in this template constitutes a public claim. Numbers cited
  externally must reference a Proof Ledger event.
