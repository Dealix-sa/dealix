# Competitive Intelligence System

A respectful, accurate, evidence-backed view of who else the buyer
might consider — and where Dealix differs.

## 1. Tracking scope

We track three archetypes:

1. **Global CRMs** — Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive.
2. **Outbound automation** — Outreach, Lemlist, Apollo, Instantly,
   Reply.io, smartlead-style tools.
3. **Local agencies** — anonymized for this repo.

We do not maintain dossiers on individuals.

## 2. Battle-card format

Every competitor gets one card with seven fields:

```
competitor_id,archetype,core_promise,buyer_perceived_pain,
dealix_counter_line,proof_artifact,collected_at
```

## 3. Update cadence

- Monthly review of public positioning.
- Ad-hoc on any time the competitor ships a major product change.
- Cards never reference private intel — only public statements and
  publicly observable patterns.

## 4. Use

- Sales console surfaces the right card based on the prospect's
  current tool stack.
- The brand_guardian agent reviews any auto-generated battle line for
  voice compliance (no insults, no false claims).
- Outbound drafts may quote a battle line only if the source is
  on the card.

## 5. Guardrails

- ❌ No reverse-engineering claims about private competitor data.
- ❌ No employee or customer-specific intel.
- ❌ No automated scraping of competitor admin surfaces.
- ✅ Public site, public docs, public price page — fair game.
