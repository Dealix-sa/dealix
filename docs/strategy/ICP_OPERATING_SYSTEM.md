# ICP Operating System
## Purpose
Define, test, and refine the ideal customer profile for Dealix.
## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.
## Review Cadence
Weekly during first 90 days.
## Initial ICP
Saudi-focused B2B companies that sell medium/high-ticket services or software and need qualified opportunities.
## Good ICP Signals
- B2B.
- Clear offer.
- Saudi or Saudi-focused market.
- Average deal size above 5,000 SAR.
- Needs pipeline.
- Has decision maker.
- Can benefit from qualified opportunities.
- Can pay for Sprint or retainer.
## Bad ICP Signals
- unclear offer.
- low-ticket B2C.
- wants guaranteed sales.
- refuses approval process.
- no decision maker.
- custom scope too large for starter offer.
- cannot define best customer.
## Initial Sectors
- ERP / CRM vendors.
- Cybersecurity firms.
- B2B agencies.
- Logistics / industrial services.
- Saudi-focused SaaS.
- Consulting / implementation firms.
## ICP Test Rule
A sector is not "good" until tested with:
- 25 leads.
- 25 DMs.
- reply evidence.
- sample/proposal evidence.
## ICP Decisions
Build / Fix / Kill / Defer / Continue.
