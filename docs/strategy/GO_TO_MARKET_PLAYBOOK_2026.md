## 1. ICP Definition — تعريف ملف العميل المثالي

**English**

The Dealix Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) is a Saudi-based, private-sector B2B company that meets all of the following criteria.

**Firmographic criteria:**
- Registered in Saudi Arabia (CR number held by Saudi national or Saudi-majority entity)
- Headcount: 20–500 employees
- Primary operating city: Riyadh, Jeddah, or Dammam
- Annual B2B revenue above 500,000 SAR
- ZATCA-affected: issues invoices to other businesses (Phase 2 mandate applies or will apply)
- At least one active CRM, ERP, or sales pipeline tool in use (even if poorly maintained)

**Pain criteria (any two must be present):**
- Sales pipeline is managed in spreadsheets or WhatsApp without scoring
- Revenue reporting is manual — weekly or monthly exports compiled by hand
- ZATCA compliance is reactive rather than embedded in the revenue workflow
- Data is distributed across tools with no single source of truth
- Outreach is inconsistent — no documented cadence, no bilingual capability

**Disqualifying criteria (any one disqualifies):**
- Consumer-facing (B2C) as primary revenue model
- Fewer than 5 people involved in B2B sales or account management
- Active regulatory investigation or public compliance dispute
- Requests for cold WhatsApp automation, LinkedIn scraping, or guaranteed outcome commitments

**العربية**

ملف العميل المثالي لـ Dealix هو شركة قطاع خاص للأعمال التجارية مقرها المملكة العربية السعودية وتستوفي جميع المعايير التالية.

**معايير الشركة:**
- مسجلة في المملكة العربية السعودية (سجل تجاري بحوزة مواطن سعودي أو كيان بأغلبية سعودية)
- عدد الموظفين: 20–500 موظف
- المدينة التشغيلية الرئيسية: الرياض، جدة، أو الدمام
- إيرادات أعمال تجارية سنوية تتجاوز 500,000 ريال
- خاضعة لهيئة الزكاة والضريبة والجمارك: تصدر فواتير لشركات أخرى (يسري أو سيسري تفويض المرحلة الثانية)
- تستخدم على الأقل أداة CRM أو ERP أو خط مبيعات واحدة نشطة (حتى لو كانت سيئة الصيانة)

**معايير الألم (يكفي توافر اثنتين):**
- تُدار خطوط مبيعات الشركة في جداول بيانات أو واتساب دون تقييم
- تقارير الإيرادات يدوية — تصدير أسبوعي أو شهري يُجمَّع يدوياً
- الامتثال لهيئة الزكاة تفاعلي لا مُدمَج في سير عمل الإيرادات
- البيانات موزعة عبر أدوات دون مصدر حقيقة واحد
- التواصل مع العملاء غير متسق — لا إيقاع موثَّق، لا قدرة ثنائية اللغة

**معايير الاستبعاد (تستبعد عند توافر واحد منها):**
- نموذج الإيرادات الأساسي للمستهلك (B2C)
- أقل من 5 أشخاص معنيين بمبيعات الأعمال التجارية أو إدارة الحسابات
- تحقيق تنظيمي نشط أو نزاع امتثال عام
- طلبات أتمتة واتساب الباردة أو كشط LinkedIn أو تعهدات نتائج مضمونة

## 2. Five-Rung Product Ladder — السلم الخماسي للمنتج

Each rung is a complete, deliverable product. Clients enter at the rung that matches their readiness and climb based on demonstrated value.

| Rung | Product | Price (SAR) | Delivery | Conversion Target (Estimated) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Free Diagnostic | 0 | 45-minute call + 1-page summary | 40% convert to Sprint |
| 2 | Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 499 one-time | 7 days, 14-section Proof Pack | 35% convert to Managed Ops |
| 3 | Managed Ops — Essential | 2,999/mo | Monthly retainer, weekly health score | 50% upgrade to Professional within 3 months |
| 4 | Managed Ops — Professional | 3,999/mo | Monthly retainer + sprint sessions | 20% upgrade to Enterprise within 6 months |
| 5 | Custom AI Engagement | 15,000+/mo | Bespoke architecture + dedicated delivery | Target: 1 active per quarter |

Note: Conversion targets above are internal planning estimates, not guaranteed rates. Actual conversion depends on client data readiness, organizational decision speed, and sprint quality.

**Rung 1 — Free Diagnostic (الفحص المجاني)**

A 45-minute structured call using the standard discovery framework. Output: a one-page Arabic-English summary of the three most significant revenue operations gaps identified. No data is collected; the diagnostic is based on founder interview. The diagnostic is the lead magnet, not a free consulting engagement. Scope is bounded: one call, one summary, one clear next step.

**Rung 2 — Revenue Intelligence Sprint (سبرنت ذكاء الإيرادات)**

499 SAR, seven days, complete Proof Pack. The client uploads data via the Source Passport process. Dealix returns a scored account list, revenue leakage analysis, ZATCA compliance status check, bilingual outreach drafts (for founder approval, not autonomous send), and a 14-section Proof Pack. See [docs/03_commercial_mvp/SPRINT_DELIVERY_PLAYBOOK.md](../03_commercial_mvp/SPRINT_DELIVERY_PLAYBOOK.md).

**Rung 3 — Managed Ops Essential (عمليات مُدارة — أساسي)**

2,999 SAR/month, month-to-month, 30-day notice. Includes: weekly automated health score, monthly KPI report, ZATCA compliance check, one sprint session per month, Proof Pack update, and access to the client room dashboard. Governance and APPROVAL_FIRST discipline apply throughout.

**Rung 4 — Managed Ops Professional (عمليات مُدارة — احترافي)**

3,999 SAR/month. All Essential deliverables plus: two sprint sessions per month, expanded bilingual reporting, quarterly business review, expansion map with upgrade signals, and priority founder access.

**Rung 5 — Custom AI Engagement (مشاركة ذكاء اصطناعي مخصصة)**

15,000+ SAR/month, scoped per engagement. Bespoke AI workflow design, dedicated delivery lead, integration architecture, and board-ready reporting. Requires minimum six-month commitment and an active Managed Ops retainer as prerequisite.

## 3. Founder-Led Sales Motion — حركة المبيعات بقيادة المؤسس

Six sequential steps. No step is skipped. The motion is governed by the same APPROVAL_FIRST principle that governs client operations.

**Step 1 — Warm Introduction (المقدمة الدافئة)**

All new contacts come from warm introductions: mutual connections, event attendees, referrals from existing clients, or LinkedIn organic content engagement. Cold outreach — unsolicited messages to people with no prior connection — is not part of the sales motion. The warm-intro requirement applies to every channel.

**Step 2 — LinkedIn Organic Post (منشور LinkedIn العضوي)**

The founder publishes two to three posts per week sharing case-safe findings, sector observations, and ZATCA/PDPL commentary. These posts generate inbound interest. Interested prospects are engaged in comments or DMs they initiate — not via automated prospecting sequences.

**Step 3 — Free Diagnostic (الفحص المجاني)**

Qualified inbound prospects are offered a 45-minute diagnostic call. The founder runs the standard discovery questions, identifies the two or three highest-priority revenue operations gaps, and delivers a one-page summary within 24 hours. No data upload required at this stage.

**Step 4 — Sprint Proposal (عرض السبرنت)**

If the diagnostic confirms ICP fit and at least two active pain criteria, the founder sends a Sprint proposal within 48 hours. Proposal format follows [docs/sales/PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE_AR_EN.md](../sales/PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE_AR_EN.md). The proposal is specific: it names the diagnosed gaps, the scope of the Sprint, and the expected Proof Pack sections.

**Step 5 — Proof Delivery (تسليم الإثبات)**

The Sprint runs over seven days following the standard SOP at [docs/ops/SPRINT_EXECUTION_SOP.md](../ops/SPRINT_EXECUTION_SOP.md). On Day 7, the founder delivers the Proof Pack in a 60-minute structured handoff call, walking the client through every section. The call ends with a clear next-step proposal: Managed Ops retainer if retainer readiness criteria are met, or a second sprint if further data depth is needed.

**Step 6 — Retainer Pitch (عرض الاحتفاظ)**

The retainer pitch is data-driven: the founder presents the adoption score, the proof score, and the specific capability gaps that ongoing Managed Ops would close. No pressure tactics. The proposition is: you have evidence that this works; here is the cost to make it continuous.

## 4. Lead Generation Channels — قنوات توليد العملاء المحتملين

PDPL-compliant channels only. The following channels are active. Channels not listed are not in scope.

**LinkedIn Organic Content (محتوى LinkedIn العضوي)**
- Frequency: 2–3 posts per week
- Content types: case-safe sprint findings (anonymized), ZATCA compliance commentary, PDPL operational notes, sector revenue pattern observations
- Engagement rule: respond to comments and messages from prospects who initiate contact. Do not use automated DM sequences or connection request campaigns.

**Referral Network (شبكة الإحالات)**
- Existing clients and contacts refer new prospects
- Referral incentive: no cash kickbacks; offer a free diagnostic for the referred party as a goodwill gesture
- Tracking: log referral source in the engagement registry

**Content and Learn Hub (مركز المحتوى والتعلم)**
- Published materials: sector reports, case-safe summaries, ZATCA readiness checklists
- Access: public, no gating that requires personal data submission
- PDPL note: any opt-in form must state data purpose, retention period, and right to withdraw

**ZATCA Events and Workshops (فعاليات ومؤتمرات هيئة الزكاة)**
- Attend, sponsor, or present at ZATCA compliance workshops, phase-transition briefings, and industry association events
- No badge-scanning or bulk contact import from event attendance lists
- Follow-up: only contact attendees who initiate a conversation at the event or request follow-up directly

**Industry Conferences (المؤتمرات الصناعية)**
- LEAP, FII, sector-specific B2B forums in Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam
- Presence purpose: brand visibility and warm introductions, not bulk prospecting
- Business cards received at events: contact only with explicit verbal permission granted at the event

## 5. Sales Cadence — إيقاع المبيعات الأسبوعي

Weekly rhythm for the founder-led sales motion. All times approximate; adjust to active sprint load.

| Day | Activity | Duration | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Review pipeline alerts: health scores, open proposals, diagnostic requests | 30 min | Updated pipeline status |
| Tuesday | Follow-up calls: prospects at diagnostic stage or post-proposal | 2 hours | Logged call notes, next-step actions |
| Wednesday | Proposal sends: draft, review, send sprint or retainer proposals | 1.5 hours | Proposals sent, logged |
| Thursday | Diagnostic sessions: scheduled diagnostic calls with new prospects | 2–3 hours | Diagnostic summaries delivered within 24 hours |
| Friday | Content publish: LinkedIn post, case-safe summary update, sector note | 1 hour | Published post, content ledger updated |

Note: Sprint delivery days run concurrently with the sales cadence. Day 5 of every sprint (governance review) takes priority over all sales activities if bandwidth conflicts occur.

## 6. Qualification Criteria — معايير التأهيل

BANT adapted for Saudi B2B context.

**Budget (الميزانية)**
- Minimum viable: can the prospect authorize a 499 SAR purchase without a committee?
- Managed Ops minimum: does the company have a recurring budget line for operations or technology above 2,000 SAR/month?
- Red flag: procurement requires a six-month RFP for all vendor engagements below 10,000 SAR

**Authority (الصلاحية)**
- Primary contact must be a decision maker or direct influencer: CEO, CFO, VP Sales, Head of Operations, or equivalent
- Red flag: the contact says "I'll need to check with everyone" without naming who "everyone" is
- Saudi context note: collective consensus matters — identify who else needs to be included in the decision and invite them to the diagnostic call

**Need (الحاجة)**
- At least two of the ICP pain criteria from Section 1 must be present and described by the prospect in their own words
- Red flag: pain is described only in vague terms ("we want to grow") with no specific operational gap named

**Timeline (الجدول الزمني)**
- The prospect has a reason to act in the next 60 days: ZATCA phase-transition deadline, new budget cycle, incoming sales hire, or recent revenue shortfall
- Red flag: "we'll revisit in 12 months" with no concrete trigger event

**Data Readiness (استعداد البيانات — Saudi addition)**
- The prospect can identify at least one data source they control (CRM export, ERP output, manual sales list) for the Sprint
- Red flag: "all our data is in WhatsApp conversations with no export" — this requires a Data Readiness engagement first

**Compliance Posture (موقف الامتثال — Saudi addition)**
- The prospect acknowledges ZATCA obligations and is not in active dispute with GAZT
- Red flag: prospect asks for help bypassing ZATCA requirements
