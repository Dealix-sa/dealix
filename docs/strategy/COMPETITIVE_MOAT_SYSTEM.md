# Competitive Moat System

## Purpose
Build Dealix advantages through learning, data, trust, and repeatable execution.

## Potential Moats
1. Saudi B2B market intelligence.
2. Arabic/English outreach patterns.
3. Sector-specific opportunity maps.
4. Trust-safe AI operating system.
5. Delivery playbooks.
6. Founder-led commercial learning.
7. Private company memory.

## Moat Rules
- A moat must improve with usage.
- A moat must be hard to copy through templates alone.
- A moat must connect to revenue, delivery, or retention.

## Monthly Moat Review
- What data did we collect?
- What insight improved?
- What workflow repeated?
- What became easier?
- What became harder for competitors to copy?

## Evidence
- sector performance
- message performance
- delivery feedback
- productization candidates
- retained clients
