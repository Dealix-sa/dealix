# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Motion

Founder-led, outbound-anchored, evidence-driven.

## The Loop

```
Warm intro / targeted DM
        ↓
    Discovery call (30 min)
        ↓
    Free diagnostic (async, 48 h)
        ↓
    Paid Revenue Sprint (SAR 499 entry → larger sprints)
        ↓
    Managed Pilot (SAR 2,999–4,999/mo)
        ↓
    Revenue Desk retainer (recurring)
        ↓
    Case study + referral ask
```

## Weekly Targets (Stage 1)

| Activity | Target |
|---|---|
| Warm intros | 5 |
| Targeted DMs | 25 |
| Discovery calls | 3 |
| Free diagnostics delivered | 2 |
| Paid sprints sold per month | 1 minimum |

## Channels

| Channel | Stage | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Warm intros | Always-on | Primary |
| Targeted founder DMs (LinkedIn / X) | Always-on | Primary, opt-in only |
| WhatsApp founder groups | Selective | Consent required |
| In-person events | Selective | Pair with follow-up sequence |
| Newsletter | Stage 2 | Defer until 5 case studies |
| Paid ads | Stage 3 | Defer until CAC < 0.5 × LTV proven |
| SEO content | Stage 3 | Defer until weekly publishing cadence is sustainable |

## Messaging Anchors

1. "We turn your existing pipeline into paid retainers in 30 days."
2. "Saudi-native revenue OS — Arabic and English, built for SAR cashflow."
3. "Trust gates, evidence on every delivery."

## Anti-Patterns

- Cold spam.
- Sending a proposal before a real call.
- Selling SaaS access without a delivered outcome first.
- Quoting in USD to a Saudi buyer.
