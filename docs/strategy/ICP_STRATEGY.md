# ICP Strategy

## Primary ICP (P0)

**Who:** Saudi B2B services companies, SAR 10M–100M revenue, founder-led or
GM-led, 10–80 employees.

**Pain:**
- Sales is the founder.
- Pipeline lives in WhatsApp and memory.
- Cannot afford a full RevOps team.
- Has tried CRMs that nobody fills in.

**Buying Trigger:**
- New fiscal year target.
- Recent loss of a key sales hire.
- Vision 2030–linked contract win that needs to be scaled.

**Buying Authority:** Founder, GM, or Head of Commercial.

**Disqualifiers:**
- Companies with under SAR 5M revenue.
- Pure consumer (B2C) businesses.
- Companies that refuse to share any pipeline data.

## Secondary ICP (P1)

**Who:** Mid-market Saudi corporates with a small internal sales ops team
that is overloaded.

**Pain:** Lead routing and proposal turnaround take too long.

**Trigger:** A failed CRM rollout.

## Tertiary ICP (P2, deferred)

**Who:** GCC-wide enterprises and government-adjacent contractors.

**Status:** Deferred until we have three P0 case studies.

## Rule

Outbound must hit P0 first. P1 is only contacted if a warm intro exists.
P2 is not contacted until Q4 of the current operating year.
