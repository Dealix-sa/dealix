# ICP Strategy

> Two to three sectors. Not more. Not yet.

## The Discipline

Founder-led outbound dies if the ICP is wide.
Wide ICP → generic message → low reply rate → exhausted founder → no revenue.

Dealix runs **2–3 ICPs at a time**, with a clear primary.

## Current ICP Cohort

### Primary — B2B SaaS targeting Saudi mid-market

- Employees: 10–200
- Revenue: 2–50M SAR
- Pain: sales pipeline below ARR ambition; outbound is uneven
- Decision-maker: Founder / Head of Sales / VP Commercial
- Trigger signal: recent hire of a sales role; product launch; new round
- Why us: qualified Saudi B2B leads with Arabic-fluent personalised hooks
- Average deal: Sprint 4,500 SAR; retainer 5–10K SAR/month

### Secondary — B2B agencies and consultancies

- Employees: 5–50
- Revenue: 1–20M SAR
- Pain: their own pipeline is feast-or-famine
- Decision-maker: Owner / Managing Partner
- Trigger signal: visible hiring of BD; recent rebrand; partner additions
- Why us: bilingual proof packs they can co-brand for their own clients
- Average deal: Sprint 4,500 SAR; retainer 5–8K SAR/month

### Tertiary — Cybersecurity / industrial services / logistics

- Employees: 20–500
- Revenue: 5–200M SAR
- Pain: long sales cycles, opportunity surfacing is slow
- Decision-maker: VP Commercial / Country Manager
- Why us: opportunity intelligence with sectoral evidence
- Average deal: Sprint 6,500 SAR; retainer 8–15K SAR/month

## Selection Rules

1. We do not add a fourth ICP until **two of the three** show ≥ 3
   completed sprints each.
2. We **remove** an ICP if 60 days of outreach produces < 5% reply rate
   **and** zero paid sprints.
3. ICPs outside Saudi (e.g. UAE) require an explicit Go decision via
   `GO_NO_GO_DECISION_SYSTEM.md`.

## ICP Scoring Rubric

When a new lead enters the pipeline, score 0–5 on:

- Fit (sector, size, geo)
- Pain (visible trigger signal)
- Authority (decision-maker reachable)
- Budget (price tolerance for our Sprint)
- Timing (is there a near-term wedge?)

Total / 25:

- **20–25:** A-priority. Personalised outreach within 24h.
- **14–19:** B-priority. Templated outreach.
- **8–13:** C-priority. Add to nurture only.
- **< 8:** Disqualify.

## ICP Anti-Patterns

- Selling into **everyone in tech in Saudi**.
- Letting an inbound from outside ICP pull the roadmap.
- Letting one large logo redefine the ICP for everyone else.
- Confusing **fit** with **excitement**.
