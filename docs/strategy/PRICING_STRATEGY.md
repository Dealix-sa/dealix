# Pricing Strategy

## Principles

1. Price for outcome, not for hours.
2. Each rung of the ladder must be self-funding before the next is offered.
3. SAR-native pricing. We do not auto-convert USD prices.
4. Annual prepay is encouraged with a documented discount.
5. No public discount wars.

## Current Ladder

| Offer | Price (SAR) | What's Delivered | Cycle |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Free Diagnostic | 0 | Async pipeline review + recommendation | One-time |
| Revenue Sprint (entry) | 499 | Single targeted analysis or list | 1 week |
| Data / Pipeline Pack | 1,500 | Cleaned pipeline + scoring + report | 2 weeks |
| Managed Ops | 2,999–4,999 / mo | Weekly drafting, reporting, governance | Monthly |
| Custom AI / Enterprise | 5,000–25,000 | Bespoke workflows + integrations | Quarterly |

## Discount Rules

- Annual prepay: 10% discount, paid in full upfront.
- Founder network referral: 1 free month of Managed Ops after referee converts.
- No ad-hoc discounts. Discount = documented rule, never negotiation.

## Pricing Experiments

Experiments are tracked in `docs/learning/PRICING_LEARNING.md`. A pricing
experiment requires:
- A hypothesis.
- A minimum sample (3 deals).
- A pre-defined success metric.
- A rollback condition.

## Out of Scope

- Bidding against a competitor on price for a deal we have not won on value.
- Free pilots that take more than 8 founder hours.
- Bundling unrelated services to inflate ticket size.
