# Dealix Growth System

## Purpose
Create a repeatable system for acquiring, converting, delivering, and retaining B2B clients.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Growth Loop
Lead → DM → Reply → Sample → Proposal → Payment → Delivery → Feedback → Retainer → Proof → More Leads

## Channels
1. Founder-led outbound
2. Samples
3. Referrals
4. Content based on proof
5. Partners
6. Retainers / expansion

## Current Channel Priority
1. Founder-led outbound
2. Sample packs
3. Proposal follow-up
4. Delivery proof
5. Client referral

## Rules
- Do not scale a channel before conversion exists.
- Do not publish proof without approval.
- Do not add channels before the first channel has learning.
- Every growth experiment must have a metric.

## Weekly Growth Targets
- 25 leads
- 25 DMs
- 5 replies
- 2 calls
- 3 samples
- 1 proposal
- 1 learning decision

## Metrics
- lead-to-contact rate
- contact-to-reply rate
- reply-to-sample rate
- sample-to-proposal rate
- proposal-to-payment rate
- delivery-to-retainer rate

## Evidence
- pipeline tracker
- revenue action log
- weekly intelligence review
- win/loss review
