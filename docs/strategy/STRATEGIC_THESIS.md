# Dealix Strategic Thesis

> One page. If you cannot defend it on one page, it is not a thesis.

## The Thesis

Saudi B2B companies (SaaS, ERP/CRM vendors, B2B agencies, cybersecurity,
logistics, industrial services) need **qualified revenue opportunities**
faster than their internal sales teams can generate them, and trust the
**Arabic-fluent, evidence-bound, founder-led** operator who provides them
more than they trust scraped-list vendors or self-serve tools.

Dealix delivers that, founder-led today, productized tomorrow, governed
always.

## The Four Pillars

1. **Arabic + Saudi-native context.** Outreach, intelligence, and proof
   are bilingual and ICP-specific. Most international tooling is not.
2. **Evidence-bound.** Every claim, lead, and outreach hook is backed by
   evidence the client can verify. No "AI magic".
3. **Founder-led trust.** The founder signs the work. The founder
   reviews the agent output. Trust is the moat.
4. **Productized ladder.** Sample → Sprint → Pilot → Retainer → OS.
   Each rung has a price, a scope, a delivery template, and a graduation
   path.

## Who this is for

- B2B SaaS targeting Saudi mid-market (5–500 employees)
- ERP / CRM vendors selling into Saudi enterprise
- B2B agencies whose own sales pipeline is uneven
- Cybersecurity / industrial services with long sales cycles
- Companies whose decision-maker outreach quality is mediocre

## Who this is **not** for

- B2C
- Companies who need leads but refuse to send anything themselves
- Companies who want a guarantee of closed revenue
- Procurement-by-RFP buyers in the first 12 months
- Buyers who cannot describe their ICP in one sentence

## What we are betting on

| Bet | Disconfirming evidence would be… |
|-----|----------------------------------|
| Arabic-native + evidence wins on reply rate | 60 days of outreach with reply rate < 5% across 3 ICPs |
| Sprint is the right wedge | Sprint-to-retainer rate < 20% after 6 sprints |
| Saudi B2B will pay 2,500–7,500 SAR for a Sprint | 5 qualified proposals at that price all close < 50% |
| Founder-led can produce 3 sprints/month | Founder hours saturated at 1 sprint/month |
| Retainers reach 5–10K SAR MRR by month 6 | 0 retainers signed by month 6 |

If any **two** disconfirming triggers fire, the thesis is reopened.

## What we are explicitly **not** betting on

- That product-led growth will work pre-PMF.
- That LinkedIn content alone produces deals.
- That outsourced SDRs improve founder-led reply rate.
- That a single enterprise logo justifies a year of customised work.
