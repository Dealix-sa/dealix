# Scenario Planning

## Purpose
Prepare Dealix CEO decisions for likely operating scenarios.

## Scenario 1 — No Replies After 25 DMs
Decision:
Fix ICP or message.
Actions:
- review sector
- rewrite DM
- test new angle
- add 25 new leads

## Scenario 2 — Replies But No Calls
Decision:
Fix CTA and sample offer.
Actions:
- send sample
- simplify ask
- qualify pain

## Scenario 3 — Proposals But No Payment
Decision:
Fix payment path, urgency, and decision-maker alignment.
Actions:
- follow up
- ask for objection
- offer PO/written approval route
- tighten proposal

## Scenario 4 — Paid But Delivery Hard
Decision:
Fix delivery scope and QA.
Actions:
- reduce scope
- improve intake
- add QA
- increase price if needed

## Scenario 5 — Delivery Good But No Retainer
Decision:
Fix client success and value framing.
Actions:
- ask feedback
- propose Revenue Desk
- identify next opportunity

## Scenario 6 — Founder Overloaded
Decision:
Document, template, defer, or delegate.
Actions:
- kill low-value work
- update checklist
- identify delegation candidate
