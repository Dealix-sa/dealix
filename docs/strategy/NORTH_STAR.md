# Dealix North Star

## North Star Metric

Qualified revenue opportunities delivered and converted into paid retainers.

## Current Strategic Focus

Revenue Sprint → Managed Pilot → Revenue Desk.

## What We Optimize For

1. Cash collected.
2. Proof generated.
3. Retainer conversion.
4. Founder leverage (hours saved per paid client).
5. Trust and reputation in the Saudi market.

## What We Do Not Optimize For Yet

- Number of features.
- Number of agents.
- Complex enterprise workflows before proof.
- Vanity GitHub activity.
- Inbound demand before outbound proof.

## Test

A piece of work passes the North Star test only if it increases at least one
of the five optimization targets above within 90 days. Otherwise it is
deferred or killed.
