# Strategic Portfolio System

## Purpose
Control which initiatives Dealix works on and which are killed or deferred.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly for active initiatives, monthly for strategic bets.

## Portfolio Categories
1. Revenue Now
2. Delivery Proof
3. Trust & Safety
4. Learning
5. Productization
6. Brand Proof
7. Delegation
8. Strategic Options

## Initiative Table

| Initiative | Category | Stage | Evidence | Decision | Owner | Next Review |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue Sprint | Revenue Now | Active | pipeline/revenue logs | Build | Sami | Weekly |
| SaaS Dashboard | Productization | Deferred | needs repeated workflow | Defer | Sami | Monthly |
| AI Outreach Agent | AI Governance | Blocked | needs approval/evals | Defer | Sami | Monthly |

## Decision Types
- Build: actively invest.
- Fix: broken but important.
- Kill: stop permanently.
- Defer: not now.
- Continue: keep operating.
- Escalate: requires CEO/trust decision.

## Go Criteria
An initiative gets Build only if it supports:
- cash
- proof
- retention
- trust
- learning
- founder leverage

## No-Go Criteria
- no evidence
- no customer demand
- no revenue/delivery link
- trust risk unclear
- founder bottleneck increases
- distracts from current stage

## Metrics
- active initiatives
- deferred initiatives
- killed initiatives
- revenue-linked initiatives
- founder hours consumed

## Evidence
- weekly CEO review
- decision log
- business score
- GitHub checks
