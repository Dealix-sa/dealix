# Dealix Control Tower

## Purpose
Create one executive control tower that connects strategy, revenue, finance,
delivery, trust, learning, productization, and founder focus.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly.

## Control Tower Inputs
- CEO Business Score
- Stage Evidence Report
- Pipeline Tracker
- Revenue Action Log
- Finance Review
- Delivery QA
- Approval Log
- Weekly Intelligence Review
- Productization Candidates
- Founder Time Log

## Control Tower Outputs
- CEO daily focus
- revenue priority
- risk alert
- delivery readiness signal
- finance signal
- stage decision
- kill/defer recommendation
- productization signal
- weekly learning decision
- monthly strategy recommendation

## CEO Question
What should Sami do next to move Dealix closer to cash, proof, retention,
trust, learning, or founder leverage?

## Rules
- Revenue priority comes before product polish.
- Trust gate comes before automation.
- Delivery proof comes before scale.
- Weekly learning comes before strategy change.
- Repetition comes before productization.
- Evidence comes before stage advancement.

## Core Metrics
- cash collected
- pipeline value
- DMs sent
- replies
- samples sent
- proposals sent
- payments pursued
- delivery QA pass
- feedback received
- retainer attempts
- trust incidents
- founder bottlenecks
- system updates
