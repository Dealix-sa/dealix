# Executive Control Plane

## Purpose
Create the CEO control layer that turns Dealix operating data into decisions, priorities, risk flags, and system improvements.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Daily for operations, weekly for decisions, monthly for strategy.

## Inputs
- CEO Business Score.
- Stage evidence.
- Pipeline tracker.
- Revenue action log.
- Finance review.
- Trust approvals.
- Delivery QA.
- Weekly learning review.
- Productization candidates.

## Outputs
- CEO focus.
- Priority queue.
- Risk alerts.
- Go/No-Go decisions.
- Kill/Defer list.
- Next system update.
- Monthly strategy signal.

## Control Questions
1. What moves cash now?
2. What creates proof?
3. What protects trust?
4. What blocks delivery?
5. What should be killed or deferred?
6. What repeated enough to template, delegate, or automate?
7. What should not be automated yet?

## Rules
- No priority without evidence.
- No automation without trust level.
- No productization without repetition.
- No stage advancement without exit criteria.
- No public claim without evidence.
- No weekly close without learning decision.

## Metrics
- CEO Business Score.
- Stage readiness.
- Cash collected.
- Pipeline value.
- Proposal-to-payment rate.
- Delivery QA pass rate.
- Trust incidents.
- Founder bottlenecks.
- System updates.
