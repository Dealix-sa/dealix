# Dealix Frontend Conversion Standard

## هدف الواجهة

الواجهة لا تشرح التقنية فقط. الواجهة يجب أن تجعل صاحب الشركة يفهم بسرعة:

1. ما المشكلة التي يحلها Dealix.
2. لماذا Dealix ليس chatbot أو CRM عادي.
3. ما أول باقة يمكن شراؤها.
4. لماذا النظام آمن.
5. ماذا يحدث بعد الضغط على CTA.

## Above the fold

يجب أن يحتوي أول جزء في الصفحة على:

- headline واضح: AI Operating Systems للشركات السعودية.
- subheadline يشرح الإيراد والمتابعة والقرار اليومي.
- primary CTA: احجز مراجعة تشغيلية.
- secondary CTA: شاهد الباقات أو الأمان.
- trust statement: no uncontrolled external sending.

## Required sections

1. Problem contrast: قبل Dealix / بعد Dealix.
2. Commercial offer cards: pain, outcome, timeline, price range.
3. Delivery method: Map, Design, Build, Operate, Scale.
4. Safety proof: draft-first, no fake ROI, approval-first.
5. Sector wedges: clinics, real estate, logistics, training, agencies, B2B services.
6. Daily targeting explanation with reputation-safe volume.
7. Final CTA.

## Copy rules

- Arabic-first with clear English product names.
- No fake clients.
- No fake metrics.
- No guaranteed revenue.
- No vague AI hype.
- Every claim must be operational.

## Conversion checklist

- CTA appears in hero and final section.
- Product cards include timeline and price range.
- Safety is visible before footer.
- Homepage explains how the first 7 days work.
- Links point to existing product, booking, and pricing pages.
- Page remains mobile-friendly.

## What changed in launch homepage

The homepage now focuses on:

- Saudi B2B operating systems.
- Revenue Command Room, Company Brain, Follow-up OS, Trust OS.
- Manual review and safe defaults.
- First offer clarity instead of platform abstraction.
- Growth loop explanation with safe outreach rules.
