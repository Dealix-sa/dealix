## Content pillars — ركائز المحتوى

Every piece sits under one pillar so the body of work compounds into authority.

1. **Revenue leakage** — where Saudi B2B revenue leaks and how to see it. تسرّب الإيرادات.
2. **Governed AI operations** — AI with approval gates, no auto-send, PDPL-aware. التشغيل المُحوكَم.
3. **Proof discipline** — estimated vs verified value, Proof Packs, no hype. انضباط الإثبات.
4. **Saudi operating context** — ZATCA, PDPL, Vision 2030 digitization, sector realities. السياق السعودي.

Pillars mirror the SEO/GEO clusters in [SEO_GEO_STRATEGY.md](./SEO_GEO_STRATEGY.md).

## Formats — الصيغ

| Format | Purpose | Primary channel |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn post | Surface a named pain, founder voice | LinkedIn |
| Sector report | Educate, establish authority, earn citations | Website + LinkedIn |
| Teardown | Show a common operations gap (anonymized) | LinkedIn + website |
| Case-safe summary | Reuse delivered proof as demand | Website + nurture |
| Free-tool result page | Convert intent | Website |

All formats are bilingual AR + EN, parallel in length and structure. No emojis, no model names, no marketing fluff.

---

## Cadence — الإيقاع

- **Weekly:** 3 LinkedIn posts (founder voice), 1 teardown.
- **Monthly:** 1 sector report (Arabic-first), 1 case-safe summary (when a Sprint is delivered).
- **Quarterly:** 1 pillar/cluster refresh for SEO/GEO authority.

Cadence is sustainable and founder-led; volume never overrides the approval gate.

أسبوعياً 3 منشورات وتشريح واحد. شهرياً تقرير قطاعي وملخص آمن. ربع سنوياً تحديث عنقود.

## Repurposing engine — محرك إعادة الاستخدام

One source asset becomes many, without diluting accuracy.

```
Sector report (source)
  → 3 LinkedIn posts (one pillar angle each)
  → 1 teardown (a single gap from the report)
  → 1 nurture email segment
  → 1 SEO/GEO pillar update
  → tool copy refresh (if relevant)
```

A delivered Command Sprint feeds the loop: Proof Pack → case-safe summary → posts → demand. This is loop L3 in [SELF_GROWTH_OS.md](./SELF_GROWTH_OS.md). No new claim is published without a delivered Proof Pack behind it.

---

## Approval gate — بوابة الموافقة

No content publishes without founder approval. Every draft passes a checklist before release:

- One CTA only (Score, Diagnostic, or Command Sprint).
- No guaranteed-revenue claims, no fake scarcity, no fake proof.
- Estimated values labeled as estimated; case references are case-safe, no PII.
- Bilingual AR + EN parallel and complete.
- Claims match [../05_governance_os/CLAIM_SAFETY.md](../05_governance_os/CLAIM_SAFETY.md) and the disclosure line is present on customer-facing assets.

لا نشر بدون موافقة المؤسس، ولكل مسودة قائمة تحقق: مسار واحد، لا ادعاءات مضمونة، قيم تقديرية موسومة، مراجع آمنة، عربي وإنجليزي متوازيان.
