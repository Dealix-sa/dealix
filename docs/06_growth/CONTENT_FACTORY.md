# Content Factory — مصنع المحتوى (Founder-led)

> Loop 4→5 of the Self-Growth OS. Turns delivered **proof** into market trust. Founder is the
> voice; the system removes the friction. Every approved Proof Pack → **1 case study + N posts**.
> Nothing publishes without **founder approval** (queue drafts, never auto-send). Status: `DOCS_ONLY`.

## المبدأ (Principle)

We do not produce generic AI noise. Content is built from real ledgers — Proof Packs, Learning
Ledger, anonymized sector patterns — and carries the founder's point of view. Inherits pillars and
cadence from `docs/growth/CONTENT_ENGINE.md`.

## حلقة البرهان → المحتوى (Proof-to-content loop)

```text
Sprint delivered → Proof Pack created → Founder approves anonymization
   → 1 Case Study (long form) → N atomic posts (3–6) → Learn article
   → distributed (manual, founder-approved) → Inbound + SEO/GEO asset
```

Rules:
- Only **approved, anonymized** Proof Packs become content. No client name/logo/quote without
  written approval (CLAUDE.md).
- Every number is linked to its Proof Pack/Claims Register entry, or framed as a hypothesis.
- Case study uses the case-study template (`landing/case-study-template.html`).

## الأركان (Pillars — from CONTENT_ENGINE)

1. AI projects fail on data + process, not model hype.
2. AI operations vs AI tools.
3. Saudi / Arabic-first business use cases.
4. Governance-first AI.
5. Proof-pack patterns (anonymized).
6. Lead intelligence / revenue ops.
7. Arabic quality as a standard.

## الإيقاع (Cadence — target, hypothesis)

```text
~2 posts / week (capacity-permitting)
1 case study / month (from an approved Proof Pack)
1 Learn article / month (sector or function)
1 benchmark rollup / quarter (only once enough data exists)
```

## الصيغ وإعادة الاستخدام (Formats + repurposing)

One approved Proof Pack repurposes into:

| Asset | Channel | Routes to |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Case study (long) | Learn / `proof.html` | Book Diagnostic |
| 3–6 atomic posts | Founder LinkedIn/X (manual post) | Get Business OS Score |
| 1 carousel / visual | Social | Get Business OS Score |
| 1 Learn SEO article | `frontend/[locale]/learn/[slug]` | Book Diagnostic |
| 1 nurture insert | Owned email (queued) | Book Diagnostic |

## بوابة الموافقة (Approval gate — non-negotiable)

1. Draft generated → 2. Proof/claims check by `proof-governance-reviewer` →
3. Founder reviews + approves → 4. Founder (or approved operator) **manually** publishes.
No auto-send, no scheduled external publishing without that approval.
