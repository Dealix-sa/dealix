# Nurture Sequences — تسلسلات التغذية

**Status: INTERNAL** — bilingual email nurture sequences. All emails are queued as DRAFTS; no external auto-send. Sequences move to BETA only after founder approval of copy and gates.

> Purpose — الغرض: define email nurture sequences for Dealix, the Saudi-first AI Business Operating System. Four sequences, each with a trigger, steps, timing, copy outline, and exactly one CTA. No external message is auto-sent; every email is a draft until founder approval. Cross-link: [WEBSITE_FUNNEL_MAP.md](./WEBSITE_FUNNEL_MAP.md), [FREE_TOOLS_STRATEGY.md](./FREE_TOOLS_STRATEGY.md), [CONTENT_FACTORY.md](./CONTENT_FACTORY.md), [../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md](../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md).

تسلسلات التغذية تحدد أربعة تسلسلات بريدية لـ Dealix، لكل منها محفّز وخطوات وتوقيت ومخطط نسخة ومسار واحد فقط. لا إرسال خارجي تلقائي؛ كل بريد مسودة حتى موافقة المؤسس.

---

## Rules — القواعد

- Every email is bilingual AR + EN, parallel in length and structure.
- One CTA per email; the sequence never offers competing actions.
- No external auto-send. Drafts queue for founder approval, per [../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md](../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md).
- PDPL-aware consent required before any sequence starts. Clear unsubscribe in every email.
- No fake scarcity, no guaranteed revenue, no fake proof.

كل بريد عربي وإنجليزي متوازٍ، بمسار واحد، بلا إرسال تلقائي. موافقة PDPL قبل البدء، وإلغاء اشتراك واضح.

---

## Sequence 1 — New lead — تسلسل العميل الجديد

- **Trigger:** lead captured via a free tool or `/start`, consent given. المحفّز: التقاط عميل بموافقة.
- **ONE CTA:** Business OS Score (if not yet taken) or Diagnostic (if taken). المسار الواحد.

| Step | Timing | Copy outline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | Welcome; restate the named pain; deliver tool result link. ترحيب وتسليم النتيجة. |
| 2 | Day 2 | Educate: governed AI operations vs chatbots; one pillar idea. تثقيف. |
| 3 | Day 5 | Case-safe pattern (anonymized); soft CTA. نمط آمن. |
| 4 | Day 9 | Direct CTA; offer the next step. دعوة مباشرة. |

---

## Sequence 2 — Diagnostic completed — تسلسل ما بعد التشخيص

- **Trigger:** Diagnostic booked or completed. المحفّز: حجز/إتمام التشخيص.
- **ONE CTA:** Command Sprint proposal. المسار الواحد.

| Step | Timing | Copy outline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | Confirm; summarize what the Diagnostic surfaced. تأكيد وملخص. |
| 2 | Day 2 | Explain the Command Sprint and its eight modules. شرح السبرنت. |
| 3 | Day 4 | Address top objection (proof, PDPL, or price) safely. معالجة اعتراض. |
| 4 | Day 7 | Send the scoped proposal as a draft for review. إرسال العرض. |

Sprint modules align with [../03_commercial_mvp/SPRINT_SCOPE.md](../03_commercial_mvp/SPRINT_SCOPE.md).

---

## Sequence 3 — Sprint-curious — تسلسل المهتم بالسبرنت

- **Trigger:** engaged with `/command-sprint` or sprint content but not yet booked a Diagnostic. المحفّز: تفاعل دون حجز.
- **ONE CTA:** Diagnostic. المسار الواحد.

| Step | Timing | Copy outline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | Reframe: the Sprint delivers a Proof Pack, not promises. إعادة تأطير. |
| 2 | Day 3 | Show what proof looks like (case-safe, estimated labeled). عرض الإثبات. |
| 3 | Day 6 | Lower the barrier: the Diagnostic scopes before commitment. خفض الحاجز. |
| 4 | Day 10 | CTA: book the Diagnostic. دعوة. |

---

## Sequence 4 — Post-sprint upsell — تسلسل ما بعد السبرنت

- **Trigger:** Command Sprint delivered, Proof Pack issued. المحفّز: تسليم السبرنت.
- **ONE CTA:** Retainer (managed operations). المسار الواحد.

| Step | Timing | Copy outline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | Recap delivered value (observed/verified tiers honest). ملخص القيمة. |
| 2 | Day 4 | Upsell recommendation from the Sprint; what continuity adds. توصية الترقية. |
| 3 | Day 8 | Address "can we do it in-house" with governance/proof framing. معالجة. |
| 4 | Day 12 | CTA: the retainer path. دعوة المسار المتكرر. |

Aligns with [../03_commercial_mvp/RETAINER_PATH.md](../03_commercial_mvp/RETAINER_PATH.md). Value tiers follow [../08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md](../08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md).

## Approval gates — بوابات الموافقة

- **Gate 1 — Consent:** no sequence starts without explicit PDPL-aware opt-in.
- **Gate 2 — Draft release:** every email stays a draft until founder approval.
- **Gate 3 — Proof claim:** no email cites a result without a delivered Proof Pack.

هذه البوابات غير قابلة للتفاوض.
