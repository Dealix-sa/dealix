# Dealix Self-Growth OS — نظام النمو الذاتي

> Master overview. Status: `DOCS_ONLY` (strategy spec). Owner: growth-strategist.
> Canonical rules: `CLAUDE.md`, `docs/00_constitution/`. Ladder: Free Diagnostic (0) →
> Command Sprint (499 SAR) → Data-to-Revenue Pack (1,500) → Managed Revenue Ops (2,999–4,999/mo)
> → Custom AI Setup (5,000–25,000 + 1,000/mo).

## القاعدة الوحيدة (The single rule)

Every growth asset routes to **exactly one** primary CTA:

- **Business OS Score** (`/risk-score`) — top of funnel, lowest friction.
- **Book Diagnostic** (`/dealix-diagnostic`) — Free Diagnostic, rung 0.
- **Start Command Sprint** (`/start` → 499 SAR) — first commercial wedge.

If an asset cannot name its one CTA, it does not ship. No competing CTAs on a page.

## النمو حلقات لا حملات (Loops, not blasts)

Dealix grows by compounding loops where each output becomes the next loop's input.
Nothing is a one-off. The master loop:

```text
Free Tool → Diagnostic → Sprint → Proof Pack → Content → Inbound → Partner → Referral → Academy → Trust
   ▲                                                                                              │
   └──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                    (Trust lowers friction on the next Free Tool → the loop tightens)
```

### كيف تغذّي كل حلقة التالية (How each loop feeds the next)

| # | Loop | Output it produces | Feeds → |
|---|------|--------------------|---------|
| 1 | **Free Tool** | Qualified lead + a score/result worth keeping | Diagnostic |
| 2 | **Diagnostic** | Named pain + revenue map + buying intent | Sprint |
| 3 | **Sprint** (499) | A delivered, governed outcome | Proof Pack |
| 4 | **Proof Pack** | Anonymized, approved evidence asset | Content |
| 5 | **Content** | Founder-led posts + 1 case study per pack | Inbound |
| 6 | **Inbound** | Warm demand that already trusts the method | Partner |
| 7 | **Partner** | 3rd-party reach (agencies, consultants) | Referral |
| 8 | **Referral** | Delivered-proof-backed warm intros | Academy |
| 9 | **Academy** | Educated buyers who self-qualify | Trust |
| 10 | **Trust** | Governance/proof reputation | back to Free Tool (cheaper, faster) |

Each loop must produce at least one of: **revenue · proof · reusable asset · next offer**
(inherited from `docs/growth/GROWTH_MACHINE.md`). If it produces none, it is theater — cut it.

## الحواجز الصلبة (Hard rules — all assets)

- No spam, no scraping, no cold WhatsApp/LinkedIn automation, no auto-send.
- No fake scarcity, no fake testimonials, no guaranteed revenue.
- No external publishing without **founder approval** (queue drafts; never send).
- Every external claim is **evidence-backed**, **hypothesis-framed**, or **rewritten safely**.
- Use module status labels when describing any capability.
