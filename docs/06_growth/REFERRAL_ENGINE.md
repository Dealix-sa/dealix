# Referral Engine — محرك الإحالات

**Status: FUTURE** — referral system specced; activates only after the first delivered Proof Packs exist to anchor proof-driven referrals.

> Purpose — الغرض: define an ethical referral system for Dealix, the Saudi-first AI Business Operating System. Who refers, an ethical incentive structure, tracking, proof-driven referrals, and approval gates. No spam, no fake scarcity. Cross-link: [SELF_GROWTH_OS.md](./SELF_GROWTH_OS.md), [PARTNER_DISTRIBUTION.md](./PARTNER_DISTRIBUTION.md), [../07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md](../07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md), [../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md](../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md).

محرك الإحالات يحدد نظام إحالات أخلاقي لـ Dealix: من يُحيل، هيكل حوافز أخلاقي، التتبّع، إحالات مدفوعة بالإثبات، وبوابات الموافقة. لا سبام ولا ندرة مزيفة.

## Who refers — من يُحيل

| Referrer | Why they refer | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Satisfied Sprint clients | They saw a Proof Pack | Post-sprint, value confirmed |
| Advisors / partners | Client relationships | Per [PARTNER_DISTRIBUTION.md](./PARTNER_DISTRIBUTION.md) |
| Network peers | Founder reputation, content | Inbound interest |

The strongest referral comes from a client who holds a delivered Proof Pack — proof-driven, not incentive-driven.

أقوى إحالة من عميل يملك حزمة إثبات مُسلَّمة؛ مدفوعة بالإثبات لا بالحافز.

---

## Incentive structure (ethical) — هيكل الحوافز الأخلاقي

- **Value-aligned, not spam-aligned:** rewards a real, consenting introduction that fits the Command Sprint, never volume of names.
- **Options:** credit toward the referrer's retainer, a donation option, or a transparent revenue share for advisors. خيارات: رصيد، تبرع، أو حصة شفافة.
- **Disclosed:** the referrer discloses the relationship to the referred company. مُفصح عنه.
- **Capped and clear:** no escalating bounties that reward mass referral. لا مكافآت متصاعدة.

No incentive is paid for cold outreach, scraped contacts, or a referral made without the referred party's consent.

---

## Proof-driven referrals — الإحالات المدفوعة بالإثبات

A referral is strongest when anchored to proof the client can stand behind:

```
1. Sprint delivered → Proof Pack issued (estimated/verified honest).
2. Client confirms value (observed or verified).
3. Founder invites a referral — optional, no pressure.
4. Client introduces a real, consenting peer.
5. Referral routes to Business OS Score or Diagnostic (one CTA).
```

Case-safe summaries ([../07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md](../07_proof_os/CASE_SAFE_SUMMARY.md)) support the referral without exposing PII.

---

## Tracking — التتبّع

- Referral source recorded at capture (referrer label, not the referred party's PII beyond consented contact).
- Status tracked through the funnel: introduced → Score → Diagnostic → Sprint → reward.
- Reward only on a closed, consenting, founder-approved outcome.
- Metrics feed [GROWTH_METRICS.md](./GROWTH_METRICS.md) (referral contribution to pipeline).

التتبّع يسجّل المصدر والحالة عبر القمع، والمكافأة فقط على نتيجة مُغلقة بموافقة.

---

## Approval gates — بوابات الموافقة

- **Gate 1 — Consent:** the referred party opts in before any nurture. موافقة الطرف المُحال.
- **Gate 2 — Founder release:** every outreach to a referral is a draft until founder approval. إفراج المؤسس.
- **Gate 3 — Proof claim:** no referral material cites a result without a delivered Proof Pack. ادعاء الإثبات.
- **Gate 4 — Reward integrity:** rewards paid only on disclosed, consenting, closed outcomes. نزاهة المكافأة.

Mirrors [../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md](../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md).

---

## Guardrails — الضوابط

- No spam, no fake scarcity, no mass referral bounties.
- No referral without the referred party's consent.
- No auto-send; no scraping; no purchased lists.
- No guaranteed-revenue language in referral copy.
