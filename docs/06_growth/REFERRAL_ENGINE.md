# Referral Engine — محرّك الإحالة

> Loop 8. Referrals are earned by **delivered proof**, not bribed into existence. Honest
> incentives only — no pay-for-fake-review, no fake testimonials, no guaranteed revenue.
> Status: `DOCS_ONLY`. Trigger lives in `NURTURE_SEQUENCES.md` (Sprint follow-up, step 4).

## المبدأ (Principle)

The best referral is a happy customer who saw a real Proof Pack. We only ask for referrals **after**
value is delivered, and we make the ask easy and honest. Incentives reward a *real* introduction
that becomes a *real* engagement — never a fabricated endorsement.

## متى نطلب (When we ask)

```text
Sprint delivered → Proof Pack approved → outcome acknowledged by client
   → THEN referral ask (T+21d in the Sprint follow-up sequence)
```

Never ask a cold or unhappy contact. Never ask before proof exists.

## الميكانيكا (Mechanics)

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Who can refer | Customers with a delivered Sprint/Pack; approved partners |
| What counts | A warm, consented introduction (referrer tells us they've asked the peer) |
| Reward trigger | The referred lead completes a **paid** engagement (not just a signup) |
| Reward type | Account credit toward next rung, or a transparent referral fee for partners |
| Disallowed | Cash for a review, incentives for testimonials, points for cold-blasting peers |

## حلقة الإحالة (Referral loop)

```text
Delivered proof → honest ask → warm consented intro → Diagnostic → Sprint
   → new Proof Pack → new happy referrer → loop repeats
```

Referred leads enter at the **Diagnostic** (rung 0) so the relationship starts with value, not a hard sell.

## نزاهة الحوافز (Incentive integrity)

- Incentives are disclosed to both sides; nothing hidden.
- The referred party is never told a guaranteed outcome.
- No "leaderboards" or fake-urgency mechanics that pressure cold spam.
- Reward only pays on a genuine closed engagement, verified in the CRM.

## القياس (Measure)

- Referral rate = referred Diagnostics / delivered Proof Packs (leading).
- Referral → Sprint conversion (lagging). See `GROWTH_METRICS.md`. No vanity "invites sent" metric.
