> Purpose — الغرض: spec four free tools as lead magnets for Dealix, the Saudi-first AI Business Operating System. Each tool gives real value, captures intent with PDPL-aware opt-in, and routes to exactly one CTA. ## Principles — المبادئ

- Value before email: show partial value, then capture.
- Every score is **illustrative and estimated**, never presented as verified or guaranteed.
- One CTA per tool. PDPL-aware opt-in, no pre-checked boxes, no purchased lists.
- Results route into the founder-approval nurture queue as drafts — no auto-send.

القيمة قبل البريد. كل نتيجة تقديرية موضّحة. مسار واحد لكل أداة. لا إرسال تلقائي.

## Tool A — Business OS Score (PRIORITY) — مؤشر نظام تشغيل الأعمال

- **Purpose** — diagnose how governed and revenue-tight a company's operations are. الغرض: قياس نضج التشغيل والحوكمة.
- **Inputs** — sector, company size, current revenue tracking method, AI usage, approval practices, proof practices (8–12 questions). المدخلات.
- **Output / score logic (illustrative)** — a 0–100 maturity index across four bands: Revenue Visibility, Governance, Proof Discipline, Execution. Each answer maps to band points; total is banded into Foundational / Developing / Governed. Output is an estimated maturity profile, not a verified benchmark.
- **CTA** — Diagnostic (book to scope a Command Sprint).
- **Lead capture** — name, work email, company, sector, consent.
- **Status: BETA target** — first implementation. الأولوية الأولى.

---

## Tool B — Revenue Leakage Calculator — حاسبة تسرّب الإيرادات

- **Purpose** — give an estimated annual revenue-leakage range from common gaps (slow follow-up, lost quotes, untracked returns). الغرض: تقدير نطاق تسرّب سنوي.
- **Inputs** — monthly leads, average deal value, follow-up speed, quote-to-close rate, known leakage points.
- **Output / score logic (illustrative)** — multiplies leakage drivers by deal value to produce an **estimated range** (low–high), with each assumption shown so it is transparent, not a promise.
- **CTA** — Business OS Score (then Diagnostic).
- **Lead capture** — name, work email, company, sector, consent.
- **Status: FUTURE.**

---

## Tool C — Proof Gap Audit — تدقيق فجوة الإثبات

- **Purpose** — show whether a company can actually prove its AI/operations results. الغرض: قياس قدرة الشركة على إثبات النتائج.
- **Inputs** — do you track baselines, log decisions, separate estimated vs verified value, keep approval records (yes/no/partial).
- **Output / score logic (illustrative)** — a Proof Readiness band (Weak / Partial / Strong) with the specific gaps named. Aligns with [../07_proof_os/PROOF_SCORE.md](../07_proof_os/PROOF_SCORE.md).
- **CTA** — Command Sprint (the Sprint closes proof gaps).
- **Lead capture** — name, work email, company, sector, consent.
- **Status: FUTURE.**

---

## Tool D — AI Governance Checklist — قائمة حوكمة الذكاء الاصطناعي

- **Purpose** — let a company self-check PDPL-aware, governed AI use. الغرض: تقييم ذاتي للحوكمة.
- **Inputs** — checklist across data handling, approval gates, no auto-send, audit logging, PII classification.
- **Output / score logic (illustrative)** — a Governance Readiness checklist result with prioritized fixes. Aligns with [../05_governance_os/GOVERNANCE_OS.md](../05_governance_os/GOVERNANCE_OS.md) and [../14_trust_os/TRUST_PACK.md](../14_trust_os/TRUST_PACK.md).
- **CTA** — Diagnostic.
- **Lead capture** — name, work email, company, sector, consent.
- **Status: FUTURE.**
