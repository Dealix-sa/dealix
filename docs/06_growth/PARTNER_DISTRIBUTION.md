## Why partners — لماذا الشركاء

Saudi advisors already sit beside the decision-maker on revenue, compliance, and systems. They extend reach without cold outreach. Partners introduce; Dealix delivers the Command Sprint and the Proof Pack. This is loop L5 in [SELF_GROWTH_OS.md](./SELF_GROWTH_OS.md).

المستشارون السعوديون قريبون من صانع القرار. يوسّعون الوصول بلا تواصل بارد. الشريك يُعرّف، وDealix يُسلّم السبرنت وحزمة الإثبات.

## Partner types — أنواع الشركاء

| Type | What they bring | Fit |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing / growth agencies | Client relationships, demand context | High |
| Management consultants | Trust, operations access | High |
| Accountants / ZATCA advisors | Compliance proximity, finance data context | High |
| System integrators | Implementation reach, IT access | Medium |

ZATCA and PDPL advisors are a natural fit: their clients already feel the compliance and revenue pain Dealix addresses.

---

## Partner offer — عرض الشريك

- **Qualified introduction** to a Saudi/GCC company that fits the Command Sprint. تعريف مؤهل.
- **Co-delivery option** where the partner stays in the relationship. تسليم مشترك.
- **Revenue share** on closed Sprints and retainers — terms FUTURE, defined per partner agreement, never undisclosed kickbacks. حصة إيراد (مستقبلي).
- **Enablement kit** so partners can position Dealix accurately. حقيبة تمكين.

No partner is paid for cold outreach, scraped lists, or spam. Compensation rewards a real, consenting introduction.

---

## Co-sell motion — حركة البيع المشترك

```
1. Partner identifies a fitting client (consenting, real relationship).
2. Partner shares the named pain; routes to Business OS Score or Diagnostic.
3. Dealix runs the Diagnostic; scopes the Command Sprint.
4. Sprint delivered; Proof Pack issued (estimated/verified tiers honest).
5. Partner stays for co-delivery or retainer continuity.
6. Revenue share applied per the partner agreement (FUTURE terms).
```

Every external message in this motion is a founder-approved draft, per [../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md](../05_governance_os/APPROVAL_POLICY.md). One CTA per touch.

## Enablement — التمكين

- **Positioning one-pager:** Dealix is a Business OS; Revenue OS is the first wedge — not a chatbot or agency.
- **Approved language:** mirrors [../05_governance_os/CLAIM_SAFETY.md](../05_governance_os/CLAIM_SAFETY.md); no guaranteed-revenue claims.
- **Co-branded case-safe summary** (when proof exists, anonymized, founder-approved).
- **Routing card:** which CTA fits which client signal (Score / Diagnostic / Command Sprint).
- **Compliance brief:** PDPL-aware handling, no scraping, no auto-send.

التمكين: صفحة تموضع، لغة معتمدة، ملخص آمن مشترك، بطاقة توجيه، وموجز امتثال.

---

## Guardrails — الضوابط

- No partner does cold WhatsApp automation, scraping, or bulk outreach on Dealix's behalf.
- No undisclosed incentives; partners disclose the referral relationship to the client.
- No partner publishes a Dealix claim or case without founder approval.
- No guaranteed-revenue language in any partner material.

لا تواصل بارد ولا حوافز غير مُفصح عنها ولا نشر دون موافقة المؤسس ولا ادعاءات مضمونة.
