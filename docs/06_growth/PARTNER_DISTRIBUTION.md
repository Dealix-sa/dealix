# Partner Distribution — توزيع عبر الشركاء

> Loop 7. Partners extend reach **after** delivery quality is boringly consistent. No spam, no
> scraping, no cold automation. Inherits `docs/growth/PARTNER_STRATEGY.md`. Status: `DOCS_ONLY`.
> Partner program surfaces: `frontend/[locale]/partners`, `landing/partners.html`, `agency-partner.html`.

## المبدأ (Principle)

Dealix is the **governed AI operations layer** partners can attach to their existing client
relationships — not a chatbot they resell. Partners bring trusted reach; Dealix owns governance
and the Proof Pack. We recruit partners through honest applications and warm relationships, never
by scraping or cold-blasting agencies.

## ملف الشريك المثالي (Ideal partner profiles)

- CRM / RevOps consultants
- ERP / accounting firms
- Marketing agencies (outbound-sensitive — align on governance first)
- GRC / cybersecurity consultants
- Training companies, general business/ops consultants

## النماذج (Models)

| Model | What partner does | Dealix owns | When allowed |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|--------------|
| Referral | Warm intro to a fit client | Delivery + proof + governance | Always (transparent tiers) |
| Co-sell | Joint Diagnostic/Sprint, written SOW | Governance + Proof Pack | When delivery is repeatable |
| White-label Sprint | Fronts the engagement | QA + governance + proof | Only when QA is boringly consistent |

Referral/share terms are written, transparent, and tied to a real closed engagement — never a
kickback for a name on a list.

## حلقة التوزيع (Distribution loop)

```text
Partner intro → Diagnostic (rung 0) → Sprint (499) → Proof Pack
   → co-branded case study (founder + partner approval) → more partner intros
```

The co-branded, approved Proof Pack is the asset that earns the *next* partner intro. That is the
loop — proof recruits partners, partners produce proof.

## ما لا نفعله (What we never do)

- No scraping partner directories or LinkedIn; no cold WhatsApp/LinkedIn automation.
- No guaranteed revenue to partners or their clients.
- No publishing a partner's or their client's name/logo without written approval.
- No white-label before QA is repeatable (`docs/growth/PARTNER_STRATEGY.md`).

## تفعيل الشريك (Enablement)

- Partner one-pager: positioning, ladder, what Dealix refuses (sets honest expectations).
- A Diagnostic link the partner can share (routes to `/dealix-diagnostic`).
- Proof Pack sample (`landing/sprint-sample.html`, `landing/proof.html`) for credibility.
