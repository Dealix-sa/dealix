# Messaging Hierarchy | تسلسل الرسائل

> One brand, one promise, three layers of message that scale from billboard to footnote.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

The messaging hierarchy gives every team a structured way to choose **the right level of message for the right surface**. It prevents drift, repetition, and accidental over-claiming.

---

## 2. The Three Layers | الطبقات الثلاث

| Layer | What it says | Where it lives |
|---|---|---|
| **L1 — Brand promise** | The single, durable promise. | Tagline lockup, OG, hero, business cards. |
| **L2 — Positioning frame** | What we are, for whom, against what. | Hero subhead, sales deck slot 1, investor slot 2, landing page H1+H2. |
| **L3 — Capability proof** | What we do, how we evidence it. | Product pages, case studies, demo, RFP responses. |

---

## 3. Layer 1 — Brand Promise | الطبقة الأولى

**Tagline (English):** INTELLIGENT DEALS. REAL GROWTH.
**Tagline (Arabic):** صفقات ذكية. نمو حقيقي.

**Promise (one line):** Dealix gives Saudi B2B teams an approval-gated Revenue Operating System that ships intelligent, evidenced deals — without ever auto-sending, auto-publishing, or auto-committing on the customer's behalf.

L1 never changes without Founder + Brand Lead approval.

---

## 4. Layer 2 — Positioning Frame | الطبقة الثانية

**Frame (English):**
"For Saudi B2B teams, Dealix is the Revenue Operating Company — an approval-gated Revenue OS plus embedded operators plus an evidence control plane — built for bilingual, relationship-led, evidence-gated markets."

**Frame (Arabic):**
"ديليكس هي شركة تشغيل الإيرادات لقطاع B2B في السعودية — نظام تشغيل إيرادات بموافقتك، مع مشغّلين مدمجين، وطبقة أدلة قابلة للتدقيق — مبنية للسوق ثنائي اللغة، القائم على العلاقات والأدلة."

L2 has three swappable parts:
1. **Audience:** "Saudi B2B teams" → can specialize ("Saudi B2B founders," "Saudi enterprise commercial directors").
2. **Category:** "Revenue Operating Company" → never change.
3. **Proof of fit:** "bilingual, relationship-led, evidence-gated" → keep three, can reorder.

---

## 5. Layer 3 — Capability Proof | الطبقة الثالثة

Capability statements always follow the **Dealix sentence pattern**: outcome → approval gate → time/evidence.

Examples:
- "Ten qualified meetings, drafted for your approval, in 30 days — across 7 KSA pilots, with full proof pack."
- "A bilingual outreach motion you control, with every send queued for approval and every action signed."
- "A pipeline you can audit — every draft, decision, and approval logged in the evidence control plane."

Capability statements always carry:
- A **range**, not a point estimate.
- A **condition** (ICP density, stage, channel mix).
- A **pilot N** or source.

## 7. Proof Statement Pattern | نمط بيان الأدلة

```
<Outcome>  +  <Approval gate>  +  <Time/condition>  +  <Source/N>
```

Example:
"6–12 qualified meetings (outcome) drafted for your approval (gate) within 30 days at ICP density ≥ X (condition) across 7 KSA pilots (source)."

---

## 8. Headline Patterns | أنماط العناوين

**Pattern A — Outcome verb:**
"Turn warm relationships into intelligent deals."
"Run a 30-day Revenue Sprint without a single auto-send."

**Pattern B — Reframe:**
"Pipeline you can audit. Outreach you can defend."
"From spreadsheet to Revenue OS — with your approval on every step."

**Pattern C — Trust-first:**
"Approval-gated by design. Bilingual by default. Evidence-led by contract."

---

## 9. Don'ts | ما لا نفعله

- Don't put L3 (specific numbers) in L1 surfaces (tagline, OG, cover).
- Don't use L1 (tagline) inside a body paragraph — it loses its weight.
- Don't restate the same L2 frame three times on one page; rotate the swappable parts.
- Don't ship L3 without a range + condition + source.
- Don't add a fourth layer; if you feel you need one, you are repeating L3.

---

## 10. Non-Negotiables Embedded | الخطوط الحمراء

Every layer respects:
- No external sending claims.
- No proof publishing without approval.
- No guaranteed revenue claims (use ranges + conditions + N).
- No pricing/contract commitments without escalation.
- No autonomous-agent framing.

## 12. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Founder + Marketing Lead.
- **KPI:** Hierarchy compliance score on sampled assets (target ≥ 90%).
- **KPI:** % of inbound conversations where prospect repeats L2 frame (target ≥ 60%).
- **KPI:** Zero non-negotiable violations across hierarchy uses (audited monthly).
