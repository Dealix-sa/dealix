> **For Saudi B2B teams** who need pipeline they can trust, **Dealix is the approval-gated Revenue Operating Company** that turns warm relationships and intent signals into intelligent deals — without ever auto-sending, auto-publishing, or auto-committing on your behalf.

## 1. Positioning Statement | بيان التموضع

**Long form:**
For Saudi B2B founders, GMs, and revenue leaders who need a defensible pipeline motion, Dealix is the Revenue Operating Company that combines an approval-gated revenue operating system, embedded operators, and an evidence control plane — so every outbound action, proof, and commitment is drafted by Dealix and approved by you.

Unlike generic CRMs, sales-engagement tools, or "AI SDR" point products, Dealix is built for Saudi B2B reality: bilingual-native, relationship-led, evidence-gated, and locally compliant.

## 4. Problem We Solve | المشكلة

Saudi B2B teams face three structural problems no Western tool solves:

1. **Volume-based automation kills trust.** Auto-sent sequences burn relationships in a market where deals close through warm intros.
2. **Bilingual outreach is an afterthought.** Generic tools treat Arabic as a translation problem; it is a craft problem.
3. **There is no evidence layer.** Founders and compliance teams need to see what was sent, drafted, or approved — and by whom.

Result: pipeline that is brittle, untrusted, and hard to audit.

---

## 5. What Dealix Does | ما تفعله ديليكس

Dealix runs an **approval-gated Revenue OS** with three operating layers:

1. **Signal layer** — captures intent, warm relationships, and ICP movement.
2. **Draft layer** — produces bilingual, ICP-tuned drafts (email, LinkedIn, partner intros).
3. **Approval & evidence layer** — every draft is queued for human approval; every action is signed and auditable.

The work is shipped through productized rungs (Free Diagnostic → Revenue Sprint → Managed Pilot → Revenue Desk Retainer → Founder Console → Enterprise Revenue OS → Partner/White-label). See `docs/product/DEALIX_PRODUCT_LADDER.md`.

## 9. Non-Negotiables Embedded in Positioning | الخطوط الحمراء

Every positioning surface (deck, landing, deck, email) must reinforce:

- **No external sending** — Dealix drafts, customer approves.
- **No proof publishing without approval** — every customer name is opt-in.
- **No guaranteed revenue claims** — ranges, conditions, pilot N only.
- **No pricing/contract commitments without escalation** — human owner closes.
- **Approval-gated automation only** — never "autonomous."
