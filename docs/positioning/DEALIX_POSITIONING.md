# Dealix Positioning

> The single positioning statement that every Dealix surface must align to.

## 1. Positioning statement

> **For** Saudi B2B founders and revenue leaders
> **who** operate high-ticket motions across fragmented tools,
> **Dealix is** the Saudi B2B **Revenue Operating System**
> **that** connects market intelligence, distribution, deal flow, delivery, finance and proof under one trust-gated AI layer.
> **Unlike** point tools (CRMs, sales engagement, marketing automation, AI SDRs),
> **Dealix is** an *operating system* — every action ladders to cash, every output is founder-approved, every signal is auditable.

## 3. Audience map

| Audience | Pain | Trigger | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder | Tools don't talk. AI is untrusted. | Plateaued growth, unscalable founder time | Operate the company from one console |
| Head of Revenue / Sales | Pipeline theatrics, low close rates | Quarter miss, board pressure | Cash-collected forecast, controlled motion |
| Head of Growth | Channel-by-channel chaos | Diminishing channel returns | One signal-driven distribution layer |
| Agency / SI | Customers ask for "AI" but distrust it | Customer pressure for outcomes | Operate Dealix under their brand |

## 4. Promise architecture

```
Core promise:      Intelligent deals. Real growth.
Operating promise: Trust-gated AI execution under founder control.
Outcome promise:   Cash collected — never just leads or pipeline.
```

## 5. Anti-promises

We **do not** promise:

- A specific revenue lift.
- A specific number of leads, meetings or replies.
- "Hands-off" growth.
- Replacement of any team member.

We do promise:

- A clear operating system.
- Drafts, queues, scores and recommendations on a schedule.
- Audit-grade visibility on every action.
- Founder-approved external action only.

## 6. Why this positioning wins

1. **It is not a feature claim.** It is a category claim.
2. **It is bilingual-native.** It works in AR and EN equally.
3. **It is defensible.** Operating systems beat tools when founders mature.
4. **It is provable.** Every claim has a surface in the console.
5. **It is trustworthy.** It explicitly limits autonomy — which is rare and credible.
