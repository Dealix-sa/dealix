# Category Creation OS | نظام بناء الفئة

> Dealix is not entering an existing category. We are naming and operating a new one: the **Revenue Operating Company** for Saudi B2B.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

The Category Creation OS is the playbook for how Dealix establishes, defends, and scales a new market category. It exists because trying to fit Dealix into "CRM," "sales engagement," or "AI SDR" misframes the buyer's problem and forfeits pricing power.

---

## 2. The Category We Are Building | الفئة التي نبنيها

**Category name:** Revenue Operating Company (ROC)
**One-line definition:** A company that runs the approval-gated revenue operating system on behalf of a Saudi B2B team — combining software, operators, and proof.

We are not:
- A CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- A sales engagement tool (Outreach, Salesloft)
- An "AI SDR" point tool
- An agency (project-based, hour-based)

We are: **a company that operates your revenue function with you, on an approval-gated OS, with evidenced outcomes.**

---

## 3. Why a New Category | لماذا فئة جديدة

| Existing category | Why it fails Saudi B2B | What we change |
|---|---|---|
| CRM | Records data; does not produce deals. | We produce drafts, decisions, and deals. |
| Sales engagement | Auto-sends at scale; erodes trust in KSA's relationship-led market. | Approval-gated only; no external sending. |
| AI SDR | Treats outreach as volume; not auditable; brand-risk. | Treats outreach as evidenced, approval-gated craft. |
| Agency | Hour-based; not productized; no software leverage. | Productized rungs; software + operator + proof. |
| Consulting | Slides, not deals. | Deals shipped, not slides delivered. |

---

## 4. Category Frame | إطار الفئة

The category frame Dealix uses in every narrative:

> "**Saudi B2B revenue is relationship-led, evidence-gated, and bilingual.** Generic Western automation breaks all three. Dealix is the Revenue Operating Company built for this reality — approval-gated, bilingual-native, evidence-led."

This frame:
- Reframes the buyer's problem (trust + evidence, not volume).
- Disqualifies generic tools without naming competitors.
- Anchors Dealix as the **default** local answer.

---

## 5. Pillars of the Category | ركائز الفئة

1. **Approval-gated automation** — every action is queued for human approval.
2. **Bilingual-native** — Arabic and English equal-first, locally written.
3. **Evidence control plane** — every output is auditable, signed, and traceable.
4. **Productized rungs** — clear ladder from Free Diagnostic to Enterprise Revenue OS.
5. **Operator + software hybrid** — we run it with you, not just sell you a tool.

## 8. Customer Language Drift Watch | مراقبة الانحراف اللغوي

We monitor whether prospects describe Dealix using our vocabulary or someone else's. Drift signals a positioning problem.

- **Healthy signal:** "It's an approval-gated revenue OS for Saudi B2B."
- **Drift signal:** "It's like Outreach but local," or "It's an AI SDR."

When drift exceeds 30% of new conversations, the category frame is re-shipped across surfaces.

---

## 9. Defending the Category | حماية الفئة

- We own the term **"Revenue Operating Company"** in KSA discourse — first to publish, first to define, first to pattern.
- We resist being benchmarked against tool categories ("vs. Outreach") by always reframing to the operating company comparison.
- We invest in **proof** (case studies, signed evidence) over **claims** (badges, ratings).
- We refuse to dilute the category by adding off-category features (e.g., generic CRM, ticketing).

---

## 10. Non-Negotiables in Category Work | الخطوط الحمراء

- No guaranteed revenue claims in category essays or decks.
- No customer logo or quote used in category content without written customer approval.
- No "we'll send for you" framing — always "we draft, you approve."
- No pricing/commitments without escalation in category-led sales motions.
- No autonomous-agent framing — the category is **approval-gated**.

---
