# Content Calendar System | نظام التقويم التحريري

> A repeatable, bilingual content rhythm — built around category education, founder voice, and approved proof.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

A predictable calendar that turns positioning into a steady stream of bilingual artifacts, keeps the team accountable, and gives partners and customers something to anticipate.

---

## 2. Inputs | المدخلات

- Quarterly category themes (set by Founder + Marketing Lead).
- Approved customer proof artifacts.
- Product release notes from the rung roadmap.
- Saudi business calendar (Hijri + Gregorian, fiscal quarters, sector events).
- Founder field notes (operator stories from active pilots).

---

## 3. Outputs | المخرجات

- Weekly content slate with owner, channel, language, status.
- Monthly editorial brief.
- Quarterly category essay.
- Monthly proof artifact (when customer approval is secured).

---

## 4. Cadence Grid | شبكة الإيقاع

| Asset | Cadence | Channel | Language |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder LinkedIn post | 3–5 / week | LinkedIn | EN + AR (alternating or paired) |
| Newsletter | 1 / week | Email | Bilingual single issue |
| Long-form essay | 1 / month | Site + newsletter | EN, with AR companion |
| Operator field note | 2 / month | LinkedIn + newsletter | AR-first |
| Customer proof artifact | ≥ 1 / month | Site + LinkedIn | EN + AR |
| Category essay | 1 / quarter | Site + LinkedIn + PR | EN + AR |
| Partner co-marketing piece | 1 / quarter per partner | Site + partner channels | EN + AR |
| Roundtable / event | 1 / quarter | In-person, KSA | AR-first |
| Podcast guesting | 2 / month | External | Mostly EN, AR when available |

---

## 5. Theme Pillars | المحاور

Every piece maps to one of five themes, drawn from the brand pillars:

1. **Trust by design** — approval-gating, evidence control plane, compliance.
2. **Growth that compounds** — operator stories of pipeline becoming deals.
3. **Closing the deal** — practical KSA B2B closing patterns and pitfalls.
4. **Results, not noise** — what we measure, what we ignore.
5. **Global mindset, local impact** — KSA-first craft with portable lessons.

Distribution target: roughly 25 / 25 / 20 / 15 / 15.

---

## 6. Weekly Slate Template | قالب الجدول الأسبوعي

| Day | Asset | Theme | Channel | Owner | Language | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | Field note | Trust by design | LinkedIn | Founder | AR | Drafted |
| Tue | Newsletter | Mixed | Email | Marketing | Bilingual | In review |
| Wed | Operator post | Closing the deal | LinkedIn | Founder | EN | Scheduled |
| Thu | Customer-approved snippet | Growth | LinkedIn + site | Marketing | EN + AR | Approval pending |
| Sun | Long-form section | Local impact | Site | Founder | EN | Drafting |

Status flow: `briefed → drafting → in review → approved → scheduled → published → logged`.

---

## 7. Monthly Editorial Brief | الملخص الشهري

Each month opens with a one-page brief covering:
- Theme weighting for the month.
- Customer proofs eligible for publication (with approval status).
- Calendar moments (Saudi events, sector conferences, Hijri events).
- Product/feature beats from the rung roadmap.
- Reframes to push (category, why-now, competitive narrative refreshers).

---

## 8. Approval Workflow | سير الموافقة

```
Brief  →  Draft (EN or AR)  →  Native bilingual pass
      →  Brand gate  →  Voice gate  →  Accessibility gate
      →  Non-negotiable gate  →  Function approver
      →  (If customer named) Customer approval
      →  Schedule  →  Publish  →  Log in registry
```

If a piece mentions a customer, includes a quote, screenshot, logo, or specific numbers from a customer engagement, **written customer approval is mandatory** before publish.

---

## 9. Bilingual Discipline | الانضباط الثنائي

- Arabic is authored, not translated.
- A piece is "ready" only when both language versions clear voice and brand gates.
- Numerals: Western Arabic (0–9) by default in both languages; Eastern (٠–٩) only in editorial Arabic upon approval.
- Cross-locale terms (Dealix, Revenue OS, Founder Console) stay in Latin script inside Arabic.

---

## 10. Production Roles | أدوار الإنتاج

| Role | Owns |
|---|---|
| Founder | Voice, field notes, category essays |
| Content Lead | Briefs, drafts, schedule, registry |
| Bilingual Editor | AR authoring + EN copy edit |
| Brand Lead | Visual + brand gate |
| Marketing Lead | Channel mix, distribution, partner co-marketing |
| Partnerships Lead | Partner-led pieces, customer approvals |

---

## 11. Tools | الأدوات

- Editorial board (Notion or Linear) reflecting the weekly slate template.
- Figma "Dealix Marketing" templates for visuals.
- Asset registry (single source of truth) in `assets/marketing/` with the naming convention from `DEALIX_MARKETING_ASSET_GUIDE.md`.

---

## 12. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending of content marketing without recipient consent.
- No publishing of customer-identifying material without written approval.
- No guaranteed-revenue framing in any calendar piece.
- No pricing or contract commitments in calendar pieces.
- No autonomous-agent framing.

---

## 13. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Content Lead, reporting to Marketing Lead.
- **KPI:** Slate completion rate (target ≥ 90% weekly).
- **KPI:** Bilingual parity rate on shipped pieces (target 100%).
- **KPI:** Number of approved customer proof artifacts per month (target ≥ 1).
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations (target 0).
