# Content Ideas Backlog

A continuously refreshed list of content ideas, scored on three axes:

- `intent_match` (does it match buyer intent?)
- `proof_strength` (do we have evidence to support it?)
- `differentiation` (does it sound like Dealix, not a generic vendor?)

Backlog lives in `marketing/content_ideas.csv`. Ideas with a composite score above the threshold and a proof pointer become candidate calendar entries.

## Sources

- Sales calls transcripts (with consent)
- Sector signals from the intelligence layer
- Customer questions captured in `customer_success/client_health.csv`
- Public market events tracked by the founder
