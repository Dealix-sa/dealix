# Events and Webinars

Dealix runs a small, deliberate event motion: one founder-led webinar per quarter, plus selective participation in Saudi GTM events.

## Cadence

- Q1: Sector deep-dive (one sector chosen from growth/sector_targets.csv)
- Q2: Founder + customer joint webinar (requires proof approval)
- Q3: Saudi GTM benchmarks release (data-grounded)
- Q4: 2027 outlook

## Operating rules

- All webinars open with the Dealix policy promise (no guaranteed claims, evidence-led).
- Customer participation requires signed approval in `proof/proof_approval_queue.csv`.
- Recordings are stored in private ops, not public, until founder approval.
