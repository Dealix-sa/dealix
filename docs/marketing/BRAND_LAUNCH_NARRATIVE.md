# Brand Launch Narrative

The story Dealix tells when it enters a new conversation, a new market, or a new sector.

## Frame

1. **Tension.** Saudi B2B GTM teams have more data than ever, and less clarity than ever.
2. **Choice.** They can keep manually stitching tools, or they can let an evidence-led system do it.
3. **Resolution.** Dealix is the revenue intelligence layer — intelligent deals, real growth, built on trust.

## Tone

- Confident, never boastful.
- Evidence-led, never speculative.
- Bilingual by default — Arabic and English parity on every public surface.

## Forbidden phrases

See `apps/web/lib/brand-tokens.ts` and `docs/brand/brand-tokens.json` — they enumerate the disallowed claims (guaranteed revenue, 100% ROI, نضمن, زيادة مضمونة, أرباح مضمونة).
