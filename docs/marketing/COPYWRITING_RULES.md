# Copywriting Rules

## 1. Read first

- `docs/brand/DEALIX_BRAND_VOICE.md`
- `docs/positioning/MESSAGING_HIERARCHY.md`

If anything below conflicts with those, those win.

## 2. Sentence-level rules

- ≤ 22 words by default.
- One idea per sentence.
- Active voice.
- Prefer concrete nouns and verbs over adjectives.

## 3. Word-level rules

- Always: "deal", "growth", "cash", "operating system", "founder", "trust-gated", "approved", "audited", "Saudi B2B".
- Never: "guarantee", "10x", "magic", "ninja", "rockstar", "synergy", "world-class" (used about us), "auto-pilot".

## 4. Numbers

- Cite source for every number.
- Round responsibly.
- Western digits unless contract specifies otherwise.

## 5. Bilingual

- Write AR natively. Do not translate from EN.
- Do not transliterate names without need.
- Honour gender and formality conventions of the audience.

## 6. CTA copy

- Action verb + object.
- "Open the Console", "Get the Diagnostic", "See the Trust Center", "Read the Sector Report".
- Avoid "Learn more" except as a tertiary text link.

## 7. Headlines

- 3-7 words for hero.
- Lead with promise or category, not feature.
- Tagline reserved for brand surfaces only.

## 8. Tone matrix

| Audience | Tone |
|---|---|
| Founder | Calm, respectful, evidence-led |
| RevOps | Operator-tight, metric-led |
| Procurement | Process-led, evidence-led |
| Partner | Plain, partnership-led |

## 9. Quality gates

- Brand Guardian voice eval.
- Trust check (no guarantees, no unverified claims).
- Bilingual symmetry check.
