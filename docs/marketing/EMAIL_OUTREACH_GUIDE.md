# Email Outreach Guide

How Dealix drafts cold/warm email outreach. Drafts only. Founder approves every send.

## 1. Doctrine

- Maximum 110 words for first-touch.
- Always cite at least one piece of public evidence (a post, an article, a job ad).
- Always offer one specific next step (sample, diagnostic, 15-min call).
- Bilingual: include AR + EN in the body **only if the persona's preferred language is bilingual**. Otherwise pick one.
- No attachments on first touch (unless explicitly requested).
- No tracking pixels (we may use opens-as-signal, never pixels-without-consent).
- One unsubscribe path, honoured permanently.

## 2. Subject line rules

- ≤ 8 words.
- Sector-specific or observation-specific.
- No "RE:" / "FW:" tricks.
- No emoji.
- No exclamation marks.

Good examples:

- "KSA logistics — open pipeline question"
- "Saudi B2B agency growth — quick observation"
- "Cybersecurity ABM — 7-day sprint?"

Bad examples:

- "🔥 Game-changing AI for your business!!!"
- "RE: Your revenue"
- "URGENT — Limited spots"

## 3. Body structure

1. **Personalisation line** (1–2 lines). Cite evidence.
2. **Observation** (1 line). Connect to a recurring pattern.
3. **Offer** (1–2 lines). Specific. Time-boxed. Bilingual if relevant.
4. **Trust note** (1 line). Founder-approved. Kill switch.
5. **CTA** (1 line). Ask for one thing.

## 4. Follow-up rules

- Maximum 3 follow-ups per thread.
- 7 days minimum between follow-ups.
- Each follow-up brings new value (a sample, a sector insight, a relevant observation) — not a "checking in".
- After 3 follow-ups without reply, move to nurture stream.

## 5. Refusal patterns

The draft is refused if:

- It has no evidence citation.
- It uses banned phrases.
- It addresses an unsubscribed contact.
- It violates the persona's refused-offer list.
- It exceeds the length cap.

## 6. Reply routing

The Reply Router Machine classifies replies into:

- **Positive — book the call.**
- **Positive — needs sample.**
- **Neutral — re-engage in 30 days.**
- **Negative — close + add to anti-ICP.**
- **Out of office** — re-send in 7 days.

Every classification is founder-reviewable.

## 7. Schema

Drafts live in `data/marketing/outreach_drafts.csv`. Replies live in `data/marketing/outreach_replies.csv`.

## 8. Compliance posture

- KSA PDPL-aware.
- No data brokers without documented compliance.
- One unsubscribe link, honoured.
- All outbound is founder-approved per send.
