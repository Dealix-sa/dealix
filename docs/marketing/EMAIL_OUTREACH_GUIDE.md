# Email Outreach Guide

> Outbound email is a craft. This guide is the rulebook.

## 1. Pre-flight checklist

- [ ] Account is in ICP segments.
- [ ] Account is not in suppression list.
- [ ] At least one trigger event or first-party signal cited.
- [ ] Persona is matched.
- [ ] Draft is bilingual where the segment requires it.
- [ ] Brand check passed.
- [ ] Founder approval recorded.

If any box is unchecked, **do not send**.

## 2. Subject line

- 4-8 words.
- Specific. Avoid clickbait.
- No emojis.
- No urgency words.

Examples (EN):
- "Riyadh expansion — one number from a peer"
- "Cybersecurity reply quality — pattern from last week"

## 3. Opening

- One line, ≤ 20 words.
- Cite the trigger or first-party signal.
- No "Hope you're well."

## 4. Body

- 90-160 words.
- One observation, one relevance line, one ask.
- The ask is concrete: a 15-minute conversation, or a one-page diagnostic.

## 5. Close

- No "circle back".
- No "looping in" mass-CC.
- A polite single line if no reply within cadence cap.

## 6. Signature

- Founder or operator's real name.
- Dealix role.
- One link only (to a specific surface relevant to the message).

## 7. Cadence

See `EMAIL_DRAFT_MACHINE.md` for step-by-step cadence rules.

## 8. Compliance

- Honour opt-outs immediately.
- Respect KSA / GCC anti-spam expectations (no purchased lists).
- Include physical or business identifier when sending bulk-style updates.

## 9. Bilingual

If the persona signal is mixed, send AR + EN in a single email with a brief language note at the top.
