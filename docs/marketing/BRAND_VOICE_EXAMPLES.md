# Brand Voice Examples

> Concrete examples — and what's wrong with the bad ones.

## 1. Hero

✅ "Intelligent Deals. Real Growth. The Saudi B2B Revenue Operating System."
❌ "Revolutionary AI that 10x's your sales pipeline overnight."

## 2. Sub-line

✅ "Connect intelligence to action to cash — under founder control, with trust-gated AI execution."
❌ "Our agents work 24/7 so you don't have to."

## 3. CTA

✅ "Open the Founder Console"
✅ "Get the Diagnostic"
❌ "Sign up for free trial now"

## 4. LinkedIn post

✅ "Five things we learned running the Distribution machine for a KSA SaaS last week — none of them involve more sends. Thread."
❌ "Just sent 1000 outbound emails using AI while you slept. DM if you want the same."

## 5. Cold email opening

✅ "Saw you opened a Riyadh office in Q1. I work with KSA B2B operators on the deal-flow side; thought one number from a peer might be useful."
❌ "Hope you're doing well! Just wanted to quickly hop on a call."

## 6. Proposal opening

✅ "This proposal scopes a two-week Revenue Sprint focused on the bottleneck we identified together: low reply quality on outbound for the cybersecurity sector."
❌ "We're excited to partner with you and guarantee 30 % more leads in 30 days."

## 7. Error message

✅ "We couldn't reach the data store. The action is queued and will retry. No external send happened."
❌ "Unknown error. Please contact support."

## 8. Trust copy

✅ "Every external action is queued for founder approval. Every approval, decline, and outcome is logged."
❌ "Our AI is safe. Trust us."

## 9. Internal CLI

✅ "drafts queued: 12; suppression honoured: 100%; approvals open: 3"
❌ "All systems nominal 🚀🔥"

## 10. Arabic versions (parity)

✅ "صفقات ذكية. نمو حقيقي. نظام التشغيل الإيرادي لسوق B2B السعودي."
❌ "ذكاءٌ اصطناعي ثوري يُضاعف مبيعاتك ١٠ أضعاف بين ليلة وضحاها."
