# Brand Voice Examples

The Brand Voice is "executive Saudi business" — clear, decisive, evidence-forward. This doc shows what that sounds like in practice and what it sounds like when it goes wrong.

**Source of truth:** this doc + `docs/marketing/COPYWRITING_RULES.md`
**Owner:** Brand Guardian (`docs/ai/BRAND_GUARDIAN_AGENT.md`)
**Trust gate:** A1 — examples are reviewed monthly.

## Hero copy

**On brand (EN):** "Dealix is the Revenue Operating System for Saudi B2B teams. We don't promise revenue. We build the factory that makes revenue measurable."

**On brand (AR):** "Dealix هو نظام تشغيل الإيرادات لفرق B2B في السعودية. لا نَعِد بإيرادات؛ نبني المصنع الذي يجعل الإيرادات قابلة للقياس."

**Off brand:** "Supercharge your sales with AI-powered automation. Get guaranteed pipeline in 30 days."

**Why off brand:** uses "supercharge", "AI-powered", "guaranteed", and promises pipeline. Three rule breaks in one sentence.

## Proof line

**On brand (EN):** "Case-safe pattern: a mid-market industrial supplier increased qualified conversations from an estimated 3 per week to an estimated 9 per week over an eight-week Revenue Sprint. Verified figures shared with the active client."

**On brand (AR):** "نمط آمن من حالة سابقة: ارتفعت المحادثات المؤهَّلة لمورّد صناعي متوسط الحجم من 3 إلى 9 أسبوعيًا (تقديريًا) خلال سبرنت إيرادات مدته ثمانية أسابيع. الأرقام المُتحقَّقة تُشارَك مع العميل النشط."

**Off brand:** "Our customers see a 300% increase in sales conversion in their first month."

**Why off brand:** unsourced stat, unverified attribution, manufactured magnitude.

## Founder post opener

**On brand (EN):** "I spent three hours this week with a Riyadh founder whose pipeline was full but whose cash was empty. The bottleneck was not in sourcing. It was in invoice cycle time."

**On brand (AR):** "قضيت ثلاث ساعات هذا الأسبوع مع مؤسس في الرياض، خط الأنابيب لديه ممتلئ والنقدية فارغة. لم تكن نقطة الاختناق في توليد العملاء، بل في زمن دورة الفاتورة."

**Off brand:** "Excited to share that we're transforming B2B sales in Saudi Arabia with our AI-powered platform."

**Why off brand:** no observation, no specifics, three banned words.

## CTA

**On brand:** "Book a 30-minute diagnostic call. We'll deliver a three-page sector-specific sample within five business days."

**Off brand:** "Get started today — no commitment, instant results!"

**Why off brand:** "instant" is a guarantee. "Get started" is empty.

## Sector-report line

**On brand:** "In a sample of twelve Saudi industrial suppliers, the median time from invoice issuance to cash collection was 41 days. Methodology and aggregation in the appendix."

**Off brand:** "Industrial suppliers wait forever to get paid. We fix this."

**Why off brand:** unsourced exaggeration; implicit guarantee.

## When responding to "what do you guarantee?"

**On brand:** "We guarantee a Revenue Factory: every stage instrumented, every external action gated, every metric tagged Estimated or Verified. We do not guarantee revenue. The market and your team determine revenue; we determine whether the factory runs."

**Off brand:** "We guarantee at least 10 qualified meetings in the first month, or your money back."

**Why off brand:** guarantee creep + manufactured refund anchor.

## Disclaimer line (mandatory)

Every external artifact carrying a numeric claim ends with:

"Estimated value is not Verified value / القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة"

## Failure modes

- **Mimicry drift:** a draft sounds like a generic SaaS landing page. Detection: Brand Guardian. Recovery: rewrite with concrete Saudi business detail.
- **Translation, not parallel:** AR copy reads as a literal translation. Detection: Arabic reviewer. Recovery: AR rewrite as parallel composition.

## Disclaimer

Voice is craft. Voice does not change outcomes. Estimated value is not Verified value.
