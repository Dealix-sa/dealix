# Brand Voice Examples | أمثلة على صوت العلامة

> Concrete, side-by-side examples that train the team — and any new contributor — on what Dealix sounds like in practice.

## 1. How to Use This Document | كيفية الاستخدام

- Read the "off-brand → on-brand" rewrites before drafting any new copy.

- Apply the editing checklist from `COPYWRITING_RULES.md` before publishing.

## 2. Landing Hero | عنوان الصفحة الرئيسية

**Off-brand**
> "Dealix is the world's leading AI-powered sales platform that revolutionizes the way modern B2B companies generate massive pipeline."

**On-brand (English)**
> The Revenue Operating Company for Saudi B2B.
> Approval-gated, bilingual-native, evidence-led — so your team ships intelligent deals without auto-sending or auto-publishing on your customers' behalf.

**On-brand (Arabic)**
> شركة تشغيل الإيرادات لقطاع B2B في السعودية.
> بموافقتك، ثنائية اللغة، مدعومة بالأدلة — لتغلق فريقك صفقات ذكية بدون إرسال أو نشر آلي بالنيابة عن عملائك.

## 3. Capability Block | كتلة القدرات

**Off-brand**
> "Dealix's AI agents autonomously prospect, send, and close deals on your behalf, 24/7."

**On-brand**
> Dealix drafts bilingual outreach, surfaces intent signals, and produces auditable proof. Every send, every commitment, every published proof is queued for your approval — and signed in the evidence control plane.

## 4. Proof Block | كتلة الأدلة

**Off-brand**
> "Our customers see 10x pipeline growth in 30 days, guaranteed."

**On-brand**
> Across 7 Saudi B2B pilots, ICP-fit accounts produced **6–12 qualified meetings in 30 days**, when ICP density was above the agreed threshold. Each proof pack was reviewed and approved by the customer before publication.

## 6. Email Outreach | بريد التواصل

**Off-brand (auto-blast)**
> Subject: Quick question
> Body: Hi {{first_name}}, are you the right person to talk about boosting your pipeline 10x?

**On-brand (drafted, queued, approved)**
> Subject: Saw your team is hiring 3 BDRs in Riyadh — quick thought
> Body: Hi Mohammed, I noticed three BDR roles posted last week in Riyadh. Most teams we work with hit a trust ceiling before a coverage ceiling — outbound that scales sends faster than it scales relationships. We run an approval-gated motion in KSA where you stay on every draft. Worth a 20-minute diagnostic this week?
> Bassam — Dealix

(Every send leaves the queue only after the customer approves.)

## 10. Product UI Microcopy | كتابة الواجهة

| State | Off-brand | On-brand |
|---|---|---|
| Empty | "No data." | "No drafts in your queue yet. Connect a signal to generate your first." |
| Loading | "Loading…" | "Drafting outreach in Arabic and English." |
| Confirm | "OK / Cancel" | "Approve draft — send queued for next window. / Decline — return to drafts." |
| Error | "Something went wrong." | "We could not send. Reason: provider rate limit. Try again in 5 minutes, or contact support@dealix.sa." |
| Approval prompt | "Send?" | "Approve sending 8 drafts now? On approve: 8 emails leave between 09:00–10:00. On decline: drafts return to the queue." |

## 11. Partner Outreach | تواصل الشركاء

**Off-brand**
> "Hey! Want to make tons of money reselling our AI?"

**On-brand**
> Hi Sarah, I'm reaching out because your agency runs the same approval-led posture we do for Saudi B2B clients. We've built a partner motion around our Revenue Operating Company — productized rungs, co-branded delivery, revenue share on shipped outcomes. Worth a 20-minute call to compare notes?

## 12. Crisis / Sensitive Reply | الردود الحساسة

**Off-brand**
> "We never do that — that must be a different vendor."

**On-brand**
> Thanks for raising this. Here is what happened, what we are doing, and the date by which you will have a written closure note. We will not publish anything about this engagement without your approval.

## 13. Bilingual Pair Discipline | الانضباط الثنائي

A piece is "ready" only when both languages clear the voice gate independently. Examples:

| English | Arabic |
|---|---|
| "Approval-gated by design." | "بموافقتك من الأساس." |
| "Bilingual by default." | "ثنائية اللغة بشكل افتراضي." |
| "Evidence-led by contract." | "مدعومة بالأدلة في كل صفقة." |
| "The Revenue Operating Company for Saudi B2B." | "شركة تشغيل الإيرادات لقطاع B2B في السعودية." |
| "Pipeline you can audit." | "خط إيرادات قابل للتدقيق." |

---

## 14. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending, no proof publishing without approval, no guaranteed revenue, no pricing/contract commitments without escalation, no autonomous framing.
