# Brand Voice Examples

Short, paired examples that show the voice working.

## 1. Landing hero

### ❌ Off-voice
> The revolutionary AI sales platform that 10x your pipeline overnight.

### ✅ On-voice
> Intelligent Deals. Real Growth. The Saudi B2B Revenue Operating
> System — trust-gated AI execution behind one founder console.

> This week the system scored 47 logistics accounts. Eight crossed
> tier A. The proposal factory drafted three retainer scopes. I
