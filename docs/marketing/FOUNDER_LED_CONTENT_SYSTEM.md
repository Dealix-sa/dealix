# Founder-Led Content System

The founder's voice is the brand voice. Everything funnels through it.

## 1. Founder posting cadence

- 2–4 LinkedIn posts / week.
- 1 sector pulse / month (longer-form).
- 1 founder note / week (internal, behind login).

## 2. Source material

- Founder's actual approvals and decisions (with anonymisation).
- Sector ranking deltas.
- Consented proof artifacts.
- Patterns from the reply router.

## 3. Format library

- **Tactical pattern** — "What I approved this week, and why."
- **Sector pulse** — "Three signals from Logistics this month."
- **Trust note** — "Why we drafted but didn't send X."
- **Mechanism note** — "How the proposal factory closed a deal in 3 days."
- **Customer outcome** (with consent) — "A pilot at [sector] ran X cycles."

## 4. Voice rules

- First-person.
- Operator-grade.
- Specific numbers, names, mechanisms.
- No guarantees, no multipliers, no claims of revolution.

## 5. Approval

Every draft sits in the queue under `marketing/content_calendar.csv`
with the founder as the sole approver.
