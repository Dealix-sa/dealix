# Founder-Led Content System

> The founder is the loudest signal Dealix has. Founder-led content is treated as a first-class operating surface.

## 1. Content shapes

| Shape | Length | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Insight | 90-160 words | Single observation from this week's operating data |
| Operating learning | 160-260 words | Lesson from a recent customer, win or loss |
| Sector pattern | 200-320 words | Pattern observed across customers in a sector |
| Reframe | 80-140 words | Reframe of a competitor or industry idea |
| Proof excerpt | 80-140 words | Excerpt of an approved customer outcome (consent-gated) |

## 2. Sources

- Operating data inside Dealix.
- Customer outcomes (only after consent).
- Sector signal from Market Domination Intelligence.
- Founder's reading and notes (curated).

## 3. Brand rules

- Voice = calm authority; never hype.
- Always cite source for any number.
- Never name a customer without consent.
- Bilingual posting cadence: at minimum one in three is AR-primary.

## 4. Workflow

```
Idea (founder or content strategist)
    ↓
Draft (content strategist)
    ↓
Brand check (Brand Guardian)
    ↓
Trust check (Trust Guardian)
    ↓
Founder review
    ↓
Scheduled / published
    ↓
Audit event
```

## 5. Performance

- Track reach, replies, saves, profile visits.
- Weekly review of which shape outperforms.
- Pattern fed back into the content calendar.
