# Founder-Led Content System

The founder is the **single most powerful distribution channel** the company has at this stage. This document is how we keep that channel healthy without burning out the founder.

## 1. Doctrine

- The founder writes (or heavily edits) every piece that goes out under their name.
- The founder's time budget for content is **20 minutes per day**, no more.
- Ideas come from the Content Engine; the founder spends time on **decisions and voice**, not blank-page writing.

## 2. Daily flow (≤ 20 minutes)

1. **Open the LinkedIn drafts queue.**
2. Review 1–2 drafts:
   - Approve as-is, or
   - Edit lightly, or
   - Reject with a one-sentence reason.
3. Publish from the founder's personal account (or schedule).
4. Add today's observation to `data/marketing/founder_observations.md` (one bullet).

The Content Engine reads `founder_observations.md` and uses recent observations to draft tomorrow's pieces.

## 3. Voice anchors

The founder's voice is:

- **Operator-grade.** Talks about pipeline coverage, deal cycle, founder calls. Not "AI" or "platform".
- **First-person.** "We saw…", "I refuse…", "Here's what worked…".
- **Bilingual switching.** Comfortable switching between AR and EN within the same week.
- **Personal but not theatrical.** No humble brags. No vulnerability theatre.

## 4. Boundaries

- **No politics, religion, or sectarian topics.**
- **No competitor naming.** We refer to "the bigger CRMs" or "the high-volume outbound tools", not to brands by name in a critical context.
- **No customer names without written approval.**
- **No financial speculation** about the company or others.

## 5. Long-form cadence

- 1 long-form (blog or LinkedIn long-post) per week.
- 1 sector observation per fortnight (medium-form).
- 1 sector report per quarter (heavy-form).

Long-form requires more time than the daily 20-minute budget — block 90 minutes once per week.

## 6. Failure modes

| Failure                                  | Recovery                                          |
|------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Founder publishes a banned phrase        | Take down; correct; document in friction log      |
| Founder publishes without approval queue | Allowed — but log retrospectively                 |
| Founder publishes a customer name        | Take down; apologise; document                    |
| Founder skips 3 days in a row            | Content Engine queues 5 alternatives next week    |

## 7. Audit

Every published piece is logged with the source draft, the approver, and the publish timestamp. The audit trail makes it possible to prove that nothing was published without founder-in-the-loop.
