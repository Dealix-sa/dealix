# LinkedIn Outreach Guide | دليل التواصل عبر لينكدإن

> LinkedIn is the primary warm-relationship surface for Saudi B2B. Dealix uses it for content distribution and 1:1 conversations — never for automation or scraping.

---

## 1. Operating Principle | المبدأ التشغيلي

- **No auto-connect or auto-DM tools.** Every connection and message is human-sent.
- **No scraping.** We do not extract or persist data outside LinkedIn's terms.
- **No mass templating.** Each DM is personal, drafted to a real signal.
- **Content first, DM second.** We earn the right to message by being useful in feed.

---

## 2. Surfaces We Use | الأسطح

| Surface | Purpose | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Founder profile | Operator voice, category education, warm pipeline | Founder |
| Company page | Category essays, customer-approved proof, hiring | Marketing |
| Employee profiles | Personal repost & operator voice | Each employee |
| Search & "people you may know" | Manual prospecting + warm intros | Founder + Sales |
| Comments | Reply to operator posts in our category | Founder + team |

## 3. Profile Optimization | تحسين الملف الشخصي

**Founder profile**
- Headline: "Building Dealix — the Revenue Operating Company for Saudi B2B."
- About: 3 short paragraphs — what Dealix is, why now, what to expect if you book a diagnostic.
- Banner: 1584×396, Deep Navy with white wordmark + one-line positioning.
- Featured: pinned category essay, pinned diagnostic CTA, one approved customer artifact.
- Profile and banner are bilingual where the platform allows (rotate language for KSA audience visibility).

**Company page**
- About is bilingual (EN + AR sections).
- Banner: 1128×191, Deep Navy with tagline lockup.
- Posting cadence is set in `CONTENT_CALENDAR_SYSTEM.md`.

**Employee profiles**
- Encouraged (not required) to use the Dealix banner template and a consistent "About" paragraph.
- All employees follow the same don'ts (no guarantees, no proof publishing without approval).

## 5. DM Workflow | سير الرسائل المباشرة

```
Identify signal (warm intro / public signal / commented on our post)
  →  Drafted DM (personal, references real signal)
  →  Self-review against the editing checklist
  →  Send (manual; one at a time)
  →  Log in CRM/Revenue OS
  →  Follow up after 5 working days if no reply (one polite nudge)
  →  Close out if no reply after two touches
```

We do not "sequence" DMs. We do not send more than two DMs to a non-responder.

---

## 6. DM Templates (use as a thinking aid, never as a paste-blast) | قوالب للاسترشاد

**Warm intro acknowledgement**
> Hi <First name> — thanks for the connect. I noticed <specific>. We've been working with KSA B2B teams on approval-gated outbound; happy to share what we've seen if useful. No pitch — just notes.

**Public-signal cold DM (after a thoughtful comment on their post)**
> Hi <First name>, your post on <topic> matched something we keep seeing across <sector> in KSA: <one-line pattern with range + N>. If you'd like, I can share the 1-page anatomy of how we run it. No pressure.

**After they engaged with founder content**
> Hi <First name>, saw you engaged with my post on <topic>. I'm running a free 30-minute diagnostic for KSA B2B teams this month — we'd look at ICP density, draft samples, and a Sprint shape. Worth a slot?

**Arabic — DM warmly**
```
مرحباً <الاسم>،
شكراً على القبول. لاحظت <إشارة محددة>. نحن نعمل مع فرق B2B سعودية على تواصل بموافقة العميل دون إرسال آلي. إن وجدت قيمة في تبادل ملاحظات، يسعدني ذلك. بدون عروض بيع.
```

---

## 7. Personalization Rules | قواعد التخصيص

- Reference **one** real signal per DM (post, role, hire, news).
- Do not flatter ("loved your work"). Show you read it by quoting a specific line.
- Use the recipient's preferred language. If unclear, mirror the language of their most recent public post.
- Do not name-drop customers without their approval.

---

## 8. Engagement Discipline | الانضباط في التفاعل

- Reply to comments on founder/company posts within 24 hours.
- Comment thoughtfully on at least 5 operator posts/week (Founder + Marketing each).
- Repost employee content when it is on-brand; do not repost generic memes or unrelated industry hype.
- Never argue publicly; take disagreements to DM.

---

## 9. Don'ts | ما لا نفعله

- No automation tools (Dux-Soup, Expandi, LinkedHelper, etc.).
- No web scraping or list extraction.
- No mass connection invites with templated notes.
- No "I see we have X mutual connections" generic opener.
- No competitor screenshots or side-by-side public attacks.
- No guaranteed-revenue language.
- No customer logos, quotes, or screenshots in posts/DMs without written customer approval.

---

## 10. Sponsored Content (if used) | الترويج المدفوع

- Used selectively, only when the conversion math is proven from organic.
- Same brand, voice, and accessibility gates apply.
- Targeting is sector + role + geo; never targeting by uploaded customer lists without explicit consent.
- All sponsored posts route to a single, dedicated landing page with one CTA.

---

## 11. Measurement | القياس

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Founder posts / week | ≥ 3 |
| Bilingual coverage | ≈ 50/50 |
| Inbound DMs from posts / week | track baseline |
| DM-to-diagnostic conversion | track baseline |
| Reply within 24h to founder DMs | ≥ 90% |
| Non-negotiable violations | 0 |

---

## 12. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending automation.
- No proof publishing without customer approval.
- No guaranteed-revenue claims.
- No pricing or contract commitments in posts or DMs.
- Approval-gated everything — including any sponsored content variations.

---

## 13. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Founder (content), Sales Lead (DM motion), Marketing Lead (company page).
- **KPI:** Inbound conversations attributable to LinkedIn (weekly).
- **KPI:** DM-to-diagnostic conversion (monthly).
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations (monthly, target 0).
