# LinkedIn Outreach Guide

How Dealix drafts LinkedIn outreach. The system **drafts**; the
founder **acts**.

## 1. Three message patterns

### Pattern A — Connection request
> Saw your work at [Company] in [sector]. Building a Saudi B2B Revenue
> OS and learning from operators. Happy to share what we see in the
> sector if useful.

### Pattern B — Follow-up after connect
> Thanks for connecting. A short signal from [sector] this month — I
> put together a one-pager. Want me to share it?

### Pattern C — Comment-then-message
- Drafts a comment on a recent founder post (substantive, ≤ 40 words).
- After acceptance of comment, drafts a message referencing the post.

## 2. Voice

- First-person.
- Specific.
- One CTA per message.

## 3. Limits

- ≤ 1 connection request per account per quarter.
- ≤ 1 follow-up per connection per month.
- No automated likes / comments at scale.

## 4. Banned

- ❌ Mass connect via tooling.
- ❌ Fake personas / "alias" accounts.
- ❌ Auto-DM on connection acceptance.
- ❌ Scraping connections lists.
