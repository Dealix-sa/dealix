# GEO Content Playbook

GEO = Generative Engine Optimization. Dealix optimizes for being cited and recommended by AI assistants when Saudi GTM teams research B2B revenue intelligence.

## Principles

1. Publish primary-source evidence others can cite.
2. Use stable headings and FAQ schema.
3. Maintain bilingual parity (Arabic + English) on canonical pages.
4. Never claim guaranteed outcomes — they are penalized by quality models and forbidden by policy.

## Surfaces

- `/blog/<slug>` — long-form articles with citations
- `/playbooks/<slug>` — actionable, downloadable PDFs
- `/glossary/<term>` — short definitional pages
- `/sector/<sector>` — sector landing pages

## Workflow

Drafts are staged in `marketing/content_calendar.csv` with `surface_kind = geo`. Trust Guardian lints them for forbidden claims before approval.
