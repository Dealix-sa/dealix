# Partner Outreach Guide | دليل التواصل مع الشركاء

> Partners are a primary distribution engine for Dealix in KSA. This guide is how we open, qualify, and earn partner relationships — without violating any brand non-negotiable.

---

## 1. Operating Principle | المبدأ التشغيلي

- Partner relationships are won by **demonstrating** the operating posture, not by promising it.
- Every partner conversation is approval-gated: nothing is committed on either side without a documented next step.
- We co-build with partners; we do not "white-label and forget."

---

## 2. Partner Archetypes | أرشيتايبات الشركاء

| Archetype | What they bring | What we bring |
|---|---|---|
| Sales / GTM agency | Local relationships, sector expertise | Productized Revenue OS, evidence layer, bilingual craft |
| Marketing agency | Brand & content production | Distribution + outcome anchoring |
| Systems integrator | Enterprise reach, technical delivery | Approval-gated operating layer |
| Vertical operator (e.g., logistics, fintech) | Sector depth + accounts | Repeatable revenue motion |
| Independent consultant | Trusted advisor relationships | Productized rungs they can resell |

---

## 3. Partner Ladder | سلم الشركاء

| Tier | Status | Commitment from us | Commitment from partner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Referral | Introduces a customer | Revenue share on shipped outcomes | One warm intro / quarter |
| Co-delivery | Delivers Sprints / Pilots with us | Co-branded playbook, training, ops support | Trained delivery lead, quarterly review |
| White-label | Operates a rung under their brand | Branded artifacts, ops support, dedicated success | Volume commitment, joint roadmap |

All tiers run on the same non-negotiables (no auto-send, no proof publishing without approval, no guaranteed revenue, no pricing/contract commitments without escalation, approval-gated automation only).

---

## 4. Outreach Channels | قنوات التواصل

| Channel | Use |
|---|---|
| Warm intro | First choice; via founders, advisors, customers. |
| LinkedIn DM | Second choice; after we have engaged with their content. |
| Personal email | Used after a warm or LinkedIn touch. |
| Events / roundtables | KSA-specific in-person openers. |

We do not cold-DM partners with templated pitches.

---

## 5. Anatomy of a Partner Email | تشريح بريد الشركاء

| Part | Rule |
|---|---|
| From | Real human, real reply path |
| Subject | ≤ 7 words, references shared territory |
| Opener | One specific signal about their work |
| Body | One sentence on shared posture; one on the partner motion; one on the ask |
| CTA | One CTA — a 20-minute call or an in-person coffee |
| Signature | Founder or partner lead, role, Dealix |

## 7. Qualification Checklist | قائمة التأهيل

A partner conversation moves forward only if all are true:

- [ ] Aligned posture (no auto-send, no proof-without-approval).
- [ ] Local fit (KSA-resident operators or active KSA practice).
- [ ] Bilingual delivery capability or willingness to staff for it.
- [ ] Comfortable with productized outcomes, not hour-based billing.
- [ ] Comfortable with revenue share / commercial terms anchored to shipped outcomes.

If two or more checks fail, we politely close the conversation.

---

## 8. Commercials | الجوانب التجارية

- Default: revenue share on shipped, paid customer outcomes.
- Specific terms are **not** committed in cold outreach or first calls — they are framed in writing after the qualification call and reviewed against `docs/product/PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md`.
- Any pricing or contract commitment requires escalation to Founder + Commercial Lead.
- Reference: `docs/AGENCY_PARTNER_PROGRAM.md`, `docs/PARTNER_LEGAL_AGREEMENT.md`.

---

## 9. Onboarding (high-level) | الإدماج

```
Qualification call  →  Written partner shape
                   →  Mutual NDA  →  Pilot engagement (1 customer)
                   →  Joint proof pack (with customer approval)
                   →  Tier assignment (Referral / Co-delivery / White-label)
                   →  Quarterly partner review
```

A partner does not enter a tier until at least one co-delivered customer outcome has shipped.

---

## 10. Co-Branding | العلامات المشتركة

- Logos used together only after **partner approval and Dealix Brand Lead approval**.
- Layout follows `docs/brand/DEALIX_LOGO_USAGE.md` §6 co-branding rules.
- No public partner announcement without partner sign-off on the post.

---

## 11. Don'ts | ما لا نفعله

- No promising revenue numbers to partners' customers on partners' behalf.
- No publishing partner case studies without written partner approval.
- No exclusivity offers without escalation.
- No revenue-share figures shared in cold outreach.
- No talking down competitors when speaking to partners.

---

## 12. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending without approval — applies to partner-side outreach as well.
- No proof publishing without written approval (partner or customer).
- No guaranteed-revenue claims.
- No pricing or contract commitments without escalation.
- Approval-gated automation only.

---

## 13. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Partnerships Lead, reporting to Founder.
- **KPI:** Active partners per tier.
- **KPI:** Partner-sourced pipeline % of total (target ≥ 30% by month 12).
- **KPI:** Partner-sourced shipped outcomes per quarter.
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations (target 0).
