# Partner Outreach Guide

How we initiate, deepen, and renew partner relationships.

## 1. Doctrine

- Partners are **selected**, not aggregated. Fewer than 10 active partners at any time.
- Reciprocity is the currency. We track intros given vs. received.
- Partners are bound by the same brand verifier and trust gates as Dealix.
- We do not pay per-referral commissions in v1.

## 2. Selection criteria

A partner is in scope if:

- They serve the same buyer (Saudi B2B mid-market) without competing head-on with us.
- They have a clean trust posture (no spammy outbound, no scraping).
- They will co-sign a brand and trust agreement.
- The mutual buyer's experience is improved by combining offers.

## 3. First touch

- Warm intro preferred. If cold, founder-to-founder.
- ≤ 120 words.
- Cite a specific reason for the fit (a shared sector, a complementary offer).
- Offer one specific next step (15-min call, or a co-delivered diagnostic).

## 4. Onboarding (after agreement)

- Joint working session: introduce respective offers, identify 5 shared accounts.
- Sign reciprocity ledger.
- Add partner to `data/growth/partners.csv` and `data/growth/distribution_machines.csv`.

## 5. Cadence

- Weekly: queue 1–2 warm-intro drafts (founder + partner approval).
- Monthly: 30-minute partner sync.
- Quarterly: founder-led review of the partner scorecard.

## 6. Reciprocity ledger

We track:

- Intros Dealix gave the partner (and outcome).
- Intros partner gave Dealix (and outcome).
- Mutual revenue (and split, if a co-deliver).

If the ratio is > 3:1 against either side for 60+ days, we pause the partnership and have a candid conversation.

## 7. Refusal patterns

- We refuse partners who violate our brand voice or trust gates.
- We refuse partners who insist on uncontrolled outbound automation.
- We refuse partners on the anti-ICP list (gambling, payday lending, MLM).
