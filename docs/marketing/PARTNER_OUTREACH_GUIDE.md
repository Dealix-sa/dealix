# Partner Outreach Guide

> Partners are KSA / GCC agencies, system integrators, and consultancies who can resell or operate Dealix.

## 1. Partner profile

| Trait | Expectation |
|---|---|
| KSA presence | Yes (HQ or active office) |
| Existing B2B motion | Yes |
| Brand quality | Aligned with Dealix posture |
| Customer base | At least 3 ICP-fit customers |
| Trust posture | No history of brand or compliance issues |

## 2. Conversation arc

1. **Initial** — peer-to-peer message; explain operating system frame; invite to a working session.
2. **Working session** — show the console, Trust Center, partner economics.
3. **Decision** — partner enablement plan; trial customer agreement; brand pack.
4. **Co-sell** — joint plan; named customers; cadence reviews.

## 3. Brand rules

- Partners receive a sealed brand pack via the partner portal.
- White-label partners use their brand; Dealix is referenced as "powered by" only when contractually allowed.
- No partner may modify the Dealix mark.

## 4. Economics

- Partner economics described in partner agreement; not in marketing copy.
- No public discount claim.

## 5. Trust posture

- Partner actions inside Dealix are governed by the same trust gates.
- Partners receive their own audit log slice.
