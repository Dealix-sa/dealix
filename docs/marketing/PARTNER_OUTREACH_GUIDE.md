# Partner Outreach Guide

How Dealix engages partners — consultancies, agencies, sector
enablers — without ever turning them into a cold-list channel.

## 1. Partner archetypes

| Archetype | Why they partner | What they get |
|---|---|---|
| Sector consultancy | Embed Dealix in their advisory motion. | Co-brand sector pulses, partner page. |
| B2B agency | Offer a Revenue OS instead of services-only. | White-label rung, rev-share. |
| Compliance firm | Pair Dealix trust gate with their audit motion. | Joint case studies. |
| Tech integrator | Implement Dealix at scale. | Implementation partner tier. |

## 2. Outreach drafts

- One curated outreach draft per partner per quarter.
- Drafts always reference a specific, public partner artifact (a
  webinar, a sector report, a hire announcement).

## 3. Partner program ledger

- Partner agreements live in `data/private_ops_seed/growth/partners.csv`
  (not in this repo).
- Rev-share, attribution, and rewards are recorded in
  `governance/partner_rewards.csv` (also private).

## 4. Banned

- ❌ Scraped partner lists.
- ❌ Cross-pitching to a partner's named customers without consent.
- ❌ Stacking incentives without partner agreement.
