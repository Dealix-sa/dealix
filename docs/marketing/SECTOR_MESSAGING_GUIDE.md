# Sector Messaging Guide

Each Saudi sector Dealix targets has its own messaging frame. This document indexes the frames; the per-sector specifics live as rows in `growth/sector_targets.csv`.

## Default frame

> For [SECTOR] GTM leaders who are tired of [PAIN], Dealix provides [CAPABILITY] grounded in evidence — without [FORBIDDEN_OUTCOME_PROMISE].

## Sectors covered

- Real estate
- Healthcare
- Logistics
- Manufacturing
- Financial services
- Government & public sector
- Retail & e-commerce
- Professional services

Each sector has an approved AR and EN headline and three approved subheads. The Brand Guardian rejects any deviation that uses guaranteed-outcome language.
