# Dealix Marketing OS

A founder-led marketing operating system. Every artifact is **drafted
by Dealix, approved by the founder**, and shipped against a published
calendar.

## 1. Marketing surfaces

| Surface | Cadence | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Landing pages (per rung) | Quarterly review. | content_strategist |
| Founder LinkedIn | 2–4 posts / week. | content_strategist |
| Sector pulse reports | Monthly per sector. | content_strategist |
| Case studies | Per consented proof. | content_strategist |
| Email outreach drafts | Continuous queue. | distribution_operator |
| Partner co-marketing | Ad-hoc, founder-approved. | growth_strategist |

## 2. Inputs

- Sector ranking and ICP segmentation.
- Approved proof packs.
- Brand templates and tokens.

## 3. Ledgers

- `marketing/content_calendar.csv` — what runs when.
- `marketing/campaigns.csv` — what's live, by status.
- `marketing/content_ideas.csv` — the idea backlog.

## 4. Marketing trust gate

- No artifact ships without a brand voice check.
- No artifact references a customer without consent.
- No artifact contains a guarantee or multiplier.
- Every artifact carries a publication-decision row in the calendar.

## 5. KPIs

- Founder time spent per artifact.
- Time from idea → published.
- Engagement per surface.
- Inbound demand per surface.

## 6. Banned patterns

- ❌ Ghost-writing claims without truth backing.
- ❌ Using AI to "fake" engagement.
- ❌ Hype copy.
