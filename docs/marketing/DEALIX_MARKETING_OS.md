# Dealix Marketing OS

The Dealix marketing system. Daily ideas, weekly insights, monthly reports — bilingual, proof-safe, no-overclaim.

## 1. Doctrine

- **Brand pillars only.** All content maps to one of the five pillars (Built on Trust, Driven by Growth, Closing Deals, Focused on Results, Global Mindset Local Impact).
- **One pillar per piece.** A piece is about *one* pillar, not five.
- **Evidence-led.** Every piece cites at least one observation, metric, or source.
- **AR + EN.** Each piece exists in both, written/edited by a human in each language.
- **No-overclaim.** No "guaranteed", no "10x", no "revolutionise".
- **Proof-safe.** Anonymise customer data by default. Identifying details only with customer co-sign.

## 2. Cadence

| Cadence  | What we produce                                                 | Approval owner    |
|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| Daily    | 1–2 LinkedIn drafts (founder-led)                                | Founder           |
| Weekly   | 1 sector insight (LinkedIn long-post or blog)                    | Founder           |
| Monthly  | 1 sector signal (long-form: blog + LinkedIn)                     | Founder           |
| Quarterly| 1 sector report (bilingual PDF + landing page + LinkedIn campaign)| Founder + product |

## 3. Content sources (whitelisted)

- Public LinkedIn (own and observed).
- Public press / sector news.
- Our own delivered (anonymised) work.
- Public sector data + KSA government statistics.
- Customer-approved disclosures.

Sources NOT permitted: scraped private content, unverified third-party data, hearsay.

## 5. Tone rules (see `DEALIX_BRAND_VOICE.md`)

- Direct, trust-first, numerate, calm.
- No exclamation marks in brand posts.
- No emoji.
- Sentences ≤ 25 words on average.

## 6. Distribution

- LinkedIn: founder-led, personal account.
- Blog: dealix.io/blog.
- Sector reports: gated landing page (`/sector-report-<sector>.html`).
- Newsletter: warm list, monthly.

## 7. Measurement

- Pillar coverage per quarter (rolling).
- Persona coverage per quarter (rolling).
- Inbound demo requests per piece.
- Sample / proof requests per piece.
- Newsletter open rate / reply rate.

## 8. Refusal patterns

We refuse to publish:

- Any piece that cannot cite at least one source.
- Any piece with banned phrases.
- Any piece that identifies a customer without their written approval.
- Any piece that auto-translates AR ↔ EN without human review.
