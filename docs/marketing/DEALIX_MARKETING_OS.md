# Dealix Marketing OS | نظام التسويق

> Marketing at Dealix is an operating system, not a campaign. It runs on the same trust posture as the product: approval-gated, evidence-led, bilingual-native.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

The Marketing OS coordinates how Dealix attracts, educates, qualifies, and equips buyers and partners — across owned, earned, and partner channels — without ever violating the brand's non-negotiables.

It exists so that any new asset, campaign, or channel motion has a known place in the system, a known owner, and a known proof gate.

---

## 2. Inputs | المدخلات

- Brand system (`docs/brand/`).
- Positioning + messaging (`docs/positioning/`).
- Product ladder (`docs/product/DEALIX_PRODUCT_LADDER.md`).
- Customer evidence (proof packs, approved case studies, ICP signals).
- Founder voice and operator field notes.

---

## 3. Outputs | المخرجات

- Inbound and warm-introduced pipeline at each rung of the product ladder.
- Category share-of-voice in KSA B2B discourse.
- Partner-led pipeline.
- Approved proof artifacts (case studies, decks, snippets).

---

## 4. The Four Engines | المحركات الأربعة

| Engine | What it produces | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| **Category Engine** | Essays, decks, talks that define and defend the ROC category. | Founder + Marketing |
| **Founder-Led Engine** | Operator-voice content on LinkedIn, podcast, newsletter. | Founder |
| **Demand Engine** | Landing pages, paid (where appropriate), email, SEO/GEO. | Marketing |
| **Partner & Proof Engine** | Customer proofs, partner co-marketing, events. | Marketing + Partnerships |

Each engine is governed by the same five gates (see §7).

---

## 5. Channel Mix | مزيج القنوات

Ranked by current importance to Saudi B2B Dealix motion:

1. **Founder-led LinkedIn** — primary distribution; operator credibility.
2. **Warm intros + partner channels** — highest-trust pipeline.
3. **Website (bilingual)** — conversion surface; landing systems.
4. **Email outreach (approval-gated)** — never blast; always draft-and-approve.
5. **Events & roundtables** — KSA-specific operator gatherings.
6. **Podcasts (guesting)** — depth + category education.
7. **Press / PR** — selective, used for category-defining moments.
8. **Paid (search + LinkedIn)** — only when the conversion math is proven.

## 7. Trust Gates | بوابات الثقة

Every marketing artifact must pass all five before publication:

1. **Brand gate** — colors, type, logo, layout pass `docs/brand/`.
2. **Voice gate** — no hype words, no guaranteed claims, ranges + conditions + N.
3. **Bilingual gate** — Arabic native, not translated; parity with English.
4. **Accessibility gate** — WCAG AA on web; alt text and captions on media.
5. **Non-negotiable gate** — no external sending, no proof publish without approval, no pricing/contract commitments without escalation, no autonomous framing.

A failing gate blocks publication.

---

## 8. Approval-Gated Outreach in Marketing | الموافقة في التسويق

Marketing-originated outreach (event invites, newsletter, partner intros) is **also** approval-gated:

- Lists are reviewed before send.
- Templates pass voice + non-negotiable gates.
- Personal sends from the founder remain personal (no bulk send under the founder's name).
- All sends honor consent and PDPL boundaries.

## 11. Workflow | سير العمل

```
Brief  →  Draft  →  Bilingual pass  →  Brand gate  →  Voice gate
      →  Accessibility gate  →  Non-negotiable gate  →  Function approve
      →  Ship  →  Log in asset registry  →  Measure  →  Iterate
```

No step is optional. If a gate fails, the artifact returns to draft.

---

## 12. Tools & Stack | الأدوات

- **CMS / web:** owned site with bilingual rendering and full RTL support.
- **CRM / signal:** the Dealix Revenue OS itself (eat-our-own-dog-food).
- **Design:** Figma "Dealix Brand," "Dealix Marketing," "Dealix Identity" libraries.
- **Analytics:** consented analytics only; respect PDPL and user consent.
- **Email:** approval-gated outbound flows; never bulk-send under founder identity.

---

## 13. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending without approval.
- No proof publishing without written customer approval.
- No guaranteed revenue claims.
- No pricing, contract, or payment commitments in marketing copy without escalation.
- Approval-gated automation only — never "autonomous."

---

## 14. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Marketing Lead, reporting to Founder.
- **North-star KPI:** Qualified diagnostics booked / week from owned + warm channels.
- **Quality KPI:** Brand consistency ≥ 90%; non-negotiable violations = 0.
