# Proof Pack Template

> النظام يميل من البداية إلى النهاية: كل شيء له دليل.

The proof pack ties every claim to evidence and tracks how far the proof has
matured. Lives at `customers/{slug}/09_proof_pack.md`.

---

## What needs proof

| Thing | Required evidence |
|-------|-------------------|
| Why we targeted the company | source URLs |
| Why we wrote this message | pain signal |
| Why we offered the Command Sprint | offer routing |
| What the client said | call notes |
| What they paid | payment log |
| What we delivered | delivery artifacts |
| What they gained | proof event |
| Whether we can publish | customer approval |

---

## Proof levels

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **L1** | We produced an artifact |
| **L2** | The client reviewed it |
| **L3** | The client approved it |
| **L4** | The client used it |
| **L5** | A measurable digital impact appeared |

**Early target: L2/L3. Then pursue L4/L5.** Public case studies require **L3+**
and explicit customer approval.

---

## Template

```markdown
# Proof Pack — {Company}
- source / evidence / assumption / confidence / recommendation /
  approval_required / next_action / owner / due_date

## Claims ↔ Evidence
| Claim | Evidence | Proof level | Customer approved? |
|-------|----------|-------------|--------------------|
| …     | …        | L2          | no                 |

## Proof levels
- L1 artifact created
- L2 client reviewed
- L3 client approved
- L4 client used
- L5 measurable impact
```

No claim is published beyond its evidenced proof level.
