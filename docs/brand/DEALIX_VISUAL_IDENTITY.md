# Dealix Visual Identity

## 1. Visual signature

The Dealix signature is a stacked composition of:

1. **The monogram** — a rounded-rect navy panel with a silver D-shape
   containing three teal revenue bars and a teal growth arrow.
2. **The wordmark** — `DEALIX`, all caps, tightly tracked, in white on
   navy or navy on white.
3. **The tagline** — `INTELLIGENT DEALS. REAL GROWTH.` in silver
   uppercase, used only when there is room to read it.

The monogram alone is the **mark**; the wordmark alone is the
**lockup wordmark**. Use the full signature whenever space allows.

## 2. The story the mark tells

| Element | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Rounded navy panel | Enterprise, trustworthy, sovereign foundation. |
| Outlined silver D | Discipline, structure, the operating system frame. |
| Three teal bars | Revenue, deals, growth — quantified outcomes. |
| Teal arrow | Compounding upward trajectory. |
| Teal swoosh | Velocity — closing motion, not standing still. |

## 3. Constructions

The mark is drawn on a 64-unit grid (see `assets/brand/source/`).
Every element snaps to a 2-unit subgrid so it stays crisp at small
sizes (favicon, app icon, social avatar).

## 4. Clear space

Reserve at least the height of the letter `E` in `DEALIX` as clear
space around the full signature. Nothing — type, photography, UI
chrome — may enter that zone.

## 5. Minimum sizes

- **Mark only:** 16 px (favicon), 24 px (UI), 32 px (cards).
- **Full signature with tagline:** 32 px monogram height minimum.
- **Wordmark only:** 18 px cap height minimum on screen.

## 6. Backgrounds

- Primary: deep navy (`#0B1220`) or slate (`#0F1726`).
- Acceptable: white (`#FFFFFF`) — use the monochrome dark mark.
- Avoid: pure black, busy photography, low-contrast greys.

## 7. Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Use SVG sources where possible. | Re-typeset `DEALIX` in a different font. |
| Keep teal accent on the revenue bars and arrow. | Re-colour the mark outside the palette. |
| Maintain clear space. | Stretch, skew, or rotate the mark. |
| Pair Latin + Arabic wordmarks vertically when needed. | Mix English tagline with translated tagline on the same surface. |
