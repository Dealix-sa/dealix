# Dealix Visual Identity

This document describes the approved visual identity for Dealix.

## 1. Concept

The Dealix mark combines five elements into a single, compact monogram:

1. **D monogram** — anchors the brand and is the icon/favicon form.
2. **Upward growth arrow** — angled through the D, signalling revenue trajectory.
3. **Revenue bars** — three rising bars inside the monogram, signalling measurable growth.
4. **Deal / list icon** — small horizontal lines, signalling pipeline and structured deals.
5. **Teal swoosh** — a single arc of Emerald Teal underlining the monogram, signalling AI energy and motion.

The lockup adds the **wordmark `DEALIX`** to the right of the monogram, and the **tagline `INTELLIGENT DEALS. REAL GROWTH.`** beneath the wordmark.

## 3. Construction rules

- The D monogram occupies a square optical bounding box. Internal elements (arrow, bars, deal lines) are aligned to a 12-unit grid inside that box.
- The teal swoosh starts from the lower-left of the D and arcs through the right edge at a 12° upward angle.
- The wordmark uses uppercase, letter-spaced **+40 to +60 units** for premium feel.
- The tagline sits below the wordmark at **45–50%** of the wordmark cap height.

## 4. Clear space

The minimum clear space around the lockup equals the **height of the D monogram** on all four sides. No other graphic element, text, or rule may intrude into this clear space.

## 5. Minimum sizes

| Variant         | Minimum width (digital) | Minimum width (print) |
|-----------------|-------------------------|-----------------------|
| Primary lockup  | 160 px                  | 30 mm                 |
| Icon (D mono)   | 24 px                   | 6 mm                  |
| Wordmark only   | 96 px                   | 18 mm                 |

Below these sizes the mark loses legibility and the icon-only form must be used instead.

## 6. Approved backgrounds

- **Deep Navy `#0B1220`** — the default brand background. Full-colour logo with teal swoosh.
- **Slate `#0F1726`** — secondary dark background, e.g. cards, modals.
- **White `#FFFFFF`** — for press, invoices, partner co-branding only.
- **Monochrome** — black on white or white on solid dark.

Do **not** place the logo on:

- Photographic backgrounds without an overlay that achieves ≥ 4.5:1 contrast on the wordmark.
- Saturated brand colours from competitors (e.g. HubSpot orange, Salesforce blue).
- Gradients that obscure the swoosh.

## 7. Tagline lockup

The tagline `INTELLIGENT DEALS. REAL GROWTH.` is part of the **primary lockup only**. In standalone settings (favicon, social avatar, narrow nav), drop the tagline. Never typeset the tagline in a non-uppercase form.

## 8. Co-branding (Dealix + customer logo)

When showing a customer logo alongside Dealix:

- Use the **horizontal lockup** with a 1px Soft Silver vertical divider between Dealix and customer logo.
- Both logos optically aligned to the same cap height.
- Always state the relationship in copy (e.g. "in partnership with", "client of", "proof-pack by").

## 9. Animation (digital surfaces)

- The teal swoosh may animate in once on first view (300–400ms ease-out arc), then settle.
- No looping animation. No bouncy easing. No pulse.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` and serve the static form.
