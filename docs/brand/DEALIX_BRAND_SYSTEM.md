# Dealix Brand System | نظام علامة ديليكس

> Tagline: **INTELLIGENT DEALS. REAL GROWTH.**
> صفقات ذكية. نمو حقيقي.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

The Dealix Brand System is the single source of truth for how Dealix shows up across product, marketing, sales, partner, and investor surfaces. It exists to ensure that every artifact — a deck, a landing page, an email, a dashboard, a contract — feels unmistakably Dealix: precise, trustworthy, growth-oriented, and locally fluent.

Dealix is a **Saudi B2B Revenue Operating Company**. The brand is not "another AI tool" — it is the operating layer that Saudi B2B teams use to turn relationships into intelligent, accountable deals.

---

## 2. Brand Pillars | ركائز العلامة

| Pillar | One-line meaning | Brand behavior |
|---|---|---|
| Built on Trust | Every action is approval-gated, evidenced, and reversible. | We never send, publish, or commit on the customer's behalf without explicit approval. |
| Driven by Growth | Outputs are measured in pipeline, conversion, and revenue — not activity. | Every dashboard ladders up to a revenue metric. |
| Closing Deals | The job is not awareness; the job is signed deals. | Copy, demos, and reports always end at "deal closed / next step booked." |
| Focused on Results | We package outcomes, not features. | Offers are scoped by result (e.g., "10 qualified meetings"), not by hours. |
| Global Mindset / Local Impact | World-class operating system, executed for Saudi market reality. | Bilingual by default; KSA-first compliance, calendars, and channels. |

## 3. Brand Promise | وعد العلامة

> Dealix gives Saudi B2B teams an **approval-gated revenue operating system** that turns intent signals and warm relationships into intelligent, evidenced deals — without ever auto-sending, auto-publishing, or auto-committing on the customer's behalf.

## 4. Audience Lens | عدسات الجمهور

- **Founders & GMs** — want pipeline visibility and a defensible revenue motion.
- **Heads of Sales / RevOps** — want approval-gated automation that their team trusts.
- **Partners & Agencies** — want a packaged motion they can resell locally.
- **Procurement & Compliance** — want clear data, consent, and approval boundaries.

Each audience meets the same Dealix voice: clear, measured, evidence-led, KSA-aware.

---

## 5. Brand Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

These rules govern every brand artifact, every demo, and every customer touchpoint:

1. **No external sending.** All outreach is draft, queued, and approved by the customer before it leaves.
2. **No proof publishing without approval.** Case studies, logos, quotes, and screenshots ship only with written approval.
3. **No guaranteed revenue claims.** We talk in ranges, benchmarks, and approval-gated targets — never "guaranteed."
4. **No pricing, contract, or payment commitments without escalation.** Anything that touches money is escalated to a human owner.
5. **Approval-gated automation only.** "Autonomous" is never the headline; "approval-gated" is.

If a piece of brand or marketing content violates any of these, it does not ship.

## 7. Brand Personality | شخصية العلامة

- **Operator, not influencer.** We sound like the calmest person in the room.
- **Bilingual native.** Arabic and English are equally first-class; never an afterthought.
- **Quiet confidence.** No hype words ("revolutionary," "magical," "10x guaranteed").
- **Evidence-first.** Every claim is backed by a number, a screenshot, or a customer-approved proof.
- **Locally grounded.** References the Saudi business calendar, Hijri dates where relevant, and KSA compliance reality.

---

## 8. Brand Anti-Patterns | ما لا نفعله

- We do not look like a generic SaaS startup.
- We do not use stock "AI brain" imagery.
- We do not promise revenue numbers in headlines.
- We do not auto-publish customer logos.
- We do not localize English copy by direct translation — Arabic is written natively.

---

## 9. Governance | الحوكمة

- **Owner:** Brand Lead (reporting to Founder).
- **Approval gate for new assets:** Brand Lead + relevant function lead (Marketing, Product, or Sales).
- **Change log:** All brand system updates land in this folder with a dated entry.
- **Review cadence:** Quarterly brand review; ad-hoc when a new product rung launches.

---

## 10. KPI of the Brand System | مؤشرات الأداء

- **Brand consistency score** across the last 20 shipped assets (target ≥ 90%).
- **Bilingual coverage** on customer-facing surfaces (target 100%).
- **Accessibility pass rate** on shipped pages (target 100% WCAG AA).
- **Approval-gate violations** in marketing (target 0).
