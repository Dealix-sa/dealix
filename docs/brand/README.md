# Brand

## Purpose
Hold the operating rules for how Dealix presents itself - what it says, what it never says, and how the company looks and sounds in public.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO (until a brand owner is named).

## Review Cadence
Quarterly, plus before any new public surface (site, deck, post, ad).

## Inputs
- Customer language from calls.
- Win/loss themes.
- Sector positioning.
- Trust rules and approved claims.

## Outputs
- Voice and tone guidelines (Arabic + English).
- Visual guidelines (logo, colours, type, layout).
- Approved positioning and tagline.
- List of claims approved for public use - and the evidence behind each.

## Rules
- No public claim is published without supporting evidence on file.
- Guaranteed revenue, guaranteed compliance, and "official" language are never used.
- Public/private boundary holds: customer names, deliveries, and internal results are not shown without explicit A2 approval.
- Arabic comes first for Saudi-facing surfaces; English mirrors it.

## Metrics
- Claims approved vs. claims requested.
- Public surfaces audited per quarter.
- Brand-related trust incidents.

## Evidence
- approved claims register in this folder.
- visual asset library (private).
- public surface audits.
- trust/approval_log.csv entries for external claims.
