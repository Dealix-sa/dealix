# DEALIX Logo Usage

**Owner:** Brand Lead
**Source of truth:** `assets/brand/` exports + `docs/brand/brand-tokens.json`

## Logo set

| Asset | File pattern | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Wordmark color | `assets/brand/wordmark/dealix-wordmark-emerald.svg` | Default on Deep Navy |
| Wordmark inverse | `assets/brand/wordmark/dealix-wordmark-navy.svg` | On White and light surfaces |
| Wordmark monochrome white | `assets/brand/monochrome/dealix-wordmark-white.svg` | Print, fax, low-fi |
| Wordmark monochrome black | `assets/brand/monochrome/dealix-wordmark-black.svg` | Print, fax, low-fi |
| Growth arrow mark | `assets/brand/icon/dealix-mark-emerald.svg` | Favicons, avatars, watermarks |
| Lockup | `assets/brand/logo/dealix-lockup.svg` | Hero placements only |

## Clearspace

Minimum clearspace around the wordmark equals the height of one uppercase D in the wordmark. No other graphic, text, edge, or photographic content may enter that zone.

For the lockup, clearspace equals one wordmark cap height around the full lockup, measured from the outermost glyph of the tagline.

## Minimum size

| Surface | Minimum width |
|---|---|
| Print | 24 mm |
| Screen | 96 px |
| Favicon (mark only) | 16 px |
| Avatar (mark only) | 48 px |

Below these sizes the wordmark breaks down. Use the growth arrow mark instead.

## On dark

- Default: Emerald Teal wordmark on Deep Navy `#0B1220` or Slate `#0F1726`.
- Acceptable: White wordmark on Deep Navy when emerald conflicts with adjacent emerald content.

## On light

- Default: Deep Navy wordmark on White `#FFFFFF`.
- Never Emerald Teal wordmark on White (insufficient contrast — fails WCAG AA for small text).

## On photography

Required: a solid color plate behind the wordmark — Deep Navy at 95 percent opacity minimum, or a White plate at 100 percent opacity. The plate must extend at least one cap height on every side. The wordmark must never sit directly on a photograph.

## Monochrome

- Black wordmark on White for B/W print only.
- White wordmark on Black for B/W print only.
- Never gray wordmark on gray.

## What NOT to do

1. Do not stretch, condense, or skew the wordmark.
2. Do not rotate the wordmark.
3. Do not change the wordmark color outside the approved palette.
4. Do not add drop shadows, glows, bevels, or strokes.
5. Do not place the wordmark on a low-contrast background.
6. Do not surround the wordmark with decorative borders.
7. Do not combine the wordmark with another brand mark in the same lockup.
8. Do not re-typeset the wordmark in a different typeface.
9. Do not change the kerning or letter spacing of the wordmark.
10. Do not crop the wordmark.
11. Do not place the growth arrow as a substitute glyph inside the wordmark.
12. Do not use the swoosh as a standalone brand mark.

## Co-branding

Co-branded lockups require Founder approval (trust class A3). The default co-brand pattern is:

```
[Partner mark] | DEALIX
```

with a thin Soft Silver vertical divider, both marks vertically centered, both marks sized to match cap height.

## Failure mode

- Wordmark placed on photographic background without a contrast plate.
- Wordmark in a non-palette color used in a deck or proposal.
- Mark rotated to fit a layout constraint.
- Co-brand published without Founder approval.

## Recovery path

1. Pull the asset.
2. Re-export from `assets/brand/` using the correct variant.
3. Re-publish.
4. Log the failure in the Brand Lead's incident note for the quarterly brand review.

## Disclaimer

Logo placement on a surface does not endorse the surface's claims. Performance and outcome language must follow `DEALIX_BRAND_VOICE.md` and the trust gates in `DEALIX_BRAND_SYSTEM.md`.
