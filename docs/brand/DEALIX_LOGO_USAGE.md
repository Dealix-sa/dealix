# Dealix Logo Usage | استخدام الشعار

> The logo is the most-seen Dealix asset. These rules protect it from drift, distortion, and misuse.

---

## 1. Approved Marks | العلامات المعتمدة

| Mark | Use case | File reference |
|---|---|---|
| Wordmark — Deep Navy | Light backgrounds, decks, documents | `dealix-wordmark-navy-en-*.svg` |
| Wordmark — White | Dark backgrounds, hero sections, video | `dealix-wordmark-white-en-*.svg` |
| Wordmark — Arabic Navy | Arabic-first surfaces, light bg | `dealix-wordmark-navy-ar-*.svg` |
| Wordmark — Arabic White | Arabic-first surfaces, dark bg | `dealix-wordmark-white-ar-*.svg` |
| Monogram "D" — Teal on Navy | Avatars, favicons, app icons | `dealix-monogram-teal-*-*.svg` |
| Tagline Lockup | Launch, investor, formal partner | `dealix-lockup-*-*.svg` |

Only these files are approved. Do not recreate, retrace, or "improve" the marks.

---

## 2. Clear Space | المسافة الآمنة

- Clear space = **height of the capital "D"** in the wordmark.
- This space must be free of: text, icons, image edges, page borders, and other logos.
- For the monogram, clear space = **half the monogram's height** on every side.

```
[ clear ]
[ clear ]  Dealix  [ clear ]  [ clear ]
[ clear ]
```

---

## 3. Minimum Sizing | الحد الأدنى للحجم

| Mark | Screen min | Print min |
|---|---|---|
| Wordmark (English) | 16px height | 12mm height |
| Wordmark (Arabic) | 18px height | 14mm height |
| Monogram "D" | 24px | 8mm |
| Tagline Lockup | 80px wide | 40mm wide |

Below the minimum, switch to the monogram or remove the mark entirely.

---

## 4. Do | افعل

- Use approved SVGs from the source library.
- Place on approved background colors (see `DEALIX_VISUAL_IDENTITY.md` §6).
- Maintain clear space on every side.
- Pair Arabic wordmark on Arabic-first pages; English wordmark on English-first pages.
- For bilingual pages, use the English wordmark and add an Arabic title in the page header — do not stack two wordmarks.

---

## 5. Don't | لا تفعل

- Do not stretch, squash, skew, or rotate the mark.
- Do not change the colors of the mark outside the approved palette.
- Do not add gradients, shadows, glows, bevels, or strokes.
- Do not place the mark on busy photography without the 40% Deep Navy overlay.
- Do not place the mark on Emerald Teal in white — only Deep Navy wordmark on teal.
- Do not redraw the wordmark in another typeface.
- Do not enclose the wordmark in a box, badge, or circle that is not part of the approved monogram.
- Do not animate the wordmark beyond a 200ms fade-in.
- Do not use the monogram and wordmark together in the same lockup (they are alternatives, not a pair).
- Do not co-brand by placing another logo flush against the wordmark — always separate with a vertical Soft Silver rule and 2× clear space.

---

## 6. Co-Branding | العلامات المشتركة

When Dealix appears alongside a partner or customer logo:

- Use a 1px Soft Silver vertical divider.
- Give each logo equal optical weight (height ≈ height).
- Dealix wordmark sits to the **left** in English layouts and to the **right** in Arabic layouts.
- Customer/partner logo must be approved by Dealix Brand Lead **and** the partner before publication (Trust gate: no proof publishing without approval).

---

## 7. Favicon & App Icon | أيقونة التطبيق

- Favicon: 32×32 monogram, Emerald Teal "D" on Deep Navy, no padding beyond 2px.
- App icon (iOS/Android): 1024×1024 monogram on Deep Navy with 18% safe-area padding.
- Notification icon: monochrome white monogram on transparent background.

---

## 8. Social Avatars | الصور الرمزية

- LinkedIn, X, Instagram, YouTube: monogram on Deep Navy.
- Slack, Discord, WhatsApp Business: monogram on Deep Navy.
- Email signature avatar: monogram on Deep Navy, 96px circle crop.

---

## 9. Approval Gate | بوابة الموافقة

Before publishing any new logo placement:

1. Confirm the mark is from the approved library.
2. Confirm clear space and minimum size.
3. Confirm background pairing matches §6 in `DEALIX_VISUAL_IDENTITY.md`.
4. For co-branding: secure written partner approval and Brand Lead sign-off.
5. Log the placement in the brand asset registry.

If any step fails, do not publish.
