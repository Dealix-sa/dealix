# Public Copy Bank — English (EN)

## Purpose

A repository of approved English copy for public use: website, email, invitations, social. Every line here is approved against CLAIMS_GUARDRAILS.md. No guaranteed revenue, no fake testimonials, no fake ROI.

## Owner

Founder / CEO with content lead.

## Daily Usage

- Copy only approved lines when needed.
- Do not publish any copy outside this bank without approval.
- Update the bank weekly with newly approved copy.

- `docs/brand/POSITIONING.md`
- `docs/brand/VOICE_AND_TONE.md`
- `docs/brand/CLAIMS_GUARDRAILS.md`

## Outputs

- Approved website copy (EN)
- Approved email and invitation copy (EN)
- Approved social copy (EN)

## Workflow

1. Draft in the Dealix voice.
2. Check against CLAIMS_GUARDRAILS.md.
3. Approve manually.
4. Add to the bank with date and approval.
5. Use only approved copy.

## Approved Copy

- Hero: "Dealix — AI Operating Systems for B2B companies in Saudi Arabia."
- Short description: "We build Saudi-first, PDPL-compliant AI operating systems with approval-first execution."
- Diagnostic CTA: "Book an operational diagnostic to identify your top 3 operational opportunities."
- Compliance: "Every action goes through human approval first, and all data stays within PDPL scope."

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every line is approved and dated.
- No guaranteed revenue or assured returns.
- No fabricated testimonials or results.
- Dealix is described as operating systems, not a chatbot, CRM, or agency.
- Copy is consistent with PDPL and approval-first.

## Risks

- Using unapproved copy damages brand and compliance.
- Guaranteed-outcome claims damage trust.
- Inconsistency between AR and EN copy.

## What Not To Do

- Do not claim guaranteed revenue or assured returns.
- Do not use fabricated testimonials or results.
- Do not describe Dealix as a chatbot, CRM, or marketing agency.
- Do not publish without approval.
- Do not bypass PDPL or approval-first rules.
