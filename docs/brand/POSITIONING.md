## 2. Category Creation | خلق الفئة

### 2.1 The category

**AI Operating Systems for B2B (أنظمة تشغيل بالذكاء الاصطناعي لقطاع الأعمال)**

This category does not exist as a recognized market label today. Dealix creates it. The category is defined by four properties:

1. **Function-level, not tool-level.** Each product is an operating system for one commercial function (revenue, delivery, follow-up, decisions), not a point tool.
2. **End-to-end, not observational.** The OS runs discovery → decision → delivery → proof. It does not just observe or report.
3. **Governed, not autonomous.** AI explores, analyzes, and recommends. Deterministic workflows execute. Humans approve critical external commitments. No autonomous external sending.
4. **Proof-native, not promise-native.** Every external results claim requires a Proof Pack: baseline → after → documented delta.

### 2.2 Why this category (and not an existing one)

| Existing category | Why it is insufficient | Why Dealix is the next category |
|---|---|---|
| Chatbot / conversational AI | Observes and responds. Cannot run a function end-to-end. No approval gates. No proof artifacts. | Dealix runs the function, not the conversation. Approval-gated, proof-native. |
| CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho) | Stores data and pipelines. Requires humans to do the operational work. Does not generate, draft, or approve actions. | Dealix generates drafts, routes approvals, and produces proof. The CRM is a data store; Dealix is the operating system on top of commercial reality. |
| Agency / managed service | Humans do the work. Black-box. No audit trail. No proof discipline. No transferable system. | Dealix builds a system the client can see, audit, and eventually operate. Transparent, not black-box. |
| Dashboard / BI (Tableau, Power BI) | Observes and visualizes. Cannot execute or decide. No action loop. | Dealix closes the loop: observation → recommendation → approval → execution → proof. |

### 2.3 Category proof points

The category is real because Dealix can demonstrate:
- 14 productized OSes, each with defined inputs, outputs, approval gates, and proof artifacts.
- A shared trust kernel (Layer 0): PDPL controls, approval classes, audit trails, no-overclaim register.
- A draft-only-by-default outbound posture (`OUTBOUND_MODE=draft_only`) with all send flags off by default.
- A Proof Pack as the renewal artifact — the only basis for external results claims.

| **CRM** | "Manage your pipeline" | "نُشغّل خط الأنابيب، لا نُخزّنه فقط" / "We operate the pipeline, not just store it" | CRMs store data. Dealix generates drafts, routes approvals, and produces proof artifacts. |

### 3.2 Dealix differentiation pillars

1. **Operating system, not tool.** One OS per function. Defined inputs, outputs, gates, proof. Not a point solution.
2. **Governed, not autonomous.** Approval gates on every external commitment. Draft-only by default. No autonomous sends.
3. **Proof-native, not promise-native.** Proof Pack is the renewal artifact. No proof, no claim. Baseline → after → documented delta.
4. **Saudi-native, not global-generic.** Saudi LLC, SAR, PDPL, ZATCA, Riyadh timezone, Arabic-first. Not a foreign product translated.
5. **Transparent, not black-box.** Audit trail completeness target: 100%. Every decision, draft, approval, and send is logged.

## 5. Competitive Frame | الإطار التنافسي

### 5.1 Direct competitors (none yet — category is new)

No company currently positions as "AI Operating Systems for B2B" in the Saudi market with the Dealix properties (function-level, end-to-end, governed, proof-native). This is the category Dealix creates.

### 5.2 Adjacent competitors (categories Dealix displaces)

| Competitor type | Examples (global) | Saudi equivalents | Dealix advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| CRM | Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho | Local CRM deployments | We operate the function, not just store data. Approval-gated drafts. Proof-native. |
| Agency / managed service | McKinsey digital, Accenture | Local digital agencies | We build a system, not black-box labor. Auditable. Transferable. |
| Chatbot / conversational AI | Intercom, Drift, Chatbase | Local chatbot vendors | We are an OS, not a chat surface. Governance + proof, not just conversation. |
| Dashboard / BI | Tableau, Power BI, Looker | Local BI deployments | We close the loop. Observe → recommend → approve → execute → prove. |
| Automation / RPA | Zapier, UiPath, n8n | Limited local presence | We add AI reasoning + proof discipline to automation. Saudi-native. |

### 5.3 Saudi-specific competitive advantages

1. **Saudi LLC, not a foreign entity.** Local incorporation, local law, local timezone, SAR pricing.
2. **PDPL-native, not PDPL-translated.** Controls built into Layer 0, not bolted on after a foreign product is deployed.
3. **ZATCA-aware.** E-invoicing readiness, Moyasar payment integration (sandbox by default).
4. **Arabic-first.** Arabic is the primary language, not a localization layer. GLM (Arabic-first LLM) in the routing chain.
5. **No-overclaim register.** A governance artifact most competitors do not have. It is a trust signal to regulators and enterprises.

### 5.4 What Dealix does NOT compete on

- **Cheapest price.** Dealix is value-priced (diagnosis SAR 4,999, pilot SAR 14,999, subscription scenario-based). We do not race to the bottom.
- **Most features.** Dealix competes on governed operations and proof, not feature count.
- **Fastest setup.** Dealix runs a 1-week paid diagnostic and a 4-week pilot. We do not promise "live in 5 minutes."
- **Autonomy.** Dealix explicitly does not compete on "no human needed." Human approval is a feature, not a limitation.

## 6. Positioning Guardrails | ضوابط التموضع

### 6.1 Always say
- "نظام تشغيل" / "Operating system" — for every product.
- "محوكَم" / "Governed" — approval gates, audit trails.
- "مُثبَت بالأدلة" / "Proven with evidence" — Proof Pack, documented delta.
- "سعودي أولاً، عربي أولاً" / "Saudi-first, Arabic-first."
- "القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة" / "Estimated value is not Verified value."

### 6.2 Never say
- "روبوت ذكي" / "Smart robot" or "AI agent that does it all."
- "مضمون" / "Guaranteed" — for ROI, revenue, or results.
- "معتمد" / "Certified" — for PDPL, ZATCA, or any regulator (we are "aware", not "certified").
- "تلقائي تماماً" / "Fully automatic" — for external sending or commitments.
- "ثوري" / "Revolutionary" or "transform" — hype words.

### 6.3 Scenario-based forecasting rules

Every external forecast must carry:
1. **Confidence level** (e.g., Medium, Medium-high, Low-medium).
2. **Explicit assumptions** (e.g., "single OS, one function, light ops").
3. **Description as a scenario**, not a guarantee (e.g., "Conservative scenario: SAR 7,500–12,000/month, medium-high confidence").
