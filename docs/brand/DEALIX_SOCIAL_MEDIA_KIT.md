# DEALIX Social Media Kit

**Owner:** Founder + Content Lead
**Source of truth:** `docs/brand/DEALIX_BRAND_VOICE.md` + `docs/brand/DEALIX_MARKETING_ASSET_GUIDE.md`

## Channels

Dealix publishes on three channels at the launch stage. Channel expansion follows demonstrated capacity, not aspiration.

| Channel | Audience | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Saudi founders, Heads of Sales, B2B operators | 3 posts per week |
| X (Twitter) | Builder community, tech-adjacent operators | 2 posts per week |
| Instagram | Visual recap of deck slides, sector cards | 1 carousel per week |

WhatsApp, Snapchat, and TikTok are NOT distribution channels for Dealix.

## Post types

| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Sector signal | Show a market pattern + Dealix angle | 1 per week |
| Sprint recap | Show what shipped, what learned (anonymized) | 1 per week |
| Founder principle | Operating principle in founder voice | 1 per week |
| Proof brief | Dated, anonymized outcome with disclaimer | 2 per month |
| Open question | Invite specific peer input | 1 per month |

No engagement-bait posts. No "agree?" prompts. No countdown threads to a hidden reveal.

## LinkedIn template — Sector signal

```
Headline: One sector + one observed shift, max 12 words.

Body:
- Pattern observed (2-3 lines, dated, sourced).
- Why it matters for B2B operators in Saudi.
- What Dealix is doing about it (one sentence).

CTA: "If you operate in [sector], I'd value your read on this — what's your version of this pattern?"

Disclosure: "Dealix observations are aggregated, dated, and never identify named accounts."
```

Length: 600-900 characters body. No more.

## LinkedIn template — Sprint recap

```
Headline: "Sprint NN closed. Here's what I learned." (no client name)

Body:
- Sector + sprint type (1 line)
- One thing the sprint surfaced (3-4 lines)
- One thing Dealix changed in the SOP because of it (2-3 lines)
- Trust gate that fired during the sprint (1 line)

CTA: "If you're running a similar play, the SOP is at [link]."

Disclosure: "Outcome figures are estimated and bound by the Dealix proof framework. Estimated value is not Verified value."
```

## LinkedIn template — Founder principle

```
Headline: One principle, no preamble. Example: "Approval is not a feature. It's a gate."

Body:
- Why this principle exists (3-4 lines).
- What it costs to violate (2-3 lines).
- How Dealix enforces it (1-2 lines, with file reference if relevant).

CTA: "Steal the principle. The Dealix file is at [link]."
```

## Carousel template (LinkedIn or IG)

5-7 slides. Each slide 1080 x 1080.

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | Title only. One question or one claim. Wordmark bottom-right. |
| 2-5 | One idea per slide. Max 30 words per slide. |
| 6 | Numbers slide. Dated, sourced, disclosure footnote. |
| 7 | CTA slide. One sentence + one link. |

## What NOT to post

1. Auto-generated motivational images.
2. Stock photos of handshakes, skylines, or laptops.
3. Engagement bait (single-word polls, "agree?" prompts).
4. Anonymous brags ("just closed a 7-figure deal").
5. Named clients without explicit written approval.
6. Anything pulled from a customer's private data — even paraphrased.
7. Cross-posted threads from competitors with no added value.

## Bilingual cadence

Alternate AR-first and EN-first weeks. Every AR post has an EN counterpart published within 48 hours, and vice versa. Translation must be parallel — see `DEALIX_BRAND_VOICE.md`.

## Approval (trust gates)

| Post type | Trust gate |
|---|---|
| Founder principle | A0 — founder publishes directly |
| Sector signal | A1 — Content Lead reviews |
| Sprint recap | A2 — Founder + Brand Lead (anonymization check) |
| Proof brief | A2 — Founder + Brand Lead (numbers check) |

## Failure mode

- A post identifies an unnamed client by accidental detail (sector + size + timing).
- A post claims a result without the disclosure.
- A carousel uses 8+ slides, reader drops off by slide 4.

## Recovery path

1. Edit or delete the post.
2. Re-publish with the correct disclosure or anonymization.
3. Log the incident; review trust gate enforcement at the next brand review.
