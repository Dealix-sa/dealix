# Dealix Brand Voice

> Voice is not vibes. This document is a policy that every copy producer — human or agent — must follow.

## 1. Voice attributes

| Attribute | Definition | Anti-pattern |
|---|---|---|
| Calm authority | Confident, not loud | "Game-changing", "revolutionary" |
| Evidence-led | Claims backed by signal | Vague superlatives |
| Operator-tight | Direct, action-oriented | Marketing fluff |
| Bilingual symmetry | Equal weight to AR + EN | "AR is a translation of EN" |
| Founder-respectful | Talks **with** the founder, not at | "We know what's best for you" |

## 2. Always say

- "Founder-approved"
- "Trust-gated"
- "Cash collected" (not "revenue")
- "Saudi B2B Revenue Operating System"
- "Intelligent deal flow"
- "Approval-gated automation"

## 3. Never say

- "Guaranteed [revenue / sales / leads / results]"
- "Auto-pilot growth"
- "AI sales rep that replaces your team"
- "Get more leads with one click"
- "10x your pipeline"
- "Magic" / "secret sauce" / "ninja"
- "Just trust the AI"

## 4. Tone matrix

| Surface | Tone |
|---|---|
| Founder Console | Calm authority, operator-tight |
| Landing hero | Calm authority, evidence-led |
| Sales proposal | Founder-respectful, evidence-led |
| Trust Center | Operator-tight, evidence-led |
| Error messages | Operator-tight, helpful |
| LinkedIn post | Calm authority, founder-respectful |
| Internal CLI | Operator-tight |

## 5. Sentence and paragraph rules

- Sentences ≤ 22 words by default. Long technical sentences allowed where precision demands.
- Paragraphs ≤ 4 sentences.
- One idea per sentence in marketing copy.
- Quantify when possible; never invent a number.

## 6. Bilingual rules

- Every founder-facing surface ships AR + EN.
- AR is **written natively**, not translated. Translators must rewrite, not transliterate.
- Numbers use Western digits unless contractually specified otherwise.
- Tone in AR matches tone in EN; do not soften AR into "polite-only".

## 7. Examples

### Hero
- ✅ "Intelligent deals. Real growth. The Saudi B2B Revenue Operating System."
- ✅ "صفقات ذكية. نمو حقيقي. نظام التشغيل الإيرادي لسوق B2B السعودي."
- ❌ "Dealix uses AI to revolutionise sales by 10x."

### Proposal
- ✅ "We will produce a sample diagnostic for your top 10 target accounts within seven days. Founder reviews before any external action."
- ❌ "We guarantee a 30 % response rate on the first send."

### LinkedIn
- ✅ "Five things we learned from running the Distribution machine for a KSA SaaS this week — none of them involve more sends."
- ❌ "Our AI sent 1000 emails for you while you slept."

## 8. Voice owners

- **Brand Guardian agent** — flags violations and proposes rewrites.
- **Eval Guardian** — runs voice evals on every agent draft.
- **Founder** — final approver.
