# Prospect Research Template · قالب بحث الـ prospect

> 12-section research brief per prospect. Auto-generated by
> `scripts/research_prospect.py` from public-source data only.
> Doctrine-aligned: no scraping, PDPL Source Passport required,
> no personal data beyond business context.
>
> **Effective:** 2026-06-01

---

## How this template is used

1. Founder provides: prospect name + company + sector hint
2. `research_prospect.py` enriches via existing prospector + ICP scorer
3. Output: this template filled in
4. Personalization agent reads the brief → generates 3-touch sequence
5. Brief stored in `prospect_research_briefs` table with 90-day expiry

## How to read this brief

**Founder reads in 3 minutes:**
1. Sections 9 + 10 — is this prospect worth pursuing? what tier?
2. Section 11 — are they "hot" right now?
3. Section 8 — what pain do we lead with?
4. Section 12 — am I clear on lawful basis?

If yes to all four → green light to generate 3-touch sequence via
`scripts/generate_sequence.py`.

If no to any → either:
- Re-research with more context, or
- Move to nurture list (90-day silent window), or
- Decline (if doctrine conflict — e.g., prospect requested
  autonomous send)
