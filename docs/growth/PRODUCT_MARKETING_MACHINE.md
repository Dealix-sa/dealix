# Product Marketing Machine

> Drives launches, sector reports, and offer-ladder marketing.

## 1. Purpose

Operate the launches, sector reports, and offer-ladder content that turn the Dealix product surface into demand.

## 2. Input

- Active offers from `docs/product/DEALIX_PRODUCT_LADDER.md`.
- Quarterly sector picks from `SECTOR_DOMINATION_ENGINE.md`.
- Brand pillars and content calendar.
- Customer outcomes from delivered Sprints (anonymised by default).

## 3. Output

- Launch plan per new offer or revision (drafted, founder-approved).
- Sector report per quarter (drafted, founder-approved, AR + EN).
- Landing page updates queued for review.
- Sales-enablement one-pagers in `docs/marketing/`.

## 4. Approval class

**A1** for content. **A2** for any external launch (announce on LinkedIn, send to warm list).

## 5. Owner

Product + content strategist + founder.

## 6. Worker name

`product_marketing_worker`.

## 7. KPI

- 1 launch per quarter (new or refreshed offer).
- 1 sector report per quarter.
- 4 sales-enablement one-pagers per quarter.
- Landing conversion (visit → diagnostic request): trend monitored.

## 8. Doctrine

- Never claim "guaranteed" anything.
- Never publish a sector report without anonymised data and a date of cut-off.
- Always provide AR + EN.

## 9. Failure modes

| Failure                                          | Recovery                                          |
|--------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Launch announces a feature that does not exist   | Refuse; founder pulls back                        |
| Sector report uses unverified data               | Pause; verify source                              |
| Landing copy claims revenue guarantee            | Verifier rejects; copy is rewritten               |
