# Revenue Sprint Proposal Template

This is the canonical proposal template. Every Revenue Sprint proposal must follow this structure. The Sales OS proposal generator fills the placeholders; humans review before sending.

## Header

- Buyer name and named contact.
- Buyer sector code.
- Sprint ID.
- Proposed start date.

## Section 1 — Bottleneck named

State, in one sentence, the bottleneck the sprint will work on. No marketing language. No guarantees.

## Section 2 — Scope (in/out)

Reference [SCOPE.md](./SCOPE.md) directly. List the in-scope items relevant to this buyer. Explicitly list the most likely out-of-scope items so expectations are calibrated.

## Section 3 — Deliverables

List the four buyer-facing deliverables per [DELIVERABLES.md](./DELIVERABLES.md). State the artefact specifically (e.g., "reviewed lead table for sector X").

## Section 4 — Timeline

Reference [TIMELINE.md](./TIMELINE.md). State the proposed kick-off date and final hand-off date.

## Section 5 — Price

499 SAR, pre-paid. Reference [PRICING.md](./PRICING.md). VAT line item shown explicitly.

## Section 6 — Refund posture

State the refund-on-request policy verbatim from [REFUND_POLICY.md](./REFUND_POLICY.md). Do not paraphrase.

## Section 7 — What we will not claim

Reference [docs/trust/NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md](../../trust/NO_OVERCLAIM_POLICY.md). State that any revenue figure shared during the sprint will be labelled as estimated, observed, or verified.

## Section 8 — Approval

- Proposal drafted by: [agent or human]
- Reviewed by: Revenue Lead
- A1 approval signature.

## Disallowed phrases

- "guaranteed revenue"
- "100% compliance"
- "fully autonomous"
- "no human needed"
- "guaranteed sales"

Catch them with [docs/trust/SAFE_LANGUAGE_LIBRARY.md](../../trust/SAFE_LANGUAGE_LIBRARY.md) before sending.
