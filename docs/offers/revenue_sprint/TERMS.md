# Revenue Sprint Terms — شروط سبرنت الإيرادات

## Purpose

The terms governing the 499–999 SAR Revenue Sprint. Plain language, parallel AR + EN. Anything not stated here is not promised.

## 1. Parties

This sprint is delivered by the Dealix legal entity registered in KSA (entity details in invoice header). The buyer is the entity named on the invoice.

## 2. Scope

The scope is the document at [SCOPE.md](./SCOPE.md) as of the invoice date. Out-of-scope requests follow [docs/delivery/CHANGE_REQUEST_PROCESS.md](../../delivery/CHANGE_REQUEST_PROCESS.md).

## 3. Payment

- Sprint is paid 100% in advance.
- Currency: SAR for KSA buyers; VAT applied per ZATCA.
- Payment terms reference: [docs/revenue/CASH_RULES.md](../../revenue/CASH_RULES.md).

## 4. Timeline

5 to 7 business days from paid kickoff. Expedite is an add-on subject to capacity confirmation.

## 5. Intellectual property

- The lead table and next-step pack delivered to the buyer are owned by the buyer for internal use.
- Dealix retains ownership of templates, methods, scoring rules, and underlying tooling.
- Sharing the deliverable with third parties beyond the buyer's organization requires written consent.

## 6. Confidentiality

- Buyer's intake content and any client-shared materials are confidential.
- Dealix may produce anonymized case-safe summaries that do not identify the buyer; explicit identification requires buyer consent.

## 7. Data and privacy

- Dealix runs a PDPL-aware practice (see [docs/02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md](../../02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md)).
- No personal contact data beyond named roles at publicly identifiable companies.
- Buyer is responsible for any data they choose to share with Dealix.

## 8. Refunds

A refund is owed when **any** of the following is true:

- The deliverable falls outside the agreed scope and Dealix cannot rectify within the original SLA.
- Dealix breaches a non-negotiable.
- A control fails in a way that materially harms the buyer.

Refund mechanics in [docs/revenue/CASH_RULES.md](../../revenue/CASH_RULES.md).

## 9. Disclaimers

- Dealix does not guarantee meetings, calls, sales, or revenue.
- Estimated value is not Verified value.
- Dealix does not issue compliance attestations or certifications.

## 10. Channels

Dealix does not send outbound messages on the buyer's behalf as part of the sprint. The buyer's team sends.

## 11. Disputes

- Raised in writing to the founder.
- Founder responds within 5 business days.
- Good-faith resolution attempted before any external step.
- Jurisdiction: KSA, in line with applicable Saudi commercial law.

## 12. Term and termination

- The sprint terminates at handover acceptance.
- Either party may cancel before kickoff; pre-kickoff cancellation is refunded less any reasonable preparation costs already incurred.
