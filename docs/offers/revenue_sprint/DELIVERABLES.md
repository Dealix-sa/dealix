# Revenue Sprint Deliverables

What the buyer receives. What Dealix stores. What "done" means.

## Buyer-facing deliverables

1. **Kick-off note.** Sent within 24 hours of payment. Confirms named bottleneck, named contact, sprint dates.
2. **Working artefact.** The one tangible thing the sprint was scoped to produce. Examples: a reviewed lead table, a documented workflow, a configured agent run.
3. **Bilingual wrap-up brief.** EN + AR. Two pages. Sections: what was done, what worked, what did not, what to do next.
4. **Proof Pack snippet.** Sections drawn from [docs/delivery/PROOF_PACK_TEMPLATE.md](../../delivery/PROOF_PACK_TEMPLATE.md).

## Internal artefacts

- AI Run Ledger entries for every agent touch.
- A signed [QA_CHECKLIST](../../delivery/revenue_sprint/QA_CHECKLIST.md) before the wrap-up brief goes out.
- A weekly Friction Log entry if anything blocked progress.

## Definition of done

The sprint is done when **all four** of these are true:
- Buyer has acknowledged receipt of the wrap-up brief.
- QA Checklist is signed by the Delivery Lead.
- AI Run Ledger has at least one entry tagged to the sprint ID.
- Payment is fully reconciled in `revenue_action_log.csv`.

## Not deliverables

- A copy of internal prompts.
- Raw agent run logs.
- Any third-party data without explicit licensing.

## Owner

Delivery Lead. Reviewed quarterly with Revenue Lead.
