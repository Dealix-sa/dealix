# Revenue Sprint Refund Policy

The buyer can ask for a refund. If the conditions below are met, the refund is issued without an argument.

## Refund-on-request triggers

- The wrap-up brief was not delivered by day 14 (calendar days, holidays excluded).
- The final artefact does not match the scope agreed in the kick-off note.
- A doctrine-level claim was made on Dealix's side that should not have been made (e.g., guaranteed revenue language slipped into a deliverable).

## How the buyer requests a refund

- One sentence by email or in-app to the Revenue Lead.
- No phone call, no escalation required.

## How Dealix handles it

- Refund up to 500 SAR: A1 approval. Founder clicks; processed within 24 hours.
- Refund 501-10,000 SAR: A2 approval. Founder + Trust Lead sign in writing. Processed within 48 hours.
- See [docs/trust/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md](../../trust/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md).

## What Dealix keeps

- The AI Run Ledger entries (private, internal-only).
- The Friction Log entry describing why the refund happened.

## What the buyer keeps

- Any work product they have already received. Dealix does not "claw back" deliverables.

## What this policy is not

- It is not a license to over-promise. Refunds count against the doctrine guard rails.
- It is not a discount mechanism. Discounts go through [PRICING.md](./PRICING.md).
