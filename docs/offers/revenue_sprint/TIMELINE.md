# Revenue Sprint Timeline

A Revenue Sprint runs two calendar weeks. The timeline is a contract: dates do not slip without a logged Change Request.

## Day-by-day

| Day | Milestone | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Payment received and reconciled. | Revenue Lead |
| 1 | Kick-off note sent. Named bottleneck confirmed in writing. | Delivery Lead |
| 2 | Sync 1 (30 min). Inputs collected. | Delivery Lead |
| 4 | Draft artefact V0 stored in private workspace. | Delivery Lead |
| 6 | Sync 2 (30 min). V0 reviewed; revisions agreed. | Delivery Lead |
| 9 | Draft artefact V1 stored. | Delivery Lead |
| 10 | Sync 3 (30 min). Final checks. | Delivery Lead |
| 12 | Final artefact handed off. | Delivery Lead |
| 13 | QA Checklist signed. Wrap-up brief drafted. | Delivery Lead |
| 14 | Wrap-up brief delivered. Sprint closed. | Revenue Lead |

## Slip rules

- A single day slip on a non-handoff milestone is allowed if logged.
- A slip on day 12 or day 14 triggers an automatic refund offer per [REFUND_POLICY.md](./REFUND_POLICY.md).
- A slip caused by the buyer not providing inputs pauses the clock; the pause is logged in the AI Run Ledger.

## Holidays

- Saudi public holidays pause the clock. The buyer is told in writing on day 1 if any holidays fall in the sprint window.

## Owner

Delivery Lead. Reviewed monthly against actual cycle-time data.
