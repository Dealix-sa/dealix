# Revenue Sprint FAQ — الأسئلة الشائعة

## Purpose

The 15 most-asked questions, with answers that survive scrutiny. No overclaims. Buyers can share this page with their team without edits.

## 1. What exactly do I get?

A reviewed table of qualified opportunities (up to 35, by tier), each with a public source and a recommended next step. Plus a next-step pack with outreach openers, call talking points, and qualifying questions.

## 2. Do you send outreach on my behalf?

No. We prepare; you (or your team) send. See [SCOPE.md](./SCOPE.md).

## 3. How long does it take?

5 to 7 business days from paid kickoff. Expedite to 3 business days is an add-on (see [PRICING.md](./PRICING.md)).

## 4. How much does it cost?

499 SAR (Lite), 749 SAR (Standard), or 999 SAR (Plus), plus VAT. KSA buyers pay in SAR.

## 5. Will I get a guaranteed number of meetings or sales?

No. Dealix surfaces qualified opportunities with evidence; conversion depends on your outreach and your buyers. "Estimated value is not Verified value."

## 6. Where do the opportunities come from?

Public sources. Every opportunity carries a source URL and retrieval timestamp. No scraping of closed platforms.

## 7. Is this PDPL-compliant?

Dealix runs a PDPL-aware practice. We do not include personal contact data without permission. We don't issue compliance certifications. See [docs/02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md](../../02_saudi_positioning/PDPL_AWARE_LANGUAGE.md).

## 8. What if I want a different sector or wider geography?

That's a different rung. See [docs/revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md](../../revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md) — Data Pack (1,500 SAR) or Custom AI engagement.

## 9. Do you use AI?

Yes, in a governed way: agents prepare drafts, humans approve before anything leaves the building. See [docs/trust/AUTONOMY_POLICY.md](../../trust/AUTONOMY_POLICY.md).

## 10. Can I get a refund?

Yes, if the deliverable falls outside the agreed scope. Full conditions in [TERMS.md](./TERMS.md) and [docs/revenue/CASH_RULES.md](../../revenue/CASH_RULES.md).

## 11. Can I share the deliverable with my team?

Yes, internally. External sharing requires your standard confidentiality posture; the deliverable is yours.

## 12. Do you sign NDAs?

We are happy to sign a reasonable mutual NDA before intake. Standard template available on request.

## 13. What is my role during the sprint?

Complete the intake form (15 minutes), confirm sector and geography, and attend the 30-minute handover review.

## 14. Can the sprint become a monthly engagement?

Yes — Managed Ops at 2,999–4,999 SAR/month. The sprint deliverable is a useful input but not a prerequisite.

## 15. Why should I trust the output?

Every row has a public source. Every output passes the QA gate ([docs/delivery/revenue_sprint/QA_CHECKLIST.md](../../delivery/revenue_sprint/QA_CHECKLIST.md)). Every claim follows the no-overclaim policy.
