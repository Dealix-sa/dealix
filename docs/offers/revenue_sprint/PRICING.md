# Dealix Revenue Sprint — Pricing

All prices in SAR. Pricing is indicative; final figure is set in the signed proposal.

## Tiers

| Tier | Price (SAR) | Qualified Leads | Sectors | Outreach Pack | Executive Memo | Timeline |
|------|-------------|-----------------|---------|----------------|----------------|----------|
| Starter | 2,500 | up to 30 | 1 | yes | yes | 7 days |
| Standard | 4,500 | up to 50 | up to 2 | yes | yes | 10 days |
| Pro | 7,500 | up to 75 | up to 3 | yes + AR/EN variants | yes + 14-day plan | 14 days |
| Custom | by quote | scoped | scoped | scoped | scoped | by quote |

## What Changes Across Tiers
- Number of qualified opportunities.
- Number of in-scope sectors.
- Depth of evidence per lead.
- Number of outreach message variants.
- Length and depth of executive memo.

## What Does Not Change Across Tiers
- QA checklist must pass before handoff.
- Trust boundaries.
- No-guarantee policy.
- Start condition (payment / PO / written approval).
- Handoff process.

## Payment
- Full amount up front, or
- 50% start + 50% on handoff (Standard and above), or
- Purchase order from authorised contact, or
- Written approval from authorised decision-maker when invoicing terms apply.

## Refunds
- If Dealix fails to deliver the agreed scope by the end of the timeline, the client may request a written remediation plan or a pro-rated refund of the undelivered portion.
- Sales outcomes (replies, meetings, revenue) are explicitly out of scope and are not grounds for refund.

## Discounts
- First-customer-in-sector discount may be offered in exchange for a written case-study approval clause.
- No discount is offered in exchange for waived QA, waived scope, or weakened trust boundaries.

## Out-of-Scope Add-Ons
Available as separate engagements:
- Outreach execution on behalf of client.
- CRM setup or migration.
- Ongoing Revenue Desk retainer.
- Custom dashboards.
