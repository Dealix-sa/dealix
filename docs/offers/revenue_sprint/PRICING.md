# Revenue Sprint Pricing

The Revenue Sprint is priced at **499 SAR**, payable in full before the kick-off note is sent.

## Why 499 SAR

- It is small enough that a department head can sign without an extended approval chain.
- It is large enough that the buyer expects (and gets) a real outcome.
- It anchors the upper rungs of the Offer Ladder. See [docs/revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md](../../revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md).

## What 499 SAR buys

- The full scope as defined in [SCOPE.md](./SCOPE.md).
- One bilingual wrap-up brief.
- A Proof Pack snippet entered into the AI Run Ledger.

## Payment terms

- Pre-paid in full via a Saudi-supported payment provider.
- VAT shown explicitly on the invoice. See [docs/revenue/INVOICE_FLOW.md](../../revenue/INVOICE_FLOW.md).
- No instalments. No deferred payment for Rung 2.

## Discounts

- No public discount. Anyone offered a sub-499 SAR price needs Founder + Trust Lead written approval (A2) before the proposal goes out.
- Bundling with a Managed Retainer at the time of sprint completion is allowed; the bundle is priced as a retainer, not as a discounted sprint.

## Refunds

- Per [REFUND_POLICY.md](./REFUND_POLICY.md): refund-on-request if the wrap-up brief is not delivered by day 14, no questions asked.

## Owner

Revenue Lead. Quarterly review against win-rate and refund rate.
