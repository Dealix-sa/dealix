# Revenue Sprint Pricing

## Tiers

| Tier | Price (SAR) | Leads | Timeline | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 2,500 | 30 | 7 days | Single sector, narrow ICP. |
| Standard | 4,500 | 50 | 10 days | 2 sectors or expanded ICP. |
| Pro | 7,500 | 75 | 14 days | Multi-sector, deeper evidence per lead. |
| Custom | Quote | Custom | Custom | Enterprise scope, NDAs, regulated sectors. |

## What Every Tier Includes
- client intake.
- qualified lead research.
- deduplication.
- A/B/C scoring.
- pain hypothesis.
- Arabic + English outreach pack.
- follow-up cadence.
- executive memo.
- 14-day action plan.
- delivery report with QA pass.
- handoff.

## Payment Terms
- 100% upfront, OR
- signed purchase order, OR
- written approval from an authorized decision-maker.

Work does not start until one of the above is recorded.

## Discounts
- No discount on Starter.
- Standard and Pro: 10% if paid upfront before kickoff.
- Custom: negotiated.

## Refund Policy
- No refund after kickoff is recorded.
- Before kickoff: full refund.

## Upsell Path
After delivery, eligible clients are offered the Revenue Desk monthly retainer:
- weekly opportunity list.
- follow-up support.
- pipeline review.
- messaging improvement.
- monthly learning report.

## Trust Note
Pricing is for opportunity intelligence and execution assets. No tier guarantees replies, meetings, sales, or revenue.
