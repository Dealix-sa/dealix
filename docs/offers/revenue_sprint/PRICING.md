# Dealix Revenue Sprint — Pricing

## Tiers

| Tier | Investment (SAR) | Leads | Sectors | Outreach Pack | Memo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 2,500 | up to 30 | 1 | yes | yes |
| Standard | 4,500 | up to 50 | up to 2 | yes | yes |
| Plus | 7,500 | up to 75 | up to 3 | yes | yes |
| Custom | by scope | by scope | by scope | yes | yes |

## What Is Included In Every Tier
- ICP confirmation.
- Qualified lead research with evidence.
- A/B/C priority scoring.
- Pain hypothesis per top account.
- Arabic / English outreach messages.
- 14-day action plan.
- Executive memo.
- Handoff session.
- QA pass against the public QA checklist.

## What Is Not Included
- Guaranteed sales or revenue.
- Guaranteed replies or meetings.
- Cold outbound on behalf of the client unless separately approved in writing.
- CRM implementation or ad management.
- Legal or compliance certification.

## Start Condition
Work begins only after one of:
- payment confirmation.
- purchase order.
- written approval from an authorized decision-maker.

## Retainer Option
Revenue Desk monthly retainer is offered after the first sprint when value
is demonstrable. Retainer scope and price are agreed separately.

## Notes
Prices are excluding VAT. Invoices in SAR.
