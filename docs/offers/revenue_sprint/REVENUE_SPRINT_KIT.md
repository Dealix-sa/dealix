# Revenue Sprint Kit — حقيبة سبرنت الإيرادات

## Purpose

The complete kit for the 499 SAR Revenue Sprint: what's in it, who buys it, what it is not. One page that anyone (founder, partner, advisor) can open to understand the product.

## Who it's for

- Saudi operators (B2B services, light manufacturing, healthcare admin, education ops, fintech ops).
- Founders or commercial leads with a defined sector and ICP.
- Buyers who want a reviewed, evidence-attached opportunity table — not a list scraped at scale.

## Who it's not for

- Buyers seeking cold-outreach automation, scraping, or list-building at scale.
- Companies without an internal decision-maker present in the conversation.
- Use cases needing certified compliance attestations (Dealix is PDPL-aware, not a certifier).

## What the kit is NOT

- A guarantee of sales.
- A subscription. The sprint is a one-time purchase; retainers are a separate rung.
- A replacement for an internal commercial team.
- A regulatory clearance. PDPL alignment is a practice we maintain, not a stamp we issue.
