# Revenue Sprint Kit

## Purpose
Provide the complete sales, delivery, trust, and learning kit for the Dealix Revenue Sprint.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly during first 10 sprints, then monthly.

## Inputs
- ICP
- target sectors
- lead sources
- client intake
- founder DM
- sample response
- proposal approval
- payment / PO / written approval

## Outputs
- sample pack
- proposal
- delivery report
- outreach pack
- QA checklist
- handoff
- feedback request
- retainer ask

## Revenue Sprint Promise
Dealix helps B2B companies identify qualified opportunities, prioritize accounts, and prepare outreach assets.

## What It Is Not
- not guaranteed sales
- not guaranteed revenue
- not legal/compliance certification
- not fully autonomous outreach
- not a replacement for the client's sales team

1. Founder DM Pack
2. Sample Pack Template

4. Payment Follow-Up Templates
5. Client Intake

8. Outreach Pack Template
9. Report Template

12. Feedback Request

## Rules
- No full delivery without payment, PO, or written approval.
- No public claim without evidence.
- No client data in public repo.
- No A-priority lead without evidence.
- No delivery without QA.
- No case study without approval.

## Metrics
- sample-to-reply rate
- reply-to-call rate
- call-to-proposal rate
- proposal-to-payment rate
- delivery QA pass rate
- feedback received
- retainer attempt rate

## Evidence
