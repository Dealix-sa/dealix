# Sample Pack — `<client>` / `<outcome>`

> Used at the "show me" stage. **Public** template — populated with real
> client data only inside `clients/<client>/` in the **private** repo.

## 1. Context (3 sentences)

- Who they are.
- What they're trying to do.
- Where they're stuck.

## 2. Signal we found

One concrete signal that proves we understand them — pulled from their
website, job posts, public filings, GitHub, LinkedIn activity, or PDPL
disclosures.

## 3. Sample (1 page max)

Pick **one** of the following, depending on the chosen outcome:

- **Pipeline Lift:** 5 named, qualified leads with rationale.
- **Outreach Pack:** 3 ready-to-send DMs personalised to real prospects.
- **Proof Pack:** a 1-page measurable result on their data.
- **Pricing Reset:** their current price card with 3 specific moves.
- **Retainer Bridge:** their top happy client + the retainer ask script.

## 4. What the full sprint would deliver

3 bullets, each one a concrete artefact.

## 5. Price + timeline

- SAR <amount>
- 7 days from sprint start
- Single point of contact: <founder name>

## 6. Next step

One sentence. Either "reply to confirm and we send the intake form" or "book
the 15-min call". Never two asks.

## Hard constraints

- 1 page printable.
- No "we are excited to" / "synergies" / "world-class" / "leading provider".
- No promises that depend on Dealix doing something it has not done before.
- All claims sourceable to public data or to the client's own data.
