# Revenue Sprint — Offer

> Owner: Founder. Status: Pilot. Approval to change: Founder.

A seven-day, founder-led engagement that produces a ranked outbound list
and a tested message pack for a Saudi B2B team.

## Who it's for

- Saudi B2B teams (founder / head of sales / head of growth) who have an
  ICP but no repeatable way to fill the top of the funnel.
- Teams with at least one closed-won deal — we tune the outbound to
  resemble their best customer, not invent one.

## What they get

1. ICP read-out: top 3 segments ranked by fit + intent.
2. Ranked list of 30–50 named accounts inside the priority segment.
3. Per-account hypothesis and primary contact handle.
4. Three outbound message variants (AR + EN) ready for human send.
5. A short report on what to do for the next 30 days.

## What we do NOT promise

- No guaranteed meetings.
- No guaranteed pipeline value.
- No data scraping that violates platform terms.
- No outbound auto-send: every message is queued for founder review and
  sent by the client.

## Duration and cadence

- Day 0: kickoff call (60 min).
- Days 1–2: research and ICP read-out (no client time).
- Day 3: read-out review (45 min).
- Days 4–5: list build + message pack drafting.
- Day 6: final report walkthrough (60 min).
- Day 7: handoff package delivered.
