# Dealix Revenue Sprint — Offer

## What It Is
A 7–14 day engagement that produces a qualified, prioritized list of
Saudi B2B opportunities and a ready-to-use outreach pack.

## Who It Is For
- B2B companies in Saudi Arabia.
- Teams with a defined offering but unclear or unstructured pipeline.
- Founders or sales leads who need actionable opportunities, not theory.

## What The Client Gets
- 30–75 qualified opportunities matching the agreed ICP.
- A/B/C priority scoring with evidence.
- Pain-hypothesis notes per account.
- Arabic and English outreach messages.
- Follow-up cadence.
- Executive memo.
- 14-day action plan.

## Engagement Shape
- Client intake.
- Founder-led research and curation.
- QA pass against the public QA checklist.
- Handoff session.
- Optional Revenue Desk retainer.

## Trust Boundary
Dealix delivers opportunity intelligence and execution assets.
Dealix does not guarantee sales, revenue, replies, or meetings.
