# Revenue Sprint — Official Offer — العرض الرسمي

## Outcome

You receive a reviewed table of qualified opportunities in your target sector, each with a source attribution and a recommended next step. You leave the sprint with a decision frame, not a guess.

## Scope (in)

- One sector, up to two sub-segments.
- One Saudi region or up to three cities.
- Up to 25 opportunities surfaced; top 5–10 prioritized.
- Source attribution per opportunity (public source URL + retrieval timestamp).
- A next-step pack: outreach openers, call talking points, qualifying questions.

## Scope (out)

- Outbound messages sent on your behalf.
- Cold WhatsApp, scraping closed platforms, automated LinkedIn outreach.
- Compliance attestation or certification.
- A guarantee of meetings, calls, or sales.

Full scope detail: [SCOPE.md](./SCOPE.md).

## Timeline

5 to 7 business days from paid kickoff to handover.

## Price

499 SAR + VAT, paid in full at kickoff. See [PRICING.md](./PRICING.md) for tier breakdown and add-ons.

## What you do

1. Complete the intake form (15 minutes).
2. Confirm the sector and target geography.
3. Attend the 30-min handover review at the end.

## What we do

1. Build the lead table from public sources.
2. Score against [docs/delivery/revenue_sprint/SCORING_RULES.md](../../delivery/revenue_sprint/SCORING_RULES.md).
3. Tailor the next-step pack to your stated ICP.
4. Run the QA gate ([docs/delivery/revenue_sprint/QA_CHECKLIST.md](../../delivery/revenue_sprint/QA_CHECKLIST.md)).
5. Deliver the pack and walk you through it.

## Terms in short

- Payment: 100% in advance.
- Refund: if the deliverable falls outside the agreed scope, we refund the diagnostic fee.
- Confidentiality: your intake and resulting deliverable are confidential.
- Full terms: [TERMS.md](./TERMS.md).

## What this offer is not

- Not a promise of revenue.
- Not a replacement for a commercial team.
- Not an automated outbound service.

Estimated value is not Verified value.
