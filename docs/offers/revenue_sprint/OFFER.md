# Revenue Sprint Offer

## What It Is
A 7–14 day engagement that produces an opportunity intelligence pack and an actionable outreach kit for a Saudi B2B company.

## Who It Is For
B2B companies in Saudi Arabia that have a real offer and a sales team, but lack structured opportunity intelligence and outreach assets.

## What The Client Receives
- 30–75 qualified opportunities matched to ICP.
- A/B/C priority scoring with evidence notes.
- Pain hypothesis per priority segment.
- Arabic + English outreach messages.
- Follow-up cadence.
- Executive memo.
- 14-day action plan.
- Delivery report with QA pass.
- Handoff with operating cadence.

## What It Is Not
- Not guaranteed sales.
- Not guaranteed meetings or replies.
- Not legal or compliance certification.
- Not fully autonomous outreach.
- Not a replacement for the client's sales team.

## Start Condition
Work starts only after one of:
- payment confirmation.
- signed purchase order.
- written approval from an authorized decision-maker.

## Trust Note
Dealix delivers opportunity intelligence and execution assets. Dealix does not guarantee replies, meetings, sales, or revenue.
