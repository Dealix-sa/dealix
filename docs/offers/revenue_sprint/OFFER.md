# Dealix Revenue Sprint — Offer

## What It Is
A 7–14 day sprint that produces qualified Saudi B2B opportunities, scored by priority, with an outreach pack and a 14-day action plan that the client's own sales team executes.

## Who It's For
B2B companies operating in Saudi Arabia that already have a working product or service and need a sharper way to identify and approach the right accounts.

## What The Client Gets
- 30–75 qualified opportunities matched to the client's ICP.
- A/B/C scoring with evidence per lead.
- Pain hypothesis per priority account.
- Arabic and English outreach messages.
- A 14-day follow-up cadence.
- An executive memo summarising findings and the recommended first move.

## What The Client Does
- Approves the intake (ICP, sectors, exclusions).
- Approves the proposal.
- Pays or issues PO or written approval before delivery starts.
- Executes the outreach themselves using the prepared pack.
- Returns feedback after the sprint ends.

## What Dealix Does
- Runs intake and clarifies the ICP.
- Researches and deduplicates leads.
- Scores leads with evidence.
- Drafts outreach pack.
- Delivers executive memo and 14-day plan.
- Runs the QA checklist before handoff.

## What Dealix Does Not Do
- Send outreach on behalf of the client (unless separately scoped).
- Guarantee sales, replies, meetings, or revenue.
- Make legal, regulatory, or compliance certifications.
- Replace the client's sales team.

## Promise Boundary
Dealix delivers opportunity intelligence and execution assets. Sales outcomes depend on the client's own execution.

## Start Condition
Work begins after one of:
- Payment confirmation.
- Purchase order.
- Written approval from an authorised decision-maker.

## Delivery Window
7–14 days after the start condition is met.

## Next Step After Sprint
If the sprint produces useful pipeline signal, the client can convert to the monthly **Revenue Desk** retainer for ongoing opportunity intelligence and outreach support.
