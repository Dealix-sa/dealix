# Revenue Sprint Case Study Template

A case study is a public document. It needs A2 approval before it leaves the building. Use this template so the trust boundary stays intact.

## Header

- Sector (high-level, never the buyer's exact identity unless they signed off).
- Sprint duration.
- Sprint date range.

## Section 1 — The bottleneck

In two sentences, what was the buyer's stated problem.

## Section 2 — What was done

The artefact, in one paragraph. Reference the in-scope items from [SCOPE.md](./SCOPE.md).

## Section 3 — Outcomes (calibrated)

Use the three-tier value vocabulary from [docs/08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md](../../08_value_os/ESTIMATED_OBSERVED_VERIFIED_VALUE.md):

- **Estimated**: what the artefact is expected to enable.
- **Observed**: what the buyer reported during the sprint.
- **Verified**: what was independently confirmed after the sprint.

Every number carries one of those three tags. No untagged numbers.

## Section 4 — What we did not do

State explicitly the work that the buyer might have hoped for but that was out of scope. This is honesty.

## Section 5 — What we learned

One paragraph. What surprised the team. What we would do differently.

## Section 6 — Approval

- Buyer wrote: "approved for publication" (recorded in their email).
- Trust Lead signed off.
- Founder counter-signed.

## Forbidden in case studies

- "guaranteed revenue"
- "100% compliance"
- "fully autonomous"
- Any verified figure that has not actually been verified.

## Owner

Revenue Lead. Reviewed monthly.
