# Revenue Sprint Scope

## In Scope
- Client intake interview.
- ICP refinement.
- Qualified lead research (30–75 accounts depending on tier).
- Deduplication and bad-fit removal.
- A/B/C priority scoring with evidence notes.
- Pain hypothesis per priority segment.
- Arabic + English outreach messages (initial + 2 follow-ups).
- Follow-up cadence design.
- Executive memo.
- 14-day action plan.
- Delivery report with QA pass.
- Handoff session.

## Out Of Scope
- Guaranteed sales, replies, or meetings.
- Sending outreach on behalf of the client (unless separately approved).
- Ad management.
- CRM implementation or migration.
- Legal, regulatory, or compliance certification.
- Procurement, contracting, or pricing negotiations on behalf of the client.
- Cold scraping of personal data outside lawful business directories.
- Translation of legal documents.

## Change Requests
- Mid-sprint scope changes require written approval.
- Anything beyond the tier's lead count or sector count is a new engagement.

## Inputs Required From Client
- Authorized point of contact.
- ICP description.
- Target sectors.
- Geography.
- Average deal size (optional but recommended).
- Exclusions list.
- Success definition.
- Approval contact for delivery and public claims.

## Confidentiality
- Client data stays in the private operations area.
- No client name used publicly without written approval.
- No case study without approval.

## Trust Note
Scope is for opportunity intelligence and execution assets, not for guaranteed commercial outcomes.
