# Dealix Revenue Sprint — Scope

## In Scope

### Discovery
- Client intake (ICP, sectors, geography, exclusions, deal size, success definition).
- Approval contact confirmation.
- Data-constraint review.

### Research
- Identification of qualified B2B accounts in Saudi Arabia matching the agreed ICP.
- Deduplication against the client's existing customer or exclusion list when provided.
- Evidence note per A-priority lead.

### Scoring
- A/B/C scoring rules documented and applied consistently across the deliverable.
- A-priority requires evidence.

### Outreach Pack
- One primary outreach message (Arabic).
- One Arabic follow-up.
- One English variant where requested.
- Optional second follow-up at Pro tier.

### Memo and Plan
- Executive memo summarising findings, top opportunities, and risks.
- 14-day action plan with concrete actions on Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14.

### Quality
- QA checklist applied before handoff.
- Trust review for public-claim exposure.

### Handoff
- Delivery report.
- Handoff document with recommended first action and operating cadence.
- Feedback request after handoff.

## Out of Scope (unless separately quoted)
- Sending outreach on behalf of the client.
- Cold-calling on behalf of the client.
- CRM implementation, migration, or administration.
- Ad campaign management.
- Legal, regulatory, or compliance certification.
- Translation of materials beyond the outreach pack.
- Custom dashboards or BI tooling.
- Long-term lead nurturing.
- Account-based marketing programmes.

## Change Requests
Any change to ICP, sector, lead count, or timeline after the start condition is met requires a written change note from the approval contact. Dealix will respond with the impact on timeline and price before proceeding.

## Cancellation
- Before the start condition: no cost.
- After the start condition and before research begins: 25% retention fee.
- After research begins: pro-rated by completed milestones.

## Confidentiality
- Client data stays in private Dealix operations storage.
- No client name, logo, or quote is used externally without written approval.
- Public learning notes are aggregated and anonymised.

## Trust Boundary
Dealix delivers opportunity intelligence and execution assets. Sales outcomes depend on the client. Dealix does not guarantee sales, replies, meetings, or revenue.
