# Revenue Sprint Scope
## Included
- ICP review.
- Lead research.
- Lead scoring.
- Outreach copy.
- Follow-up cadence.
- Executive summary.
- Action plan.
## Excluded
- Guaranteed replies.
- Guaranteed meetings.
- Guaranteed revenue.
- Legal advice.
- Sending messages without approval.
- Managing paid ads.
- Full CRM implementation.
- Long-term sales management unless upgraded to Managed Pilot or Revenue Desk.
