# Offer Card — Sales Command Center (4–6 weeks)

## One-liner

> لوحة قيادة يومية للمدير العام: pipeline، follow-ups، approvals، stuck deals، next action.

## Target customer

- Founder / GM of a 10–100 person B2B company.
- Already has audit + WhatsApp OS results.
- Wants visibility without micromanaging.

## Buyer persona

- **Role:** Founder, GM, VP Sales.
- **Mindset:** "I cannot read every thread. I need a daily snapshot."
- **Trigger:** scaling team; or, "I am flying blind."

## Pain solved

- No daily visibility into pipeline status.
- Stuck deals go unnoticed for weeks.
- Approvals pile up in personal inboxes.
- No link between ops data and decisions.

## Promise without overclaim

- "تشوف كل يوم 5 أشياء محددة تحتاج قرار."
- "تقرير يومي خلال 5 دقائق من بداية يومك."
- "كل stuck deal يظهر بعد 3 أيام بدون حركة."

## Scope

- Pipeline dashboard (read-only connectors to CRM + WhatsApp + email).
- Daily digest (top 5 follow-ups + 5 next actions).
- Stuck-deal detector (no movement in 3 / 7 / 14 days).
- Approval queue summary (number of items, oldest, who is blocking).
- Weekly value report.
- 4 weekly review calls with the founder.

## Out of scope

- Acting on the dashboard (we don't send, we don't update CRM).
- Replacing the CRM.
- Forecasting revenue (separate module; not in week 1).

## Timeline

- Week 1–2: connectors + dashboard build.
- Week 3: daily digest in production.
- Week 4: stuck-deal detector + approval summary.
- Week 5–6: review + tweak + first renewal.

## Required access

- Read-only API access to the CRM.
- Read-only access to the WhatsApp Business data (already in OS).
- A signed consent record.

## Deliverables

- Command center dashboard.
- Daily digest email.
- Weekly value report.
- 4 weekly review calls.
- Renewal decision.

## Success metrics

- Founder reads the digest 5+ days a week.
- Stuck deals are flagged within 3 days of inactivity.
- 0 missed approvals > 48h.

## Pricing status

`approved_range_required` — first 5 deals set the range, founder finalizes.

## Upsell path

- Proposal & Proof Pack OS (offer 4) once visibility is stable.

## Objections

- **"We already have a CRM dashboard."** — "CRM dashboards are inputs. Ours is the decision layer above them."
- **"We don't have time to read daily digests."** — "5 minutes. If you can't spare that, the gap is bigger than the tool can fix."

## Forbidden claims

- "نحل محل المدير العام." (we don't)
- "نتوقع المبيعات بالـ AI." (we don't, separate module)

## Safe claims

- "5 دقائق في اليوم."
- "كل stuck deal يظهر."
- "كل approval له عمر."
- "تقرير أسبوعي للمجلس."

## Proposal sections

1. Context (post-WhatsApp-OS findings).
2. Dashboard scope.
3. Daily digest format.
4. Stuck-deal rules.
5. Out of scope.
6. Success metrics.
7. Pricing (range; founder finalizes).
8. Renewal criteria.
