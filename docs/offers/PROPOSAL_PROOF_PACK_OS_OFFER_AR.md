# Offer Card — Proposal & Proof Pack OS (4–8 weeks)

## One-liner

> نولّد عروض مدعومة بـ proof packs. كل claim له evidence_level. كل عرض له payment handoff يحتاج موافقة.

## Target customer

- Sales lead or founder of a 20+ person B2B company.
- Sends 5+ proposals a month.
- Wants stronger proposals without burning the founder's time.

## Buyer persona

- **Role:** Head of sales, founder, sales engineer.
- **Mindset:** "Our proposals are slow and look weak next to the big players."
- **Trigger:** losing deals to better-presented competitors.

## Pain solved

- Proposal prep takes 4+ hours per deal.
- No consistent proof points.
- Founder reviews every proposal (bottleneck).
- Pricing + scope conflate in the same doc.

## Promise without overclaim

- "نخفض وقت إعداد العرض من 4 ساعات إلى 30 دقيقة."
- "كل claim له evidence_level ظاهر."
- "كل payment handoff يمر على approval قبل الإرسال."

## Scope

- Proposal template library (per offer).
- Proof pack library (case studies, before/after, ROI discussion rules).
- Draft generation (with evidence-bound claims).
- Approval queue (founder reviews before send).
- Payment handoff policy (no link, no final price in draft).
- 4 weekly review calls.

## Out of scope

- Auto-sending proposals.
- Final pricing (founder approves per deal).
- Generating payment links.

## Timeline

- Week 1–2: template + proof pack library.
- Week 3: draft generation in production.
- Week 4: approval queue + payment handoff.
- Week 5–8: rollout + review + first renewal.

## Required access

- Past proposals (to extract structure).
- Approved case studies (with permission).
- Founder's signature on the trust policy.

## Deliverables

- Proposal templates.
- Proof pack library.
- Draft generator.
- Approval queue.
- Payment handoff policy.
- 4 weekly review calls.

## Success metrics

- Proposal prep time drops ≥ 50%.
- Every proposal carries ≥ 3 evidence-bound claims.
- 0 trust preflight violations.
- 0 final prices in drafts (founder adds them).

## Pricing status

`approved_range_required` — first 5 deals set the range.

## Upsell path

- AI Operating System for SMB (offer 5) once proposals are stable.

## Objections

- **"We use a Google Doc template."** — "We add the proof + the approval + the audit trail. The template is the easy part."
- **"AI proposals look generic."** — "Ours are evidence-bound. Generic is a feature of the prompt, not the tool."

## Forbidden claims

- "نكتب العروض بدل فريقك." (we add a layer)
- "نضمن نسبة إغلاق أعلى." (no guarantees)

## Safe claims

- "وقت أقل."
- "proof ظاهر."
- "approval قبل الإرسال."
- "سجل تدقيق."
