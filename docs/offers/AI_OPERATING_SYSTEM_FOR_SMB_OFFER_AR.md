# Offer Card — AI Operating System for SMB (8–12 weeks)

## One-liner

> نظام تشغيل كامل للشركة: workflows + dashboards + reports + draft factory + approval gates.

## Target customer

- Founder of a 30+ person B2B company.
- Has done 2+ of the previous offers.
- Wants the full OS, not another tool.

## Buyer persona

- **Role:** Founder, COO, GM.
- **Mindset:** "We've stitched 4 tools together. We want one layer above all of them."
- **Trigger:** scaling team; or, "we are paying for 6 SaaS and using 2."

## Pain solved

- Tool sprawl (6 SaaS, 2 used).
- Reporting fragmentation.
- Approval bottlenecks.
- No daily decision layer.

## Promise without overclaim

- "تشغّل الشركة على نظام واحد واضح."
- "تقرير يومي واحد بدل 6."
- "approval واحد بدل 4."

## Scope

- Discovery: 2 weeks.
- Architecture: 2 weeks.
- Implementation: 4–6 weeks.
- Training: 1–2 weeks.
- 4 weekly review calls during implementation.
- Quarterly business review.

## Out of scope

- Replacing your CRM (we integrate).
- Sending outbound campaigns (we don't do cold).
- Generating final prices (founder approves).

## Timeline

- Week 1–2: discovery + architecture doc.
- Week 3–8: implementation in sprints.
- Week 9–10: training.
- Week 11–12: stabilization + first QBR.

## Required access

- Admin access to the systems we integrate.
- A signed consent record.
- Founder's time: 2 hours/week for the duration.

## Deliverables

- Architecture doc.
- Implemented OS layer.
- Training materials.
- QBR template.
- 1 year of support (renewable).

## Success metrics

- Number of distinct SaaS reduced by ≥ 30%.
- Daily digest adoption ≥ 5 days/week.
- 0 trust preflight violations.

## Pricing status

`founder_approval_required` — every deal, no range yet.

## Upsell path

- Custom Enterprise OS (offer 6) for companies that outgrow SMB shape.

## Objections

- **"We need to keep our CRM."** — "You do. We layer on top."
- **"We don't have time for 12 weeks."** — "The discovery alone takes 2 weeks. If you can't invest 2 weeks, the OS isn't the right shape."

## Forbidden claims

- "نستبدل فريقك." (we don't)
- "ROI مضمون." (never)

## Safe claims

- "نظام تشغيل واحد."
- "approval موحد."
- "تقرير موحد."
- "تدريب + QBR."
