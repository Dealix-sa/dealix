# Offer Card — Custom Enterprise OS (12+ weeks)

## One-liner

> حل مخصص للعمليات المعقدة: discovery, architecture, implementation, governance, training.

## Target customer

- COO / VP Operations of a 100+ person company.
- Multiple sales motions, multiple regions, complex approval flows.
- Ready to invest in a custom layer.

## Buyer persona

- **Role:** COO, VP Ops, Chief of Staff.
- **Mindset:** "Off-the-shelf doesn't fit. We need a layer on top of our stack."
- **Trigger:** scaling beyond what current tools can support.

## Pain solved

- Ops fragmentation across regions.
- Approval bottlenecks at scale.
- Compliance + governance in regulated industries.
- Multiple decision-makers, no single layer.

## Promise without overclaim

- "نبني النظام على العمليات مالتك، مش على template."
- "governance مدمج، مش bolt-on."
- "تدريب شامل لكل المستويات."

## Scope

- Deep discovery (4 weeks).
- Architecture + governance doc (4 weeks).
- Implementation (12+ weeks, in sprints).
- Training (2 weeks, three levels: founder, ops, end users).
- Quarterly business review for the first year.

## Out of scope

- Replacing your ERP / CRM.
- Sending outbound campaigns.
- Auto-replies to clients.

## Timeline

- 6+ months total. The 12-week figure is a floor.

## Required access

- Executive sponsor with decision authority.
- 1 program manager from the client side.
- Admin access to the systems we integrate.

## Deliverables

- Architecture + governance doc.
- Implemented OS layer.
- Training program (3 levels).
- QBR template + first 4 QBRs.
- 1 year of support (renewable).

## Success metrics

- Custom-defined with the client in the discovery phase.
- We do not commit to numbers we cannot measure.

## Pricing status

`founder_approval_required` — per deal. No public range.

## Objections

- **"We have a custom-built ERP."** — "We layer on top of the ERP, not on top of the data."
- **"We've been burned by custom software."** — "We are a layer, not a replacement. The risk is lower."

## Forbidden claims

- "نستبدل الـ ERP." (we don't)
- "ضمان على الـ uptime." (out of scope; we use your infra)

## Safe claims

- "custom layer."
- "governance مدمج."
- "training شامل."
- "QBR ربع سنوي."

## Proposal sections

1. Context + executive sponsor alignment.
2. Discovery outputs.
3. Architecture + governance.
4. Implementation plan (sprints).
5. Out of scope.
6. Success metrics (custom).
7. Pricing (founder approval).
8. Renewal + QBR.
