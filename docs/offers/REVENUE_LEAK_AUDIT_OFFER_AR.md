# Offer Card — Revenue Leak Audit (3–5 days)

> **Status:** Default first offer. All offers ship with this same shape.

## One-liner

> نطلع لك 10 فرص ضايعة في 5 أيام. بدون ربط، بدون عقد، بدون سعر في العرض.

## Target customer

- Marketing & advertising agency, 5–50 people, Saudi Arabia.
- Founder / owner is the buyer.
- Already has WhatsApp as their "informal CRM".
- Knows they are losing follow-ups but cannot quantify.

## Buyer persona

- **Role:** Owner, GM, or head of sales.
- **Age:** 28–45.
- **Mindset:** "We are busy, but not growing fast enough. Something is leaking."
- **Trigger event:** lost a big client, missed a renewal, end of quarter underperformance.
- **What they fear:** spending money on another tool nobody uses.

## Pain solved

- "I have no idea how many leads we lose every week."
- "Our follow-up is chaos. WhatsApp is full of unanswered threads."
- "I know we are losing deals. I cannot prove it."

## Promise without overclaim

- "نحدد على الأقل 10 فرص ضايعة في 5 أيام."
- "نعطيك تقرير واحد + خطة أسبوع."
- "ما نلمس بياناتك بدون موافقة، وما نرسل رسائل."

## Scope

- One 60-minute observation session with the founder or sales lead.
- Pull 2 weeks of WhatsApp + email + CRM data (with permission).
- Tag 10 specific lost opportunities.
- Map each to a fix.
- Deliver a 1-page report + a 30-min debrief.

## Out of scope

- Sending any outbound message.
- Pricing the next offer.
- Implementing a fix.
- More than 10 findings (we cap to keep it sharp).

## Timeline

- Day 1: observation session + permission grant.
- Day 2–3: pull + analyze data.
- Day 4: build the report.
- Day 5: deliver + 30-min debrief.

## Required access

- Read-only access to WhatsApp Business (with their consent).
- Read-only access to the CRM (if any).
- Read-only access to the inbox (if relevant).
- A signed consent record (template in `templates/launch/`).

## Deliverables

- 1-page audit report (PDF or Markdown).
- Spreadsheet of the 10 leaks with severity + fix.
- 30-min debrief call.
- 1 specific quick win to run this week.

## Success metrics

- Client receives 10 specific findings (not 10 generic).
- 1 quick win is implemented by the client within 7 days.
- 0 trust preflight violations during the audit.

## Proof needed

- One case study (after the first audit).
- 0 client logos without permission.
- Numbers must come from real data, not estimates.

## Risk flags

- Client wants the audit for free and has no follow-up intent. Walk away.
- Client asks us to send a campaign on their behalf. Out of scope. Decline.
- Client wants pricing during the debrief. Hold. Schedule a separate call.

## Pricing status

`draft_only` — no number set. Founder quotes per deal in week 1.

## Upsell path

After the audit, the natural next step is:

- WhatsApp & Follow-up OS (offer 2) if WhatsApp chaos is the dominant leak.
- Sales Command Center (offer 3) if pipeline visibility is the gap.
- Either, after a short pilot.

## First call pitch (30 sec)

> "أنا من Dealix. نعمل audit لمتابعة عملائك — 5 أيام، تقرير واحد، 10 فرص ضايعة. بدون ربط، بدون عقد. لو ما طلعنا شي يستاهل، ما نكمل. تحب تجرب؟"

## Two-minute pitch

> "أنت ضايع فرص في الواتساب والمتابعة. ما عندك وقت تقعد تحلل. Dealix يدخل 5 أيام، يشوف الـ pipeline + الواتساب + الإيميل، يطلع لك 10 فرص ضايعة محددة + خطة إصلاح. ما نلمس بياناتك بدون إذن، ما نرسل رسائل، ما نعدك بأرقام. لو التقرير ما استفدت منه، تنسى الموضوع كله. تحب نبدأ؟"

## Objections

- **"كم يكلف؟"** — "السعر نحسمه بعد ما تشوف التقرير. لو ما استفدت، ما في سعر."
- **"كم من الوقت؟"** — "5 أيام من يوم ما تعطيني الوصول."
- **"ليش ما هو مجاني؟"** — "الaudit الجاد مو مجاني. اللي مجاني في السوق إما template أو عرض. هذا شغل يدوي على بياناتك."

## Forbidden claims

- "نضمن لك زيادة 30% في الـ pipeline." (no guarantee)
- "Audit مجاني مع التزام بالنتائج." (no free + no commitment)
- "Dealix منافس لـ HubSpot." (not the framing)

## Safe claims

- "نحدد 10 فرص ضايعة على الأقل."
- "5 أيام، تقرير واحد."
- "ما نرسل رسائل بدون إذن."
- "كل تقرير مبني على بياناتك الحقيقية."

## Proposal sections (when the audit is sold)

1. **Context** — what we will look at, what we will not.
2. **Scope of work** — day-by-day.
3. **Access** — what we need from you.
4. **Deliverables** — the report + the debrief.
5. **Out of scope** — what we will not do.
6. **Success metric** — 10 specific findings + 1 quick win.
7. **Pricing** — `draft_only`; founder quotes.
8. **Next step** — sign the consent + book Day 1.
