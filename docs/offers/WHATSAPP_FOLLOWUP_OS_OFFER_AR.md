# Offer Card — WhatsApp & Follow-up OS (2–4 weeks)

## One-liner

> نرتب الواتساب والـ inbox عندك. routing، تصنيف، قوالب، تقارير يومية، approval قبل أي رد.

## Target customer

- Sales / customer success lead in a 5–50 person B2B company.
- Has audit findings about WhatsApp chaos.
- Willing to give explicit consent for routing.

## Buyer persona

- **Role:** Head of sales, sales lead, customer success lead.
- **Mindset:** "I spend half my day in WhatsApp and still miss things."
- **Trigger:** post-audit; or, "we have a VA, but we need visibility."

## Pain solved

- Lost replies in WhatsApp groups.
- No visibility into who replied, who didn't, who needs a nudge.
- No daily digest of follow-up status.
- Risk of auto-replies that damage the brand.

## Promise without overclaim

- "نخفض وقت فرز الواتساب من 40 دقيقة إلى 5 دقائق."
- "تشوف كل يوم 5 أشياء محددة تحتاج متابعة."
- "كل رد يمر على approval قبل الإرسال."

## Scope

- Routing setup (which threads go to which person).
- Tag taxonomy (consultation, follow-up, booking, complaint, etc.).
- Approval queue (founder reviews before any send).
- Daily digest (top 5 follow-ups for tomorrow).
- 4 weekly value reports.
- Training for the team on the new flow.

## Out of scope

- Cold WhatsApp outreach. (We do not do this.)
- Auto-replying to clients. (We are read-only by default; send requires approval.)
- Removing human judgment from sales conversations.

## Timeline

- Week 1: setup + training.
- Week 2: rollout with first 5 threads.
- Week 3: full rollout.
- Week 4: review + first renewal conversation.

## Required access

- WhatsApp Business API access.
- A signed consent record.
- Admin access to the routing tool.
- A weekly 30-min review call.

## Deliverables

- Routing rules.
- Tag taxonomy.
- Approval queue dashboard.
- Daily digest email.
- 4 weekly value reports.
- Renewal decision (continue, expand, or pause).

## Success metrics

- Average reply time drops ≥ 30%.
- Lost threads drop ≥ 50% (measured by tagged "no_reply" count).
- 0 auto-send violations.
- 0 cold WhatsApp messages.

## Pricing status

`approved_range_required` — first 5 deals set the range, founder finalizes.

## Upsell path

- Sales Command Center (offer 3) once WhatsApp flow is stable.

## Objections

- **"We already use a VA."** — "VA + Dealix > VA alone. The VA gets a daily digest, not raw threads."
- **"Can the bot reply for us?"** — "We don't recommend auto-reply. We can draft, you approve, you send."
- **"This is just WhatsApp Business, right?"** — "We add routing, tags, approval, and reporting on top. Different shape."

## Forbidden claims

- "نرد على العملاء 24/7 بالـ AI." (we don't auto-reply)
- "نرسل رسائل cold بالـ AI." (never)
- "نحل محل فريقك." (we add a layer)

## Safe claims

- "نخفض وقت الفرز."
- "كل رد يمر على موافقة."
- "تقرير يومي واضح."
- "نتوافق مع PDPL."

## Proposal sections

2. Routing + tag design.
3. Approval flow.
4. Daily digest format.
5. Weekly value report.
6. Out of scope (cold, auto-reply, replacement).
7. Success metrics.
8. Pricing (range; founder finalizes).
9. Renewal criteria.
