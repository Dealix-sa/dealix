# Offers

## Purpose
Hold the canonical definition of every commercial offer Dealix sells, so sales, delivery, finance, and trust read from the same source.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly until the first 10 paid deliveries, then monthly.

## Inputs
- Customer calls and replies.
- Proposal outcomes.
- Delivery feedback and QA results.
- Retainer conversion data.
- Pricing experiments.

## Outputs
- Canonical offer definitions (name, price, scope, inclusions, exclusions).
- Upgrade path from sample to retainer.
- Pricing guidance for sales conversations.
- Inputs to `docs/revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md`.

## Rules
- No offer is sold that is not defined here.
- Every offer states what is included and what is explicitly out of scope.
- No guaranteed revenue or compliance claims may appear in any offer.
- Price exceptions require A2 founder approval and are logged.

## Metrics
- Conversion rate per offer.
- Average deal size per offer.
- Time from proposal to payment per offer.
- Retainer conversion from each offer.

## Evidence
- `docs/revenue/OFFER_LADDER.md`.
- proposal notes.
- payment records.
- delivery reports.
