Operating doctrineSOAEN — no step without evidence

  • Source — every fact has a legitimate origin
  • Owner — every opportunity has a single owner
  • Approval — no external action without consent
  • Evidence — a reviewable proof pack, always
  • Next Action — scheduled, not implied
