## 7-Day Revenue Proof Sprint — Rung 1 (recomputed at canonical price)

```text
Price: SAR 499 (one-time)
Founder hours: ~2–3 (verified-product delivery, not 12)
AI/LLM cost: < SAR 10
Direct delivery cost: ~SAR 10
Gross margin: ~85% (per the canonical offer ladder)
```

Note: at 499 SAR the Sprint is a customer-acquisition / proof product, not a
margin product on its own — the economics work because it converts to the
1,500 SAR Pack and the 2,999–4,999 SAR/mo retainer (see LTV in
`docs/UNIT_ECONOMICS_AND_MARGIN.md`).

## Pricing rule

```text
If a service consumes heavy time without building reusable assets, raise price or stop selling it.
If a service builds proof + product assets, a limited pilot discount can be acceptable with explicit learning goals.
```
