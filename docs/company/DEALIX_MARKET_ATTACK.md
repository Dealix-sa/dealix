# Dealix Market Attack System

This is the playbook for how Dealix enters and expands inside Saudi B2B.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

| Trait | Value |
|---|---|
| Industry | Mid-market services + B2B SaaS in Saudi Arabia |
| Revenue band | SAR 5M – SAR 100M annual |
| Headcount | 30 – 300 |
| AI maturity | Already using SaaS, weak operating capability |
| Trigger | Recent leadership change, Vision 2030 mandate, PDPL pressure |

## Beachhead

Saudi B2B services (consulting, training, marketing, agency) where the
buyer feels AI pressure but does not have an internal engineering team.

## Wedge

The **Free AI Ops Diagnostic** (rung 0) — 60-minute audit of one process,
delivered as a Proof-Pack-style artifact. Converts to Sprint at ~30%.

## Qualification triggers

A diagnostic graduates to a paid Sprint when ALL of:
1. Buyer can name a financial owner of the problem.
2. Buyer has a recurring data source we can audit (CRM, ERP, sheets).
3. Buyer can commit to a 7-day window for the sprint.
4. Buyer signs the Source Passport (governs what we may use).

## Channels

| Channel | Status |
|---|---|
| Warm list (founder network) | ACTIVE |
| LinkedIn (founder posts only, no automation) | ACTIVE |
| Partner referrals | RAMPING |
| Paid ads | OFF until SAR 10K MRR |
| Content (Arabic playbooks) | ACTIVE |
| Outbound email (approval_center gated) | ACTIVE |

## Expansion

Inside a closed account, expansion path:
- Sprint -> Data-to-Revenue Pack -> Managed Retainer -> Custom Setup -> Governance Review.
- Each step requires Proof Pack + Capital Asset from the prior step.

## Doctrine

- No scraping. No cold WhatsApp. No LinkedIn automation.
- Every channel send routes through approval_center.
