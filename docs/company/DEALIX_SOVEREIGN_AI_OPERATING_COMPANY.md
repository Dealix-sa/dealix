# Dealix — Sovereign AI Operating Company

Status: Constitution-aligned, v1
Owner: Founder

## 1. What Dealix Is — ما هي Dealix

Dealix is a Saudi B2B Revenue Operating Company.
We build and operate the revenue function for Saudi B2B companies using a governed AI agent system.
We are not a SaaS toy and not a marketing agency. We are an operating company that runs revenue work on behalf of clients, with human approval gates.

Dealix شركة سعودية لتشغيل الإيراد B2B.
نبني ونشغّل وظيفة الإيراد لشركات B2B السعودية عبر نظام وكلاء ذكاء اصطناعي محكوم.
لسنا أداة SaaS ولسنا وكالة تسويق. نحن شركة تشغيلية تدير عمل الإيراد نيابةً عن العملاء بضوابط بشرية.

## 2. Sovereignty Principles — مبادئ السيادة

1. Data sovereignty — Saudi-first data residency posture, PDPL aligned.
2. Decision sovereignty — every external action requires a human (founder or accountable owner) to approve. No autonomous external sending. Ever.
3. Model sovereignty — provider-agnostic LLM gateway; no lock-in; routing is policy-driven.
4. Process sovereignty — every workflow is policy-as-code; no hidden runbooks.
5. Evidence sovereignty — every claim is backed by a stored artifact; nothing said without proof.

## 3. The Non-Negotiables — الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending — drafts and queues only.
- No proof publishing without founder approval.
- No guaranteed revenue, ROI, growth, or SLA claims the company has not signed.
- No pricing, contract, or payment-term commitments by any agent.
- Approval classes A1 / A2 / A3 enforced at runtime and in evals.

## 4. Operating Posture — الوضع التشغيلي

- Bilingual: Arabic-first, English second.
- Saudi-regulated posture: PDPL, ZATCA, SAMA touchpoints respected.
- Founder-in-the-loop on revenue motions.
- Audit-on by default; nothing material happens without a log line.

## 5. Why "Operating Company" — لماذا شركة تشغيلية

A product company sells software and walks away.
An operating company runs the function and is accountable for outcomes.
Dealix is built so that an AI agent system, a founder, and a thin human team can run the revenue function for many clients with the discipline of an enterprise.

## 6. What we DO sell — ما نبيعه

- Pilot engagements that produce measurable signals (qualified meetings, drafted proposals, structured pipeline).
- Operating retainers where Dealix runs the revenue motion under founder governance.
- Trust artifacts: scorecards, audit trails, governance reports.

## 7. What we do NOT sell — ما لا نبيعه

- Promised revenue numbers.
- Volumes of cold outreach.
- "Magic" AI claims without evidence.
- Anything that requires bypassing the approval system.

## 8. Governance Pillars — أعمدة الحوكمة

- Agent Registry — every agent declared.
- Trust Guardian — runtime enforcement.
- Eval Gate — release enforcement.
- Control Plane — observable state.
- Audit Log — immutable history.
- Policy-as-Code — version-controlled rules.

## 9. The Sovereign Claim — الادعاء السيادي

Dealix can be operated end-to-end with:
- One founder.
- A governed agent system that drafts but never sends.
- A control plane that exposes every internal state.
- A Saudi-regulated data and identity posture.

That is what "Sovereign AI Operating Company" means here.
