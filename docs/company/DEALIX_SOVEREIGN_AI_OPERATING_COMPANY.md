# Dealix — Sovereign AI Operating Company

This document is the elevator description of what Dealix is, as a
company, in the language of its operating model.

## What Dealix is

A Saudi B2B revenue engine in which:

* AI does the **research, scoring, drafting, classification, evaluation,
  and recommendation**.
* Deterministic workers do the **execution** of internal workflows.
* The **founder** approves every external-impact action.
* Every decision is **logged**.

## Why "sovereign"

Because the founder, not the AI vendor and not the AI itself, owns the
operating loop. The trust boundary is enforced in code, not in policy
docs.

## Why "operating company"

Because Dealix is not "an AI tool" — it's a company that uses AI as the
default operator and humans as the default approvers.

## How to verify this is real

```
make sovereign-operating-stack
```

When the verifier prints `summary: N/N passed`, the operating stack
described in this folder is wired and runnable.
