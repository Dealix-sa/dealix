# Dealix Autonomous Enterprise OS

Dealix runs as an AI-native enterprise OS:

- One founder, one cockpit (Founder Console).
- AI agents prepare every move.
- Humans approve every external move.
- Policy-as-Code is the trust gate.
- The audit log is the corporate memory.

## The 7 operating loops

1. **Intelligence** — research builds the lead intelligence base.
2. **Approval** — agents propose, founder approves.
3. **Outreach** — only approved drafts move forward.
4. **Reply handling** — classified, queued, scored.
5. **Samples & proposals** — drafted, never auto-priced.
6. **Payment capture** — drafted reminders, founder sends.
7. **Delivery + retention + proof** — gated by approval.
