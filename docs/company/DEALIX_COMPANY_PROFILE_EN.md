# Dealix — Company Profile

## Definition

Dealix builds AI operating systems for Saudi B2B companies. We are not just a chatbot provider, not just a CRM implementer, and not just a dashboard vendor. We build a daily operating layer connecting revenue, follow-up, WhatsApp, proposals, reports, executive decisions, and governance.

## Positioning

Companies do not simply lack tools. Opportunities are lost across:
- WhatsApp
- Email

- Sales teams
- Proposals
- Reports
- Unclear executive decisions

## Dealix Systems

### 1. Revenue Command Room OS
A daily command room for opportunities, follow-ups, proposals, hot leads, and next actions.

### 2. WhatsApp / Inbox Follow-up OS
A system that classifies conversations, drafts replies, and prevents follow-up leakage with human review.

### 3. Company Brain OS
A daily decision brain that reads company signals and produces executable leadership decisions.

### 4. AI Trust & Compliance OS
Policies, permissions, human approval gates, and data governance for responsible AI adoption.
