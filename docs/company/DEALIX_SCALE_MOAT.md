# Dealix Scale / Moat System

What makes Dealix hard to copy and easy to keep.

## Data moat

Every paid engagement produces:
- A source-passport-bound dataset.
- A Proof Pack with sector-specific findings.
- A Capital Asset (reusable artifact: dashboard, scoring model, runbook).

After 25 engagements, Dealix holds a sector-specific knowledge graph
that no competitor without our delivery pipeline can replicate.

## Governance moat

Every customer-facing output carries `governance_decision`. Every
external send passes approval_center. Every claim has a source_ref.
This is enforced in code (`dealix/governance/`, `policies/`).

Switching to a non-governed competitor means losing the audit trail
that the customer needs for PDPL + procurement compliance — a real
switching cost.

## Distribution moat

- Bilingual operating model (Arabic-first business, English-first eng).
- Saudi-Arabia-hosted compute path (Railway region + KSA partner).
- Partner referral program with shared Proof Packs.
- Founder-led LinkedIn presence with sector content.

## Switching cost

Every retainer client accrues:
- A continuously updated Proof Pack (renewed monthly).
- Capital Assets that integrate with their CRM/ERP.
- Audit trail that survives compliance reviews.

Cancelling means losing the audit trail, the capital assets, AND the
single source of truth Dealix has built for their commercial ops.

## Cadence

| Cadence | What we ship |
|---|---|
| Weekly | New Capital Asset published to the registry |
| Monthly | Sector Proof Pack refresh per retainer |
| Quarterly | Governance review + customer business review |
