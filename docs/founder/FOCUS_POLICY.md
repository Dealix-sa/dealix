# Focus Policy

> What we do, what we kill, what we defer. The CEO uses this to say "no"
> with a clean conscience.

## We Do

1. Anything that converts a conversation into cash within 14 days.
2. Anything that turns a paid sprint into a retainer.
3. Anything that produces evidence (case study, proof pack, public artifact).
4. Anything that reduces founder hours per paid client.
5. Anything that closes a Trust gate (approval matrix, risk classification).

## We Kill

1. Features without a revenue, delivery, trust, or founder-leverage reason.
2. Internal tools that duplicate an existing workflow.
3. Public claims that lack evidence on file.
4. Outbound channels that have not produced a reply in 30 days.
5. Meetings that do not change a decision in `DECISION_LOG.md`.

## We Defer

1. Any new revenue stream until the previous stream has paying evidence.
2. Hiring until a role has a written scorecard and recurring revenue to cover it.
3. Cross-border expansion until Saudi proof is repeatable.
4. SaaS productization until 3 retainers exist with a shared workflow.

## Decision Test

For any new ask, the CEO answers four questions in order. If any answer is "no",
the item is deferred or killed.

1. Does it move cash, proof, retention, or trust in the next 30 days?
2. Is there an owner other than "the founder will figure it out"?
3. Is the cost (hours, cash, attention) below the expected return?
4. Does it pass the No-Overclaim Policy?
