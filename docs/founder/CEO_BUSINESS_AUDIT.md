# CEO Business Audit

## Purpose
Audit Dealix as a company, not only as a repository.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Score Areas
| Area | Weight | Meaning |
|---|---:|---|
| Revenue Execution | 20 | Are we moving toward cash? |
| Sales Pipeline | 15 | Do we have enough qualified opportunities? |
| Financial Control | 15 | Do we know cash, burn, runway, MRR? |
| Delivery Readiness | 15 | Can we deliver what we sell? |
| Trust & AI Governance | 10 | Are risky actions controlled? |
| Learning System | 10 | Are we improving weekly? |
| CEO Cadence | 10 | Is the founder operating from command, not chaos? |
| Product Discipline | 5 | Are we avoiding overbuild? |

## Score Meaning
| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 90–100 | Operating company system |
| 75–89 | Ready internal, execute market push |
| 50–74 | Fix before scale |
| 0–49 | Setup incomplete |

## Required Market Evidence
- 25 leads.
- 25 DMs.
- 3 samples.
- 1 proposal.
- 1 payment / PO / written approval follow-up.
- 1 weekly review.
- 1 system update.

## Rules
- No score above 70 without pipeline evidence.
- No score above 80 without proposal evidence.
- No score above 90 without payment, PO, delivery, or retainer evidence.
- No SaaS expansion before repeated manual proof.

## Evidence
- private pipeline tracker.
- revenue action log.
- finance files.
- delivery report.
- QA checklist.
- approval log.
- weekly review.
- GitHub Actions.
