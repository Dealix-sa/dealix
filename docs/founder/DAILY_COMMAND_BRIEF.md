## Rules
- Brief must be updated daily before the first work block.
- Every decision required must have an owner and a deadline.
- No brief is complete without evidence-backed numbers.
- Sensitive client data stays in the private repo, not here.
- Brief never includes guaranteed revenue or compliance claims.

## Metrics
- Brief completion rate.
- Number of decisions resolved within 24h.
- Forecast accuracy week over week.
- Approval turnaround time.

## Evidence
- Filled brief for each weekday.
- Decision log entries.
- Approval log entries.
- Linked weekly CEO review.
