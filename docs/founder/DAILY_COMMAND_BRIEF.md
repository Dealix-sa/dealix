# Daily Command Brief

## Purpose
Give the founder one screen of operating reality at the start of each day so the highest-leverage move is obvious within 60 seconds.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Daily, every morning, before the first focus block.

## Inputs
- Company State Schema (revenue, sales, delivery, trust, product, learning).
- Pipeline tracker.
- Approvals waiting.
- Decisions outstanding.
- Risks logged.
- Yesterday's evening evidence.

## Outputs
- The single most important action of the day.
- Approval decisions made.
- Decisions queued or resolved.
- Risks acknowledged.
- Focus block scheduled.

## Sections

### Money
- Cash collected this week.
- Cash expected this week.
- MRR.
- Pipeline value.
- Best next close.

### Sales
- New leads since yesterday.
- Replies.
- Calls booked.
- Proposals out.
- Follow-ups overdue.

### Delivery
- Active clients.
- Reports due today.
- QA needed.
- Blocked deliveries.
- Client at risk.

### Trust
- Approvals waiting.
- A3 actions blocked.
- Sensitive items pending.
- Open incidents.

### Decisions Required
- Decision 1 (owner, deadline, options).
- Decision 2.
- Decision 3.

### Today's One Move
A single sentence: the highest-leverage action the founder will take today, and what it produces.

## Rules
- The brief is generated from the Company State Schema; numbers are not guessed.
- The brief lists no more than three Decisions Required; overflow goes to the Decision Queue.
- Unknown values are marked "unknown", never invented.
- The brief is reviewed before any outreach, delivery, or product work begins.
- A2/A3 approvals captured in the brief are mirrored to `trust/approval_log.csv`.

## Metrics
- Days the brief was written and reviewed (streak).
- Decisions closed within their deadline.
- Approvals turned around inside 24h.
- Days the "Today's One Move" was executed by end of day.

## Evidence

- founder/decision_log.md (private ops).
- founder/approvals_waiting.md (private ops).
