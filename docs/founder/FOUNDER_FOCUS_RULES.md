# Founder Focus Rules

The founder's bottleneck is attention, not ideas.

## The Golden Path
Everything that does not serve this path is deferred:

```
Lead → Reply → Call → Payment → Delivery → Proof → Retainer
```

## Focus Matrix

| Item | Revenue Impact | Urgency | Risk Reduction | Founder Leverage | Decision |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Fix payment | 10 | 10 | 8 | 9 | BUILD NOW |
| New dashboard animation | 2 | 1 | 0 | 1 | DEFER |
| Delivery QA | 8 | 8 | 9 | 8 | BUILD NOW |
| Mobile app | 1 | 1 | 0 | 1 | KILL/DEFER |

## Sprint admission rule
Any item under 7/10 on Revenue Impact, Risk Reduction, or Founder Leverage does not enter the current sprint.

## Daily admission rule
Three items max on the daily list. The fourth waits.

## Weekly admission rule
One strategic bet per week. Everything else is execution on the existing bets.

## The "no" library
Common requests that should be declined or deferred by default:
- One-off custom features for a single prospect with no payment.
- Vanity dashboards with no decision attached.
- Free pilots without a written success criterion.
- Speaking events that do not bring qualified pipeline.
- Internal tooling that does not raise revenue, lower risk, or save founder hours.
