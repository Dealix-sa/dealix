# Board-Level Operating System

## Purpose
Operate Dealix with investor-grade discipline before having investors.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Monthly.

## Board-Level Questions
1. Are we moving toward cash?
2. Are we creating proof?
3. Are we improving retention?
4. Are we protecting trust?
5. Are we learning faster each month?
6. Are we overbuilding?
7. Are we default-alive or dependent on hope?
8. What must be killed, deferred, or doubled down?

## Board Pack Sections
1. Executive Summary
2. CEO Business Score
3. Revenue
4. Pipeline
5. Finance
6. Delivery
7. Trust & AI Governance
8. Learning
9. Productization
10. Risks
11. Decisions Needed

## Rules
- No monthly review without numbers.
- No strategic change without evidence.
- No product expansion without repeated workflow.
- No investor-facing claim without proof.
- No autonomous AI expansion without trust approval.
