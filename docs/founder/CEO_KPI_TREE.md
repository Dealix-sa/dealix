# CEO KPI Tree

## Purpose
Connect daily actions to company outcomes.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## North Star
Cash + proof + retention from Saudi B2B revenue operations.

## Level 1 Metrics
- cash collected
- MRR
- paid sprints
- delivery proof
- retainer conversion

## Level 2 Metrics
- leads
- DMs
- replies
- calls
- samples
- proposals
- payment attempts
- delivery reports
- feedback

## Level 3 Inputs
- founder hours
- message quality
- ICP quality
- sample quality
- proposal quality
- payment path clarity
- trust approvals

## KPI Tree

```
Cash Collected
├── Proposals Sent
│   ├── Calls Booked
│   │   ├── Replies
│   │   │   ├── DMs Sent
│   │   │   └── Message Quality
│   │   └── Qualification Quality
│   └── Proposal Follow-Up
└── Payment Path
    ├── Payment Link / Bank Transfer / PO
    └── Decision-Maker Approval

MRR
├── Delivery Quality
├── Client Feedback
├── Retainer Ask
└── Client Health Score
```

## Rules
- Daily work must move at least one Level 2 metric.
- Weekly review must connect Level 2 metrics to Level 1 outcomes.
- Do not optimize vanity metrics.
