# Company Risk Register

## Purpose
Track strategic, operational, financial, trust, delivery, and AI risks.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Risk Classes
- Revenue Risk
- Delivery Risk
- Trust Risk
- AI Risk
- Financial Risk
- Founder Risk
- Product Risk
- Legal/Compliance Risk
- Reputation Risk

## Risk Table

| Risk | Class | Likelihood | Impact | Severity | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| No first paid sprint | Revenue | High | High | Critical | 25 DMs/week + samples + proposal follow-up | Sami | Active |
| Overbuilding before proof | Product | High | Medium | High | No Overbuild Policy + stage gates | Sami | Active |
| Unsafe AI output | AI/Trust | Medium | High | High | approval matrix + evals + no A3 automation | Sami | Active |
| Weak delivery quality | Delivery | Medium | High | High | QA checklist + delivery gate | Sami | Active |
| Founder distraction | Founder | High | High | Critical | daily command + kill list | Sami | Active |

## Severity Rule
Critical risks require weekly CEO review.

## Evidence
- weekly CEO review
- approval logs
- business score
- audit results
- incident log
