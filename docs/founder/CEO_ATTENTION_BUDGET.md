# CEO Attention Budget

The CEO has a fixed weekly attention budget. This doc defines it, allocates
it, and surfaces the rules that protect it.

## The budget

- **Total focused hours per week**: 30 (assume 50 working hours, 20 lost to
  meetings, follow-ups, and overhead)
- **Move bucket target**: ≥ 12 hrs/week (40%)
- **Manage bucket envelope**: ≤ 12 hrs/week
- **Make bucket envelope**: ≤ 6 hrs/week (only for differentiated work)

See bucket definitions in [FOUNDER_TIME_AUDIT](FOUNDER_TIME_AUDIT.md).

## Allocation across the week

| Slot | Hours | Bucket |
|---|---|---|
| Morning deep work (Mon–Fri) | 10 | Move |
| Customer conversations | 5 | Move |
| Weekly review (Friday) | 2 | Move |
| 1:1s with contractors | 3 | Manage |
| Approvals queue review | 2 | Manage |
| Pipeline review | 2 | Manage |
| Hiring | 2 | Manage |
| Differentiated production | 4 | Make |

## Protection rules

1. **Mornings are non-negotiable** — no meetings before 11:00 KSA
2. **One "no" per week minimum** — turn down at least one meeting that does not advance Move
3. **Inbox in batches** — 2 windows of 30 min each, end of morning + end of day
4. **No reactive Slack/WhatsApp during deep work** — phone in another room
5. **Weekly review is sacred** — Friday 14:00, on calendar, blocked

## Failures

- Two consecutive weeks where Move < 8 hrs → the daily brief surfaces it
- Three consecutive weeks where Move < 8 hrs → CEO drafts a Recovery Plan
  decision in [DECISION_LOG_SYSTEM](DECISION_LOG_SYSTEM.md)
