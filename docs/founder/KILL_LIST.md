# Kill List

The strongest CEO is not the one who builds the most. It is the one who
knows what to stop.

## Kill Immediately
- features not tied to revenue, delivery, trust, or learning
- offers with unclear scope
- clients asking for guaranteed results
- public claims without evidence
- manual work repeated more than 10 times without templating

## Defer
- mobile app
- complex enterprise dashboard
- white-label platform
- advanced SaaS billing
- large content engine before first proof

## Review Monthly
- low-converting sectors
- low-quality channels
- slow delivery formats
- unused features
