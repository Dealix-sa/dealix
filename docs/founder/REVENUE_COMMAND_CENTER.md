# Revenue Command Center — مركز قيادة الإيراد

The single screen the founder reads every morning. Generated by
`scripts/founder_daily_command.py` into `reports/founder/daily_command.md`.

## Purpose

Turn the scattered state of the business — pipeline, approvals pending, delivery
in flight, proof produced — into **one prioritized action list** the founder can
act on in under 10 minutes.

## Inputs

| Source | What it provides |
|---|---|
| `data/revenue/pipeline.jsonl` | Every revenue event + stage + amount + governance state |
| `data/revenue/proof_assets.jsonl` | Proof Packs + Capital Assets produced |
| `data/growth/first_30_targets.csv` | The warm-list of targets and suggested offers |
| `customers/*/` | Per-customer workspaces and their next-action boards |
| `reports/revenue/REVENUE_BOARD.md` | The current revenue board snapshot |

## The five numbers (daily)

1. **Cumulative revenue (SAR)** — sum of `won` events.
2. **Open pipeline value (SAR)** — sum of in-flight offers.
3. **Items awaiting approval** — count of `pending_founder_approval`.
4. **Active deliveries** — customer workspaces in delivery.
5. **Proof Packs produced** — count of capital assets registered.

## The daily decision

The command center always ends with **one** recommended next action, chosen by
this priority order:

1. Clear anything `pending_founder_approval` (unblocks revenue).
2. Advance the furthest-along paid engagement to Proof.
3. Convert a delivered diagnostic into a sprint offer.
4. Approve the next warm-intro draft from the target list.

## Cadence

- **Daily:** run `python scripts/founder_daily_command.py`.
- **Weekly:** run the [Weekly CEO Review](WEEKLY_CEO_REVIEW.md).

## North-star (90-day)

8–15K SAR MRR + 30–40K SAR one-time ≈ **40–55K SAR cumulative** by day 90.
