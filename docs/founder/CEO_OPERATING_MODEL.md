# Dealix CEO Operating Model

## Purpose
Define how Dealix is operated by the founder as a company, not as a collection of tasks.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Operating Philosophy
Dealix operates through systems, evidence, and weekly learning.
Revenue drives.
Trust governs.
Delivery proves.
Learning improves.
Productization follows evidence.

## CEO Responsibilities

### 1. Capital Allocation
Decide where founder time, money, engineering, brand attention, and trust are spent.

### 2. Revenue Direction
Keep the company focused on cash, proof, and retainer conversion.

### 3. Trust Governance
Prevent overclaims, unsafe automation, and public/private boundary failures.

### 4. Talent and Delegation
Document and delegate only repeated workflows.

### 5. Product Direction
Build only after manual proof and repeatability.

### 6. Learning
Convert weekly market feedback into system updates.

## CEO Weekly Questions
- What moved revenue?
- What blocked revenue?
- What did customers actually respond to?
- What delivery issue repeated?
- What should be killed?
- What should be productized?
- What must not be automated yet?

## CEO Metrics
- cash collected
- pipeline value
- proposals sent
- paid sprints
- delivery QA pass rate
- client feedback
- retainer attempts
- trust incidents
- founder bottlenecks
- learning decisions
- playbook updates
