# Founder Personal OS

## Purpose
Protect the founder's focus, energy, and decision quality.

## Rules
- Revenue work before admin.
- Decisions before polishing.
- Evidence before strategy changes.
- Sleep/health are operating assets.
- No late-night major strategic decision without review next day.

## Daily Focus Blocks
1. Revenue block.
2. Delivery/Trust block.
3. Systems block.
4. Review block.

## Weekly Founder Review
- What drained energy?
- What created leverage?
- What repeated?
- What should be delegated?
- What should be killed?

## Metrics
- revenue hours
- admin hours
- repeated manual work
- decisions made with evidence
- founder bottlenecks
