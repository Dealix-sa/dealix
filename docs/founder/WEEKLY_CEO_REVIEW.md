# Weekly CEO Review

The structured review the founder runs once per week, ideally on the same day and hour.

## Agenda (60–90 minutes)

1. Revenue Review
   - Cash collected this week vs last week.
   - MRR Δ.
   - Pipeline movement by stage.
   - Deals stuck and why.
2. Sales Review
   - Outbound volume (DMs, emails, calls).
   - Reply rate, call-booked rate, paid rate.
   - Best-performing message and worst-performing message.
3. Delivery Review
   - Active clients, overdue items, at-risk items.
   - QA pass rate.
   - Time per deliverable vs target.
4. Trust Review
   - Approvals waiting and aging.
   - Incidents this week.
   - Claims that came close to over-claiming.
5. Learning Review
   - Top three insights from `EXPERIMENT_LOG.md`.
   - What to keep, change, kill next week.
6. Decision Log Update
   - New decisions to log.
   - Decisions due for review.

## Outputs
- Updated `COMPANY_HEALTH_SCORE.md`
- Updated `DECISION_LOG.md`
- Updated `RISK_REGISTER.md`
- One-paragraph weekly summary committed to `docs/learning/MONTHLY_STRATEGY_UPDATE.md` (rolling).

## Rule
A week without a CEO review is a week the company drifted.
