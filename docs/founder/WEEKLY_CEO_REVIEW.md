# Weekly CEO Review — المراجعة الأسبوعية للمؤسس

A 45-minute weekly ritual that keeps the 90-day plan honest. Run every Sunday
(start of the Saudi work week).

## Agenda (45 min)

| Block | Time | Question |
|---|---|---|
| Numbers | 10 min | Where is cumulative revenue vs. the 40–55K SAR / 90-day target? |
| Pipeline | 10 min | What moved? What stalled > 7 days? What needs an approval decision? |
| Delivery | 10 min | Are active sprints on the daily rhythm? Any blocked Proof Pack? |
| Proof → Upsell | 5 min | Which delivered engagement is ready to become a retainer? |
| Friction | 5 min | Top 1–2 friction-log items. Fix, automate, or accept? |
| Decision | 5 min | One bet for next week. Write it down. |

## The decision rules (from the plan)

- **Revenue ≥ 40K SAR cumulative + 3 retainers by day 90 →** open Wave 3
  (Enterprise Trust).
- **Revenue < 25K SAR by day 60 →** stop building new offers; double down on
  sales (warm-list re-engagement, content cadence, price tests).
- **Founder time per sprint > 5h after customer 5 →** halt new sprint sales;
  push sprint automation to top of the P0 list.
- **Any non-negotiable would be violated →** refuse the work; pick the safe
  alternative.

## Inputs to read before the review

1. `reports/founder/daily_command.md` (latest)
2. `reports/revenue/REVENUE_BOARD.md`
3. `reports/verification/dealix_full_verification_latest.md`
4. The week's customer workspaces under `customers/`

## Output

A 5-line memo appended to `reports/founder/` capturing: the numbers, the one
bet, and any decision-rule that fired.
