# Weekly CEO Review

The 60-minute meeting the founder holds with himself (and AI) every week to close the previous week and open the next.

## Purpose
Force a structured weekly close: read every loop, score every system, pick the next week's three priorities.

## Owner
Sami (Founder).

## Review Cadence
Weekly. Monday 09:00–10:00 KSA.

## Inputs
- The week's daily briefs.
- The week's approval log.
- The week's pipeline activity.
- `docs/learning/WEEKLY_INTELLIGENCE_REVIEW.md` (this week's).
- `DEALIX_COMPANY_OS_SCORECARD.md`.

## Outputs
- Updated scorecard.
- Updated decision log.
- Next week's top 3 priorities written in `dealix-ops-private/founder/weekly_ceo_review.md`.
- Adjustments to autonomy policy if any.

## Rules
- Meeting cannot end without a written next-week plan.
- Every `FIX` system must have an owner and a date.
- Every customer commitment from this week must be reconciled.

---

## Agenda

1. **Money (5 min)** — cash, MRR, collected, outstanding.
2. **Pipeline (10 min)** — counts by stage, conversions, dropouts.
3. **Delivery (10 min)** — active sprints, samples shipped, proof packs delivered.
4. **Trust (5 min)** — approvals, violations, autonomy changes proposed.
5. **Learning (10 min)** — read the weekly intelligence review.
6. **Scorecard (10 min)** — update every row.
7. **Next 3 (10 min)** — choose the three priorities for the new week.

## Metrics
- Number of weeks in a row the review was held.
- Number of `FIX` items closed week-over-week.
- Number of doctrine updates triggered this quarter.

- Updated `DEALIX_COMPANY_OS_SCORECARD.md` every Monday.
