# Risk Register

Every material risk to Dealix, with owner, mitigation, and review date.

## Categories
- Revenue risk (cash, concentration, churn)
- Delivery risk (capacity, quality, SLA)
- Trust risk (over-claim, data, legal, regulator)
- Product risk (downtime, data loss, bug class)
- Founder risk (overload, single point of failure)
- Market risk (competition, sector shift, macro)

## Template

```
## Risk ID
DLX-RSK-0001

## Title
Short name.

## Category
Revenue / Delivery / Trust / Product / Founder / Market

## Description
What can go wrong.

## Likelihood
Low / Medium / High

## Impact
Low / Medium / High

## Current Mitigation
What is in place today.

## Planned Mitigation
What will be in place by [date].

## Owner
[Name / Role]

## Review Date
YYYY-MM-DD
```

## Standing risks
- Founder is the single point of failure for closing and approvals.
- Cash concentration in fewer than five clients.
- Over-claim in an outbound message or proposal.
- Data leak through a public repo or export.

## Rule
A risk without a review date is not a managed risk. Every risk has a date.
