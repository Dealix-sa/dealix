# CEO Risk Register

> Active risks the founder is tracking. Mirrors `docs/trust/` risk classifications
> but is owned by the CEO, not the Trust system.

## Format

| ID | Risk | Category | Severity | Likelihood | Owner | Mitigation | Trigger to Escalate | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Categories

- **Revenue** — cash, pipeline, churn, concentration.
- **Delivery** — quality, capacity, dependency on the founder.
- **Trust** — overclaim, opt-out, regulator-relevant incidents, AI safety.
- **Product** — broken CI, security, data loss, integration drift.
- **Founder** — burnout, single point of failure, focus erosion.
- **Market** — competitor moves, regulatory change, demand shift.

## Severity Scale

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1 | Minor — fix in normal cadence |
| 2 | Moderate — fix this week |
| 3 | Serious — fix this sprint, requires CEO decision |
| 4 | Critical — drop other work, requires A3 approval to continue exposure |

## Review Cadence

- Daily — severity 4 items in the Daily Command Brief.
- Weekly — full register reviewed in the Weekly CEO Review.
