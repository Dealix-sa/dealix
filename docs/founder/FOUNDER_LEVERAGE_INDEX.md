# Founder Leverage Index

The company is built to serve the founder as the best CEO they can be.
So every week we ask one question:

> Did Dealix make Sami stronger this week, or just busier?

## Weekly Score

| Area              | Score | Signal                                                  |
|-------------------|------:|---------------------------------------------------------|
| Sales leverage    | 0–100 | AI prepares leads/messages, founder only approves       |
| Delivery leverage | 0–100 | reports generated from templates and QA                 |
| Decision leverage | 0–100 | decisions queued with evidence                          |
| Trust leverage    | 0–100 | risky actions blocked automatically                     |
| Learning leverage | 0–100 | system updates playbooks weekly                         |

## Founder Bottleneck Signals
- founder writes every message manually
- founder formats every report
- founder remembers follow-ups manually
- founder decides without evidence
- founder fixes repeated workflow issues manually

## Target
Founder should spend most time on:
- strategy
- key sales
- approvals
- partnerships
- capital allocation
- product direction

## Anti-targets
- writing weekly reports by hand
- copy-pasting outreach
- remembering invoices in their head
- doing QA every time without checklists
