# Decision Quality System

## Purpose
Improve founder decisions by requiring evidence, options, risk, and review.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## Inputs
- CEO decision queue.
- Evidence.
- Bottleneck analysis.
- Revenue metrics.
- Trust risk.
- Founder focus policy.

## Outputs
- Better decisions.
- Fewer reactive actions.
- Clear build/fix/kill/defer choices.
- Reviewable decision history.

## Rules
Every important decision must include:
- context
- evidence
- options
- risk
- expected impact
- review date

## Decision Types
- Build
- Fix
- Kill
- Defer
- Approve
- Reject
- Escalate

## Bad Decision Signals
- no evidence
- no owner
- no deadline
- no expected impact
- made because of excitement only
- violates trust policy
- creates complexity without revenue impact

## Metrics
- Decisions logged weekly.
- Decisions reviewed.
- Decisions reversed.
- Decisions with evidence.
- Bottlenecks resolved.
