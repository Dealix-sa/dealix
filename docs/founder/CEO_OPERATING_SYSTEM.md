# CEO Operating System

## Purpose
Keep the founder focused on the highest-leverage decisions.

## CEO Responsibilities
- Strategy
- Key sales
- Pricing decisions
- Trust approvals
- Capital allocation
- Hiring decisions
- Product direction
- Major partnerships

## CEO Must Not Spend Most Time On
- Random feature building
- Manual formatting
- Unqualified leads
- Low-value support
- Untracked decisions

## Daily Rule
Start with Daily Command Brief before GitHub, email, or coding.

## Weekly Rule
No week closes without a Weekly CEO Review.

## Monthly Rule
No month closes without a Strategy Review.

## Operating Mode
The founder is a *layer* in the system, not the system itself. The control
plane prepares state and decisions; the founder approves and steers.
