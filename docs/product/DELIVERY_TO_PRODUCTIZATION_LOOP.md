# Delivery to Productization Loop

## Purpose
Use delivery repetition to identify product, automation, and SaaS opportunities.

## Signals
- same intake questions repeated
- same lead scoring repeated
- same report sections repeated
- same QA failures repeated
- clients ask for dashboard
- clients ask for ongoing updates

## Rules
- 3 repeats = document.
- 5 repeats = template.
- 10 repeats = automate.
- customer asks repeatedly = SaaS candidate.

## Evidence
- delivery reports
- QA logs
- client feedback
- productization_candidates.md

## Loop Owner
Sami / Founder.

## Cadence
Reviewed weekly during the operating rhythm, alongside the
client_success_dashboard and the qa_score_log.

## DORA Anchor (forward)
When a productization candidate ships as software, its operational
quality is measured by the DORA software-delivery metrics:
- deployment frequency
- lead time for changes
- change failure rate
- time to restore service

The OS does not require these metrics now — they activate once
there is a deployed product with releases, not just an internal tool.
