# Customer Feedback Loop (Product)

How product turns customer signals into changes.

## Sources
- Client feedback forms (`docs/client_success/FEEDBACK_LOOP.md`).
- Direct asks captured in weekly status updates.
- Patterns across multiple client reports.
- Founder field notes from sales calls.

## Triage
- Each signal tagged: bug, gap, enhancement, idea.
- Bugs and gaps enter `FEATURE_INTAKE.md` with high priority.
- Enhancements and ideas wait for a recurring pattern.

## Loop close
- When a signal becomes a feature, the customer who raised it hears that it shipped.
- When a signal is deferred or killed, the rationale is logged for re-use.

## Rule
A customer who gives feedback once and never sees a response stops giving feedback. Close the loop, always.
