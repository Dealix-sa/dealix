# Dealix Product Ladder

Seven rungs. Each rung is a self-contained offer with a clear scope,
a price guardrail (no exact number outside the guardrails table), and
a clear next rung.

## 1. Rungs

| # | Offer | Time-box | Outcome | Next rung |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Free Sample / Diagnostic** | < 1 hour | A scored snapshot of the prospect against Dealix's lens. | Revenue Sprint |
| 2 | **Revenue Sprint** | 7 days | An approved-to-act set of drafts, scoring, and one queue. | Managed Pilot |
| 3 | **Managed Pilot** | 90 days | A scored pipeline, an approval cadence, and a proof artifact. | Revenue Desk Retainer |
| 4 | **Revenue Desk Retainer** | Monthly | A founder cockpit run weekly with a defined cadence. | Founder Console |
| 5 | **Founder Console / Command Center** | Annual | Always-on Revenue OS with full agent registry. | Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS |
| 6 | **Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS** | Multi-year | Multi-BU rollout with control plane and PMO. | Partner Revenue OS |
| 7 | **Partner / White-label Revenue OS** | Partnership | Dealix-powered offering inside a partner's brand. | — |

## 2. Anchors per rung

Each rung documents:

- Who it's for (segment + persona).
- What the customer signs.
- What artifacts they get.
- What internal Dealix machines run for them.
- What the renewal motion looks like.

## 3. Banned claims

- ❌ "Guaranteed revenue" / "Guaranteed leads" / "Guaranteed ROI".
- ❌ "Set-and-forget" / "Fully autonomous".
- ❌ "10x" or any specific multiplier promise.

Pricing numbers do **not** appear in this doc — they live in
[PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md](PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md).
