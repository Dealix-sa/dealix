# Dealix Product Ladder | سلم منتجات ديليكس

> A buyer can enter Dealix at any rung and climb without re-procurement. Each rung is productized: scoped outcome, fixed shape, evidenced delivery, approval-gated motion.

---

## 1. The Seven Rungs | الدرجات السبع

| # | Rung | Buyer | Outcome | Time | Commitment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Free Diagnostic | Any qualified Saudi B2B team | Written ICP + warm-graph read + sample drafts | 1 week | None |
| 2 | Revenue Sprint | Founder / Head of Sales | First evidenced pipeline + approved proof | 30 days | One-time engagement |
| 3 | Managed Pilot | Head of Sales / Revenue | Repeatable approval-gated motion across 1–2 ICPs | 60–90 days | One-time engagement |
| 4 | Revenue Desk Retainer | GM / VP Sales | Ongoing approval-gated revenue desk | Monthly | Rolling, 30-day notice |
| 5 | Founder Console | Founder | Direct console for approval, audit, signal | Monthly | Annual recommended |
| 6 | Enterprise Revenue OS | BU Head / CRO | Multi-team OS with governance + integration | Annual | Annual + SLAs |
| 7 | Partner / White-label | Agency / SI / Vertical operator | Productized Revenue OS resold or co-delivered | Annual | Annual + revenue share |

Each rung honors every Dealix non-negotiable.

---

## 2. Rung 1 — Free Diagnostic | التشخيص المجاني

- **Purpose:** Show, not tell. Give the buyer a written read on their ICP and a sample of how Dealix would draft for them.
- **Inputs:** A 30-minute call + access to publicly available company info + the buyer's two ICP examples.
- **Outputs:** 2-page written diagnostic (EN + AR), 4–6 sample bilingual drafts, a proposed Revenue Sprint shape with ranges + conditions + pilot N.
- **Trust gates:** No sends. No customer-name use without approval. No pricing commitments.
- **Owner:** Founder or Sales Lead.
- **KPI:** Conversion from diagnostic to Sprint (target ≥ 30%).

---

## 3. Rung 2 — Revenue Sprint | سبرنت الإيرادات

- **Purpose:** First evidenced pipeline in 30 days; first approved proof artifact.
- **Inputs:** Signed engagement, ICP definitions, list seed, identity for sending (customer-side), bilingual copy intake.
- **Outputs:** Bilingual draft set queued for customer approval; sends only after approval; 30-day proof pack reviewed and approved by customer.
- **Scope guardrails:** One sector, one geography, two channels (email + LinkedIn 1:1), one approval cadence (default 2× daily).
- **Trust gates:** Approval-gated sends only. No proof publishing without sign-off. No revenue guarantees.
- **Owner:** Revenue Desk Operator + Founder.
- **KPI:** Approved drafts/week, reply rate, qualified meetings booked, proof pack approval.

---

## 4. Rung 3 — Managed Pilot | الإدارة التجريبية

- **Purpose:** Turn the first evidenced motion into a repeatable one across 1–2 ICPs.
- **Inputs:** Sprint learnings, expanded ICP, additional senders, integration touchpoints (CRM, calendar, mailbox).
- **Outputs:** Repeatable approval-gated motion, weekly approval cadence, monthly proof pack (customer-approved).
- **Trust gates:** Same as Sprint, plus weekly evidence digest signed by both sides.
- **Owner:** Revenue Desk Operator + customer's revenue lead.
- **KPI:** Approved-draft throughput, qualified-meeting rate, cost-per-qualified-meeting trend.

---

## 5. Rung 4 — Revenue Desk Retainer | عقد مكتب الإيرادات

- **Purpose:** Ongoing operating layer; Dealix becomes the customer's approval-gated outbound function.
- **Inputs:** Monthly retainer, signed Master Service + Data Processing Agreements, governance cadence.
- **Outputs:** Daily approval queue, weekly evidence digest, monthly customer-approved proof pack, quarterly business review.
- **Trust gates:** Roles & approvals matrix in writing; escalation path documented; PDPL boundaries enforced.
- **Owner:** Revenue Desk Lead.
- **KPI:** Net new pipeline / month, qualified-meeting rate, retention, NPS, proof-pack approval rate.

---

## 6. Rung 5 — Founder Console | كونسول المؤسس

- **Purpose:** A product surface where the customer's founder/GM directly approves, audits, and steers without operating overhead.
- **Inputs:** Console access (per user), connected mailbox/calendar/CRM, single-sign-on.
- **Outputs:** Single pane for approval queue, signal radar, proof pack drafts, audit trail.
- **Trust gates:** Approve / Decline / Edit on every draft; signed audit log; downloadable evidence at any time.
- **Owner:** Product Lead.
- **KPI:** Active console days/month, approval latency, % drafts approved, proof artifact generation cadence.

---

## 7. Rung 6 — Enterprise Revenue OS | نظام الإيرادات للمؤسسات

- **Purpose:** Multi-team rollout with governance, identity, and integration appropriate for enterprise.
- **Inputs:** Annual engagement; legal, security, compliance review; SSO/SAML; data residency confirmation; integration plan.
- **Outputs:** OS rolled out across multiple BUs/regions; governance dashboard; quarterly business review at executive level; published SLAs.
- **Trust gates:** Roles matrix, escalation paths, audit, PDPL/ZATCA alignment, signed evidence retained per agreement.
- **Owner:** Enterprise Account Lead + Customer Success.
- **KPI:** Adoption per BU, governance compliance, quarterly outcome attainment, renewal.

---

## 8. Rung 7 — Partner / White-label | الشريك / العلامة البيضاء

- **Purpose:** Partners resell or co-deliver Dealix rungs (typically Sprint → Retainer) under co-branded or partner-branded shape.
- **Inputs:** Partner agreement, training, certification, joint roadmap.
- **Outputs:** Productized motion the partner can deliver; co-branded artifacts where approved; revenue share on shipped outcomes.
- **Trust gates:** Partners operate under the same non-negotiables; partner-side approvals run through the Dealix evidence layer.
- **Owner:** Partnerships Lead.
- **KPI:** Partner-sourced shipped outcomes, partner pipeline %, partner NPS.

---

## 9. Cross-Cutting Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء العرضية

- **No external sending** at any rung without explicit approval per send window or per ruleset.
- **No proof publishing** at any rung without written customer approval.
- **No guaranteed revenue** — all targets are ranges with conditions and pilot N.
- **No pricing/contract/payment commitments** at any rung without escalation to Founder + Commercial Lead.
- **Approval-gated automation only** — at every rung, including Console-driven flows.

---

## 10. Climbing Patterns | أنماط الترقي

| From → To | Typical trigger |
|---|---|
| Diagnostic → Sprint | Customer sees the draft quality and ICP read; books Sprint. |
| Sprint → Managed Pilot | Sprint produced evidenced pipeline; customer wants repeatability. |
| Managed Pilot → Retainer | Two-ICP repeatability is stable; customer wants ongoing motion. |
| Retainer → Founder Console | Customer's founder wants direct visibility and audit. |
| Retainer → Enterprise OS | Multi-team / multi-region governance is required. |
| Any → Partner | Partner contracts the rung for their client. |

---

## 11. Rung Anatomy Template | قالب الدرجة

Every rung is documented with the same anatomy:
- Purpose
- Buyer & decision-maker
- Inputs
- Outputs (what ships, what is signed)
- Scope guardrails
- Trust gates (the non-negotiables made concrete)
- Owner
- KPI
- Climb-up trigger
- Sunset criteria (when the rung is no longer the right fit)

---

## 12. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner of the ladder:** Founder + Product Lead.
- **KPI:** Ladder progression rate (cohort-based: Diagnostic → Sprint → Pilot → Retainer).
- **KPI:** Average revenue per customer over 12 months.
- **KPI:** Retention by rung.
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations per rung (target 0).
