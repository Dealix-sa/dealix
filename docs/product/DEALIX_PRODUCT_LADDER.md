# Dealix Product Ladder

The Dealix offer ladder. Seven rungs, from a free diagnostic to enterprise revenue intelligence. Buyers move **up** the ladder one rung at a time. We never skip rungs without a documented reason.

## 1. The 7 rungs

| # | Offer                              | Price band (SAR)             | Cycle             | Target buyer (primary)        |
|---|------------------------------------|------------------------------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| 1 | Free Sample / Diagnostic            | 0 (cost-controlled)          | 1–3 days          | Pipeline-Starved, P-CRO       |
| 2 | Revenue Sprint                      | 499 – 4,999                   | 7 days            | P-CRO, P-Founder-Agency, P-Implementer |
| 3 | Managed Pilot                       | 5,000 – 14,999                | 30 days           | P-CRO, P-Cyber-SalesDirector  |
| 4 | Revenue Desk Retainer               | 5,000 – 25,000 /month          | Monthly, 3-mo min | P-CRO, P-CEO-MidMarket        |
| 5 | Founder Console / Command Center    | 15,000 – 40,000 /month         | Monthly, 6-mo min | P-CEO-MidMarket, P-Founder    |
| 6 | Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS  | Custom (50,000+ /quarter)      | Quarterly         | Enterprise CEO + CRO          |
| 7 | Partner / White-label               | Revenue share                 | Multi-year        | P-Founder-Agency, P-Implementer |

Price bands are placeholders; final pricing is set per engagement and recorded in the proposal. No public pricing claims revenue guarantees.

## 2. Rung 1 — Free Sample / Diagnostic

- **Target buyer.** Pipeline-Starved teams; P-CRO at companies < 200 headcount.
- **Promise.** A 1–3 day diagnostic on a single revenue question, returned as a bilingual one-pager.
- **Deliverables.** Diagnostic one-pager (AR + EN), pipeline-health snapshot, recommended next rung.
- **Trust note.** No customer data leaves the engagement scope. No external outreach. No commitments.
- **CTA.** "Request a sample."
- **Proof required.** A previously-anonymised diagnostic.
- **Upsell path.** → Revenue Sprint.
- **Channels.** LinkedIn, warm intro.

## 3. Rung 2 — Revenue Sprint

- **Target buyer.** First-time customers ready to invest 7 days and 499–4,999 SAR.
- **Promise.** A 7-day Revenue Sprint: diagnostic + draft pack + proof pack + recommended next rung.
- **Deliverables.** Source Passport, DQ score, account scoring set, 10 founder-approved drafts, bilingual proof pack.
- **Trust note.** Drafts only — nothing leaves without founder approval. Optional founder shadow session.
- **CTA.** "Start a Revenue Sprint."
- **Proof required.** Sector-relevant sample.
- **Upsell path.** → Managed Pilot or Revenue Desk Retainer.
- **Channels.** LinkedIn, warm intro, email.

## 4. Rung 3 — Managed Pilot

- **Target buyer.** Customers who delivered Sprint with measurable lift; or P-CRO at companies 100–500 headcount.
- **Promise.** 30-day managed pilot of one revenue motion (outbound, ABM, inbound).
- **Deliverables.** Approved sequence, scored account list, weekly drafts, monthly pipeline report.
- **Trust note.** All outbound is founder-approved. Customer keeps kill switch.
- **CTA.** "Pilot a motion."
- **Proof required.** Sprint delivered + sector proof.
- **Upsell path.** → Revenue Desk Retainer.

## 5. Rung 4 — Revenue Desk Retainer

- **Target buyer.** P-CRO, P-CEO-MidMarket who want monthly operating support.
- **Promise.** Monthly Revenue Desk: scoring, drafting, queueing, reporting.
- **Deliverables.** Monthly account scoring refresh, ≥ 30 drafts/month, weekly approval queue, monthly pipeline report.
- **Trust note.** Founder-approved outbound only. Customer keeps kill switch.
- **CTA.** "Operate my Revenue Desk."
- **Proof required.** ≥ 1 delivered Pilot or Sprint.
- **Upsell path.** → Founder Console / Command Center.

## 6. Rung 5 — Founder Console / Command Center

- **Target buyer.** P-CEO-MidMarket; companies 100–500 headcount with > 10M SAR ARR.
- **Promise.** A single founder operating pane with sales, approvals, workers, trust, finance, distribution, delivery, retention, proof, control, audit, evals, product, security.
- **Deliverables.** Live console + monthly operating cadence + co-located founder reviews.
- **Trust note.** Customer owns the kill switch; Dealix operates within scope.
- **CTA.** "See the Founder Console."
- **Proof required.** ≥ 90 days of Retainer.
- **Upsell path.** → Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS.

## 7. Rung 6 — Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS

- **Target buyer.** Enterprise CEO + CRO at companies > 500 headcount or > 100M SAR revenue.
- **Promise.** Custom revenue intelligence operating system: data integrations, custom scoring, multi-team approval workflows, custom reporting, training.
- **Deliverables.** Scoped per engagement. Quarterly cadence.
- **Trust note.** Multi-party governance; founder + customer-executive co-sign.
- **CTA.** "Scope a custom engagement."
- **Proof required.** ≥ 2 retainers delivered in their sector.

## 8. Rung 7 — Partner / White-label

- **Target buyer.** P-Founder-Agency, P-Implementer who want to resell or co-deliver Dealix.
- **Promise.** Reseller / white-label arrangement: shared revenue, shared delivery, shared brand-discipline.
- **Deliverables.** Partner kit, training, co-delivery cadence.
- **Trust note.** Partner is bound by the same brand verifier and trust gates.
- **CTA.** "Become a partner."

## 9. Doctrine

- **No "guaranteed" anything.** Each rung promises a *process*, not an *outcome*.
- **Bilingual default.** Every rung's proposal is AR + EN.
- **Trust gate always.** Every rung names the human approval gate.
- **No skipping.** Rung-to-rung movement requires the listed proof.
- **Refusal is free.** We may refuse a customer if the rung doesn't fit.
