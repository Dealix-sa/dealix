# SaaS Readiness Gate

## Purpose
Decide when Dealix should move from service-led Revenue Sprint to software product.

## Required Before SaaS
- 3 paid Revenue Sprints.
- 2 completed delivery reports.
- 1 approved proof asset.
- 1 retainer attempt.
- 10 repeated workflow executions.
- clear workflow bottleneck.
- client demand for self-serve or dashboard.
- trust controls tested.
- support burden understood.

## SaaS Candidate Signals
- clients ask for recurring dashboard.
- same report repeated many times.
- manual QA becomes bottleneck.
- lead scoring rules become stable.
- data model stabilizes.

## Not Enough
- founder excitement.
- pretty dashboard.
- one client request.
- no payment proof.
- no delivery proof.

## Decision
Go / No-Go / Defer
