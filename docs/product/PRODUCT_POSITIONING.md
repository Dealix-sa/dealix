# Product Positioning (per rung)

> Each rung is its own product. Each product has its own positioning statement.

## Rung 1 — Free Sample / Diagnostic

> **For** ICP-fit founders **who** want proof of method before any spend,
> Dealix's **Free Diagnostic** delivers **a real diagnostic on a real motion within days**,
> backed by signal evidence, with **no guarantees and no obligation**.

## Rung 2 — Revenue Sprint

> **For** founders with one bottleneck they want to break,
> the **Revenue Sprint** is **two weeks of evidence-led work on one revenue lever**,
> ending in a shipped artifact and a documented learning.

## Rung 3 — Managed Pilot

> **For** revenue teams ready to operate a cadence,
> the **Managed Pilot** runs **a six-week operated motion against one defined outcome**,
> under full trust gates and QA.

## Rung 4 — Revenue Desk Retainer

> **For** companies that have proven the motion and want monthly operation,
> the **Revenue Desk Retainer** runs **Distribution + Delivery as a monthly cadence**,
> with weekly drafts, monthly reviews and quarterly business reviews.

## Rung 5 — Founder Console / Command Center

> **For** hands-on founders who want to operate the company from one screen,
> the **Founder Command Center** is **the console + retainer hybrid** that puts the founder at the centre of the operating system.

## Rung 6 — Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS

> **For** larger organisations with sovereign-data needs,
> the **Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS** is **a multi-tenant deployment with KSA data residency**, SSO, audit export and SLA.

## Rung 7 — Partner / White-label Revenue OS

> **For** agencies and system integrators in KSA and GCC,
> **Partner OS** is **the white-label Revenue Operating System** they operate under their own brand.

## Cross-rung notes

- Positioning is for messaging; pricing lives in `PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md`.
- No rung claims a guaranteed outcome.
- Every rung's claim must be supported by a surface in the product.
