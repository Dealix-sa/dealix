# Product Positioning | تموضع المنتج

> Product positioning translates the company positioning into how each rung of the ladder is described, demonstrated, and defended.

---

## 1. Frame | الإطار

Dealix the **product** is the approval-gated Revenue OS plus the evidence control plane plus the Founder Console. The rungs of the ladder are how the product is **delivered** to a buyer at their stage.

The product positioning therefore answers two questions for any buyer:

1. **What is the product I am buying?** — an approval-gated Revenue OS, run with you.
2. **At what shape am I buying it today?** — Diagnostic, Sprint, Pilot, Retainer, Console, Enterprise OS, or Partner.

---

## 2. Product North Star | نجم المنتج

> A Saudi B2B revenue leader can run their entire outbound and pipeline motion through Dealix — drafting, approving, sending, and auditing every action — without ever auto-sending or auto-publishing on their customer's behalf.

Every roadmap decision passes a single question: **does it bring us closer to that north star without breaking a non-negotiable?**

## 6. What the Product Is Not | ما ليس المنتج

- Not a CRM. Works alongside HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, or none.
- Not a sales-engagement scaler. Will not auto-send at volume.
- Not an AI SDR. Approval-gated, not autonomous.
- Not an analytics dashboard. We produce drafts and deals, not just charts.
- Not a marketplace. We are a Revenue Operating Company, not a directory.

## 7. Rung-Level Positioning | تموضع كل درجة

**Free Diagnostic** — "See the read before you commit. We give you a written ICP read, a warm-graph read, and sample bilingual drafts in one week."
**Revenue Sprint** — "Your first evidenced pipeline in 30 days. Approval-gated sends, customer-approved proof pack."
**Managed Pilot** — "Turn the Sprint into a repeatable motion across 1–2 ICPs."
**Revenue Desk Retainer** — "Your ongoing approval-gated outbound function, run with you."
**Founder Console** — "One pane to approve, audit, and steer. Built for the operator chair."
**Enterprise Revenue OS** — "Multi-team OS with governance, integration, and SLAs."
**Partner / White-label** — "Productized rungs partners deliver under their brand or co-brand."

Each rung is positioned with the same outcome→approval-gate→time/condition pattern from `docs/positioning/MESSAGING_HIERARCHY.md`.

## 8. Proof Discipline | انضباط الأدلة

- All product proof artifacts (case studies, screenshots, numbers) are customer-approved before publication.
- Numbers are ranges with conditions and pilot N.
- We publish anonymized patterns when named attribution is not approved.
- Reference: `docs/PROOF_AND_CASE_STUDY_SYSTEM.md`.

---

## 9. Demo Discipline | انضباط العرض

- Demos use a real, approved sandbox tenant — never an internal customer's data.
- Every draft shown in a demo is annotated as a draft, with the approval gate visible on screen.
- Demos never show "auto-send" flows.
- Demos always close on the next-step (book a diagnostic, kick off a Sprint).

---

## 10. Defending the Position | حماية التموضع

- We resist being reduced to a tool ("Dealix vs. Outreach") by always reframing to the Revenue Operating Company category.
- We invest in evidence-led product narratives (signed proof packs, exportable audit logs) over feature-list arms races.
- We refuse off-category features that violate non-negotiables — even when prospects request them.

---

## 11. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending — at any rung, on any surface.
- No proof publishing without written customer approval.
- No guaranteed-revenue claims in product copy, demos, or sales decks.
- No pricing/contract/payment commitments without escalation.
- Approval-gated automation only — never "autonomous."

---

## 12. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Product Lead + Founder.
- **KPI:** Console adoption (active days/month per active customer).
- **KPI:** Approval-latency on draft → decision (target tight, per rung).
- **KPI:** Proof-pack approval rate (target ≥ 90%).
- **KPI:** Ladder-progression rate by cohort.
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations (target 0).
