# Dealix Product Positioning

## 1. Category

**Saudi B2B Revenue Intelligence Operating System.**

We are not a CRM. We are not a marketing automation tool. We are not a sales-enablement library. We are the operating layer between a Saudi B2B founder's intent and the work that needs to happen on Monday morning to grow revenue.

## 2. One-sentence positioning

> Dealix is the revenue operating system that turns Saudi B2B intent into trust-gated, founder-approved action — with AI assistance and human approval on every outbound move.

## 3. For whom

- Saudi B2B founders, CROs, heads of sales.
- Mid-market (20–500 headcount).
- High-ticket motion (≥ 5,000 SAR/mo or ≥ 15,000 SAR one-off).
- Buyers who accept human-approved automation and refuse uncontrolled outbound.

## 4. Not for whom

- Consumer brands.
- Sub-2,000 SAR/mo motion seekers.
- Buyers who demand fully autonomous outreach.
- Sectors on the doctrine black-list.

## 5. Competitive contrast

| If the buyer is comparing to…       | What we say                                                                |
|-------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| HubSpot                              | HubSpot is a great CRM. We don't replace it — we operate on top of it, in Arabic + English, with a founder approval queue. |
| Salesforce                           | Salesforce is a great system of record. We connect to it; we don't compete. We make Monday morning happen.                |
| Apollo, Outreach, Salesloft          | Those are excellent for high-volume outbound. We're for high-trust, founder-approved outbound. Different motion.            |
| Generic AI-SDR tools                 | We don't believe "AI that sells for you" is the right doctrine for Saudi B2B. We believe in AI that drafts; founder approves. |
| Consultancies                        | Consultants give you a deck. We give you a desk that operates every week.                                                  |

## 6. Three things we will never claim

- Guaranteed revenue / guaranteed sales.
- Fully autonomous outbound.
- "World's first" anything.

## 8. Buyer's job-to-be-done

> "I want to grow Saudi B2B revenue, in Arabic and English, with AI assistance that I can audit, edit, and stop — operated by a desk I trust on a budget I control."

That sentence is the single most important sentence in the company. Every offer, deck, and proposal is judged against it.
