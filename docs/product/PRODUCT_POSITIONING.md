# Product Positioning (per rung)

A one-line positioning for each rung of the ladder. Use these on the
landing page, in the founder console, in proposals, in proof packs.

| Rung | One-line |
|---|---|
| Free Sample / Diagnostic | "See where your revenue motion stands — bilingual, scored, no sales pitch." |
| Revenue Sprint | "Seven days, one queue, founder-approved drafts ready to go." |
| Managed Pilot | "Ninety days running the Revenue OS — scored pipeline, audit log, one proof artifact." |
| Revenue Desk Retainer | "A monthly cockpit, run with you weekly, built around your approval rhythm." |
| Founder Console | "Your always-on Revenue Operating System — drafts in, approvals out, audit by default." |
| Enterprise Revenue OS | "Multi-BU Revenue OS with a control plane, agent registry, and executive observability." |
| Partner / White-label | "Dealix's engine inside your brand — governed, audited, partner-co-controlled." |

## Voice rules

- ✅ Concrete: "drafts", "scored", "approved", "audit".
- ✅ Bilingual: every line ships with an Arabic counterpart.
- ❌ Hype: no "AI-powered revolution", no multipliers, no guarantees.
- ❌ Vague: no "transform your business", no "next-generation".
