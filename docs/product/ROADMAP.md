# Roadmap

The rolling product roadmap. Three horizons.

## Now (this month)
- Items in active build.
- Each item has an owner, a customer evidence link, and a target date.

## Next (next 60–90 days)
- Items committed but not yet started.
- Each item has a customer evidence link and a rough size.

## Later (beyond 90 days)
- Items under consideration.
- No commitment, no dates.

## Movement rules
- An item moves from Later to Next only with a logged decision in `DECISION_LOG.md`.
- An item moves from Next to Now only when capacity is available and the dependency chain is clear.
- An item can be killed at any horizon with a logged reason.

## Cadence
- Updated weekly in the Weekly CEO Review.
- Restructured monthly in the Monthly Strategy Review.

## Rule
The roadmap is short on purpose. If it is too long to read in five minutes, it is hiding indecision.
