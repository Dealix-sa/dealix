# Product Principles

How Dealix decides what to build.

## Principles
1. **Revenue first.** A feature must support cash collected, MRR, retention, or founder leverage.
2. **Customer evidence.** A feature needs a named customer or a recurring pattern across customers.
3. **Trust by default.** Every feature respects Claim Guard, suppression, and approval rules.
4. **Boringly reliable.** Reliability outranks novelty.
5. **Small surface.** Fewer, better features over many, fragile ones.
6. **Reversible.** Default to changes that can be rolled back.
7. **Documented.** Every feature ships with a one-page user note.

## What we say no to
- Vanity features with no revenue or trust impact.
- Bundles of "nice to have" with one real customer signal.
- Custom code for one prospect with no payment.
- Features that violate Claim Guard or `APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.

## Rule
Product is a means to revenue and trust. It is not a portfolio of demos.
