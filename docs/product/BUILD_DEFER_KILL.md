# Build / Defer / Kill

The simple decision frame applied to every feature.

## Build now
- Strong customer evidence.
- Clear revenue impact.
- Owner available.
- Risk understood and acceptable.

## Defer
- Promising but not urgent.
- No owner today.
- Risk not fully understood.
- Single prospect with no payment.

## Kill
- No customer evidence after three sweeps of the intake queue.
- Replaced by a better solution.
- Violates Claim Guard or `APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.
- Will not earn its keep in 12 months.

## How decisions are recorded
- One line per item in `ROADMAP.md`.
- A killed item leaves a short note explaining why.

## Rule
Killing well is a leadership skill. Reward the team for honest kills, not for stockpiling ideas.
