# Product Distribution OS

How each rung of the Dealix offer ladder reaches its buyer.

## 1. Doctrine

- Each offer has **one primary distribution channel** and **at most one supporting channel**.
- Each offer has a **single, named owner** end-to-end (positioning → outreach → proposal → close → delivery handoff).
- Each offer is **always paired with a proof artefact** before going out.

## 2. Distribution matrix

| Offer                              | Primary channel             | Supporting channel       | Proof required                  |
|------------------------------------|------------------------------|-----------------------|---------------------------------|
| Free Sample / Diagnostic            | LinkedIn (founder-led)        | Warm intro             | Previous anonymised diagnostic  |
| Revenue Sprint                      | LinkedIn + warm intro         | Email                  | Sector-relevant sample          |
| Managed Pilot                       | Founder call → proposal       | Partner co-sign        | Sprint delivered                |
| Revenue Desk Retainer               | Customer review meeting       | LinkedIn (founder-led) | Pilot delivered                 |
| Founder Console / Command Center    | Co-located founder review     | Partner co-sign        | Retainer 90+ days               |
| Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS  | Direct executive engagement   | Partner co-sign        | 2 retainers in sector           |
| Partner / White-label               | Partner network               | Founder call           | Sprint + partner-fit case       |

## 3. Funnel per rung

```
Awareness (content + sector report)
  ↓
Engagement (LinkedIn comment / DM / warm intro)
  ↓
Discovery call (founder-led)
  ↓
Diagnostic / Sample (rung 1)
  ↓
Sprint (rung 2)
  ↓
Pilot (rung 3)
  ↓
Retainer (rung 4)
  ↓
Console / Enterprise (rungs 5–6)
```

Sideways:

- Partner (rung 7) is a separate motion — enters at Sprint or Retainer level depending on the partner's maturity.

## 4. Owner mapping

| Offer                              | Default owner                 |
|------------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Free Sample / Diagnostic            | Founder                       |
| Revenue Sprint                      | Founder                       |
| Managed Pilot                       | Founder + Distribution Operator|
| Revenue Desk Retainer               | Founder + Distribution Operator|
| Founder Console / Command Center    | Founder                       |
| Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS  | Founder                       |
| Partner / White-label               | Founder + partner contact     |

In v1 the founder is the named owner everywhere. The roadmap is to delegate selected rungs to a Sales Operator as evidence accumulates.

## 5. Conversion targets (placeholder)

| Stage transition                | Target rate          |
|---------------------------------|----------------------|
| Engagement → Discovery call     | ≥ 25%                |
| Discovery call → Diagnostic     | ≥ 50%                |
| Diagnostic → Sprint             | ≥ 40%                |
| Sprint → Pilot or Retainer      | ≥ 50%                |
| Retainer → Console / Enterprise | ≥ 25%                |

Targets are reviewed quarterly. They are operating heuristics, not promises.

## 7. Doctrine reminder

- No public pricing claims a guaranteed outcome.
- No outreach pitches a rung the buyer's persona has on their refusal list.
- Every offer is paired with the documented proof artefact before going out.
