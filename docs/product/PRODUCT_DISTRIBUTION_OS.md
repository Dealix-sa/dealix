# Product Distribution OS | نظام توزيع المنتج

> How each product rung is matched to channels, motions, and partners — so the right buyer enters at the right rung, with the right trust posture.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

To make product distribution a system, not a campaign. Every rung has a primary motion, a primary channel, and a primary owner.

---

## 2. Distribution Map | خريطة التوزيع

| Rung | Primary motion | Primary channel(s) | Secondary channel(s) | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free Diagnostic | Inbound + founder warm | Site, LinkedIn, newsletter | Partner referrals, events | Sales Lead |
| Revenue Sprint | Diagnostic conversion | Diagnostic call | Founder DMs, partner intros | Sales Lead |
| Managed Pilot | Sprint expansion | Sprint review meeting | Founder relationships | Revenue Desk Lead |
| Revenue Desk Retainer | Pilot expansion | Quarterly business review | Founder relationships | Revenue Desk Lead |
| Founder Console | Retainer add-on / direct | Console product surface | Founder content | Product Lead |
| Enterprise Revenue OS | Outbound enterprise | Direct + warm intros | Partner intros, events | Enterprise Account Lead |
| Partner / White-label | Partner program | Partner program page + warm intros | Events, partner field | Partnerships Lead |

---

## 3. Motion Patterns | أنماط الحركة

### Inbound motion (Diagnostic → Sprint)
- Content earns the visit.
- Landing page books the diagnostic.
- Diagnostic produces evidence and a written Sprint shape.
- Sprint closes by mutual written go.

### Warm-intro motion (any rung)
- Founder / partner makes the intro.
- First call references the introducer and the shared problem.
- We open at the rung the buyer is ready for — sometimes directly into Pilot or Retainer.

### Partner-led motion (Sprint or Pilot)
- Partner qualifies and brings the customer to a joint diagnostic.
- Partner delivers under the same non-negotiables, using the same evidence layer.
- Revenue share applies on shipped, paid outcomes.

### Enterprise motion (OS)
- Multi-thread: BU sponsor + procurement + security + commercial.
- Pre-Sprint security review and DPA agreed before pilot.
- Pilot evidence informs OS rollout shape.

---

## 4. Channel Discipline | انضباط القنوات

- **Owned (site, newsletter, product):** the primary surface for sustained inbound. Tested, measured, iterated.
- **Founder-led (LinkedIn, podcast, essays):** the primary surface for category education and warm pipeline.
- **Partner-led (referrals, co-marketing, events):** the primary surface for distribution diversity.
- **Earned (PR, press):** selective, for category-defining moments only.
- **Paid:** only when conversion math is proven from organic; small, single-page tests; never as the primary growth lever.

---

## 5. Rung-to-Channel Fit | ملاءمة الدرجات للقنوات

| Rung | Best channel | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Diagnostic | Owned + founder-led | Low friction; conversion-driven. |
| Sprint | Diagnostic conversion | Trust is highest immediately after diagnostic. |
| Pilot | Sprint review | Evidence-led upgrade conversation. |
| Retainer | QBR conversation | Operational continuity sells itself. |
| Console | Retainer + founder content | Product surface is best demonstrated, not advertised. |
| Enterprise OS | Direct + warm + partner | Multi-stakeholder, requires human depth. |
| Partner / White-label | Partner program + warm | Trust-based, peer-to-peer. |

---

## 6. Trust Gates Per Distribution Step | بوابات الثقة لكل خطوة

- **Lead capture:** no pre-checked consent; PDPL-clear forms.
- **First call:** no pricing/contract commitments — we frame ranges and next steps.
- **Diagnostic delivery:** written artifact only; no customer-name use without approval.
- **Sprint go:** signed engagement before any send is even drafted for the customer's identity.
- **Sprint sends:** approval-gated per the customer's approval cadence.
- **Proof publication:** written customer approval required at every step.
- **Renewal / expansion:** governance review + evidence digest must be current.

---

## 7. Distribution Telemetry | قياس التوزيع

| Metric | Where it's measured | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Diagnostic bookings | Owned analytics, forms | Weekly |
| Diagnostic → Sprint conversion | CRM | Weekly |
| Sprint → Pilot conversion | CRM | Monthly |
| Pilot → Retainer conversion | CRM | Monthly |
| Channel attribution | First-touch + last-touch | Weekly |
| Partner-sourced share | CRM tag | Monthly |
| Cycle time per rung transition | CRM | Monthly |

We do not chase vanity metrics (impressions, raw click counts) outside paid experiments.

---

## 8. Distribution Anti-Patterns | الأنماط المرفوضة

- Auto-DMs / auto-connects.
- Mass-templated outbound under any identity.
- Pricing in cold copy.
- Customer logos used as social proof without written approval.
- Paid-led growth before organic conversion math is proven.
- Partner deals signed without a tier and a clear non-negotiable agreement.

---

## 9. Capacity & Throughput | الطاقة والمعدل

- Each Revenue Desk Operator caps active engagements at a stated number to preserve approval-cadence quality.
- The Founder caps active diagnostics/week to preserve quality of first-call experience.
- Partners take overflow only after they are tiered (Co-delivery or White-label).
- Throughput planning is reviewed monthly against pipeline reality.

---

## 10. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending without approval — applies to Dealix, customer, and partner senders.
- No proof publishing without written approval.
- No guaranteed-revenue claims in any distribution surface.
- No pricing or contract commitments without escalation.
- Approval-gated automation only.

---

## 11. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Sales Lead, with Marketing Lead for inbound, Partnerships Lead for partners, Enterprise Lead for enterprise.
- **KPI:** Diagnostic bookings / week.
- **KPI:** Rung-progression conversion rates.
- **KPI:** Channel mix (target ≥ 30% partner by month 12).
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations (target 0).
