# Productization Command Center

## Purpose
Identify which manual Dealix workflows deserve templates, automation, or SaaS features.

## Owner
Sami / Product owner.

## Review Cadence
Monthly.

## Inputs
- repeated delivery work
- customer requests
- founder bottlenecks
- QA failures
- repeated manual tasks
- revenue evidence

## Outputs
- productization candidate
- build/fix/kill/defer decision
- automation priority
- SaaS readiness signal

## Rules
- Manual success 3 times = document.
- Success 5 times = template.
- Success 10 times = automate.
- Repeated customer request = product candidate.
- No SaaS feature without revenue or delivery evidence.

## Metrics
- repeated workflow count
- time saved
- customer requests
- delivery speed improvement
- revenue impact

## Evidence
- delivery logs
- customer feedback
- weekly reviews
- feature intake
