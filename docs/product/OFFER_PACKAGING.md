# Offer Packaging | تغليف العروض

> How each rung is packaged into a buyable, deliverable, evidenced offer — with clear scope, clear inclusions, clear exclusions, and clear trust gates.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

To eliminate scope drift, price-on-the-fly, and bespoke proposals. Every Dealix offer is **productized**: same shape across customers, same evidence, same gates.

---

## 2. Packaging Principles | مبادئ التغليف

1. **Outcome-named, not hour-billed.** Each offer names the result, not the inputs.
2. **Scope is finite.** Inclusions and exclusions are both listed.
3. **Trust gates are part of the offer.** Approvals, evidence, and audit are listed as deliverables.
4. **Ranges, not promises.** Numbers are ranges with conditions and pilot N.
5. **Climb-up path is explicit.** Every offer references the next rung up.

---

## 3. Offer Anatomy | تشريح العرض

| Field | Required content |
|---|---|
| Name | The rung name (e.g., "Revenue Sprint") |
| One-line outcome | The single sentence the buyer remembers |
| Audience | The buyer this offer is for |
| Inclusions | Bulleted, specific, evidence-shaped |
| Exclusions | Bulleted, specific, to prevent scope creep |
| Trust gates | The non-negotiables made concrete for this offer |
| Inputs needed from customer | Bulleted |
| Outputs shipped to customer | Bulleted, signed/approved where relevant |
| Time | Calendar duration |
| Roles | Customer-side + Dealix-side |
| Climb-up | The next-rung trigger |
| Price posture | Range + condition + escalation owner (see `PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md`) |

## 4. Free Diagnostic Package | باقة التشخيص المجاني

- **Outcome:** A written read on ICP density, warm-graph, and sample bilingual drafts in one week.
- **Inclusions:** 1 × 30-minute diagnostic call; 1 × written diagnostic (EN + AR); 4–6 sample bilingual drafts; 1 × proposed Revenue Sprint shape.
- **Exclusions:** No sends. No CRM integration. No long-form research.
- **Trust gates:** No customer-name use without approval. No pricing commitment. No revenue guarantee.
- **Inputs:** 30 minutes of buyer time; 2 ICP examples.
- **Outputs:** Written diagnostic + sample drafts + Sprint shape.
- **Time:** 1 calendar week.
- **Climb-up:** Sprint, scoped from the diagnostic shape.

---

## 5. Revenue Sprint Package | باقة سبرنت الإيرادات

- **Outcome:** First evidenced pipeline + first approved proof pack in 30 days.
- **Inclusions:** ICP refinement; bilingual draft library; approval-gated send cadence; weekly evidence digest; 30-day proof pack (customer-approved).
- **Exclusions:** Multi-ICP roll-out; new domain warming; new sender provisioning beyond agreed identities.
- **Trust gates:** All sends approval-gated. No proof publishing without customer approval. No revenue guarantee.
- **Inputs:** Signed engagement; ICP definitions; approved sender identities; bilingual intake.
- **Outputs:** Draft library; approval queue; sent record; weekly digest; final proof pack.
- **Time:** 30 calendar days.
- **Climb-up:** Managed Pilot when the customer wants the motion repeatable across 1–2 ICPs.

---

## 6. Managed Pilot Package | باقة الإدارة التجريبية

- **Outcome:** Repeatable approval-gated motion across 1–2 ICPs over 60–90 days.
- **Inclusions:** Sprint motion expanded; weekly approval cadence; monthly proof pack; CRM/calendar/mailbox integration; bilingual operator pair.
- **Exclusions:** Multi-region rollout; enterprise governance; partner co-delivery.
- **Trust gates:** Approval-gated everything; weekly evidence digest signed by both sides; PDPL boundaries enforced.
- **Inputs:** Sprint learnings; expanded ICP; integrations; governance contacts.
- **Outputs:** Approval queue, monthly proof pack, integration touchpoints, runbook.
- **Time:** 60–90 calendar days.
- **Climb-up:** Revenue Desk Retainer.

---

## 7. Revenue Desk Retainer Package | باقة عقد مكتب الإيرادات

- **Outcome:** Ongoing approval-gated revenue function, operated with the customer.
- **Inclusions:** Daily approval queue; weekly evidence digest; monthly customer-approved proof pack; quarterly business review; documented escalation path.
- **Exclusions:** Console deep customization; multi-BU enterprise rollout; partner-side delivery.
- **Trust gates:** MSA + DPA in place; roles & approvals matrix in writing; PDPL/ZATCA boundaries.
- **Inputs:** Monthly retainer fee; named approvers; integration access.
- **Outputs:** Operating cadence + signed evidence + monthly proof pack + QBR.
- **Time:** Monthly, rolling, 30-day notice.
- **Climb-up:** Founder Console (direct visibility) or Enterprise Revenue OS (multi-team).

---

## 8. Founder Console Package | باقة كونسول المؤسس

- **Outcome:** Direct console where founder/GM approves, audits, and steers.
- **Inclusions:** Per-seat console; signal radar; approval queue; downloadable evidence; SSO; bilingual UI.
- **Exclusions:** Custom development; bespoke integrations beyond the supported catalog.
- **Trust gates:** Approve / Decline / Edit per draft; signed audit log; PDPL/ZATCA aligned.
- **Inputs:** Seat license; connected mailbox/calendar/CRM; SSO config.
- **Outputs:** Console access; ongoing approval surface; exportable audit trail.
- **Time:** Monthly with annual recommended.
- **Climb-up:** Enterprise Revenue OS.

---

## 9. Enterprise Revenue OS Package | باقة نظام الإيرادات للمؤسسات

- **Outcome:** Multi-team Revenue OS with governance, integration, SLAs.
- **Inclusions:** Multi-BU rollout; SSO/SAML; governance dashboard; executive QBR; defined SLAs; named CSM; security review.
- **Exclusions:** Customer-specific code branches; off-category modules.
- **Trust gates:** MSA + DPA + Cross-Border Transfer Addendum where relevant; named approver per BU; quarterly governance audit.
- **Inputs:** Annual commitment; security & legal review; integration plan; named exec sponsor.
- **Outputs:** OS deployed across BUs; governance dashboards; SLA reporting; annual proof bundle.
- **Time:** Annual.
- **Climb-up:** Enterprise expansion (regions, BUs) or Partner enablement on top.

---

## 10. Partner / White-label Package | باقة الشريك

- **Outcome:** Productized Revenue OS partners resell or co-deliver under partner or co-branded shape.
- **Inclusions:** Partner agreement; training & certification; branded artifact pack; joint roadmap; revenue share on shipped, paid outcomes.
- **Exclusions:** Unbounded customization; commitments to partner's customers beyond the standard rung specs.
- **Trust gates:** Partners operate under the same non-negotiables; partner-side approvals run through Dealix evidence layer.
- **Inputs:** Partner agreement; trained delivery lead; commercial alignment.
- **Outputs:** Partner-shipped customer outcomes; revenue share statements; quarterly partner review.
- **Time:** Annual.
- **Climb-up:** Higher partner tier (Co-delivery → White-label) by performance.

---

## 11. Bundling & Discounting | الباقات والخصومات

- We do not bundle off-category items.
- Discounting requires escalation per `PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md`.
- "Founding customer" pricing is allowed only with documented limits and a sunset date.
- Annual commitments may earn a documented credit, recorded in the proposal.

---

## 12. Don'ts | ما لا نفعله

- Don't write bespoke proposals from scratch. Use the package templates.
- Don't promise outcomes outside the package's range + conditions + N.
- Don't include features that violate non-negotiables (auto-send, autonomous publish).
- Don't quote prices in cold copy or first calls without escalation per the guardrails.
- Don't ship customer logos in templates without approval.

---

## 13. Non-Negotiables | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending without approval.
- No proof publishing without written customer approval.
- No guaranteed-revenue claims in any package.
- No pricing/contract/payment commitments without escalation.
- Approval-gated automation only.

---

## 14. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Product Lead + Commercial Lead.
- **KPI:** % of deals closed in standard package shape (target ≥ 80%).
- **KPI:** Scope-change requests per package (track and reduce).
- **KPI:** Proof-pack approval rate (target ≥ 90%).
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations per package (target 0).
