# Offer Packaging

> What's in each rung, what's out, how to talk about it.

## 1. Common to every rung

- Trust-gated execution.
- Founder approval on every external action.
- Bilingual AR + EN deliverables.
- Audit-grade visibility.
- KSA-aware delivery (calendar, time-zones, holidays).

## 9. Packaging style guide

- Use plain phrases ("two weeks", "one lever") not invented terms.
- Use AR + EN parity.
- Never use the word "magic", "secret", or "10x".
