# Offer Packaging

How to package each rung of the offer ladder so it is **sellable on its own** and **upgradeable to the next rung**.

## 1. Packaging anatomy

Every offer is packaged with the same nine slots:

1. **Name.** Short, memorable, no jargon.
2. **One-line promise.** Promises a process, not an outcome.
3. **Who it's for.** Persona + sector.
4. **Who it's not for.** Anti-fit list.
5. **Deliverables.** Concrete artefacts.
6. **Trust note.** What we will NOT do.
7. **Price band.** SAR range; final price set per engagement.
8. **Time-box.** Days/weeks/months.
9. **Upgrade path.** Which rung is next.

## 2. Packaging templates

### Revenue Sprint (template)

- **Name.** Revenue Sprint.
- **Promise.** A 7-day Revenue Sprint that gives you a diagnostic, a draft pack, and a bilingual proof pack.
- **For.** P-CRO, P-Founder-Agency, P-Implementer at companies 20–500 headcount.
- **Not for.** Consumer brands; sub-2,000 SAR/mo motion; companies that demand uncontrolled outbound.
- **Deliverables.** Source Passport, DQ score, account scoring set, 10 founder-approved drafts, bilingual proof pack.
- **Trust note.** Drafts only — nothing leaves without founder approval. Optional founder shadow session.
- **Price band.** 499 – 4,999 SAR.
- **Time-box.** 7 days.
- **Upgrade.** Managed Pilot or Revenue Desk Retainer.

### Revenue Desk Retainer (template)

- **Name.** Revenue Desk.
- **Promise.** Monthly Revenue Desk: scoring, drafting, queueing, reporting — operated for you.
- **For.** P-CRO and P-CEO-MidMarket at companies 50–500 headcount.
- **Not for.** Sub-15K SAR-revenue accounts; companies refusing trust-gated motion.
- **Deliverables.** Monthly account scoring refresh, ≥ 30 drafts/month, weekly approval queue, monthly pipeline report.
- **Trust note.** Founder-approved outbound only. Customer keeps kill switch.
- **Price band.** 5,000 – 25,000 SAR/month.
- **Time-box.** Monthly, 3-month minimum.
- **Upgrade.** Founder Console / Command Center.

## 3. Variant rules

- Variants are allowed (e.g. "Revenue Sprint — KSA Logistics edition") but must inherit the same nine slots.
- Variants must declare what's **different** (additional deliverable, different time-box, etc.).
- Variant prices stay within the parent rung's band.

## 4. Forbidden language in packaging

- "Guaranteed", "fully autonomous", "10x", "revolutionise", "unlock", "synergy", "best-in-class".
- Vague verbs without a deliverable ("we engage", "we activate").
- Emojis.
- Exclamation marks.
