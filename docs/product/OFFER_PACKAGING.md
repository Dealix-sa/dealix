# Offer Packaging

How each rung is packaged into a deliverable, a contract, and a
shipping process.

## 1. Per-rung packaging

For every rung the packaging spec includes:

- **Inclusions** — exact artifacts the customer receives.
- **Exclusions** — explicit "not included" items.
- **SLA** — response time and artifact cadence.
- **Roles** — Dealix-side owner + customer-side owner.
- **Approval points** — where the founder is involved.
- **Renewal model** — how the rung continues or ends.

## 2. Rung specs (summary)

### Free Sample / Diagnostic
- Inclusions: bilingual one-pager, founder review session (30 min).
- Exclusions: any outbound, any send.
- SLA: artifact within one business day.
- Renewal: converts to Revenue Sprint or closes.

### Revenue Sprint
- Inclusions: scoring run, one queue (max 50 drafts), trust-gate setup.
- Exclusions: ongoing operations, retainer hours.
- SLA: queue ready by day 4; founder sessions on days 1, 4, 7.
- Renewal: converts to Managed Pilot or closes.

### Managed Pilot
- Inclusions: weekly cycle, scoring refresh, drafts, one proof artifact.
- Exclusions: agency-style content production at scale.
- SLA: weekly approval cycle; MBR at day 60.
- Renewal: monthly retainer on day 90.

### Revenue Desk Retainer
- Inclusions: monthly scoring refresh, weekly approvals, MBR.
- Exclusions: agency content, ad operations.
- SLA: 24-hour response on approvals.
- Renewal: monthly auto-renew; 30-day notice.

### Founder Console (annual)
- Inclusions: full agent registry, audit ledger, console access.
- Exclusions: bespoke engineering outside agent registry.
- SLA: annual roadmap + quarterly governance review.
- Renewal: annual.

### Enterprise Revenue OS
- Inclusions: control plane, multi-BU governance, PMO support.
- Exclusions: line-of-business operations outside scope.
- SLA: enterprise SLA per signed contract.

### Partner / White-label
- Inclusions: partner agreement, co-brand templates, governance.
- Exclusions: end-customer support (partner-owned).
- SLA: partner-specific.

## 3. Banned packaging patterns

- ❌ "Unlimited everything."
- ❌ "Money-back guarantee on revenue outcomes."
- ❌ Hidden exclusions discovered after sign.
