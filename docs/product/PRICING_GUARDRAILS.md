# Pricing Guardrails | حدود التسعير

> Pricing at Dealix is a trust act. These guardrails keep the team from drifting into ad-hoc quotes, undefended discounts, or commitments that violate a non-negotiable.

---

## 1. Purpose | الغرض

To set a single, repeatable framework for how Dealix prices each rung, how quotes are produced, and when escalation is required. The guardrails apply to every customer-facing surface — sales calls, decks, emails, proposals, landing pages, and the product UI.

---

## 2. Pricing Principles | المبادئ

1. **Price the outcome, not the hour.** Every rung is priced as a productized package.
2. **Ranges before commits.** In discovery, share ranges + conditions; commit only in a written proposal after qualification.
3. **Escalation is the default.** Any concession beyond the guardrail threshold escalates to Founder + Commercial Lead.
4. **Documented, not freelanced.** Every quote uses an approved template, recorded in the deal record.
5. **No commitments in marketing.** Marketing copy describes posture and ranges, never specific commitments.

---

## 3. Posture by Surface | الموقف حسب السطح

| Surface | Pricing content allowed |
|---|---|
| Landing page | Posture only ("Productized rungs. Talk to us for a quote.") |
| Cold email / LinkedIn DM | No prices. No discounts. |
| First call | Ranges + conditions + how the proposal will be built. |
| Written proposal | Specific numbers, scoped to the package, signed by Commercial Lead. |
| MSA / SOW | Final commercial terms after legal review. |

---

## 4. Rung Pricing Posture | تسعير الدرجات

> Specific numbers live in the active pricing sheet (Finance), not in this document. The posture below is durable.

| Rung | Pricing model | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Free Diagnostic | Free, time-boxed | Qualifies the buyer; no obligation. |
| Revenue Sprint | Fixed fee, one-time | Scoped to the package shape. |
| Managed Pilot | Fixed fee, one-time | Scoped to 60–90 days, 1–2 ICPs. |
| Revenue Desk Retainer | Monthly subscription | 30-day notice; quarterly true-up. |
| Founder Console | Per-seat subscription | Monthly with annual credit. |
| Enterprise Revenue OS | Annual contract | Custom terms within guardrails; security/legal review required. |
| Partner / White-label | Revenue share + base | Tier-dependent; annual minimums where applicable. |

---

## 5. Discount Guardrails | حدود الخصومات

- **Up to a low single-digit %** — approved by Sales Lead, documented in deal record.
- **Mid single-digit to low double-digit %** — approved by Commercial Lead, documented with rationale.
- **Above the low double-digit % threshold** — escalates to Founder + Commercial Lead, with written rationale and a sunset date.
- **"Founding customer" pricing** — capped quantity, documented cohort, mandatory sunset clause.
- **No discount stacking** — at most one discount lever per deal.
- **No undisclosed side letters** — any concession lives in the contract.

(Numeric thresholds live in the Finance pricing sheet and are reviewed quarterly.)

---

## 6. Escalation Triggers | محفزات التصعيد

Any of the following requires immediate escalation to Founder + Commercial Lead:

- Discount above the documented threshold.
- Non-standard term length.
- Non-standard payment terms (deferred, milestone-based, performance-based).
- Promised feature or scope outside the package.
- Promised SLA tighter than the published one.
- Custom legal terms not in the template MSA / DPA.
- Revenue, conversion, or pipeline guarantees.
- Exclusivity, MFN, or "best customer" clauses.

Escalation is documented in the deal record before any verbal or written commitment.

---

## 7. Quote Hygiene | نظافة العروض

- Quotes are produced from the approved proposal template.
- Every quote includes: scope (inclusions + exclusions), trust gates, time, price, term, payment terms, owner.
- Verbal numbers are framed as ranges with conditions; specific commitments are made in writing only.
- Quote validity is time-boxed (typically 30 days).
- Once signed, the quote and its inputs are archived in the deal record.

---

## 8. Currency, Tax, and Compliance | العملة والضريبة

- Default currency: **SAR**. Other currencies allowed by exception with FX-risk note.
- VAT applied per ZATCA rules; invoices are ZATCA e-invoicing compliant.
- Cross-border arrangements include the appropriate Cross-Border Transfer Addendum.
- PDPL boundaries reflected in DPA before any data is exchanged.

---

## 9. Pricing Non-Negotiables | ممنوعات التسعير

- **No revenue guarantees**, ever. Pricing is for the work; outcomes are framed as ranges + conditions + pilot N.
- **No "pay-only-if-you-grow" deals** without an explicit, escalated structure approved by Founder + Commercial Lead.
- **No verbal price commitments** in cold copy, first calls, or events.
- **No bundling of off-category items** to justify pricing.
- **No pricing displayed on customer-facing surfaces** beyond posture (rung names, productized shape).

---

## 10. Partner Pricing | تسعير الشركاء

- Partner economics are documented per tier (Referral / Co-delivery / White-label).
- Revenue share applies to shipped, paid outcomes.
- No revenue-share figures are shared in cold partner outreach.
- Partner-facing pricing follows the same escalation triggers as customer-facing pricing.

---

## 11. Audit & Review | المراجعة

- Pricing sheet reviewed monthly by Finance.
- Discount log reviewed monthly by Commercial Lead.
- Outliers (escalated deals) reviewed by Founder.
- Annual pricing review: posture, package shape, partner share.

---

## 12. Workflow | سير العمل

```
Discovery  →  Qualification call (ranges + conditions, no commitments)
          →  Package selection  →  Proposal draft (template)
          →  Guardrail check (Sales Lead)  →  Escalation if triggered
          →  Customer review  →  Negotiation within guardrails
          →  MSA + DPA (legal review)  →  Sign  →  Archive
```

A deal cannot move to signature without passing the guardrail check and, where required, escalation approval.

---

## 13. Non-Negotiables (recap) | الخطوط الحمراء

- No external sending without approval.
- No proof publishing without written customer approval.
- No guaranteed revenue claims tied to pricing.
- No pricing, contract, or payment commitments without escalation.
- Approval-gated automation only — pricing for "autonomous" agents is not a thing we sell.

---

## 14. Owner & KPI | الملكية والمؤشرات

- **Owner:** Founder + Commercial Lead, with Finance for the active pricing sheet.
- **KPI:** % of deals closed at list / within guardrail (target ≥ 80%).
- **KPI:** Average effective discount (track and reduce).
- **KPI:** Escalations completed before commitment (target 100%).
- **KPI:** Non-negotiable violations in commercial conversations (target 0).
