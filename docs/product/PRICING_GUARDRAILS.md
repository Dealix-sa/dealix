# Pricing Guardrails

This document defines **bands** and **principles**, not exact public
prices. Exact numbers live in `data/private_ops_seed/product/pricing.yaml`
(not in this repo) so the founder can adjust without a code change.

## 1. Bands

| Rung | Band | Cycle |
|---|---|---|
| Free Sample / Diagnostic | No-charge / pilot fee. | One-time. |
| Revenue Sprint | Low entry SAR band. | One-time. |
| Managed Pilot | Mid-band quarterly fee. | One-time (90 days). |
| Revenue Desk Retainer | Mid-band monthly. | Monthly. |
| Founder Console | High band, annualised with monthly cycle. | Annual + monthly. |
| Enterprise Revenue OS | Enterprise SAR band, multi-year. | Multi-year. |
| Partner / White-label | Partner-specific (rev-share + setup). | Per-partner. |

## 2. Principles

- All prices are SAR.
- All tax-bearing invoices are ZATCA Phase 2 compliant.
- No public commitments to a specific price outside an issued proposal.
- No agent is allowed to commit a price or a discount in any draft.
- Discounts must be approved by the founder; recorded in the
  proposal register.

## 3. Audit

- Every signed proposal carries the band it lands in.
- A discount > 15 % from band median requires a recorded reason.

## 4. Banned patterns

- ❌ Auto-discounts triggered by a model.
- ❌ Mid-engagement price hikes outside the signed terms.
- ❌ Public "from $X" hero numbers that mislead the buyer.
