# Pricing Guardrails

We do not publish a single fixed price for every offer. We do publish **bands** and the **rules** that determine where in the band a specific engagement lands.

## 1. Price bands (canonical)

| Rung                              | Band (SAR)            | Cycle           |
|-----------------------------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| Free Sample / Diagnostic           | 0                      | One-off         |
| Revenue Sprint                     | 499 – 4,999             | One-off, 7 days |
| Managed Pilot                      | 5,000 – 14,999          | 30 days         |
| Revenue Desk Retainer              | 5,000 – 25,000          | Monthly         |
| Founder Console / Command Center   | 15,000 – 40,000         | Monthly         |
| Enterprise Revenue Intelligence OS | 50,000+                 | Per quarter     |
| Partner / White-label              | Revenue share, 10–25%    | Per closed deal |

All amounts are in SAR. Currency conversion is not allowed in proposals without explicit founder approval.

## 2. Within-band positioning rules

| Factor                                          | Push the price up                  | Push the price down              |
|-------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Buyer is in a sector we want for sector reports | No (no discount for our convenience)| Yes — strategic value to Dealix  |
| Buyer is a strategic logo                       | No                                  | Yes — discount documented        |
| Buyer accepts case-study disclosure             | No                                  | Yes — disclosed                  |
| Engagement complexity                           | Yes                                 | No                               |
| Bilingual + sector-specific                     | Yes                                 | No                               |
| Customer is a partner                           | No                                  | Yes — partner pricing applies    |

Every within-band move must be documented in the proposal's `price_rationale` field.

## 3. Hard rules

- **No discount without a documented rationale.** Verifier flags missing rationale.
- **No price above the band.** Above-band requires a new offer (or moving to the next rung).
- **No price below the band floor.** Sub-floor pricing is a doctrine violation.
- **No "limited-time" pricing.** We do not run scarcity-based pricing.
- **No "introductory" pricing that auto-increases.** The price stays as agreed.
- **No revenue-share without legal agreement.** Partner/white-label requires a signed contract.
- **No proposals quote a guarantee.** Verifier blocks.

## 4. Refund and cancellation

- **Revenue Sprint.** Time-boxed; non-refundable after deliverables start (Day 1). Pause-able with founder approval.
- **Managed Pilot.** Refundable pro-rata in first 5 days. After that, the pilot runs.
- **Revenue Desk Retainer.** Month-to-month after the 3-month minimum. Cancellation honoured at month-end.
- **Founder Console.** 6-month minimum. After that, month-to-month.
- **Enterprise.** Per contract.

The Payment Capture Machine logs every invoice, payment, refund, and cancellation event.

## 5. Taxes & jurisdiction

- All proposals state KSA VAT treatment explicitly.
- Default jurisdiction: Riyadh, KSA.
- Cross-border engagements require explicit founder approval.

## 6. Approval

Any pricing decision outside this document requires founder approval and a documented note in the proposal record.
