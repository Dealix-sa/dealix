# Buyer Persona System

> Personas Dealix engages with. Each persona has motivations, decision criteria, fears, language.

## 1. Personas

### 1.1 Founder-CEO (KSA SaaS / B2B)
- **Motivations:** Predictable cash, founder time freedom, defensible growth.
- **Decision criteria:** Outcome architecture; trust; bilingual capability.
- **Fears:** AI overreach, brand damage, vendor lock-in.
- **Language:** Action-oriented, founder-respectful.

### 1.2 Chief Revenue / Sales Officer
- **Motivations:** Quota predictability, team productivity.
- **Decision criteria:** Pipeline health metrics, integration to CRM.
- **Fears:** Pipeline theatre, lost deals, team friction.
- **Language:** Operator-tight, metric-led.

### 1.3 Head of Growth
- **Motivations:** Channel efficiency, signal density.
- **Decision criteria:** Reply quality, CAC payback.
- **Fears:** Spamming brand, sender reputation.
- **Language:** Diagnostic, experiment-led.

### 1.4 COO / VP Operations
- **Motivations:** Process visibility, audit-readiness.
- **Decision criteria:** Audit log, controls, sovereignty.
- **Fears:** Compliance gaps, untraceable actions.
- **Language:** Process-led, evidence-led.

### 1.5 Agency Operator (Partner)
- **Motivations:** Operate Dealix profitably under their own brand.
- **Decision criteria:** White-label posture, support quality.
- **Fears:** Partner conflict, margin compression.
- **Language:** Partnership-led, plain.
