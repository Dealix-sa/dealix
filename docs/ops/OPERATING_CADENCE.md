# Operating Cadence

## Daily

- Generate Daily CEO Brief.
- Review pipeline.
- Review approvals.
- Send or approve outreach.
- Check delivery blockers.
- Log one decision.
- Update execution ledger.

## Weekly

- Weekly CEO Review.
- Revenue review.
- Delivery review.
- Trust review.
- Learning review.
- Update Company OS Scorecard.

## Monthly

- Strategy review.
- Pricing review.
- Product roadmap review.
- Financial review.
- Hiring/delegation review.

## Enforcement

- Daily: `.github/workflows/dealix-company-os.yml` runs verification on
  every push to `main`.
- Weekly: founder closes `docs/founder/WEEKLY_CEO_REVIEW.md`.
- Monthly: snapshot of scorecard archived under
  `docs/learning/MONTHLY_STRATEGY_UPDATE.md`.
