# CEO Management System

## Purpose
Define the complete management rhythm for Dealix.

## Daily Management
Commands:
- make control-tower
- make stage
- make daily

Output:
- one CEO focus
- one revenue action
- updated pipeline
- trust review

## Weekly Management
Commands:
- make ceo-weekly
- make weekly-close
- make finance
- make strategic-decision

Output:
- weekly war room
- learning decision
- system update
- strategic decision

## Monthly Management
Commands:
- make board
- make monthly
- make business-score
- make audit

Output:
- board pack
- strategy review
- finance review
- capital allocation decision

## Quarterly Management
Output:
- ICP review
- pricing review
- hiring trigger review
- productization review
- SaaS readiness review

## Rules
- Daily: revenue action.
- Weekly: learning decision.
- Monthly: finance and strategy review.
- Quarterly: reset strategy if evidence says so.
