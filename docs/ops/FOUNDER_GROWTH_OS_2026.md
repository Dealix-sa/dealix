# Dealix Founder Growth OS 2026

## Executive intent

Dealix should operate as a founder-led commercial machine:

1. HubSpot is the CRM and commercial ledger.
2. Dealix is the intelligence and command layer.
3. Products are packaged as clear paid offers.
4. Sales agent outputs remain draft and approval-first.
5. External communication becomes live only after reputation, consent, suppression, and audit gates are complete.

## Research-backed operating principles

### Email delivery

Google requires all senders to Gmail accounts to set up SPF or DKIM, valid DNS, TLS, low spam rate, and accurate message formatting. Bulk senders must set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC, alignment, and one-click unsubscribe for marketing messages.

Source: https://support.google.com/a/answer/81126

### WhatsApp production messaging

WhatsApp Business Platform supports programmatic messaging at scale, but template messages require opt-in and approved templates. Meta also warns against unauthorized automated or bulk messaging outside official tools.

Source: https://developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp/overview

### HubSpot product strategy

HubSpot products represent goods or services sold, can be associated with line items, deals, quotes, invoices, payment links, and subscriptions. This supports Dealix service packaging into a clean sales catalog.

Source: https://knowledge.hubspot.com/products/create-and-manage-products

## Founder Growth OS pillars

### 1. Offer stack

| Offer | First sale motion | Expansion |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue Command Room Sprint | diagnostic and pipeline clarity | monthly revenue operations retainer |
| Company Brain Sprint | executive memory and decision layer | weekly CEO operating system retainer |
| Follow-up Recovery Sprint | missed follow-up recovery | multi-channel follow-up OS retainer |
| AI Sales Agent Setup | sales drafting and qualification | monthly AI sales operations retainer |
| AI Trust and Governance OS | internal AI safety and controls | quarterly AI governance audit |
| Client Success Sprint | delivery visibility and proof packs | client delivery OS retainer |

### 2. Daily commercial rhythm

Every operating day should produce:

- 100 accounts researched or refreshed
- 40 targets verified
- 25 sales packs or drafts
- 10 to 15 founder-approved contacts
- 3 to 5 call attempts
- 1 to 2 discovery calls booked
- 1 proposal or mini-diagnostic offer

### 3. HubSpot operating queue

HubSpot should hold:

- companies
- contacts
- products
- deals
- tasks
- notes
- calls
- meetings
- line items

Dealix should generate:

- pain hypothesis
- offer fit
- discovery questions
- next action
- proposal brief
- negotiation limits
- renewal opportunity

### 4. Financial cockpit

Track weekly:

- cash collected
- open pipeline
- weighted pipeline
- offer conversion rate
- proposal win rate
- retainer conversion rate
- average setup value
- MRR
- churn risk
- stalled deals
- founder hours per closed deal

### 5. Automation ladder

| Stage | Automation | Permission level |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Read HubSpot and generate reports | allowed |
| 1 | Generate sales packs and drafts | allowed |
| 2 | Create approved products and tasks | owner-approved |
| 3 | Create approved notes and deals | owner-approved |
| 4 | Queue approved messages | owner-approved |
| 5 | Send production messages | requires DNS, consent, suppression, audit, rate limits |
