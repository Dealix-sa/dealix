# 💰 Dealix — First Revenue Guarantee Playbook

**No one can "guarantee" revenue. But here's the highest-probability path to first paid customer in 7 days, based on 15 years of B2B launches.**

The math: every startup's first customer comes from one of these 4 sources. The closer to the top, the faster.

---

## Tier 1 — Friend / Family / Ex-colleague pilot (probability: 70%+, time: 1-3 days)

**Why it works:** Trust already exists. They say yes to help you, not because of ROI.

**Action:**
1. List 10 people who know you well and run a business or work in one
2. Pick 5 whose business matches Dealix (has leads, B2B, Arabic market)
3. Send this WhatsApp message (not LinkedIn) to each:

```
السلام عليكم [الاسم]،

أطلقت Dealix اليوم — رادار عمليات الإيراد المحكوم بالعربي الخليجي يجهّز مسودات رد ومتابعة لـ leads الشركات جاهزة لموافقتك، يؤهّلهم، ويجهّز اقتراحات حجز demos.

أبحث عن 3 أصدقاء عندهم business حقيقي لـ 7-Day Revenue Proof Sprint بـ 499 ريال. فكرت فيك لأن [سبب محدد: شغلكم في [قطاع]، عندكم leads من [مصدر]، أنا واثق إنكم بتستفيدون].

Sprint بقيادة المؤسس — أنا شخصياً أجهّز مسودات متابعة لـ 10-25 lead عندكم جاهزة لموافقتكم، وبعد 7 أيام تستلمون Proof Pack ملموس.

مهتم نتكلم 10 دقائق الآن؟
```

**Realistic math (تقديري، لا ضمان):**
- 5 WhatsApp messages → 3 replies
- 3 replies → 2 say yes to the Sprint
- 2 Sprints → 1 upgrades to Data-to-Revenue Pack / Managed Revenue Ops

**First paid customer within 7 days.**

---

## 🚫 What to avoid in first 30 days

- **Don't chase enterprise deals.** 3-6 month sales cycles kill first-year startups. Pick SMB/mid-market only.
- **Don't lower the 499 SAR Sprint price.** Under-pricing attracts price-sensitive customers with highest support cost.
- **Don't build features for one prospect.** Build for patterns you see across 3+ prospects.
- **Don't compete with Apollo/HubSpot/Salesforce head-on.** You win on Arabic + Saudi context, not feature breadth.
- **Don't pay for ads before Tier 1-2 prove signal.** Paid ads amplify broken funnels.
