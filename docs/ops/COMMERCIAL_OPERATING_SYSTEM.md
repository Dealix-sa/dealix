# Commercial Operating System

## Purpose

Give Dealix a repeatable operating rhythm for founder-led commercial launch.

## Daily loop

1. Research.
2. Verify.
3. Draft.
4. Human review.
5. Approved outreach action.
6. Diagnostic.
7. Proposal.
8. Delivery.
9. Proof.
10. Retainer.

## Required ledgers

- Lead pipeline.
- Deal pipeline.
- Proposal log.
- Proof ledger.
- Objection bank.

## Safety

All external-facing actions are approval-first until controlled-live infrastructure is separately approved.
