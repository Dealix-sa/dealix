# Operating Readiness Levels

## Purpose
Classify how mature each Dealix workflow is before automation or scaling.

## Owner
Sami / Founder CEO.

## Review Cadence
Weekly.

## ORL 0 — Idea
Workflow exists only as a concept.

## ORL 1 — Manual
Founder can execute it manually.

## ORL 2 — Repeated
Executed manually at least 3 times.

## ORL 3 — Templated
Template and checklist exist.

## ORL 4 — QA Controlled
Workflow has QA and approval gates.

## ORL 5 — Semi-Automated
System drafts or prepares; human approves.

## ORL 6 — Delegatable
Another person can execute from SOP.

## ORL 7 — Productization Candidate
Repeated enough to consider product feature.

## ORL 8 — Internal Automation
Low-risk internal actions can run automatically.

## ORL 9 — Governed Autonomous
Autonomy exists with approval, audit, monitoring, and learning loops.

## Rules
- No workflow jumps more than one ORL level per review.
- A3 workflows cannot be automated.
- External commitments require approval.
- Productization requires repeated evidence.

## Metrics
- ORL by workflow.
- workflows moved up.
- workflows blocked.
- incidents by ORL.

## Evidence
- weekly review.
- QA checklist.
- approval log.
- delivery records.
- customer feedback.
