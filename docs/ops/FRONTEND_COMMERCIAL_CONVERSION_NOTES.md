# Frontend Commercial Conversion Notes

## Research-backed decisions

The commercial launch page is structured around:

- Clear value proposition above the fold.
- One primary action: book a diagnostic.
- Pain-first narrative before product detail.
- Short packages with pricing ranges.
- Trust and safety language before asking for commitment.
- Mobile-friendly card layout.
- No fake client logos, fake ROI, or exaggerated guarantees.

## Why

Potential buyers need to quickly understand:

1. What business pain Dealix fixes.
2. What starts in the first 7 days.
3. What is safe and review-first.
4. What they need to do next.

## Implementation notes

- Route: `/commercial-launch`.
- Primary CTA: `/book`.
- Secondary CTA: `/pricing` and `/offers`.
- Uses existing global CSS tokens and cards.
- Avoids new dependencies.
