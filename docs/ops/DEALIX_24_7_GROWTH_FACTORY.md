# Dealix 24/7 Growth Factory

## Purpose

Run Dealix as a continuous revenue operations machine that researches,
scores, drafts, routes, follows up, and reports with minimal founder input.

## Core Principle

The machine prepares and prioritizes. Sami approves external commitments.

## Machines

### 1. Market Intelligence Worker
Finds sectors, signals, directories, competitor ecosystems, public company
lists, and buyer patterns.

### 2. Lead Discovery Worker
Builds lead queues from approved public sources.

### 3. Enrichment Worker
Adds website, sector, public contact path, buyer role, Saudi relevance,
and fit notes.

### 4. Lead Scoring Worker
Scores leads A/B/C/Reject.

### 5. Outreach Draft Worker
Creates personalized founder-led draft messages.

### 6. Approval Queue Worker
Creates batch approval files for Sami.

### 7. Sending Queue Worker
Prepares Gmail drafts or manual send queue after approval.

### 8. Follow-Up Worker
Schedules follow-ups based on last touch and reply status.

### 9. Reply Router Worker
Routes replies to sample, proposal, nurture, lost, or manual review.

### 10. Sample Factory Worker
Creates sample pack tasks and drafts.

### 11. Proposal Factory Worker
Creates proposal drafts after qualification.

### 12. Finance Follow-Up Worker
Tracks payment / PO / written approval follow-ups.

### 13. Delivery Trigger Worker
Starts client workspace after payment/PO/written approval.

### 14. Proof & Content Worker
Converts approved learning into safe content ideas.

### 15. CEO Report Worker
Generates daily mission control, stoplight, and acquisition report.

## Safety Gates

- No external sending without approval.
- No proposal sending without approval.
- No pricing exception without approval.
- No public proof without approval.
- No A3 action automatic.

## Founder Input Target

15 minutes/day:
- approve/reject lead batch
- approve message version
- approve proposal
- review red flags

## Compliance Anchors

- CST: respect Saudi anti-spam telecom rules.
- SDAIA / PDPL: respect personal data protection law obligations.
- NIST AI RMF: Govern / Map / Measure / Manage as continuous discipline.
- Internal trust kit: doctrine guardrails and founder approval gates.
