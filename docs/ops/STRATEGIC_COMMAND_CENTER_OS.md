# Dealix Strategic Command Center OS

## Purpose

The Strategic Command Center is the founder and client-facing control room for Dealix. It turns services, sales agents, targeting, company brain, delivery, and safety into one daily operating screen.

## Strategic lanes

### Revenue War Room

Shows hot opportunities, overdue follow-ups, open proposals, negotiation risks, and next best action.

### Targeting Engine

Shows today's sector focus, companies to research, verified targets, messages drafted, founder-approved contacts, and suppression checks.

### AI Sales Agent OS

Shows voice mode, allowed identity, current drafts, objections detected, negotiation guardrails, and required approvals.

### Company Brain

Shows daily CEO decision, risks, commercial memory, client context, future radar, and knowledge gaps.

### Client Delivery OS

Shows active projects, acceptance criteria, client risks, proof pack status, and renewal opportunities.

### Safety and Trust Gates

Shows outbound mode, email readiness, WhatsApp readiness, approval queue, claim checks, and audit trail status.

## Daily operating rhythm

1. Review safety state.
2. Review revenue bottleneck.
3. Pick sector wedge.
4. Generate company-specific sales packs.
5. Approve only the best drafts.
6. Run discovery calls.
7. Convert qualified prospects into sprint proposals.
8. Produce proof pack for every pilot.

## Why this sells

A client does not buy AI because it is AI. A client buys clarity, speed, less leakage, better follow-up, better delivery, and fewer risky decisions.

The command center makes those outcomes visible.

## Sales positioning

Dealix should present the command center as a CEO daily decision layer, sales follow-up discipline system, company memory system, AI approval layer, and delivery proof system.

## Launch rule

The command center can generate drafts and recommendations. It does not send external messages in baseline mode.
