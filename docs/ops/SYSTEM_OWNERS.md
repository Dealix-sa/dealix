# System Owners

Design the company as if a team already exists, even if every owner
today is Sami. The point is to make delegation later cheap — if you
don't know who will own a system next, you stay the owner forever.

| System            | Owner Today | Future Owner               | Review Cadence |
|-------------------|-------------|----------------------------|----------------|
| Founder OS        | Sami        | CEO / Chief of Staff       | Daily          |
| Revenue OS        | Sami        | Head of Sales              | Daily          |
| Delivery OS       | Sami        | Delivery Lead              | Weekly         |
| Trust OS          | Sami        | Ops / Compliance Lead      | Weekly         |
| Product OS        | Sami        | Product/Engineering Lead   | Weekly         |
| Finance OS        | Sami        | Finance/Ops                | Weekly         |
| Learning OS       | Sami        | Strategy/Ops               | Weekly         |
| Content OS        | Sami        | Content Lead               | Weekly         |
| Client Success OS | Sami        | CS Lead                    | Weekly         |

## Owner Responsibilities

Every owner is responsible for:

1. The system's metrics contract (`OPERATING_METRICS_CONTRACT.md`).
2. The system's loop (`OPERATING_LOOPS.md`).
3. Escalations from their system (`ESCALATION_MATRIX.md`).
4. Whether the system reduces or increases founder load
   (`docs/founder/FOUNDER_LEVERAGE_INDEX.md`).
