## 🚦 Execution rules

1. **Do NOT bulk-blast.** Send max 5 personalized per hour. No scraping, no cold WhatsApp/LinkedIn automation.
2. **Log every message in `docs/ops/pipeline_tracker.csv`** (or Google Sheets copy). No PII in logs.
3. **Respect opt-outs** immediately — add `opted_out` status, never message again.
4. **Target compliant segments only:** founders + business accounts + partner prospects. NO B2C mass DMs.
5. **Speed of reply** matters more than speed of sending. When someone replies: respond within 30 minutes.
6. **No guarantees, no sourceless stats.** Every claim is "estimated" or "case-safe pattern" or backed by a documented Proof Pack. Every external send is a human decision.
