## Execution rules

1. **Do NOT bulk-blast.** Send max 5 personalized messages per hour, manually.
2. **Drafts-only.** Dealix never sends external messages on a customer's behalf
   without explicit human approval. Every outreach message here is sent by the
   founder personally, by hand.
3. **Log every message in `pipeline_tracker.csv`** (or Google Sheets copy).
4. **Respect opt-outs** immediately — add `opted_out` status, never message again.
5. **Target compliant segments only:** founders + business accounts + partner
   prospects. No B2C mass DMs. No scraping, no automation.
6. **Speed of reply matters.** When someone replies, respond within 30 minutes.
