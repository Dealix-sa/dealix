## Execution rules | قواعد التنفيذ

1. **Do NOT bulk-blast.** Every message is personalized and sent manually,
   max 5 per hour. There is no automated outreach.
2. **The founder approves every external message before it is sent.**
   Dealix drafts; the founder reviews and approves; nothing auto-sends.
3. **Log every message in `docs/ops/pipeline_tracker.csv`** (or the Google
   Sheets copy).
4. **Respect opt-outs** immediately — add `opted_out` status, never message
   again.
5. **Warm + business segments only:** founders, business accounts, and
   partner prospects. No B2C mass DMs, no cold WhatsApp, no purchased lists.
6. **Speed of reply** matters: when someone replies, respond within 30 minutes.
7. **No claims of guaranteed sales, conversion rates, or ROI as fact.**
   Use "estimated" or "case-safe pattern". Frame results as
   evidenced opportunities, not promises.
