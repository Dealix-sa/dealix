# Hermes Escalation Matrix

## Purpose
Make escalation fast and clear when Hermes agents find risk, blockers, or high-impact decisions.

## Escalation levels

| Level | Meaning | Owner | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | Routine review needed | Workstream owner | Next review cycle |
| E2 | Important blocker or customer impact | Workstream owner + Founder | Same day review |
| E3 | High-risk AI, security, finance, or launch concern | Founder + accountable lead | Immediate review |
| E4 | Critical company or customer impact | Founder-led incident review | Incident process |

## Escalation by agent

| Agent | Escalate when | Escalate to |
|---|---|---|
| Founder Chief of Staff | Decision is blocked or weekly outcome is at risk | Founder |
| GTM Intelligence | Enterprise deal has proof, pricing, or procurement blocker | GTM Lead and Founder |
| Customer Success | Customer health turns red or onboarding stalls | CS Lead and Founder |
| AI Governance | AI workflow lacks evaluation, review, or data clarity | AI/Product, Security, Founder |
| Security and Reliability | P1 risk, incident, dependency concern, or release blocker | Engineering Lead and Founder |
| Finance and Board | Runway, hiring, or spend question needs decision | Founder |

## Escalation rules

- Every E2+ escalation needs an owner.
- Every E3+ escalation needs a decision record or incident record.
- Every E4 escalation follows the incident process.
- No escalation is closed without next action or explicit acceptance.
