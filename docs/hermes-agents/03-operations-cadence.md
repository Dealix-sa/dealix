# Hermes Operations Cadence

## Purpose
Define how Hermes agents support the founder and company operating rhythm.

## Daily cadence

| Agent | Daily output | Owner review |
|---|---|---|
| Founder Chief of Staff | Founder brief and blocked decisions | Founder |
| GTM Intelligence | Priority account briefs and deal risks | GTM Lead |
| Customer Success | Red/yellow account summary | CS Lead |
| Security and Reliability | Readiness and incident watch note | Engineering Lead |

## Weekly cadence

| Agent | Weekly output | Meeting |
|---|---|---|
| Founder Chief of Staff | Weekly executive memo | Weekly Business Review |
| GTM Intelligence | Pipeline and channel learning summary | GTM Review |
| Customer Success | Customer health and proof summary | CS Review |
| AI Governance | AI workflow and evaluation summary | Product/AI Review |
| Security and Reliability | Risk, incident, and dependency summary | Ops/Security Review |
| Finance and Board | Runway and decision narrative | Founder Review |

## Monthly cadence

- Review active agents.
- Review agent owner map.
- Review agent outputs and usefulness.
- Review AI/data/tooling cost.
- Retire unused agents.
- Promote useful agent drafts into official playbooks.

## Quarterly cadence

- Refresh agent missions.
- Refresh risk levels.
- Review automation boundaries.
- Review customer trust signals.
- Review strategic leverage created by agents.
