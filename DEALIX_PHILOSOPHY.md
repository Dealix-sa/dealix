# Dealix Philosophy

## English

Dealix is a Saudi-native Revenue Company Operating System.

It is designed to help the founder identify opportunities, sell focused offers, deliver with quality, govern risk, learn weekly, and convert proof into recurring revenue.

- AI prepares.
- Workflows execute.
- Trust governs.
- The founder approves critical moves.
- Metrics improve the system.

## العربي

ديلكس هو نظام تشغيل شركة إيراد سعودي.

مصمم ليساعد المؤسس على اكتشاف الفرص، بيع عروض مركزة، التسليم بجودة، حوكمة المخاطر، التعلم أسبوعيًا، وتحويل الإثبات إلى إيراد متكرر.

- الذكاء يجهّز.
- الأنظمة تنفّذ.
- الثقة تحكم.
- المؤسس يوافق على القرارات الحساسة.
- والمقاييس تطور النظام.

## The 12 Systems

| # | System | Directory | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Founder OS | `docs/founder/` | The CEO control tower, decisions, risks, focus rules |
| 2 | Revenue OS | `docs/revenue/` | Model, offer ladder, pipeline, proposal and closing rules |
| 3 | Delivery OS | `docs/delivery/` | Playbooks, intake, QA, handoff, case study |
| 4 | Trust OS | `docs/trust/` | Approvals, claim guard, suppression, retention, incidents |
| 5 | Learning OS | `docs/learning/` | Experiments, win/loss, message and sector performance |
| 6 | Finance OS | `docs/finance/` | Billing, payments, refunds, MRR, cash control |
| 7 | Client Success OS | `docs/client_success/` | Onboarding, weekly reports, retention, renewal |
| 8 | Acquisition OS | `docs/acquisition/` | Sourcing, ICP, outreach, follow-up |
| 9 | Product OS | `docs/product/` | Principles, roadmap, intake, build/defer/kill |
| 10 | Content OS | `docs/content/` | Strategy, voice, channels, proof library |
| 11 | People OS | `docs/people/` | Role map, hiring triggers, scorecards |
| 12 | Investor / Partner OS | `docs/investor/`, `docs/partners/` | Data room, partner program, market thesis |

## Verification

Run `python3 scripts/verify_company_os.py`.

- **PASS** — all 12 systems exist with their canonical files.
- **FIX** — non-core systems are incomplete.
- **BLOCKED** — one or more core systems are missing.
