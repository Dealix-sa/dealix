# AI Policy

> AI is a tool, not a strategy. State explicitly what AI may and may not do for this client.

## Governing principle

- **AI augments humans; it does not replace accountable decisions.**
- **Every AI output that reaches the client or a customer must have a human accountable owner.**
- **No AI output is published without review where stakes are material.**

## Prohibited AI uses

- Autonomous sending of outbound messages to client customers.
- Generating financial figures presented as final without reconciliation.
- Generating legal language presented as final without client legal review.
- Scraping third-party data without explicit permission.
- Any decision affecting PDPL/GDPR data subjects without a human in the loop.
