# Diagnostic Sprint Offer

## Purpose
A low-risk, high-clarity first engagement to map operational leaks and recommend the right OS.

## Scope
- 3 discovery sessions (45 min each)
- Process map of 2 critical workflows
- Leakage report with SAR estimate
- Recommended OS roadmap

## Deliverables
- Diagnostic report (PDF)
- Recorded walkthrough (optional)
- Proposal for next phase

## Timeline
5 business days

## Pricing
Fixed fee: 5,000 – 10,000 SAR depending on company size.

## Governance
- Human-reviewed outputs only
- No client data leaves encrypted storage
- PDPL-compliant handling
