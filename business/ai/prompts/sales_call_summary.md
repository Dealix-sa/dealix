# Prompt: Sales Call Summary (v1)

Read raw call notes (founder-provided). Produce:
1. 5-bullet summary of what was discussed.
2. Buyer's stated pain (verbatim quotes).
3. Identified next step (with owner + date).
4. Open questions / blockers.
5. Recommended Dealix offer match.

Rules:
- Quote buyer verbatim where possible.
- No invented intent.
- AR + EN.
