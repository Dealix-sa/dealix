# Dealix Lead Scoring Model

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Fit | 40 | Sector, size, geography match to Dealix ICP |
| Pain Clarity | 30 | Does the lead express a specific operational pain? |
| Budget Signal | 20 | Is there a budget or procurement timeline? |
| Urgency | 10 | Is there a deadline or competitive pressure? |

## Tiers

- **A (80–100)**: Ideal — fast-track to diagnostic
- **B (60–79)**: Good — nurture with targeted content
- **C (40–59)**: Possible — lightweight outreach, low priority
- **D (<40)**: Disqualify or revisit in 90 days

## Rules
- Score must be recalculated after every interaction.
- A lead can be downgraded if pain turns out to be vendor shopping.
- Budget signal requires verbal confirmation or RFP mention; do not assume.
