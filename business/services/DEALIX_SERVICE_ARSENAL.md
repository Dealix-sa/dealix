# Dealix Service Arsenal

## Positioning

Dealix is a Saudi B2B AI Operating Systems company. The strongest service model is not selling generic AI tools. It is selling operating systems that remove daily operational leakage in revenue, follow-up, delivery, and executive decision-making.

## Core service lines

### 1. Revenue Command Room OS

**Buyer:** CEO, founder, sales director, commercial director.

**Pain:** Leads, proposals, follow-ups, and account actions exist across WhatsApp, email, sheets, CRM, and memory.

**What Dealix builds:**

- opportunity ledger
- daily action queue
- hot account list
- proposal follow-up tracker
- sales manager briefing
- executive command report

**First offer:** 7-Day Revenue Command Room Sprint.

**Expansion:** monthly revenue operations retainer.

### 2. Company Brain OS

**Buyer:** CEO, founder, operations director.

**Pain:** Company knowledge is scattered and decisions are reactive.

**What Dealix builds:**

- company source of truth
- decision memory
- risk radar
- future opportunity radar
- weekly board memo
- daily CEO decision brief

**First offer:** 14-Day Company Brain Sprint.

**Expansion:** executive intelligence retainer.

### 3. Follow-up Recovery OS

**Buyer:** sales manager, customer service manager, clinic manager, real estate office owner.

**Pain:** Replies are late, conversations are not owned, and prospects disappear.

**What Dealix builds:**

- inbox and WhatsApp categorization model
- follow-up queue
- reply drafts
- escalation policy
- response SLA dashboard

**First offer:** Follow-up Recovery Sprint.

**Expansion:** managed follow-up operations.

### 4. AI Sales Agent OS

**Buyer:** growth lead, sales director, founder.

**Pain:** Sales team needs consistent, high-quality qualification, negotiation, and response handling.

**What Dealix builds:**

- approved sales voice
- company-specific pitch memory
- objection library
- qualification scripts
- negotiation guardrails
- draft responses for email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and calls

**Important:** The agent acts only as an authorized company representative with clear identity rules and approval gates. It must not pretend to be a real person unless that person explicitly owns and approves the communication.

### 5. AI Trust and Governance OS

**Buyer:** CEO, compliance, operations, IT.

**Pain:** Employees use AI without policy, review, data boundaries, or audit trail.

**What Dealix builds:**

- AI use policy
- data handling SOP
- approval classes
- prompt and output review log
- risk register
- incident response outline

### 6. Client Delivery OS

**Buyer:** agency owner, consultancy, service company.

**Pain:** Scope, acceptance criteria, client updates, and proof of work are inconsistent.

**What Dealix builds:**

- intake system
- scope cards
- delivery board
- client update templates
- proof pack
- renewal prompts

## Product ladder

| Stage | Offer | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Entry | Diagnostic | Identify leakage and high-value system |
| Sprint | 7-14 day OS build | Prove operational value fast |
| Retainer | Monthly operating system | Keep system updated and run daily |
| Enterprise | Custom OS | Integrate deeply with client workflows |
| Platform | Dealix SaaS modules | Standardize recurring revenue |

## Sales rule

Sell one pain first. Do not sell all services in the first meeting. Use the pain to choose the system.
