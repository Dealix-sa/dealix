# Clinics / Healthcare — Dealix OS Profile

## Visible Public Signals
- Low Google review count or recent reviews
- Social media only promotional, no educational content
- Online booking not available or broken

## Likely Operational Leakage
- Reputation blind spots (negative reviews unnoticed)
- High no-show rate
- Manual booking and follow-up

## Strongest Dealix Offer
- Review & Reputation OS + Operations & Delivery OS

## Openers
- AR: "كيف تتابعون رضا المرضى بعد الزيارة؟"
- EN: "How do you track patient satisfaction after the visit?"

## Diagnostic Questions
1. What is your no-show rate?
2. How many reviews do you get per month?
3. How do you handle a negative review today?

## Objections
- Privacy concerns → Explain PDPL-compliant process.
- Staff too busy → Automate the ask, not the response.

## Proof Plan
- L1: Review velocity before/after
- L3: Patient satisfaction score improvement

## First 7-Day Quick Win
- Automated post-visit review request (WhatsApp/SMS)

## Upsell Path
- Reputation OS → Operations OS → Custom patient CRM
