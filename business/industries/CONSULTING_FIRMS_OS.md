- Founder-heavy content, no team brand
- Proposals take days to prepare
- Revenue tied to hours, not outcomes

## Likely Operational Leakage
- Time leakage on unbilled work
- Proposal chaos (no templates, no versioning)
- No productized services

## Strongest Dealix Offer
- Revenue OS + Custom Enterprise OS

1. How long does it take to prepare a proposal?
2. Do you have productized service packages?
