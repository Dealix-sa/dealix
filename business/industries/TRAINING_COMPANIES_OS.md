# Training Companies — Dealix OS Profile

## Visible Public Signals
- High course catalog but low completion posts
- Heavy ad spend, thin organic engagement
- No alumni community activity

## Likely Operational Leakage
- Leads go cold after inquiry
- No follow-up engine post-course
- Low LTV due to one-off purchases

## Strongest Dealix Offer
- Revenue OS + Client Acquisition OS

## Openers
- AR: "كم نسبة إكمال المتدربين للبرنامج؟ وهل تتابعونهم بعد الانتهاء؟"
- EN: "What is your course completion rate? And do you follow up after graduation?"

## Diagnostic Questions
1. How many leads inquiry per month vs enroll?
2. Do you have a re-enrollment sequence?
3. What is your cost per acquired trainee?

## Objections
- Using competitor LMS → Diagnostic comparison offer.
- No budget → Pilot with revenue-share.

## Proof Plan
- L2: Completion rate improvement chart
- L4: Graduate hiring partner testimonial

## First 7-Day Quick Win
- Re-engagement email/WhatsApp sequence for cold leads

## Upsell Path
- Acquisition OS → Alumni community → Certification revenue
