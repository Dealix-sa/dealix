# Marketing Agencies — Dealix OS Profile

## Visible Public Signals
- Inconsistent portfolio updates
- Low client testimonial velocity
- Late or manual invoicing mentions

## Likely Operational Leakage
- Scope creep without change orders
- No retainer upsell system
- Time not tracked against proposals

## Strongest Dealix Offer
- Revenue OS + Command Center

## Openers
- AR: "هل لديكم نظام واضح لرفع قيمة العقد مع العميل الحالي؟"
- EN: "Do you have a clear system to increase contract value with existing clients?"

## Diagnostic Questions
1. What is your average client retention in months?
2. How do you track scope changes today?
3. What percentage of leads become retainers?

## Objections
- Too expensive → Show retainer expansion math.
- Not now → Offer a free scope-creep audit.

## Proof Plan
- L1: Before/after retainer expansion case
- L3: Testimonial from similar agency

## First 7-Day Quick Win
- Scope change tracker + automated invoice reminder

## Upsell Path
- Command Center → Custom dashboards → White-label client portals
