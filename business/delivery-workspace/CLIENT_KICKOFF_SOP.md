# Client Kickoff SOP

## Day -2
- Send kickoff confirmation + agenda + customer responsibilities checklist.
- Confirm attendees (commercial owner + operator).

## Day 0 (kickoff)
1. **Intro (10 min):** scope, goals, definitions.
2. **In-scope / out-of-scope matrix (20 min):** write it down live.
3. **Access grant (15 min):** customer adds Dealix to CRM (read), Google Business (admin if Review OS), WhatsApp Business (operator role).
4. **Friction interview (30 min):** what's costing them money today.
5. **Deliverable plan (15 min):** confirm first 3 deliverables and due dates.
6. **Next steps (10 min):** weekly review slot, draft approval cadence.

## Day +1
- Send written kickoff summary (`scripts/generate_client_brief.py --account-id <id>`).
- Customer signs off on the in-scope/out-of-scope matrix.

## Anti-patterns
- Skipping the in-scope/out-of-scope matrix.
- Booking the kickoff without confirming the operator's attendance.
- Sending a kickoff summary that overpromises.
