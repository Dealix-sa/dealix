# Deliverable Acceptance Flow

1. **Draft** — Founder produces, demo-marks if applicable.
2. **Internal review** — Founder walks through against `ACCEPTANCE_CRITERIA_TEMPLATE.md`.
3. **Delivery** — Sent to commercial owner with a clear "by Friday EOD, please confirm" ask.
4. **Customer review** — Customer accepts, rejects with notes, or asks for changes.
5. **Iterate** — Up to two iteration rounds in scope; further iterations become a CO.
6. **Accept** — Recorded via `record_client_approval.py`.
7. **Archive** — Final artifact saved to `business/proof/exports/<account-id>/`.

If silence past the acceptance window: counts as accepted per `ACCEPTANCE_CRITERIA_TEMPLATE.md`.
