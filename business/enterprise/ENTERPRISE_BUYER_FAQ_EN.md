# Enterprise Buyer FAQ — Dealix

## What is Dealix?

A Saudi-first company that builds business operating systems on top of the tools you already use: CRM, WhatsApp, reviews, reports, billing. We don't replace your stack — we connect, govern, and produce proof.

## How is Dealix different from a marketing agency?

Agencies run campaigns. Dealix builds an operating system that outlives the contract: flows, dashboards, approvals, proof reports. Not a one-off campaign.

## Do you use AI on our data?

Not by default. Default mode is deterministic. Enabling LLM-assist requires your written opt-in, and every call is logged with provider + model + prompt version + review_status.

## Do you send messages on our behalf automatically?

Never. Every outbound message goes through human review (Dealix founder + your commercial owner). This is enforced in code (`tests/test_no_auto_send.py`).

## Where does our data live?

In your systems. Dealix processes copies within the scope of the SOW only, in a region of your choice (KSA by default).

## What is the contract length?

- Diagnostic Sprint: 7 days.
- Managed retainer: month-to-month with 30-day notice.
- Enterprise project: master agreement + SOWs per milestone.

## How much do you cost?

- Diagnostic Sprint: 499 SAR.
- Data Pack: 1,500 SAR.
- Managed Ops: 2,999 – 4,999 SAR/month.
- Custom Enterprise: 5,000 – 25,000 SAR/month.

Scope-dependent. No hidden fees.

## How do we measure success?

Every deliverable carries written acceptance criteria. The weekly report tracks: friction points resolved, deals closed, revenue converted, reputation lifted.

## What if we're not satisfied?

Full data return within 14 days of termination notice. Unused work in the active SOW is refunded per the SOW terms.

## Are you SOC 2 / ISO 27001 certified?

We hold operational scaffolds aligned to SOC 2 Type I. Formal certification is a roadmap item and not a claim today. We disclose this plainly.

## Who owns the IP we build together?

Your IP, your delivered systems. Dealix retains the right to reuse the underlying templates and tooling that are part of the Dealix product.
