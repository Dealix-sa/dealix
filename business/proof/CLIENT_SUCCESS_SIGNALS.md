# Client Success Signals

Quantitative and qualitative indicators that a customer is healthy or at risk.

## Healthy
- Weekly review attended without escalation.
- Approval SLA met (<= 2 business days response).
- Customer-driven new feature requests.
- Renewal conversation initiated by the customer.
- Referrals offered.

## At risk
- 2+ skipped weekly reviews.
- Approval SLA missed.
- Customer says "we'll think about it" on basic deliverables.
- Customer asks for discount without scope change.
- Customer's commercial owner changes mid-engagement.

## How we respond
- Healthy → expansion conversation (see `RETAINER_EXPANSION_PLAYBOOK.md`).
- At risk → run `generate_retention_risk_report.py`; founder personal call within 48h.

## What we measure
- Time-to-first-value (days).
- Number of approvals per week.
- Number of friction items resolved per month.
- Net Promoter Score at month 3 and month 6.
