# Retainer Expansion Playbook

## When to start the conversation
Month 2 weekly review, after two healthy proof items are in the vault.

## Expansion paths
1. **Adjacent OS module**: customer on Revenue OS → add Review OS or Delivery OS.
2. **Tier up**: Managed Ops basic → Managed Ops pro.
3. **Headcount**: same module, expanded to another business unit.
4. **Custom**: ad-hoc engagement that becomes a new SOW.

## Anti-patterns
- Don't pitch expansion before the first proof item.
- Don't bundle expansion into a discount discussion.
- Don't expand into a scope where the customer can't dedicate a commercial owner.

## Script
"You've seen Revenue OS produce X. The natural next layer is Review OS — same operating rhythm, same proof cadence, focuses on protecting your reputation while we scale outbound. Want to scope it for next quarter?"

## Output
- New SOW from `STATEMENT_OF_WORK_TEMPLATE_*.md`.
- Logged in `deals.ledger.json` with `parentDealId`.
