# Red Flags and Disqualification

## Red Flags
1. **No budget clarity after two conversations.**
2. **Asks for free work as a 'test'.**
3. **Decision maker refuses to join discovery.**
4. **Unrealistic timeline (<7 days for full OS).**
5. **Previous vendor fired for non-payment.**

## Disqualification Criteria
- No confirmed pain.
- No budget access in 90 days.
- Ethical mismatch (requests fake reviews, spam, etc.).
- Legal risk (unlicensed activity, sanctions).n

## Process
1. Flag in CRM.
2. Notify founder if disqualifying.
3. Archive politely: "We may not be the right fit right now. Let's reconnect in 6 months."
