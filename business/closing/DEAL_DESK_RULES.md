# Deal Desk Rules

## Purpose
Ensure every proposal is profitable, deliverable, and aligned with positioning.

## Checks Before Proposal
- [ ] Pain confirmed in discovery notes
- [ ] Budget range discussed
- [ ] Decision process mapped
- [ ] Timeline is realistic
- [ ] Proof plan included

## Checks Before Signature
- [ ] Legal review if >50k SAR
- [ ] Delivery team capacity confirmed
- [ ] Payment terms agreed
- [ ] Exit clause included

## Roles
- **Account Lead**: Discovery, proposal, negotiation
- **Delivery Lead**: Scope feasibility, timeline
- **Founder**: Approval for custom pricing or >50k SAR
