# Discovery Call Script — English

## Introduction (2 minutes)
"Thank you for your time. This is not a sales call. Our goal is to understand your biggest operational pain right now."

## Questions
1. What is the biggest problem draining your team's time today?
2. How do you track performance? Do you have one clear number?
3. What was the last thing you tried to improve that did not work?
4. What does success look like in 90 days?
5. Who owns the budget? What is the approval process?

## Listening
- Write everything down.
- Do not defend.
- Do not sell.

## Close
"Thank you. We will send you a diagnostic summary within 24 hours. If it makes sense, we start a 7-day pilot."
