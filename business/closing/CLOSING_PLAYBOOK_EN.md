# Closing Playbook — English

## Principles
- We do not sell the product. We sell the measurable outcome.
- Every proposal must include a proof plan.
- We never promise a specific ROI.

## Closing Stages
1. **Discovery**: 20 minutes. Listen only.
2. **Proposal**: Customized based on confirmed pain.
3. **Proof**: 7-day pilot or diagnostic report.
4. **Negotiation**: Full pricing transparency.
5. **Signature**: Clear contract with easy exit.

## Handling Hesitation
- "We need time to think" → Schedule follow-up within 48 hours.
- "Price is high" → Offer pilot or phase-one option.
- "We have another vendor" → Offer free diagnostic review to compare.

## Prohibitions
- No guaranteed ROI promises.
- No unethical pressure.
- No automated sending.
