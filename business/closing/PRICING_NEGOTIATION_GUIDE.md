# Pricing Negotiation Guide

## Rules
1. **Never discount without reducing scope.**
2. **Always offer a pilot instead of a discount.**
3. **Show the cost of inaction in SAR per month.**

## Tactics
- Anchor high, justify with proof plan.
- Offer payment terms, not price cuts.
- Use phased delivery to reduce initial commitment.

## Scripts
- "We can reduce the first phase to fit your budget. The full scope delivers the full outcome."
- "Instead of a discount, let's run a 7-day pilot. If you see value, we proceed at full scope."

## Approval
- Any discount >10% requires founder approval.
- All pricing changes logged in CRM.
