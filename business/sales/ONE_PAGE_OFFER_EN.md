Dealix is a Saudi company that builds AI operating systems for B2B companies. We do not sell tools. We build operating systems that work daily in your company — with human approval before anything goes out.

Your team sells and delivers every day, but you cannot see the full picture. Conversations are scattered. Follow-ups are lost. Proposals sit unanswered. And as a founder, you make decisions on gut feeling, not data.

## The Solution

8 operating systems, each solving a specific problem:

3. **WhatsApp/Inbox Follow-up OS** — disciplined follow-up, AI drafting, human approval
4. **Email Outreach Review OS** — email that reaches the inbox, full authentication, unsubscribe compliant
5. **SMS Notification/Follow-up OS** — consent-based SMS, STOP/opt-out, every message logged
6. **AI Trust & Compliance OS** — AI governance, PDPL compliance, audit trail
7. **Client Delivery OS** — disciplined delivery, proof of work, professional handoff
8. **Controlled Live Outbound OS** — controlled outbound, verified targets, human approval, no spam
