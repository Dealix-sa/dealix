# Service Ladder (English)

## Philosophy

The Dealix service ladder is built on one principle: start small, prove value, then expand. We do not sell a full operating system on day one. We start with a diagnostic, then a sprint, then a full build, then a monthly retainer. Each stage proves value before we move to the next.

## Stages

### Stage 1: Diagnostic

- Duration: 20–30 minute call + report within 2–3 days
- Price: Free or SAR 0–1,500
- Deliverables:
  - Quick gap report (where opportunities are being lost)
  - Recommendation for the first OS that fits the company
  - Priority map (what solves the biggest pain first)
- Goal: Understand the situation, build trust, determine fit
- No commitment after. The client decides.

### Stage 2: Sprint (7-Day)

- Duration: 7 days
- Price: SAR 5,000–14,000 (depending on product)
- Deliverables:
  - First version of the OS (MVP)
  - Dashboard or follow-up queue or initial system
  - Proof-of-work report (what was built, what changed)
  - Team training session
- Goal: Prove the system works in the client's real environment
- Decision after: Expand to 14 or 30-day build, or stop

### Stage 3: Full Build (14-Day or 30-Day)

- Duration: 14 or 30 days
- Price: SAR 8,000–35,000 (depending on product and duration)
- Deliverables:
  - Complete operating system (all features in the 14 or 30 day scope)
  - Integration with client data sources
  - Full playbook
  - Team training (live + recorded)
  - Monthly proof pack
  - Handoff documentation
- Goal: System is fully operational and used daily
- Decision after: Monthly retainer or full handoff

### Stage 4: Retainer (Monthly)

- Duration: Monthly, ongoing
- Price: SAR 2,500–18,000/month (depending on product and scope)
- Deliverables:
  - Ongoing system management and updates
  - Periodic improvements based on data
  - Monthly report (KPIs, what improved, what needs work)
  - Technical support
  - Monthly proof pack
- Goal: Maintain the system, improve it, ensure usage
- Cancellation: 30-day notice

## Upgrade Rules

- No upgrade without a proof pack from the previous stage
- No upgrade without explicit client approval
- Upgrades calculate the difference between what was paid and what is due (no double payment)
- Discount does not exceed 10% on setup in the first 30 days
- Every discount requires a documented Change Request

## Notes

- All prices in Saudi Riyal and include VAT where applicable
- Provider costs (WhatsApp API, SMS gateway, email sending) are billed to client separately
- We do not guarantee revenue, ROI, or specific conversion rates
- Client pays 50% on signing and 50% on Day 14 for builds, or 100% upfront for sprints
- Monthly retainer is paid upfront on the first day of the month
