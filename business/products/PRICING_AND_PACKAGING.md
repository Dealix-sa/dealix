# Pricing and Packaging

## Principle

- Full transparency: setup + monthly are explicit
- No hidden onboarding fees
- No silent auto-renewal
- 14-day refund on setup
- 30-day cancellation on monthly retainer
- All prices in SAR, include VAT where applicable
- Provider costs (WhatsApp API, SMS gateway, email sending) billed to client separately

## Packages

### Diagnostic (Day 0)

- Duration: 20–30 min call + 2–3 day report
- Price: SAR 0–2,000 (free or nominal)
- Includes: gap report, OS recommendation, priority map
- Excludes: any build, any implementation, any ongoing support
- Purpose: determine fit and first OS to deploy

### 7-Day Sprint

- Duration: 7 days
- Price: SAR 4,000–14,000 (depending on product)
- Payment: 100% upfront
- Includes: MVP of the OS, initial dashboard/queue, proof-of-work report, team training session
- Excludes: full feature set, integrations beyond scope, ongoing management
- Purpose: prove the system works in the client's real environment
- Refund: 50% refund if cancelled before Day 3

### 14-Day Build

- Duration: 14 days
- Price: SAR 7,000–22,000 (depending on product)
- Payment: 50% on signing, 50% on Day 7
- Includes: sprint deliverables + reply classification, risk signals/cadence engine, playbook v1
- Excludes: full 30-day features, ongoing management, training beyond one session
- Purpose: expand the system to be operationally useful
- Refund: 14-day refund on setup portion if cancelled before Day 7

### 30-Day Build (Managed OS)

- Duration: 30 days
- Price: SAR 10,000–35,000 (depending on product)
- Payment: 50% on signing, 50% on Day 14
- Includes: full OS build, integrations, full playbook, training (live + recorded), proof pack, handoff document
- Excludes: ongoing management after Day 30 (that is the retainer)
- Purpose: full operational system used daily by the team
- Refund: 14-day refund on setup portion if cancelled before Day 14

### Monthly Retainer

- Duration: monthly, ongoing
- Price: SAR 2,500–18,000/month (depending on product and scope)
- Payment: upfront on first day of month
- Includes: system management, updates, improvements, monthly report, proof pack, technical support
- Excludes: new OS builds (priced separately), major scope changes (Change Request required)
- Cancellation: 30-day notice, no penalty
- Purpose: maintain, improve, and ensure usage of the deployed OS

## Bundling Logic

### Bundle: Revenue Stack

- Revenue Command Room OS + WhatsApp/Inbox Follow-up OS
- Bundle discount: 10% on combined setup
- Rationale: pipeline visibility + follow-up execution work together

### Bundle: Outbound Stack

- Controlled Live Outbound OS + Email Outreach Review OS + AI Trust & Compliance OS
- Bundle discount: 10% on combined setup
- Rationale: outbound requires email infrastructure and compliance layer

### Bundle: Knowledge Stack

- Company Brain OS + AI Trust & Compliance OS
- Bundle discount: 10% on combined setup
- Rationale: brain requires compliance governance

### Bundle: Full Company OS

- Any 4+ products in a single engagement
- Bundle discount: 15% on combined setup
- Rationale: full transformation warrants deeper discount
- Requires: 30-day build minimum, retainer recommended

### Bundle: Delivery + Revenue

- Client Delivery OS + Revenue Command Room OS
- Bundle discount: 10% on combined setup
- Rationale: delivery visibility + revenue visibility = full operational picture

## Discount Policy

- Maximum 10% discount on setup in the first 30 days
- Maximum 15% discount on monthly retainer after 6 months of continuous engagement
- Every discount requires a documented Change Request with reason
- No "friend" discounts, no "well-known company" discounts, no undocumented discounts
- Bundling discounts are applied automatically per the bundling logic above
- Discounts do not stack (a client gets the highest applicable discount, not the sum)

## Payment Terms

### Setup / Build

- 7-Day Sprint: 100% upfront on signing
- 14-Day Build: 50% on signing, 50% on Day 7
- 30-Day Build: 50% on signing, 50% on Day 14
- Diagnostic: free or invoiced on completion if nominal

### Retainer

- Paid upfront on the first day of each month
- First month prorated if starting mid-month
- 30-day cancellation notice required, no penalty
- No silent auto-renewal — explicit renewal required

### Enterprise / Custom

- Bespoke terms, separate invoice
- Milestone-based payments available
- May require separate MSA (Master Services Agreement)
- Payment terms negotiable (net-15, net-30 for established enterprises)

## What Quotes Never Include

- Guaranteed ROI numbers
- Guaranteed revenue increases
- Guaranteed conversion rates
- Fake client testimonials
- Fake case studies
- Undocumented discounts
- Unrealistic time promises
- Hidden onboarding fees

## Quote Validity

- Quotes are valid for 14 days from issue date
- After 14 days: setup and monthly pricing must be re-validated before issuing a new quote
- No automatic extension
