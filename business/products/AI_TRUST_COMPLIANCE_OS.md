# AI Trust Compliance OS

## Product Definition

A governance and compliance layer for Saudi B2B companies using AI in their operations. The AI Trust Compliance OS ensures every AI action has an audit trail, human approval log, data classification, access control, and policy enforcement so the company can prove it controls its AI, not the other way around.

## Problem

Companies adopt AI tools but cannot prove to clients, regulators, or their own board that AI is governed. There is no audit trail, no approval log, no data classification, and no policy enforcement. Trust erodes when AI acts without evidence of control.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies using AI in sales, operations, or customer-facing workflows
- Companies that need to prove AI governance to enterprise clients or regulators
- Founders who want to build trust with clients before AI scales
- Organizations handling sensitive client or employee data with AI

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies not using AI in any business process
- Teams that already have a mature AI governance framework with full audit trails
- Companies unwilling to enforce human approval for AI actions
- Organizations that do not handle any sensitive data

## Business Outcome

A governed, auditable AI operation where every AI action has an approval log, data classification, access control, and policy enforcement so the company can prove it controls its AI to clients, regulators, and the board.

## Data Required

- List of AI tools and workflows in use
- Data classification policy (if any)
- Access control matrix
- Existing approval workflows
- Client or regulatory compliance requirements
- List of AI actions that need governance

## Workflow

1. Intake: audit all AI tools and workflows in use
2. Classify data touched by AI
3. Map approval requirements per AI action
4. Build audit trail and logging layer
5. Configure access controls and policy enforcement
6. Set up compliance reporting
7. Weekly governance review
