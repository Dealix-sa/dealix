# Offer Intelligence OS

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted system that analyzes a Saudi B2B company's offers, pricing, packaging, and competitive positioning to identify gaps, weaknesses, and improvement opportunities. The Offer Intelligence OS ensures the company's offers are sharp, defensible, and aligned with what the market actually wants.

## Problem

Companies build offers in a vacuum, price based on guesswork, and cannot defend their pricing when clients push back. Offers are not compared to competitors, packaging is unclear, and the founder cannot answer "why this price?" with evidence.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies with 2+ active offers
- Founders who cannot defend their pricing with evidence
- Companies that have not reviewed their offers in 6+ months
- Teams that want to identify pricing and packaging gaps

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies with no active offers
- Teams that already have a mature offer and pricing intelligence system
- Companies unwilling to share pricing and competitive data
- Organizations that do not want to review or change offers

## Business Outcome

A structured offer analysis with pricing gaps, packaging weaknesses, competitive positioning, and improvement recommendations, so the company can defend and improve its offers with evidence.

## Data Required

- Current offers, pricing, and packaging
- Competitor offers and pricing (if known)
- Past proposal win/loss data
- Client feedback on pricing and packaging
- Sales team pricing objections
- Value proposition per offer

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect offers, pricing, and competitive data
2. Analyze offer structure and clarity
3. Compare pricing to competitors and value delivered
4. Identify packaging gaps and weaknesses
5. Generate improvement recommendations
6. Human review and prioritization
7. Monthly offer review

## Objection Handling

- "We know our offers well." — Knowing is not the same as having evidence. We add structured analysis, competitive comparison, and defensible recommendations.
- "We cannot share competitor data." — We work with what you know and what is public. We start with available data and build up.
- "AI does not understand our market." — AI structures the analysis. You validate with market knowledge. Human approval is required for every recommendation.
- "We do not want to change pricing." — This system identifies gaps. Whether you change is your decision. At least you will know where you stand.
