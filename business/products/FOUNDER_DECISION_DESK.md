# Founder Decision Desk

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted decision support system for Saudi B2B founders that captures, structures, and tracks every key business decision with context, alternatives considered, expected outcome, and post-decision review. The Founder Decision Desk ensures decisions are intentional, logged, and reviewable so the founder can learn from patterns and avoid repeating mistakes.

## Problem

Founders make dozens of decisions daily with no record of context, alternatives, or expected outcomes. When decisions fail, there is no way to review what was considered. The same mistakes repeat because there is no decision log or pattern analysis.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B founders making 10+ key decisions per week
- Founders who want to improve decision quality over time
- Companies where the founder is the sole decision-maker and wants a system
- Teams that want to preserve decision context for future leaders

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Founders who do not want to log or review decisions
- Companies with a mature decision logging and review process
- Teams that want fully automated decision-making (this is human-first)
- Organizations unwilling to assign time for post-decision reviews

## Business Outcome

A structured decision log with context, alternatives, expected outcomes, and post-decision reviews, so the founder can see decision patterns, learn from outcomes, and make better decisions over time.

## Data Required

- List of recent key decisions and their context
- Business goals and priorities
- Decision categories (hiring, pricing, product, partnership, etc.)
- Past decision outcomes if available
- Founder's decision-making style and preferences

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect recent decisions and business goals
2. Build decision log template with context and alternatives
3. Configure decision categories
4. Log new decisions with expected outcomes
5. Schedule post-decision reviews
6. Run pattern analysis on logged decisions
7. Monthly decision review session

## AI Role

- Structure decision context and alternatives
- Draft expected outcome statements
- Detect decision patterns and biases
- Generate monthly decision review report
- Suggest decision framework improvements

## Human Approval Role

## Objection Handling

- "I do not have time to log decisions." — The system drafts the log entry from a quick voice note or message. You review and approve in under a minute.
- "We already have meeting notes." — Meeting notes are not a decision log. We add structure: context, alternatives, expected outcome, and post-decision review.
- "AI cannot make decisions for me." — Correct. This system does not make decisions. It logs, structures, and reviews them so you make better ones.
- "Our decisions are too sensitive to log." — The log is private to you and can run in your environment. You control access.
