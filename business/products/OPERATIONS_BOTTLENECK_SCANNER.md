# Operations Bottleneck Scanner

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted system that scans a Saudi B2B company's operational workflows to detect, classify, and prioritize bottlenecks across sales, delivery, finance, and communication. The Operations Bottleneck Scanner ensures the founder knows exactly where the business is slowing down and why.

## Problem

Companies lose time and money because bottlenecks are invisible. The founder feels the slowdown but cannot pinpoint where it is, why it happens, or how to fix it. Decisions are made on gut feeling instead of evidence.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies with multi-step operational workflows
- Founders who feel the business is slow but cannot pinpoint where
- Companies with delivery, sales, or finance bottlenecks they want to identify
- Teams that want evidence-based bottleneck resolution

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies with very simple, single-step operations
- Teams that already have a mature operational monitoring system
- Companies unwilling to share operational data
- Organizations that do not want to act on bottleneck findings

## Business Outcome

A structured bottleneck report that identifies, classifies, and prioritizes operational bottlenecks across the business, with evidence and recommendations, so the founder can fix what is actually slow.

## Data Required

- Operational workflow documentation (sales, delivery, finance, comms)
- Cycle time data per workflow step (if available)
- Team member assignments and handoff points
- Known slowdown areas
- Past bottleneck incidents
- Tool and system inventory

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect workflow documentation and cycle data
2. Map operational workflows end-to-end
3. Detect slowdown points and handoff delays
4. Classify and prioritize bottlenecks by impact
5. Generate bottleneck report with recommendations
6. Human review and action planning
7. Monthly bottleneck review

## Objection Handling

- "We already know our bottlenecks." — Knowing is not the same as having evidence and prioritization. We add structured detection, classification, and resolution recommendations.
- "We do not have workflow data." — We start with what you know and map from interviews. Data makes it sharper, but we can begin with documentation sessions.
- "AI does not understand our operations." — AI structures the analysis. You validate with operational knowledge. Human approval is required for every finding.
- "We do not have time to fix bottlenecks." — Knowing where the pain is helps you prioritize. Even one resolved bottleneck saves time and money.
