# WhatsApp Inbox Followup OS

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted WhatsApp inbox system that captures, classifies, drafts replies, and tracks follow-ups for Saudi B2B companies using WhatsApp as their primary sales and support channel. Every inbound message is logged, prioritized, and answered with human-approved drafts so no conversation is lost.

## Problem

Sales and support teams run on WhatsApp, but inboxes overflow, follow-ups are missed, there is no shared visibility, and the founder cannot see who replied, who is waiting, and who is at risk. Conversations die in personal phones.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies using WhatsApp for 20+ daily sales or support conversations
- Founders or sales teams losing track of replies and follow-ups
- Companies that need shared inbox visibility across a team
- Teams that want draft replies approved before sending

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies not using WhatsApp for business
- Teams with under 5 daily conversations where a personal phone is enough
- Companies that already have a mature WhatsApp Business API setup with full team adoption
- Organizations unwilling to require human approval before sending

## Business Outcome

A shared, tracked WhatsApp inbox where every message is logged, classified, prioritized, and answered with human-approved drafts, so no conversation is lost and the founder has full visibility.

## Data Required

- WhatsApp chat history (export or API)
- Contact list and labels
- Sales script or FAQ document
- Team member assignments
- Existing follow-up rules or SLAs

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect WhatsApp history and contact labels
2. Connect WhatsApp inbox (API or managed export)
3. Classify inbound messages (lead, support, follow-up, risk)
4. Generate priority queue
5. Draft replies for human approval
6. Track follow-up status and SLA
7. Daily inbox report

## AI Role

- Classify and prioritize inbound messages
- Draft replies based on scripts and past conversations
- Detect at-risk or stalled conversations
- Generate daily inbox summary
- Suggest follow-up timing

## Human Approval Role

- Approve every reply before send
- Escalate or reclassify messages
- Decide on at-risk conversation recovery
- Review daily inbox report

## Objection Handling

- "We already use WhatsApp Business." — WhatsApp Business is a tool, not an operating system. We add classification, priority, follow-up tracking, and human-approved drafts so nothing is lost.
- "Our volume is too low." — If you have under 5 daily conversations, a personal inbox may be enough. Start with the AI Revenue Diagnostic.
- "AI might send wrong replies." — Every reply is human-approved before send. AI drafts, humans decide.
- "We cannot connect WhatsApp API." — We can work with managed exports first, then upgrade to API when you are ready.
