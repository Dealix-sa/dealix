# Authorized Sales Agent OS

## Product definition

Authorized Sales Agent OS is a Dealix operating system that helps a company understand buyers, diagnose pain, prepare sales conversations, respond to objections, and negotiate next steps using an approved company voice.

It is not a spam bot. It is not an identity-masking tool. It is a controlled sales operating layer.

## Problem

Many Saudi B2B companies have sales opportunities but no consistent system for:

- identifying customer pain;
- adapting the message by sector;
- answering objections;
- negotiating scope and price;
- logging next actions;
- maintaining a consistent sales voice.

## Ideal customer

- B2B services companies.
- Clinics with high inquiry volume.
- Real estate teams.
- Training centers.
- Logistics and operations businesses.
- Agencies that need a managed sales desk.
- Founder-led companies where follow-up depends on one person.

## Business outcome

Create a repeatable sales operating system that prepares strong, company-specific drafts and decision guidance so a human or authorized representative can act faster and more consistently.

## Data required

- Company profile.
- Offer catalog.
- Approved tone and voice.
- Allowed sender names and titles.
- Target list with source URLs.
- Objection history.
- Pricing guardrails.
- Approval policy.

## Workflow

1. Build company voice profile.
2. Load offer and pricing guardrails.
3. Analyze target company pain signals.
4. Recommend offer fit.
5. Generate draft message and negotiation path.
6. Run policy and claim checks.
7. Send to founder/client review queue.
8. Log approved next action.
9. Classify replies.
10. Prepare proposal or follow-up.

## AI role

- Pain signal extraction.
- Message adaptation by sector.
- Objection mapping.
- Negotiation script drafting.
- Reply classification.
- Proposal brief preparation.

## Human approval role

- Approve identity and sender title.
- Approve claim accuracy.
- Approve external-facing drafts.
- Decide whether to progress, hold, or discard.

- 10 reviewable sales drafts.
- Objection bank.
- Negotiation playbook.

### 14 days

- Reply classification workflow.
- Proposal brief workflow.

### 30 days

- Weekly performance review.
- Offer optimization report.
- Retainer plan.

## Proof pack

- Number of verified targets.
- Number of drafts approved.
- Reply themes.
- Objections observed.
- Meetings or proposals moved forward.
- Next 30-day recommendation.

## Pricing range in SAR

- Diagnostic: 1,500–3,000 SAR.
- Sales Agent Sprint: 7,500–18,000 SAR.
- Managed Sales OS Retainer: 5,000–25,000 SAR/month.

## Risks

- Misrepresentation if identity is not authorized.
- Deliverability damage if used as bulk automation.
- Reputation risk from unsupported claims.
- Buyer distrust if messages are too generic.

## Acceptance criteria

- Every target has a source URL.
- Every pain point is labeled as hypothesis or verified fact.
- Every external-facing draft has approval status.
- No fake ROI, fake testimonials, or unauthorized identity.
- Authorized representative mode requires explicit authorization.
