# Revenue Command Room OS

## Problem

Saudi B2B companies lose revenue because leads, WhatsApp follow-up, proposals, and owner decisions are scattered.

## Outcome

A daily command room that shows what to follow up, who owns it, what proposal is next, and what proof exists.

## First offer

7-Day Revenue Command Room Sprint.

## Deliverables

- Lead ledger.
- Follow-up queue.
- Proposal queue.
- Daily founder actions.
- Weekly proof pack.

## AI role

Generate drafts, classify urgency, suggest next actions, and summarize risks.

## Human role

Review all drafts and manually send. No auto-send.

## Pricing range

5,000–12,000 SAR for the 7-day sprint.

## Acceptance criteria

- Every lead has owner and next action.
- Every external message remains draft-only.
- Weekly proof pack is generated.
