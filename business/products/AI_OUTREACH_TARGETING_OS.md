# AI Outreach Targeting OS

## Product Definition

An AI-powered outreach targeting system that researches, scores, and prioritizes Saudi B2B prospects based on firmographics, pain signals, and fit signals, then drafts personalized first-touch messages for human approval. The system ensures outreach reaches the right company, the right person, with the right message, under full human control.

## Problem

Outreach is wasted on the wrong targets. Sales teams manually research prospects, guess fit, send generic messages, and burn their reputation on low-quality outreach. There is no systematic way to score and prioritize who to contact first.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies running outbound outreach to 50+ prospects
- Founders or sales teams who want to stop guessing and start targeting with evidence
- Companies with a defined ICP but no systematic prospect scoring
- Teams that want personalized first-touch drafts without manual research

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies with no outbound sales motion
- Teams that already have a mature, data-driven targeting system with full adoption
- Companies unwilling to verify targets before outreach
- Organizations that send generic blast messages and do not care about quality

## Business Outcome

A scored, prioritized prospect list with personalized first-touch message drafts, under human approval, so outreach reaches the right companies with the right message and reputation is protected.

## Data Required

- Ideal Customer Profile definition (industry, size, location, pain)
- Existing prospect list (if any)
- Product or service value proposition
- Past outreach history and response rates
- Any available firmographic or intent data sources

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect ICP and value proposition
2. Research and score prospects against ICP
3. Prioritize prospect list by fit and signal
4. Draft personalized first-touch messages
5. Human approval of targets and messages
6. Log approved targets and messages
7. Weekly targeting quality review

## AI Role

- Research and score prospects against ICP
- Detect pain and fit signals from public data
- Prioritize prospect list
- Draft personalized first-touch messages
- Suggest targeting improvements based on response data

## Human Approval Role

- Approve every target before outreach
- Approve every first-touch message before send
- Decide on targeting rule changes
- Review weekly targeting quality report

## Objection Handling

- "We already have a prospect list." — A list is not a scored list. We add fit and signal scoring, prioritization, and personalized drafts so you reach the right company first.
- "We do not have enough data on prospects." — We research from public sources and your ICP. We start with what you have and build up.
- "AI might target the wrong companies." — Every target is human-approved before outreach. AI proposes, humans decide.
- "We do not want personalized messages, we just blast." — This system is not for blasting. If you want quality outreach with reputation protection, this is for you.
