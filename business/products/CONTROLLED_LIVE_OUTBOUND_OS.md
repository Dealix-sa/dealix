# Controlled Live Outbound OS

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted outbound system that operates email, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn outreach for Saudi B2B companies under strict human approval, verification, opt-out, rate-limit, and logging gates. The system automates the operating workflow, not spam, ensuring every outbound message is approved, verified, and logged before it goes out.

## Problem

Companies want to automate outreach but risk reputation, compliance, and deliverability by sending without controls. Generic blasts damage domain reputation, WhatsApp numbers get banned, and there is no audit trail for what was sent, to whom, and why.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies running outbound across email, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn
- Founders or sales teams who want to scale outreach without losing control
- Companies that need an audit trail for every outbound message
- Teams that want to protect domain and WhatsApp reputation

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies that want to blast without human approval
- Teams that do not want to verify targets before outreach
- Companies with no outbound sales motion
- Organizations unwilling to enforce opt-out and suppression rules

## Business Outcome

A controlled, audited outbound operation where every message is human-approved, verified, opt-out compliant, rate-limited, and logged, so outreach scales without damaging reputation.

## Data Required

- Approved prospect list with verified contact info
- Message templates per channel
- Suppression and opt-out list
- DNS and authentication config (email)
- WhatsApp Business API access (if WhatsApp live)
- Rate limit preferences per channel

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect prospect list, templates, and suppression rules
2. Verify targets and source URLs
3. Load message templates for human approval
4. Configure rate limits and opt-out enforcement
5. Send approved messages in controlled batches
6. Log every send with audit trail
7. Track replies and opt-outs
8. Weekly outbound review

## AI Role

- Verify targets and flag unverified prospects
- Draft personalized messages from templates
- Detect opt-out requests and update suppression list
- Generate weekly outbound performance report
- Suggest message improvements based on reply data

## Human Approval Role

- Approve every message before send
- Approve target list before each batch
- Review weekly outbound report
- Decide on rate limit and channel changes
- Resolve flagged compliance issues

## Objection Handling

- "We already use an outreach tool." — Most tools do not enforce human approval, source verification, opt-out, and rate limits together. We add the control layer that protects your reputation.
- "We want to send more volume." — Volume without control damages reputation. We start controlled, then scale safely as trust builds.
- "AI might send wrong messages." — Every message is human-approved before send. AI drafts, humans decide.
- "We do not want limits." — Limits protect your domain and WhatsApp number. We can increase them gradually as reputation builds.
