# Board Memo OS

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted system that generates structured board memos for Saudi B2B companies, summarizing performance, decisions, risks, pipeline, financials, and strategic priorities in a format ready for board members. The Board Memo OS ensures the founder never scrambles before a board meeting again.

## Problem

Board memos are written at the last minute, in inconsistent formats, with missing data and no evidence. The founder spends hours assembling slides and notes instead of focusing on strategy. Board members get incomplete information and cannot make informed decisions.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies with a board or advisory group
- Founders who produce board memos quarterly or monthly
- Companies that need structured, evidence-backed board reporting
- Teams that want to standardize board communication

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies without a board or advisory group
- Teams that already have a mature board reporting system
- Companies unwilling to share performance and decision data
- Organizations that do not hold formal board meetings

## Business Outcome

A structured, evidence-backed board memo generated on schedule, covering performance, decisions, risks, pipeline, financials, and strategy, so the founder walks into board meetings prepared and confident.

## Data Required

- Performance metrics and KPIs
- Decision log entries
- Risk register
- Pipeline and revenue data
- Financial summary
- Strategic priorities and goals
- Previous board memos (if available)
- Board meeting cadence

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect performance, decision, risk, and financial data
2. Build board memo template with standard sections
3. Generate memo draft from operational data
4. Human review and approval of each section
5. Format for board distribution
6. Deliver memo on schedule
7. Post-meeting action tracking

## AI Role

- Generate memo sections from operational data
- Draft executive summary
- Detect missing data and flag gaps
- Summarize decisions and risks
- Suggest strategic priorities based on trends

## Human Approval Role

- Approve every section before inclusion
- Approve executive summary
- Decide on strategic framing
- Review post-meeting actions
- Approve final memo before distribution

## Objection Handling

- "We do not have a formal board." — If you have an advisory group or investors, this system helps. If not, start with the Founder Diagnostic.
- "We already have a memo template." — A template is not a generator. We add AI drafting from operational data, gap detection, and action tracking.
- "AI might misrepresent our performance." — Every section is human-approved. AI drafts, you verify and frame.
- "Our data is too scattered." — That is what this system solves. We gather and structure from whatever sources you have.
