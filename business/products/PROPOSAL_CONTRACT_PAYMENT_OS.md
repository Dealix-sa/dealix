# Proposal Contract Payment OS

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted system that manages the flow from proposal drafting to contract generation to payment collection for Saudi B2B companies. The Proposal Contract Payment OS ensures every proposal is structured, every contract is reviewed, and every payment is tracked, with human approval at each stage.

## Problem

Companies draft proposals manually, contracts are copy-pasted from old templates with errors, and payment tracking is scattered across WhatsApp and email. The founder loses deals because proposals take too long, contracts have mistakes, and payments are not followed up.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies sending 5+ proposals per month
- Founders who are the bottleneck in proposal and contract creation
- Teams that need structured proposal templates and contract review
- Companies that struggle with payment follow-up and tracking

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies with under 5 proposals per month where manual works fine
- Teams that already have a mature proposal-to-payment system with full adoption
- Companies unwilling to enforce human review of contracts
- Organizations that do not want structured proposal templates

## Business Outcome

A governed proposal-to-payment flow where every proposal is structured, every contract is reviewed, and every payment is tracked, so deals close faster and cash flow is visible.

## Data Required

- Existing proposal templates and samples
- Contract templates and legal requirements
- Pricing and packaging structure
- Payment terms and methods (Moyasar, bank transfer, etc.)
- Client list and active proposals
- Past proposal win/loss data if available

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect proposal templates, contracts, and pricing
2. Build structured proposal generator
3. Configure contract review workflow
4. Set up payment tracking and follow-up
5. Generate proposal drafts for human approval
6. Track contract signing status
7. Track payment status and send follow-up reminders
8. Weekly proposal-to-payment report

## AI Role

- Draft proposals from structured inputs
- Flag contract risks and missing clauses
- Generate payment follow-up reminders
- Track proposal-to-payment pipeline
- Summarize weekly proposal performance

## Human Approval Role

- Approve every proposal before send

- Approve payment follow-up messages
- Review weekly proposal report
- Decide on pricing or term changes

## Objection Handling

- "We already use proposal software." — Software is not a flow. We add AI drafting, contract risk flagging, payment tracking, and human approval at each stage.
- "Our proposals are custom, not template-based." — We build structured generators that adapt to your custom approach while adding consistency.
- "AI might draft wrong proposals." — Every proposal is human-approved before send. AI drafts, humans decide.
- "We do not want payment automation." — We track and remind, not auto-charge. Human approval is required for every follow-up.
