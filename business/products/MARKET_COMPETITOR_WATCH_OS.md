# Market Competitor Watch OS

## Product Definition

An AI-assisted market and competitor monitoring system for Saudi B2B companies that tracks competitor moves, market signals, pricing changes, new product launches, and customer sentiment, and summarizes them into a weekly intelligence brief. The Market Competitor Watch OS ensures the company never gets blindsided by market shifts.

## Problem

Companies do not have time to monitor competitors and market signals systematically. The founder hears about a competitor move weeks late, misses pricing changes, and reacts to market shifts instead of anticipating them.

## Ideal Customer

- Saudi B2B companies in competitive markets with 3+ identifiable competitors
- Founders who want early warning on competitor and market moves
- Companies that need a weekly intelligence brief to stay ahead
- Teams that do not have time for systematic market monitoring

## Who Should Not Buy It

- Companies in markets with no identifiable competitors
- Teams that already have a mature competitive intelligence system
- Companies unwilling to define their competitor list
- Organizations that do not act on market intelligence

## Business Outcome

A weekly market and competitor intelligence brief that tracks moves, pricing, launches, and sentiment, so the founder can anticipate shifts instead of reacting late.

## Data Required

- Competitor list (names, websites, key offerings)
- Market definition (segment, geography, customer type)
- Past competitor moves or market shifts
- Key signals to track (pricing, product, hiring, sentiment)
- Source list (websites, social, news, review platforms)

## Workflow

1. Intake: collect competitor list and market definition
2. Configure signal tracking per competitor
3. Scan public sources for moves and changes
4. Classify and prioritize signals
5. Generate weekly intelligence brief
6. Human review and approval of brief
7. Monthly trend analysis

## AI Role

- Scan public sources for competitor and market signals
- Classify and prioritize signals
- Generate weekly intelligence brief
- Detect trends and patterns
- Flag high-priority moves for immediate alert

## Human Approval Role

- Approve competitor list and signal priorities
- Review and approve weekly brief before distribution
- Decide on response actions for high-priority moves
- Review monthly trend analysis

## Objection Handling

- "We know our competitors well." — Knowing is not monitoring. We add systematic signal tracking, weekly briefs, and early alerts so you never miss a move.
- "We do not have time to read intelligence." — The weekly brief is short and prioritized. High-priority alerts come separately so you act immediately.
- "AI might get the signals wrong." — Every brief is human-reviewed before distribution. AI scans, humans validate.
- "Our market is too small for this." — If you have 3+ competitors, monitoring helps. If you have fewer, start with the Founder Diagnostic.
