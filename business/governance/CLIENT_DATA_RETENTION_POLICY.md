# Client Data Retention Policy

## Principles
- Collect only what is necessary.
- Store encrypted at rest.
- Delete when no longer needed.

## Retention Periods
| Data Type | Retention | Reason |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Lead contact info | 2 years or until opt-out | Sales pipeline |
| Proposal documents | 7 years | Legal/compliance |
| Diagnostic reports | 3 years | Reference and improvement |
| Communication logs | 2 years | Dispute resolution |
| AI-generated drafts | 90 days | Reduce exposure |

## Deletion
- Annual audit of stale data.
- Client may request deletion via email.
- Deletion confirmed within 30 days.

## Compliance
- Aligned with Saudi PDPL.
- Data stays in-region where possible.
