# Master Service Agreement (MSA) — Outline

**Operational scaffold. Not legal advice.** Retain qualified counsel before signing.

## 1. Parties
Dealix SA ("Provider") and `[Customer legal name]` ("Customer").

## 2. Services
Provider will deliver business operating system services as detailed in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this MSA.

## 3. Term
Effective from the Effective Date until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Active SOWs survive termination through their stated end dates.

## 4. Fees & payment
- Fees as stated in each SOW.
- Net-30 unless otherwise agreed.
- VAT handled per KSA regulations through Customer's accounting system.
- Late payment: pro-rated late fee per applicable law.

## 5. Confidentiality
Each party will protect the other's confidential information with the same care it gives its own, for a period of 3 years after disclosure.

## 6. Data protection
- A separate Data Processing Addendum (DPA) governs personal data handling.
- Customer remains data controller; Provider is data processor for personal data processed under any SOW.
- Sub-processor list available on request.

## 7. Intellectual property
- Customer-supplied data: Customer's property.
- Deliverables specified in a SOW: Customer's property upon final payment.
- Underlying Provider tooling, frameworks, prompts, and templates: Provider retains.

## 8. Warranties
Provider warrants services will be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards. **NO** guarantee of business outcomes is made.

## 9. Limitation of liability
Aggregate liability of either party is capped at fees paid under the SOW in question over the 12 months preceding the claim.

## 10. Indemnity
Mutual indemnity for IP infringement and gross negligence as commonly stated in B2B service agreements.

## 11. Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach with 30 days notice and right to cure. Customer receives a data return within 14 days of termination.

## 12. Governing law
Saudi Arabia, with disputes resolved in Riyadh courts unless otherwise agreed.

## 13. Independent contractor
Provider is an independent contractor; this MSA does not create an employment relationship.

## 14. No automated outbound
Provider will not auto-send messages on Customer's behalf. All outbound is human-reviewed and approved by Customer's commercial owner before sending.
