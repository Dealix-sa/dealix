# Client responsibilities

A successful Dealix engagement requires the customer to bring four things.

## 1. A named commercial owner
- One person who can approve drafts, accept deliverables, and decide priorities.
- Time commitment: ~90 min/week for the weekly review + ad-hoc Slack/WhatsApp for draft approvals.
- This is not delegable to an assistant.

## 2. Access to the customer's existing tools
- CRM (read access at minimum, write for the Managed Ops tier).
- WhatsApp Business or shared inbox (for drafting; sending stays with the customer).
- Review console (Google Business Profile, Trustpilot, etc.).
- Reporting tool / spreadsheet of historical results.

## 3. Operational data
- Last 90 days of leads with outcome (closed-won / lost / no-response).
- Last 90 days of WhatsApp conversations (or a sample) with permission.
- Current pricing and offering catalog.

## 4. Willingness to change one thing per week
- Workflow improvements compound. One change per week, executed cleanly, beats five changes that don't stick.

## What Dealix does NOT need from the customer
- Customer's bank credentials.
- Customer's IdP master password.
- Customer's customer-facing social media credentials.
- Customer-employee performance reviews.

## What happens when the customer doesn't show up
- 2 missed weekly reviews → SOW pauses; clock stops.
- 4 missed weekly reviews → SOW terminates; final data return.
