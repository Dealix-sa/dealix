# Acceptance Criteria Template

Attached to each SOW. Defines when a deliverable is considered complete.

## Per deliverable

| # | Deliverable | Acceptance criteria | Reviewer |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Workflow map | Customer signs off on visualized lead → close flow | Commercial owner |
| 2 | Friction log | Top 5 frictions identified with revenue impact estimate | Commercial owner |
| 3 | Command Center setup | Dashboard loads on first attempt with live data | Operator |
| 4 | Approved persuasion angles (3) | Each angle reviewed and signed off in writing | Commercial owner |
| 5 | Outreach drafts (10 accounts) | Drafts reviewed; tone matches brand | Commercial owner |
| 6 | First proof report | Delivered on agreed day; metrics traceable | Commercial owner |
| 7 | 30-day retention review | Held within 30 days of go-live | Founder + Customer commercial |

## Default acceptance window
5 business days from delivery. Silence past the window = acceptance.

## Rejection path
- Customer notes specific gaps in writing.
- Provider has 5 business days to address.
- Re-review.
- If still rejected, escalate to MSA dispute path.

## What is NOT acceptance criteria
- "Hit X revenue target" — Provider does not guarantee outcomes.
- "Customer feels good about it" — must be objective and reviewable.
- "Looks nice" — design tweaks are change requests, not acceptance gates.
