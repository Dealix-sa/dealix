# Pricing Discount Policy

| Offer | Setup | Monthly |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Diagnostic Sprint | 499 | — |

| Managed Ops (basic) | 5,000 | 2,999 |
| Managed Ops (pro) | 10,000 | 4,999 |
| Custom Enterprise | per scope | 5,000–25,000 |

## Discount tiers

| Discount | Approver | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0–10% | Founder unilateral | Volume / multi-quarter prepay |
| 10–20% | Founder + commercial owner | Strategic logo, with case-study consent |
| 20–30% | Founder + customer board sign-off | Multi-year + multi-tenant pilot |
| >30% | **Forbidden** | Walk away or restructure scope |

## Strategic rationale required at any tier >10%

Write the rationale on the quote: which signal makes this discount worth it (logo, case study, partner channel, market reference). No "they asked for it" rationales.

## Prepay incentives (preferred to discounts)

- 3-month prepay → 1 month free.
- 12-month prepay → 2 months free + free Diagnostic Sprint refresh.

## Forbidden

- "Free first month" without contract commitment.
- "Pay only if results" — Dealix does not offer outcome-contingent pricing.
- Discount stacking (prepay + percent-off) without explicit deal desk approval.

## Audit

Every discount above 10% must be logged in the SOW with the strategic rationale and reviewer name.
