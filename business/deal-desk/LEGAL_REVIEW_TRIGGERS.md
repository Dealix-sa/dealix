# Legal Review Triggers

When Dealix engages outside legal counsel before signing.

## Always
- First engagement with a new enterprise (revenue > 100M SAR/year).
- Multi-tenant deals.
- Government / semi-government entities.
- Any deal with non-standard MSA terms.

## Often
- Indemnity clauses outside the standard MSA outline.
- Liability caps below 12-month fees.
- Data residency requirements outside KSA.
- Sub-processor restrictions.

## Sometimes
- IP assignment changes from default (customer owns delivered IP; Dealix retains tooling).
- Non-compete clauses asking Dealix to stop serving the customer's competitors.
- Marketing rights clauses.

## How
1. Founder flags the trigger in the quote review.
2. Quote is held at `pending_review` until counsel weighs in.
3. Counsel's redlines go back to the customer in writing.
4. Customer agrees → SOW signed. Customer disagrees → walk-away or restructure.

## Disclaimer
Dealix is not a law firm. Templates in `business/contracts/` are operational scaffolds. For any meaningful deviation from the templates, retain external counsel.
