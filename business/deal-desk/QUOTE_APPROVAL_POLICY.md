# Quote Approval Policy

## Quote lifecycle
1. **draft** — Generated by `scripts/generate_quote.py`. Not yet reviewed.
2. **pending_review** — Default state after generation.
3. **approved** — `scripts/approve_quote.py` with a reviewer name.
4. **sent** — `scripts/mark_quote_sent.py` records the manual send via customer channel.
5. **accepted** — Customer signed. Moves to `deals.ledger.json`.
6. **rejected** | **expired** — Closed; reason captured.

## SLAs
- Quote must reach `approved` within 24 business hours of `pending_review`.
- Sent quotes auto-expire 14 days after `sentAt` unless explicitly extended.

## Required fields
- `accountId`, `offer`, `setupPrice`, `monthlyPrice`, `scope`, `outOfScope`, `validUntil`.

## Banned in quotes
- The words "guaranteed", "نضمن", "100% ROI", "money-back if not satisfied", or anything that implies a result.
- Any line item without a price.
- Any scope that contradicts the offer's standard inclusions/exclusions.

## Reviewer responsibilities
- Compare setup/monthly against `business/pricing/PRICING_POWER_SYSTEM.md` floor.
- Check the scope wording for clarity.
- Verify the offer is on-catalog or explicitly tagged as custom.
- Sign with full name; "Sami" alone is not enough on enterprise quotes.

## Audit trail
Every state change is recorded in `business/_data/quotes.index.json` with `reviewer` and timestamp. The reviewer is responsible for the words on the quote.
