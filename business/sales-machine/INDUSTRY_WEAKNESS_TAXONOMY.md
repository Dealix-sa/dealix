# Industry Weakness Taxonomy

## Marketing Agencies
- Weakness: Scope creep, manual reporting, no retainer upsell system
- Signal: Inconsistent client retention, late invoices

## Training Companies
- Weakness: Low course completion, no follow-up engine, leads go cold
- Signal: High ad spend, low LTV

## Clinics / Healthcare
- Weakness: Reputation blind spots, no-show rates, manual booking
- Signal: Low Google review velocity, high cancellation

## Real Estate
- Weakness: Lead response time >1 hour, no nurture sequence
- Signal: High lead volume, low close rate

## Logistics
- Weakness: Fragmented tracking, SLA breaches, manual POD
- Signal: Customer complaints on delivery delays

## Consulting Firms
- Weakness: Time leakage, proposal chaos, no productized services
- Signal: Revenue tied to founder hours

## Retail
- Weakness: Stock-outs, slow reordering, no loyalty engine
- Signal: Inventory carrying cost high, repeat purchase low

## B2B Services
- Weakness: Long sales cycles, weak follow-up, no account expansion
- Signal: Pipeline looks full but closes slowly
