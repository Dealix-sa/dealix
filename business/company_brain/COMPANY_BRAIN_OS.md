# Dealix Company Brain OS

## Purpose

Company Brain OS turns scattered company knowledge into a living operating memory that helps leaders decide what to do today, what to watch next, and what to build for future leverage.

## What it contains

### 1. Company Identity Brain

- mission
- positioning
- target customers
- strongest offers
- proof assets
- brand voice
- forbidden claims

### 2. Commercial Brain

- ICPs
- pain signals
- offer mapping
- objection intelligence
- negotiation ranges
- proposal history
- follow-up history

### 3. Operations Brain

- recurring processes
- bottlenecks
- owner map
- SLAs
- delivery risks
- automation opportunities

### 4. Client Brain

- client goals
- current scope
- acceptance criteria
- promised outcomes
- risks
- proof pack
- renewal opportunities

### 5. Future Radar

- market shifts
- competitor signals
- regulatory changes
- technology opportunities
- partnership targets

## Daily outputs

- CEO daily decision
- top five commercial actions
- stuck opportunities
- client delivery risks
- automation opportunities
- weekly board memo

## Implementation sprint

### Day 1-2: capture

Collect current docs, offers, messages, customer notes, CRM exports, WhatsApp categories, and operational routines.

### Day 3-4: structure

Build the knowledge map, source-of-truth folders, tagging system, and decision memory.

### Day 5-7: command outputs

Generate daily CEO report, sales action list, client risk list, and weekly memo.

### Day 8-14: operating rhythm

Install review cadence, update responsibilities, and proof pack workflow.

## Data rules

- Do not put private client secrets into prompts.
- Keep sensitive data scoped to need-to-know.
- Every generated recommendation needs source references or uncertainty label.
- Every external communication draft needs human review.

## Sales angle

Company Brain OS is sold as the missing memory and decision layer for companies that have data but no daily decision system.
