# Brand Intelligence OS

## What it does
Brand Intelligence OS turns brand identity into an operational system, not just a logo or colors.

## Deliverables
- Brand diagnosis
- Positioning statement
- Visual direction
- Tone of voice
- Messaging pillars
- Offer language
- Social templates
- Landing page copy structure
- Sales one-pager
- AI brand-safe prompt library
- Content approval workflow

- Differentiation

### Visual
- Color direction
- Typography direction
- Layout rules
- Social post system
- Presentation style

### Voice

- Executive wording
