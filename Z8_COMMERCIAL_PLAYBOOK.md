# Z8 Commercial Playbook

## Positioning

Dealix sells a daily operating console for companies that need better follow up, sales discipline, leadership visibility, and proof.

## Entry offers

1. 7 day command sprint.
2. 14 day growth operating system.
3. managed command room.

## Best first sectors

- clinics
- agencies
- training companies
- logistics and B2B operations

## Daily sales routine

1. Pick one sector.
2. Prepare 10 accounts.
3. Generate draft routes.
4. Review approvals.
5. Send only after human approval.
6. Record owner decisions.
7. Produce proof summary.

## Discovery questions

- Where do opportunities get lost today?
- Who follows up with leads?
- What channels are active?
- What reports does leadership actually trust?
- What needs approval before outreach?

## Close path

Start with a small sprint. Show operating proof. Then expand to a monthly command room.
