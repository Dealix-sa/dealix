# Scale Observability Plan

## ماذا نراقب؟
- lead flow.
- sales conversion.
- delivery load.
- invoice collection.
- client health.
- automation backlog.
- incidents and postmortems.

## Alert examples
- no new qualified leads for 3 days.
- invoice open more than 7 days.
- client health red.
- delivery hours exceed package budget.
- failed readiness workflow.
