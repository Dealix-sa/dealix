# Dealix — First Revenue Plan / خطة أول إيراد

**Goal:** First diagnostic → first paid Command Sprint → first Proof Pack.
**Posture:** Manual, founder-led, approval-first. No automation, no guarantees.

## The ladder / السلّم
| Rung | Offer | Price (SAR) | Entry |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Free Diagnostic | 0 | Start path → `docs/sales-kit/START_HERE.md` |
| 1 | Command Sprint (7-day) | fixed sprint price | `docs/commercial/sales/P1_REVENUE_INTELLIGENCE_SPRINT_OFFER_AR.md` |
| 2 | Data Pack | one-off | from sprint findings |
| 3 | Managed Ops | monthly retainer | post-sprint upsell |
| 4 | Custom AI build | scoped | enterprise |

## Path to first riyal / الطريق لأول ريال
1. Shortlist 10 warm targets from `data/outreach/first_30_targets.csv`.
2. Founder approves ≤5 outreach drafts (`data/outreach/approval_queue.csv`). **Manual send only.**
3. Land 2 free diagnostics.
4. Convert 1 diagnostic → paid Command Sprint.
5. Deliver the sprint → assemble Proof Pack (`customers/<slug>/10_proof_pack.md`).
6. Offer the upsell from real, verified findings.

## Guardrails / الضوابط
- No real external send without founder approval (see `docs/03_governance/NO_EXTERNAL_ACTION_WITHOUT_APPROVAL.md`).
- No guaranteed revenue or outcome language.
- No customer name published without written consent.
- No cold WhatsApp / LinkedIn automation / scraping.

## Definition of "first revenue won"
- One Command Sprint paid for (real invoice, evidence on file).
- One Proof Pack delivered with verified outcomes.
- One upsell conversation opened from that proof.
