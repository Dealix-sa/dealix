# Founder Launch Command Center

## Daily operating target

- 20 researched companies.
- 10 verified opportunities.
- 5 founder-reviewed outreach drafts.
- 3 human-approved outreach actions.
- 1 diagnostic conversation booked.
- 1 proposal or follow-up moved forward.

## Morning checklist

1. Review yesterday's replies.
2. Update lead ledger.
3. Pick today's top 10 companies.
4. Generate drafts only.
5. Review every message manually.
6. Log every action.

## Evening checklist

1. Update pipeline stage.
2. Write proof notes.
3. Prepare next follow-up draft.
4. Record blockers.
5. Decide tomorrow's top wedge.

## Founder rule

Do not chase all sectors at once. Start with the sector producing the clearest pain.
