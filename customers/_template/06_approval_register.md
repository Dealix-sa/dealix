## Standing rules / قواعد ثابتة
- A0/A1 (internal draft / analysis): may be generated internally.
- A2 (customer-facing draft): **founder review required** before use.
- A3 (external action — email/WhatsApp/LinkedIn/publish/payment link): **explicit founder approval required.**
- A4 (financial/legal/security): explicit approval **plus** evidence.
- A5 (destructive): forbidden unless the founder explicitly requests it.
