# Sales Capacity & Quotas

## قاعدة السعة
كل شخص أو قناة لها قدرة أسبوعية محدودة. لا نبني forecast بدون حساب capacity.

## صيغة مبسطة
- prospects reviewed per week
- messages approved per week
- discovery calls booked
- proposals sent
- pilots closed
- retainers converted

## Quotas أولية
Founder:
- 20 prospects reviewed/day
- 5 high-quality touches/day
- 3 discovery calls/week
- 2 proposals/week
- 1 pilot/week

Growth Operator:
- 100 prospects researched/week
- 30 drafts/week
- CRM cleanup يومي

Partner:
- 5 qualified referrals/month
- 1 discovery/month minimum
