# Dealix — Retention and Expansion Playbook
# دليل الاحتفاظ والتوسعة

**Version:** 1.0

---

## Retention Philosophy

Retention is built in delivery, not after. A client who saw real value renews. A client who didn't sees no reason to stay. The playbook below is about making value visible and expansion natural.

---

## Monthly Rhythm

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Week 1 | System health check — is everything running? |
| Week 2 | Usage report sent to client |
| Week 3 | Identify 1 new automation opportunity |
| Week 4 | Monthly review meeting (30 min) |

---

## Health Check Signals

Watch for:
- Workflow run volume declining (client stopped using it)
- Approval queue backing up (client not reviewing)
- Error rate increasing
- Client not responding to reports

If any signal is present: proactive call within 48 hours.

---

## Expansion Identification

After each delivered project, ask:
1. Which workflows in their environment are still manual?
2. Which other teams could benefit from the same system?
3. Are there new systems being added that could be integrated?
4. Is the volume increasing in ways that justify a larger engagement?

---

## Expansion Playbook

1. **Identify:** Document the manual workflow in a 1-page brief
2. **Quantify:** Estimate operational hours wasted (not guaranteed savings)
3. **Propose:** Send a 1-page expansion proposal (not a full deck)
4. **Price:** Follow PRICING_GUARDRAILS.md floor prices
5. **Frame:** "You've seen what we did in [Department A] — [Department B] has the same pattern"

---

## Churn Prevention

| Risk Signal | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| No logins for 2 weeks | Call — is the system working? |
| Missed monthly review | Reschedule + short health check |
| Complaint about a workflow | Fix within 24h, document root cause |
| Asking about competitors | Understand the gap and address it |
| Budget review / cost cutting | Quantify operational impact before renewal conversation |

---

## Renewal Process

1. Remind founder 60 days before retainer renewal date
2. Send renewal brief 30 days before: what was delivered, value observed, next 3 months plan
3. Confirm renewal 15 days before end date
4. If at risk: escalate to founder for personal call

Estimated value is not Verified value / القيمة التقديرية ليست قيمة مُتحقَّقة
