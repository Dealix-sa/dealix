# Dealix — Channel Playbooks
# دليل القنوات التشغيلي

**Version:** 1.0 | All channels require founder approval before execution.

---

## Channel 1: Email

**Best for:** FM, maintenance, contracting, real estate, B2B services

**Rules:**
- Max 15 new outreach emails per day
- Subject line: specific, < 60 chars, no spam words
- Body: 3-5 short paragraphs, Arabic primary for KSA
- Must include specific company signal in first paragraph
- CTA: one clear ask (usually: "20 minutes this week?")
- No guaranteed outcomes. No fake metrics.

**Template structure:**
1. Specific signal line (what you noticed about them)
2. The pain they likely have (operational, specific)
3. How Dealix addresses it (brief, not salesy)
4. Low-risk entry offer
5. Single clear CTA

**Follow-up:** Max 2 follow-ups per thread. Wait 7 days. Different angle each time.

---

## Channel 2: LinkedIn (Manual Only)

**CRITICAL: No automation. Founder sends all messages personally.**

**Best for:** Consulting, UAE market, international companies, executive-level contacts

**Rules:**
- View profile before connecting (shows genuine interest)
- Max 10 connection requests per day
- Max 8 messages per day (across connections + direct)
- Connection request: 300 char limit, personal reference, no pitch
- Message after connecting: wait 24-48 hours, then send value-first message

**Connection request template:**
"رأيت أعمالكم في [sector] — مجال أتابعه عن كثب. أودّ الاضافة لشبكتي إن مناسب."

**Post-connection message:**
Wait 2 days, then: research-based opener + soft mention of what Dealix does + question, not pitch.

## Channel 3: Website Forms

**Best for:** Legal firms, accounting, conservative sectors where direct email may seem presumptuous

**Rules:**
- Founder submits the form manually after reviewing content
- Max 5 forms per day
- Content must be personalized — not a generic inquiry
- Follow the same persuasion rubric (min 82/100)
- Use the contact form professionally, not as a mass-fill

---

## Channel 4: Phone Intro

**Best for:** FM, maintenance, contracting — decision-makers who prefer voice contact

**Rules:**
- Founder makes all calls. Agent prepares the brief.
- Max 8 calls per day, 9-12am or 3-5pm local time
- Brief includes: company name, buyer title, likely pain, offer, 3 questions to ask
- Never read a script — use brief as preparation, not verbatim

**Call structure:**
1. Introduce: "أنا [الاسم] من Dealix — نعمل مع شركات [sector] على تأتمة [pain]"
2. One question to confirm pain: "هل فريقكم الآن يعاني من [specific pain]؟"
3. If yes: "عندنا طريقة بسيطة نبدأ فيها — تجربة خلال 7 أيام فقط. هل 20 دقيقة هذا الأسبوع ممكنة؟"
4. If no: "فهمت — ما الجزء الأكثر صعوبة في [related workflow]؟"

---

## Channel 5: Referral

**Best for:** Legal, accounting — highest conversion channel in conservative sectors

**Rules:**
- Ask 3 people maximum per day for referrals
- Referral ask must be earned (good relationship, genuine fit)
- Always brief the referrer on what to say: "يشتغلوا مع شركات [sector] على أتمتة [pain]"
- Send the referrer a 3-line brief they can forward directly

**Referral ask script:**
"عندك معرفة في [target sector]؟ نتخصص في [pain area]. لو مناسب، نفسي تعريفنا — 3 سطور أقدر أبعثها لك."

---

## Channel 6: Partner Referral

**Best for:** UAE market, enterprise contacts, sectors where partners have existing trust

**Rules:**
- Partner referrals are pre-approved (no individual approval per message)
- Partner must understand Dealix positioning: governed AI operations, not tools
- Provide partners with a 1-page brief on each offer
- Track partner referrals in CRM with source_ref

---

## Blocked Channels (Non-Negotiable)

The following are ALWAYS blocked. No exceptions. No approvals can unlock them:

- Cold WhatsApp automation
- LinkedIn automation (any tool that automates sends)
- Web scraping
- Auto-dialing / robo calls
- Mass email blasting
- Purchased lead lists without explicit opt-in
