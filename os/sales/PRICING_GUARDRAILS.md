## Minimum Price Floor by Offer — الحد الأدنى للسعر لكل عرض

No project is contracted below these floors. Any exception requires founder signature and written rationale.

لا يُبرَم أي عقد تحت هذه الأسعار الدنيا. أي استثناء يتطلب توقيع المؤسس ومبرراً مكتوباً.

| Offer — العرض | Minimum — الحد الأدنى | Typical Range — النطاق المعتاد | Currency |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI Workflow Audit | 5,000 SAR | 5,000 – 25,000 SAR | SAR |
| Agentic Pilot | 30,000 SAR | 30,000 – 150,000 SAR | SAR |
| Full Agentic System | 150,000 SAR | 150,000 – 750,000 SAR | SAR |
| Monthly Retainer | 8,000 SAR/month | 8,000 – 80,000 SAR/month | SAR |
| Expansion Module | 25,000 SAR | 25,000 – 250,000 SAR | SAR |

**International clients:** Quote in USD. Reference rate: 1 USD = 3.75 SAR (fixed peg). Confirm USD amounts in writing before contract.

## Discount Rules — قواعد الخصم

| Rule — القاعدة | Limit — الحد | Approval Required — الموافقة المطلوبة |
|---|---|---|
| Maximum discount — any engagement | 15% | Founder verbal approval |
| Discount > 15% | Blocked | Founder written sign-off required |
| Discount on first engagement with a new client | 0% — no discount | Non-negotiable |
| Discount in exchange for scope reduction | Allowed | Must be documented in CR or SOW amendment |
| Verbal discount promise | Not valid | Any discount must be written in the proposal or SOW |

**Rule:** Do not lower price under pressure without reducing scope. Price reduction without scope reduction damages margin and sets a precedent. If a client pushes back on price, first explore scope reduction options before any discount.

**القاعدة:** لا تخفض السعر تحت الضغط دون تخفيض النطاق. أي تخفيض في السعر بدون تخفيض في النطاق يضر بالهامش ويخلق سابقة سيئة.

## Payment Terms — شروط الدفع

| Project Type — نوع المشروع | Structure — البنية | Notes — ملاحظات |
|---|---|---|
| Audit (< 30,000 SAR) | 100% upfront before start | No exceptions |
| Pilot (30,000–150,000 SAR) | 50% on SOW signing, 50% on delivery | Milestone-based for projects > 60,000 SAR |
| Full System (> 150,000 SAR) | 40% on signing, 30% at mid-milestone, 30% on final delivery | Milestones defined in SOW |
| Retainer | Monthly in advance, first month on contract signing | Auto-invoiced monthly |
| Expansion Module | Same as pilot structure | Treated as a new project |

**Late payment:** Retainers paused if invoice > 14 days overdue. Project work suspended if milestone payment > 21 days overdue. Notify founder at day 7 overdue.

**الدفع المتأخر:** يُوقف العمل في حالة تأخر دفعة مرحلة أكثر من 21 يوماً. يُبلَّغ المؤسس في اليوم السابع من التأخر.

## Currency Rules — قواعد العملة

- **Primary currency:** SAR (Saudi Riyal) for all GCC clients.
- **International clients:** USD. Convert using 3.75 SAR/USD fixed peg. Never quote in both currencies simultaneously in the same document — choose one and state it clearly.
- **No currency ambiguity:** Every proposal and SOW states the currency explicitly in the payment section header.
- **VAT:** Add 15% KSA VAT to all SAR invoices where applicable. International clients: confirm VAT applicability with founder before invoicing.

## What Never to Do — ما يُمنع فعله

These are non-negotiable prohibitions in the pricing process:

| Prohibited Action — الإجراء المحظور | Why — السبب |
|---|---|
| Quote a price verbally without written follow-up | Verbal quotes create disputes. All prices confirmed in writing within 24 hours. |
| Say "we will figure out pricing later" | Price ambiguity delays projects and damages trust. Price before build. |
| Give a ballpark without understanding scope | Uninformed ballparks become anchor points that are impossible to adjust upward. |
| Agree to a fixed price when data quality is unknown | Unknown data quality is the biggest fixed-price risk. Scope the data review first. |
| Accept "payment on results" or contingency fees | This implies guaranteed outcomes — which we do not offer. |
| Start work before payment of first installment | No exception. Work begins after signed SOW and first payment confirmed. |

## Pricing Escalation Path — مسار التصعيد في التسعير

1. Sales call produces a budget signal → log in CRM → generate proposal draft.
2. Proposal draft generated by agent → founder reviews before sending.
3. Client requests discount → founder decides: reduce scope, or approve limited discount in writing.
4. Client requests exception to payment terms → founder decides in writing.
5. Any price below floor → founder written approval required before proposal is finalized.
