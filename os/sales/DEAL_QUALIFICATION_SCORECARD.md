## BANT+ Scoring Framework — إطار تقييم BANT+

Complete this scorecard at the end of the discovery call. Score each factor and total the result. The total determines the next action.

أكمل هذه البطاقة في نهاية مكالمة الاكتشاف. سجّل نقاط كل عامل واحسب المجموع. المجموع يحدد الإجراء التالي.

## Factor B — Budget | الميزانية

*Is there confirmed budget for this type of project?*
*هل هناك ميزانية مؤكدة لهذا النوع من المشاريع؟*

| Score | Condition — الشرط |
|---|---|
| **3 points** | Budget explicitly confirmed and available now (≥ 10,000 SAR for audit / ≥ 30,000 SAR for pilot) |
| **1 point** | Budget signal only — "we plan to allocate" or "it's in the next quarter's plan" |
| **0 points** | No budget, no timeline for budget, or refused to discuss |

## Factor A — Authority | صلاحية القرار

*Is the contact authorized to approve the contract, or do they have a clear path to that person?*
*هل للمتصل صلاحية الموافقة على العقد، أو لديه مسار واضح لصاحب القرار؟*

| Score | Condition — الشرط |
|---|---|
| **3 points** | Contact is the direct decision maker — can sign the contract |
| **2 points** | Contact is an influencer or champion with documented access to the decision maker |
| **0 points** | Decision authority unknown, or contact is gatekeeper with no clear path forward |

**Score: [0 / 2 / 3]** — Notes: [_________________________]

## Factor N — Need | الحاجة الفعلية

*Has the contact articulated a real, specific operational pain that Dealix can address?*
*هل عبّر المتصل عن ألم تشغيلي حقيقي ومحدد يمكن لديليكس معالجته؟*

| Score | Condition — الشرط |
|---|---|
| **3 points** | Real workflow pain articulated clearly — specific process named, impact described |
| **1 point** | Vague interest — "we're exploring AI" or "we want to be more efficient" without specifics |
| **0 points** | No clear operational need, or need is outside Dealix's service scope |

**Score: [0 / 1 / 3]** — Notes: [_________________________]

## Factor T — Timeline | الجدول الزمني

*Does the contact have a specific timeline for starting the project?*
*هل لدى المتصل جدول زمني محدد لبدء المشروع؟*

| Score | Condition — الشرط |
|---|---|
| **3 points** | Decision and start within 3 months — specific quarter or event driving urgency |
| **1 point** | Timeline 3–6 months — planned but not urgent |
| **0 points** | No timeline, or "whenever the time is right" |

## Factor D — Data Ready | جاهزية البيانات

*Is the data Dealix needs to build the solution accessible and usable?*
*هل البيانات التي تحتاجها ديليكس لبناء الحل متاحة وصالحة للاستخدام؟*

| Score | Condition — الشرط |
|---|---|
| **3 points** | Data source identified, accessible (API or export), and sample data can be shared |
| **1 point** | Data exists but needs preparation — system migration, format conversion, or access approval |
| **0 points** | Data is unavailable, entirely unstructured without a cleanup path, or locked in an inaccessible system |

**Score: [0 / 1 / 3]** — Notes: [_________________________]

## Factor S — Success Defined | تعريف النجاح

*Has the contact articulated what success looks like in measurable terms?*
*هل حدّد المتصل شكل النجاح بمقاييس قابلة للقياس؟*

| Score | Condition — الشرط |
|---|---|
| **3 points** | Clear KPIs or outcomes agreed — e.g., "reduce report prep from 8 hours to 1 hour per week" |
| **1 point** | Vague success definition — "we want things to be faster" or "less manual work" |
| **0 points** | No success criteria defined and contact unwilling or unable to define them |

## Score Review Cadence — دورية مراجعة الدرجات

- Re-score every prospect after each substantive touch (call, email reply, meeting).
- Prospects in Nurture re-scored every 30 days.
- Parked prospects re-scored at the 90-day mark.
- If a prospect's score increases to ≥ 10 from a parked state, reactivate to active pipeline.
