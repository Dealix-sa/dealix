# Dealix — Deal Qualification Scorecard
# بطاقة تأهيل الصفقات

**Version:** 1.0 | Run this after first reply or call.

---

## Qualification Dimensions

Score each dimension 0-10. Minimum to advance to proposal: 60/100.

| Dimension | Weight | Question to Answer |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Problem clarity | 10 | Can they articulate the specific operational pain clearly? |
| Decision authority | 10 | Are we talking to someone who can say yes? |
| Budget signal | 10 | Have they indicated budget availability or similar spend? |
| Timeline urgency | 10 | Is there a specific event driving timing? |
| Data access | 10 | Can they provide sample data or system access for audit? |
| Internal champion | 10 | Is there someone internally pushing for this? |
| Fit score | 10 | Is the company fit_score ≥ 70 (Tier A or B)? |
| Pain confirmed | 10 | Did they confirm the pain we identified during call? |
| Governance fit | 10 | Do they have governance concerns we can address? |
| Relationship quality | 10 | Did the first conversation feel collaborative? |

**Score 80+:** Hot — prioritize proposal within 48h
**Score 60-79:** Warm — advance to proposal with normal timeline
**Score 40-59:** Lukewarm — continue nurturing, not ready for proposal
**Score <40:** Not qualified — move to nurture or archive

---

## Disqualification Criteria (Any One Fails the Deal)

- No budget whatsoever
- No decision authority and no clear path to decision-maker
- Requires guaranteed outcomes (we cannot offer these)
- Requires cold WhatsApp or other blocked channels for their workflow
- Sector is incompatible with our offers
- Company fit_score < 55 (Tier D)
