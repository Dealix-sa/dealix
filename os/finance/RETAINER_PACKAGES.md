# حزم الاشتراك الشهري

---

## Standard Retainer Tiers

### Tier 1: Essentials — 8,000 SAR/month
- Monthly system health check
- Bug fixes (up to 4 hours/month)
- Monthly usage report
- Email support (response within 48h)
- Best for: Audit clients who want basic ongoing support

### Tier 2: Operations — 15,000 SAR/month
- Everything in Essentials
- Workflow improvements (up to 8 hours/month)
- Monthly founder review meeting (30 min)
- Expansion planning
- Priority support (response within 24h)
- Best for: Pilot clients with active workflows

### Tier 3: Growth — 25,000 SAR/month
- Everything in Operations
- New workflow additions (1 per month)
- Bi-weekly check-in meetings
- Quarterly expansion proposal
- Dedicated founder attention
- Best for: Full OS clients scaling operations

### Tier 4: Enterprise — 50,000+ SAR/month
- Custom scope
- Dedicated availability
- Weekly meetings
- Multiple workflow owners
- Custom integrations
- Best for: Large enterprise clients with complex environments

---

## Retainer Activation Rules

1. Retainer requires a delivered project (not sold standalone)
2. Minimum 3-month commitment
3. Payment: monthly in advance
4. Cancellation: 30-day written notice
5. Retainer floor: 8,000 SAR/month — never below

---

## Upsell from Retainer

Quarterly, review with client:
- Which workflows are still manual?
- Which departments could benefit from AI?
- Is current workflow performing at expected quality?
- Propose expansion scope with clear ROI framing

Never promise ROI outcomes — frame as operational improvement estimates.
