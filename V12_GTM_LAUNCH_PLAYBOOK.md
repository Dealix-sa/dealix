# V12 Go-To-Market Launch Kit

## Purpose

V12 turns Dealix from sales materials into a real launch system.

## What it includes

- launch checklist
- first 30 company plan
- 7 day campaign calendar
- proposal folder plan
- onboarding checklist
- KPI dashboard summary
- 4 week close plan

## Daily launch routine

1. Run the GTM kit.
2. Review the first 30 company plan.
3. Pick the top 10 for proposal folders.
4. Review sector materials from U11.
5. Prepare draft routes only.
6. Record owner decisions.
7. Update proof.
8. Review weekly close plan.

## First revenue sprint

Week 1: book three operating reviews.
Week 2: close one paid sprint.
Week 3: deliver proof and expand.
Week 4: propose monthly command room.

## Rule

All commercial actions are reviewed before external use.
