# One Year Roadmap

## Q1 — Surgery and Sales

Goal: create clean spine, sell first 3 services, produce proof packs.

Targets:
- 3 paid clients
- SAR 25k to 40k revenue
- 3 proof packs
- 1 repeatable playbook
- 1 service that can be delivered without new code

## Q2 — Repeat and Systemize

Goal: deliver each service 3 times with consistent quality.

Targets:
- 10 clients served
- 2 to 3 retainers
- SAR 30k to 60k MRR
- Proof Pack Generator v1
- Capital Ledger actively updated

## Q3 — Productize Repetition

Goal: extract modules from repeated delivery.

Targets:
- Client Workspace v1
- Data Quality Dashboard
- Top Opportunities module
- Approvals module
- Proof Pack module
- 5 to 8 retainers

## Q4 — Enterprise Readiness

Goal: prepare for enterprise pilots.

Targets:
- AI Control Tower
- LLM Gateway
- RBAC plan
- Audit export
- Governance Pack
- First enterprise pilot
- SAR 150k to 300k MRR ambition
