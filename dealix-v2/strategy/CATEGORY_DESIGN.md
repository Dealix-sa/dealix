# Category Design

## Old Category

AI agency / automation agency / chatbot provider.

## New Category

Arabic-first AI Operations Partner.

## Problem With Old Category

Most AI vendors sell tools, demos, or automations without operating model, data readiness, governance, proof, or continuous improvement.

## New Belief

AI only creates business value when embedded into data, workflows, approvals, quality checks, and proof systems.

## Dealix Point of View

AI is not a feature. AI is an operating capability.

## Arabic Message

الذكاء الاصطناعي لا ينجح لأنه ذكي فقط؛ ينجح عندما يدخل في عملية واضحة، ببيانات جاهزة، موافقات، جودة، ومقياس أثر.
