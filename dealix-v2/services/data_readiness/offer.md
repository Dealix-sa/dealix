# Data Readiness Assessment

## Promise

Assess whether company data is ready for AI workflows and identify gaps before implementation.

## Deliverables

- Data source inventory
- Data quality score
- PII/sensitivity flags
- Integration gaps
- Readiness report
- Recommended next sprint

## Score

- Source coverage: 20
- Completeness: 20
- Consistency: 15
- Freshness: 10
- PII handling: 15
- Access clarity: 10
- Business mapping: 10
