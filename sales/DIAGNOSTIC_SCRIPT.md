# Free Diagnostic — Script / سكربت التشخيص المجاني

**Goal:** in ~20-30 minutes, find where revenue leaks and decide together
whether a Command Sprint is worth it. No pressure, no guarantees.
الهدف: خلال ~20-30 دقيقة نكتشف أين تتسرّب الإيرادات ونقرّر معاً إن كان الـ
Command Sprint يستحق. بلا ضغط، بلا ضمانات.

> Disclaimer: Estimated outcomes are not guaranteed outcomes /
> النتائج التقديرية ليست نتائج مضمونة.
> This is a conversation script for the founder. No auto-send; any follow-up
> message is drafted for approval.

---

## 0. Before the call / قبل المكالمة

- Confirm it is a **warm** contact (inbound, referral, existing relationship,
  or opt-in) — never a cold/scraped target.
- Read the prospect row in `data/growth/first_30_targets.csv` (consent basis).

---

## 1. Open / الافتتاح (2 min)

> "Thanks for the time. The goal today is simple: I'll ask a few questions to
> find where revenue might be leaking in your funnel, and you'll leave with a
> clear read whether or not we work together."

«شكراً على وقتك. هدف اليوم بسيط: أسأل أسئلة لأكتشف أين قد تتسرّب الإيرادات،
وتخرج بصورة واضحة سواء عملنا معاً أو لا.»

---

## 2. Diagnose / التشخيص (15 min)

Ask, listen, note the leak:

| # | Question (EN) | السؤال (AR) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Where do new leads come from today? | من أين تأتي الـ leads اليوم؟ |
| 2 | What happens in the first hour after a lead arrives? | ماذا يحدث في الساعة الأولى بعد وصول lead؟ |
| 3 | How is lead data stored — clean or messy? | كيف تُخزَّن بيانات الـ leads — نظيفة أم مبعثرة؟ |
| 4 | Who decides which lead to talk to first, and how? | من يقرّر مع من تتحدّث أولاً، وكيف؟ |
| 5 | How much manual prep per week before outreach? | كم ساعة عمل يدوي أسبوعياً قبل التواصل؟ |
| 6 | Can you prove ROI to management today? | هل تستطيع إثبات العائد للإدارة اليوم؟ |

Listen for the biggest leak: messy data, slow first-response, no
prioritization, no proof.

---

## 3. Reflect the leak / عكس التسرّب (3 min)

> "From what you said, the biggest leak looks like **<X>**. A Command Sprint
> would take your own data, clean and score it, surface the opportunities
> hiding in it, and hand you a Proof Pack you can show management — with every
> outbound action drafted for your approval."

State value as an **estimate** with `~` and the disclaimer. Never promise a
number.

---

## 4. Offer the next step / الخطوة التالية (5 min)

- If fit: introduce the **Command Sprint**
  ([`COMMAND_SPRINT_ONE_PAGER.md`](COMMAND_SPRINT_ONE_PAGER.md)).
- Set expectation: data ownership via Source Passport, 7-day governed
  delivery, Proof Pack at the end.
- If not a fit: say so honestly; offer the free tools / a referral.

---

## 5. After the call / بعد المكالمة

- Draft the follow-up (do **not** auto-send) → queue in
  `reports/revenue/outreach_approval_queue.md` as `approval_required`.
- Log the conversation outcome in the sales ledger.
- Founder approves before any message goes out.
