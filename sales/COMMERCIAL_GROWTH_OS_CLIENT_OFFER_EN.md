An intelligent commercial operating system that works safely alongside your
team:

- **Lead sourcing** from allowed sources or your own data.
- **Account qualification** with transparent ICP scores and rationale.
- **Bilingual message drafts** (Arabic / English).
- **Channel cards** for WhatsApp / email / manual channels.
- **Smart reply suggestions** for inbound messages.
- **Negotiation drafts** within approved guardrails (no automatic discounts).
- **Booking options** ready to send.

- **Follow-up tasks** (D1 / D3 / D7).

- **A truthful proof report** based on real numbers.

> All while respecting approvals, opt-in/opt-out, and legal/contractual limits.

## Who it's for

- Saudi B2B companies with lead flow but no follow-up system.
- Sales teams losing opportunities across channels.
- Companies that want to scale outreach safely and compliantly.

## Who it's **not** for

- Anyone wanting high-volume cold email / WhatsApp.
- Anyone demanding guaranteed revenue or results.
- Anyone without lawful data sources.

## What the client must provide

- A list of accounts/leads with their sources (`source_url`) or permission for
  allowed public sources.
- Client rules (allowed channels, approvals).
- Success criteria and metrics.
- A named point of contact.

## What Dealix will **not** do

- Will not send WhatsApp/email/messages without your explicit approval.
- Will not write to your calendar without permission.
- Will not grant a discount, set a final price, or accept a contract for you.
- Will not invent clients, testimonials, or results.
- Will not promise guaranteed results.
- Will not scrape prohibited sources.

## Safety & trust rules

- Default mode: draft-only, no external send.
- Every live action passes safety gates, approvals, and an audit log.
- Full respect for opt-in/opt-out and platform limits.

## Kickoff checklist

- [ ] Sign the scope document (with approval).
- [ ] Receive data/sources.
- [ ] Define allowed channels.
- [ ] Define success metrics.
- [ ] Assign a point of contact.

## Success criteria

- Number of qualified cards ready for review.
- Draft quality (truthful, no claims).
- Meetings booked with approval.
- A clear daily decision list.
- Zero safety/compliance violations.
