# Command Sprint Terms — شروط الـ Sprint (Rung 1)

Terms for the **7-Day Revenue Intelligence Sprint — 499 SAR**. Plain, fair, and
governed.

## What the customer gets

A 7-day, fixed-scope engagement that turns their commercial data into a scored
revenue map plus a Proof Pack. Shape defined in
`docs/delivery/PAID_SPRINT_HANDOFF.md`.

| Day | Deliverable |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Intake, Source Passport, data-quality score |
| 3–4 | Sprint scope + scored revenue map |
| 5–6 | Draft pack, governance review, Proof Pack (score ≥ 70) |
| 7 | Executive brief + upsell recommendation |

## Price & payment

- **499 SAR**, one-time.
- Payment via approved channel only (Moyasar link in sandbox by default).
- **No live charge is created without explicit founder approval** (non-negotiable #8).

## Scope boundaries

- One company, one dataset, one revenue map.
- Out-of-scope requests → a Change Request, not silent scope creep.
- We do not guarantee sales outcomes (non-negotiable #5). We guarantee the
  deliverables above and an honest, sourced read.

## Data & privacy

- Customer data is processed under a Source Passport (origin, owner, consent).
- No PII in logs (non-negotiable #6).
- No scraping of third-party data (non-negotiable #1).

## Definition of done

- Proof Pack score ≥ 70 + at least one Capital Asset registered.
- Every customer-facing artifact ends with the bilingual disclaimer.

## Refund / dispute

If the Proof Pack does not reach score ≥ 70 due to our delivery, the sprint fee
is refunded. Data-quality blockers on the customer side are surfaced on Day 2,
before work proceeds.
