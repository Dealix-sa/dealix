# Command Sprint — Diagnostic Script (Founder)

> A 20–30 minute structured conversation. Goal: find the single clearest revenue
> breakpoint and decide honestly whether a Command Sprint is a fit. No selling
> pressure. No promises. Capture answers into `customers/<slug>/02_diagnostic_summary.md`.

## 0. Frame (1 min)
"This is a short diagnostic, not a pitch. I'll ask about how revenue moves through
your business today. At the end I'll tell you honestly whether a Command Sprint
would help — and if it wouldn't, I'll say so."

## 1. Business shape (5 min)
- What do you sell, and to whom?
- How do deals come in today — referral, inbound, outbound?
- Roughly how many opportunities are open right now?

## 2. The breakpoint (10 min)
- Where do deals most often stall?
- What's the last thing that "fell through the cracks"?
- If one thing in your revenue process worked better next month, what would it be?
- What do you currently use to track this? (CRM / sheet / WhatsApp / nothing)

## 3. Evidence (5 min)
- Can you share (with consent) the data behind that breakpoint? (pipeline export,
  a sheet, recent proposals) — *no logins shared, no scraping.*
- What does "success in 30 days" look like in your words?

## 4. Fit decision (5 min)
Score the fit:
- Clear, single breakpoint? (y/n)
- Data available to work from? (y/n)
- Decision-maker engaged? (y/n)
- Realistic 7-day scope? (y/n)

**3–4 yes → propose Command Sprint.** 0–2 yes → honest redirect / nurture.

## 5. Close
- If fit: "I'll send a short proposal — Command Sprint, 7 days, the five
  deliverables, manual payment, 7-day refund. No guarantees on revenue; what I
  guarantee is the work and the refund window."
- If not fit: name what they should do instead. Add to nurture.

## Never
- Never promise revenue, ranking, or a specific number.
- Never auto-send anything. Every follow-up is founder-approved.
