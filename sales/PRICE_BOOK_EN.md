# Dealix Price Book (English)

> Single source of truth: `os/config/pricing.yml` and `os/config/packages.yml`.
> This document is a human-readable view — on any conflict, the YAML wins.
>
> **All prices are exclusive of 15% VAT (ZATCA).**
> **Sharing any price with a client requires founder approval (gate G03).**

Dealix is not sold as a service — it is sold as a monthly operating system:
**Audit → Pilot → Production → Managed OS → Usage/Add-ons → Command Center**

## 7) Payment Terms

- **Projects:** 50% on signature, 30% after MVP/Pilot, 20% on launch.
- **Subscriptions:** monthly in advance, 6-month minimum, setup separate.
