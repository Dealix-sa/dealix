# Command Sprint — One-Pager (Founder Sales Kit)

**For:** Saudi B2B companies (agencies, consulting, training, IT services, business services).
**What it is:** A 7-day, founder-led engagement that turns your messiest revenue
breakpoint into one executable decision. Dealix is an AI Business Operating System
— not a CRM, not a chatbot.

## The problem we start from
Most companies don't lack tools — they lack a clear next decision. Opportunities
stall, follow-up slips, proposals drift, and there is no single source of proof for
"what should we do next?". Command Sprint starts at the clearest breakpoint and ends
with a decision you can act on.

## What you get in 7 days
1. **Revenue Map** — where revenue leaks today, ranked, from your own data.
2. **Proof Register** — every claim traced to a source. No invented numbers.
3. **Executive Command Brief** — one page: what's happening, why, the one decision now.
4. **Next Action Board** — prioritized, owned, dated actions ready to execute.
5. **Approval Register** — every external action passes your explicit approval first.

## How it runs
| Day | What happens |
|-----|--------------|
| 1 | Diagnostic + data intake (with your consent) |
| 2–3 | Revenue Map + Proof Register |
| 4–5 | Command Brief + Action Board |
| 6 | Governance review — claims verified, approvals logged |
| 7 | Proof Pack delivery + executive review |

## Boundaries (what we never do)
- No guaranteed revenue or ranking — we deliver clarity and a decision, not promises.
- No cold outreach. No scraping. No data from behind a login.
- Nothing is sent or charged automatically — human approval throughout. PDPL-aware.
